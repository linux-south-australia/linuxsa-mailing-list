Re: Modem Speeds

On connection the modem reports modem-PC serial port connection rate (DCE)
by default not the modem-modem connection rate (DTE) which is why you are
getting the 115200 and 57600 number.

Put "M1" on the modem init line (as in "AT &F M1") for the modem to report
the connection speed.
