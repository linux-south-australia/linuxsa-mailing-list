Re: Wiping a drive

> Hi all!
> As a branch from another thread, I'm now curious.  How would
> those on the list go about overwriting every physical
> byte/sector of a linux drive/partition, using linux tools?  This
> is something that is often done in situations involving data of
> a confidential or secure nature, and I was curious as to the
> methods that might be used in a linux environment to achieve
> this action.
> Don.

dd if=/dev/null of=/dev/hda bs=X count=Y

where X and Y good for your drive/partition. Don't blame me if someone will
use it for real. :)
