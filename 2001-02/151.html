> Which one(s) are/is the best one(s) to use: I got this list from somewhere
> ages ago cant remember where. What about the SAARD one??

The best ones to use are the high precision clocks from CSIRO.
The SAARDNet one is also a secondary on a standard computer.

If the delay from the CSIRO clocks is an issue, or you are
using NTP to sync the Kerberos KDC, Win NT domain controller,
or Novell time server then you should by a GPS time source
which you do *not* redistribute outside of your network.
You can also use CDMA mobile phones as a good time source.
