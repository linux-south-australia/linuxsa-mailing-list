RE: Buying Pemtium iii 933 or AMD 1.2 GHZ

HIGHLY recommend the AMD cpu. Even if you were going for an AMD 900Mhz
Athlon, that would outperform a Pentium III 933 !

Origininally, the Intel 815 chipset had a much faster IDE controller than
the VIA KTA-133 Chipset. When the Via KTA-2-133 was released, the gap
between the Intel and Via controller was filled, leaving the AMD the far
better choice as a processor.

This may not suit you of course, if you prefer to spend more money, for a
lower performing product ;-)
