aDSL now works ...

Hi,

Well, after a 5 or 6 hour outage by Telstra'a aDSL network last night (They
would not even respond to a PADI), I now have aDSL via a local ISP working
great.

My next big problem after Telstra came back up was that Mandrake 7.2, which
uses Roaring Penguin PPPoE, decided to add 'noauth' to the pppd command line.

This caused pppd to refuse both CHAP and PAP auth and Telstra dropped the
link.

After I eliminated the 'noauth', everything came up, and download speeds
are great.

I am now looking forward to a fixed IP at some time in the future, but now
have to get wireless in ...

Well done In^H^H, ummm, local ISP.
