If you wnat a really 'polished' install, try Mandrake.

Otherwise, try RH, just be aware of your hardware before you install.
ie know what video, sound and NIC (Network Interface Card) cards and 
what type and which port the mouse is on (though both installs will pick 
about 90% of hardware correctly)

happy installing :)
