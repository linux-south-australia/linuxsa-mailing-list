Unfortunately I don't experience this problem under Mandrake 7.2. Try
the following:

* from the GUI press ctrl+alt+F2 (or F2 or F3 up to F6)
* log in as root
* look at:
  /var/log/messages (use tail -[number] messages | less)
* look at ctrl+alt+F1
 you may see X debug messages

enough to diagnose your problem. And try to turn off your HTML mail. I
know it's a pain, but...
