The Anna Korvathingo virus

Howdy,

This is by no means the best solution to the recent Anna vbs problem, but
it's handy.

Add the following lines to your sendmail.mc file (usually
/etc/mail/sendmail.mc with redhat (6.2 anyway..))

====
HSubject:		$>Check_Subject
D{MPat}Here you go, ;o
D{MMsg}This message may contain a worm virus.

SCheck_Subject
R${MPat} $*		$#error $: 550 ${MMsg}
RRe: ${MPat} $*		$#error $: 550 ${MMsg}
====

and then remake your sendmail.cf file...

 # m4 sendmail.mc > sendmail.cf

then copy that into /etc and restart sendmail (back up the original .cf
first...)

I don't know how to escape the ) in the subject line!

If anyone has better solutions, please post them :)
