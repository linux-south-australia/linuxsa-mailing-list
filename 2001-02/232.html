RE: Sendmail Question

> Ideally it would be nice I could use a database like the access.db
> instead of fiddling with Sendmail's rather esoteric rules...

yep as easy as this ;-0

You need to put

this.domain.com         smtp:[22.22.22.22]
another.domain.com      smtp:box.name.com

in /etc/mailmailertable (RedHat)

Note [] means 'do not do a mx lookup'
