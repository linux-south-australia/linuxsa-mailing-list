Re: Deny List project

At a deep level, I've skimmed all of these words in this thread and 
this keeps coming back to me:

- Why not run your system securely in the first place?

The problem with any form of deny list has partially do to with a 
false sense of security that it might generate - the next big hack is 
guaranteed to come from somewhere that you don't expect!

Simon
