"Alain Satre" <alain@messagebay.com> wrote:

> To restart sshd over and over again, redirecting the output to null?

It's pretty simple - if you didn't install it, how can you be sure it's
sshd?  FWIW, I've post-mortem'ed several boxes where things
like this were done to it.  It's a fairly common thing.  Seeing as
you can't trust the state of any of the binaries/configuration files,
the most prudent course of action is usually to blow the machine
away and reload from good, known media (ie, don't copy files
off his neighbour, as you can't be sure they haven't been trojan'd
either).
