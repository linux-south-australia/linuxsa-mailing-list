Re: The "From" Escape

On Wed, Feb 07, 2001 at 04:04:36PM +1030, David Lloyd wrote:

 > Does anyone know where the "From" escape gets added? Is it at:
 > * MUA or MTA level?
 
Yes :-)

(i.e., some MUA's do it, and some MTA's make up for the fact that the 
MUA hasn't done it before they transmit the message or write it to its
final destination).

 > Better still, is it really that useful?

Definitely.  The separator between two mail messages is a blank line 
followed by a line beginning with the word "From" with a capital F, 
followed by a space.

If you type that string into the body of an email address and it gets
delivered without escaping the "From ", the destination user's mail 
utility will think that it represents a message boundary, and will
either truncate the message or claim that the mailbox is corrupted.

Changing From to \From is quite necessary, believe me.
