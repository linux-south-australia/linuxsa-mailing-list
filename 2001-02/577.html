cool commandlines

the recent talk of tar reminded me of a cool commandline I used the
other day.

The reason I post it it to highlight a good use of the r-tools that so
many people like to flame me about.[1]


$> set noglob
$> (rsh dtwcmx2 tar cf - `pwd`/*.[t,txt,init,l]) | tar xvf -

The was a good solution for me. I had to transfer about 100meg of
complex[2] directory structure from one machine to another, and preserve
all attributes, and the source machine had *no* disk space left.
