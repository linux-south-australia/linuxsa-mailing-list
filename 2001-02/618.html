Re: Backup Drive/Tape problems

Robert!

> The DAT24 responds to commands such as "mt -t /dev/st0 rewind" but gives
> autosense errors when given "mt -t /dev/st0 retension". We are using
> "tar xvbf 20 /dev/st0 / --exclude [/path/filename}" to backup. However

Whilst I can't answer directly, if the tape is giving autosense errors
on then I'd be inclined to say that it may be a tape drive issues rather
than a data issue. You should try using /dev/nst0 as well - st0 will
rewind after commands and such and could rewind underneath you if things
go slightly awry during a backup...
