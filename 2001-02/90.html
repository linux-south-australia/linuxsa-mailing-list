Martin,

Try NTPD or rather NTPDATE from http://www.eecis.udel.edu/~ntp/

instructions on using NTPDATE are here: 
http://www.eecis.udel.edu/~ntp/ntp_spool/html/ntpdate.htm

basically just run something like:

"ntpdate ntp.adelaide.edu.au" will update the time on your machine to the 
correct time, and will get it from ntp.adelaide.edu.au
