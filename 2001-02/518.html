it is possible run video over coax at distances approaching this figure
- though it requires careful choice of camera and some equalisation
hardware to combat attenuation of the high frequency components of the
video signal.
 
> Any chance of two laptops and some sort of network,(once againthis might
> be awkward, most LAN technologies wil probably require a repeater I woudl

you can use fibre, for example, without a repeater.

there are many products that will let you run compressed video and audio
over ethernet or phone/ISDN line. depending on your needs, this might be
one method to bridge the 1km distance (ie: use telstra's cabling...) and
then send the video to the laptop for capture. drop me a line if you
would like more info.
