Re: Open SSH V2.5

You need to goto your openssh_src_dir/contrib/ and copy ssh.pam (or
something like that) to /etc/pam.d

It should look something like

#%PAM-1.0
auth       required     /lib/security/pam_pwdb.so shadow nodelay
auth       required     /lib/security/pam_nologin.so
account    required     /lib/security/pam_pwdb.so
password   required     /lib/security/pam_cracklib.so
password   required     /lib/security/pam_pwdb.so shadow nullok
use_authtok
session    required     /lib/security/pam_pwdb.so
session    required     /lib/security/pam_limits.so

Root logins (even using SSH) are kinda bad, login and $ su -
There are sniffers that take advantage of an SSH bug.

The SANS group has a nice newsletter with vulnerabilities for all
platforms (you can mix and match which ones you want to receive).  It
comes out once a week.
