Maybe not. Are they on the same IDE channel? Silly as may sound, you may
have an IDE configuration problem. I just finished upgrading to 4.0.1
and in there was a `fix' to solve problems like this. Basically it said
that it would force a transfer mode instead of trying to be clever and
talk to the drive.

Try moving the drives onto seperate cables, and/or upgrading to a
different kernel.
