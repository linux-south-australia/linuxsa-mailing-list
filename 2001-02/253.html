Re: auto-respond

An alias.
Pipe the input into a script.
That is all majordomo does to get the mail from your mailer.

edit /etc/aliases or wherever it is on your system.
eg 

testemail:  "|/usr/local/bin/emailtestscript"


Dont forget to run newaliases.

Of course you need to write email script, all it needs is something that
pipes email to sendmail.
if you pipe somethign like:

To: someone@somewhere.com
Date: Thu, 8 Feb 2001 19:12:23 +1030 (CST)
Subject: test reply

blah blah blah


to sendmail -t that should probably work.

You need to extract the email address, I would use perl, or whatever
scripting lagnauge you are most familiar with.

The above in general should work I think, the detail particularly of the
message format might be a tad out.
