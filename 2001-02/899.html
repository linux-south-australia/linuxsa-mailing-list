Re: Sendmail-LDAP

> Pre wrote:
> 
> Hi all,
> 
> I am am running sendmail with openldap (linux rpm) on redhat 6.1. The
> ldap server is configured and responds well to ldapsearch uid=*. In
> order to integrate sendmail with LDAP, PAM is installed with
> pam_ldap and nss_ldap (all of them are rpm). While sending mail to
> LDAP users locally with command sendmail -v <LDAP username> is returns
> error "unknown user"

Have you added the line:

passwd:	files ldap

to /etc/nsswitch.conf and installed nss_ldap (available as an RPM as
well as from http://www.padl.com/).

Are you using sendmail's LDAP routing or LDAP maps? You may want to add
some mail attributes to your accounts (depending on which you are
using).

Have you tried another PAM application (I usually test with something
like POP3 - you can telnet to port 110 on the local machine and log in)?

Hope it helps,
