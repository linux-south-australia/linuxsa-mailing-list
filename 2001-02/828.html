Re: Deny List project

For one the site is a LOT faster.
For two you have full control of your list still.  It's on your server.
For three it is not designed for anti-spam but for denying hackers access to
your systems.  It's a way of getting hacker/script kiddie/malcontent IP
address' and adding them to your hosts.deny file so they can't connect to
your servers.  Thus protecting your servers.

That's about it for now.  I started it because I am a member of the cobalt
list and lot of folks there have been trying to compile everyone else's
list's together.  Well, now we just have a centralized location.
