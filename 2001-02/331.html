Re: Copying the MBR from one HD to another

At 09:59 PM 2/11/01 -0800, Adam W wrote:
>Hi all,
>Just say i wanted to dupe one HD to another, lets say i have a 1 Gb HD and i
>wanna throw all the data off that one Gig HD onto a 20gig hard drive and
>throw the old 1 gig away and use the 20 gig.
>How would i copy the MBR so the 20gig is bootable??
>
>or is it jus a matter of activating the partition in fdisk??

Neither ...

1. Partition the 20G as you want it, but probably make it similar to the 1GB
2. Copy your root file system and others to the new 20G drive.

   - mount /dev/hdb1 /mnt/root
   - cp -avf /root /mnt/root

   and so on ... However, it assumes you have everything in /

3. lilo -r /mnt/root

4. shutdown and swap drives including jumpers.
