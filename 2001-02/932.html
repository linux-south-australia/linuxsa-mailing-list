Re: QNX

I looked at QNX some years ago, before I heard about Linux.  The claims
are that QNX is an immensely compact and scaleable operating system,
operating in embedded systems (such as an intelligent appliance) through
to large multiprocessor systems (like SACBH - the grain handling
people).  Their demo 3.5" floppy disk could boot a 386, start up an
X-windows system, find the modem, dial up your ISP (you had to enter the
phone number and password) and fire up a passable browser.  It worked! 
They wouldn't be drawn on a price for a one-off application.  Major
application is in industrial control systems.
