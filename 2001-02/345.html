Hi Evan,

You might also want to consider that the Linux RAD environment
is the web, not the desktop.  That is, PHP4 and Zope more than
Kylix.  Web-based applications are probably a good strategic
move, as they will automatically work on the next generation
of wireless-attached PDAs whereas I wouldn't expect Kylix for
PalmOS anytime soon.

Also that it might be difficult to show Linux's lower cost
of network administration with just one host.  For example,
the Windows NT cost of adminitsration is so high that I
doubt your company has all the security hot fixes installed.
