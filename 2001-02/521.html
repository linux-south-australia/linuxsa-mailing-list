Re: Buying Pemtium iii 933 or AMD 1.2 GHZ

Have people had any success with VIA chipsets?  I had tried a "Frys Bundle"
motherboard and cpu.  I had a Intel pentium 533Mhz with a Shuttle mother board,
AV61 Via chipset.  Everything I tried to get this board working failed.  My
first sign was memory dumps on RH6.2 when doing an ls, or find, or locate.  I
tried everything I could with no success.  I have switched to a FIC m/b with
intel 440BX chipset and a celeron 700Mhz. Its working great now, not a single
problem from install.  But I dont think Ill ever buy a VIA chipset again.
