RE: terminal emulators

Monday, February 12, 2001, 8:58:50 AM, David wrote:

> Which win32 terminal emulators are people using to access linux? Penguinet
> has slow screen refreshes and doesn't seem to emulate properly in telnet
> mode, but is fine for ssh. 
> I'm particularly after something that emulates the linux terminal.

I use TeraTerm Pro 2.3 (with ttsh 1.5.1) and SecureCRT 3.1

Tera  Term Pro is handy for ease of setup, public key support etc, and
it's  free.  Whereas  SecureCRT  costs  money  but it's Linux terminal
emulation is pretty spot on.
