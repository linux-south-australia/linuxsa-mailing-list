> Ideally it would be nice I could use a database like the access.db

What about adding an entry to your virtusertable?  If you are wanting to
forward mail addressed to <username>@otherdomain.com on to the MTA
"that.mta.com", then something like this might work:

@otherdomain.com %1@that.mta.com

You may also have to tell sendmail to accept mail for otherdomain.com:

Cwlocalhost otherdomain.com

Another way might be to change the MX record for "otherdomain.com".

Just some thoughts.
