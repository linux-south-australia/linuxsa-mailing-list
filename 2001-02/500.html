RE: Buying Pemtium iii 933 or AMD 1.2 GHZ

Many people tend to lean towards the Intel architechture because it has the
"Brand Name" or the "Reputation".
... often the same reasons why people choose say a Windows NT server over
using a Linux Server ;-)

In the early days, eg, K5 processors AMD chips were rather on the dodgy
side. And also when the athlons were released there were a few dodgy
mainboards (eg, AMD Irongate).

Good idea to check www.tomshardware.com for some benchmarks to compare the
brands and speeds. But again, if you prefer to spend more for a slower chip,
and lately I have found to be lower quality chip, then you will be rewarded
with some nice bonus features such as :

* A free fan (valued at $12)
* Groovy little pentium III sticker to place on your case
* A pretty box

Average price goes as follows :

AMD Athlon 1 GHZ : $400 approx
Intel Pentium III 1 GHZ : $700 approx

again keeping in mind the athlons faster and better, but you have to spend
$12 on a fan ... (and you don't get the groovy sticker and pretty box).

Another aspect you must keep in mind the AMD Boards are averaging approx
$20 - $50 chepaer than the Intel 815 boards, however, you don't get dodgy
on-board video.

Again, hope this helps a litte.

Jeremy
