Re: [Sort of OT] Username Conventions

Nicholas Morrison wrote:

> I've seen (I can't bloody remember where) a document arguing that
> because of limiting factors such as "there will ALWAYS be two users
> sharing the same name", you should have no 'standard' for naming
> users.

I disagree. We have a standard and even when there are two people in the
same year with the same name (happened last year), they still have
different usernames (well, the students anyway, staff are a different
matter). We use the old 'first letter of the first name, first five of
the last name and a 2 digit number on the end' scheme. So my username
would be:

mgedde01

If Michael Geddes (if he existed) were to attend the same school, he'd
be:

mgedde02

We have had students up to 04, but could have up to 99.

It works. :-)

Hope it helps,
