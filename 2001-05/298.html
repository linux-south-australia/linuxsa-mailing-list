Re: CABLE net access in SA

Andrew,

My 2nd hand experience is that cable was fine till it got over-subscribed
which is consistent with what you say Andrew. Although recently a lot of
people have been going onto the DOCSIS network which require a different
(DOCSIS) modem. Their subsequent responce times have improved by an order
of magnitude. 

I don't want to get locked into a service which is going to degrade as it
did in the east people were suffering for months waiting for upgrade to
services to cope with the load.

Incidently I dont think optus ever suffered the problem Telstra did. As I
have friends on both and OPtus people were always wrapped with their
connection.
