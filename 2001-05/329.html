RE: CABLE net access in SA

primus != The Internet just as ADSL != The Internet :)

I was using my own ADSL service this morning across the time period 
when these other providers were down.

Everyone has failures in infrastructure or network links at times, 
tho - the questions that matter are along the lines of 'how often', 
'how well do they handle these failures' and 'do they lie about them'

:)

NB we operate multiple high bandwidth upstream links to multiple 
national providers. I can tell you from first hand and recent 
experience that we 'loose' at least one of them at least once a 
month, sometimes far more often than that. We generally just route 
around the failures (and they're detected with a real time monitoring 
system and most of the routing-around is automatic as a result, in 
cases where BGP doesn't do it for us immediately)
