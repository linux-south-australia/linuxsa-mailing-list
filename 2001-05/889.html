Re: Indial Server options [was: Re: ISDN PRI capacity]

>The 3661 chasis still has a RRP of ~$33K. Can a smaller (read: cheaper)
>router be employed to acheive a similar affect? I seem to recall Mark
>(Newton) saying something about a 1600 beeing suitable.

If you still want to own the hardware (my employer too can arrange virtual
dialins - especially cheap if you order during June) the Cisco 2600 series
can take some of the same cards used in the 3600 systems, including an E1
(PRI/Onramp30) interface. It might not have the grunt you need, however, so
you're back in the same boat as a 2nd hand 5200.

There are a few vendors (ie: digi, arial) selling E1/modem cards for linux
systems, but the pricing isn't particularly attractive when you look at the
dedicated alternatives. Livingston/Lucent PM3's are getting very cheap and
plentiful on the 2nd hand market.
