Re: Hanging problem.

Hi grant,

I have seen slightly similar things happen as well.

1. I am no ssh guru but on establisihing a session there is a minor lag in
key establishment but I wouldnt expect it to be noticeable.

2. Have you run tcpdump from a terminal window and just looked at how the
traffic was flowing? Was there noticeable lag between packets going from
one box to the celeron?

Depending of course on the volume of traffic you may need to filter things
so you can see it. 
Is the network switched? if so you may be ok in terms of noise traffic. 

you may want to monitor each nic to see if one is interfering with the
other. (I have seen other devices sharing the same IRQ which resulted in
similar behaviour as they each took their turn.) You may want to check the
configs on the cards.

Regards

Evan
