Unix userids are effectively case sensitive.  For example, my userid is
different to the another, DavidN, which I just created.  Userids are also
the standard mailbox name (ie to the left of the @).  A case insensitive
MTA would probably be broken.  I notice that sendmail very nicely sends
DavidN's mail to me.  That's what I mean by broken.
