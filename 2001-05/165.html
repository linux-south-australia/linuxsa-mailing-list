Re: IDENTD is it really needed for sendmail?

Michael Kratz wrote:

> on a system that is running sendmail a its primary MTA does one really have
> to run IDENTD?

No.  Trusting information returned by identd is poor, at best.  I make
it a practice to disable Sendmail's ident lookups (setting confTO_IDENT
to zero in the mc file does this, from memory), which also helps when
talking to sites who drop (as opposed to reject) traffic to ident ports.
