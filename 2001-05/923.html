right now. 256/64kbps, with 500Mb included, and no minimum contract length,

$129 setup
$89/month
$0.18/excess MB

+ cost of ADSL modem ($595 for Alcatel Ethernet Bridge -- you can now get a
DLink one for $495... damn!)

connected to my modem, I have a switch and two computers (one firewall, one
desktop), and plan to add a few more over time. I host a domain from home
over this, presently running bind, sendmail, apache and sshd, and have no
restrictions (AFAIK). I can even serve up as much data as I want, without
incurring a penalty (though at 64k, it's not going to be a lot of data ;) )
