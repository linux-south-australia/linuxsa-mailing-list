AusCERT just e-mailed an advisory on this virus this afternoon, it's
imaginatively called the "Homepage.HTML.vbs" virus. It's the usual
story, affecting Outlook users who haven't applied patches or clamped
down on vbs attachments. It sends itself to everyone in the victim's
address book before trying to delete all traces of itself. The advisory
isn't on AusCERT's web site (auscert.org.au) yet though.
