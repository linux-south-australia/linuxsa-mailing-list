Topic: CVS over-the-net linuxSA links/FAQs thing.

me that it's all been suggested/done/rejected/discussed
before, please e-mail me _off-list_ about it.

that you should not waste bandwidth telling me that?

Ah, now what I wanted to say was that at this point on
the www:

http://www.linuxsa.org.au/links.html

there's the potential to create a useful set of 
starting links and mini-FAQs etc.
(So far, so obvious.)
And what might be a good idea to create at that point
a useful set of links and FAQs could be some sort
of remote, over-the-net CVS sort of thing.
This would cut the administrative load down to
very little. Then a groups of trusted [well, relatively
speaking] individuals in linuxSA could contribute
page creations and modifications to build up a useful
set of links etc.
And if any garbage gets into it, the administrator
could back out the changes.

Personally, I don't think I'm old enough to
accept such a level of responsibility.
I just want to suggest things for other people to do.
I wouldn't mind being one of the contributors though.
