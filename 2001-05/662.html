Re: Email addresses.

Nicholas Morrison wrote:
> 
> Is it legal (RFC-wise) to use single quotes in the username part of an
> email address?
>  eg  timbo_o'lochlan@somewhere.not

The rules (RFC822) say that you can have any character
to the left of the @ except whitespace and some special
characters.  An apostrophe is not a special character.

The rules about the left of the @ are pretty liberal,
as this simplifies gatewaying into non-SMTP mail systems.

It also means that the text left of the @ is case
sensitive whereas text to the right of the @ is
case independent in English.
