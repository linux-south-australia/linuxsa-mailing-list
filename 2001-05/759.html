Re: Hanging problem.

On Wed, 23 May 2001, Grant wrote:

> I have a celeron 566, 256 MB SDRAM (2 x 128 Kingmax), 20.5 GB IBM hard
> drive, two realtek NIC's. This is over a 100 Mbps LAN.

Try an FTP. If it exhibits bursty behaviour in the FTP too, check that
the Ethernet ports and the switch ports are all set to full duplex, or
set to half duplex if they're plugged into a hub.

If it's a cross-over cable, it's OK to have full duplex but you may
want to experiment with this.
