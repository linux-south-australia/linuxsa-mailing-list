RE: [Sort of OT] Username Conventions

8 characters for a username is a well known standard.  We used to have long 
usernames because Novell allowed us to (and setup prior to my time).  Linux 
was fairly friendly too so this was ok.  Enter Solaris.  It told me to 
bugger off and rightly so.  We had to come up with a standard convention 
for usernames to limit them to 8 characters.  In a school where we have 
3200+ usernames, we have about 4 duplicates which were easily changed.  Not 
a bad failure rate for the scheme.

The moral?  get a scheme in place very early on to avoid extra work at a 
later date.

Mark Collis
Trinity College
