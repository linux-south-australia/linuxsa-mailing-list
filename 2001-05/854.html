Re: tape backup solutions

Matthew Geddes wrote:
> 
> Amanda uses tar/gzip or dump. I know tar and gzip can tell if there's a
> data integrity problem. I'm sure it wouldn't take much to filter your
> backup "stream" through something like PGP either.

Be a tad careful here.  The more stuff you put between the
disk and the tape the more tape stalls you will have.

We found it better to disable compression in Amanda and
let the tape drive do the compression.  Then the backup
process wasn't CPU-bound and the streaming tape drive
always had pending blocks to write.

As for the integrity of the restore, it's easily
checked for the important stuff by using Tripwire
once you get the files back.
