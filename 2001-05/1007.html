Evan!

> I recently was forced (thanks BPA) to change my nic and RH7.1 recognised
> the new one and asked if it should update the settings for eth0 etc. I
> answered yes but now all I can get to is the router (freesco).
> I have specified the router as the gateway but it doesnt want to make it
> through any suggestions are appreciated.

If you're running X, then use "netcfg" (either as root, sudo root or
su'ed to root) and redo the settings for eth0. If you're not, then go
wander into /etc/sysconfig/network/network-scripts and look at
ifcfg-eth0 (or eth1 or eth2 depending on your device names) for a
starter...
