I would suggest you upgrade to a 2.4 kernel and use the new iptables for
portforwarding. Anything past 2.4.2 should be able to handle internal
and external port forwarding. I have had huge pains using 2.2 kernel
with port forwarding, rinetd mangles your logs (everything appears to
come from the firewall machine). There is even a section of the 2.4 NAT
howto on this problem.

Good luck
