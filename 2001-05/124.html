[ANNOUNCEMENT] SA AUUG Chapter Meeting

UPCOMING AUUG - SA MEETING
--------------------------

AUUG Inc, the Australian Unix and Open Systems User Group, would like to
invite you to the next SA chapter meeting. Its details are:

Date:        2 May 2001
Venue:       Marcellina's Restaurant, Hindley St
Time:        7 PM
Speaker:     David Bullock on XML

The meeting will be in Bebes room which is a part of Marcellina's
Restaurant. Marcellina's is fully licensed and serves excellent Italian
and Australian style food. Our meetings are very informal and our aim is
to help all users of Unix and Unix like operating systems meet each
other to talk technical matters and get to know one another face to
face.

Everyone is welcome to attend!


David Lloyd
