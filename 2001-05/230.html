Success on a Toshiba Tecra 8100

I finally managed to get X running for a Toshiba Tecra 8100. It looks
like you either:

1) Install RedHat 7.1
 - works seemlessly

2) Install XFree86-4.0.3
 - probably a more painful solution

I'd go (1) because 7.1 comes with a 2.4.X series kernel and they support
laptop stuff better.

Now I've finally managed to introduce my housemate to the world of Linux
and he kinda got overwhelmed by the number of options in GNOME...and
apart from having to explain to him the benefits of not having
administrator rights _all_ the time I think he'll go with it.
