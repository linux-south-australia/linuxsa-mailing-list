Hi,

> Question 1:
> It sounds like this means that a UPS is now of doubtful value,
> if one is using a UPS for the sole reason of preventing
> crash-time damage to file systems.
> Is that right?
> If that's true, I may have just wasted A$1350.

UPSs Advantages include:
No down time for short outages.
Lower risk of hard ware dammage due to interactions between OS and UPS to
allow OS shutdown for longer outages.
Smothing of power fluctuations, can have some good to equipment.
Protection of power spikes, up to and including lightening (you might not
have a UPS but you will have your computer).
Protection of your modem from same.

You wouldn't have wasted that money in my opinion.
