Found out why Connect.com does not handle PPP from Linux ...

Hi,

Some of you might remember that I posted about a month ago complaining that
I was having problems with GRIC via Connect.Com.

The problem was that Connect would not accept a PPP dialup session from
Linux (via rp3-config etc). However, it would accept Windows dial-up
connections.

I have just figured it out by dialing Connect in Adelaide.

It turns out that they will only correctly handle dialup if you let PPP do
the authentication, and not if you try to authenticate before starting PPP!

Our very own Internode gets it right both ways (hmmm, Simon, you were fond
of that song, wern't you :-)

Now to bang on Connect's door, but I suspect they won't bother listening to
their customers :-(
