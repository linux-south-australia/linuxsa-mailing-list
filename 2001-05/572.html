The IEEE has finally made access to the 802-series of standards
free of IEEE charges, at least for standards older than six months.
The list of released standards is attached; it includes standards for
Ethernet (including Gigabit Ethernet), 8021.Q VLANs and 802.11
wireless.

http://standards.ieee.org/getieee802/

The terms of use do not permit mirroring or collecting the
standards together in a corporate-wide archive.  This is a
shame as the entire set of released standards is 51MB.
