Adam, you have just enunciated a common misconception. VMware is offering
you a product that is not GPL'd. Moreover, it is a product that some people
find very useful.

They are doing nothing that is illegal, immoral or fattening, in my view.

>You have to *pay* (what a dirty word) for their software.

Ummm, TANSTAFL. If you do not like the price, use an alternative. You can
use Wine (GPL'd and thus free, except for the cost of your time in getting
it running), Win4Lin, not free, but much cheaper than VMware, or Plex86
which is free and promises to do as much as VMware does, but is not there
yet (www.plex86.org).

I wrote an article on the alternatives. It is up on www.linuxworld.com.

Are you going to pay for my time in providing you with the service of
spelling out the alteratives for you?
