One of the easiest ways to fix these problems is to boot from the
installation CD.

I haven't seen RH7.1 yet, but I imagine that something like:

LILO: linux single root=/dev/hdb1??? initrd=

Will get you up under Linux.

Then a simple 'lilo -v' will fix it as long as your lilo.conf is OK.
