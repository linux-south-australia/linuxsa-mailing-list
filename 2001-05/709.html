Perhaps the roles of mail delivery agent and mail transfer agent
are being confused.

Sendmail acting as a mail delivery agent can apply whatever casing
rules it pleases.  This can do nice thing like downcase "ROOT" to
"root", or bounce mail addressed to "daemon".

Sendmail acting as a mail transfer agent (ie 'relay') can do none
of those things.
