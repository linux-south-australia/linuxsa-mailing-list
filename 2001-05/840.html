Re: tape backup solutions

For the local box I have a dds4 tape drive, and I use tar called from a
bash script. The script workes out what needs to be backed up, makes a
list and runs tar. Then it emails me and tells me it done. It has
whatever features you script, and works fine for simpleish setups.
