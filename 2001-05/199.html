> Would the violation be due to PAM? Or something else?

No, not PAM.  TFTP doesn't do any authentication.

I presume you're trying to get files out from the TFTP server to
another machine?

Here are some things to try:
- upgrade to the tftpd from RH7.1
- specifying /tftpboot on the tftpd command line
- downloading 'filename'
- downloading '/tftpboot/filename'

Hopefully one of those suggestions will work.  Failing that, try
running strace over tftpd to see what happens.
