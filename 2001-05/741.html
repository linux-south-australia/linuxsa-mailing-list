Re: hda - irq timeout

> Can someone please tell me what the following message relates to?
> 
> May 19 03:40:55 marvin kernel: hda: irq timeout: status=0xd0 { Busy }
> May 19 03:40:55 marvin kernel: ide0: reset: success

I would say that the ide driver is expecting an I/O operation to complete
within a certain time and it isn't.  The drive remains busy.  Linux then
attempts to reset the the bus, which suceeds.  It's not a good thing, but
it also might just be a harmless quirk.  (I wouldn't count on that, though.)
