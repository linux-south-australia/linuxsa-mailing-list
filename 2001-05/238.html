Re: Networking question

We recently upgraded our network to Cisco 3640 and 3660 routers from
Portmaster PM3's.

We also have several customers using Banksia WebRamps to connect. After the
upgrade none of the WebRamp customers could send email larger than a few
bytes. We found out that reducing the MTU of the WebRamp to around 700
solved this problem.

It was the first time that I had seen this issue but it is apparently quite
common.
