I continue to wonder whether those people who are still holding out
waiting for "cheap" ADSL will ever run the Australian prices through
a currency converter to find out what it costs in US dollars.  Australian
ISPs in general have prices which are comparable to US ISPs -- Even
though US ISPs don't have to worry about multiple billions of dollars
per year to finance the circuits across the Pacific Ocean (which are
paid in US dollars, by the way, which is why the currency coverter would
yield a halfway decent comparison point). 

Compare an Australian $39.95 dialup account to a US $39.95 dialup account
and you can even put up a pretty good case to say that .au ISPs are
sometimes 50% the price of US ISPs.
