> It is expected and required behaviour of a company to destroy (kill)

be consider enlightened self-interest to keep your competitors alive.

> It seems to me that the majority of people would be unhappy if they
> realized that companies not only are not moral entities, they are
> actually required to act amorally.

If a companies morality is judged by the decisions that its management
makes, I see no reason to believe that they are required to be without
morals.  Many companies exhibit clearly good morals, for example by
donating large amounts of money to worthwhile charities.
