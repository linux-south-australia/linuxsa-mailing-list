Re: IP PortFW

I found the reverse proxy stuff in apache does it quite cleany.
Something like this:

<VirtualHost xxxx.xxxx.com.au:80>
    ServerAdmin ant@steadycom.com.au
    ServerName xxxx.xxxx.com.au
    ProxyPass / http://xxx.internal/
    ProxyPassReverse / http://xxx.internal/
</VirtualHost>
