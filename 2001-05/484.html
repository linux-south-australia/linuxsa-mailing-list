> I woudl like ot know baout your experiecnes, and in particular woudl like

There's Glade for developing GUIs in GTK, which you can combine with
other stuff like gIDE. gIDE still has a way to go, though.

KDE has kDevelop, which has a GUI builder and some form of project
management. Never used it, though.

> Also isterested in anyother IDEs that are avaialbe for linux i386 for
> C/C++ and java, ideally with graphical gui builders.

There's JBuilder from Borland, and I believe there's a free community
edition. Also Sun put out Forte for Java (Community edition is free) but
don't try running these on anything less than a PIII 600+ 256MB RAM etc.
