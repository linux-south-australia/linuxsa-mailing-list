Re: ISDN PRI capacity

"Andrew Reid" <andrew.reid@plug.cx> wrote:

> If I've got 1 ISDN PRI module with the appropriate connection into it,
> how many 64Kbit connections will it sustain? Is it 30 or something
> around that?

30 - you get 32 by 64K channels in a 2.048 meg stream - 30 of those are
"B" (data) channels, 1 is "D" (signalling - used for call setup and
teardown) and 1 is for clock synchronisation.
