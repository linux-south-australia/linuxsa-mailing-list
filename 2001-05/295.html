Re: CABLE net access in SA

On 09 May 2001 09:21:55 +0900, Evan Bourlotos wrote:

> It seems attractive more so than ADSL at this point. Any opinions?

If the Telstra/Optus cable services in other states are anything to go
by, I'd completely disagree.

Once the cable service gets particularly popular, you've got a lot of
data moving through the network. My understanding is that Telstra/Optus
provided these services, but didn't upgrade the cable network to
accomodate for the extra traffic.

+--------------------+                   +----------------------+
|     Cable Users    |<----------------->|    Telstra/Optus     |
+--------------------+                   |       Network        |
                                         +----------------------+

The link between the 'Cable Users' and 'Telstra/Optus Network' has got
extremely congested. This meant that when you got home at 6pm, you'd
have nice long ping times and slow access.

I'd be more likely to lean to ADSL. It's more likely to be taken
seriously by the providers.
