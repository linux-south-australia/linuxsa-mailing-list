Re: some sort of network problem???

> Is there something new with the 2.4 kernel, or am I missing something
> stoopid. What do I even do to start testing this sort of thing? I mean I
can
> ping and collect mail and browse some sites perfectly - tho sending mail
can
> be glitchy.

Yes, it's called TCP Explicit Congestion Notification. You'll find it in
your kernel configuration under Networking Options. You can either turn it
off permanently by recompiling your kernel to exclude support for it, or you
can do a

echo 0 > /proc/sys/net/ipv4/tcp_ecn

To turn it off until the next reboot.
