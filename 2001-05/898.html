Well, I have looked at the trace, and the interesting thing is that the MSS
for your SMTP connection is 1460, but the MTU for one of your links is 500.
Do you have a PPP connection to Big Pong? Have you set an MTU of 500?

It would seem that your segments are being sent out with 500 bytes of data
in them, but when you add the TCP and IP headers (20 bytes each) you get
540 bytes, which causes fragmentation. I suspect that some of the fragments
are getting dropped, and none of your data gets through. 

This would happen just when it goes to transfer the body of the message.
