Thanks for all your help Mike,

	I have managed to get it going correctly by compiling in the "Special UDMA 
Feature" in the 2.4 kernel. This overrides the bios limitation imposed by 
promise (see /usr/src/linux/drivers/ide/pdc202xx.c for more info) and all 
drives are running at full speed as they should.

thanks again for your help
