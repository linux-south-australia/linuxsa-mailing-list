Ummm, Scott, the driver seems to be part of your kernel, and has found the
card! Check that script you are using, and if it objects to the error
caused when you tried to re-load the driver, you could hack the script so
it does not try to check for the result of the insmod/modprobe, or you
could check for the driver by looking at interrupts before trying to
insmod/modprobe the driver.

If the script is failing because it can't load the driver, try doing the
dialing steps manually, or hack the test out and let it fall thru to the
next step!
