Re: Email addresses.

Nicholas Morrison wrote:
> 
> Is it legal (RFC-wise) to use single quotes in the username part of an
> email address?
> 
> I notice Microsoft Exchange Server allows it...
> 
>  eg  timbo_o'lochlan@somewhere.not
> 
> Just throwing another thread into this funky carpet we're weaving :)

Don't know to be sure (I'd guess not), but there's no RFC that defines
usernames on server operating systems that I know of. It may be legal
under RFC822 or it's successors, but it is more likely that there will
be some system that will barf. It's often easier to keep it to
alphanumeric chars.
