Re: cron jobs for services

Corey Gilmore wrote:

> I was wondering what kind of solutions people use to keep services
> running.  Apache, mysql, bwbar (www.kernel.org) and a few others come to
> mind.

init and inittab is the simplest, if your processes don't daemonise
themselves (but you'll need to be root to shutdown a process, as you
do with most packages).  If they do, Dan Bernstein has, as part of
his daemontools package[1], a program called fghack for forcing a
process to wait until the child exits.  Most daemons written these
days are being reasonable and having a non-daemon flag on them these
days.
