David Lloyd wrote:

> * the TST light flashes on the modem
> * pings to my ISP either fail totally or take up to 30 seconds

It's almost certainly the modem retraining. The reason for it negiating
a different speed is the real mystery.

Perhaps it thought it could connect faster than it really can, and needs
to fall back.

Maybe there's some crosstalk or other interference on the line that's
lowering signal quality enough for the modem to notice, but not enough
to terminate the connection.
