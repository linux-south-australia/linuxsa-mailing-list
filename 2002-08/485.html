I would tend to agree with you.  I've tried most of the obvious GUI apps
out there, xcdroast, eroaster, cdbakeoven, k3b, arson, and theyre just the
ones I can name off the top of my head that I remember trying.

So far each of them is lacking at least one or more things to make it the
single app I can use for all my cd burning, so I end up using several to 
cover all bases.  I'd say cdbakeoven and k3b have come closest so far, k3b
being a bonus in that it builds in dvd ripping functionality as well.

Unfortunately nothing is quite as good as Easy Cd under Windoze from the 
point of view of having everything working well under one umbrella.
