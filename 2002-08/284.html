> There are more plug ins for xmms than you can poke a stick at, but I
> prefer the mp3c frontend

> 
> With lame as the encoder. It supports CDDB lookups and adds an id3tag as
> well. There are links to the other bits you from the above site.
> 
> I use:
> 
> lame -h -V 3 -k - "%1"
> 
> as the string for encode on the fly in the config, which gives high
> quality, variable bit rate mp3s with no audio filtering.
