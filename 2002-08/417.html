DVD's going

Watched two DVD's over the weekend. The Planets and Dune.
I ended up using 
Ogle -u cli /dev/cdrom (as my system does not have a /dev/dvd)
Ogle looked at the dvd, found that the region did not match the cdrom, and 
proceeded to decrypt the data. This took 30 seconds or so. The ogle log is 
very detailed and tells all. The dvd's played ok from here. I could not get 
the gui to work. Not sure why. 
The other problems  - ogle does not seem to have a good configuration file 
that is easy to edit.
I could not adjust brightness yet. I think they are working on this.
