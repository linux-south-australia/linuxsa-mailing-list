Here is the gist of it....

Regionalising of DVS and other software, is a way to control the supply
and demand in an area. But more importantly controlling prices..
Australia is in Region 4 which means that you can't play a US (Region 1)
regioned DVD. This is a simplification in order to keep it short..

Mod Chips for the playstaion are now *legal*... the court case means
that Sony must honour the warranty if the mod chip was installed by an
authorised service centre. The ACCC will be interested in hearing from
anyone where Sony is saying that "the warranty is void because you ahve
installed chip-X"...etc

Brian, given it is of a technical nature we probrably won't see anything
in the paper untill Tueday's Australian.... It doen't have much
mainstream interest, especially for the Advertiser which uses two pages
for a headline.. :P
