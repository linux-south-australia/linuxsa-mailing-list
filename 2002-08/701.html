Re: Learning Perl

Hi There,
    I would highly recommend "Learning Perl" by Randal L.
Schwartz, which is an O'Reilly Book, and the follow on book
(which name escapes me at the moment), and finally
"Programming Perl" also an O'Reilly book. These seem to be
the best around. I think (but am not sure) that Larry Wall
actually wrote one of these other books so this is another
good sign. O'Reilly have a reputation of getting the best
people to write their books. Some of them are not cheap, but
the books are usually well worth the money you will pay.
    Cheers!
        Andrew.
