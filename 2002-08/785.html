On Wed, Aug 28, 2002 at 03:00:57PM +0918, michaeld@senet.com.au wrote:
> Quoting Alex Wallis <awol@prepaidonline.com.au>:
> > P.S. Why do people insist on sending duplicate emails via CC: when it's
> > obvious we are all subscribed to this mailing list anyway? As if there
> > isn't enough spam already?
> 
> Some people hate being CC'd on a mailing list because of the extra email.  
> Personally, my LinuxSA mail goes into a folder and (mostly) remains unread 
> (since I normally read mail via a webmail interface), whereas "personal" mail 
> sits in my Inbox until I deal with it.  I like it that way :)

simply put this:

# Discard duplicate messages
:0 Wh: msgid.lock
| formail -D 8192 msgid.cache

in your .procmailrc.  I have and it means I only ever get one
copy of any email regardless of how times I'm featured on the 
To or Cc etc headers.
