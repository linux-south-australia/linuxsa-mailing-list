[ANNOUNCEMENT] New FreeBSD CVSup Mirror now available in .au

I'm pleased to announce the immediate availability of a new
fully-fledged CVSup Mirror right here in Adelaide, South Australia --
cvsup5.au.freebsd.org.

The mirror carries a complete copy of the FreeBSD CVS repository and
includes mail and gnats along with it. The mirror is updated once per
hour from cvsup-master.freebsd.org.

A mirror of the FreeBSD WWW site is also available from
http://freebsd.alfred.cx/. An limited set of ISO images and the like
will be mirrored via FTP at a later date[1]. Sadly, a larger
AARNet-size mirror is a little beyond our reach, due to the fact that
we still have to pay for bandwidth and server hardware :-)

Connected to the Internet via 10Mbps microwave (through Internode),
bandwidth-related bottlenecks should not be a problem.

I hope this addresses the concerns of those of you who thought that
FreeBSD needed an Adelaide CVSup server satisfactorily. Please note
that if the machine that the mirror is currently sitting on becomes
overwhelmed by the load, I will move it to a more powerful server. 
Time will tell, though!

If there are any problems with it, please contact the maintainer (me!)
either via e-mail, cellphone or paper-plane.

Cheers,

   - andrew
