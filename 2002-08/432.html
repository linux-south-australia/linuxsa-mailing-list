re: firewire ide drives

Hi all,

I gave the IDE->FireWire/usb2 caddy a shot over Firewire under Linux over the
weekend.
It worked fine, and the firewire SBP-2 driver worked just like the usb-storage
driver in providing a psuedo-scsi interface for the external drive.

The only gotcha I hit was that you need to rescan the scsi bus after loading
the module in order to recognise that a drive has been added or removed.
The usb-storage driver didn't require this, so I presume that this isn't truly
required and will be fixed for firewire sbp-2 sometime in the future.
