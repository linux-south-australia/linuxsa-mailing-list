RE: The Death of MP3 & Jpegs ?

Aren't the Jpeg "developers" trying to extract a royalty on all Jpeg images
now also. I'm sure I read something about this recently.

Whatever the case it really is not going to stop anyone who wants to
get/create MP3s as there is always an underground market. It's just the
developers of MP3 players that will suffer big time. Given that most are
currently free anyway it's a bit sad but I guess they will possibly have to
take on more Advertising or such to cover the licensing expenses.

Can't really see how they will be able to police the number of decoders and
encoders.
