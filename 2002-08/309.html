M$ and the Linux Magazine.

Hi All,
    I thought I had seen a few strange things in my time,
but I am sure this one beats alot. I subscribe to Linux
Journal, but occasionally I buy some of the other linux Mags
at the newsagent to see if I am missing out on anything
important. Well today I bought a copy of "Linux Magazine"
June 2002 edition (www.linuxmagazine.com), and as I flicked
through it I came across (on page 26) a double page spread
from the evil empire trying to bribe linux users and isps
with free copies of Windoze 2000 server if they hosted M$
info for them. The url for what they are bribing with is
www.microsoft.com/isp. It was a good thing I was in the
"reading room" when I saw this as I almost had a nasty
accident while laughing. I have seen linux adds appear in
many popular PC mags, but I never thought I would see direct
targeting by M$ in Linux mags for a few years to come.
    Cheers!
        Andrew.
