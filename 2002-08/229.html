Re: POP/SMTP question

On Sat, 10 Aug 2002, Shane Warner wrote:

> I am not seeing an entry for lo in the route table.

I have had problems with not being able to talk to the machine I am on 
when my firewall rules do not allow connections from 127.0.0.1 or from lo.

Check your firewall rules. If there is a default deny and no rule allowing 
LO, that is likely your problem.
