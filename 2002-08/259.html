Re: Slow boot up with Debian Linux

On Mon, Aug 12, 2002 at 01:49:50AM +0100, Pigeon wrote:
> May I take a few minutes of someone's time to ask for a couple of hints?
> I have installed the Debian distro (a couple of years old) and it boots
> horrendously slowly.

everytime I've seen slow boots it's something to do with
hostnames and/or name resolution, eg. sendmail (that stupid
"can't find my hostname" type error) or dns (/etc/resolv.conf) etc.
Also check the machine isn't trying to mount something off 
another machine.
