Re: floppy drive

Robyn et al:

Try (in an xterm):

***
[root@localhost /root]# mount /mnt/floppy
[root@localhost /root]# ls /mnt/floppy
***

Or...
***
[root@localhost /root]# mount -t vfat /dev/fd0 /mnt/floppy
***

Or...

***
[root@localhost /root]# mount -t ext2 /dev/fd0 /mnt/floppy
***

Or if you're like my TAFE students...

***
[root@localhost /root]# mount -t auto /dev/fd0 /mnt/floppy
***

Generally if you mount something manually, then KDE and GNOME normally
notice what you've done. Naturally you don't want to use the command
line so only try the above (i.e. logging in as root and mounting the
floppy drive manually) if what Robyn says doesn't work.
