Re: Flashing TST and Connection Throttling

Ian Loxton wrote:

> CS is the Clear Send light, telling your modem when the line is free
> for it to send data.

Actually, it tells your computer when the modem is free to send data,
and if you use hardware flow control AND have your computer's data
rate set substantially higher than your modem's (e.g. 115K2 vs whatever
the speed your modem is connected to the remote end at) then you may
see it flashing quite often when sending a lot of traffic (or modem
renegotiating, etc.).
