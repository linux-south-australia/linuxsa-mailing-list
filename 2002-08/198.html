It sounds like you might be getting some intermittent noise on your phone
line that is causing you modem to lose carrier signal. The modem will be
unresponsive while it tries to regain carrier signal. Usually a modem will
give up before 30 seconds and hang up, but you can alter that by modifying
the value in the S10 register of the modem.

Lower connect speeds are less sensitive to line noise, so you may want to
try forcing the modem to connect at a lower speed if you value a more
consistent performance from you modem over speed.

Or you can try to find the source of the intermittent noise. Some models
of phones are notorious for making intermittent noise on a phone line when
on hook, so you may want to try unplugging any other phone devices you
have on the same line as the modem.
