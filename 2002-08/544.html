The tenforty project is just starting up, but this is what my hopes are 
for it, despite the fact that I am not doing the programming:

1. The core of the program will be like a kernel, and will include the 
more universal types of calculations. For example, both the U.S. and 
Australia want to know what total wages are.


 2. A "conf" type of file will configure the "kernel" into almost any 
country's tax preraration program that will generate text output for 
automated insertion into the "fill in forms."

What are the rules...

The guy that works on the kernel works for everybody. However, each 
country at a bare bone minimum will have to have its own CPA to 
interpret its country's tax rules to help the programmer to work the 
conf file and tax forms. At full strength, I hope that this project will 
have individual cpa firms maintaining individual forms or parts of them, 
or being responsible for parts of the conf file in each country.  That 
way the project will fall under the "savings is greater than the cost" 
category.

So, if anybody wants to work on this thing, we would be happy to have 
help. :-)
