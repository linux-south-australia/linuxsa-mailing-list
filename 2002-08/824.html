RE: apt-get

We are on an Internode Business ADSL link, on which they provide unmetered
access to some of their internal servers, including
redhat.internode.on.net. We use fmirror once a week (on weekends) to pull
down any changes to it and then build our own apt-rpm server out of it,
including any extra rpms we drop into the RPMS.extras folder.

Sorry, this is behind our firewall and isn't publically accessable.

Might not be an option for you, but it works for us.

For more information about Internode unmetered access, have a look at
http://adsl.internode.on.net/adsl_unmetered.html. Perhaps we could talk
Internode into making their ftp server apt-rpm friendly?
