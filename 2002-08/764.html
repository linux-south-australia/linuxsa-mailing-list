Re: floppy drive

Hi

If you're using KDE you need to right click on your desktop and choose
Create New -> Floppy device. Then with that open go to the "Device" tab
and from the drop down box choose "/dev/fd0 (/mnt/floppy)" and click on
OK and you're done. A floppy disk icon should appear on your desktop.

Robyn
