Screensavers

On the recent topic of screensavers, I used to love the xmatrix option
of xscreensaver but now that I have two monitor xinerama setup it looks
ugly when the messages "wake up neo", "follow the white rabbit", etc.
pop up spread between the two monitors... liveable but just annoying and
so I removed xscreensaver as I don't like any of the other modes.

I also recently thought I'd give ksetisaver a go, which looked
promising, although again isn't so great with xinerama... and using
enlightenment rather than kde I couldn't get it to run fullscreen
anyway...

Anyway, basically what I was wondering is are there any decent
alternatives to xscreensaver? A simple matrix-style screensaver would be
very nice... I miss it already :-(
