Michael,

Under Ghostscript's page on "Printer Compatibility"[1], which is a list of 
Ghostscript printer devices, there is a listing for a Tandy LP 800 (with 
LaserJet III emulation).

As a suggestion, check out the Tandy/Radio-Shack site on their Printers 
page[2] and see whether the features on their LP 800 matches the features 
of your machine.

If you've got a match, you should be very close to a solution.

Good Luck!

Cheers,

Peter
