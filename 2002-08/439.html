Re: traffic calculator?

On Fri, Aug 16, 2002 at 03:38:29PM +0930, Ben Roberts wrote:

> thanks for this link... this may be what im after. although does anyone 
> know of any package that not only does apache traffic, but maybe smtp, 
> dns....etc..????

It may be a little hard to do with the one package. Depending on your
setup, you may be able to use simple firewall rules to watch the
traffic flow.

Your mail server's log files could be processed and spat into a
database, using the domain name as the key. I'm not sure how you'd go
about measuring DNS traffic, as BIND doesn't usually spit out anything
useful about, well, anything.

Perhaps the other DNS servers out their may be able to help you in
that respect. That said, DNS isn't exactly a huge bandwidth pig. Try
measuring your total, monthly DNS traffic to see just how much it's
costing you. It may not be worth worrying about.
