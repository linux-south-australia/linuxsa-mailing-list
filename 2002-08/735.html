The Death of MP3

Got this in this mornings post....   not good news I am afraid.   I would
stock up on your MP3 goodies before it all disappears >:|

Matthew

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Götz <mailto:waschk@gmx.FREEBSDnet minus bsd>  writes "The licensing terms
of Thomson and the Fraunhofer Gesellschaft, who are the owners of the mp3
patents, have changed. Now not only mp3 encoders but also mp3 decoders
require a license. This page lists the
<http://www.mp3licensing.com/royalty/software.html>; fees -- it's $0.75 per
decoder. As a consequence, Red Hat has already removed all mp3 players from
 the Rawhide development version."
