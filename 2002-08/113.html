Re: Samba slow

steve donaldson said:
> I still have a similar problem from win2k to a large 300mb zipped
> file.  Takes forever to select, in fact everything else just grinds to
> a  halt when I have selected it.  Must admit I did not look into why...


I had the same problem and I tracked it down to PC-cillin. PC-cillin was
scanning zip files for viruses whenever I selected one. Do you have any
real time virus scanners running on your win2k box like PC-cillin?


By disabling realtime scanning on zip files it fixed the problem for me.
That is, if you don't mind not having your zip files scanned
automatically. :)
