Re: eth driver for Dell OptiPlex GX260 Small Minitower - solved

For anyone who is interested - the driver is the e1000 driver from Intel:
http://downloadfinder.intel.com/scripts-df/proc/T8Clearance.asp?url=/2897/eng/e1000-4.3.2.tar.gz

version 3.1.23 doesn't support the adaptor in the GX260, but the most
recent version (at the Intel site) does.

Inspite of the sugestion at the Installation and Use page:

This file describes the Linux* Base Driver for the Intel PRO/1000 Family
of Adapters, version 4.2.x. This driver is intended for 2.2.x and 2.4.x
kernels; it is known to build properly on 2.2.x kernels through 2.2.20 and
on 2.4.x kernels through 2.4.18. Intel focused testing on Intel
architectures running kernels 2.4.7. This driver includes support for
Itanium -based systems.

http://support.intel.com/support/network/adapter/1000/linux/e1000.htm

The (4.3.x - note that the page discusses 4.2.x) driver _will_not_ compile
under 2.2.20 (and so presumably under any 2.2 kernel) but builds fine
under 2.4.18.
