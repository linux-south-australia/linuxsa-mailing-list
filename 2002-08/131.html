Hi James,

One group that would be particularly interested in your efforts would 
be the Linux Forum on the CompuServe network.

Access them through http://go.compuserve.com/LinuxForum

Why would they be interested?
1) CompuServe is owned by AOL and they have a good membership base that 
comes across from AOL for the Forums and the knowhow, something that is 
not common on the AOL network unless chat rooms are your thing <sigh>

2) The current batch of CompuServe access software is CompuServe 7, 
same - or nearly so - to AOL 7 with CompuServe badging. From AOL 5 the 
CompuServe version has been a clone of the AOL version.

Public Access to almost all of the CompuServe network is free and the 
Linux Forum in particular are a very friendly bunch of knowledgable 
folks, very similar to the great folks that hang out on this list.

Yell out if you have any problems getting into their Forum.
