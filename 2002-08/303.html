Re: MYOB under Wine (or something else?)

I _really_ would not do this.

The manager of a business in Adelaide purchased a new laptop (running
Win2K). MYOB Premier V6 was installed and after 4 hours the data file
(300Mb) was corrupt. I restored the datafile from backup and again in 4
hours it was corrupt. The machine was rebuilt, and again the data file
was corrupt.

I contacted MYOB support and they advised me that they see this once
every month. MYOB is not a stable application on Windows and running it
under WINE will probably cause you grief.

I hate M$, however until application developers start
developing/supporting real operating systems then small business will be
stuck with M$.

The worst thing we (Linux advocates) can do is provide people with a
solution that is not technically sound.

BTW The laptop now runs Premier 6 on Win98.
