Re: Web Based E-Mail Agent

On Mon, Aug 26, 2002 at 01:29:02PM +0930, Brett Hales wrote:
> Does anybody know of a good web based e-mail agent.
> 
> The requirement is to only send mail, they need to be able to modify
> their reply_to address.
> 
> Preferably written in mod perl.

If you only need to send mail, but users need to modify the from address, you probably don't need to look at a full-blown mail client (like IMP, JawMail and squirrelmail). You could:

  * Write your own in mod_perl in less than 50 lines of code (including comments and excessive amounts of error checking) or
  * Do an internet search for something like: "form mail mod_perl download" and find one that suits your licensing environment.

Or you could offer a prize (probably beer. everyone likes beer) to the LinuxSA subscriber that writes the mail client that resembles your specs the most in return for letting you / your company use it or whatever. :-)

Hope it helps,
