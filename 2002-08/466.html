Re: Restricting FTP Access

Its not as simple as you might think, especially if you are using wu-ftp. A lot of 
reading and if I was you a change to proFTP would be in order.  wu-ftp is not 
known as a secure ftp server.  proFTP is easier to configure with an apache 
style .conf file. Either way reading is your friend.
