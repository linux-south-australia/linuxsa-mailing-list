Re: Auto Logon Scripts

Hi, this is totally off the top of my head, and will require some
tweaking and fixing (others may be able to add more info), but you could
probably add something to the end of /etc/rc.d/rc.local along the lines
of:

su - user1 -c /bin/bash < /dev/tty0 > /dev/tty0 &
su - user2 -c /bin/bash < /dev/tty1 > /dev/tty1 &
su - user3 -c /bin/bash < /dev/tty2 > /dev/tty2 &

Ant
