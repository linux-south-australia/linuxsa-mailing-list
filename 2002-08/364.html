I assume that the 2 machines are configured identically as far as where they 
dial, account used etc?  Are they actually using the SAME jack on the wall 
to connect to the SAME landline?

> (1) Same modem/cables in different machines.

Definately try this.  Also, how are you measuring packet loss?  ping?  if 
so, try upping the wait time to 5 sec to see whether it's just a delay.  

Delay could be caused by modems retraining.

> (2) Different OSs on same machine with/out different modems/cables.

Shouldn't make a difference, unless something's really odd.

Have you checked for wierd route tables with extra (bogus) default routes?
