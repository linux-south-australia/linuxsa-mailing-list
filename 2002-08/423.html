xbox linux iso available

If anyone has a chipped xbox sitting around they might want to take a
trip to <http://xbox-linux.sourceforge.net/download.php>; and get the
first version of the bootable linux iso. Its pretty basic, and has no
video, but the ethernet works, and you can.. err.. telnet in or watch it
serve web pages. You dont need development hardware to have a play with
it any more. Oh - you can play mp3s with it so it is good for something!
I suspect a full release of a distro wouldnt be far away now.
