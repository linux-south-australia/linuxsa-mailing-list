Hi Martin..

The two ways I know are:

A) If you just want to increase the time between checks, edit
"/etc/sysconfig/rhn/rhnsd" and modify the "INTERVAL=120" option.

or

B) To totally disable it, do an:
# /etc/init.d/rhnsd stop

and then use the chkconfig command to stop it from starting on reboot:
# chkconfig rhnsd off

Hope that helps.
