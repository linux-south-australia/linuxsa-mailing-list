Why settle for 'mock when you can do:

/usr/X11R6/bin/xscreensaver-hacks/phosphor -root -program 'watch ttyp2'

where 'ttyp2' is the tty of a user remotely logged into that box.

Works too :-)

Unfortunately, the Linux world thinks 'watch' is a program to repeat a
command over and over and display the results. The rest of us know it's
a TTY snooping program.
