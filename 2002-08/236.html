Re: Flashing TST and Connection Throttling

Hi David,

one way is to book a fault call with Telstra and get them to do a MOLDS 
test on your line.
Modem On-Line Diagnostic Service

you dial a 1800 number they give you, it talks back and forth with your 
modem and tests out the line along the way.

No charge and believe me, the techs were very nice guys to deal with 
and very helpful.

Our problem is that we are on a pair-gain line so the best we can ever 
hope for is 31.2, the average for the line is a consistent 28.8 both 
ways so we cant grumble about that.

Try them out. Oh, ask them for a 4 or 5 minute test in case it shows 
some pattern. Normally they give it a 1 minute test.
