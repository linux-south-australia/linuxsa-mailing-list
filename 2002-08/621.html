Re: serial laser printer

Hi.

Have you got a serial break-out box? What may be happening is that your
cable is set up as DTE and the printer is actually DCE. I've forgotten
what the standard cable is, but if the interface is ok, and you cannot
talk with it then this is probably the problem. 

With a break out box, you can tinker with the pinouts until the lights
come on - the main culprits are usually pins 2 and 3 - these are the
transmit and receive pins, and they get switched between DTE and DCE.

Regards,

Michael
