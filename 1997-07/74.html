Hi Helen,

What about just deleting the Linux partitions, create an extra dos
partition of whatever size you want, then put back Linux?  You will
end up with a C: drive and a D: drive, but that's the easiest
solution.  There are two other alternatives if you insist on having
your one dos drive expanded.
1: Major shuffling around of partitions by creating a DOS partition at
   the end of the disk, copying everything there, and optionally
   recreating the DOS partition at the beginning of the disk and
   copying everything back.
2: Buy, beg, borrow, or steal a copy of Partition Magic.

BTW, 100MB of swap for Linux is probably overkill.
