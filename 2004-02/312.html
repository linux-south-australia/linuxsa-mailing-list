Re: OT SMS to Optus mobile from email?

Re: OT SMS to Optus mobile from email?Another option would be to get a Nokia
handset and data cable, attach it to your Linux box and run gnokii.  Gnokii
can send and receive SMS messages using an attached mobile phone, and it
wouldn't be too hard to write a little bit of Perl or shell script to grab
the email message and pipe it through gnokii and have it sent as SMS.
Advantage with this approach are that it doesn't require an active internet
connection, and you're not dependent on third party gateways (which can be
important for things like network monitoring).  Disadvantage is obviousl the
cost of running a second mobile phone just for SMS'ing.

Also the Internode Email to SMS gateway might be worth a look if you don't
want to roll your own, or be bothered with ICQ stuff...
http://www.internode.on.net/products/sms.htm ... I've used the internode
service in the past and found it pretty good.

Jim.
