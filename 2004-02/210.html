IIRR, most usbkeys/memory/disk/storage/pendrives are accessed as scsi
devices, such as /dev/sda1 and are often formated as type vfat.

you will have to have a kernel with atapi scsi emulation, usb and
usbstorage compiled as modules ... well thats how mine now works =)

i broke my knoppix CD so i'll have to make another and give it a go =)

its possible you could use a standard knoppix disk and have your
network monitoring code and data on the usbkey...

that would be easier than modifying a knoppix install, burning, testing,
repeat...
