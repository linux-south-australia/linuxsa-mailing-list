KDE has an autologin function, and also some kiosk capabilities as
well.  Currently it's configured using various parameter settings in
config files, but it works. http://www.kde.org/areas/sysadmin/

I had an older version of KDE setup at one of my sites for public 
internet access, with ppp setup to autodial, Mozilla for browsing (v0.99,
should give you an idea of the age!).  It worked really well, at least 
that's what all the survey forms indicated at the time...

Given the fact that it was Joe Average from the public who were using
the PCs it wasn't bad, seeing as all the other PCs in that project were 
NT Workstations :P  Mine had a very mysterious hard drive failure overnight 
and was reinstalled ;)
