OT: choosing ADSL...

Quick question.
I've looked through the archives and looked at whirlpool and have 
finally decided to get Netspace ADSL.

Before i take the plunge: any good reasons not to?

Other contenders:
iinet - read they were bad on linuxsa
big pond + optus - everyone says they are hopeless
internode - i use them for my business DSL, but have experienced poor 
service with them (they don't answer support requests)
tgp - lot of people say they are bad on whirlpool

I couldn't find too much bad stuff written about Netspace and their data 
caps seemed competetive/generous.

I also like the fact that if all your friends are on Netspace too, you 
can transfer data between them for free (i want to rsync stuff!). I 
couldn't tell if this was the case on other ISP's. Also they have 
mirrors and free gaming networks. (The one thing they don't have is free 
Internet radio).
