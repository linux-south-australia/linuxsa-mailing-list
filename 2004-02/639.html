Internode Mirror

Internode are changing mirror from FreeBSD to Linux. I wonder whether 
this was a technical decision driven by issues with BSD, or what.

I love my Internode mirror. But it has had some problems recently.

I don't want to start a flame war with our many BSD friends on list. And 
BSD clearly is not dead. But I am curious as to why Internode who I 
always viewed as BSD orientated would change.

I guess I am also a bit curious about BSD. They seem a loyal bunch. What 
has BSD got going for it these days that might draw a Linux user? Not 
unhappy with Linux. Just curious.
