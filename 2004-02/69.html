US father names son 'Version 2.0'


A proud US father and self-confessed engineering "geek" has named his son 
after a computer software term.

Jon Blake Cusack, from Holland, Michigan, told local newspapers the US 
practice of adding "Junior" or "II" after a boy's name was too common.

So, when his son was born last week, he decided on the name Jon Blake 
Cusack 2.0, as if he were a software upgrade.

Mr Cusack admitted that it took months to persuade his wife, Jamie, to 
accept the idea.
