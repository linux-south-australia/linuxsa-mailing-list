Re: LCA 2004 - secure computer kiosks

I have done autologin with gdm before. I used it for a demo machine 
which auto logged in to demo mode after a few seconds if you didn't login.

Have a look in your /etc/X11/gdm/gdm.conf (under debian).

I can't remember if I used AutomaticLogin or TimedLogin. I think it must 
have been the later, because I had a few seconds to log in as another 
user before it went kiosk on me.
