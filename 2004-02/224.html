Re: OT: choosing ADSL...

> Anyone know what % of Adelaide Metro area houses can get ADSL (or
> cable)? And what that number will be once all exchanges are ADSL
> enabled? (It's not enough to have an ADSL enabled exchange; you've
> also got to be within 3.5km of it).

The distance isn't a "fixed" figure as it depends on the type of copper used 
to connect you to the exchange (larger diameter cu gives less loss and 
greater distance).

Also, Telstra are (as at March) extending the allowable loss which will 
extend the range to 4km or a bit more (again depending on the type of 
copper).

Telstra are ALSO rolling out some minimuxes in RIMs which means people who 
couldn't get ADSL now can (or will be able to).

So, in March, maybe try again.

If none of these help, then add yourself to the Agile demand register 
(http://cgi.agile.com.au/demand/) and see if Agile (Internode) can help.
