[ANNOUNCE]: SA-AUUG Monthly meeting: Linux Gaming ][: 11/2/2003,7pm @ Marcellina

The South Australian chapter of the Australian Unix User Group (AUUG)
formally invites you to attend their monthly meeting. Details are as
follows:
+----------------------------------------
| SA-AUUG Chapter Meeting
+--------+-------------------------------
| DATE   | 11th February 2004
| TIME   | 7pm Onwards
| VENUE  | Marcellina Pizza Bar
|        | 273 Hindley Street
|        | Adelaide
+--------+------------------------------
| TALK   | Linux Gaming ][
+--------+------------------------------

1. Linux Gaming ][, the Epic Continues...

    Following the success of the last installment, Brett Lymn is back for
    round two. Having just got a new ATI Radeon he's been hard at work
    getting those games running with awesome frame rates and groovy 
graphics.
    All this under Linux!

    Brett is an infamous NetBSD hacker so this shift to Linux for gaming
    brings to it knowledge of how he tamed the savage penguin to make
    gaming enjoyable for all.

2. Chat Back!

    A great time to just generally chat to your fellow Unix 
Admin/Hacker/Freak
    and munch on some pizza and drink some beer. All are welcome.

Hope to see you there.
