> Our Sydney branch is on the 4th floor of a building and require some

> that it is too far by the time it goes across the building/ down the

>
> 1) just asking him to cable it anyway and see if it works.
> 2) Putting some sort of repeater or Hub/switch at level 2 or

You could get away with a hub or switch somewhere in the path to reduce 
the actual cable length, but just running the cable on it's own is 
asking for trouble. At a college I once worked at there was a 114m run; 
it caused no end of intermittent problems for the people at the end of 
that run, and we were only running a 10mbit network.

Hubs/switchs are fairly cheap (you don't need a high end Cisco switch 
for such a temporary run).
