Re: Open Source Is Fertile Ground for Consiracy Theorists

Yeah. Welcome to real life Russell. Its not like everyone isn't aware of 
this.

Open source is not a synonym for naive. There are places thoughout the 
process where code could be inserted. Projects tend to lock up the 
packaging and distribution side using webs of trust and keysigning.

My guess is that the upstream code repositories is where things are most 
vulnerable. But that is where the most expertise is and the eyes most 
likely to find problems.

You see the same sorts of arguments about outsourcing to India as well.

Governments are used to dealing with problems like this. They run police 
forces that need to be overseen. They hand out billions in social 
security and healthcare payments that have to be overseen. And they are 
in a unique position when it comes to enacting law and catching criminals.
