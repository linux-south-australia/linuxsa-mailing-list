Laredo Tirnanic <laredo@labven.co.za> wrote:
> Stuff it. Why should we pay money for someone else's abuse of the
> internet/ emails.

You shouldn't have to, but you do, anyway.  You pay to receive the email,
you pay to filter it, you pay to read the one's that skip through your
filter, and you pay through the loss of the false positives.  You might
even pay for the filtering software.  Paying for messages that you send
would replace all of that, and probably cost you less.
