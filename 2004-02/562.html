> don't remember a time without matric.

Matric was introduced (at my school anyway) in 1969, my last year. At 
that time country schools (maybe city schools too??) had two streams of 
study, Area or PEB. Area was the curriculum decided by the school 
according to local needs so as we were a country school (Yankalilla) it 
had a high Agriculture component. PEB (Public Education Board) being 
the forerunner of the SABSA whatever they call themselves and the 
curriculum was set by them and applied across the State Education Dept. 
So Matric was open only to PEB students not Area students. I got my 
Leaving Area Certificate and that was the end of my schooling at the 
time.
