You mentioned you were using RH9 - Fedora Core 1 is the next logical 
step up from this, and it's actually pretty great for what you 
describe.  Last weekend I replaced my brothers machines running Windows 
XP, with reasonably trashed installations, to those running a 
reasonably stock standard Fedora install with some extra games thrown 
on.  Things such as the GIMP (graphics), Openoffice (like 
word/excel/etc), Evolution (email), Totem (media player), are all there 
on the CD's, or easily installable via third party sources such as 
dag's apt repository.

Probably the easiest installations I've ever done.

They love it - Quake3, for example, runs about three times as fast :-)
