Re: Mandrake or Rehat?

On 11/02/2004, at 10:26 PM, David WOLVERTON wrote:
> Any thoughts on whether Mandrake or Redhat will be better (for my 
> needs)
> when it comes to ease of use, ease of installation of extra programs 
> as well
> as ease of maintanance?

I would suggest not setting yourself targets to achieve when you first 
start out with Linux. Build a box a few times in different ways. Screw 
things up, recover from mistakes, make backups and restore from backup. 
Have a play with Debian and Gentoo without any expectation that you'll 
be able to make it work, just so you can see the underlying system 
before you retreat back to GUI-land.

Linux is different, so looking at your Windows environment and saying 
"now I want the same thing in Linux" is going to lead to a loss of 
hair.
