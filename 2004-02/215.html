> Quick question.
> I've looked through the archives and looked at whirlpool and have 
> finally decided to get Netspace ADSL.

> Before i take the plunge: any good reasons not to?

Probably best asked on Whirlpool.  

I personally feel better about supporting local companies.  So, worth 
considering other Adelaide options such as Adam, if Internode isn't how you 
feel is best for you to go.  At least with an Adelaide company you can 
physically go and see them if something happens where as Netspace are far 
far away (well, Melbourne).

Internode do have a number of good extras in Adelaide such as Citilan 
(which given you're posting from an AU email address could be useful).

> internode - i use them for my business DSL, but have experienced poor 
> service with them (they don't answer support requests)

Internode have spent a lot of time and energy on improving their customer 
service.  I'd at least suggest reconsidering and reevaluating them.  If not, 
my suggestion is to evaluate other SA companies, with Adam being my 
suggestion.

A number of people on linuxsa are with Adam, as is the Adam network manager.
