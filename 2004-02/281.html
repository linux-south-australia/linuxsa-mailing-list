Re: OT: choosing ADSL...

I'll chuck in my 2cents worth.

Friends have used Adam and generally they've been happy. I'm using 
Internode and overall I am very happy with them. I've always had prompt and 
helpful tech support, whether by email or voice. They even helped me set 
the 'net connection for my LAN. My latest incident was me, in an effort to 
fix a problem with LAN/net connectivity dying, 'configuring' my dsl-300 
beyond apparent repair. Internode replaced this unit with a 2nd hand 
working one, although mine would have been outside the 18mth support 
period. Fixed the same day I dropped it in! I have no hesitation 
recommending Internode, based of course on my personal experience.

I concur with others that the sales responses need to improve. I tried to 
get info for one of my clients, to get them connected via adsl. The 
response was too long and my client is now with Adam.
