> They also made the following comment:  "The Perforce system is a

That is correct. Any commercial use requires a licence, and they are not 
cheap. Bitkeeper is another case where a licence is required for 
commercial use.

If I was involved in another start-up, I would suggest the use of an 
open-source alternative to Perforce if at all possible. Subversion perhaps 
or something else. However, I would not give up the ability to do cheap 
and easy branching. It is just so useful with a big project (80 
engineers).
