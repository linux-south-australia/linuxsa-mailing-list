Prabhakar Kalasani <kalasani@sena.com> wrote:
> when i'm trying to dial a modem pc card , I could able to send modem
> string through /dev/ttyC8 , but I'm not getting any response by saying
> "OK" from the target.

You didn't say what sort of modem it is.  There are two reasons that I
commonly see that explain your problem.  One reason is that the modem
is set to not echo or confirm AT commands.  This might be done for a
dial in+out modem.  The other reason is that the modem is a "winmodem",
and you need a special driver.

What sort of modem have you got?  If you're not sure, run lspci and see
if that helps.
