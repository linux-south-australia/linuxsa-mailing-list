I have to agree here, well to some degree ;-)
I use Internode ADSL now and am really happy with the quality and speed, 
and the ability to get someone on the phone is still good even though they 
are growing fast.

speaking to sales, they have failed to get back to me. I'm sure they are

I'm not having a go at Internode, just pointing out that they most likely 
have some growing pains.
