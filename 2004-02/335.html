Linux Australia and You

Hi all,

Just in case you weren't aware, I was elected to the Linux Australia
committee at their AGM at Linux.Conf.Au 2004.  Linux Australia has
elected a president (Pia Smith), vice president (Stewart Smith),
Secretary (Andrew Cowie), Treasurer (Mark Tearle) and 3 committee
members (Rusty Russell, Mikal Still and myself).  Except me, it's a good
team :)

I'm here to be a servant to the Open-Source community (at least until
the next AGM :) and I'm a local South Aussie, so if you think Linux
Australia should be doing something they're not, or shouldn't be doing
something they are, or you have a great idea (and the enthusiasm to help
make it happen), please feel free to contact me on mrd at linux dot org
dot au, and I'll do what I can to represent your opinions to the Linux
Australia executive!

If you want to keep up with what Linux Australia is doing :

o You can read our committee meeting minutes on-line at
http://linux.org.au/minutes

o join our mailing list to discuss everything open-source, linux and
Linux Australia related on-line via the linux-aus mailing list (see
http://lists.linux.org.au/listinfo for details)

o bother me at LinuxSA meetings for a run-down of what's happening

o come to our Linux.Conf.Au 2005 conference in Canberra (promises to be
better than LCA2004 was :-P

o Join Linux Australia (see
http://www.linux.org.au/membership/?page=signup)

It's an exciting time to be part of the OSS community, and Linux
Australia is bubbling over with that enthusiasm, so put them thinking
hats on, volunteer and get involved, and let's see if we can further
that goal of Linus - world domination! :-)
