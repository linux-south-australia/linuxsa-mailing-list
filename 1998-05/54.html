> THE USENIX ASSOCIATION UPCOMING EVENTS
> 
> June 15-19, 1998
> 1998 USENIX TECHNICAL CONFERENCE & EXHIBITION
> Marriott Hotel, New Orleans, Louisiana
> Co-sponsored by FreeBSD Project, Linux International, NetBSD Foundation,
> Inc., and OpenBSD Project
> WEB SITE:  http://www.usenix.org/events/no98/
> 
>       Linus Torvalds, developer of Linux, and Richard Stallman,
>       founder of the GNU project, will participate in this year's major
>       gathering of the UNIX community. Includes track of 28 talks plus
>       evening BoF sessions showcasing the latest developments and
>       interesting applications in FreeBSD, GNU, Linux, NetBSD, OpenBSD,
>       Samba, and more.  Share ideas and actual code with developers
>       and avid users of freely distributed software.
> 
>       Other highlights:
>       *Cutting-edge research puts you ahead of the technology curve
>       *Keynote by the "Amazing" Randi, professional skeptic
>       *22 in-depth tutorials from Eric Allman, Tom Christiansen, Evi
>          Nemeth, Marcus Ranum, and other expert instructors
>       *Dennis Ritchie with a perspective on the original UNIX paper
>       *Eric Raymond and Kirk McKusick on "The Cathedral and the
>          Bazaar" software development model
>       *"Beyond Wearable Computing" by Steve Mann
>       *The latest products to test drive in the Exhibition Hall
