Re: Sound problems with 2.0.33 Kernel

On Thu, 21 May 1998, Johnson, Brandon BR wrote:

> I have an AWE 64 Gold sound card and I went through the kernel
> configuration to enable sound for soundblasters and compatibles. ( at
> least what I though was enough to enable ) everthing compiles properly
> and after a reboot the sound just doesn't work. Is there anyone out
> there that has similar hardware that has got the kernel to recognise and
> use the sound card?
> Details:
> P233MMX 64MB SDRAM
> 3.2Gig HDD
> AWE 64 Gold 
> 	Int 7 
> 	I/O 220
> 	DMA 1 and 5
> 	MIDI 330
> O.S.  Redhat 5.0
> Kernel 2.0.33
> If there are any details that I have left out please ask.
> 

Is this a Plug and Play card? If it is, it needs to be configured using
isapnptools (included with RedHat 5.0) before it will be recognised by
linux. This means that the sound driver must be built as a module and
installed after the card has been configured. This works for me with my
Vibra 16.
