Re: Can't make rexec and rsh work on redhat 5

On Mon, 11 May 1998 plockley@slack.pcsa.net.au wrote:

> I've been trying to get rshd and rexecd on my linux box to accept an
> incoming rsh connection.  I've uncommented the lines for each in 
> inetd.conf and added the client into my .rhosts file in my home directory.
> But I keep getting permission denied.
> I've even added lines in my hosts.allow to open up the security, but still
> no luck.

Perhaps the permissions on the .rhosts file is incorrect ? (The rsh utilities
insist that the file isn't world readable, nor group writeable) Try:
	chmod 600 ~/.rhosts
