Re: red faced

here's my usual checklist for this problem:

step 1: (digital monitors only) check the colour temp/colour balance 
controls. (i have seen digital monitors 'forget' such settings at random)
if that fails
step 2: check the cable.  both ends, sometimes internal breaks. check for 
bent pins
 if that fails:
step 3: plug the monitor into a different computer 
if it's ok on the other computer, plug it back into yours, else get the 
monitor looked at, after double-checking the cable. 
if the monitor is OK elsewhere but no red on yours:
step 4: try a different video card, 16 bit is slow but RELIABLE. (and most 
people have one you can borrow)
by now you should know the problem :-)
