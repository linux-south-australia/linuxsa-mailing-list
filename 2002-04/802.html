Re: sound recording

Hi ya Bruce, I have been their. A signal from a music source like a tape or 
turntable, into the amp will help. Then manipulating a mixer should give you 
some sound.

My setup Turntable > Amp > RCA to line in > DAP (mixing/recording software) > 
create .aiff file (these are big !) > encode to ogg vorbis.

There is a learning curve - keep your best stuff for later.

Brian Marr

DAP - digital audio processor -takes an input signal and can capture it to 
file. Broadcast 2000 and other software will do this.
