Re: Compressed Filesystem

Charles,

> Anybody know of any good compressed filesystems? I found one called e2compr,
> but Its old, incomplete and only works for 2.2 kernels.

Depends on your application but this may help... Linux now supports a
transparent decompression extension to the ISO9660 filesystem (primarily used
for CDs).  It was only introduced late in the 2.4 series.

You need a special/new version on mkisofs and and some other tools which are
avalible at http://www.kernel.org/pub/linux/utils/fs/zisofs/
