Where I am, (not right now, but usually)
we are behind 4 proxies, 2 of these are behind separate firewalls,
and that bouncer program that I previously mentioned works a treat,
simply run ssh on port 443 on my server, download the windows bouncer client
run it, by specifying a local port, the proxy ip and port, and the ip that I
want to connect to,
then I open up putty and tell it to connect to localhost on the port I
specified, and off we go
a sshing, works beaut.
Bouncer runs in dos/windows/unix/linux, etc, etc, etc.
