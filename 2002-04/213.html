Red Hat 7.2 should run well on this machine, but what OS you choose
depends on what you want it for.

I'll also mention that the "megabytes" you refer to for RAM
are different from the "megabytes" (or "gigabytes") you refer
to for hard disk space.

For more information, and the IEEE standard way to write these
sizes, please see:
