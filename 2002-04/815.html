Fun

I received in the snail mail today this "OEM System Builders" promotion
where Microsoft send you some "stuff" if you email them and in "100
words or less tell us what you think of Windows XP and/or Office XP."

So I went in the competition.

Here's my entry:

"In 100 words or less, tell us what you think of Windows XP and/or
Office XP."

I personally believe that Windows XP is one of the poorest products on
the market, simply because of Microsoft's efforts to undermine the GPL
and Open Source systems.  Take the EULA stating that a GPL program such
as VNC can't be run on Windows because it allows "remote control" of the
desktop, violating the terms of use.  Imagine if I bought Windows, used
it, and then Microsoft come back at me and say "Did you know you can't
run the software you want, because we said so."  Next thing you know I
won't be able to use my bone china egg cup if I boiled my egg without a
Sunbeam toaster, because Sunbeam want to force me to use a Sunbeam egg
cup.  But I don't like Sunbeam egg cups.




I wonder if I'll win.
