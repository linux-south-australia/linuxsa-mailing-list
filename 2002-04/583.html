I always found with these bios's that if you change the system spec, it
advises you and gives you the option to continue with new settings or enter
into the bios..
maybe take out some memory (if possible) and it may give you that option.

another thing I noted, is that some bios's require you to press the correct
key(s) at a particular time (like older compaq systems - when the curser
moves over to the right-hand/top of the screen)
