Thanks for all the fish ... (was The internet in 2004)

Hi,

Thanks to everyone who sent me the reference to the Internet in 2004.

As an aside, I have discovered that it is possible to learn from anyone, 
and I am not too proud ...

At Connectathon recently, one of the exhibitors had only two people in the 
booth, and, despite the fact that they had based their product on Samba on 
Linux, these two people knew distressingly little about both.

However, a little gem that I came away with from them was:

  ls -ax | grep '[bd]d' 

Which is a compact way to find out if smbd, nmbd and winbindd is running.

While the camp that I was in arguably had more competent people, our file 
system was nothing much to write home about, as only root could create or 
write to files on it :-)
