> If you check up on mgetty, you'll notice that there's several packages,
> "mgetty", "mgetty-voice", "mgetty-sendfax", and possibly "mgetty-common"
>
> If you're running hylafax for the faxing, then you shouldn't need the
> -sendfax
> extras for mgetty.  All you'll be needing mgetty to do is look at an
> incoming
> call, decide whether it's voice, data, or fax then do something with it,
> ie: call hylafax if its a fax call, start a ppp session if its data and so
> on
>
> I'm not overly familiar with hylafax, but it may even do the call answering
> stuff itself without needing an external getty program.
>
> Just blow away mgetty-sendfax before you install hylafax........
