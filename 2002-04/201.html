Re: Redhat 7.2 display and mouse conflict

Mark:

Try disabling "gpm" (which is probably enabled).

Here's one way to do it:

* press ctrl+alt+F2
* login as root
* type "service gpm stop"
* type "chkconfig --level 35 gpm off
* press alt+F7

Man pages you might want to look at are:

man gpm
man chkconfig

Running service is just like saying /etc/rc.d/init.d/[service]
