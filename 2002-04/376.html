Hello,

As for the internal network com issue, why not try nmap (once installed) and
check to see if telnet/ssh ports are even open: -note, do this from an
internal machine.

nmap 192.168.1.1

This tests for open ports on the target machine. They will come up as
"filtered" if ipchains/iptables is filtering that port. ie, letting eth0 in
but not eth1 (assuming that eth0 is leading to the ADSL modem)

This may help.
