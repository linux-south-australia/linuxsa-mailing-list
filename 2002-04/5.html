RE: POP3 Delays on checking email (using Outlook etc)

Well there is no sign of a 30 second delay in the logs below.

Are you sure that your problem isn't as simple as a DNS configuration 
error on your windoze box, rather than on your linux one?

This sounds a bit like windoze trying to resolve a DNS name, and 
taking 30 seconds to fail to do so, before failing over to a local 
hostname to access your linux system (or some other similar botch on 
the windows size).

Try using tcpdump and watch what comes out of your windows box. I bet 
you see a query over and over, for -something-, for 30 seconds, 
before it tries to touch the linux box at all.

Cheers,
Simon

>hint. What is happening is the POP3 server is trying to do a reverse DNS

>10.0.0.3       matt.domain.com       matt
