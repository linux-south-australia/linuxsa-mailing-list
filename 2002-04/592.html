Re: Replacing a dying HDD

I think you needed an option to cp to tell it not to dereference symlinks
though?

I always used to use the tar method to move my filesystems around..
(rather than dump/restore or dd). I figured i got a free defrag with every
transfer! :)

(cd /mnt/src;tar -clpf - .)|(cd /mnt/new;tar -xf -)

Note that /mnt/src can just be / if you want -- the l option tells tar not
to change filesystems, so it won't go into /proc and start copying kmem, nor
will it move into the /mnt/new location as long as /mnt/new is a different
partition or disk!
