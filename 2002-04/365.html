Re: VNC

When I was struggling to get my firewall just to work, I had a similar
problem.

Here is what I did to solve it, but it may not work for you.
Also, I am not exactly a firewall guru, I just did it ugly but made it
work...

Anyway, here's what I did:

I went into Mandrake Control Center and selected services.

I remeber having a choice of 3 firewalls. I chose ipchains - (for a firewall
guru, this might be the wrong answer) and then I used emacs to edit
/etc/rc.d/rc.local.

I then configured the firewall to accept and transmit from port 5800 to
5803.

When you set up VNC, you might connect to 192.168.x.x:0 or :1 or :2...

When I set up VNC within X, it told me that my X server was at 0 or 1 or
2...

Likewise, I needed the corresponding port 5800 or 5801 or 5802... to accept
communications for it to work.

James "Firewall Newbie" Leone
