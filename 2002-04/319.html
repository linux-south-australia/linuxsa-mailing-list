Re: Mgetty Dialup and Hylafax together on one machine? Conflicts??

Hi.

rpm -qa|grep mgetty
mgetty-1.1.21-2
rpm -qa|grep hylafax
hylafax-4.1-1rh6

They work together. The mgetty-sendfax package conflicts with hylafax, but
you don't need it, just rpm -e mgetty-sendfax and you should be away.

If you check at hylafax.org, there is a way of configuring faxgetty (part
of hylafax) to answer all calls and hand off data calls to the system.

Michael
