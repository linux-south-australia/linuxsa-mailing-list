I am now running an input line from speaker outputs for speaker set B (hey, in 
the '70s when this amp was made, this was hi-fi at its best!) into line in on 
the soundcard. Command line now looks like this:
sox -c 2 -w -t ossdsp /dev/dsp -r 44100 track<nn>.wav

I am manually breaking the recording at each track, CTL-C , up arrow, change 
nn=>nn+1, RTN

It's coming out of the PC speakers at a reasonable quality level for what they 
are. Test burn #0001 later today. This, for me, is outstanding progress. I 
bought the CD burner for my wife for Xmas, so she could do this stuff, and 
today is the first day of actually attempting to cut tracks.
