RE: FreeBSD -- 2 identical drives, different geometries...

Hi.

I remember having this problem in a linux install I did some time back. In
the end I passed an option statement to lilo with the geometry of the
second drive in it. Something like "hdc=x,y,z" x,y,z being CHS. My
problems went away. Does Freebsd have a similar option?

I think the difference is caused by the different drive sensing, the first
drive geometry is passed from the bios, the second is sensed by the os, or
something like that.

Regards,

Michael
