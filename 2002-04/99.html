Re: RE: [OT] Notebook "riser wedge"

Agree Richard,

current thinking says that the keyboard (for all keyboards) should be 
at 0 deg to -5 degrees from the user so that the wrists are not bent in 
an inappropriate manner. Easy way is never to raise the keyboard up on 
the legs, just let it sit flat on the desktop and it should be close 
enough.

I have a slight positive slope on my desk but I have embedded my 
keyboard into the desk so very little is higher than the surrounds 
which have had EVO matting placed to support the wrists.

Cheers,  Ian
