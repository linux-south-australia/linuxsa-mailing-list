What *is* there in /var/run?  How old is this machine?

You can get the date of the last kernel compile from uname.  That
could be an original from CD-ROM, though.

The reason some of the files are older is because they hadn't been
modified since that date.  The creation timestamp (ls -cl) will give
you more useful information than the last modification timestamp.
