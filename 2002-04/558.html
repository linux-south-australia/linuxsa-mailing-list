I can't find what I specifically read, but the below suggests what I
swear I've seen; mkisofs should just be able to pipe normally to
cdrecord. When I read it, my cdburner at the time did not have any
buffer underrun technology so I didn't bother. I was too proud of my
linux box never burning a coaster. :)

4.27 What will cdrecord do when it stops getting input from the pipe?

It will finish writing. So you can just couple your favourite backup
tool with cdrecord using a pipe, like in "bru -size=640m -f - | cdrecord
dev=0,1,0 speed=2 -". You have to take special care within the backup
utility if the backup spans multiple CD-Rs.
