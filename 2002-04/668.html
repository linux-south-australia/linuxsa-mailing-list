Re: Title bar and Domain name

On Sun, 21 Apr 2002, steve@lansol.com.au wrote:
> Hi all,
> 
> Does anyone know how to make RH7.2 display the full domain name in the
> title bar when using a terminal window or Putty.

As root, put this in /etc/sysconfig/bash-prompt-xterm
--snip--
#! /bin/sh
echo -ne "\033]0;${USER}@${HOSTNAME}:${PWD/$HOME/~}\007"
--snip--
and chmod 755 /etc/sysconfig/bash-prompt-xterm

Any new xterm like terminals should now have the full hostname(1) in the
title bar.
