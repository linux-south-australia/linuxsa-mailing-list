You mean GPG, right? :-)

1024 bits RSA is still good enough for incidental, day-to-day stuff.  If you 
want something to stay secure "in storage" go 2048 or even bigger.  It all just 
depends how paranoid you are.

Having said that, factoring algorithms are always improving, so better yet, 
just don't generate anything needing encryption :-)
