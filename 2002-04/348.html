Re: Intel ether express pro

On Thu, 11 Apr 2002, TOM wrote:

> has any one put two Intel NIC's into one FreeBSD or Linux server to double
> the bandwidth?
> any idea's comments or "watch out for this" appreciated

Well, I have had two GigE adapters in one machine, and you can saturate 
the PCI bus bandwidth [1], if that is what you mean. 'course, interrupt 
processing can be a problem too, without interrupt mitigation and jumbo 
frames.

[1] If you use the new AMD 760MPX-based mobos, this will be less of a 
problem, but only if you can find a GigE card that is 64-bit and 66MHz.
