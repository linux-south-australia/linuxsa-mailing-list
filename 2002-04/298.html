I played around with it last night and following DSL's suggestion a few
beers and I managed to get it online and working with reboots etc.

Boot, drink a stubby,hit keyboard, swear, reboot, yell at kids, re-edit the
ppp/options file (not sure if required with RP's pppoe , removed
chap-secrets, pap-secrets, new pap-secrets, more beer,

redoing nics and pppoe options it straightened itself out, internode was
