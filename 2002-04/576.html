RE: IDE geometry detection & getting to BIOS

Regarding the BIOS issue, here's a list of things people suggested (just
in case anyone else ever needs a list of ideas to try to attempt to get
into the BIOS of an unknown PC). I haven't tried all of them yet, as I
haven't had opportunity to take the machine down again... I'll try that
on the weekend, I think.

Here's the complete list of BIOS key combinations that I'm aware of...
please contribute any extras that you've seen (preferably real ones
you've seen, not just a possible or probable combination) :).

DEL
ESC
CTRL-ESC
F1
F2
F4
F10
F12
S
D
CTRL-ALT-S
CTRL-ALT-D
CTRL-ALT-F1
CTRL-ALT-F2
CTRL-ALT-ESC

Of course, it would be ideal if someone knew the hashing algorithm that
manufacturers used to select which of these they use on which machines
or motherboards... :)
