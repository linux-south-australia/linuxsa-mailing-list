I'll also add as an object lesson in the effectiveness of the Apple 
software update mechanism... this morning on my auscert mailing list

So I ran my Apple software updater (two mouse clicks). Bingo, there 
it is, a few mb's only. One more mouse click to select it, another 
one to do a one button install.

3 minutes later my software is patched and up to date.

You just can't beat this for 'convenient security'. Sincerely, it rocks.
