> Mar 31 10:58:34 mattonline ipop3d[12308]: pop3 service init from 
> 10.0.0.3 Mar 31 10:58:34 mattonline ipop3d[12308]: Login user=matt
> host=[10.0.0.3] nmsgs=0/0
> Mar 31 10:58:34 mattonline ipop3d[12308]: Logout user=matt
> host=[10.0.0.3] nmsgs=0 ndele=0

The 'host=' entry on those lines should hopefully give you a bit of a
hint. What is happening is the POP3 server is trying to do a reverse DNS
lookup on your IP address.

Try putting into your /etc/hosts file:

10.0.0.3       matt.domain.com       matt

And then see if it makes any difference. If it does, you'll want to set
up a local DNS server for your network.
