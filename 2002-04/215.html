Re: Highlander Laptops?

The only advice I tend to give out regarding laptops is:

Make sure you can get them easily fixed in Adelaide and for a number of 
years after you purchased it.

Laptops tend to have lots of custom parts that can be hard and/or expensive 
to get or even require specialist tools.

If you buy a laptop it _IS_ worth getting a brand name (Compaq, Toshiba, HP, 
IBM etc) as these can on the whole be easily fixed.

I've had success with Compaq and appreciate that they have a repair store on 
Frome St in the City.  

The Highlander laptops may indeed have an easy way of getting them fixed (I 
have no experience with them) but it is something worth checking.
