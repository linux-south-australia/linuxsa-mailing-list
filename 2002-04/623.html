ip masquerading and possible MTU problems

I'm using a Linux red hat 7.2 box to do ip masquerading to share my ADSL
connection on my internal lan. It was working fine, but now I find some web
sites stall and fail to load, for example slashdot. This is apparently
classic symptoms of MTU problems. I've lowered the MTU on my internal hosts
to 1492 (the recommended fix) and even 1412. The linux box is using RP-PPPOE
and CLAMPMSS=1412. The MTU's on the linux box's internal lan ethernet card
is 1492 and the MTU for the ethernet card that goes to the adsl modem is
1500 (RP-PPPOE insists that it be this). PPP0 (the PPPOE adapator) has MTU
set to 1492. 
Has anyone got any suggestions on what I can try next? Could it be something
other than an MTU problem?
