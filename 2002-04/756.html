> How does the paste option differ from simply turning off

Vim has online help, which is why you won't find details in the manual page.

Try
1. :help
2. /paste
3. control-]
(and perhaps some other incidental commands)
to navigate to the appropriate help section.

In short, it changes the values of other related indentation and 
tabulation options, as well as a few such as "ruler", "textwidth", and 
"showmatch".
