Re: Intel ether express pro

On Wed, 10 Apr 2002, James Leone Home wrote:

Hmmm, James, why is someone on PacHell.net posting on LinuxSA?

> I have two Intel/Pro lan cards, but I am only using 1 for now, I don't know
> how to do that but would like to learn.

'tis easy, as I am sure Mark Newton would say. They should both be 
recognized since one is recognized. 'dmesg | grep fxp0' should find them.

Then edit /etc/rc.conf and add a line for:

ifconfig_fxp1="inet 10.1.1.241  netmask 255.255.255.0"

Change the addresses to suit your needs ... :-)
