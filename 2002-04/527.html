Most of these cards just work, but, it seems, when there are problems, 
they are un-solvable.

My problem was with the 735 chip-set in the el-cheapo mobo I was using. It 
did not provide ISA interrupt steering. As I was trying to use a PCMCIA 
card, not a cardbus card, I was SOL.

The other thing I have noticed is that these PCMCIA to PCI bridge cards 
are simply not easy to find. Frys, for example, does not carry them as a 
separate item. You have to buy them with an 802.11 card.
