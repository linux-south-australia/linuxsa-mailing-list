Re: hylafax: waiting for modem to become ready (fwd)

Hi.

HylaFax gives this message when it cannot communicate with the modem.

Are you running faxgetty? If so, there is a real problem, and you will be 
getting hundreds of 'modem wedged' messages as well in your faxmaster email 
inbox. If not, then I think this may not be as bad as it sounds. Try 
sending another fax. What happens?

If you are running a send-only fax server, you don't need to run faxgetty, 
but I would reccomend you do, because it seems to keep better track of 
where the modem is at.
