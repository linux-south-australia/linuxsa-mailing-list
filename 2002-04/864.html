If it's one of the NICs listed here:
<http://www.freebsd.org/releases/4.5R/hardware-i386.html>;
(perhaps the EN2212/EN2216), then it's on the installation disks in
the "ed" ethernet device driver.

As I said to you privately, you could try to get a CD-ROM drive or a
different network card: it might be easier than some of the
"alternative" installation methods!

You could also try installing over PLIP
<http://www.freebsd.org/doc/en_US.ISO8859-1/books/handbook/plip.html>;
or copying the files from an MS-DOS partition
<http://www.freebsd.org/doc/en_US.ISO8859-1/books/handbook/install-dif
f-media.html#INSTALL-MSDOS>.

It sounds like your network card is supported, however, so you
shouldn't need to worry about any of these ways. :-)
