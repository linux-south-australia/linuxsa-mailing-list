LinuxSA has a new home!

Well people.  

The problem with the home of LinuxSA has been solved.

Digital-SA has come to the rescue.  In the spirit of the Linux
International-Digital relationship DEC has offered us their
meeting room facilities for use.

We now have access (on a long term regular basis) to the following :
	o Seating for 60-100 people (depends how squishy we get :)
	o Tea/coffee facilities (we have to clean up).
	o PC LCD projector

Thanks go to Mike Valiseff (DEC SA sales manager) and the rest of the
DEC team.

Matthew
