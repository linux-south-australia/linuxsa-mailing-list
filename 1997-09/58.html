Here's the mail I wrote (plus correction thanks to Phil Nitschke):

> I seem to remember someone from LinuxSA wanting a typing tutor.  Maybe
> I'm just remembering wrong, but there's one available here in case
> anyone's interested:
> ftp://ftp.math.orst.edu/pub/typist.tar.Z
> 
> Instructions for those unfamiliar with building software under Unix:
> ncftp ftp://ftp.math.orst.edu/pub/typist.tar.Z
> mkdir typist
> cd typist
> tar xvzf ../typist.tar.Z
> Edit Makefile as follows:
>   change LESSONDIR to equal "/usr/local/lib/typist"
>   change LIBS to equal "-lncurses"
>   change TARGETS to equal "typist"
> make
> As root:
>   cp typist /usr/local/bin/typist
>   mkdir /usr/local/lib/typist
>   cp *.typ /usr/local/lib/typist
