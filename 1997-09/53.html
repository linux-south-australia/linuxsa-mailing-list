	I use the 3Com 3C905 10/100 TX card under RH4.1. Apart from some 
initial problems recognising the card (solved by a fellow LinuxSA 
member's suggestion to put the IRQ and I/O address in /etc/conf.modules), 
it now works like a gem. I'm getting ~1-1.5 Mb/s transfers over a busy 
university campus 10Mb/s network. Don't know bout the price 'down under' 
but it costs me ~RM200 (AUD95) over here. I also have a 3Com 3C509B which 
works straight off RH4.1.
