Re: another adsl

Hi Simon,
I'm currently using iPrimus with a flat-rate 256/64 ADSL link.

It uses PPPoE & all my intranet hosts are tuned for MTU 1454.
I spent a lot of hours debugging & getting upto speed with PPPoE.
Whilst its DHCP, my IP address has never changed, but the ACX gets reset
at 9:02 every morning which is annoying. 

So a *permanent* straight TCP/IP over ethernet, MTU=1500, fixed IP would
be fantastic.

1500MB data threshold would be ideal for a SOHO user with a small
intranet.

Having the flexibility to supply one's own equipment is good too.
I see a business opportunity for you to sell termination equipment as
well, the choice is upto the customer.
Once my 12 month contract expires, I get to keep my Alcatel Speed Touch
Home ADSL-bridge/router, so my next contract will be a service-only
contract.
