There's a minor difference between the Aviators and the Lucents.  The
former are 802.11 FHSS, maximum 2 Mb/s.  The latter are 802.11b DSSS,
11 Mb/s.  Because of the spectral qualities (FHSS vs. DSSS) they're
completely incompatible.  I have both in my house, but only one of the
latter, which is rather like the sound of one hand clapping.  I'd like
to find another.
