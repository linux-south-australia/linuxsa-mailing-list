Re: Why use DocBook versus Word

On Mon, 23 Apr 2001, Richard Sharpe wrote:

> OK, I have heard of those sort of things with earlier versions of Word, and
> can believe that they would get that big if people insist in inserting
> graphics rather than SVGs (like EPS).
> 
> However, what of the recent versions of Word? Word 2000?

I think, perhaps incorrectly, that the question you are asking is: "in a
closed environment requiring no editable document exchange with people or
computers external to the project, is Word a reliable development
platform?"

I think the answer is "yes". I also think that such environments are
relatively rare, and I doubt that you have one :-) 

My personal interest is in general purpose document solutions involving
common business interactions locally, globally and over the Internet,
where I believe the answer is "Word? You're mad!".
