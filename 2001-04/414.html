Planning to - to charge an additional fee per month for >1 fixed IP's

Those who are familiar with how to construct an IP subnet will 
appreciate why the next multiple after 1 customer IP is 5 customer 
IP's. Hint: reserved, broadcast, default router IP means 3 are taken 
up out of the subnet before customer IP's are allocated)

Here's the irony - some people feel that 1500 is huge, but others 
think the 250-500 included by Telstra in their plans is way too small.

I'm in favour of structuring things with a 1500 Mb cap but with out a 
huge financial penalty involved. The aim here is to let you have a 
'busy' month once in a while without an excess bill, not to have you 
download 1499 MB's per month. So its a trade - I don't charge you 
much different to Telstra but include a lot more MB's, and you are 
nice to me and don't deliberately rip me off, and we both win.

That's the theory, anyway.
