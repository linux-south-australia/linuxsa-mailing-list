Re: Why use DocBook versus Word

At 09:02 PM 4/23/01 +0930, michael wrote:
>Hi.
>
>
>What'd a SVG ?

Scalable Vector Graphics. There is a new W3C standard based around XML for
that. It does the same as postscript does. 

Using a graphic of a drawing when you are going to print (to a postscript
printer, most likely) is just plain stupid. You get crap results and much
larger doc files as a result. SVGs are better.
