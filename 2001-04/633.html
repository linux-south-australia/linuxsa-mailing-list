Re: sendmail stall on start

Adam,

Have you got a FQDN or just a hostname in the /etc/hosts file?

Eg, your hostname might be 'woja' but your FQDN should be something like
'woja.nonexistant.domain'. Generally, you would have the following in your
/etc/hosts file:

127.0.0.1      localhost.localdomain       localhost
192.168.1.1  woja.nonexistent.domain  woja
