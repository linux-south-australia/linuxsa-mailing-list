If you're running RH, you can use the X program 'printtool' 
(one or two 't's...  can't remember) to set up parallel or
networked printers.

If the printer is postscript-capable, it's an absolute breeze.

HP laserjets are generally cool for this sort of thing :)

If you're running something other than RH, then May The Lord Have
Mercy On Your Soul.
