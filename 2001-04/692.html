Re: POP3 server - MANDRAKE 7.2

"Adam W" <woja@optushome.com.au> wrote:

> I am running the ipop3d and sendmail services from the INETD - and i get no
> errors in the logs when INETD starts up.

Curious as to why you are running sendmail from inetd - no one does that.

> I do however get this in my log when i try receive my mail:
> 
> Apr 20 20:08:29 woja ipop3d[805]: pop3 service init from 192.0.0.1
> Apr 20 20:09:49 woja ipop3d[805]: Login user=adam host=[192.0.0.1] nmsgs=0/0
> Apr 20 20:09:49 woja ipop3d[805]: Command stream end of file while reading
> line
> user=adam host=[192.0.0.1]
> Apr 20 20:09:49 woja inetd[612]: pid 805: exit status 1
> 
> Is there any pop3 config file i have to edit??

This appears to be a DNS issue, most likely reverse name lookup.  From your
log, it has taken 1 minute 20 seconds from the pop service starting by initial TCP
connection to the "Login" line, by which time your client appears to have timed
out.  To prove this is the case, trying upping the pop server timeouts in your
mail client.

BTW, 192.0.0.1 is not a private IP address range.
