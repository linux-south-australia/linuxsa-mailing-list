Re: Port 80 scans 15 seconds after dial in??

All the 'scans' are to port 80.  portsentry is a bit of an alarmist in
this regard.  Think of a user trying to goto a webpage.  It doesn't load
as quickly as it wants -- hit reload.  Still not loading -- hit reload.
Someone does this 7 or 8 times and you have your scan log.

Assuming you have a dynamic ip, it may just be that someone has a dyndns
name pointing to that ip and forgot to update it.

Then again, it could be malicious ;)
