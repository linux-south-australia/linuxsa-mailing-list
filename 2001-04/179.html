The folks at cygnus (now redhat) have done some work porting all sorts
of 'nixy things to Win32, throught the Cygwin project.

This includes Win32 ports of pretty much all the things you would expect
to see on a 'nix box, including perl, openssh, squid, apache, binutils
etc.

There is also a port of XFree86.  I have not used this in anger, but it
seemed to be OK for something in the early stages of porting.  I believe
it has (or had) some issues running on Win9x due to non-reentrant GDI,
but WinME, NT and 2000 seem to be OK.

Can be found at http://sources.redhat.com/cygwin/
