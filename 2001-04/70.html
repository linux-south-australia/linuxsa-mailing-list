GNOME 1.4 released

After a few last minute bugs were resolved, GNOME 1.4 has been released to much
cheer and fanfare!

http://download.gnome.org is the place to go to grab it all.

In addition to GNOME 1.4 there also has been released "GNOME 1.4 5th Toe" which
is a collection of non-core GNOME programs that are well-behaved GNOME 1.4
programs that are supposedly really cool.

some time to get it all down, so "caveat emptor".
