Re: Printer pointers

On 20 Apr 2001 13:01:56 +0930, David Drury wrote:

> I wish to create a text to postscript printer (ie plain text input,
> postscript our and sent to a laser printer).

Is the aim for plain text to go in one end and have PostScript come out
the other?

> I understand this can be done with a filter (and ghostscript) in the
> printcap file, but upon reading the man page ( that came with LPRng), it
> doesn't specifically mention how to do this.

If you're trying to do what I think you are (plain text --> postscript),
then enscript might be of use.

I've used it before to get emails printed out automatically (through an
alias to enscript) which it did a lot better than cat email | lpr

Have a look at the enscript website
(http://people.ssh.fi/mtr/genscript/) and see if it does what you want
it to.
