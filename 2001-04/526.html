Re: problem in Sendmail 8.11.3

Valid how?  Valid in that you can relay from the ip, or valid in that it
should resolve?

You may want to look at your access files
/etc/mail/access
/etc/mail/relay-domains

access should look someting like
# by default we allow relaying from localhost...
localhost.localdomain           RELAY
localhost                       RELAY
127.0.0.1                       RELAY
192.168                         RELAY
or something (where 192.168 is a trusted/secure/controlled by you domain)

Check the sendmail help pages, www.sendmail.org
