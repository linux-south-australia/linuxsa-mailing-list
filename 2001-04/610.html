Also, one thing here confuses me -- does the ADSL modem have an IP Address?
To be honest, I expected it not to -- I thought that it would/could be
IP-invisible... ie: from the ethernet plugged in to my end of it, I see the
ISP's network, and from their end, they see my machine... I guess not, as
it's a router. So now my mental impression of it is as follows (at least in
my planned configuration):


(note that "int" always means on my side, and "ext" is on the internet's
side)

 My LAN (192.168.1.x)
         |
     hub/switch
         |
 My Gateway (int: 192.168.1.1)
         |  (ext: [1])
         |
   ADSL Modem/router (int: [2])
         |           (ext: [3])
         |
--------------------------------
      Phone network etc
--------------------------------
         |
    ISP Router (int: [4])
         |
     THE INTERNET



OK, I expected [1] to be my assigned IP address (whether dynamic or static),
[2] and [3] to be nothing, and [4] to be the ISP's router address, as per a
