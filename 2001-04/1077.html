Re: Why use DocBook versus Word

Here is an example:

> OK, off the top of your head: How do you write a table in DocBook?

<table frame="all">
<title>Example Table</title>
<tgroup cols="2" rowsep="1" colsep="1">
<thead>
<row>
  <entry>Row One</entry>
  <entry>Row Two</entry>
</row>
</thead>

<tbody>
<row>
<entry>EG</entry>
<entry>EG</entry>
</row>
</tbody>
</tgroup>
</table>

That creates a 2 column table, framed all round without a separator in the middle column. Naturally that's just a snippet and would be contained within a larger document.
