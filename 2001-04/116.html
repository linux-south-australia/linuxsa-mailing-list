be sure that Jordan is with Wind River not because he was taken over

Note that the FreeBSD project isn't jkh.  We have a core team of 9
people (3 of them Australian :-) and over 250 developers with direct
access to the source tree.  If jkh were to leave, we'd miss him, but
the project would go on.
