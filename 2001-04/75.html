Re: Finding IRQ's

At 08:44 AM 4/3/01 +0000, you wrote:
>list
>how do you find the IRQ used by ISA devices installed in the system?
>
>pci is easy cat /proc/pci but what is is for ISA devices?

You can see all used interrupts with:
cat /proc/interrupts

See IO's with:
cat /proc/ioports
