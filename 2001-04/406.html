Re: another adsl

DSL (with rare exceptions) already -is- ATM based under the hood.

It's only Telstra's stupid wholesale 'service', which is screwed in 
ways that I won't even begin to do justice to in a short rant here, 
that stops us having end to end access to the ATM PVC between the 
customer premises equipment and 'our end'.

We are working on deploying our own hardware (initially in the 
Adelaide square mile CBD area) which will let us offer far more 
interesting services than those which Telstra limits us to with their 
wholesale service.

But in the meantime, that's what we have, and that's what I've been 
planning a service over - as any ADSL is better than no ADSL - its 
-very- nice indeed, and such a step beyond a modem that it's 
mind-boggling.

So other than John's view that we ought to ignore Telstra and change 
the world ourselves (we're working on it :) ) - has anyone else got 
any other thoughts about the service I proposed in my message? Would 
you use it?

Simon
