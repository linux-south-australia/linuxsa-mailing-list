Re: Wierd Magic with Masquerading

> Thanks, David. Can you suggest anything that will allow me to do this
> with Linux? FreeBSD is cool, but I'm working with Linux here.

Nope.  If there is something I don't know about it.  Since you're talking
about your border firewall you should be running no application except
the firewall components: don't hold on to Linux for religious reasons,
take whatever does the job for you.  FreeBSD costs the same as Linux,
and they so close to each other in operation that you'll be immediately
comfortable if you change.  FreeBSD comes with the most impressive array
of network tools, such as priority queuing (so that telnet traffic
goes out before ftp), traffic shaping (so that your ftp traffic only
consumes a fixed amount of your limited bandwidth), proper NAT, PPPoE,
vlan trunking, and gee, probably everything else you might want.
