I use the WebGear stuff from MIMP. You can get a 2 PCMCIA card kit with one
ISA adapter for a little over $200. However, a PCI card will cost a bit as
well.

They work very reliably in Linux :-), they work in Win98, but hang every
now and then, and were a bitch to get going under Windows NT, but seem to
work. I have no experience getting them going under Win2K.
