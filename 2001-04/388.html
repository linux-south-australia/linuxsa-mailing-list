DNS lookup?  What happens when you lookup (from your redhat 6.1 machine)
the name for the ip address of the machine you are telneting from?  Do
that by "nslookup a.b.c.d", where a.b.c.d is the ip address.  It should
quickly come back with a hostname.  If it doesn't that will be your
problem.
