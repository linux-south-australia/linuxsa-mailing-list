Re: another adsl

On 14 Apr 2001 21:27:23 +0930, Andrew Galdes wrote:

> what hardware is involved in an ADSL connection? what prices and where to get 
> it. do any companies (telstra for eg) offer hardware? 

For a client-end situation, essentially a router or ADSL 'modem' that
sends and receives the data, a spiltter box for the telephone/ADSL to
work in harmony and an ethernet connection, I beleive.

My understanding is that Telstra supplies this when you sign up with
them and is included in there subscription price. I dare say Internode
will have a similar offer, so don't go jumping on the Telstra boat too
early :-)

As far as prices, I think Telstra builds the price of the router into
the cost of your setup and monthly fee. Again I'm not exactly sure as I
don't use/like ADSL.

For the Service Provider, things are a little more complex.
