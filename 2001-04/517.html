p.s. RE: Linux/BSD and ADSL cards

As a p.s., I just checked the Alcatel site :

http://www.alcatel.com/consumer/dsl/

and more specifically:

http://www.alcatel.com/consumer/dsl/supuser.htm

and there is a set of drivers for the Alcatel USB attached ADSL modem 
already, for PC, Mac and *linux*.

So if you want to save a few dollars over ethernet, and you know you 
want just one linux box plugged directly into the ADSL, that's one 
other option.

I still personally prefer straight out good old ethernet. One nice 
aspect of the Speedtouch Home unit (the ethernet bridge) is the 
configuration process needed : *absolutely nothing*. You just plug it 
in and wait for the ADSL carrier lights to turn green.
