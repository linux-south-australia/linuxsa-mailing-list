Re: Why use DocBook versus Word

Hi.

The version used was '97. We were able to open it on a win2k box with
word2000 and plenty of memory. Changing it was another matter, but while
it was open it got printed to ps and run through ps2pdf. To produce
hardcopy, the consultants brought in a NT box for the same reasons. Word
and large documents have outbloated the capabilities of w9x.

If you have to use it for large documents, split it into smaller sections.
Doing it in one hit is courting disaster IMO. I'm tempted to take a look
at Lyx...

What'd a SVG ?
