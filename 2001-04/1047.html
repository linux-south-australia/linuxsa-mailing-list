Ian,

A Simple formula to lower you bill.

Bill = Download Amount * Cost of Downloading

As Download Amount tends to 0 so to does Bill. ERGO Download less.

Cost of leaching is pretty much constant (ie the ISP will not make a loss so
that you will not lose any sleep).

Simon and other ISP's are not in the business of providing your download
fixes.

They provide a service which you pay for and they really do not care about
the size of your bill,
that's your problem.

If you want to bitch about your bill then there is only a couple of things
you can do:

1) As mentioned above, download less
2) Attempt to get the powers that be to make a determination that
ISP's are not allowed to make profit and in fact should make a loss and keep
providing you huge downloads for basically no cost.
3) Change to an ISP that gives you what you want and then quickly collapses
when it runs out of money.

This is not a personal swipe at you, just some general observations about
some of your
recent posts.

Rob.
