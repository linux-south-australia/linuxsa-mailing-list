Another thought, There must be a md5sum for the iso. Check for it on the
mandrake site. It is a checksum for the whole file, that will confirm that
you have had an error-free download. Here is how it works:

Say the md5sum from mandrake says something like this:

d8acc45b1c8f4ec60cdd89834628f592  mandrake.iso

you type:

md5sum path/to/downloaded/mandrake.iso

and you should see

d8acc45b1c8f4ec60cdd89834628f592  path/to/downloaded/mandrake.iso 

If they are the same, you know your download was 100%
