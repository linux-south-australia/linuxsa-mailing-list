Re: telnet connection refused

At 04:32 PM 4/11/01 +0930, David Drury wrote:
>Another thought,
>
>Maybe you are trying to telnet to the box as root ?

Ummm, in which case you will get connected, and if all else is working, you
will get a prompt for a username and password, and will then fail. That is,
assuming you haven't modified /etc/securetty or /etc/pam.d/telnet.

>Maybe hosts.allow needs to be edited/hosts.deny ?

In that case, you will get connected, and then shortly after, get
disconnected, when tcpd determines that it does not like you. 

You see, there are clues in there as to what is going on ...
