Re: Ipchains

Mick Collins wrote:
> 
> Ok. Could someone help me find out what ports I should have to have open to
> run
> 
> Mirc : 6667 , 7000
> ICQ : ???
> Proxy : 8080
> E-Mail : ???
> Httpd (Apache) : ???
> 
> Or what would be recommended staying open or staying closed

mail will be 110 for POP, 143 for IMAP and 25 for sendmail (in most
cases) and apache will be 80 (and 443 if you're using HTTPS). I'm not
sure about ICQ, but when you've set up your firewall, you can tell
ipchains to log the denied traffic and then use these logs to find the
ICQ ports.

Also, check out the various IPchains/Firewall HOWTOs. They are a very
good source of information.

Hope it helps,
