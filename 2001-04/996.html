Re: [OT] Cisco 827 vs Alcatel Bridge

In my personal view, there isn't much to worry about here. Alcatel 
have a decent rebuttal of the issues on their web site by the way.

If you are going to firewall yourself anyway, then everything outside 
of your firewall (including the alcatel box) is outside==untrusted 
anyway.

In -theory- there is an avenue for compromise here. In practice there 
are simpler ways to steal your data (like stealing your whole 
computer)

Your Mileage May Vary :) - and it's also highly likely that Alcatel 
will just released fixed firmware in the near future - and that's the 
real point, the folks who raised this issue were probably pissed at 
Alcatel for not fixing the bugs. They will now :)
