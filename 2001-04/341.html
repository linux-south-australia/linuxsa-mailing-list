Screwy Time on workstations

We run Win2K workstations off a RH 6.2 box with Samba-TNG-alpha.

On returning from a contract in Melbourne I found the times on the
workstations way out of sync.

The login.bat is:

@echo off
net time \\server1 /set /yes
net use g: \\server1\groups
net use p: \\server1\public

Now the server1 time is correct, as are the workstations after you fix them
manually.
But when you login the times go way out again.

The staff tell me this all happened around the  daylight saving change over.
