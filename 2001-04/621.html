So we have [1] being my ISP's assigned address, [2] being 10.0.0.138, and
used only for configuration, [3] not existing, and [4] being the ISP's
router to the rest of the internet. As far as my gateway is concerned, I
need only set up the routing table such that eth1 (the ext ethernet) is the
default route, eth0 (the int ethernet) is the 192.168.1.x route, and ensure
that eth0 is pretty secure (IP chaines, etc), and all will be dandy. The
ADSL modem is invisible, and just passes packets from one side to the other
no matter what, yah?
