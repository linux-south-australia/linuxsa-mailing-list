Consider the price of (say) 3 wireless cards and access point versus 3
NIC cards + hub + cable (good stuff is pricy) plus time for installing
the cable + nifty cable hiding stuff if you are going to be neat.

Trust me, wiring a house properly and neatly (read: invisibly) will take
all day and alot of work + cable hiding stuff (which can be as expensive
as the cable itself.)

also, consider the fact that one of your machines may be a laptop or
PDA, are you going to put a datapoint in every room? probably not.

I would (and will when I get a place) go with wireless. 

I invisinge very cool situations where people come over to my
house/apartment/whatever, and pull out the pda/laptop and get instant
access to the internet/intranet. very cool.
