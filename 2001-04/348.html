Re: Netmask Details

On 12 Apr 2001 17:03:40 +0930, syahrul nizam wrote:

> does anyone know what's the ppp setting for iprimus especially under KDE's 
> kppp? I've tried contacted them, but they just have the support & settings 
> for win and mac...not for linux. Thanx in advance!

You really need to be more specific. iPrimus ADSL uses a form of PPP.
They're likely to have different account types (Permanent, Business,
Home/Unlimited etc.) all with varying settings.

Also, the settings required for establishing a PPP link will not differ
from program to program. They're probably not going to differ too much
from the Windows ones.

Essentially, you need to know:

      i) The phone number that you are connecting to
     ii) The username/password pair that you connect with
    iii) The authentication system they use (whether or not they use PAP
         or CHAP
     iv) Your modem device and possibly baud rate

KPPP isn't that hard to follow once you've got those details. It might
even have a Wizard that asks you relevant questions and sets up the link
accordingly.

Also, try opening up a terminal during the connection process and (as
root) type the following command:

tail -f /var/log/messages | egrep '(ppp|chat)'

Which will show you all the gory details of what PPPD and chat are doing
(or not doing) to get you connected.
