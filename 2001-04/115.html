Re: Server Shares and worms

You might have better luck (or at least less flames) posting this to a
Windows list ;)

I would not put any executables in a share that is world read/writable.
You should try and avoid putting executables in directories that are
writable (by anyone but administrators) as well.

Make sure that you are anal about updating your virus definitions on ALL
of your machines, and especially your servers.

corey
