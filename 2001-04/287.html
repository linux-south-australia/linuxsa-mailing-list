Invitation: AUUG-SA meeting

The local AUUG exec members (Sarah Bolderoff, Greg Lehey and David
Newall) would like to invite you to the April meeting of the SA
chapter of AUUG, the first meeting since we reconstituted.

What:   Beer, Pizza, AUUG-SA committee elections
Where:  Marcellina's, 273 Hindley St.
When:   Wednesday 11th April, 7:00 pm

Agenda

0. Welcome
1. Sign up new members
2. Nominations for officeholders
3. Election of officeholders
4. New president to take the chair (and buy a round of beer)
5. Random technical paper
6. Other business

We'll meet in the Bebes (pronounce Beebs) room.  It's at the rear of
273 Hindley St., and they should answer to the name AUUG.  If they
look confused, mention "Greg Lehey" and watch them look even more
confused.

Committee elections
-------------------

The initial committee will have three members:

  Chairman,     responsible for overall direction and important decisions.
  Secretary,    responsible for correspondence, including minutes of the
		meetings.
  Treasurer,    responsible for the money.

Currently we have the following nominations for the committee.

   Name			Position

   David Bullock	Chairman
   Ben Close		
   David Lloyd		Secretary
   Andrew Rutherford

Other nominations are welcome and, as the agenda shows, can be done
immediately prior to election.  Self-nominations are allowed.  All
committee members must be full members of the AUUG: student members
are not eligible, but if either of our student members wishes to
upgrade to full membership, or if a non-member wants to join, you can
do it on the night.

As promised, guests (i.e. non-members) are welcome.  You won't be able
to participate in the voting, however.
