Run (as root) this command:

tail -f /var/log/messages

While you're doing this, go to the box your having trouble and SSH -v
from it.

>From what you've said above, you may have some strange/different
configurations on the box that you can't ssh from. If you installed
OpenSSH from RPM, apt or whatever, you'll probably find that the
configuration files are in /etc/ssh/(ssh_config|sshd_config). Read them,
RTFM, and look through the example files on the internet.

Possibly you've got two different protocol versions of SSH trying to
connect to each other and not understanding what the other is saying.
That might explain the funny packet length errors.
