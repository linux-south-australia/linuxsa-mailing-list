Re: gateway setting with two ethernet

192.168.1.33 and 192.168.1.32 are on the same subnet if you use the default 
of 255.255.255.0
This is going to cause you no end of grief.

perhaps you might like to include the results of these two commands and you 
will have a better chance of someone being able to help you.

ifconfig -a
netstat -nr
