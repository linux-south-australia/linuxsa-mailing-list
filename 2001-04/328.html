Re: Fw: Printing question

On Tue, Apr 10, 2001 at 06:55:30AM +0930, Laptop2 wrote:
> How do you stop/restart the printing process under RH7? Under Solaris they
> had the commands lpshut (to stop lpd daemon) and lpsched (to start/restart
> the lpd daemon). I couldn't find any such commands for RH7. Do I need to do
> SIGHUP on the lpd daemon to stop it? If so, how do I do this and how do I
> restart it after I have stop it. Thanks
> 
>  Martin Stacey
>  IT Support Manager
>  Safcol Australia Pty Ltd

lpc is what you need, try man lpc for all the options.
to stop the print queue 'lpc stop lp' and start again 'lpc start lp'.
You may need to specify which printer e.g lp0, lp1.
