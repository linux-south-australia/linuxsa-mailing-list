I have used Xwin32 by StarNet and found it quite good. Unfortunately it is 
a rather expensive commercial package but if I remember correctly it 
permits a 2 hour connection under unlicensed conditions. It is available 
from places like tucows and commercial from Accunet in Melbourne.

A free demo is available directly from from www.starNet.com/x4u/ssh.htm

You will  need to edit the Xaccess and xdm-config files to permit access 
from remote hosts.
