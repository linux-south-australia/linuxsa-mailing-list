Re: LVM and Software RAID (was: Re: New hdd installation)

On 19 Apr 2001 16:56:09 +0930, michael wrote:

> I would have thought that there is an *inherent* benefit in offloading
> raid management from system CPU. That is not true?

Have a look at http://www.vinum.org/ and read the article on
performance.

With processors so damn fast these days, a hardware RAID controller
finds it hard to keep up.

Possibly when you're doing a lot of CPU work, having a RAID controler
with its own CPU would be beneficial, but generally I think that (on
decent fast machines) Software RAID (well, at least Vinum) comes out
infront.
