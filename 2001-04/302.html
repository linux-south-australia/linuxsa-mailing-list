Re: telnet connection refused

At 03:48 PM 4/11/01 +1000, Grant wrote:
>Your /etc/inetd.conf does not allow telnet connections.

Hmmm, Mark Newton has commented on this subject before ... It is perhaps a
little more complex that that.

There are a couple of potential problems here.

1. The inetd RPM might not be installed ...
2. inetd might not be running.
3. Your /etc/inetd.conf might have telnet commented out or absent
4. The telnet daemon (part of the telnet-server RPM, IIRC, might not be 
   installed ...
