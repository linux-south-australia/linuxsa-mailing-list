Re: Linux/BSD and ADSL cards

At 05:40 PM 4/17/01 +0930, Dan Shearer wrote:
>Hello everyone,
>
>Is it true that there is no driver for Australian-approved PCI DSL cards?

The approved vendor is Alcatel. There was a hue and cry in the UK about the
USB versions of these modems not being supported under Linux, so Alcatel
has promised to provide a driver in source form. It should be available by
now.

Since the same chipset is used in the PCI card, as is used in the USB modem
(AFAIK), it should not be too hard to develop a driver. Just a simple
matter of programming :-)
