Re: Resolution and video RAM

This is if your video card can handle packed-pixel 24 bit colour.  Some
cards either do not have support for packed pixel 24 bit, or do not have
code to support this format in some versions of some of the XFree86 servers
(Mach64 for example).

In this case you would need 1600x1200x4 = approx 7.3Mb which probably means
8Mb of video RAM.

Cheers,
Martin
