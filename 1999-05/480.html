Stupid mistakes not to make

I was adding yet another kernel to boot on a machine I manage recently.

I retyped the entry rather than cutting and pasting it, and added:

   append="MEM=256"

When I rebooted, Linux panic'ed very early. A quick look revealed that it
should have been 

   append="MEM=256M"

I wonder what the syntax is if you have 1GB of memory :-)
