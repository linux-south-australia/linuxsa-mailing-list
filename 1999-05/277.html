Presentation:

    All You need is Make by Richard Sharpe
    ====================

	To some people it seems like writing software and getting 
	it to compile and link and all those things requires some 
	arcase skills that are not easily acquired.

	This talk will attempt to take you through some of 
	the steps needed to get a number of different programs working. 
	We will look at simple programs through to more complex 
	programs, like Ethereal.

	Be warned, though.  I am not well versed in arguments about 
	'void main(void)', and have never used that form.  I just 
	write code.
