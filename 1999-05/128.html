Re: HP JetDirect Print server's compatibility with Linux.

Hi ,

 The problem solved. You can give the filter for the printer
 directly instead of the filter for jetdirect as was previously
 done in our environment.

 In case if someone needs to know the entry in printcap file,

##PRINTTOOL3## REMOTE ljet4 300x300 letter {} LaserJet4 Default {}
prname:\
        :sd=/var/spool/lpd/prname:\
        :mx#0:\
        :sh:\
        :rm=ipaddress:\
        :REGISTRAR_PORT=:\
        :if=/var/spool/lpd/prname/filter:

and the filter I selected HPlaserjet 4 from the printtool's filter menu.
