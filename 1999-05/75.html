Note for 'newbies' - Linux is the production of an all-volunteer crew. 

- It is perfectly acceptable to be critical of aspects of Linux, that is
fine, provided that your aim is to highlight areas for improvement.
- The one thing that you must _never do_ is make an inferred demand, or just
whine and winge.
