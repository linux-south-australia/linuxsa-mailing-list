QVT Term bugs me.. but I guess that's because I'm used to CRT, and I use the
"sz" and "rz" commands a lot, which CRT supports automatically.

whole range of QVT programs - QVT/FTP, QVT/Browser, QVT/whatever, all seem

rather shell out $15 for a license for CRT.

ain't.
