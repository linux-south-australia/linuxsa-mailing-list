Re: Squid Cache size.

Tim Ansell wrote:

 > Just a quick question, i have 2 drives for squid cache, drive A and
 > drive B, how do i say you can used up to 4 gig of drive A and 2 gig of
 > drive B?

cache_dir /diskA/cache-directory 4096 16 256
cache_dir /diskB/cache-directory 2048 16 256
                                 ^^^^
                           Size in Kbytes

Make sure that /disk{A,B}/cache-directory is readable, writable and
executable by whatever uid you run squid under.
