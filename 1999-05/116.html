Re: Newbie/Pro list

There has been much written about newbies and their tendancy to clog this
message with questions about this or that.

I find it easy enough to delete the questions I am not interested in, and
occassionaly I see a question that make me stop and re-evaluate my
understanding of a particular area.

Of course, some time ago, I was a newbie with respest to UNIX and all my
experience with VMS or PRIMOS, or EXEC-8 wasn't much use, because things
are different in UNIX/Linux land.

Also, more recently, I was a newbie with Samba coding, and even more
recently, with Ethereal coding, but persistence and asking questions paid
off, and now I have just committed the first lot of SMB decode source to
the Ethereal CVS tree.

So, my attitude is: Newbies are welcome. I will not flame them, but may
ignore them if time is pressing.
