Re: Internet banking and Adelaide bank

BankSA/Advance Bank/St George have a Windows application.

It doesn't tunnel over HTTP, but opens its own non-IANA-
registered port.

We get lots of complaints from our users saying it doesn't
work from their machines.  We tell them they it is because
we filter Internet access to their machines.  If they ask
why, I explain the Ping of Death, etc, etc.

They then have the choice of being totally responsible
for their machine's security (with the proviso that if
they are hacked the only thing we will do to help them
is compulsorily unplug their machine from our network),
riding with the filtering, or using the SOCKS proxy.

I have written to the banks explaining that the use
of a MIME type and tunneling over HTTP would allow
their application to be used in corporate environments.

I've also pointed out the silliness of coding for a
particular manufacturer's OS.

No response.
