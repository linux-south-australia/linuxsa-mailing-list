Re: YMF719 OPL3-SAx

On Sat, 29 May 1999, asianPERSON wrote:

> I have the sound card above..... i compiled a 2.2.6 kernel for it
> At boot, the card is recognized .. 
> [ OPL3-SAx (YMF719) detected ]
> 
> i look at /dev/sndstat/
> in the audio devices and synth devices...nothing appears....
> anyone have experience with these cards..?
> i read the kernel docs on the OPL-SA cards...it only talked about keeping them as modules...

I think you should try doing a: cat /proc/sound 
This will show you what is installed.

Most likely you'll have to use /dev/dsp and /dev/sequencer to play sound.
TURN DOWN YOUR SPEAKERS
Try a cat somewavfile > /dev/dsp

You should hear the wav but the speed of play may be wrong, which is ok.
If you can hear sound doing that then all is fine.
