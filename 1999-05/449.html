The address is an MX record, a name that doesn't have an address but assists
in directing mail around.

	nslookup -q=mx hr-m.b-m.defence.gov.au

Gives (in short)

	hr-m.b-m.defence.gov.au	preference = 20, mail exchanger = digger1.defence.gov.au
	hr-m.b-m.defence.gov.au	preference = 10, mail exchanger = cig.defence.gov.au

Which is says try delivering mail addressed to the address to the machines
at the addresses cig and digger1.  

My guess is that some one has moved on from the department of defence, and 
their mail is bouncing in a non-standard way.
