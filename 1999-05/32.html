Re: ftp permissions

On Tue, 4 May 1999, Benjamin Close wrote:

> Hi All,
> 	Currently I'm downloading RH6.0 and intend to burn it to CD 
> (Which will be able to be borrowed).
> 
> However, I'm using NCFTP (get -CR *) and I lose the permissions on the
> files. Anyone know of something better to mirror with? 

AFAIK the permissions are unimportant. Well I should say, they are
important, but if they are all the same, it doesn't matter.

More specifically, all files must be readable, some must be executable,
so:

chown -R root.root /some/redhat/dir
chmod -R 755 /some/redhat/dir

Makes them all readable and all executable. Ugly and kludgy but it does
work. At least it did with 5.2 and I'd be surprised if it's changed with
6.0. I'll find out for sure when I burn my copy!
