Re: Installing Network in RedHat 6

> I installed Redhat 6 and at the time I didn't want to install network =
> now I do how do I do it.  Any help appreciated.

Use netcfg, unfortunately you do need X running, and you must be root.
Login as root and get X started (through 'startx') and the control-panel
should startup too.

The utility 'linuxconf' also will allow you to acheive the same result.
