At the risk of starting another fine war like last time I replied to
someone, but...
I use samba inbetween my PCs.
Thus I can use either a command line "copy" or "cp" depending on OS, or
else drag-n-drop with a GUI.
Now, both those options provide the same as has already
mentioned....but...
with samba (or nfs), you don't even need to copy the file in the first
place! Just access it in-place.

All other options are copying a file, presumably to work on it.

So, I propose my option 3:
 - do nothing.

(followed by whatever everyone else was going to do with
/home/nickm/choosethisoneguys)

Now, that has to be the easiest task of all. No typing required, you
just load your application/editor/compiler and fire away.
