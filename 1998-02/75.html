If you want to be part of a "community" with a technical bent, then APANA is
an option to consider instead of an isp. It offers both dial up and
permanent connections to its members, and an active technical support news
group, and very reasonable costs (its strictly non commercial usage though). 

While many of its people are BSD supporters :-( they are generally very pro
Unix of any form.
