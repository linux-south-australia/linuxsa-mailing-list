The Linux emulator for Windows.  

MS Capitulate is the newest product in the Microsoft range of quality
software.  It comes complete with built in X11 Windows Librarys; C/C++,
Fortran and Pascal Compilers; all the common UNIX utilities (Perl, Grep, Awk
etc.); a choice of Mail Delivery and Mail User Agents; integrated TCP/IP
Networking, Packet Routing, Firewalling and IP Masqurading; support for Long

consultants are able to help with your query (24 turnaround).
