That's what I get for trusting online documentation. Check this out from a
doc on multi-OS installation at the http://www.freebsd.org/ site.


                          4.   Special Considerations

   Most operating systems are very picky about where and how they are
   placed on the hard disk. Windows 95 and DOS need to be on the first
   primary partitiin on the first hard disk. OS/2 is the exception. It
   can be installed on the first or second disk in a primary or extended
   partition.
