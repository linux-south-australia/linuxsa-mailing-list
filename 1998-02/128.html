With 

Mnemonic is a OpenSource browser which is being developed as
a modular browser.  It is based on GTK (ala gimp) and free
jvm and SSLeay + a few other bits and pieces.  It is being
developed under Linux (although there have been successes on
other platforms).

It should work well with gnome (the one that Red Hat Advanced
Development labs is working on).

Check out

	http://www.mnemonic.org  

For the home page, but also check out a report that worked its
way into Rueters.  Ignore the wrong URL on this page.

	http://nt.excite.com:80/news/r/980226/10/internet-browser

Don't forget the other free browsers such as arena (which apparently
is being worked on by Yggdrassil (sp?).
