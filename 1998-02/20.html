Well we no longer support free software!

Rather we support Open Source Software! :)

You can read Eric S Raymond's announcement at

http://earthspace.net/~esr/open-source.html

as well as the rationale behind it.  This change in terminology is aimed
squarely at reassuring the corperate market, and is proposed witht the
support of Linus Torvolds, John Hall, Larry Augustin, and best of all
Keith Bostic and Richard Stallman.  This means that it is likely that the
FSF and BSD communities will also make this switch, making this a
universal move throughout the Open Source Community.

I personally see this as a good thing.  We shouldn't have to spend a
paragraph in every letter/article explaining that Free != free.  Now with
this change we have eliminated the primary source of confusion.

Andrae Muys
