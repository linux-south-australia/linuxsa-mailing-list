Newbies

If you are reading this and you are a relative newbie to Linux, then you
might consider subscribing to the Linux-Newbie mailing list.

Nobody ever gets flamed in the newbie list so you don't have to feel bad
about asking really newbie questions. As an example, I've just read a
message where someone asked "what is cp anyway?".

TIPS for posting to Linux newbie.

1. Don't spell properly, this guarantees immediate help of a sympathetic
nature as they will assume you are an American or very young.

2. Address people as "dude", this has the same effect as tip 1.

3. Use a false email address for those really dumb questions (this is what
I do).


you can subscribe to linux newbie by posting to majordomo@vger.rutgers.edu
and in the body of your message enter:

subscribe linux-newbie  your@email.address.here
