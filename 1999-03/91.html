Re: Modem problems fix - Re: pppd delay (fwd)

On Thu, 4 Mar 1999, Mark Newton wrote:
Alan Kennington wrote:
>> The dreaded +++ sequence is not a likely cause, by the
>> way, because it needs an idle time on either side.
>
> Not on all modems.  And sometimes the manuals lie about this too.

Hearsay suggests that Mark is correct: Some modems do not enforce
the idle on either side of the +++ sequence.  Something about a
patent, if you can believe that.  However the idle time is not how
we know it's nothing to do with the +++ sequence.  We know it's
not this sequence because it puts the modem into command mode, and
the modem will not go back online without a specific command, which
command will not be forthcoming.  If it was the +++ sequence the
symptom would be that the link stops transmission but remains
connected, and nothing ever gets though until the connection is
redialed.
