TCP port 10752

>>>>> "Alan" == Alan Kennington <akenning@dog.topology.org> writes:

  Alan> Question: Does anyone know what TCP port 10752 is for?

Here's a theory.  10752 is 0x2a00.  If your hacker was writing his own 
code, he may have neglected to do an network-endian conversion which
means he was trying to access port 0x002a, which is 42 - the
nameserver port.

I know this can happen, as I've made a similar mistake myself.
