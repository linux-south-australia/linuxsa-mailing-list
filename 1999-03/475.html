Re: Viruses

> Generally a virus can't hide, you can always see it in the process table.

Windows viruses show up in the task list.  They show as things like
"Adobe Illustrator", and "Microsoft Word".  A Unix virus would show
as things like, "ps", and "sh", and "grep".  If you are unsure,
just think exactly what it is that a virus does and you'll say,
"D'oh".
