PostgreSQL vs mySQL (again)

Hey guys & gals,

I know that this SQL question has come up before about performance &
features of various SQL servers, but I just have to make a comment.

I have just installed Oracle 8 on a 266 Mhz NT 4.0 server, and am playing
with the tiny demo databases.

In short - Oracle's flagship product, running on Microsoft's flagship
product is a total dog !

I am not kidding when I say that a poorly configured version of mySQL OR
PostgreSQL running on an old 386sx would be at about the same performance
level. - go forth and use either product with confidence.
