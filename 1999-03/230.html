Commercial: Job Vacancy: Software Engineer with Integra Australia

Integra Australia currently have a position available for an experienced
Software Engineer to work on one of our defence projects. We are looking for
computer scientists or engineers with approximately 5 years experience since
graduation.

It is essential the Software Engineer has good skills and experience with C,
C++ and UNIX. Experience with SunOS, GUI design, signal processing, VME,
transputers, OCCAM, VXWORKS or YACC would be an advantage. Experience with
RF hardware would also be viewed favourably.

The Software Engineer should have good oral and written communication skills
and be able to work by themselves and as part of a team. 

If you have a defence security clearance or are an Australian citizen and
are interested in working for Integra Australia then please send your resume
to mike@integra.com.au or contact Mike Verhulst on 8260 8208 for further
information. 

Applications can be sent to Operations Manager, Integra Australia Pty Ltd,
