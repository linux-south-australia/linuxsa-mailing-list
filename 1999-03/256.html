RE: Wireless Networking

Paul said:
> What rates are possible over UHF? I like the idea of longer range etc.

As a *really* rough rule of thumb, the higher the speed the higher the
frequency, the higher the frequency the shorter the range or the more
directional the link (or both). Quite reasonable speeds are achievable on
UHF/SHF - have a look at:

http://www.hamradio.si/hid.html / http://www.hamradio.si/prmap.html
http://hydra.carleton.ca/
http://www.tapr.org/~n6gn/index.html
http://www.nt.tuwien.ac.at/~kkudielk/Radio/Packet/
http://www.ve7ubc.ampr.org/56k.html
http://www.tapr.org/tapr/html/Ftaprfhss.html
