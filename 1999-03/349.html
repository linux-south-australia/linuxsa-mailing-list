Samba T-shirts and Polo Shirts,

Hi Folks,

The Samba Team produces Samba T-shirts in the US and sells them. The small
'profit' on the T-shirts goes towards Samba activities, like sending people
to the CIFS conference that is held once a year in the US.

We are now thinking of making these T-shirts in Australia as well for the
Australian market, and I am doing a little survey to see who might be
interested.

We will be producing high-quality T-shirts and Polo shirts with the Samba
logo on the front, and on the back we will have the slogan: "Opening
Windows to a wider world" and the web site.

The T-shirts will be white, and the polo shorts will be white with a blue
or other colour collar.

We are thinking of selling these items for:

T-shirts:    Sizes 14-22 ---> $20.00 + 2.95 postage if required
             Sizes 24-26 ---> $22.00 + 2.95 postage if required

Polo Shirts: Sizes 14-22 ---> $30.00 + 2.95 postage if required
             Sizes 24-26 ---> $32.00 + 2.95 postage if required

*Postage will not be charged where the shirts are purchased at an event
like AUUG meetings, LinuxSA meetings, etc.

Here are the questions:

  1. Would you refer a T-shirt or a polo shirt at the prices listed above?

  2. What size would you prefer?

  3. Would you buy one at a LinuxSA, AUUG, or other meeting if they were
     for sale there?

  4. Would you be prepared to send a cheque to me for one of the Samba 
     T-shirts or polo shirts?

  5. Would you buy more than one T-shirt or Polo shirt and use them as 
     gifts?

  6. Would you like other colours, like light blue?

These shirts will be printed here in Adelaide and will only be for sale in
Australia.
