Re: dosemu

On Sat, 2 Mar 1996, Robert Mibus wrote:

> how can i enable the com ports under dosemu ? (dosemu-0.98.1-2 from rpm
> on rh5.2 pocketbook)

edit /etc/dosemu.conf and adjust the com port settings to the relevant
settings. Mine are listed below as an example.

$_com1 = "/dev/mouse"   
$_com2 = "/dev/ttyS1"             
$_com3 = ""            
$_com4 = "/dev/modem"
