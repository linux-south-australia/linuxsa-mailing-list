Ah ha!

You have found the problem. esd does require a 16bit soundcard. It puts it
in 16bit 44( or 33)khz mode then remixes multiple sounds and many other
things up to this mode. It communicates via ioctl like you said (it can
also hook /dev/dsp so that apps naturally use esd - very nice).
