Hi.

Boot your PC from a linux boot floppy. If you haven't one, you can make one
from your RedHat CD (if you have one). Just look at the utilities under
dosutils on the CD (or on the ftp download sites).

So:
1. make a floppy disk
2. boot with a parameter saying to the kernel that your root partition will
be your harddrive
    (root=/dev/hd??  , where ?? stands for the HD Linux partition).
3. correct the lilo table in /etc/lilo.conf
4. run lilo
5. reboot and all should be fine.

Hope this helps.
