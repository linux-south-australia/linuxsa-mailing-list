How to...

RedHat Linux V5.2

Anyone have any suggestions on how to do the following...


1. Measure the traffic flow on a PPP link and when the traffic flow gets to a
certain level trigger a script to bring up a second link..

Scenario is..

Two sites with a 33.6k modem link between them. Most of the time the traffic
level is low, just mail traffic and non time-critical data.
Occasionally a file transfer/database access will take place between the two
sites, in which case either bring up a second 33.6k modem link (using line EQL)
for the duration (plus a buffer of a few minutes incase it happens again), or,
bring up a 64K ISDN link for the duration (plus buffer time.) as well as the
33.k modem link.


2. Overflow excess traffic onto a another PPP link

Scenario is..

Imagine three linux PC's in a triangle formation with a PPP links between A & B,
B & C, C & A.
If the traffic flow from A to B is swamping the PPP link, then send the excess
traffic via C to B..
