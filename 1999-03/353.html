tar comes with RedHat 5.2 - its in /bin/tar
When i use it i use:
tar xvzf *filename.tar.gz
the x means it will extrace
the v means vebose (Doesnt rave on :)
z mean it passed it thru "gzip" if its a .tar.gz or .tgz (Alot of packages
nowdays are both GNUziped and Tar'ed)
f means its a file (Has to be last letter in switches)
