IIRC RH5.0 and on where all libc6 (glibc2).

But it is always better to train the Newbies in how to do it themselves so...

To check what version is needed for a particular rpm do the following

	rpm -qRp <packagename>

Which does a <q>uery on what is <R>equired by an uninstalled <p>ackage
called <packagename>.  This should tell you what you need to know.

Do a 'man rpm' and have a play :)
