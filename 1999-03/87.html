Tharmarajah ,
 
To repartition your 3Gb drive into Win95 and Linux partitions you should take a
look at FIPS, the First Interactive Partition Splitter.  This utility should be
included on your Redhat CD, or any Linux site.  Read the docs, but the basic
idea is:

1.	Defragment your Win95 drive so all your data is at the start of the
disk.
2.	Run FIPS to repartition the disk.

Instead of FIPS, you could also use Partition Magic or something similar. 
Partition Magic 4 even has Linux ext2fs support!
 
Hope this helps,
