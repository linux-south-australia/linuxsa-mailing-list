Hello All,
If you're interested in receiving Linux Jobs by email as they are
added to the active database, you are now able to subscribe to the
Linux Jobs Mailing List.

You can either:

	echo "subscribe jobs" | mail majordomo@lists.linux.org.au

or anually add yourself at:

	http://lists.linux.org.au/

It's very simple, and not entirely pretty, but it'll do the job
for the moment. I'll happily entertain suggestions for improvement
if you have them.
