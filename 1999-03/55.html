Hello,
	I've just started using esd standalone to allow muliple sounds to
play at once (mainly icq & mp3's) and I found that most applications could
not access the esd sever unless I did the following.

esd -port 5000 &
export ESPEAKER=:5000

This sets the server to a port and lets everything know where the port is.
