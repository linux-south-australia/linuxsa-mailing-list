Re: newsgroup

> I'm new in LINUX, so could anybody help me on how to create or setup
> newsgroup in LINUX.

Do you mean create a newsgroups server, or setting up a reader client to
browse through newsgroups in your ISP news server?

If it is the former, just configure netscape:

In the command line type:

netscape

Then, in Edit -> Preferences -> Mail & News -> Newsgroups Server click
Add and add the name of your ISP News Server.

To post news to the groups you choose you also have to configure your
identity.
