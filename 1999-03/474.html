> > Generally a virus can't hide, you can always see it in the process table.

You have to agree that it's far more difficult for a virus to get anywhere
under linux..

The only "reported" virus for linux was really a trojan horse..

file calls and attaches itself to all files touches..

especially when it comes to binaries and such, which typically is
different between systems.
