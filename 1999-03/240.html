Hi there,

The POP/IMAP server that comes with Red Hat Linux uses PAM, so you
should be able to authenticate against anything that has a PAM module.

Are you trying to authenticate via any particular means, or will
anything do as long as it's not /etc/passwd?

> I've been looking around for a while and I haven't found anything yet. 
> I would also prefer not to have IMAP enabled.

You can disable imap by commenting it out from /etc/inetd.conf.
