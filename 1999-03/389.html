> heyas
> Mozilla.org has a closer-to-alpha release out now (M3).
> Has anyone downloaded and tried it out?

I'm using mozilla as of early yesterday morning (ie, CVS sources, not the
actual release), and have found the following:

- Nice small memory footprint. Doesn't want 20 meg just for a simple page.
- Don't compile it unless you know your way around linux
- Rendering engine is fast, but seems to take up alot more CPU than
  netscape did. Prolly because of all the debugging stuff.
- Rendering engine is slightly buggy.
- Whole thing is slightly buggy. Milage will vary. 

Oh, and you'll need a truckload of space to compile it.
