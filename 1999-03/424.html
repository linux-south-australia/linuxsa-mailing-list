Mathew,
	Have had this a couple of times, first make sure you have plugged the
connectors into the m/board the right way around, secondly are you using
the port connectors that came with the m/board itself, most important !
	
	While the D connectors to the modem etc are standard the connector config
between the m/board & the D connector lead are not.
