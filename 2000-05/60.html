Howdy Alex,

Hmm. I had the same problem on a bunch of Netgear 'tulip' NICs. Apparently
netgear changed the chipset to a tulip clone and the tulip driver has been
broken for them since. I temporarily solved the problem by manually
manipulating the arp cache with permanent entries for local machines. The
longer term fix was to ditch the Netgear card. It was a FAS310X I think.

Hope this helps,

Michael
