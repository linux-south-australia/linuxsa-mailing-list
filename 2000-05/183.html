I added:


HSubject: $>local_check_header_subject
D{loveletterMessage}"553 Your message may contain a worm."
Slocal_check_header_subject
RILOVEYOU       $#error $: ${loveletterMessage}


to the very end of /etc/sendmail.cf, and then
/etc/rc.d/init.d/sendmail restart  ed.

Now, if I try to send a message with ILOVEYOU in the subject, it rejects
with that error message.

I got that from slashdot, for what its worth - I have very little concept
of that horrible looking sendmail.cf file yet :(
