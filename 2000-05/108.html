RE: Email Logging

*  - When the size of the dumped emails reaches a preset size, send an email
* to the administrator.
*
* - The Administrator will then come in, stick a blank CD in the burner and
* dump the archive onto CD for storage.
*
* Can anyone see any problems with the above?

I recently sent on my favourite attachment of all time (badday.mpg - a guy
beats the crap out of his PC) to some friends at work. That's a five meg
binary. I dunno the actual figure, but I think that would come to around 6
meg as an Email. Say I sent it to 10 people in the company (my friends).
That's 66MB (including the original outgoing mail sent by me) chewed up in
one hit. Considering that most Emails are actually not work related in a
typical work environment, and that heaps of mail is internally resent junk
(which means the size multiplies by the number of recipients) you could be
looking at a lot of backups...
