> most USB devices should work under Linux if they conform to industry standards.

I am not aware of any usb modem actually working under linux. I understand your
distinction between the usb interface, and modems. My limited knowledge, and
single encounter, with a usb modem was that it was controllerless and required

on a donated 3Com usb 56k modem. Since 3com established the initial winmodem

modem and it is not a simple matter of patching setserial into the (patched) usb
part of the kernel.
