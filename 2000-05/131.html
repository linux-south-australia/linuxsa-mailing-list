Re: A-BIT BP6 dual-celery mobo experience?

I have used the Gigabyte GA6BDX MB and dual Celeron 366 combination for
about 7 months. It has worked well clocked to 550Mhz @ 100Mhz Bus speed.
A simple switch combination on the board changes the speed. Stability
has been excellent. There two heafty fans attached to the processors.
Temperature and Fan contols are well documented in the bios.
