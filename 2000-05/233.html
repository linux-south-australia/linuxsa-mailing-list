[ANNOUNCE] Leaving South Australia

People,

This is an announcement that I don't like to make, but in the same way I
am glad it is happening.

As of the end of this month I will be no longer in Adelaide.  I am moving
to Melbourne to do some contracting.  

Four years ago, when I started LinuxSA, I wanted to create a group that
would teach people something on a regular basis, and a mailing list that
was open to all and useful to a lot of people.

I feel that LinuxSA has become just that.

Although I will be taking leaving the organiser role that I have shared
with Geoffrey for most of the last four years, I am sure Geoffrey will 
continue to operate LinuxSA as we have in the past.  

What I would like the group to do as a whole?  If people want LinuxSA to
grow then stand up and be counted.  Many hands make light work, and so 
if there is anything that you think you can do then contact 
organisers@linuxsa.org.au and make a suggestion.

It has been a great four years, and I am sure that LinuxSA will continue
with great growth and will continue to be a valued part of the South 
Australian Linux community.

Warmest regards to all,

Matthew Tippett
LinuxSA Founder and exiting Co-organiser.
