If this is accurate (ie, what we're seeing is what you're getting), then
your continuation lines are screwed (the lines following the first Received:
and In-Reply-To: header).  Should be indented by either space(s) or tab(s).
Your clients are probably falling over trying to parse these, then dropping
the connection to IMAP (which I think was your original problem).
