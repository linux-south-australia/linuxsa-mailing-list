Re: Mobo Suggestion Please [OT]

> I have been given an AMD K6-2/400 chip. I have looked at the specs of my
> current mobo (KTX MB-8500TVX with a Cyrix P-166+) and it recommends for
> AMD only from K5 PR-75 to PR-200.

I currently use an old Asus TX97-X MB with my K6III/400 with no probs. 
Speed isn't as good as the 100MHz FSB boards, but it still screams along. 
The board came with a P233MMX in it...  If you have a TX chipset, and a
flashable BIOS, you should have no problems if you (first) load a BIOS for
your board that supports the new chip.

There are a number of good newsgroups about this (search for AMD, from
memory, and comp.periphs.mainboard.asus is good, altho they are really only
for Asus users, there are a number of good URLs posted with info).

Hope this helps any and all.
