I understand what you mean by that it will increase the complexity of the
event, and
by no means do I disagree with that. However, what has me interested in
this event
is the possibility of looking at other distributions and other
open-source OSes.  I can
intsall RedHat and while I am more than happy with it, I would like to

to investigate the other distrubtitions available; preferably in an
environment where I

took 2 weeks to get

I agree that there should be a main distrubition, and RedHat appears to
be the best choice

install other distrubitions
and/or OSes. That could kind of be a, install at your own risk type of

that do know how to install / configure / fix could help with specific
problems. Basically if you
are aiming at the linux newbie, they will install whatever you tell them
to install; be that RedHat.
The main people that are going to be interested in the other stuff, is
those that have
already got their feet wet so to speak and are looking around to see what
else is available.
