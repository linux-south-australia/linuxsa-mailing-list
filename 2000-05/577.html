Re: Email receipts

> What if the PMG had to generate a delivery receipt for every mail item they
> handled?

Hardly a big expense per receipt; and the question smacks of hysteria
anyway.  At worst it doubles the number of messages you must handle: one
tiny message out for each, presumably much larger, message in.  That's at
worst.  With system performance doubling every  year or two a doubling of
messages doesn't seem so bad.  In reality the number of itty bitty receipts
is much, much less than the number of incoming messages.
