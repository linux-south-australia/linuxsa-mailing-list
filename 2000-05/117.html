IANAL, but, as far as I know, there is no legal issue for you here.
Email sent/recieved by employees (at work, of course) is the property of
the employer, and can be treated like any other written work an employee
creates for you.

A quick search round the 'net hasn't shown anything up immediately, but
you can try browsing from http://www.nla.gov.au/oz/law.html if you like.
