Re: Samba Domain logins

At 09:35 AM 5/20/00 +0930, Chuckles wrote:
>i would like the client to maintain profiles locally

Hmmm, this has some ramifications ... For Win9X clients, the profiles area
must be in the home share/drive area. 

Please see the updated discussion of logon processing that I provided for
Samba 2.0.7.

The only way I can see that this will work for Win9X clients, because of
the way they actually logon is to set:

   logon home = C:\\

or something like that. I am not even sure that you can do this. I will not
have time over the next few weeks to try this, as I have lots of Linux
courses to deliver over the next few weeks.

For NT clients, you can set logon path, which will control where profiles
are kept and that can point to the local hard disk.
