Re: perl question (slightly off topic)

localtime($some_time);

where $some_time is a unix time in seconds since epoch.

eg

$time=localtime(931234565);
print"$time\n";  

will return 

[jtan@host jtan]$ ./test
Tue Jul  6 13:46:05 1999 

As for a starting place on Perl programming,
man perl 
man perfunc
man perlbook

The last will give you info on the cmael book, which is to perl what K & R
is to C.
