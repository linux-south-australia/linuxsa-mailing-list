There's a paragraph on the SA Govt web site about an intra-state migration
programme called "Bring Them Back Home", at http://www.sa.gov.au. There is
a contact link there which I just used, pointing out Glen's recent posting
in the LinuxSA web archive and adding a few comments of my own. Soon after
I received what appears to be a manually generated message from a Janette
Peucker, Peucker.Janette@saugov.sa.gov.au, who works for the Department of
Premier and Cabinet.

So if people have _polite_ and _constructive_ opinions about government IT
investment in SA, you might like to send them in to the Premier's staff.
From the response I received I'd suggest that the email address
premier@saugov.sa.gov.au should work, although I didn't know that at the
time and used the rather awkward web form at
http://www.ministers.sa.gov.au/premier/contact_us/contact.html. Well
thought-out opinions are much more likely to be noticed.
