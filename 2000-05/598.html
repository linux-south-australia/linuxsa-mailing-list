PGP/GPG and Mozilla

Hi All,

I've been spoiled. So far, most of my personal email I've been doing
with Eduora Pro 4.1 under Windows 98. Having had a look at Mozilla, and
noting its support for multiple email accounts (just like Eudora, and
for me, an essential feature), I'm considering changing my personal
email over to Mozilla (or Communicator 6 as the case may be).

However, one thing that I really liked about Eudora on Windows was the
plugin support for PGP. Install PGP, transfer your keyring, and away it
goes. All you had to do was point and click to
encrypt/decrypt/sign/verify (and type in the passphrase, of course).

Now, I'd really like that support with Mozilla. Can anyone tell me if
GPG or PGP for GNU/linux offers this kind of plugin/interface with
Mozilla?
