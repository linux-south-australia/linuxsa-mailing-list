Re: eth0 time out

Alex Wilkinson wrote:
> 
> I deleted all entries in the routing table.
> And message stopped ?

But not for the right reason I'm afraid.

What's happening is that you packets are now being discarded
by the IP software as no route to the destination is available
(because you cleared the routing table).

As all the packets are being discarded by the IP software, none
of them are reaching the ethernet interface.  Thus there are
no ethernet transmissions to time out.

You'll have to post more about the specifics of your setup before
you get anything more than experience-directed supposition.
