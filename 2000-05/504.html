Re: multi mobo power supplies and fur seal trading

Hi all,

Yes you are correct, there is the "Power Good" wire (orange lead) which does
tell the motherboard when the power supply has become stable. Youll prolly
find though that many motherboards now ignore this signal. There is no
problem with connecting this wire to multiple motherboards.

As for the power supply needing a load to "turn-on", youll find that every
power supply brand is different when it comes to this.
Some will turn on and others wont.  As for how much load is needed, this
also depends on the power supply unit and who it is made by.

BTW... if your after a pinout for the AT connector, it is here:
http://www.ccmr.cornell.edu/~putnam/pcpsu_connector.html
took me quite a while to find that :(
