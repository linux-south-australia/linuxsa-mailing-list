Hi all,

Time for the LinuxSA May 2000 Meeting:

  When:   7:00pm on
          Tuesday, 16th May, 2000
  Where:  2nd Floor
          Compaq Computer
          139 Frome Street
          Adelaide SA
  Cost:   FREE
  Who:    Anyone and everyone.
