Invitation - Linux Evening

--=====================_27040755==_.ALT
Content-Type: text/plain; charset="us-ascii"


The Bookshelf would like to invite you to a special Linux Evening on May 24th
6pm for a 6.30pm start at 91 Currie St, Adelaide. Come along and meet Dan
Shearer from Linuxcare who will provide both an 
entertaining and informative discussion about the merits of Linux and how one
can change over from Windows NT to Linux.

Greg Lehey, author of "Porting UNIX Software" and "The Complete FreeBSD", will
talk about the free operating systems derived from Berkeley UNIX

Free operating systems such as Linux and BSD are growing at an exponential rate
and are reshaping the IT landscape. They are becoming the operating environment
of choice for many network management and enterprise applications, as well as
for database and web development. 

We are pleased to have Apple Computers present on the evening to demonstrate
the latest Macs ( ibook, imac, and G4).  Refreshments will be served and there
will be lot's of prizes to be given away on the evening.

Please RSVP by emailing Grace Fitzpatick, grace@bookshelf.com.au or by phone.
