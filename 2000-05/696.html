If you're into VMware, as I am, you'll also like the look of Win4Lin, does a similar
sort of thing, but the networking etc interfaces directly with the host OS.
I am logged into my ISP through Linux, and IE5 works straight up after installing
Windoze, no stuffing around with network settings or anything (apart from installing
the NDIS adapter).  Looks great.   The only disadvantage is that unlike VMware
I cant fiddle around with Samba networking or anything, so both are installed on my
PC (not the laptop).

I bought my copy from www.everythinglinux.com.au (no plugs, but their service is
great)

Regards
