My opinions are much the same - I think we should have an approved default
distro like RedHat (& perhaps offer Corel for that windows feel?), and offer
several other distros if you explicitly request them.

IMHO Newbies should be encouraged to get RedHat installed so that there will be
plenty of people on the LinuxSA mailing list who can help out when things go
wrong.  There's no point installing XYZ if there's only one active mailing list
person who can help.

When it comes to non-newbies we should offer a variety of distros - Debian comes
to mind - for people who may be comfortable with one distro and don't know much
about some others  (Like me for example :-)  

All just IMHO.
