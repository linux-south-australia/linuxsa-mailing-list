RE: multi mobo power supplies and fur seal trading

Not all switching power supplies will self start without adequate load. It
has nothing to do with any load sensing but rather to do with getting the
oscillator fired up.

Low temperatures and lower quality Capacitors with high ERD's are also a
great way not to get PS's to fire up. Lot's of you will have probably
experience the balky PC when cold (ie <10 deg C) requiring multiple kicks in
the guts to get it running.

Generally HDD's are great to fire up the PS due to their high spin-up
currents.
