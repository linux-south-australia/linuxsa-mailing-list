It's not entirely helpful BUT I am subscribed to about 5 different
general e-mail lists:

* PPP problems

Mainly caused by kppp. I don't care what KDE enthusiasts say...avoid it
like the plague. It causes unknown, unfixable, stupid problems..

* Netscape problems

Netscape itself on linux, is, essentially broken. Most Netscape problems
are induced by Java (that idiotic programming language which claims to
be universal but is really a Windows programming environment that's
somehow been ported to Unix). You don't seriously think Sun believed
they could make money out of the OpenSource community do you?

In other words:

i) DROP kppp imediately

This will probably fix your problem

ii) Take a good look at the last 200 lines of /var/log/messages

It might give you hints :-)

iii) Try using something less buggy than Netscape to test your network
connections

Try lynx, ping whatever. Make sure your Internet connections work before
using Netscape :-) No Internet connection, DNS or other service just as
important and any browser won't work as expected.
