* An ``event'' t-shirt is likely to sell well, and the more you sell the
* cheaper it's likely to be.  An exclusive design for organisers would give
* a lovely mystique, but does divide the production run which would affect
* the bottom line.

* This is rather like the penguin badges, which were really neat.  And just
* like those badges we need a design.  If I might suggest: the winning
* designer should get a royalty, which could be in kind, such as the first

Why don't the organisers/volunteers wear the actual Installfest T-Shirt, and
orders can be taken on the day with the T-Shirt being mailed out to
purchasers... This way:
1) It's a single design
2) The volunteers stand out in the croud with their "uniform"
3) The merchandise is modelled for buyers
4) Supply doesn't exceed demand

The downside is that less T-Shirts will be sold, but what the hey; are we
here to make money, or keep the "free software" spirit alive? :)
