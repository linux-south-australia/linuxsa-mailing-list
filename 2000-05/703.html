Wanna be a kernel hacker?

Here is another call to arms.

So if you :-

  a) Have plenty of free time
  b) Have a disposable machine (read, crash re-install crash ...)
  c) Want to get your name in the kernel credits.
  d) Want to see Linux 2.4 ASAP.
  e) Want to make a small start on the road to Kernel demi-godness
  f) Can code C

Then have a look at 

  http://linuxtoday.com/news_story.php3?ltsn=2000-05-25-011-04-NW-KN

And see what is in the pre 2.4 waiting list that perks your interest.

If there are any Uni group projects out in the wings, then here might be
something to cut your teeth on.

Of course if you don't want to get too deeply involved but can see something
that you might be able to assist with bug reports, go on and have a try.
