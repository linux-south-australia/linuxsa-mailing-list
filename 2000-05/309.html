Re: A Small Perl Question...

MWP wrote:
> I would like the result to be:
> text: hello 1234
> text: goodbye 1234

How about:
-------------------------------
#!/usr/bin/perl

$text="hello";
&out($text);
$text="goodbye";
&out($text);

sub out {
  my $out;
  my $in;
  $in=$_[0];
  $out="text: $in 1234";
  print "$out\n";
}
-------------------------------
