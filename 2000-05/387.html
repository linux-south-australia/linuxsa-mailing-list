Re: Mobo Suggestion Please [OT]

Go to www.epox.com for info on socket 7 mainboards. Have used K6-2 350 on Epox
oards no problems. Only thing to watch out for is good quality pc100 memory.
Had a lot of trouble until I changed to Legend Intel Certified PC100. Local
supplier can be found at www.ultrago.com
