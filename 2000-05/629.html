Re: Superdisk Drives

Hi...

> Does anybody have any idea how well supported LS-120MB Superdisk
> Drives are supported under linux?? I'm using Mandrake-6.0 at the
> moment, ....I'm looking a purchasing one in the future
> and want to make sure it will work, even if I have to update my
> kernel or distribution that's no problem..

Yep... I have one.  Works OK with RedHat 6.x.  Not sure if it worked out of the
box... I've rebuilt my kernel many times since installation.  As long as you've
got IDE floppy support in your kernel it should work.  Your LS-120 drive will
appear as /dev/hdaX where X is a, b, c, or d, depending on how you connected
your drive.  You'll probably need to add your own /etc/fstab entry.
