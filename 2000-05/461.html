Re: Off topic... TI 924 Terminal...

* Andrew Williss (bigwill@riverland.net.au) wrote:
> I have just scored an old TI 924 terminal (it was free...)
> Can anyone tell me how to get a set -up type screen on the thing to
> change baud rates etc...
>  I thought F3 got you there on most consoles but not this one...
> Any help appreciated.

I find the best tactic with unknown dumb terminals is to hold down a
modifier key (shift, alt, control, etc), and go through each and every
key on the keyboard. If that doesn't work, try a different modifier key :)

Some of the nicer terminals will have the setup key clearly marked... then
it's just a case of finding the right modifier combo.

If you get -really- stuck, you could just leave it at its default baud
rate :)
