USB Modems

Has anyone had any thoughts on the value or other, of USB modems?

I have heard differing views -one party says there are speed gains
-another says there are none. One of the advantages is not having to
bother with a separate power supply. One of the disadvantages could be
in not having a shielded cable. If they mingle with each other behind a
PC they may be prone to interference.
