I suggest your definition is quite correct. You need to polish it into
"proper English" for your markers of course. I think you're searching
for a more complex answer to a simple question. The complex part about
"scaleability" is trying to get a system which is right for the
customer/end user. For example, a GeForce2 [insert the best graphics
card] may very well be a good card for multimedia use, but can your
customer afford 4 of them (one for each multimedia tech)? In the real
world it's a case of balance budgets, needs, desires, politics AND the
ability to expand without too much drama (scaleability)....not easy. But
that's what your course should HELP you to do...
