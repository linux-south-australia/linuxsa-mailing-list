as David Newell pointed out, check your DNS.

next tim eyou have an internet related problem, try pinging. Its (one of)
the simplest internet things you can do, and it's broke then your links
broke. However, you should ping an IP address, not a machine name. If
you can ping an IP address, but not a hostname, then you have DNS
problems.
