Re: InstallFest 2000 Summary

AUUG is holding their winter conference in the last the week of June.  Invited
speakers include: Dirk Hohndel, CTO of SuSE; Mark White, Vice-President Red Hat
Asia-Pacific; John Terpstra, Vice President of Community Relations for TurboLinux;
Darren Reed, the primary author of IPFilter; Paul Russell, Linux Kernel IP Firewall
maintainer; David Sifry, Linuxcare co-founder and Chief Technical Officer; and
Andrew Tridgell, the author of Samba.  I'm thinking that if we hold the InstallFest
in the first week of July we might be able to borrow some of these people.  For
there to be any chance we will have to pull our fingers out.  Who will organise
a venue?  We need a volunteer, not a maybe.

Putting my AUUG hat on for a minute, I should also say that for the princely sum
of $150, students can go to the conference and hear ALL of these people, and others.
It is going to be a GREAT conference.  http://www.auug.org.au/winter/auug2k/.
