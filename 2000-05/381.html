Re: persistent sound volume setting

Just use any of the standard audio apps that have a volume "control"  -
rplay, from memory, has a little prog aptly named 'volume' - just make
the last command in your /etc/rc.d/boot.local or rc.local something like
/usr/local/bin/volume r5 l5  (or whatever is applicable)
That would fix the prob
