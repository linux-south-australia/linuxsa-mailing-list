Not knowing the Win98 tools I can't help you on that front.  *BUT* what you
can do is install Linux and configure the network card (without plugging
it in).

Do a '/sbin/ifconfig -a'.

And you should see a line of

  eth0      Link encap:Ethernet  HWaddr 00:04:AC:DD:EF:A0  

Where the HW MAC addresses is on that line.

Got to love teh transparency of Linux.
