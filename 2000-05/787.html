I can't tell you that, but can't you move one of them to another
interrupt?  You appear to have 3, 7 and 9 free.

> I'm also interested to know what rtc and cascade are?

Real Time Clock and the input from the slave interrupt controller
(IRQs 8 to 15).  This is still the original interrupt design which has
been unchanged since the PC-AT, introduced in 1985.  The design uses
two interrupt controllers, the "master" (0-7) and slave (8-15).  The
slave registers an interrupt by interrupting the master, in this case
on IRQ2.
