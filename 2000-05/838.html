Re: Monitor refresh probs and RedHat6.2

Hi Kym
Try running "XF86Setup" which will give you a graphical configuration
option, or "Xconfigurator" which will give you an ncurses-based
configuration (the command line version of a GUI :-)
Occasionally XF86Setup will not start due to your video not being
detected properly or something, but I have had few probs with it.
(By the way, i guess you've tried the monitor on another PC or under
Windows (dont try that at home though! ;-) to make sure the monitor is
not at fault?)
Good luck
