Re: [OT] Coming soon - high speed services over the phone network

Significantly, no mention of prices!

Suppose you get 1 Mbit/sec on your connection.
You're unlikely to get a charge of less than 20c/MB.
That's 1000000 * 86400 * 0.2 / 8000000 = $2160/day
if you use it fully.
Even if you use only 1% of it, you're up for $21.60/day,
or about $7889.40 per year.
(100% usage will cost, therefore, at least $788,940.00/year.)

I think anyone with a teenage kid at home should be very
wary about getting this kind of "service".

What's needed, clearly, is a linux module of some sort
to throttle the IP link when a pre-set budget is exceeded.
And that won't protect you from DoS attacks.

Anyone who can develop a "budget throttler" at the IP level
will be quite popular in the ADSL era in Australia unless
pricing methodology changes.
