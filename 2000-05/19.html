RE: A gamer's approach to managing a network of Linux/UNIX systems

This has /so much/ potential. Different rooms being the various system
accounts. Each monster skin represents a thread, and the size of the monster
represents the hierarchy of the thread. Super cool.

Every room is connected to a central corridor, and you need special keys to
get in each room. Root has got the superkey to get in anywhere, but also has
access to a room full of every key type (to use that user's authority - "su
accountname") and also every account skin (to use as that user - "sh -
accountname").

I'm getting carried away here. Too much fun. :)
