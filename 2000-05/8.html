RE: Sound Blaster Vibra 128 sound card

I suspect that the Vibra128 card I have in my machine is the reason why my
DVDs play so badly under Win98. The sound loses sync with image, and audible
distortions (pops and crackles) turn up with PowerDVD.

Sound setup was a breeze under my Mandrake 7.0-2 system, once I set my BIOS
to "non-pnp OS". Woops. :) Everything seems to be working very well. Don't
know about DVD playback under linux tho.
