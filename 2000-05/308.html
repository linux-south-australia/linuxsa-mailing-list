John:

> Whenever I try to run a program that requires X, after typing su to
> access root privleges (installing programs, setup, etc)... I get the
> following error message.

Try man X firstly.

Also try something similar to this:


[lloy0076@localhost lloy0076]$ xhost +localhost
localhost being added to access control list
[lloy0076@localhost lloy0076]$ su -
Password: 
[root@localhost lloy0076]# export $DISPLAY=":0"
[root@localhost lloy0076]# [run your program]

Obviously you enter your password! It should work...this adds localhost
to the access list, sets up the default display as ":0". Do a man xauth
and man xhost as well for information.
