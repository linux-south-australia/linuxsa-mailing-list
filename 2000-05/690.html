While I was dozing off last night I was thinking about the proposed
Installfest, and the T-shirt designs for it and something came to
mind.....


A large open door (representing Open Source) looking out onto a scene
with a green garden in the background, and in the foreground, Tux is
sitting on a tilled tract of dirt, with a watering can on one side of
him, and seed packets on the other side.  The seed packets are either
representations of the various Linux distributions (RedHat, Suse, etc)
or simply have the word 'Linux' on them.  Underneath the picture:
"Sowing the seeds of Linux". 

Another idea is to have Tux with confetti behind him for the "Festival"
part of Installfest. I thought of fireworks, but we want to keep the
little guy safe and out of harms way.
