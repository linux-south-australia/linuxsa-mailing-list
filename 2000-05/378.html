On Sat, 13 May 2000, BlackAdder wrote:

> > having recently had some experience with some nVidia TNT2 cards with 32MB
> > of memory under RH6.2, I was interested in forcing the cards into 16-bit,

<SNIP>
> 
> startx -- -bpp 16
> for 16 bit mode

And to make it run in one of these modes by default add the line:

DefaultColorDepth 16 

to the "Screen" Section of your XF86Config file (under /etc/X11/XF86Config
in redhat).
