The rule for each month of Telstra Bigpond modem connection is:

amount of bill = max($20, RX*19c/MB, TX*19c/2.75MB),

where RX is the amount of data you receive from the net,
and TX is the amount you transmit to the Internet.

Generally I find that I transmit more than 2.75 times the
amount I receive.
So I end up paying the 3rd amount of the max-function.

And 19c/2.75MB = 6.9 cents/MB.
