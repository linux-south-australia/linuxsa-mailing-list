I bought a BP6 and two 366 MHz Celerons mid last year.  I managed to
overclock the Celerons to 550 MHz with very acceptable stability.  To
do this takes a little time and the CPUBurn utility to stress test the
CPUs

     http://users.ev1.net/~redelm/

(slowly tweak up the clock and then run the stability test, you
can also add a little voltage to improve the stability at the cost of
generating more heat.)
