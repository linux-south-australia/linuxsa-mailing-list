Re: multi mobo power supplies and fur seal trading

there might be some funky load sensing in the power supply that says "hmm
no load to *these* lines, so I'll not supply power"

but i doubt it.

all at power supplies that I have played with will supply the voltages on
all lines wether anythinhg is there or not.

then again, I have never tried to get voltage out of one pair of wires in
the motherboard loom.

things to keep in mind with these power supplies is that not all the
linesare ground or +ve voltages. there are negative voltages there too. I
believe this is because some devices (namely sound cards and serial ports)
need a negative voltage rail as a reference.
