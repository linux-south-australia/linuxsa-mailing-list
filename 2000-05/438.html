Re: Simple question on grepping recursively

Unix.
find . -exec grep -il "10.1.1.1" {} \;

linux
find . -type f -exec grep -il "10.1.1.1" {} \;
Could be '-t file' instead of '-type f'

-i ignores case
-l shows the file.(man grep)
