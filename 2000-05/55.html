..  or  `less /var/log/messages`  from anywhere in the filesystem.  Pressing
`SHIFT-F` after less displays the first screen of data will cause it to
"follow" the end of the file.  Try it and see :)  It's handy to have that
command running on on terminal while you do other sysadminy-type things.
