fscking /fus'-king/ or /eff'-seek-ing/ adj. 

[Usenet; common] Fucking, in the expletive sense (it refers to the Unix
filesystem-repair command fsck(1), of which it can be said that if you
have to use it at all you are having a bad day). Originated on scary
devil monastery and the bofh.net newsgroups, but became much more
widespread following the passage of CDA. Also occasionally seen in the
variant "What the fsck?"
