Re: MTA of Choice

Andrew Reid wrote:
> 
> I would like to ask you all what MTA you use. Sendmail (Ug..), Qmail,
> Postfix or whatever.. I am interested in hearing what you have to say about
> these products and what your experiences have been.

sendmail

It's simple enough to configure using the /etc/sendmail.mc M4 file.

It's real strength is simply that it is the standard.  Which means
that if you want to do something unusual (like LDAP, or SSL, or ...)
then someone has been there before, done it, and written a web
page about it that Google can find.  The good unusual features then
get rolled into a later version.

By contrast, Qmail is a total bitch in a large mail environment.
It's faster, it's nicer.  But when you want to apply outgoing
mail quotas or something reasonable but odd, then you are on your
own.
