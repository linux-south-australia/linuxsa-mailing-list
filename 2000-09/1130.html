Re: Wireless LANS, and the Samsung MagicLan

David Drury wrote:
> As someone on AARNET, and has considered wireless networking as a solution
> on occasions, what are the rules/ where can I read about them ?

Hi David,

You can use wireless to link any two university entities for
the purposes of education, administration and research.

You can read about the Ministerial Determination on our
web site at:

  http://www.aarnet.edu.au/corporate/publications/

Note that the rules will change again before the end of the
year.  This is not AARNet's fault -- the Department of
Communications views AARNet's Determination as untidy and
insists that AARNet become a telecommunications carrier.

Once this happens, you'll need to fill out some paperwork
for the link and pay your share of the Universal Service
Obligation (possibly $0, we'll have to see).

There'll be more info dropping into the mailboxes of university
IT Directors across the next few months as we fight through the
paperwork that is the Telecommunications Act and its Regulations
and Determinations.

Glen
