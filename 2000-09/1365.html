Dan!

> Hi, I'm experiencing a significant reduction in data rate through my
> modem.

I assume that you are dialing into your network and not using ssh over
the internet (or telnet even) through a third party.

> I think this is a result of packet loss (I'm losing ~30-50% of
> packets to local network hosts at the university network - I can't test
> what kind of loss I would get within the network between machines
> excluding mine so I can't give a comparison here).

Try letting a "ping" to various parts of the network go for a
significant amount of time (5 minutes or more) and see what type of
packet loss that ping reports. Someone may also be able to enlighten you
(or the man pages may be detailed enough) to tell you what the output of
"ifconfig" is saying - I believe that if you understand the parameters
returned it's quite informative.
