I would suggest that someone just declares a date, and gives 2 weeks for it
to get around, and without any really really good objections, that becomes
the national (global?) date. The person suggesting the date would really
want to be the one co-ordinating the national effort... any volunteers?
Hmmm... just got an email from Anand (who spelt my surname wrong -- it's
Russell, not Russel... :) suggesting August 26th -- sounds fine to me...

IMHO, I'd suggest around the same time of year... July/August/September or
thereabouts... Of course, the sooner we make the date, the better, in some
ways... Also, there has been some talk here at LinuxSA of doing a summer
installfest... comments?

In terms of promotion, organisation, etc... I'd suggest that we attempt to
work together much closer than we did this time -- and seeing as we will
have the one date, this will force us to do so... Also, if we can make some
sort of projection or target for numbers of people, PCs, and installs, it
could generate interest...
