RE: Vi or Emacs?

G'day

After attempting to use both Vi and Emacs I have come to the conclusion that
using combination of `echo "line of text" >> file` and `sed` to correct
mistakes is the way to go. 

It may not be quite as powerful but, you don't have to learn any obscure
commands. It also gets you out of the habit of forgetting you are not using
vi and typing in :wq in every single word processor or editor that you use
and ending up with 'i' and 'x' dotted throughout your file.

Dave
