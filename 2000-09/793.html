> For anyone who is interested:
> 
> The cacheing nameserver is only that - it needs some more config files for
> the local network in /var/named with associated changes to the
> /etc/named/conf, which can be generated from /etc/named.boot after you
> change it to reflect your network.
> 
> Don't forget to set PEERDNS=no in
> /etc/sysconfig/network-scripts/ifcfg-ppp0 otherwise the /etc/resolv.conf
> will be overwritten with your isp's nameserver.
> 
> The DNS-HOWTO, and a couple of google searches were of great assistance.
