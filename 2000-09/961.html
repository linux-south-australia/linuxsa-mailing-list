Re: JPEG Resizing util...

> Does anyone know of a small util for linux (console only, no X) that
> will resize jpegs??

Jpeg is a lossy compression, which means that you do not get back the
exact same picture that you had before compression.  This is a pity
because to resize a jpeg you need to decompress, resize and recompress.
If you can stand the additional loss (of accuracy), djpeg can scale to
half, quarter or eight size; or can output in a variety of other formats
such as gif, bmp and pnm, and I dare say you can find any number of
scaling utilities for those formats.  Use cjpeg to recompress.

These utilities do not need X, nor indeed do they need a console.
