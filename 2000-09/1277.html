Re: hdb to hda

On Wed, 27 Sep 2000, steve wrote:
> I am about to remove my hda drive (windows) from the machine and make
> hdb (linux) the only drive in the comp.  Are there any problems I
> should be carefull to watch out for? An article or howto would help if
> anyone can direct me to one.

Nothing huge but a couple of things to remember when doing this:

1> If you had lilo installed in the MasterBootRecord then removing the
drive will remove lilo hence a boot disk would be handy, one which knows
where the new root is - you can build one using:
	dd if=/boot/somekernelimage of=/dev/fd0
	rdev /dev/fd0 /dev/hda1   
	(assuming hda1 is the new location after the drive is removed)
	
2> You'll probably need to relabel the partitions listed in /etc/fstab so
staring up in single user mode or with the shell being sh is probably
advisable

Apart from that, Congrats on a Linux only box :)
