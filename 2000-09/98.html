RE: 56k dial in server

Your basic needs will be a PRI and a specific dial-in server (Cisco, Ascend
and any of other big guys make them (I'm sure there will be more suggestions
as to good boxes to use, but I've been out of the dial-in side of the
Internet for a good god knows how long :> ))......

Anyway, normal modems wont allow a connection at 56K due to the way they
work (basically they try to pretend they're a broken ISDN unit (and no,
don't correct me all you smart ppls out there, I'm trying to make it
simple)).

If your looking at setting up less than 20 lines your best bet is say "Sorry
guys, 33.6 is all you get", otherwise it might pay to go for a bigger
solution.

Hope this helps a bit :)
