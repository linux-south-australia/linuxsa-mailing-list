anyway, it's simple to just create a new link... 

cd /etc/rc.d/rc3.d/
ln -s ../init.d/crond ./S40cron  # semantics of ln are the same as for cp...
rm old/S40cron  # this is just a softlink, and is probably broken anyway...

that should do it... but do make sure that all relevant files exist :)
