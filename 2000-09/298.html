Bootup message about partition check (newbie query)

Hi.

I've seen error messages fly past when booting up my box about some sort
of hard drive or partitioning problem.  After taking a look around
/var/log, I took a look at /var/log/dmesg and found the messages there:

Partition check:
 hda:hda: set_multmode: status=0x51 { DriveReady SeekComplete Error }
hda: set_multmode: error=0x04 { DriveStatusError }
