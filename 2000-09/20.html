Re: Postfix question: See what incoming mails are being processed...?

On Fri, Sep 01, 2000 at 09:24:20AM +0930, Ian Hogben wrote:
> Using postfix, is there any way I can see what Emails are currently being
> recieved? I sometimes get a completely clogged link from a large Email
> attachment, and I can't tell who it's to/from, or how big it is.

The log would tell you. Unfortunately, the answer for a question
about 'how big it is' get answered after all part of one message savely
written to disk, in other words, after the message got received.

If you're on the small link, just like me, I suggest to use your
'upstream' machine as primary mx, then from that machine all mail
would be forwarded to 'real' machine. With such approach, you would
see the message size while be delivered to the 'real' machine.

Hope that I'm not missing something :-)
