RE: additional cdrom

Saturday, September 30, 2000, 4:21:49 PM, steve wrote:

> Hi all, after much searching and reading I have to give in.  I have added a
> sony cdrom burner and and as far as I can tell it has been installed as hdb.  I
> can see the driver mentioned in the /var/log/messages as cd-r cdu92e atapi
> cdrom dr.

> I have absolutely no idea how to mount this drive as a cdrom as the fstab file
> already has a cdrom listing in it..

It should be as simple as creating a mount point (eg: mkdir
/mnt/burner )

then issuing a command like this (when you have a cd in the drive)

mount /dev/hdb /mnt/burner -o ro -t iso9660


> On a side issue does linux suffer from buffer under-runs similar to windoze?

Not as frequently. Depends on your setup. I tend to avoid ATAPI
cd burners due to the limitations of the IDE bus.
