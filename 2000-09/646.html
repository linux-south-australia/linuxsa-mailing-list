RE: /proc/net/dev

if your using a perl script to do the feeding of information you could
remove the breakage with a statement along the lines of

@cols=split(/\s/, $line);
if($cols[2]){
  $device=$cols[0];
  $value=$cols[2];
}else{
  $device=$cols[0];
  $value=$cols[1];
}

I've done this off the top of my head, but i'm sure you could convert it to
whatever language you need (or fix any perl errors i have in it)...... the
"/\s/" in the split command means split at any whitespace......

It doesn't fix the cause of the problem, but it should mean your proggy will
work.....
