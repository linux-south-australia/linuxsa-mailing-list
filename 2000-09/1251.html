Re: Questions (SAMBA, PPPD)

At 02:33 PM 9/27/00 -0600, Michael Kratz wrote:
>A few of questions for the experts:
>
>SAMBA Related:
>
>I cannot open my FrontPage web properly which is stored on my Linux Box 
>through SAMBA, it comes up with messages saying that I cannot access the 
>files. I think it might have something to do with file locking, not sure??

Well, it might be, but possibly not.

Who is the owner of the files, and who are you connecting to the Samba
server as.

You can determine the answer to the second question with:

  smbstatus

and look at who you are connected as.

If you are not the owner of the files, or are not in the group that owns
the files, then you can't access them, as Samba respects Linux permissions.
