You didn't mention it in your email but it is
extremely important that PHP3 be un-installed b4
putting PHP4 on.

Oh and un un-installing the PHP3 RPM (That is
installed with Redhat) does not remove PHP3 references
from httpd.conf, these conflict and prevent Apache
running.

sort of got the impression you didn't like - or

well I think it rocks, so don't listen to bad press
about it. It's just as good as anything else out there
(And 10000000000000000000000 times better than
postgres). Only real contender in my book is
Interbase.
