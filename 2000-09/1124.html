RE: Any thoughts about a national install day?

If we do a web site for the event, I would be happy to arrange hosting of
it, and possibly some of the design of it.

If this were to happen it would be worth having a registration of interest
form so we can have a rough idea of how many people are likely to turn up.
There's no point allowing for 1000+ people if 10 are going to turn up and
vice versa.

I think the idea on the whole has a lot of merit, and it may be worth while
starting to set-up a working group for this.

If other people agree that setting up a working group would be worthwhile
can you please e-mail me at installfest-wg@loony.net.au and I will start
keeping track of how many people are interested in perhaps helping out.

I guess this is me saying I'm happy to help start arranging things if there
are other people willing to help as well.

If I get more than about 10 people interested in setting up some kind of
working group I am happy to change the address I listed above to a mailing
list.  Alternatively if anyone thinks there is a better way of doing this
I'm open to ideas
