FYI - SuSE for SUN sparc

SuSE has announced a version of their distribution for the Sun SPARC
architecture. It's available as four ISO images from their ftp site
ftp://ftp.suse.com/pub/suse/sparc and mirrors. This mean s that SuSE now
supports PowerPC, Alpha and SPARC in addition to i386.
