Hi.

You can force it in lilo.conf by adding an append line to the existing
setup and then running /sbin/lilo again to update the system. Here is
the relevant section out of mine:

image=/boot/bzImage
        label=linux
        append="mem=128M"
        read-only
        root=/dev/hda11
