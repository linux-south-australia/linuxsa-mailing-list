RE: Limits of grep?

Hi there,

To do this on a great number of files try this from the directory the files
are in;

find . -type f -exec grep -l "what you are looking for" {} \;

The syntax is interesting, have a look at man find to see what is happening.

What you are trying to do is to with the asterisk is to glob all the files
onto a single
command line which is limited to 255(?) characters. I am most willing to be
corrected
on the 255.
