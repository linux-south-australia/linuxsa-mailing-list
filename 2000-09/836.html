That was a "feature" present during the trial (called splashscreen)
that is not part of the commercial offerings from various retailers.

You will need to select an ISP who can provide you with an ADSL
service, whether that be delivered by Telstra's cable or another
carrier's unbundled local loop cable (Telstra's which has been
declared by the ACCC).  Once you choose an ISP, you won't be
able to "swap and hop" between service providers without having
a second or subsequent lines installed as the ADSL part of your
phone line is being run by your ISP's wholesale access provider.
