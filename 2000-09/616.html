Re: Perl Syslog Problem

David Lloyd wrote:

> I've discovered my problem. According to the documentation, Syslog.pm
> will try an INET socket by default. Because I'm running syslogd locally,
> it's using a UNIX socket (more eficient apparently)

Plus it's not subject to a Denial of Service attack.  To enable
syslogd to log over a network, you need to start it with (from
memory) a -r flag.

Handy when you want to centralise your log files.  The con
side of the coin is the same (your log files are centralised).
