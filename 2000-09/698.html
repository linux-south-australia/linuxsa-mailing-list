http://windowsupdate.microsoft.com

you MUST use Internet Explorer for this.

It will check your computer and show you the updates that you need. Also if 
you download critical updates notification, it will run in the background 
and check with MS every now and then, if there are any critical updates a 
dialog box comes up saying there are new critical updates giving you the 
option of downloading them or not.

Works on 98, 98SE, ME, 2000, NT4 not sure about 95 might work give it a try.
