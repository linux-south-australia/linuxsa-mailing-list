Re: Binding a script to a key on a Keyboard

Mike,

In addition to the responses about binding keys under "bash", another
option is provided by the "kbd" utilities - see the man pages for
loadkeys(1) and keymaps(5).  I've used this to bind strings to
the function keys.  Take a look at "Keyboard-and-Console-HOWTO"
for other ideas.
