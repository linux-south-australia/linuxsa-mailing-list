L1nuxS4 Hacking Contest

Hi,

If there is a to hacking contest, we'd better sort out some rules and 
scenarios.  I like the idea of giving each team a copy of a known 
vulnerable OS (say, RH 6.2) and having them install/configure it and 
attempt to hack each other in the mean time.  The ultra-secure Gibson 
known as the "ABM Workgroup Server" could in the mean time be logging it 
all, and be the ultimate jewel for your hacking crown.  To keep it fun, 
it might be necessary to have rules about telling people your exploits 
after they are owned so that they can secure against it and keep playing.

The prize for most skr1ptx0r success would of course be a copy of OpenBSD?
