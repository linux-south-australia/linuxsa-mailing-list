lilo & Windows ME

Hi All

I woke up this mornig with great ambitions of installing Window ME
onto the third hard drive in my PC, but I cannot get lilo to like the 
third drive. It spits this error when I execute lilo;

[root@jock /etc]# lilo
Added linux *
Added win
Warning: BIOS drive 0x82 may not be accessible
Added me
[root@jock /etc]# 


The story goes;
I have Win 95 installed on hda, I have linux on hdb and Windows me on
hdc being drive 0x82. The drives that linux and Windows ME are on are 
the same size, model and manufacture.

When Linux boots it shows the drive configurations as;

hda: SAMSUNG SV0432D, 4112MB w/480kB Cache, CHS=524/255/63
hdb: QUANTUM FIREBALL EL2.5A, 2445MB w/418kB Cache, CHS=621/128/63
hdc: QUANTUM FIREBALL EL2.5A, 2445MB w/418kB Cache, CHS=5300/15/63
hdd: ATAPI 32X CD-ROM drive, 128kB Cache
Uniform CDROM driver Revision: 2.56
Floppy drive(s): fd0 is 1.44M
<clip>
Partition check:
hda: hda1 hda2 < hda5 >
hdb: hdb1 hdb2 < hdb5 hdb6 hdb7 >
hdc: [PTBL] [621/128/63] hdc1


I have a very basic lilo.conf which I have tried different configs    
under other=/dev/hdc1 etc, but I cannot seem to figure what else to   
try.

My lilo.conf is;

boot=/dev/hda
map=/boot/map
install=/boot/boot.b
prompt
timeout=50
default=linux
image=/boot/vmlinuz-2.2.14-6.1.1
   label=linux
   read-only
   root=/dev/hdb5
other=/dev/hda1
   label=win
other=/dev/hdc
   label=me

Any ideas "really" appreciated. Thanks!

Oh yeah, I almost forgot to mention that if I exchange the ide cables
and make the drive that ME is on the primary master, it boots ok.
