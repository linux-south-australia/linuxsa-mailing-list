Bring it along to the next meeting, and find someone who knows something,
and let them loose. Get them to explain everything that they do, and
hopefully come out with a working X, and having learnt something...
Alternatively (additionally?), make an attempt to describe your problem in
some detail on an email to the list... include hardware details, relevant
settings from XF86config, all error messages, including relevant ones in

tried :)
