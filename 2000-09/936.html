Debian Donation

Hi all,

After selling the last of the Debian 2.2 CDs at yesterdays meeting we

project.

Thanks to all those who purchased CDs (even those who bought them just
because they felt sorry for me).  Special thanks to Richard Russell

Costs
---------

Revenue

---
