Netscape 6.0

Hmm!

After downloading Mozilla M17, I decided to try out Netscape 6.0....

* rendering
 - as fast as mozilla

* startup lag time
 - fantastically slow startup

* features
 - a few more than mozilla

* menu speed
 - sounds a funny item, but the menus are MUCH faster than Mozillas

* ease of use
 - provided one chucks out the crappy modern theme (which took me four
installs to actually get it appear in the theme selector), it's quite
good

* mail
 - what mail? It's supposed to have one but it doesn't....

I think I'll stick with it. Mozilla is just an absolute dog to use...the
menus are slow...everything except the rendering in Mozilla is
*slow*....
