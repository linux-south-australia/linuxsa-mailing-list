Re: Sound Alerts

On Mon, Sep 04, 2000 at 06:00:53PM +0000, Subba Rao wrote:
> 
> One of our key systems has been configured to create a sound for an alert
> on the system. We have setup several alerts on this system. The sound driver
> used on this system is from OSS. Now, what happens when multiple alerts are
> set off at the same time? Do the alerts get queued to the driver?
It used to be that on my system, the 2nd/3rd/etc. got dropped. Solutions
abound, what I did was this:

1) run 'esd' as a background process (also used by enlightenment and gnome)
2) use 'esddsp [program to make sound]'

that should play the sounds simultaneously.
Another solution (albeit more expensive) is to have a SoundBlaster Live!
card (like my current one) which can natively do multiple channels at
once. (up to 64 IIRC).
