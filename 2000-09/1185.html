Re: Wireless LANS, and the Samsung MagicLan

> Aviator 'Home': 1 ISA + 2 PCMCIA cards, no base station required, $199

It's actually 2 ISA to PCMCIA converters and 2 PCMCIA cards.

I have a set of these and they work really well (old 486 in front room
with modem attached and laptop on couch).  

I have never tried them with any other types of 802.11 cards.  I do
suspect there might be issues as these cards even have problems
talking to each other when it's linux to windows.

BTW $199 for *two* cards is extremely cheap.  Just try and get any
type of PCMCIA card for < $100.
