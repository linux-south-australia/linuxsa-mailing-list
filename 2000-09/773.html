Optus@Home and Linux

Well, I never thought it would happen, but I had an Optus tech say "well
that makes life easier" when I told him I was running a Linux box.

The basic history is....

My Nortel Networks Cable Modem 100 drops out and takes 5 to 10 minutes to
get going again (I even contacted Nortel head office to try and get some
docs (none exist)).  Anyway, seems like the "area" I'm in has some RF
issues, and Optus is having trouble diagnosing them.  When I told the techie
I was running a Linux box on the cable modem, he asked me to do some pings
every 30 seconds or so and log them.

Apparently all the other users in my area who are experiencing similar
problems are running Whingedoze boxes, and Optus is having trouble working
out any possible patterns.

Here's hoping Linux will save the day :)
