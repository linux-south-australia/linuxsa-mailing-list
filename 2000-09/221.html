I've got one too, happy to give it away to be honest - it's
gathering dust in my shed - maxed out with RAM (AFAIK) but
no disk - also with a 19" monitor (unknown state).

These things netboot, if you hadn't already guessed - make a
reasonable colour XTerm - plenty of real estate :)

BTW, you *can* use these monitors on PC's, you just need the
right cable and you need to understand what frequency and res
to run XFree86 in to make it work :)

My second 19" Sun monitor runs (with an NxBNC to VGA cable)
with the following modeline at 16bpp on the S3 Trio64 (generic)
video driver (and card obviously):

ModeLine "1152x864" 89.95  1152 1184 1280 1472 864 868 873 906 -hsync -vsync

works a treat :)

for a half reasonable bottle of port (unopened!) :-)
