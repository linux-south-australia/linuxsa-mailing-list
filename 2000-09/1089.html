telnet?-ask Defense

Hi all,

Been thinking about all these security issues brought up in the past
couple of weeks on the list.
Surely it's just people like me that only use dial-up connections to an
ISP and maybe have a small home network that are ignorant of these
things?

Anyway I was wondering how many people use insecure protocols such as
telnet?
So I had a look at some SARNO traffic statistics:

For the month of September (up till 24th) only 0.114% of all traffic was
using telnet. Still that's 1586.29MB!
I'm assuming that this traffic is just through the universities, CSIRO
and Defense - organizations I would have thought would be especially
security conscious.
Especially as 14% of telnet traffic was through Defense!!!

Makes me feel like I'm not so ignorant after all - perhaps just deluded
by numbers.

Cheers,
