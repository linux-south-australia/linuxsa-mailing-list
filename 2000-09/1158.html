RE: rdate questions

Why not use NTPD Ian,
This is a better standard to go with when getting the time from the 
Internet.

And these are the available servers in Australia, including three in SA.
You should use the secondary stratum servers for your connection. All three 
of the servers in SA AFAIK are secondary stratum. There is a list of all 
time servers in the world somewhere thats where I got these from, but cant 
remember where it is.

I recommend ntp.adelaide.edu.au, this is what I use for my computers.
