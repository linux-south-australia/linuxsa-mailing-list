Thank you and Freesco Firewall

Hi guys,

Thanks to David Lloyd who came over on Saturday night and gave us a very 
understandable tutorial on Networks and Security. We all had tea, drank coke 
and talked computers.

I would like to say why I use Freesco. I wanted to share my modem with the 
other computer(s) in the house, but not have my Linux system up all the time. 
The Freesco box is a 486 dx2-66 with 8MB ram, 100MB hard drive, a network 
card and a 56k modem. It was very easy to set up (as compared with the Linux 
Router Project) and it uses dial on demand to connect to the Internet.
