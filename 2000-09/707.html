Re: ADSL Modem Alternatives

Has anyone read the fine print on the Helstra ADSL stuff?

If you buy your Internet access off them (reasonable package, including the
cost of ADSL gear) you are allowed to have only one user per connection.

Now, this refers to only one computer connected, and only one person sitting
at that computer.  What happens if I connect my gateway/firewall/etc to the
ADSL modem and then use IPMasq to "pretend" that many users are only one
user?

Has anyone come up agains this yet?  What do you think Telstra will do about
that?
