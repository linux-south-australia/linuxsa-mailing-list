> What do you expect from a window manager? Is it bells

> want a cluttered desktop full of icons that i will

> top and bottom panel like KDE, and while they are

> ENTER BLACKBOX
> ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
> 
> All window managers have their faults, nothing is
> perfect after all. But for simplicity and speed (as
> well as stability) you can't go past BlackBox. OK so
> it's not so well documented, and alt-tab doesn't seem
> to work, but hey it's open sourced after all. But the
> best of all is that the tar ball of the source is
