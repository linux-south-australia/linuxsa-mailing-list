RE: Any thoughts about a national install day?

I would tend to make it during school holidays (get the kiddies while
they're young), I'd also tend to give a months notice, if not more, this way
it would be possible for some smart cookie to issue a press-release to all
the major computer magazines.

I'd be happy to volunteer a bit of my time, but it will be VERY limited due
to there only being 24 hours in a day (any one want to ad another 24 hours
to the day?)

Depending on time and date and expected numbers I may be able to help out
with a Melbourne location as well.

I think it would also be good to have a few bits and pieces of information
about any companies that offer any Linux based training, as any newbies who
come along would more than likely want a bit of training to gain confidence
so they can play around a bit more.

Well that's my 2 cents worth :)
