Re: Missing Files

Seing as there seem to be no replies on this thread with an answer that is
100% easy I will try mine..

'rpm -q -f <file>'

to query the package that owns a particular file.  

'rpm -q --whatprovides <file/capability>'

to find out what package provides a particular file.

If you know what files are missing (and their paths) you will be fine.

Why do you want to find out what files are missing? Why can't you just install
the package and force it not to clobber existing files?
