Re: PHP and dl's

I compiled PHP4(Mysql, IMAP, TTF etc) with apache as a DSO module..
Instead of doing --with-apache=../apache-whatever, compile apache with
all the stuff you want to minus php4, and use the --enable-shared=max
switch..

Then when configuring php4, --with-apxs=/path/to/apxs/in/apache/bin/dir
