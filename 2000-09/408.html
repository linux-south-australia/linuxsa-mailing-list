Re: Linux and RAM

At 10:12 PM 9/10/00 +0930, Wayne Simes wrote:
>Hi
>
>I have just bought a new system with a Gigabyte Motherboard and 128 MB SDRAM.
>
>I have installed Linux 6.2 on it and it speeds along nicely.
>
>BUT, when I do a top it seems to only list 64 MB RAM.
>
>What does this suggest ?

For some reason, Linux thinks there may be only 64MB of memory there.

Try to add 'append="MEM=128M"' in the lilo.conf section for Linux and rerun
lilo.
