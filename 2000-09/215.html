> > The second Australian Open Source Symposium (AOSS 2) will be held in

> > AOSS is run by developers, for developers. Our goals are to promote the
> > sharing of information and experience, give the community a place to
> > interact, and nurture and harness synergies between Open Source projects.

> > Just as Open Source is a little different, so is AOSS. While we welcome
> > formal papers, we are actively encouraging informal (but well prepared)

> > papers.

> > The first AOSS event was a resounding success. If you are an Open
