There has been alot a port scanning lately in the AP area, trying to
ID's for valid IP address for exploiting other systems anonymously -
using your ID/IP details. , or using you box as a relay to carryout
exploits on other boxes.

Guys, if you're going to have boxes "exposed" to the Net setup a test
area first and scan your boxes yourself with Nessus or nmap. With your
firewalls use Ethereal to see what port talks to what, this will help
you secure your services properly, and then as a final check run Nessus
or nmap to check its integrity.
