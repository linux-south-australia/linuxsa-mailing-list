I can thoroughly recommend 3com cards. I use nothing but in my home
network (well ok, the gf's box has an Accton 1207e but only until I
can get another 3c905). I use a mix of 3c905-TX and 3c509 cards (two
segments, one 100mb on 10mb).

Admittedly I haven't got a BP6 to try these on, but they've worked in
all the systems I've owned in the last 3 years and all the OS's I've
run on them. Also I'm not using crossover cables, I have an Intel
100Mb 9 port hub and a Cabletron SEHI-24 10mb hub. (both of which also
work beautifully).

my 2 cents worth.
