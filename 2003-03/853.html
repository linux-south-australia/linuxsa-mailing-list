I don't have an explanation, but I can at least verify what you are saying.

A few of my clients have dropped off zip disks with their data on it and 
they didn't work, even though other zip disks worked.

On both occassions I was told that the zip drive was formatted in Windows.

It could be a coincidence, but my guess is that if you had not formatted 
your disk in windows it would work out of the box.

But then again, I could be way off... but if you have an unformatted 
disk, give it a try.
