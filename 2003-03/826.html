    You know I realised that after I sent the email but as you know you can't retract it then. After two attempts I installed Vector Linux 3.2 on a pentuim machine and I'm just looking at it now. It seems to be a simple version and according to Partition magic it has only used 445.7 meg of the 873 Meg partition and I made the swap partition 128 meg in all.  It has Xtree for it's file manager, AbiWord for Word processing and Opera 6.11 for the Web browzer. The distribution is dated 1999 and has support for older machines and monitors.I like it's LILO boot loader.It doesn't insist on passwords and has XFCE or ICEWM as it's Window manager. On the desktop of ICEWM it has a short cut to both a modem-connect and an ADSI.connect, plus the usual things you use like instant messenger. In short I like it.
