> Would be worthwhile to know if DNS lookups work on the IPCop box itself,
> but you can switch between auto/manual on the web interface. You may
> find some clues to your problem in /var/log/messages as well.
> 
> On the 'Dialup' page in the web interface, at the bottom, there is the
> DNS setup area. If you have it set to automatic, and it isn't working,
> try getting the dns server's address from your ISP and setting the DNS
> to manual and put the IP address in the entry box.
> 
> Also, make sure your client machines have their DNS set to the IP
> address of your IPCop box.
