Lindows vs. Windows: Court Battle Delayed

Microsoft must produce more evidence to support its trademark lawsuit.
Scarlet Pruitt, IDG News Service
Monday, March 03, 2003

A federal court has ordered Microsoft to produce more than 300 boxes of 
evidence to support its claims in a trademark suit against software startup 
Lindows.com, pushing back the jury trial in the case from April until December.

The delay, announced by Lindows Monday, comes as a welcome relief for the 
San Diego, California, company, which said in a statement that it is 
grateful for the extra time to prepare for the case.
