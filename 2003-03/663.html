> Geoffrey D. Bennett wrote:
> 
> > I'd blame the TTL of 1 second rather than the proxy server.  I've seen
> > named not want to pass along records with 1 second TTLs before.
> 
> Yep.  It's a quantisation issue.  Incoming response has
> TTL of 1.  Drop it into cache.  Look up cache and build
> response to forward to client.
> 
> Now if the clock ticks at the wrong time the record will
> be flushed from the cache before the response is built.
> 
> You need to build a forwarded response from a cache as
> the "additional information" comes from the cache (such
> as resolving CNAME to A records).
