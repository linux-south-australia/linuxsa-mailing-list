Re: Network cards

On Tue, 2003-03-04 at 00:05, James Dean wrote:
> Does anybody know of any network cards that I can buy that can do 
> diskless boot wothout the need for a boot ROM? I want to play with 
> making Xterms, but I don't want to go have to burn ROMS if I can avoid it.

We use 3com and Intel cards and buy them with boot roms. We just plug
'em in and let them boot. I'm sure you can do the same with other cards
too. If you're a cheapskate like myself, you can also just dump
something onto a floppy to take the place of the boot rom (netboot or
etherboot project?).

As a Linux bootloader, take a look at pxelinux, which is part of the
syslinux/isolinux package.

Hope it helps,
Matt
