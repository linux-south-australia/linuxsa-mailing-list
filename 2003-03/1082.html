Green line at the left side of the screen?

Anyway, if your Dell model uses the Intel 8281OE graphics card, here is 
where you want to go:

http://support.intel.com/support/graphics/linux/graphics.htm


ftp://aiedownload.intel.com/df-support/2702/ENG/release_linux.pdf

The release notes above were key, however, there were some things I 
changed to fit my needs for SuSE Linux 8.1. This is what I did:

1. I downloaded the XFC_i810 rpm file:
http://downloadfinder.intel.com/scripts-df/Detail_Desc.asp?agr=N&ProductID=798&DwnldID=2702

2. I installed it by double clicking on it

3. I ran sax2 to reconfigure my monitor. (I noted that the graphics card 
was detected. I kept the VESA monitor settings as detected, but whilst 
in Sax, said that the desktop was to be 1064x...)

4. I created this symbolic link: (Note SuSE uses /usr/X11R6/bin/ and not 
/etc/X11)
ln -sf /usr/X11R6/bin/XF86_SVGA /usr/X11R6/bin/X

5. I loaded the driver:
modprobe agpgart

6. I rebooted.

It worked alright from here.

Manually editing the XFree86Config was not necessary.
