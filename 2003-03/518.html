Actually it is possible to select an option from the third level of menu (In
Mozilla anyway) despite the incorrect positioning. Just hold your left-mouse
button down on the second level menu, while the disjointed third level menu
is visible.  Then continue holding the button down while you move the
pointer to the third level menu.  Then you can release the mouse button once
over the third level menu and make your selection as normal in the third
level menu without it disappearing.   For some reason, holding the mouse
button down stops the third level menu from disappearing when you move your
mouse.

Nothing to do with the flash debate at hand, I was just playing and thought
it was an interesting "undocumented feature" :-)
