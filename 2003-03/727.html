On Fri, 7 Mar 2003, stephen white wrote:

> Network speeds are blazingly slow compared to disk speeds. A single IDE 
> can flood a Gigabit connection.

Like all generalizations, I am sure that this one needs qualification.

I would believe this to be the case for sequential reads, but don't expect 
it to be the case for random reads or writes, or even, perhaps, when the 
data actually has to be pulled from the platters, rather than coming out 
of the drive cache.
