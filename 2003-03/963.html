RE: traceroute & iptables

Richard,

> Anyway, It turns out that I've filtered these packets using 
> iptables. However, I'm not convinced that I want to allow 
> just any high-ported UDP packet in... What do other people do?

Generally traceroute does send UDP packets in a particular range. Not
sure where you are dropping, but you could try something like this in
your firewall file.

/sbin/iptables -A INPUT -i eth0 -p udp --dport 33434:33523 -m state
--state NEW -j ACCEPT

Modify to suit your config and you should be fine.
