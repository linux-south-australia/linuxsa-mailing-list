Smoothwall doesn't allow you to easily change it after the fact:

Either:

a) assign an IP to both red/green ethernets during to install (ie. 
reinstall)
b) login to it and edit "/var/smoothwall/ethernet/settings" and assign stuff 
to the red interface.  Then reboot

That's about it.

I'd probably go the a) option as I'm unsure whatelse it may change.
