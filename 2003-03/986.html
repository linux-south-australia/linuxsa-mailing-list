Arafura Connect's fang 20.0.0 goes GPL (announcement)

ANNOUNCEMENT

The Public availabilty of-

Formerly A News Group - Arafura job tracking program
Copyright (C) 2001-2003 Jason Nunn <jsno@arafura.net.au>
Originally Written as a commercial product for Arafura Connect 


I am releasing Arafura Connects job tracking system as GPL software. This
has been agreed to under the current Management. Like iptrk, many hours 
have been spent carefully mounding fang to Arafura Connects needs.

If you are a service company in the IT industry, and you need to track
customer jobs/work orders, then you will find fang most useful with
possibly no modification needed.

Please don't be discouraged by the lack of documentation. It's a rock
solid program with 1000's of hours of up time. So, bare in mind (when you
are pulling your hair out trying to install it ;), that it's an internal
project that has only be made available to the public. Documentation will
be scarce for a while !. Patience !.

For a further overview, see webpage-

http://jsno.arafuraconnect.com.au/proj_ac/iptrk.html (homepage)

Distribution available at-

http://jsno.arafuraconnect.com.au/rel/ac_projects/fang-20.0.0.tar.bz2
