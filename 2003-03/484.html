Hi folks,
 
I'm after any advice on setting up a wireless network for the teachers
who will be getting new laptops soon, about 19 of them.
 
We need to network the staff room, easy plug an AP into the 3Com switch
there. Also the Library on the other side of the school, ditto to adding
an AP off the Library.
 
Ball park information I have been given.
 
Go Cisco AP's if you have the cash w/ external 15db antennas to give
you the most bang for your buck and to get a signal out to other places,
classrooms etc.
 
3Com AP's have suggested a midrange solution price wise and we run 3Com
3300 or better switches around the campus.
 
Netgear / D-Link come in at the bottom.
 
What experience have folks had.
