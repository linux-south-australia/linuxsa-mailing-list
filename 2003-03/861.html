Burning knoppix with a Sony CRX160S

While I'm at it:

Had problems buring knoppix (698mb) with my Sony crx160s scsi cdrw
drive. Apparently, many people have good luck with the -overburn
cdrecord option, but it is not supported by the sony drive.

The knoppix burn would complete, except for the fixation, which would
fall over with a scsi sense error.

Eventually, I read someplace that on a long burn, some drives lose track
of the TOC data or something like that, and should be used with the -dao
(disk at once) option. This worked for me.
