Re: Which Distribution?

On Fri, 14 Mar 2003 03:37 pm, johnroberts@ozemail.com.au wrote:
> I have an older Pentium II 233 128Mb of RAM with a 4 Gig hard drive and I
> have heard a lot of good stuff about Mandrake and Red Hat but don't know
> which version/distribution would best suit my machine.  Can anyone help?
>

You should be able to get either Mandrake or Red Hat to fit in less than 2 
Gig.  128MB of RAM should also be sufficient, though I cant remember if I've 
ever used either with less than 256 MB.

It really comes down to a matter of personal preference.  Install one of them 
and if you dont like it, try the other one...

FYI:
Red Hat's latest version is 8.0
Mandrake's latest version is 9.0 (although a higher version number, its 
equivalent to red hat's version 8.0)
Both RH8.1 and MD9.1 are coming soon.

Enjoy.
