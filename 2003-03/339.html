Re: RTL8139 cards.

Have had no probs. Theres 2 drivers, the 8139 and the 8139too. 8139too
recently became the standard in most distros, but previously was
considered less stable. Its likely you've hit an obscure bug in it. I'd
suggest trying the other one...
