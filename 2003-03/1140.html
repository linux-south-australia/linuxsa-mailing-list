RE: DSL-100D

As far as I remember, the driver only worked with RedHat 6.2, and I
think even then we had trouble getting it working.  Things might have
changed since then - I don't know.

I've heard other reports that they work OK, but personally I would steer
clear from them if possible.

The Netcomm NB1300 is a good sturdy modem/router which also operates in
bridge mode (just like a standard ethernet ADSL modem)
