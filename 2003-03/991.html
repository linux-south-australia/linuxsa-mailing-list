>ANNOUNCEMENT
>
>The Public availabilty of-
>
>iptrk- A promiscuous IP packet tracker for networks
>Copyright (C) 2000-2003 Jason Nunn <jsno@arafura.net.au>
>Originally Written as a commercial product for Arafura Connect 
>Perl scripts written by Peter Green <peter@arafura.net.au>
>
>
>I am releasing Arafura Connects commercial flagship as GPL software. This
>has been agreed to under the current Management. This is the heart of our
>ISP and RadioLAN operation. Without it, we would be unable to track static
>network connections for billing, detect hackers doing nasty stuff to our
>equipment (a weekly happening), detect equipment outages, unusual traffic,
>and various viruses that wrath havoc on our (and customers) networks.
>
>What started in December 2000 as a hack to trace a hacker (who at the time
>costed the company $3000 in traffic), has grown into a very powerful tool.
>Many thousands of hours has gone into this project. It has slowly evolved
>into a very powerful networking instrument. I trust you will find this
>program of great value in your organisation.
>
>Please don't be discouraged by the lack of documentation and difficulty in
>installation. It's a rock solid program with 1000's of hours of up time.  
>It runs under the most heavy hacker attacks.
>
>So, bare in mind (when you are pulling your hair out trying to install it
>;), that it's an internal project that has only be made available to the
>public. Documentation will be scarce for a while !. Patience !.
