Re: Mozilla/Phoenix Weirdness

I have "solved" my problem or rather diagnosed what the problem was.

As soon as I attempted to render:

 * http://www.debian.org/
 * http://www.wikipedia.org/

..it would crash. So, I decided to see what those pages contained. They
contain Internationalisation characters! Debian has these characters
down the bottom; as soon as I would scroll down the bottom Mozilla hung.
Wikipedia has them at the top. It would crash almost as soon as I
attempted to load Wikipedia.

I have downgraded my system to Microbits Debian GNU/Linux RC1 and it
works now!
