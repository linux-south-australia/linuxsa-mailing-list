I dont know if there would be a way to signal vmware to signal and
perform standby mode on the OS within. The vmware manual would be your
best hope for possible information. If you want to achive similar
results from the command line, try running vmware from the command line,
then when you want to "suspend" it hit ctrl + z to stop the vmware
process running. Then do an fg or bg %1 (assuming its job 1 on that
shell. The disadvantage here is that if you accidentaly close the shell,
bye bye vmware process....
