Wireless & WEP

Folks,
    Alot of people keep going on about how insecure WEP is on wireless 
networks. However,  I think some people are missing the big picture. 
There is a lot of wireless networks around the world and in Adelaide. 
Many of these have wep on, many off. The reality is Wep is still a good 
idea. Yes it's insecure, can be easily broken (although the claims of 6 
hours seem a little far fetched) but random Joe Bloggs wireless cracker 
is much more likely to try and get into a network without wep than one 
with wep.

 From my understanding, wep is still a good choice. Tools like the BSD 
Airtools WepCrack and air snort, still take a large amount of data to 
crack keys without 21bit weak iv's. I just think all the hype about how 
insecure wep is getting is out of hand. Rolling wep keys do a great job 
of solving the problems. Sure there is still problems but lets not 
instantly discount a security measure. If you want security use IPSec, 
but don't discount a first line of defence.

Cheers,
