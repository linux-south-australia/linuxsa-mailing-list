Ah,

> Yes, I know... but I'm asking about [La]TeX vs *roff here... not TeX
> vs LaTeX.... :)

LaTeX makes a lot of typesetting decisions for you and you have to go
out of your way to upset them. For example, page sizes, font kerning,
where to put pictures. *roff without macros is much like having to
specify the precise details of every itsy bitsy thing.

Most *roff authors - including a certain grog@lemis.com - have their own
set of favourite macros for the *roff's. LaTeX is fairly standard.
