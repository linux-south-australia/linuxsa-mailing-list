look in /usr/include/linux to see if that file exists.

On my RH80 system:

[mgeddes@hilarious mgeddes]$ rpm -qf /usr/include/linux/limits.h

kernel-source-2.4.18-24.8.0

You should probably check to make sure that those two packages are
actually installed (or at least one of them).
