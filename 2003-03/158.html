Yes,
One of the engineers here would like to say something about Acer laptops (we 
had a rash of them here a while ago) but it's not at all polite.   If you 
want to come and see some of our ex-laptops drop me an email).

My advice - buy Compaq, IBM, Dell, Toshiba.  The cost is often the same or 
very similar to lesser brands and the reliability is often much greater.

(I'm pretty hard on laptops - I'm a network engineer - I'm forever shutting 
them down, moving them, starting them in wierd and wonderful places - Compaq 
ones haven't let me down - and plus you can buy extended warantys for them)
