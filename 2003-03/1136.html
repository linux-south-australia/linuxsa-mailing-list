solved: RE: drivers for SMC Ultrachip-83C790QF and 83C690LJ

This worked with the card set to soft io/irq (jumper #1).

> I run a Ultrachip-83C790QF on my Leaf Bearing Linux 2.4.18 firewall.
> The driver I used was;
> 8390.o
> and smc-ultra.o
>
> my /etc/modules includes;
> # 8390 based ethernet cards
> 8390
> smc-ultra io=0x320 irq=11

For the 83C690LJ, the 8290 and wd drivers worked.

I should say that both of these were in the ethernet-HOWTO
</embarrassment>, however the io/irq values were the key to getting the
Ultra to work (the 83C690LJ was a rider on the Ultra question).
