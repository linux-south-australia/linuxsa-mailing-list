Some quotes

KMail has long been criticized for its lack of "compatible" OpenPGP and 
PGP/MIME support. Not any longer! KMail 1.5 (which is part of KDE 3.1) and 
the Ägypten Project make OpenPGP/MIME available to KMail users.

And this

OpenPGP/MIME is another format in which OpenPGP-signed mails can be sent. The 
other, traditional way, which KMail versions < 1.5 were already able to 
handle, is commonly called "clearsigning".

OpenPGP/MIME has several advantages over clearsigning, the most visible of 
which is that attachments, too, can be signed and encrypted, which is not 
possible with clearsigning.
