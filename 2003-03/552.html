Hi Robyn,

I'd blame the TTL of 1 second rather than the proxy server.  I've seen
named not want to pass along records with 1 second TTLs before.

I can only presume that in between named getting the response from
ns?.sun.com and it forwarding the response to the client it notices
the TTL and expires the record, so the client doesn't get to see the
answer.  This would explain why it works some times, but not others.
