yes, in evolution I get a little "locked padlock" symbol at
the bottom with the message "This message is digitally signed.
Click the lock icon for more information.".

It's a mime message, the headers are:
Mime-Version: 1.0
Content-Type: multipart/signed; micalg=pgp-sha1;
protocol="application/pgp-signature"; boundary="wac7ysb48OaltWcw"
Content-Disposition: inline

his text appears inside:
Content-Type: text/plain; charset=us-ascii
Content-Disposition: inline

then the signature appears inside:
Content-Type: application/pgp-signature
Content-Disposition: inline

so I guess you haven't got a mime handler for that type?
