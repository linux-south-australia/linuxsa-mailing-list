Do yourself a huge favour and stay away from internal modems, especially
the DSL-100. 

Your best bet is to get a good ethernet based modem, any of these will
work a bajazillion times better. The Dlink ones work particularly well,
but I've also heard good things about the Billion and Dynalink modems.
