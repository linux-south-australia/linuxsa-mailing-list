Re: Text formatting -- *roff, [La]TeX, etc

Richard Russell wrote:

>> Can anyone tell me what the hell the name means? :)

The first formatter program (i.e. the program that the
Unix guys convinced Bell Labs to buy a PDP-11 in the
first place) was called roff.  Then came the variations
like troff (usually pronounced t-roff), nroff (n-roff),
etc (etc-roff :-).  I don't know why it was called roff,
though.

I actually wrote a Uni paper back in '96 using groff and
ms macros, from memory.  Startled the heck out of the
lecturer.
