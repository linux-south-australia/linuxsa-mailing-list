Re: how to's for noob

Can you do me the following:

 # lspci > /tmp/lspci.output

..and post the contents of /tmp/lspci.out to us?

This runs a command, lspci, and puts its output in lspci.output. You can
see the contents of that using "vi", "pico" or just plain "cat
/tmp/lspci.output". This will give us a clue as to what ethernet card
you have.
