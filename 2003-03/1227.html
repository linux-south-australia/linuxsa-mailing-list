the sony may be expensive, but i would say they are the best monitors in
my office. we do have a range of similarly spec'd monitors, and i would
choose the mitsubishi or the philips ... or an ADI. don't go for the
cheaper Benqs or LGs. i have replaced more of those over the years thand
i care to mention. 

oh and check the warranty period, some manufacturers offer up to three
years by default, others only six months ...

if you're tempted by an LCD take a first look at the Samsung, they are
the biggest manufacturer ATM and have just brought online several new
plants in SE Asia to fill the demand, they're the ducks nuts and better
priced than most others, especially in the larger formats. IIRR the
17" LCDs just released? are the cheapest for $/inch =)
