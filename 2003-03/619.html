> Hi all,

> We use `userman` to add users. Its easy to script, uses perl, has lots
> of options (e.g. allows '-p $PASSWORD') and we use it on Solaris and
> Linux (Should work on FreeBSD etc. as well). 
> 
> http://www.drock.de/index.php/scripts
> 
> And we use SoPpasswd (Sort of Pronounceable) to generate passwords.
> 
> http://www.dreamzpace.com/perl.html
> 
> Very easy to catch its output as a variable:
> 
> e.g. (Note this does not necessarily work)
> #!/bin/bash
> USER=$1
> PASSWORD=`./generate`
> userman -a $USER -p $PASSWORD
> smbpasswd -a -s $USER $PASSWORD <--- Tie in Samba
> echo $USER $PASSWORD >> /root/passwordlist.txt
> 
> 
> Hope this help someone :)
> 
> Cheers
