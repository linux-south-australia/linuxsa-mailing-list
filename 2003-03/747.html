Err, yeah, anyway, but as people said previously, there isn't much 
innovation going on in the mainstream free and non-free DE's.

They are all based on the 2D desktop WIMP metaphor, which I think is 
containstraining factor. It has been done to death, most changes are 
going to be tweaks and incremental improvements. This is why it is hard 
to point at anything Gnome and KDE are going and call it innovation.

To get real user interface innovation, you need to look at the alternate 
metaphors. Document streams is a good example, that weird-arse 3D 
environment MS's research division was playing with a few years ago is 
another.
