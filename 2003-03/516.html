Hi Kylie,

There are 2 things that come to mind after reading the article,

1. The dublin-core set of META standards (if used by the web page creator and 
by the webcrawlers, circumnavigates the issue).
2. keywords and URLs can be placed within an html page to register with 
crawlers.

So, yes, I see the pitfalls of using flash, but it's not something that can't 
be dealt with if web pages adhere to META standards.
In addition, the increasing interactivity demanded of web pages compels 
developers to make their pages supply the dynamics. Flash delivers, I've not 
seen any real alternative other than javascript.
