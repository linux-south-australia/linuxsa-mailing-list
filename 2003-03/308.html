Re: Sendmail stalls on init

Never mind... I figured it out... The VPN with my HQ is down, and so the
internal DNS resolution is down... So it tried and tried, but never got an
answer for the DNS queries... Until it timed out enough times and gave up...

Making a small (but really unwanted) change in my local DNS forwarder
domains to allow it to use other servers than the ones I provided in the
config (risking resolution by external DNS servers, and thus possibly
screwing up LAN address resolution) solved it for now...

Cheers!
