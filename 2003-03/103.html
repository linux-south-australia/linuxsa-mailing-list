Here's an idea that is poorly thought through, and untested... however,
with some comment from this list, it may well be of use...

As filesystems can be mounted over real directories, do the following:

on the / partition:

/var/log/logfile -> /root/var/log/logfile

on the /var partition:

/var/log/logfile -> /root/var/log/logfile

Now, before /var is mounted, logs go elsewhere on the / disk, and
afterwards, they still go to the same place... Potential problems (that
I haven't begun thinking about) are:

if a logfile is created anew on boot, the symlink will get blatted.
I'm sure there are other problems, but I've done enough thinking for
this week...
