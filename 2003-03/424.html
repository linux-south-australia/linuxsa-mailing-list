Re: /etc/mail/virtusertable

Adam Smith wrote:
> I would like to change my
> virtusertable configuration so that everything sent to ian@ bounces, but
> everything else accepts.  How can I do this?
> 

Try putting a line in /etc/mail/access, eg:

   ian@bugman.cx		REJECT

And rebuilding the access database:

   makemap hash /etc/mail/access < /etc/mail/access
