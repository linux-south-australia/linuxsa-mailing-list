The only people i have heard say anything good about WEP are those selling devices supporting it. Search for 'WEP security' at google and read the research done.

In summary of what i have read: The 24bit IV used repeats its self every 2^24 (or 16.7m) packets. I understand that repeated IV's means repeated keys and repeated keys is bad practice. This is made worse when more than one device is using that key (there is no standard as to where that IV starts so most vendors start at 0).

I have no experiance with this myself so the above is based totally on what i have read.
