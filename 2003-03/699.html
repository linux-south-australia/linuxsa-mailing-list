lol.

a) WEP is crackable fairly quickly (esp 40 bit) so you'd need to change quite 
often,
b) without a synchronised time source the change over would kill connectivity,
c) how do both ends know what to change to?  You need not just a secret, but a 
long list of secrets.  Do you repeat WEP keys?  Or are they effectively use 
once?  Use once is lots more secure, but they you have standard key exchange 
problems.  No matter what way you go, you need to securely transfer these code 
books.  Not trivial to do securely and reliably, or on a large scale.

Do the IPsec shuffle, baby!
