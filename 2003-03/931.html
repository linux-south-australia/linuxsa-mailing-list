Linux.Conf.Au Helping

Last night at the LinuxSA meeting, people asked if the sign-on page for the 
Linux.Conf.Au 2004 Helpers mailing list could be linked to LinuxSA's home page.

In a break with tradition, the LinuxSA home page has been updated (wow!) with 
that link.  Thanks G.

So if you'd like to help Linux.Conf.Au 2004 in Adelaide be a fantastic success, 
go to http://www.linuxsa.org.au and click on "LCA2004 Helper's Mailing List" 
which will take you to "http://www.netcraft.com.au/mailman/listinfo/lca2004-
helpers".
