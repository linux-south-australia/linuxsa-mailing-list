I'ts not *entirely* irrelevant.

>  This is running off an
>   onboard Intel 845GE display adapter - a Gigabyte GA-8GE667 Pro
>   motherboard. The install detects the video adapter as a 845 series
>   and runs it from the 810 driver.

In your monitor device section enter something like this:

Section "Monitor"
    Identifier   "Monitor0"
    VendorName   "NEC"
    ModelName    "37fa"
    HorizSync 28-60
    VertRefresh 40-90
EndSection

Obviously your Identifier, Vendor Name and Model Name will be slightly
different. 

What you want to fiddle with is the HorizSync and VertRefresh. Try:

HorizSync 31-36
VertRefresh 40-90

..and see what happens. I know that looks horrid and probably will look
horrid but if you can get *soemthing* to display you can inch  the
numbers UP until it falls over again.

that are way too high, the buffer doesn't cope and basically spits the
dummy at you.]
