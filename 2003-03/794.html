NTP accuracy

Hey All,

I've just set up an NTP server for use withing our WAN and I was just
wondering what sort of accuracies you'd expect from this. Our setup is
that we have a couple of telehoused machines, one of which I've set up an
NTP server. We have ADSL at each of our offices with VPN tunnels back to
one of the telehoused machines.

Watching it for a while I noticed that the offset from the (Stratum 1)
upstreams was varying between 0.01ms and 300ms. This was over the space of
10-20 minutes. Is it normal for it to vary by so much? I do realise that
even 300ms is 0.3 of a second! :)
