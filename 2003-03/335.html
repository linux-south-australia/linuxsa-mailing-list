[ANNOUNCEMENT]: SA-AUUG March Meeting Wed 12/3/2003 (Note ChangedVenue)]

* To all attendees, Please note the change of venue *

The South Australian chapter of the Australian Unix User Group (AUUG)
formally invites you to attend their monthly meeting. Details are as
follows:

+----------------------------------------
| SA-AUUG Chapter Meeting
+--------+-------------------------------
| DATE   | 12th March 2003
| TIME   | 7pm Sharp ( We must be out by 8:30 )
| VENUE  | Ngapartji Multimedia Center
|        | 211 Rundle Street
|        | ADELAIDE 5000
+--------+------------------------------
| AGENDA |
+--------+------------------------------

1. Open Source Software and the Role of Linux in the Public Sector

   Greg Lehey is the president of AUUG and a member of AUUG's
   committee "Open Computing for Government".  Last month AUUG
   presented a paper to Commonwealth and State agency CIOs and CTOs at
   a seminar arranged by the National Office of the Information
   Economy (NOIE).  Greg was involved in the presentation, and 
   he will present the paper again and discuss the differences in
   focus between the Government and AUUG.

2. Discussion about the upcoming AGM
   Next month sees the SA Chapter re-established for two years.
   To lead the chapter into the future, members will be informed about
   the upcoming annual general meeting where elections will take place.
 
----------------------------------------
At the conclusion of the meeting many members of the group continue
to talk as they wine and dine at one of the fine local restaurant
establishments to which all are invited.

Hope to see you there.

Regards,
  SA-AUUG Secretary
