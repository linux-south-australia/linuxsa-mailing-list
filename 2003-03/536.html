Ok I did some more "digging" around and basically it appears to me (correct
me if I'm wrong anyone) that only one of the 4 sun.com authoritive name
servers are configured for this wwws.sun.com zone correctly.

ns8.sun.com, ns2.sun.com and ns7.sun.com all produce similar output to your
dig, but with differing IP addresses (ok this could be simply load
balancing).  However, ns1.sun.com produces the sane dig output I got from my
own nameserver. Basically I think I was just lucky and my nameserver
recursed through to ns1.sun.com.

So IMHO Robyn it appears to me the problem is not at your end.  I know that
doesn't help getting to the site though, but I guess you could always use
the IP in the meantime providing they are not running name based virtual
hosting :)
