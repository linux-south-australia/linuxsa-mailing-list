i assume you're doing the port forward for mail...

i have used in the past stunnel under linux and bounce under freebsd
to forward a specific port for session based stuf like smtp, pop and
imap. setup an entry in (x)inetd to accept on localhost on a port of
your choice, connecting to  the remote server on a high port of your
choice, where (x)inetd runs another copy of stunnel or ...
which terminates the connection to the local service.

what app/service are you trying to forard?
