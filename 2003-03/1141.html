Re: DSL-100D

simplemoe@ozemail.com.au wrote:

> Has anyone had experience with a DSL-100D under linux,
 > and had any problems getting it running? I know there
 > are drivers available for it on the d-link website.

One consideration is that the ITeX Apollo2 chipset
might cease to be available in the future, as ITeX
are reorganising under the supervision of a bankruptcy
court.

So if the Linux driver doesn't have source then
don't go near it, as the D-Link DSL-100D model
might cease to be sold sometime later in the year
if things go poorly at ITeX.

When this sort of thing has happened in the
past, manufacturers haven't kept their binary
modules up to date with changes in the kernel.

If source is available then the community has pretty
much picked up this task themsleves.
