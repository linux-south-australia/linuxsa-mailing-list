This looks very much like the ataraid drivers are not being loaded. Linux
see the drives as standard IDE devices, with out this support.

Looking at a standard 2.4.20 kernel I do not see support for ataraid  with
the highpoint 372, so unless you can find support in a AC kernel or from
some other source, I say you are buggard.
