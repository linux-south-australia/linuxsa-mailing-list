Re: NTN: Build our own domain?

Ian Loxton wrote:
> I want to investigate how to build a own domain name and handle
> emails in that name for our project. I have an offer of space on a
> site with a fixed IP. What do I do now?

Find a domain hosting service, this could be the guys doing the web 
hosting, your registrar, or someone else. Register the domain, and 
delegate it to their name servers. Make sure you register the domain 
with yourself as the admin contact, so you retain control over it.

Get your web hosting service to set up your (probably virtual) web 
server, get your domain hosting service to add an A record pointing 
www.yourdomain to the web server's IP address.

Find an email hosting service, again this could be the guys doing the 
web hosting, your registrar, or someone else. Get that setup and get an 
MX record set up for your domain pointing to the mail server. Get them 
to set up the email addresses you want.

Sit back, relax, enjoy the new domain.
