Sure, you can ask...

We use Legato Networker, running on Linux controlling 6 SDLT drives and 
258 slots.

SDLT drives hold 110Gb uncompressed - some of our tapes have up to 350Gb 
on them (mainly finance data and indexes).  We do a quarterly full and 
incrementals everyday.  The tapes go offsite and all is well with the 
world.  Really easy...

Of course, this box is not the only box that is backed up.  There are 
workstations and other servers that also get backed up.  That's probably 
another TB or so... no biggie (just painful putting all those tapes into 
the jukebox every week, but someone has to do it...)
