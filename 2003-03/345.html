James,

Back in the olden days before linux reached 2000 lines of C code,
I used to do something similar on the office Sun 4 workstation.
Except that I ran the sounds out of the procmailrc file.
Arrival of mail from the boss caused 3 dog barks.
Mail from the big boss interstate caused the sound of bubbles then a gong.
Each mailing list caused a different kind of sound effect.
Very busy mailing lists caused the sound of a tap going drip drip drip.
Mail from the girlfriend made the sounds "chirp bark chirp bark crash chirp".
This caused some amusement when people heard it while I was out of the office.

But eventually I turned it off when I got my own unix computer at home
because it kept waking me up.

I imagine that if one had the time, one could program procmail to
choose an MP3 file suited to the meaning of the mail.
Oh to be young and free....!
