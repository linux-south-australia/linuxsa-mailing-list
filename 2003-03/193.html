Geeze Alan,
I reckon you made the whole thing a bit hard on yourself.

Sendmail configuration is pretty easy - get an m4 file, change the settings 
to match (usually a slight variation on the vendor supplied one), build it 
(m4 ../cf/cf.m4 <yourm4file> > yourfile.cf - copy it into place.  Mine 
hasn't greatly changed in year.   Dunno why that's any harder than anything 
else.  It's a damn sight more configurable.

If you're moving files around etc then you've not taken advantage of the 
compile/configure time options to have your files where you want.  But I 
have to say /etc/mail and /etc/aliases has been pretty standard for a while 
now on most things I've played with.
