Linux.Conf.Au: Looking for help

Hi all,


We're looking for some more people to help with Linux.Conf.Au -
specifically "babysitting" lecture theatres.

What this means is:
- helping a speaker hook up his/her laptop to the AV system,
- introducing them to the audience,
- keeping track of time, ringing a bell when its question time,
- reminding the audience to fill in evaluation forms,
- stopping the lecture if the speaker runs over time, so as the next
lecture  has their alloted time.- Doing all of this for one lecture theatre, for the morning or afternoon,
for each of the 4 days of the main conference (and optionally, helping the
miniconfs if you're going anway).

In exchance for such service you get: a 50% discount on your registration
(you choose the category), a special helpers t-shirt, and public
recognition from me at the conference open/close.

The list of the current slots that are taken and a summary of what you
need to do, can be found here:  http://msdavies.net/helpers-schedule.html

First in, first serve!  Help our conference today!
