One of my goals is to leverage all other accumulated experience from 
whatever fields are available. To that end, I will read the PDF James 
refers to so I can glean best practices and other ideas.

I also plan to respond to the other points made.

There is, however, one thing I think we need to remember. The accounting 
industry _seems_ to be highly labor intensive, and I think we need to make 
it possible for people to run tests and submit results easily.

However, there is no doubt that developing the test suites will involve 
labor, and my hope here is that we can leverage the desire of many people 
who can code (even a little) to specify and code at least initial tests, 
and have these tests and scripts work their way up to being accepted 
through a process of review.
