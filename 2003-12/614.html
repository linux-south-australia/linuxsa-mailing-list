Just maintain your own killfile.  It's easier in
the long run.  .procmailrc saith:

  :0
  * ^From:.*glen\.turner.*@.*aarnet\.edu\.au
  /dev/null

Lot's of email clients have client-side filtering
(which is fine if you just use that one client).
For example, Evolution makes it easy to set up
"set status Read" filter based on sender.

This is nicer than a killfile, as you don't have
to read the tripe but can still access their
occasional on-topic message.
