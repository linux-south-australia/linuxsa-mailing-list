Re: Modems and "Soft" Modems under Linux

Hi Peter

There is a command line tool

lspci

that will tell you everything that is connected to the PCI bus and it
will tell you what Linux thinks your modem is.

Or you could try

http://linmodems.technion.ac.il/packages/scanModem.gz

unpack the file and run it from a terminal. The text files it creates
will tell you 

1. What your modem is
2. Hopefully where to go to get a driver
3. What the driver is called

Let us know if you need anymore help.

Robyn
