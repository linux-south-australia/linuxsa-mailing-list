SB Live! 5.1 (emu10k1) no output on lfe and center

Hi!
I just bought the Sound Blaster Live! 5.1 Digital and connected it with my 5.1 
active soundsystem. 

By activating the emu10k1 in kernel and recompiling (make install) and 
installation of emu-tools, and configuration by emu-dspmgr with the following 
options

emu-config -a 

emu-dspmgr -a"fx8:Front L"
emu-dspmgr -a"fx9:Front R"
emu-dspmgr -a"fx10:Rear L"
emu-dspmgr -a"fx11:Rear R"
emu-dspmgr -a"fx12:Analog Center"
emu-dspmgr -a"fx13:Analog LFE"

I reached, that now sound comes out of the two front and the two rear 
speakers.
But I still have the problem, that no sound comes out of my center speaker and 
my subwoofer, bass (lfe). 

Does anyone have an idea what to check or what to do?
