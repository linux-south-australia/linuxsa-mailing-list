Re: Mounting a DD hard drive image

Mike O'Connor <mike@pineview.net> wrote:
> I wondering if any one knows the magic required to mount a dd dump of a hard
> drive image.

Trim the stuff that's before your filesystem.  For example, if it's a
dd of the complete disk, remove the first 'track' of data.  That will
leave you with a file containing just the filesystem you want.
