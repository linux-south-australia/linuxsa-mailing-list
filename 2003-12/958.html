Re: Policy Makers' Positions

W. Sierke wrote:

> Why should they (pollies) care? A bunch of voters bleating about tax dollars
> going to Microsoft isn't going to hold up against all the perceived benefits


I'm not arguing benefits, I'm arguing price.  The marginal cost of
a Microsoft sale to a government desktop is is the cents.  Microsoft
are getting near $100.  I can't think of any other industry with
such an imbalance of revenue to cost.  There's plenty of fat there
for negotiation, and if that fails then there's now a viable
alternative.

Price strikes me as being the core reason for an agency which is
already running a set of software to go through the disruption of
changing to another set of software.

 From our point of view there are other reasons.  But putting myself
in the shoes of a CIO at a gov't agency, I think price is the
main driver for a change.
