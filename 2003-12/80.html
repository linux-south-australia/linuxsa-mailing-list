RE: internode adsl pricing OT

Bandwidth is not just bandwidth.
Im on a BADSL connection at home and a perm connection at work. For home
there hasn't been a single glitch in 9 months (at least not that I have
noticed). At work we havent had a problem in nearly 3 years, but then again
with 100Mb/s you can expect some decent redundancy/fault tolerance on their
behalf. Sure you pay more but you get the piece of mind knowing it will
'always' be working. Im sure if I read the EULA they have some mention of
99.x% avalibilty for business and somewhat less for residential.

Having said that, I got a friend onto 2Mb SHDSL through Internode. It would
be significantly cheaper for him to get 4x512 SDSL connections including the
phone charges. As he is paying 9c a MB which is a bit rich. Might have to
get him to move across. 

PS IP addresses are scarce.. Hence IPV6 (well part of the reason) although
they arent so scarce as to jack business prices up that much.

Im sure the 'node boys will pick me up on any of this if im wrong. :)

Michael
