Sunday Morning Service Callout

I had a service callout this morning for a home puta that I installed a
week ago.  He said the floppy was not working.

I remembered pulling the IDE cables apart after the install to put in
his old 2GB disk, so he could access all his old Windoze data from
linux, so I thought that in my haste to leave, I must have connected the
floppy drive cable the wrong way around - common mistake and not easy to
detect unless you test the floppy (and who ever tests that ?). My
mistake obviously, so I promised him a free service call first thing
Sunday morning, since Im in Christmas mode.

Well, I drive all the way out to Salisbury, dodging the silly season
traffic, checked the cable, looked fine. Stick a disk in, mount it,
reads fine. Hmm .. whats the problem I wonder ?  "Oh yeah - I get those
files come up, but look - click on this file here (on the floppy) - and
nothing happens. Aha - it's a .LNK file to a spreadsheet. Some twit has
created a shortcut to an excel document on the floppy, and handed him
the floppy. Brilliant !!

Dontcha Just Love Christmas.
