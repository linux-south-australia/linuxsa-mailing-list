Here's one opinion for you:

Slackware.

The installer that comes with Slackware will let you answer 'no' enough 
times to get a working installation on a 120Mb HD.
Your problem is just that the installer won't run on 8Mb memory, so 
you'll have to mount the HD in some other machine while installing.
 From memory, even a very shaved down installation left me with very 
little space left on my HD, as well as very little functionality on the 
portable.
