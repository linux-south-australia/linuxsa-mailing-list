> I just did this myself, for some reason the 3c59x driver under the SuSE 
> 9.0 2.4.x default kernel is broken with my hardware, so I thought I'd 
> give 2.6.0 a go.
> 
> I used the kernel.org 2.6.0 kernel. The compile process is slightly 
> different with 2.6.0 than the 2.4.x branch, but by doing:
> 
> make mrproper
> make xconfig
> (trimming down everything that I don't have, etc)
> make bzImage modules modules_install install
> 
> it seemed to work, except I too had some modules not load (SYM85x SCSI, 
> 3c59x network) and had no console.
> 
> I fixed the no console by removing the vga=  line from lilo.conf;
> and I fixed the module loading by editting /etc/sysconfig/kernel   and 
> adding the missing modules to the line:
> 
> INITRD_MODULES="sym53c8xx ataraid jbd ext3 3c59x"
> 
> then ran mkinitrd as root.
> 
> Everything seemed to work a treat after that. I didn't need to upgrade 
> the modutils or mkinitrd packages but then I installed from a 
> downloaded copy of SuSE 9.0 (only took 2 1/2 days or so to download the 
> 3.8Gb of i386 branch --  cable internet is not bad at all   ;) .
