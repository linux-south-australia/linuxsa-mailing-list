Re: Policy Makers' Positions

davidn@rebel.net.au wrote:

> A group of politicians have kindly agreed to appear on a panel at the
> Linux and Open Source in Government conference, to be held in Adelaide
> in January.  I am canvassing ideas for questions to ask them.  What
> would *you* ask a politician regarding Linux and Open Source if you
> had the opportunity?

What about something like;

  - What is the SA Government doing to support local businesses that
    employ programmers/designers etc to build and support Opensource
    software that is heavily used in Australia Business and Government?

  - What is the SA Government doing to encourage OpenSourceSoftware
    developing business to start in SA or move to SA?

  - What is the SA Government doing to move to publically Open Standards
    from closed proprietry file formats?

  - What research has the SA Government done in the fields of use of
    OpenSourceSoftware in local business and its own departments?

Optional questions, not well worded, but you get the drift;

  - What is the SA Government doing to protect local software
    development, Opensource or otherwise from foriegn buyouts?

  - What is the SA Government doing to protect local investment
    in local companies to stop foriegn patent pirating?
