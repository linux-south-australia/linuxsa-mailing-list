Canon or Nikon and you really can't go wrong - they have the best lenses 
in the business.  Now, do I mention where I work? Hmmmmmmmm - I have a 
couple of Canon cameras at home and have had nothing but joy from these 
devices *grin*.  I found the processing speed of the Canon A5 Zoom a 
little tedious, but things have improved since then (trying to take photos 
of an Air Show with a Canon A5 Zoom is a joke).

Seriously though, Canon or Nikon are pretty much the only choice if 
quality matters because they really do have the best lenses in the 
business.  Get whichever one is cheaper of the same spec in your price 
range... you will need to be a little gentle with the battery covers on 
the Canon's though...
