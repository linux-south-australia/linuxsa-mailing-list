I'd dearly love something to make samba configuration easier...!

If there's one thing that yanks my chain about open source, it's this 
ridiculous mentality that unless you know off the top of your head which 
arcane and ludicrous text file to edit in order to make something 
happen, then you're clearly a newbie and a halfwit.  In fact, my way of 
thinking is if it's as simple as editing a text file to configure 
something, why NOT make a spunky tool with which to do it?  That way 
there's minimal likelihood of mistyping a : with a ; (for instance), or 
a . with a , and therefore hours saved troubleshooting that exact thing, 
which NOBODY can tell me is an uncommon occurrance.

Sure, if you want to do wild, crazy, complicated stuff then directly 
editing the file is fine, but I can't see anything wrong with saving 
yourself some grief on the simple stuff... :)
