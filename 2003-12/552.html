Re: Passwords

At 2003/12/16 09:16+1030  magpie writes:
> 
> The below is cut and pasted from an Linux Today article on security.
> The bit I am interested in is the comment about passwords. How can we make
> our passwords more secure ? or how do we avoid passwords being easily
> cracked/sniffed ?

Don't use passwords. What is becoming rather common with breakins in
the open source world is one project machine being compromised, the
passwords being sniffed and then these being used to login to other
machines, even for other projects.  Although people should be using
different passwords for different machines, in practice people don't,
and its impossible to enforce.

So wherever possible, turn off password logins and only only allow ssh
key based authorisation.
