Well actually, the term "fundametalist" originated as a term of honour -
one who accepted and believed the "fundamentals" of the (Christian) faith,
as documented in the bible, and didn't add extra things to suit their
opinions and lifestyles (cf various extremist cults etc).  Since them the
term has come to mean something somewhat different.

The tone of your email suggested that there was something wrong with being
a "Southern Baptist", and I was suggesting that you are lumping a lot of
people together under the one banner.  Googling for "Southern Baptist"
gave me this <http://www.sbc.net/aboutus/basicbeliefs.asp>; which is a
pretty standard Christian statement of faith.  Whether you agree with it
or not is irrelevant to the discussion, but rather it shows that the
"Southern Baptists" are a pretty much mainline Christian group, albeit
conservative.
