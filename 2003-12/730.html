> the domain master.  Every couple of minutes, the samba box is coming

I had the same problem with my network. It was okay when I used a hub but
the master browser issues struck when I installed a switch, despite
setting smb.conf to ensure my Deb server became master. In the end. I
found the easiest solution was to hack the registry on a WinXP box and
turn off the masterbrowser setting. Do a reg search. I have no more issues
finding things in the workgroup now.
