Apple 15" Albook - Linux X11 Problems

I've sent the email below to the debian PPC lists, but there may be
somebody here that knows what's going on.

Essentially, I've got one of the new 1.25ghz 15" "Albooks" (Aluminium
Powerbooks); OS X works on this fine, but I'm trying to get a Debian
install on it working fine (which was rather interesting, seeing as the
Debian CD's don't support the ATA chipset in it).  This is all working
fine, but X11 refuses to work; the ati or radeon drivers simply will not
find a card in the system, so no go.

newer card; maybe it's a framebuffer issue, maybe it's a PciID issue,
maybe the radeon or ati drivers in XFree86 need to be told wierd and
strange things.
