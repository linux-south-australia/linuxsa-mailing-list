Re: Compaq DLT Drive in Linux

Greetings,
Last I was told by a hardware engineer, all the lights flashing on the right 
means the hook to load the tape hasn't caught. Could be a duff tape, or a 
dying/dead drive, if this is the only case of error, its probably the 
former. Unless you have more problems, then I wouldn't worry, if it 
continues, then the drive has gone bye-bye. 

cya
