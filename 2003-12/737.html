Adam Ingerman wrote:
> Some people like forums, some like mailing lists, but just having both doesn't

I have found, often those that don't have a sensible email
account will use web based forums.

Many mail lists refuse subscriptions from hotmail, yahoo,
aol, etc email accounts.

This is probably a good thing, but a pain in the ass if
one really does want to participate and you don't have
a real email account, yet.

Also when I was travelling, email was not easily accessable
and web based things were far easier. (less hassle anyway.)

You'd be surprised how many Australian web/inernet cafes
*do*not* allow Java Applets in their browsers =(
so no ssh fer you! Typically I used ssh to mutt my mail,
but this is always not possible.
