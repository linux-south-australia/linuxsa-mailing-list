Re: [OT] Advice on Digital Cameras

Damien,

Check out www.dpreview.com for good reviews.

I bought a Canon A40 a year ago, my dad got an A80 recently. Both work
very well and use commodity CompactFlash and AA batteries. I used gPhoto
to download shots and movies off them. Canon appear to be very popular
as indicated on the number of reviews clicked on DPreview.com. I think
Nikon, Fujifilm and Sony are just as good too.

One word of advice, buy a set of high capacity (1800mAh+ for AA)
rechargable batteries and take along a charger.

Cheers,
Joseph Tan
