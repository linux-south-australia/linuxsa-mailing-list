> 'Warning : Can't open initial console'

Is the message really "Warning: unable to open an initial console"?
It makes it much easier to find the source if you tell me the real
message, instead of making one up!


> mine hangs after the message

The message, that is the one that I found, means that /dev/console
couldn't be opened, which is most likely to mean that it's been removed
or it's a character special node for hardware that doesn't exist.
In either case the system doesn't really hang.  The startup messages
aren't likely to appear, but eventually I would expect you'd get your
normal login prompt.  If you have a "console=" option in your boot
command line you might try getting rid of it.
