Authoring PC media to DVD under Linux

To any interested,

I've just cobbled a Howto together to display how to author PC movies
(ie. .mpg, .avi, .asf, .wmv, .mov) to DVD suitable for playback on
standalone DVD players, complete with graphical menus under Linux.

This came about after Santa dropped me off a shiny brand new combo
DVD/CD +/-RW burner for Xmas (Sony DRU-510AK).

The shear frustration of finding either extremely limited or outdated
information for Linux, led me to assemble any relevant tidbits I could
gather. Plus include all of my own notes on the experience.

Howto located here ->
http://mightylegends.zapto.org/dvd/dvdauthor_howto.html

Hope it helps at least someone.
Any feedback/questions welcome.


Regards,
