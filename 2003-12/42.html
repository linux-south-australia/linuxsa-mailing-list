Windows 'Longhorn' WinFS impacts - discussion

Hi 

As most of us are aware M$ will release a new Windows late next year or
early 2005 codename Longhorn.
See http://msdn.microsoft.com/Longhorn/understanding/pillars/default.aspx
for an architectural overview.

I think the biggest impact will be the new WinFS file system, which is
essentially an SQL server as the FS.

Given that Linux can happily dual boot and handle FATxx and NTFS files
systems, I was wondering if anyone has started thinking about how WinFS will
be handled?

I have not been able to find much down and dirty industry comment about Unix
v Longhorn.  Lots of newsy cr*p, but nothing of significant substance yet.

Does anyone know of OSS Longhorn interoperablility projects?
