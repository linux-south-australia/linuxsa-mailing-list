Value for money wise, check out the Canon A70.  It's under $500 at
ht.com.au.  3MP, 3x optical zoom, and more importantly, great quality
(megapixels do not make for a great image, good lenses and CCDs do).  The
A80 is $638 at eyo.com.au if your budget will stretch that far, and apart
from having a higher resolution it also sports a funky flip out LCD screen.

Personally, I just bought a Fuji S5000 for AU$730 from Hong Kong, including
all taxes and postage.  Around $950 when bought locally.
