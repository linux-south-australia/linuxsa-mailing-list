Go with whatever you feel most comfortable with.

I was a die-hard Slackware user until I switched to Gentoo around
Slackware 8.

My personal reason for the switch was that Gentoo has package management
& Slackware doesn't.
By package management, I mean be able to install/uninstall programs AND
be able to handle cross-package dependencies.

Gentoo does take a while to install a fully fledged desktop with all the
bells & whistles (around 3 days on an XP 1800+, 512MB RAM), as it's not
a binary based distro like Slack, Redhat, Mandrake & most others.
But once setup & running, it takes little to no effort to maintain &
upgrade.
