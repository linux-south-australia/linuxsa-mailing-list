Re: [OT] Advice on Digital Cameras

Canon digital cameras.

I've had two so far -> they're great cameras, well supported under linux.  
Canons have great optics 

$500 should get you a Canon A60 (2MP, 3x optical zoom - optical zoom is 
important as "digital zoom" ain't worth a cracker) or for a little more an 
A70 (3.2MP with 3x optical and it'll do 640x480 video clips).

CF cards are cheap - at 2MP 64MB or 128MB should do you nicely.

I went around Italy for my (our? :-) honeymoon last year with a Canon 
IXUS300 (equiv to the A60 but a bit smaller) and it took lovely photos.
