RE: Modems and "Soft" Modems under Linux

In my experience, hard internal modems are near impossible to find anymore.
Hard externals are still everywhere and both internal and external softs are
everywhere. I myself use a hard external modem (Swansmart Turbo II) and it's
had zero trouble being picked up in every linux distro and Unix I've tried
(RH7.3, 8, 9, Mdk 9.1, Gentoo 1.4, Debian Woody, Slack 9.0, 9.1, FreeBSD
5.1, Knoppix 3.2, Vector Linux SOHO 3.2). I did have trouble in Gentoo until
I realised that I'd forgotten to emerge ppp.......... :-D
