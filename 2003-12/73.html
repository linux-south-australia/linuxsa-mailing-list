Ratbags games development - SAMG meeting 7pm, December 5 at the WEA

The multi-million dollar internationally successful Adelaide-based computer
games'
company, RatBag, is a world leader in racing car games. The more than 50
staff
(and now recruiting) at RatBag produce games for Sony Play Station 2, X-Box
and
other computer platforms.

Four of the highly motivated technical staff from RatBag will present
another
talk to the SA Microprocessor Group Inc at the WEA, 223 Angas St Adelaide on
Friday December 5 from 7:00 pm

Confirmed speakers are:
Cam Dunn, CTO
Tony Albrecht (Engine Lead)
Robin Maddock (Lead Programmer)
Amy Watson (Programmer)

Topics covered will be:
Designing content-driven game systems
Tools demonstration
Cross-platform development

Further information is available at
http://samg.yi.org/meeting.php?meetingIndex=34

Any LinuxSA members (or others) interested in attending will be most
welcome.
