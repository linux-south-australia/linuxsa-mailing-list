For some obscure reason (I was not working in the PS at the time) I attended
a briefing session on the whole of SA Government messaging when 'saugov' was
implemented.
The idea at the time was to have a single address for a person working in
the PS.  One of the issues was staff mobility, so no department specific
addresses -  or so the discussion went at the time.
The other reason was the departments are renamed, sliced and diced whenever
there is a cabinet reshuffle or an election.

The only benefit is that it is relatively easy to guess an email address of
PS employees.  The 'saugov' is sucky, but WTH - live with it.
