Re: Unusual entry in Snort log

David Creed wrote:

> Should there be anything else I should look for as being suspicious
> activity?

Tuning IDS's's is a bit of an art.  For your setup, you could probably
ignore most of the destination port 80 traffic as it's going to be
"legitimate" (plus your POP3 and NTP traffic to specific hosts).  If
you're using a smart host for your email, then SMTP traffic to
non-smart-hosts would be a giveaway that something wasn't kosher.  The
same with, say, DNS; if your using your provider's DNS cache, then DNS
traffic to elsewhere would be suspicious.
