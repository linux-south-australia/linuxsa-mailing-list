Re: Policy Makers' Positions

In terms of software development by Government departments/agencies - I'm
in two minds as to whether we need Legislation at all (desktop
applications are a completely separate issue).

I believe there is a significant movement already from those "on the
ground" in government departments/agencies that already rely heavily on
OSS and will continue to do so in the future. Given my first hand
experience, I wonder whether it's more useful to encourage things from the
bottom up, rather than starting at the top.

Damian.
