[Announce]: SA-AUUG, Catchup, drinkup, revive December Meeting (10/12/2003)

Hi All,
    Well December has snuck up on us all again, which means the end is 
neigh! Well at least the end of the year.
So in the good old tradition of AUUG, it's catchup, drink up and revive 
time.

Come join us at Marcellina's for a friendly chat, with beer, wine, pizza 
and many other good foods and beverages. There's no set agenda, no set 
speaker but there'll be the usual friendly people. The mad NetBSD 
hacker, the long bearded FreeBSD kernel guru and many other exciting 
regulars who all just enjoy a chat, a good beer and some good times.

Non members are most welcome, but only SA-AUUG members get the FREE pizza!
