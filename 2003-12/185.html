If it confirms to the 802.11b standard, it sure can.  There used to be an
issue with the hex->string conversion on WEP was slightly different for the
Airports, but that's fixed now.  Airport == 802.11b, Airport Extreme ==
802.11g; they conform to the standards.

> you don't have to buy the apple access point do you?
 
> does an 802.11g card work in a 802.11[a|b] network?

The Apple Airport Extreme cards?  Sure does.  Doubt you'd find many 802.11a
networks around the place in any case, but they work perfectly @ 11mbit on
802.11b.
