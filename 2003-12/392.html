RE: Sendmail blocking TLD

Mark,

> I'm using Sendmail's access.db to block name.TLD but it won't 
> block TLD's.

Access.db does block TLDs :)

Snip from /etc/mail/access
---
localhost                       RELAY
127.0.0.1                       RELAY
au      REJECT
com     REJECT
net     REJECT
---

That will block anything from .au domains, .com domains and .net
domains. I'm assuming that is along the lines of what you want to
achieve. 

After changes don't forget to rebuild the .db and restart sendmail.
