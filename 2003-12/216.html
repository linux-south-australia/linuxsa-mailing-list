Re: We got mentioned in parliament

Innovation is a red herring.

F/OSS is no more about innovation than closed source 
development. Mostly it is about building workable software.

Innovation is an individual pursuit, and could happen on any 
platform and with any license.

A particular company sometimes uses the word 'innovation' where 
other people might use the word 'commercialization', 'refinement 
of an existing idea' or 'theft'.
