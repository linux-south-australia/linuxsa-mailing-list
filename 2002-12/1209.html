No true..  Like I said - I don't have a problem with Linux personally - I
couldn't give a rat - or I would not still use Linux by preference.  I like
Linux because support is there in whatever form - 24x7 - for free - and
commercially too if I need it.  But Windows 'free' forums are more helpful
by and large - at least certainly less hostile to newbies.

People choose Windows because the available (commercial) support is much
more mature and the platform is unified.

Unix people can't even agree on standard email formats let alone a standard
architecture - it's the great undoing of Linux and Window's strength.

On a good note - I see the Linux Standards Base is taking off - that will
see Linux mature in to a sellable item - I hope..  Standardising Linux will
see some major changes in the world's IT economy.  Who knows - the LSB may
even help the global IT industry recover because it will present more
'competition' to the established 'unified' platform that shall remain
nameless..
