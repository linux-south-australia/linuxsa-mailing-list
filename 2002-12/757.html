RE: Intrusion Detection - Snort

Of course it's worth knowing what's happening on your network, sometimes 
just to be able to proactively fix things and/or log things to look at after 
the fact.  Some of these things may just be broken configs on 
machines/routers/etc


It's possible to do the proactive stuff, but it's difficult to tune 
correctly and ultimately may not be worth it (as described before).
