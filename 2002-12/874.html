don't buy them, they're pretty crap.  i'd get APC ones if i we're you.  why
are they crap?  we've had several power failures over time and the APC ones
just tick over.  the staying power of a coupla SOLA ones we had is pathetic.
not to mention they don't last nearly as long.

when you say a large one, i think, if you've got money to spend, that they
have this modular thing which is quite a large box but if a battery goes you
just plug in another without powering down anything.  all hot swappable....
