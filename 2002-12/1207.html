RE: [NEWS] 'Linux is too much like Windows'

feeling a bit 'groggy' today Greg?  :-)

My main aim is to let some fresh air in to this very stale debate.  Windows
is fine - as I have said before - I have been using Linux since I brought my
first 'IBM' type computer about 4 years ago - I took all of 1 month to
switch to Linux - even before I had a clue how Windows works.

My point is - that's my choice.

For those who choose to use Windows - I can see why.  I prefer not to but I
can see where they are coming from.  The standard 'RTFM' Linux community
reply and archaic interfaces can send anyone's head in to a spin..  So my
point is I don't blame people for choosing Windows - at least until Linux
can be used without being told to bugger off and read the 500 page manual
that assumes prior knowledge in computer science.

I don't mind doing that because I have a natural interest in computers - but
customers don't - they just want to pay for something that works..  And
Windows does work..  It just is a hell of a lot more boring than Linux - but
at least it works..

And my MUA rocks - because everyone uses it.  No one uses Mutt - so it's
useless to me.
