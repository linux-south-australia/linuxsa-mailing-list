Temperature

For those in other parts of the Globe we are having a minor heat wave at the 
moment.

I stuck a RadioShack thermometer on this PC last night. It is one that shows 
the ambient temperature as well as the temperature at the end of its sensor. 
I had the sensor just hanging behind the fan on the power supply. 
The room temperature stayed on 30C most of the day and the fan showed 37C. I 
took the cover off the PC thinking it may make a difference, but it is still 
showing 35C.
I will try getting it on to the processor next, but it is only rated to 70C.
