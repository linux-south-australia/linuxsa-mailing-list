RE: [NEWS] 'Linux is too much like Windows'

True - but Linux is the only real competition left in that market segment.
If Linux loses influence - everyone will suffer.  Lack of competition helps
no one - as we all know too well through the adventures of Uncle B....

It will always be around but Linux deserves more than to sit in back rooms
running back end servers.  It's too good for that - but no one can manage to
unify it - and that prevents it from making moves out of the back rooms..

I am hoping this will change - soon..  It's a movement a lot of us would
like to see move further..

People who compare Microsoft and Linux are missing the point... 
Microsoft is a company, Linux is a movement.

It's like comparing McDonalds with the Government. There will always be 
a Government, even if it changes.
