Slightly confused. wget --mirror will get a copy of the remote file, and
ALL of the remote file, but only if it has changed size or date since the
last time you ran the command.

As I understand it, rsync will compare the two files and by an extremely
clever process that only gods like Andrew Tridgell completely understand
(he and Paul Mackerras wrote it), only transmits the parts that have
changed across the network. How well this works for an iso image I cannot
say, it works extremely well for the small files I have used it for sofar.
