[OT?] PSPICE-Spice Model Converter

Not sure quite whether this is OT or not. It's to do with helping me
to use Linux for something I currently use DOS for, so maybe it isn't
totally OT.

I have the circuit simulator PSPICE for DOS. This is based on the
original Spice, which can be freely downloaded from Berkeley and
compiled for Linux.

Unfortunately the PSPICE authors changed the format for transistor
models slightly. Now, there are two formats, each of which has a
couple of parameters that the other one doesn't, and they're not
simply different names for the same thing.

Some semiconductor manufacturers provide models in both formats, but
more provide only PSPICE. It would therefore be extremely useful if
someone could point me at a program, or even just the algorithm, to
convert between the two formats.

Anyone know of any such?
