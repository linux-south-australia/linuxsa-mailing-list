Try changing your root directory to /mytemp5 before running lilo.

"man chroot"

If all your partitions are mounted under /mytemp5, then running 
"chroot /mytemp5 lilo" should do the business.

It's worked for me on a couple of Redhat boxes (7.x) when running in
a rescue environment and having all the system partitions mounted under
a subdirectory.
