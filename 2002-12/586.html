Re: Gimp-print

On Wed, 2002-12-11 at 09:19, Brian Marr wrote:
> Suse 8.0 Cups and Gimp-print
> 
> Gimp print is outputting large stair stepping text. Output from Kword and Open 
> Office is ok. I have looked at the Cups printer drivers and also the print 
> configuration in Gimp - but no luck. Can anyone suggest where I should be 
> looking ?

It's a CR/LF problem. Many printing systems have an automatic
translation that you can turn on. I can't remember specifically how to
turn it on under CUPS/gimpprint, but you could try looking at the config
files (usually under /etc/cups) and the PPD file for your printer
(usually under /etc/cups/ppds).

Hope it helps,
Matt
