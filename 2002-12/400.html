RE: ADSL

I agree totally any broadband plan < 1gb is dangerous and possibly
irresponsible on the ISPS part to even offer it. Justifying it by comparing
to dialup plans is really not correct as people will get thrilled with the
speed and will go further than they normally would in their browsing at
least until they get bored.

I have friends and family who are light users and would enjoy broadband
speeds but they would flip at the excess they would get hit with as they
would do > 500mb in a month and you can't make it more expensive to
increase the allowance because it prices them out of the ball park.

Evan
