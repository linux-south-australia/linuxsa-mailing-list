The settings i used were, Q0 V1 E1 S0=0 &C1 &D2  add this to the
additional options to pass to the modem when asked.

This is without the AT at the start as e-smith puts this in.

This will get you e-smith working just fine.
it took me hours to figure out what e-smith was doing and I only found
it out by looking through the /etc/ppp/peers file.
