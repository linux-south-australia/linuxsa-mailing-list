Re: drwxrwxrwt

Andrew,

> What does the "t" at the end of the permissions  " drwxrwxrwt "  mean?

It sets the sticky bit on the directory. Any files created in that
directory will get the directory owners and group rather than the
default owner and group of the process creating the directory.
