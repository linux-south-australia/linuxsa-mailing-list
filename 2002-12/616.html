Hi Adam,

Check That you are allow to XDMCP to the server in /etc/X11/xdm/Xaccess , I
assume gdmconfig does this.

xdm -nodaemon -debug 100

This will provide some very useful debuging information. If you can't find
your problem email me the output off the list and we'll have a look at it.
