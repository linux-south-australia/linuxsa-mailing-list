Re: Cisco Catalyst 2950-24

Andrew Reid wrote:

>Has anyone had any experience with these beasties? I assume that
>they're pretty rock-solid (if the experience that we've had with our
>existing Cisco infrastructure is anything to go by), but I'd be
>interested in hearing from anyone who particularly dislikes them or
>would recommend against them.

They don't do ISL, which can suck if you have an existing Cisco switched 
network and legacy switches like 1900's. Nowhere is this well documented 
either.

There are also reports of data randomly hopping between switchports when 
it shouldn't on 2950's. ie: it starts behaving like a hub rather than a 
switch! Hopefully the newer software fixes such behaviour.
