Major Topic tangent---> Re: OT Re: Aussie bom weather sat images

I remeber "Reason Magazine" at USC.

I used to work at the Hoose Library of Philosophy there, and the Library
was a subscriber to Reason Magazine.

The magazine was first acquired at the request of Professor John
Hospers, who's claim to fame is having received one Electoral Vote for
President of the United States in 1972.
(http://www.sots.state.ct.us/RegisterManual/SectionVIII/Pol3b.htm)

It is a right beautyful library, and I was lucky enough to work there to
earn some much needed $ to get myself through school. The sayings on the
wall are neat, and there is a full collection of the works of Saint
Augustine under the stained glass windows they show on "the web tour of
the library."
(http://www.usc.edu/isd/locations/ssh/philosophy/phil_libbldg.html)
Although there is a picture of Professor Hospers on the wall, "the web
tour" does not seem to provide it.

And someone that I learned the most from while at USC, Dr. Ross Scimeca
(http://www.usc.edu/isd/locations/ssh/philosophy/ross.jpeg) the librarian.

Oh well, enough daydreaming for me...

James Leone
