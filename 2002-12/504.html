Re: distro to recommend for newbie

Debian has a lot of I18N and L10N features, and IMHO it's not
particularly difficult (actually, because it's got less gloss than say
RedHat, you can see what's going on easier, and hence, learn
quicker... IMHO). If you want a glossy installer, use one of the
debian-based distros... Or, do a Mike O'Connor, and make a magic CDROM
that simply installs a pre-setup debian image... Mike, what was that
tool called that you used to do that?
