Where is it picking up the 66000 from?  ie: what have you tried already?

Normally you need to append something like this to your lilo prompt:

<linuxlabel> ide0=ata66

If it works (check the output on the console when it loads the ide drivers
during bootup), then add an append line to your lilo.conf. (man lilo.conf)
