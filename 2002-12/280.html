Re: [OT] - Suggestions for Enterprise Servers

Andrew,
I may have misled some. We have the latest fandangled SCSI hot swap <insert
cool buzzword here> raid arrays alread all we're really doing is swapping
mobo and cpus

After a lot of feedback and realistically costing it. We decided to go for
2 x Xeons 2.4 ghz on an Intel SHG2 dual processor board
with 2gb of RAM.

I read a review on comparison of the 2.0 MP (multi) and 2.x DP (dual) and
we decided a decision best left till later as the MP outperformed all by
virtue of the 4 cpus and hyperthreading (also on the DP version) But Cost
etc came into it and paying 3.5k for cpu was too much when u add mobo and
ram etc. So we went down to 2.4ghz.

Considering what we run well on ATM we needed something to ease the bumps
on the heavy load which Im sure will be more than covered with the setup.
