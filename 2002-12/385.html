[NEWS] m-Net launches open space wireless LAN

 m.Net's wide-ranging program to energise the mobile
 broadband industry has taken a major step forward this month
 with the roll-out of one of the world's largest open space
 wireless LANs and the launch of Gallery 4.

 A service organisation aimed at assisting companies to
 capitalise on opportunities in the mobile world, Gallery 4
 has quickly captured the imagination of development
 companies, content creators and service providers.

 The organisation has already attracted significant
 international interest, and a senior representative of
 Singapore's Infocomm Development Authority joined us for the
 launch.

 Meanwhile, the roll-out of m.Net's wireless LAN spanning the
 inner city of Adelaide is due for completion early next
 year. The network will provide a valuable addition to
 m.Net's advanced wireless network test bed and will also be
 available for public and commercial use.

 m.Net has been named a finalist in the national iAwards,
 which will be presented in May 2003 by Senator Richard
 Alston, Minister for Communications, Information Technology
 and the Arts.

 For more info, visit www.mnetcorporation.com.au
