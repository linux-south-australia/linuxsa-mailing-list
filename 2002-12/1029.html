LInux Beta Testers Needed

Hi There,

Microbits, a company based in Stepney Adelaide, is developing  a
user-friendly, easy-to-use desktop Linux "distribution" in order to
provide low cost, OEM solutions to some of our customers. Our customers
may include students, home office users and small business operators. 

The Microbits Linux Team are currently focussing on the student user,
however we believe it would be suitable for home office users with
little, to no, modification.

We are inviting people to become part of the beta testing program
for this distribution. This would involve installing the
distribution onto your own computer-having taken a backup of your
existing data or using a blank hard drive-and using the system as you
normally would in addition to attempting to "deliberately break it".

Microbits will provide an open forum for you to discuss the
distribution, submit bug reports and make suggestions. In addition, the
Microbits Linux Team (David Lloyd and Troy Vodopivec) will be available
during business hours to listen to your suggestions or bug reports.

If you are interested, please contact David Lloyd or Troy Vodopivec by
e-mail or by telephone 08 8362 9220. You can also contact David Lloyd on
0402 440993.

We're hoping to be able to distribute the beta by 23 December 2002.
