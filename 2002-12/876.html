>I was wondering if anyone has any experience with

If you want good Linux support, then don't bother IMO.

I've tried at least three different Sola models and was unable
to get any kind of software monitoring/auto-shutdown to work.
Incidentally I still have one at home which I'm planning on 
replacing at some stage....

All our Linux servers run off APCs (more expensive but worth it) and
the support, both vendor and open source community is excellent in
my experience.

There are others around with good Linux software support no doubt...
