These cards have proven to be a bit of a problem in some laptops due
to the large amount of I/O memory they occupy: 52 kB.  My Dell
Inspiron 7500 only has 48 kB space available, so I had to do some
serious hacking on the driver to get it to work.

Apart from that, they're FHSS cards.  802.11 had three specs: DSSS,
FHSS and infrared.  802.11b only has DSSS, so they're not
interoperable with 802.11b (802.11 DSSS is interoperable).
