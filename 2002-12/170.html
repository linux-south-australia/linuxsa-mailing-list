Much as I hate to admit it, Vegemite is just a clone of Marmite.
Follow the link above for relative dates.  Even the name was adapted.
Vegemite doesn't mean anything; Marmite is the French word for "stock
pot".

More interesting is why Vegemite is so popular here.  I used to eat
Marmite when I lived in the UK, but most of the Poms thought I was
crazy.  Even more interesting is that the original extract was
developed in Germany by the chemist Justus von Liebig.  There's not
much trace of his stuff in Germany any more.
