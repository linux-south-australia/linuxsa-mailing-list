got any old textbooks?

As part of ComputerbankSA sending computers to East Timor, we have been 
looking at shipping - and one thing that came up as a way of value-adding was 
the fact that they are DESPERATE for old textbooks...there is an enormous 
lack of such over there, and they would be hugely appreciated..

SO!

Can you help? Contact us if you'd like to supply old books, have a contact at 
a uni or tafe who may help, want to make a donation towards costs of 
freighting, want to subsrcirbe to a journal on behalf of the East Timorese 
people for a year - in short, whatever you can do!

Soon many of you who are students will be looking ruefully at overstocked 
bookcases with slightly out of date books they need to replace....

At this time of year, when so many are looking at the great stuff they got for 
Xmas, lets think about the people with very little....and learning empowers 
people to help themselves!
