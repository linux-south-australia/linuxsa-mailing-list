[INSTALLFEST]: Thanks to All

Hi All,
    As the day has past, the chaos surpassed, the sound of fans now 
faded. I'd like to thank all that came along and helped out in anyway at 
Installfest 2002 Unplugged. The day went very smoothly with only a few 
minor hickups that were quickly resolved. Thanks especially to those 
that spent most the day on the registration desk, the dedicated that 
were in the building not long after 7:30 am and those that stayed back 
to help clean up the venue. Thanks also to the small group that were 
ever dependable at each and every Installfest meeting and also on the 
day. It was another day that will be remembered for years to come.

Finally thanks to our sponsors, Internode, AUUG, aarnet, NetCE, UniSA & 
Coopers. Without their support the event just wouldn't have happened.

Till next year,
    Benjamin Close

PS: If you would like a reminder of the event, there is still a number 
of TShirts available, if you would like one please email me.
