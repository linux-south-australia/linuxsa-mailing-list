Richard,

Few questions:

-Does it look elsewhere for anything like passwords and dns? This could be a
factor. 

-Is it only when X (and kde, gnome...) is running? 

-You could try hdparm to test hard disk speed. 

-Last thing would be to recompiling the kernel. 

It may also be interesting to view "cat /proc/cpuinfo" and "cat
/proc/meminfo". See that it detected your hardware correctly.
