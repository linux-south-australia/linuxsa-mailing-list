Re: monitor problem with SuSe 7.3 on a Sun Ultra Sparc

Old Sparcs are 1152x900x72 (not sure on sync)

Newer ones run at 1024x768 or 1280x1024

You _should_ be able to wire it to the right speed at the openprom level.

Worse comes to worse I have a genuine Sun 17" (Sony) with an adapter to 
convert Sync and adapt to VGA you can have for a reasonable fee.
