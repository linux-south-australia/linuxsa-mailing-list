The page:
http://adsl.internode.on.net/faq/padsl-flatrate.htm#flatrate3

states that 500Mb is designed for light users, who normally use modems,
and are just after faster access speeds with normal modem-type activity.
If you aren't one of those users, I'd expect you to choose the plan the
next level up.

I'm sure that if the average distribution of customers on this plan were
going over their limits frequently, Internode would have reviewed this
plan by now.  The fact that it still exists must mean that the average
customer is getting the full benefits out of their plan.
