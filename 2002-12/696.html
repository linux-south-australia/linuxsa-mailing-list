Linux, FreeBSD, *nix audio packages

Hi,

Just thought you'd be an appropriate bunch of people to tell about this:

The Eastman Computer Music Center (sic) has put together a brilliant
package of audio packages for *nix.

Included are DAP, Wavesurfer, Audacity, Vspace, PD (Max-like tool, by
Miller Pucket, the Max guy), and loads more of analysis, editing and
synthesis tools.

There's a list of packages here:

  http://lulu.esm.rochester.edu/kevine/turnkey/explore.html

and the main site is here:

  http://lulu.esm.rochester.edu/kevine/turnkey/home.html

(FreeBSD users will be pleased to note that most of the packages listed
are already in the ports collection!)


Cheers,

Nick!
