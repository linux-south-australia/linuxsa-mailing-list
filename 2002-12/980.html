contracting methods (possibly OT)

ok possibly OT (but less so than guns and elections), but I
know there's several (at least) people in here who are
contractors, so....

any advice on the best method of doing it?
I know so far:

1) have own company (flexible but high costs, lots of
   paperwork and rapidly losing the tax benefits it once had)
2) go through a "management" company like CXC or Entity
   Solutions etc (a mob of accountants basically)
   (lower cost, if you're not doing a contract no cost to
   you, less paperwork etc - but not particularly flexible
   if you want to branch out into other things as well (like
   selling computers (for eg))
3) go through an agency (like Icon)
   (costs much more than (2) but simpler for you, no flexibility)

what about being a sole trader (like plumbers etc)?

or dealing with tax etc?
