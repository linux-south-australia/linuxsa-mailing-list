> > check if call waiting is on

> You'd probably know if the dropouts were caused by call waiting.  Call
> waiting introduces a beep on the line when a call is waiting, and this
> causes the modem to hang up.  When you hang up while a call is waiting,
> your line immediately starts to ring, so if your *don't* get a ring
> signal immediately after your modem hangs up it's fairly safe to assume
> the dropout was *not* caused by call waiting.
