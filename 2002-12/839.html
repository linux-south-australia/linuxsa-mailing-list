The "sensors" package (comes with SuSE and probably a good many other 
Linux distro's), when properly configured will give you CPU and 
motherboard temperatures (well ok, it'll give you temps if you have 
supported sensor hardware - my Abit BX2.0 m/b does, as does the dual 
celeron board in my server).
