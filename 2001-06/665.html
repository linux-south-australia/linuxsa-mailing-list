Re: Hard Disk Duplicating

David Lloyd wrote:
> If you want a reliable backup of the WHOLE disk, then I don't know
> whether "dd" is the best thing to use. There are a lot of processes that
> may or may not have bits and pieces in a state of flux even when the
> computers doing "nothing"...

DD is fine, just bring it back to init level 1, and then remount root
read only first...

init 1
mount -o remount,ro /
dd if=/dev/hda of=/dev/hdb

or whatever should do it (for identical drives, or if the backup drive
is larger)
