Re: Strange hanging of server

To add another suggestion to the possibilities, I have had a few (three or four)
complete freeze-ups over the past year and all of them have been when X has been
running. I even had RH 7.1  freeze after being up with X for a few days.  No
hard evidence to point to, just observations. You don't leave X running on your
server, do you?

My own workhorse, a server/gateway from e-smith  (very cool) based on RH7.0 and
has been up now since last upgrade for 51 days and is a samba files and print
server with DHCP and NFS running.  It also streams MP3's for the kids, so it is
worked a bit.
