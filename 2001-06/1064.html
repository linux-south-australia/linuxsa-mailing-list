 > I don't actually have any broadband at home, and cannot get ADSL in

surprised:

PROSPECT        08 82691000-82697999   
PROSPECT        08 83420000-83426999  
PROSPECT        08 83429000-83429999 
PROSPECT        08 83441000-83449999

Note that that's from a list from last month;  there might be more

Check the Telstra ADSL qualification page:
http://www.telstra.com.au/bigpond/adslsq/

provider, not just Telstra.

So use a different provider.  Be aware that in almost every conceivable
aspect of telecommunications, Telstra probably have the worst deal on the
market.  ADSL is absolutely no exception.

 > Does anyone think this is unfair as well?
 > Does anyone have the TIO/ACCC's email addy handy, because I'd like to post

You won't get anywhere with that.  Telstra are required to offer services
on terms which are consistent with their terms and conditions, not on terms

they stick to them religiously the TIO won't give a damn.
