Re: Strange hanging of server

This probably isn't your problem, but I once saw a very similar problem.
The network was on 10mbit co-ax, and it turned out to be a dodgy
connector. This was really weird because only some protocols were
effected. You think it'd be all or nothing, but the co-ax must have been
so on the boundry of failing that the actual data streams made a
difference!. In the end, one of the T pieces was replaced and all was
well! Still, I the chances of this scenareo would be one in a million,
I'd think!
