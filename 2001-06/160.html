Exim and LDAP

Hi all,

I've just started looking into exim as a mailer and it seems pretty
nice. Has anyone out there used exim and LDAP? In particular, I would
like mail routing decisions to be made based on the contents of an LDAP
directory. For example:

*	DNS contains one MX record
*	mail arrives on this machine, which forwards the mail onto one of
perhaps many "internal" mail servers
*	the server that the mail lands on depends on the value associated with
a certain attribute (or attributes) in their LDAP entry

Has anyone done anything like this? If so, what were your experiences?
