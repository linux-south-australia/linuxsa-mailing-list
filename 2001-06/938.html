Re: LOG UTILITIES

David Lloyd wrote:

> There's an environment variable under "sh" in FreeBSD 4.2 and probably
> in bash called "PAGER".

I'm surprised the other David didn't pick you up on this; the environment
variables exists as part of the process space (surprisingly enough, in
the environment), and therefore are under "everything", with the
possible exception of init, which would be the only process that doesn't
inherit its environmentals from its parent ('cause it doesn't have one).
"PAGER" is one of those "by convention" variables where a process can
either deem to use it or not; it's their choice.
