Re: how to install man pages

Ben Williams wrote:
> i have just compiled and installed netpbm. i have all its man pages
> installed under /usr/local/netpbm/man - how do i get these to show up if i
> do a "man giftopnm" (giftopnm being one of the components with a man page)
> for instance? is it just a matter of copying the files under the appropriate
> dir under /usr/man ? or do in need to modify an config files or do some
> other magic?

Update your MANPATH variable to include that directory, even better update 
MANPATH for everyone in /etc/bashrc or whatever, or move the man files.  If 
you're going to move, move then to /usr/local/man for software you install and 
leave /usr/man for distribution-installed software's man pages.
