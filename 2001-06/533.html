Depends on your ISP's connectivity into AARNet.  The
mirror is attached with a GbE interface to a local GbE
network so you should be getting disk I/O rates in
the ACT and from TransACT.  We encourgage other ISPs
to run GbE into our ACT switch but haven't had many
takers to date.

Further afield you should get at on average about 100Mbps
with a minimum of 12Mbps.

All this should improve significantly throughout the
remainder of the year, as our move to carrier status
should allow us to fully cooperate in local peering
efforts such as SAIX.
