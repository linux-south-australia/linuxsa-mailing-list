NetCraft Australia is a wholly owned South Australian company who
specialise in developing a variety of key solutions in the area of
internetworking and data communications based on the Linux operating
system and Cisco hardware. We are a growing and dynamic organisation
with a strong customer focus and a commitment to quality service. We
pride ourselves on being a leading provider of Linux based business
solutions.

We are seeking suitably qualified and experienced systems engineers to
extend our service capabilities. A key requirement of the role is the

network security.

administration, internetworking protocols and a strong LAN and WAN
background. Experience with Cisco switches and routers is highly
desirable.

You will have sound problem solving skills, the ability to work within
a team and with clients. In addition you will have a commitment to
deliver, be well-organised and able to perform under pressure.

Appropriate tertiary qualifications and/or experience.
