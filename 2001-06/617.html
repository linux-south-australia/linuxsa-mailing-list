Re: Putty dropping connection

I recommend you consider running screen as well. My vote for most useful full screen console app after vim and mc.

start session with just 
$ screen
ctrl-a d to disconnect from session

to reconnect to session
$ screen -r 

to view sessions
$ screen -list

Then you can detach and reattach as you like - great stuff while you are waiting for compiles or database imports to run. And more secure than leaving putty open on your workstation while at lunch.
