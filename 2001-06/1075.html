Re: International Keyboards

Hi There,
    This may not help, but try your nearest Foreign Language
bookshop, they often sell books and software using these
foreign languages. If they don't have one, they may be able
to get one for you. Failing that, try your local foreign
chamber of commerce (these guys are trying to promote
business and trade between whichever countries they
represent). Finally, try the local Embassy or Consulate who
often also have a trade section or list of contacts that can
help you. If all else fails, let your fingers do the walking
on the net, find a Spanish computer shop in the Yellow
Pages, hope they speak English and ring them up and mail
order one. Try Yahoo or another jump off site as often they
now have links to local sites ie. yahoo.com.au. Hope this
helps.
    Cheers!
        Andrew.
