Its probably an intel pro 100/ve (similar name, totally different chip..
grrrr). They are supported in newer kernels. I have put a netcraft
version of RH6.2 on one of these (updated to kernel 2.2.17 out the box)
and it didnt find it, but a further update to 2.2.19 and it was fine (of
course 2.4.x is fine as well).
