Re: [OT] Telstra ADSL 'Unlimited' Introduces Download Limit

Stephen White <spwhite@chariot.net.au> wrote:
> The cost of the cable remains constant whether it is used or not.
> Charging by use doesn't match the physical reality of the costs involved.

The download capacity of a cable is constant whether you have one
subscriber or a lot.  Unlimited-use charging regimes attract users who
download huge volumes of data, which reduces the total number of people
that you can spread the costs over.  Charging by use makes obvious sense:
it permits lower subscription costs.
