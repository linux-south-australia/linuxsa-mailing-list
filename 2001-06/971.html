On 27 Jun 2001 13:05:05 +1000, Grant wrote:

> Or the list could be setup to bounce any emails that are not in plain
> text.

Which conveniently ducks around the issue -- providing help and support
to "newbies" who are trying to "get into" Linux. The solution isn't to
turn our backs and say "Get stuffed, you use HTML Mail".

I don't like HTML Mail, but it's much better to do what other prominent
members of this list do -- let them know that we don't like HTML mail
and answer the problem.

If it's really an issue, we should be putting together something as per
FreeBSD-questions (have I said that already?)

flamed.

Is it that much of an issue that we need to write something up? It'd be
good from one point of view, but I question the necessity -- I think the
majority of people have been handling the situation admirably.
