Re: [OT] Telstra ADSL 'Unlimited' Introduces Download Limit

>My opinion on this is that the most rational way to organise
>limits without artifical month-edges is to use a leaky bucket
>approach.

Just to add another spin on this, Adam Internet do byte accounting on a
weekly resolution (so you only have to wait until sunday if you download
something big), and don't cut off anyone's access upon reaching the quota
either. Customers are offered the opportunity to be charged for additional
downloads, or have continue to have unrestricted access in 15 minute
sessions.

We find this to be the fairest approach to monthly untimed style accounts.
