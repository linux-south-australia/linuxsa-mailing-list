Re: Hard Disk Duplicating

Note that using bs=32k or whatever your harddisks optimum
multiple-block-read size is, will result in higher performance.

hdparm -m32 /dev/hda
hdparm -m32 /dev/hdc

dd if=/dev/hda of=/dev/hdc bs=32k
