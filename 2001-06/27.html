LDAP Authentication with dot usernames

Just playing around with username conventions, and I suddenly realised
that Novell, in all its greatness and power doesn't seem to support dots
in the usernames.

Now, I know that NDS is vaugly related to LDAP, so I thought I'd ask if
LDAP supports dots in the usernames.

For example, my username (andrew.reid) would be completely rejected by
Novell, I assume for the reason that they use dots to denote OUs:

"andrew.Users.Glenunga"

So obviously

"andrew.reid.Users.Glenunga"

would denote another object called "reid" in the tree.

Does the LDAP Authentication play nice with dot-usernames? I don't like
our current username convention, and I'm considering a
firstname.lastname one.

TIA!
