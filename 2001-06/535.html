Hi, 
I use Internode's email to SMS gateway to handle sending SMS messages to my
phone, and I use Netsaint for network services monitoring.  Netsaint is
pretty simple to configure (even for a relative newbie like myself), and the
email to SMS gateway makes notifications dead simple (netsaint wants to
email administrators by default).  
One thing to watch, Internode charge on a "per message" basis, and when
things go wrong Netsaint can send a lot of messages, but you can cut down
the number with configuration options.
Jim.
