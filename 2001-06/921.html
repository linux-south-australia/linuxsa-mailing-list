He hasn't got the raid device (/dev/md0) up yet - the ext2 fs is built on
top of that, not underneath.

Its a RAID superblock he needs, not an ext2 one.

ANdrew - what version of raid are you running? the 0.3x-ish era, 0.5x-ish
era or 0.9x-ish?
