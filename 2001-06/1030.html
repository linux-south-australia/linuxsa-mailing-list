Re: RedHat

On 28 Jun 2001 11:11:53 +0930, sammiej@ozemail.com.au wrote:

> Hi my name is Vern Wingard and i have just installed RedHat 7.1 on my
> hard drive which i partitioned and the file system is FAT32. I was 
> wondering how i could format my drive running RedHat to a Linux format ?

I'm assuming that you've already installed, so it's probably not an
option to run 'mkfs.ext2 /dev/hda[1-16]'

What you're probably wanting to do is reinstall your system, creating
the root partition as ext2.

> At the moment my other drive is running Win98. Can i have a dual boot 
> system?

Most definately. The LInux boot LOader does this for you (after a bit of
configuration). http://www.linuxdoc.org/HOWTO/mini/Linux+Win95/ may be
of some use to you.
