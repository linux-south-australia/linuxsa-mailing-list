James Mclean [mailto:james@adam.com.au] wrote:
> I need to perform a search on a database, however I only want to
> search by the
> first letter of a field.


i'm sure you'll get about a hundred replies to this, but i want to anyway
;-)

leave off the first % - i.e. do:

$sql = "SELECT * FROM artists WHERE artist LIKE '$artist%' ORDER BY artist
LIMIT 0,30";

the % is basically a wildcard. is you leave it off the start, it will find
things that have $artist at the start then anything after that.

hope that helps :)
