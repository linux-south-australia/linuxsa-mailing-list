I found to get my SCSI card of similar modea (19840 I think from memory),
I had to compile the SCSI support into the Kernel. It wouldn't work as a
modual. This was on a RH box booting from IDE.

For RH boxes booting from SCSI you need mkinitrd and need to reference it
in the lilo.conf. I've no idea whether its neccessary for debian.
