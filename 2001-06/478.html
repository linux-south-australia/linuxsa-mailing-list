Harvey,

The way you rectify the problem is by searching through the kernel source 
looking for ENOBUFS, which helps you understand what the cause is.  I did
a quick and incomplete search of /usr/src/linux/net/ipv4 and came up with
one likely contender, in tcp_ipv4.c's tcp_v4_connect().  That error occurs
when sock_wmalloc() returns null.  It's an exercise for the reader to work
out what that means!
