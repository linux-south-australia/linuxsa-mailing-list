RE: [OT] Telstra ADSL 'Unlimited' Introduces Download Limit

Anybody who still thinks that ISP can offer unlimited accounts should
take note of this.

> ... well at least we enjoyed downloading well over 100 GIG over the
> three months we've been with Telstra ;-)

Even clueful people will cheerfully act in a way that would send their
ISP broke, and then brag about it.  You can't provision a network with
enough bandwidth to give good performance if people are going to abuse
it--and they will.  If you want to give your customers a fast experience
you have to make them pay for it in proportion to how much they use.
Even if you're prepared to give a slow experience, and that's a viable
business model, you still need to find a way to make sure that a minority
don't ruin it for everybody else; and that means limits of one sort or
another.
