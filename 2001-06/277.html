Hi Adam,

There is a lot to be said for taking your first steps into linux.  First,
congratulations.

Second: its similar, but also very different.

Where to start:  Dymocks Book Store in Rundle Mall (adelaide of course) have a
growing number of linux programming books:

    Matthew, Neil & Stones, Richard, Beginning Linux Programming, WROX Press
Nov 1999, ISBN1-861002-97-1
    Wall, Kurt, Linux Programming by Example, QUE 2000, ISBN 0-7897-2215
    Harlow, Eric, Developing Linux Applications with GTK+ and GDK, New Riders
1999, ISBN 0-7357-0021.

All are quite good and there are more titles available.
