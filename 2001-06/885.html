> I like less a _lot_ more than more, and considered making an alias so `more`
> ran `less`... However, less often doesn't exist on the systems I use. Also,

There's an environment variable under "sh" in FreeBSD 4.2 and probably
in bash called "PAGER". You set that to "less" or "more" or something
similar. Setting that leaves "more" alone allowing you to access it if
you ever need to...

(For example, less can be an absolute pain if you run a command that
returns no value).
