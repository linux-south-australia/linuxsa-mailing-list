Re: Network printing on the Adelaide Uni system

On Fri, 15 Jun 2001, I. T. Riley wrote:

> I am attempting shake of the Microsoft shackles but am not yet able to
> print on the network (Novell Netware) printers on the Adelaide Uni
> system.  Without access to printing facilities, becoming a full time
> Linux user remains a vain hope.  I am running Progeny Debian and am
> just a novice Linux user.  I would appreciate any helpful suggestions
> from those familiar with our situation.  Thanks, Ian

The best solution from many points of view is to get the Netware admins to
enable the Unix standard printing protocol lpd (or the Internet Printing
Protocol, if Netware is modern enough to do it.) That way all kinds of of
systems can print without knowing anything about Novell. But they probably
won't, citing authentication or other reasons.

So the next best thing is to read the Netware bit of the Netware
Administrators Guide (NAG version 2) available at linux.senet.com.au or
other places as recently discussed. The ncpfs package can do all you want
and more. You existing Novell logins should just work.

If you want a super-kosher Netware environment, use Caldera Linux which
has official Netware code in it.
