Re: Secure Website

Hiya,

I would strongly suggest speaking to the certificate vendor (eg verisign,
thwawte, whoever).

They tend to have reasoanble FAQs/online odcs.

I dont think you should be able to(trivially) change the details on the
cerificate yourself(presumably including date), as this would sort of
make the idea of the third party verification authrority redundant.

Are you sure that it is the certificate you were issued that has expired?

It may be the vendor certificate supplied with your browser is out of
date, particularly if it is an older browser - but possibly so even with
a recent browser.
