Brand awareness

July's issue of Wired shows the results of a servey of Tech brand
awareness.  Linux rates in the top twenty brands in nine of the ten
countries listed.  It's number one in France and Germany, number three
in Spain, number five in China, number six in Japan, and number ten
in Brazil.  The only country listed that didn't include Linux in the
top twenty brands was United States.  It beat Microsoft in five of the
ten countries, and beat Windows in six of them.

Survey source: Landor Associates 2001 Global ImagePower Survey.
