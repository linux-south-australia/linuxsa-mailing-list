You can configure the RS-232 powerfail lines in two ways: as
RS-232 serial connections that software than uses to query
the UPS, or as wires, with lines like DCD and DSR being
used to communicate the UPS status.

For multiple operating system support (Unix, NT, NetWare)
you are best off configuring the serial port in the
'dumb wire' mode and treating the distribution of those
wires as an electronics exercise.

Then you can't have the software on Box A failing
causing boxes B and C not to shut down.
