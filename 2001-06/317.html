[INVITATION] AUUG - SA Meeting

INVITATION
----------

The local chapter of AUUG-SA is meeting this Wednesday.

DATE:        Wednesday, 15 June 2001
TIME:        7:00 PM
PLACE:       Marcellina's Restaurant (Hindley Street)

This is an excellent chance to get together over dinner and light
discussion and meet fellow unix users. We have members who use all types
of "unix like" operating systems, some of which are Linux, FreeBSD,
OpenBSD and the commercial Unixes such as Solaris and HP-UX.

We generally have dinner at around 7:30 PM and a short technical
presentation after that.

Everyone is welcome to come along; you do not need to be a member of
AUUG to join us!

David Lloyd
(AUUG - SA Chapter Secretary)
