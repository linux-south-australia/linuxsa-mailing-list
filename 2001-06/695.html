Re: spaces in filenames

Richard Sharpe wrote:

> OK, I have spent a tough few days teaching Linux to people and my brain is
> starting to fade ... hit me with an example if IFS where they make a
> difference.

Given:

  #!/bin/sh

  t() {
      echo "Arg count:" $#
      COUNT=1
      for i; do
          echo "$COUNT:" "'"$i"'"
          COUNT=$(expr $COUNT + 1)
      done
      echo
  }

  t $*
  t "$*"
  t $@
  t "$@"

Produces:

  [dt@slave dt]$ ./test1 "hello there" Richard
  Arg count: 3
  1: 'hello'
  2: 'there'
  3: 'Richard'

  Arg count: 1
  1: 'hello there Richard'

  Arg count: 3
  1: 'hello'
  2: 'there'
  3: 'Richard'

  Arg count: 2
  1: 'hello there'
  2: 'Richard'


The last one being the correct result, as it's preserved the "argument
grouping" correctly.  Any better?
