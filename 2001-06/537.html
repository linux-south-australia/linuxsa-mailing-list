SAGE-AU conference

Hi all,

SAGE-AU is coming to town.  Here's some info...

Forwarded message:

> The conference is the ninth Annual Conference of the System
> Administrator's Guild (SAGE-AU), a professional body representing
> system administrator and their profession in Australia, and affiliated
> with other SAGE organisations internationally.  Current membership
> stands in excess of 700 members, with regional chapters in all
> Australian states
> 
> This year's conference has a number of topic areas covered, many
> directly related to Linux administration.  The conference is being held
> at the Grosvenor Vista hotel in Adelaide, and features a number of
> well-known international speakers, including Randal Schwartz ("Learning
> Perl"), Monty Widenius (MySQL RDBMS creator), Elizabeth Zwicky
> ("Building Internet Firewalls", founding President SAGE-US) and Geoff
> Halprin (eSmith, Vice-President SAGE-US).  This year's conference is
> structured as three days of tutorials, followed by a two day conference
> program.  There is a wide cross-section of presentations ranging from
> tecnical "nuts-and-bolts" sessions, through to relevant business issues
> such as risk management.
> 
> The conference web site can be found at 
>               http://www.sage-au.org.au/conf
> 
> 
> If there is any other information you need, please feel free to contact
> me!
