Re: installing scsi on freebsd

On Thu, Apr 10, 2003 at 12:10:03AM +0930, Daniel Moore said:
>    Hmmm I've not been able to find any information on how to install 
> new hardware after the initial os install process and I want to install 
> a scsi card and subsequently an AIT tape device to a freebsd system. Can 
> anyone point me in the direction of some resources or give me a breif 
> walkthrough? Is it as simple as just putting in the hardware and rebooting?

Assuming your kernel has the right driver support, yes, it's usually as
simple as installing and booting.

Check your /usr/src/sys/i386/conf/? kernel config file.

NB: building a FreeBSD kernel is 1,000 times easier and more reliable than
building a Linux kernel.
