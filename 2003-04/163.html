Re: Where to get redhat 9 and mandrake 9.1

Adam,

Don't take this as picking on you.

Mandrake is on the brink of bankruptcy.  Buy an official Mandrake version and 
support the company that puts the distro together and funds the development of 
the software that goes in.

Red Hat turned un-profitable this past quarter, after being profitable for a 
while.  They've also been the most profitable linux distributor.  It's the same 
thing, buy an official version and support the company that puts a lot back 
into the community.  If they do well, more money gets channelled into open 
source software development.

Everything Linux (http://www.everythinglinux.com.au) and Linux Systems Lab 
(http://www.lsl.com.au) here in Australia both sell official versions of both 
Red Hat and Mandrake.

I've got Red Hat 9 on pre-order now from one of them :-)
