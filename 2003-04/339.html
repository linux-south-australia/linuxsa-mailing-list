> Will Linux become the OS standard?

Of course not.

> What will that mean for Microsoft, Windows, and Office?

Who cares?  Perhaps Office would be made available under Linux.  I still
wouldn't be able to run it on my platform of choice.

If you think you can satisfy your computing needs without recourse to
proprietary software then you just aren't expecting enough from your
computers.  Somebody, somewhere, has an application that you'd find
quite useful, and you can only get it on their choice of platforms.

hardware and operating system, there will always be the haves and the
have-nots.  Windows is closer to that common platform than anything
else available today.
