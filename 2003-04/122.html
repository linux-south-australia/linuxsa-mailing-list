> This is quite normal & is not specific to switching to fullscreen.
> Rather, the CD burning process takes precedence over all other CPU
> intense activities. Hence the queuing.
> This is needed to ensure the fifo buffer is kept topped up with data
> while the burning process is happening, & so ensure (to a certain
> extent) that you won't have a failed burn.
>
> Regards,
> Rick
