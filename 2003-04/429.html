Re: [NEWS] "Solaris on course to merge with Linux"

Who wants to merge?

We have used Solaris for many years, however we have had great trouble
trying to run it on Intel hardware. We can take a vanilla PC and put
linux on and it runs reliably, but the same machine will be flaky with
Solaris X86. Sun will sell us a guaranteed 1.4Ghz machine for circa
$4000 but won't sell us an x86 machine that goes any faster. They
will, however, sell us a dual 1.4Ghz machine.

My guess is that Sun know that Solaris has fundamental problems with x86
hardware.
