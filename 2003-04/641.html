Re: replacement UPS batteries

Steve,

The best option is to remove the batteries from the UPS as a pack and take
it to a company called Power Cell at 228 Halifax St. and ask them to make
you a replacement. Powercell import the cells themselves and also have the
necessary equipment to make up the packs as required. They normally deal
with industry but are happy to do walk ins.

Good Luck
