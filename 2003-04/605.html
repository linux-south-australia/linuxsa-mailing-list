RE: uptime = 1 day, Grrrrr....

Personally, I've never quite understood the obsession with high uptimes.
I understand when people have needs that require high availability, or
lots of uptime, but, isn't the test for satisfying that usually a
percentage figure, rather than a number of days figure? -- ie, isn't it
more important to have (say) a 99.999% uptime (which is about 5 mins per
year) than to have 500+ days uptime -- which could be followed by a four
day rebuild, really...
