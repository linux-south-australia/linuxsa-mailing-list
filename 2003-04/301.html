Just a short word of caution, the VIA C3 is excellent for battery life and
low heat production but is pretty poor in terms of performance.  Toms
Hardware had an amusing benchmark a couple of years ago where a Celeron 500
would easily beat a 1Ghz+ VIA C3 in business benchmarks, and the gulf was
far wider when it came to anything that uses the FPU.

I had a C3 866 and it was great because I could make a fanless box (yes, it
runs that cool) but the lack of performance became annoying.
