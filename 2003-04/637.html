> I won't be going. I don't feel the same this year Ian.

Brian,

Are you referring to the recent events in Iraq? If so, I suspect that you
may have the purpose of ANZAC day confused. The opportunity to acknowledge
and thank (often in person) the people who acted so selflessly in previous
theatres of war, has nothing whatsoever to do with whether you agree with
war or not.

I encourage anyone who hasn't attended a dawn service and the ANZAC day
march to do so this Friday. Go down to the Torrens parade ground, have a can
of beer with a veteran, ask him or her what the war was like, and what they
did. Take it as an opportunity to listen and learn from the past while you
still can.

After all, ANZAC day is there, lest we forget.

Learn from the past.
