Re: [OT] ANZAC Day, April 25th, Lest We Forget

With apologies to our overseas friends.

To me Anzac Day made me feel - well "Australian". Going to dawn services 
engendered a feeling of being Australian in me. Maybe the loss brought us 
together in mourning and grief. This was followed by a celebration of this 
country. 
That feeling seems to have vanished this year. Some of the things that I 
valued about Australia have in recent times been lost, trodden on, and walked 
over.

Brian Marr
