[OT] The Great Debate: Education vs business expectations

SA AIIA Business and Education Breakfast Forum
 - Come and hear "The Great Debate" - The debate that will
 illuminate, on the one hand, the difficulties facing the
 education system in meeting the demanding and changing needs
 of high growth, innovative IT businesses and on the other,
 what are businesses' expectations of the education and
 training system and what is missing.
 - Participate in a forum discussion on the above topic
 following a 30 minute semi-formal debate with a team from
 the Education Sector and a team from the Business sector to
 set the scene.
 - Take the opportunity to network with your ICT and
 education industry colleagues before (with a light Danish
 breakfast) and after over a coffee or juice.

Date: Wednesday, 30th April 2003

 Time: 7.30am - 9.30am (Breakfast from 7.30am to 8.00am in
 the main atrium, Debate in Lecture Theatre, N210, upstairs
 from the atrium)

Venue: Adelaide Institute of TAFE, Currie Street, Adelaide
Cost: $20 for Members and $30 for non-Members

Register: Online at 
<http://www.aiia.com.au/cgi-bin/event_registration_form.cgi?183
 or call CEC on 02 6260 5470

Who Should Attend?
 Business leaders, training managers, IT managers,
 educationalists, training providers, education planners and
 advisors, recruitment advisors, and anyone interested in
 networking with the business and educational leaders and

 The Great Debate - "Is the Current Education System Capable
 of Meeting Industry's Needs for IT Graduates?"
