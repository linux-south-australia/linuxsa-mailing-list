[OT] MS Office 2K keeps de-registering itself

It is being reported

http://slashdot.org/articles/03/04/17/1344202.shtml?tid=109&tid=128

http://www.theregister.co.uk/content/4/30323.html

 that starting Tuesday April 15th, many copies of the
 Enterprise edition of Office 2000 have been demanding to be
 reRegistered every time that Office is restarted.  So far,
 Microsoft has apparently not been able to offer a fix other
 than a complicated procedure that gives you 50 additional
 boots before Office turns itself off or setting the clock
 back two years -- which isn't acceptable in many corporate
 environments.

 This is largely affecting corporate users often with tens of
 thousands of PCs each affected and is apparently due to a
 bug, not a virus.  How would you like to be CIO of a company
 with maybe 80000 potentially inoperative copies of Office
 2000 on your hands?
