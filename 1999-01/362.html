Re: dosemu

Robert Mibus wrote:
> 
> anyone know how to mount a dosemu image?
> ive tried a few times but can't get it to go.
> i want to copy files to it as it can't see my cd or floppy or hdd.

Try:

mount -o loop -t <msdos> /tmp/imagefile /mnt/mountpoint

Should do the trick, providing that you have the loopback device
compiled into the kernel.
