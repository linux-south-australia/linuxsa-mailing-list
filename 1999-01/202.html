A MATTER OF TIME

>> So my question to all the Linux gurus out there is, How can I 
>> configure my box so it automatically does the necessary time and 
>> timezone setting changes at the appropriate times of year.

I can't help laughing. I have three watches and all manner time devices 
in this house. My wife has more! On the appointed day I just walk around 
and change the time. Just another chore twice a year on a boring Sunday 
morning. Changing my PC takes about as much time as it did to write this 
reply. Factoid: There are five time zones in Australia. Only two states 
in the US do not observe DST -- Indiana and Arizona. There is a cesium 
clock in Adelaide which on dialup sends the time to the millisecond in 
ASCII. But you may as well dial the naval observatory in Washington for 
the cost of a 12-second call. Or listen to radio. The ABC and BBC have 
it down to the micro-second. I think today is Sunday but it really 
doesn't matter.
