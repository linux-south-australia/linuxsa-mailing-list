Re: no software reboot?

Darryl (and others),

I have a 386 here which does the same thing.  I fixed it by specifying
an extra parameter in lilo.conf to tell the kernel which reboot method
to try with my motherboard.  Add one of the following to your lilo.conf

append="reboot=bios"
append="reboot=cold"
append="reboot=bios,cold"    <--- this is what I use

For further info, see /usr/src/linux/arch/i386/kernel/process.c
and grep for "reboot".
