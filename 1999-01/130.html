Re: easy modem question

Alan,

> The problem is [...] that the communication seems to be buffered for
> 7 seconds out of every 8, adn then all 7 seconds worth are let
> through in just 1 second.

Out of *every* 8?  Or is it that this problem occurs apparently at random?
You would expect to see this when the telephone line is noisy.  Modems
try hard to recover, and while they are doing so they stop sending.  The
data which you send to the modem is buffered (up to a point), and then
when the modems recover from the line glitch they send what they have.

I agree that it could be the ppp module, and I also would not be surprised
if it turned out thus.  None the less:

1. Have you had your line checked?
2. Do you dial more than one destination?
3. If 2 is yes, does it happen to all destinations?
