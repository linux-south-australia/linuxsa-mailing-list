Re: Connection Speed

> Is there a way I can interrogate the ppp connection to determine the
> connection speed??

No, not once the connection is complete.  This is the simple answer.  The
more complicated answer is, yes, a program could send the escape sequence
to your modem (+++ by default), and then put it back on-line (ATO), or
request a retrain (ATO1), and capture the connect speed--the modem will
say, "CONNECT 56000" (if you are really lucky :-).  However the escape
sequence is probably changed to something untypeable by the ppp connect
script; and even if it isn't, it probably would be much the same as when
you first dialed, so why not just capture the information then?

Look at your chat script (or whatever you use to dial), and make it
display the full connect message.  (After seeing "CONNECT", send nothing
and then look for ^M)
