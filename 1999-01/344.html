>Oh, yea, there is one other thing before I go. How do you add items to the

I had to do that to make some icons/buttons for Netscape and Realplayer.

Edit the "/~/.fvwm2rc95" file and it reads much like a really 
simple sort of ".ini" file, (you can set what is on the "buttons" bar too).
I just hashed together the entries I needed from all the other ones 
in there. 

There's probably a better method somewhere, but that worked so I
didn't bother finding it (I plan to try out KDE before my Xwin gets
anymore complex anyway).
