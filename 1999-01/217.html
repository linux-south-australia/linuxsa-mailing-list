Outside your local domain?  Do you mean you can't connect to the proxy
server from outside your local domain, or you can't access sites
(through the proxy server) that are outside your domain?

A common problem with the default squid configuration is the initial
access controls.  By default, no-one has access to your proxy server.

If you want to let everyone in the world access your proxy server
(probably not a good idea) you can use:
http_access allow all

A better idea would be:
acl mydomain src 192.168.1.0/255.255.255.0
http_access allow mydomain
http_access deny all

(replace 192.168.1.0/255.255.255.0 with [your ips]/[your netmask])
