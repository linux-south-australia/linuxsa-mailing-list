Looks like you haven't recompiled the new kernel and configured it to
your system. That would probably explain why PPP doesn't work, as it
is usually compiled as a module, which has (most likely) not been
installed or updated by RPM.

Either that, or you have not updated /etc/lilo.conf to reflect the
new kernel image in /boot, and then run lilo to update the boot
records of your machine. This would cause it to still be loading the
old kernel, and the old kernel will not find the newer modules
installed by the new packages.
