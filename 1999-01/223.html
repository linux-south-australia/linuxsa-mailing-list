Re: ifup and ifdown

> I've been using ifup to connect to my ISP and ifdown to disconnect. Does
> anybody know if there is a verbose option, or any way that I can see what's
> happening when ifup runs. I can't find a man page or any form of help on my
> system. 

Turn on 'Debug connection' in netcfg, then run
'tail -f /var/log/messages' as root before trying to connect.
