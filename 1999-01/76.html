Re: xterm colors

Ben Kramer wrote:

 > where can I find a list of valid colors for seting in my
 > xterm ?

/usr/X11R6/lib/X11/rgb.txt

Or you can specify them as "#123456" to give intensity 0x12 to red,
0x34 to green and 0x56 to blue.  Remember that some shells will require
quote marks to stop the # from being seen as a comment.
