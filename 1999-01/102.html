Re: delay in ftp & telent

You need to set up names for your other machines, ie the windows machines,
in your /etc/hosts file. what it is probably trying to do is look up their
host names and when it doesn't find it then it will go on.

the entries should look something like:

====================================

#
#/etc/hosts
127.0.0.1            localhost@localdomain    localhost
192.168.3.199    spyro
192.168.3.200    windows1
192.168.3.201    windows2

====================================

Doesn't matter what the names are, as long as they have something.

As for the FTP problem, you will have to send the log file. If you use
CuteFTP you can copy the log to the clipboard then past it in to an email.
