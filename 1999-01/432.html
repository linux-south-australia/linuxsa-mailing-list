Re: firewall initialisation

I use /etc/rc.d/rc.local myself.
For a more 'professional' way you could put the ipfwadm commands into
your own shell script in /etc/rc.d/init.d/my_ipforwarding
Then, add links to it from /etc/rc.d/rc.3 and rc.0 rc.6 etc. to put it
up and down. See the other init.d/etc scripts for examples.
Toby
