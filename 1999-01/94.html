Re: make: *** Warning: Clock skew detected. Your build may be incomplete.

I had this exact same problem not so long ago...

My problem was that I had installed redhat when my clock was set in the
future, eg I installed it on the 20th of December 1998, but the system clock
was set to something in the future at the time due to a dead battery on the
motherboard. Then when I started using linux, I noticed the wrong clock time
and changed it. Then from then on I had that error message that you are
getting, and also something like "Makefile has modification time in the
future!"

The way I fixed it was to 'touch' man touch every file called Make and
Makefile on the whole system, I can't remember the exact command that I used
but it was pretty complex (maybe someone else on the list may answer that
one) and that made the date of every make file on the system to the current
date, and it worked in the future. I don't know if this could cause
problems, it depends on your setup and if you rely much on dates for
compiling stuff. If not, then I reinstall looks like it is in the cards :))

Cheers,
