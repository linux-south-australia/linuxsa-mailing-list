In my experience, clicking on 'I do not wish to get spam mail' or
similar is almost a guarantee that you WILL get spam. Similarly, when
you get an email that says at the bottom 'reply to this email including
the word UNSUBSCRIBE to be removed from this list' is also a guarantee
of receiving more spam. Both are used as email adress harvesting and
confirming devices by unscrupulous spammers. It took me a little while
to cotton on to this, but my email is much less spammy since....
