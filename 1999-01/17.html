if you are using redhat, type
  rpm -qa | grep netscape
then 
  rpm -qa `rpm -qa | grep netscape`

and it will  remove the packages listed in 1st command. then mount your
redhat cd, and 
  rpm -ivvh /mnt/cdrom/RedHat/RPMS/netscape-*
(replace /mnt/cdrom with where your cd is mounted.)

hope this helps.
