Re: Time servers

>I know this has been answered before, but cannot find the Email, what
>are the available time servers in Adelaide.

I don't know a great deal about setting the time over the Internet. But I do
know that there is more than one protocol for sending time data over the
Internet. As far as I can tell, the main two protocols are time and SNTP,
and you need different programs to retreive the date. I don't know of an
SNTP program for linux. But I know rdate uses time.

Here are the servers that I know about:

date:
www.senet.com.au

SNTP:
www.iweb.net.au
ntp.adelaide.edu.au
www.terra.net.au

Others not in adelaide, IE, atomic clocks and others:

SNTP:
time.nist.gov
ns.arc.nasa.gov
tick.usno.navy.mil

Hope that helps a little.
