Re: Power Saving

Michael Doyle wrote:

> G'day
>
> Qusestion, how do I make my monitor shut down under RedHat v5.2 as it
> did under winblows.

First you must compile APM (Advanced Power Management)  in to the kernel.
Then you set the options you want in bios. Ie 2 minutes suspend  & monitor
blank to DPMS
Done!

Linux now will control your monitor.

Under X you may need to add:

option powersave

to the section where you put your video card details

Note: make sure your monitor can handle DPMS before using it else you
could blow your manitor.
