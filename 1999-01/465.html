Consumer laws are pretty much the realm of each state. But keep in mind that
there are lot's of laws that _CAN'T_ be negated by any amount of documentation.
You have rights.

And I'll still bet turning the computer on doesn't negate your rights either.

get legal advice first the courts will probably not be impressed. Not to
mention cooling off periods etc.
