No he doesn't. err no I don't. When you get spam email, you often get a
little polite looking note at the bottom that says something like 'reply
to this email with the word UNSUBSCRIBE to have your email address
removed off our list'. Or somesuch, it has thankfully been a few months
now since I got the last one!. My experience is that by doing as they
ask and replying, you do not get your email address removed from any
list. Rather, you are confirming to the spammer that the email address
that they assume is legit is actually legit, and then it seems the
floodgates open... Hey, if you don't believe me, try it yourself!

Or maybe not, it really, really isn't worth it.
