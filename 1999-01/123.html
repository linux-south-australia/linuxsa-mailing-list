I'm a little confused.  Do you mean "EISA the computer buss"?  In what way
do your modems fail?  It is common (I would say normal) for modems to drop
out each day.  There are many reasons for this, including telephone line
noise, and telephone exchange resets.  Other factors to consider are
inactivity timers, both at your end and your ISP.  I presume you have not
set your end to hang up on inactivity (which can be done using PPP options
or modem configuration),  however your ISP might.  Well, Telstra bigpond:
Probably not :-)  (They do know what they are doing!)

Since modem dropouts are a very common I feel sure that your problem is
something which everyone would be interested in following...
