Problem when I use dialing programs, such as Kppp, wvdial, or
networkconfig to dial out, I get modem modem not responding, yet all
these programs can auto config the modem.

I use pppd connect 'chat "" atdtxxxxxxxx ogin: xxxxxx word: xxxxxx'
/dev/cua1 38400 mru 296 lock debug crtscts modem defaultroute netmask
255.255.255.xxx, to dial out and this works every time.

The reason for wanting to use Kppp is the redial on disconnect function
as I'm connected 24/7 and suffer line dropout every now and again and
want to reconnect auto.
