Re: GNOME and KDE

KDE rocks.

Lots of little annoying bugs, but they're -far- outweighed by the sheer
"niceness" of the interface, and the little things, like being able to
type "/" in the "execute command" box and having the / directory open in
a kfm window.

But I recommend that anyone who hasn't yet tried it to -do so-.  It's wm's
like KDE that are -great- to show your non-linux friends and bosses, as
they are so sleek and professional.

(IMHO)
