I haven't been following this thread (unfortunately), but I should add
that I use internal modems wherever possible , always have. They are
reliable, usually more so, and shouldn't be difficult to debug. Cheap
Taiwanese models work best and stay away from Dynalink (incompatable
command set). They have the distinct advantage of not having a switch that
can be turned off.
