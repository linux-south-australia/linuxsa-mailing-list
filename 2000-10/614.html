RE: Exchange Type Mail server.

Have a look at Teamware Office from Fujitsu.  We've played with the demo &
just bought the product for use on our yet to be installed mail/web server.
Very impressive for the price, $1000 per 100 users.
It runs on NT as well as Linux, we'll be running it on Linux, for obvious
reasons.

http://teamware.fujitsu.com.au/teamware/Products/index.htm
<http://teamware.fujitsu.com.au/teamware/Products/index.htm>; 

We looked at a number of others, HP Openmail also, which gives almost
identical functionality to Exchange, but the price was a bit excessive.  (we
even thought about Exchange, but dismissed it as an option after about 2.5
seconds!! ;-)
Just a footnote on HP Openmail, if you have less than 50 users, u can use
the product for free, minus support options.  So depending on your client
base this could be an option for you.

http://www.openmail.com/cyc/om/00/index.html
<http://www.openmail.com/cyc/om/00/index.html>;
