On a side note for C compilers/debuggers, Borland have a free full
featured compiler/debugger set available on their website. The only
difference is its command line based (Windows). The current issue of APC
has on DVD (What about the poor soles who don't have a DVD player?) an
older version of Borland's C IDE available for free.

Cygwin is also a Windows C compiler/debugger which exists in a bash
environment. Quite interesting to see all your favourite tools running
under Windows. Beta versions appear to have been free, but now they are
under RedHat stable they may not be free (I couldn't find dload links
when looking for documentation for the beta I have). But I didn't look
very hard. :)
