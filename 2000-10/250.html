RE: Plot Programs xview info

I would give gnuplot a go http://www.gnuplot.vt.edu/  for what you are
after.

I know it works, works well and is not to hard to drive.

It is basically a pretty decent mathematical graphing tool, and in fact you
should find copies floating around the Uni. I know we used it quite a bit in
Mech Eng when I did my degree.

It is also free as you would expect, and has been around for quite some
time. It works on lots of platforms including win32 and can output to many
different terminal options.

It is the package I automatically go to if I wish to do some quick look see
graphing, especially if you have as you say x,y coords on a file.
