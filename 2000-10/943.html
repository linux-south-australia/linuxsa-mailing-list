Hmmm, I never bothered with this before. Would that ICMP be Destination
Unreachable, Port Unreachable? If so, it does not mean that the computer's
arms were not long enough to reach that nice bottle of Grandfather port you
have on the shelf, but rather that you probably do not have tftpd listed in
/etc/inetd.conf or you have not restarted inetd.

>  DdG> Obviously I haven't configured something quite right... any
