Re: DMA etc

In my experience, DMA has to be set manually. Look in:

* /proc/ide/ide[number]/[device name]/settings

(eg /proc/ide/ide0/hda is the first primary master, or /dev/hda which is
my primary master drive)

Or execute hdparm /dev/hda (substitute hda with the drive name)

You'll probably find that dma is not set (if hdparm doesn't mention it,
it's not set, if it equals zero it's not set).
