Re: Linux-based document/image management systems

> Has anyone come across any Linux-based Image/document management systems in
> their wandering around the internet?
> 
> What I am interested in is a system that can scan, store and retrieve
> documents, with a Web-based search and retrieval system, where searchs can
> be based on any attributes added to the document or comments added, etc.

_The_ bible on full text retrieval is "Managing Gigabytes :
Compressing and Indexing Documents and Images", Second Edition, 1999.
It has an accompanying, open-source, indexing and retrieval system for
text, images, and textual images.   This is covered by a GNU
public licence.


You can get to it from

	http://www.cs.mu.oz.au/mg/


Probably worth a look.  However, although it is in use as a backend to
a web based library

	http://www.nzdl.org/cgi-bin/library

the software itself is a set of console applications.
