Hello Steve.

You need to do some research, by the sounds of your question.

What service is doing a reverse DNS lookup?  Do you need to run a DNS server?
DHCP has nothing to do with DNS lookups.

I'm guessing that telnetting or ssh'ing or ftping or similar to your box is
taking a long time.  This being the case, and if the cause is DNS lookups,
you may want to get a DNS server happening, depending on the size of your
network, so that the DNS queries work.  Also note that it's possible to
stop some services from doing reverse DNS lookups.

Read the DNS-HOWTO.  It helped me, it may help you :)
