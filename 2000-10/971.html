Re: bttv

Robyn Manning wrote:
> 
> Hi guys,
> 
> Has anyone got a Fly Video bt878 working with RH7? I'm using the bttv
> module from RH7.
> 
> I get good picture, though only 1 Channel (10), can't tune in any other
> channels as 10 is on all of them and no sound.
> 

The tuner module has something to do with the i2c-* modules for it's
control.

I think with a 2.2 kernel and the modules supplied with RH7, you need to
use i2c-old (or modprobe bttv to let the kernel sort it out).

I upgraded to a 2.4 test kernel and associated modules, and got a newer
bttv that used i2c-algo-bit (and i2c-core), and it all worked much
better.

The newer drivers also understand a lot of the more obscure cards.
