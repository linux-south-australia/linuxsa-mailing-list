Re: Newbie question.

Quoting Daniel Falkenberg <falken@xavier.sa.edu.au>:

> How do I kill a terminal from another terminal? I.E terminal 1 has frozen
> on me and of coarse I
> don't want to restart is there a command like...
> 
> killall tty1... ?

If something has died, it will actually be a program rather than a tty (a tty is a device). You
need to kill the program that's running on that tty. If that don't work killall -9
[programname]. You send your log report script into an infinite loop or something?
