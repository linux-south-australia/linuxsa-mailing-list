Yes -- but remember, it's all about probabilities, and conditions can change
that will change the real-life application of those probabilities. EG:
consider how secure a 64 bit key used to be, and how secure it is now. The
likelyhood of guessing the key hasn't changed, but the technology has.
mechanisms for checking integrity that were ok years ago are no longer
valid, as the technoilogy has changed, so there are other problems now that
we didn't used to have.
