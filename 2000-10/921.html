At 11:16 PM 10/27/00 +0930, Mick Collins wrote:
>   Hay all.   Just asking is it hard to install a ADSL connection  in to a

About as hard as it is to switch off HTML in mail.

First, you cut a slot in the box, than you drop the Ethernet card into the
box, and make sure that it is pressed up against the slot. Probably better
to cut the slot into one of the narrow sides of the box.

Can't imagine why you would want it in the RedHat 6.0 box, rather than your
computer though :-)
