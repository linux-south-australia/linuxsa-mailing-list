Re: four free 9600 bps modems to give away

Hi,

> PS. I guess that in 2 years, even 56k modems will
> be history. If ADSL gets serious, the 56k modem is going
> to start looking old-fashioned.

I'd tend to disagree with your time period here. I'm still seeing 288
modems on occasions. If you don't have a digital modem server, then 336 is
the Max you'll get. Until digital servers drop below the $10000 mark and
ISDN and phone line rental prices drop too, then many people running small
dialup services will still be having old racks of modems.

Also 'till the ADSL laws get sorted out, I don't see serious competition
and thus price drops happening, so I think the standard will be around for
a while.
