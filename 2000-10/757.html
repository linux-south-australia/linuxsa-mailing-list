Re: SSL problems with squid

Glen Blundell wrote:

> Anyway, using the configration above I have SSL problems using IE5.  Before
> you blame IE5 let me tell you some stuff (IE5 may still be to blame)

In the default configuration, squid only allows SSL connections on port
443. https://something.com:442/ will fail. Probably nothing to do with
your problem, but you never know with MS.

These lines in squid.conf affect it:

acl SSL_ports port 443 563
http_access deny CONNECT !SSL_ports
