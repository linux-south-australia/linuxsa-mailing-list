Re: Wireless LANs and the law

Richard Sharpe wrote:
> Hmmm, what is the definition of a third party? Is an employee who dials up
> from home and uses the network for personal purposes, and the company pays
> FBT, does that employee become a third-party for carrier purposes?

The Act talks about supply to the "general public" as being to people
outside of the "immediate circle" of the link owner. You can read about
who belongs to the "immediate circle" in various circumstances at
http://www.austlii.edu.au/au/legis/cth/consol_act/ta1997214/s23.html#immediate_circle.
