Re: squid multiples

"Russell, Richard (DEH)" wrote:

> For some reason, squid seems to start sixteen copies of itself. This is
> fifteen too many (it's only a local cache for myself)...

Depending on the version of squid, there should be only one or two
_processes_ running.  However, bear in mind that if you're running
a multi-threaded version of squid (and they do exist), Linux's ps
shows each thread as though it were a process, so what you're seeing
may not be what you're getting.
