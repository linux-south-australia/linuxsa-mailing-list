Re: Newbie question.

At 12:12 PM 10/16/00 +1030, Daniel Falkenberg wrote:
>How do I kill a terminal from another terminal? I.E terminal 1 has frozen
on me and of coarse I
>don't want to restart is there a command like...
>
>killall tty1... ?

Well, you could do: 'killall login' :-) But that might be a bit drastic.

Use 'ps ax | grep tty1' which should show you the PID of the login process
running behind tty1, and then a 'kill -9 <PID>' should do the job.
