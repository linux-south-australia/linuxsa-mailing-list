Looks to me like they are dead! I recommend that you phone your ISP and ask 
them what their nameserver addresses are, alternatively, enable the 
usepeerdns option in your ppp setup.  If you do have it enabled, then call 
your ISP and tell them that their server is giving you bad addresses, and 
you want the correct addresses.

If they don't give it to you then it's time to change ISPs :)
