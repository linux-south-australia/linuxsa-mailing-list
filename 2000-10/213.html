Re: Ethereal capture filter

At 11:43 AM 10/6/00 +0900, Richard Sharpe wrote:
>At 08:01 AM 10/6/00 +0930, Dieter Jansen wrote:
>>Also, are any of you aware of analysis tools or plugins that
>>can help to analyse traces for timeout issues?

Hmmmm, we haven't come to grips with the notion of plugins that analyse
issues like this.

One of the big problems is that Ethereal dissectors are too specific to
dissecting and displaying. They are full of calls to display the info.

For Ethereal 2.0, I am pushing the notion of seperating the dissecting
library more fully from the display library, so analysis tools can use the
dissecting library to do interesting things.
