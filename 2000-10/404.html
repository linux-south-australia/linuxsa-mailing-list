Re: Visio Drawings

Paul Schulz wrote:
> 
> Has anyone any suggestions about how to deal with 'Visio' drawings
> on a linux system (ie. getting them into another managable format).

Your only hope is Corel Draw, as there's no free converter that I know of.

Unfortunately, Corel's web site is written by a PR person and there's
no listing of all the supported file formats.  There's also no trial
version to try converting a typical file, so you'd be buying in the dark.

Because Visio documents are an OLE paradise, it's not practical
to write a standalone conversion program.
