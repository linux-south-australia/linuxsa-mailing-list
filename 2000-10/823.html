RE: iPrimus account.

I was 'kindly' supplied one of these for work, I wasn't overly impressed
with it but I didnt have any major problems once I had the correct drivers
installed. I spoke to the distributer and they basically didnt think to
much of them either except for price. 

It sounds like the problem you are having is not a problem with the modem
hardware but maybe an incompatability with Linux, given it works under
win2k. With regards to ICQ I am pretty sure it doesnt use TCP (which is
what HTTP/FTP use) to stay connected (like it does for other services) but
something more like a ping (possibly using UDP) telling icq host your still
online. 

Good luck
