Re: ADSL

>I've heard that once you've gotten it installed it's reasonable (for
>what it is)...about $80 to $100 per month...or thereabouts. But is it
>true you have to buy a router (at ~$4000) to cope with it, or is this
>hearsay?

Telstra's ADSL install cost varies from $189 to $399 depending on the length
of contract you get with them. Pricing info can be found at
http://www.telstra.com.au/adsl/price.htm

I doubt adding a router to the service would help a PC cope with the load
any less than a NIC by itself would, unless you offload any firewall
functions to a router.

Remember that Telstra suggest that 2Mb/s shared among 200-300 ADSL
(<1.5Mb/s) users is a workable oversubscription level, so there won't be
much load to deal with. Most ISP's in Adelaide are offering more bandwidth
than this to 200-300 modem users .. do the math and work out why you'd want
to pay twice as much to possibly end up with less speed than a modem.
