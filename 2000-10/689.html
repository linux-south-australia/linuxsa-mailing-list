Hi.

My first successful program was a continuous sine-wave plot on the
Minitran service offered by Monash uni to the Melbourne secondary schools.
The program was 'written' by pushing out holes on a series of punch cards
and then wrapping them in a job request card.

The output (or some 100 pages of it, anyway) was returned the next week
with a terse note asking that the school discipline the individual
concerned. Unfortunately for society, the teacher saw the funny side of
the situation and made a farcical attempt at doing so, with much hilarity
in the class.

About 1972 I think.

Michael
