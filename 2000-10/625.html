which isn't much fun on a permanent link. Onramp Xpress at $275/month

Adam Internet already have customers in Littlehampton connecting using
ORHH, so your odds are good (despite my earlier post :). They can offer
the Data-over-voice ISDN option to permanent links (so 2x18c gets you
128K untimed calls) but don't have it working for casual access just
yet.
