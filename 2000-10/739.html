> 		conversions are what restricts the modem to a theoretical

> 		more realisticly 28.8K.  If Telstra had used integrated RIMs

> the second A/D
> 		conversion and allowing 56K modem operation, also ISDN
> services would be
> 		available right now and ADSL would only require adding new
> cards to each of
> 		the RIMs and not changing all the interfacing infrastructure
