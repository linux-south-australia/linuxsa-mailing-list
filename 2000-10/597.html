Re: Apache permissions

Hooo, this one got me for over a month.

Linuxconf probably rooted your httpd.conf
It replaces the line <Files ~ "^\.ht"> with <Files ~> which denies access to 
all files. Find this line in httpd.conf and replace it with the original, 
you will find that your server now works again.
