Re: Auto fsck at bootup-RH6.1

Don Collins wrote:

> 1)  How do I force it to automatically do a full e2fsck at boot when the
> maximum mount count is reached?

"shutdown -F" forces a fsck on reboot.

As an aside:

    dumpe2fs /dev/hda_whatever_root 2> /dev/null | grep -i 'mount count'

should show you something like:

    Mount count:              8
    Maximum mount count:      20

> 2) Why is it still telling me maximum mount count is reached when I've
> fsck'ed the partitions; and how do I fix it?

Not sure why the count hasn't been reset, but hopefully the above will
solve your problems.
