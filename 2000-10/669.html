And the kids of today think that life is tough! Hell, in my day we didn't
have computers at school. We only had one computer in Darwin, and they
wouldn't let us near that one, so we wrote our programs on mark-sense cards
(like CrossLotto these days) and sent them to Angle Park in Adelaide. A
week later, back came our listing, full of mistakes, so we rewrote some
mark sense cards and so forth.

And further back, people actually knew how to repair paper tape. By $DEITY,
the kids of today have got it easy. Now, if only we could get rid of that
stupid PC BIOS (see www.linuxbios.org).
