Those rules are for the old 10base2 cables, the thick black co-axial cables,
like for ya tv. The 5-4-3 rule is in regards to the way the network is
setup. i cant remeber the exact rule.
there was a debate about "rules" for installation a while back, so check out
the mailing list archive @ www.linuxsa.org.au for the discussion. Presonaly
i would not use 10base2, with the cost of cat 5 cable, and hubs cheap, then
10baseT would be the best way to go, but of course it really does depend on
your network toplogy. An idea is to borrow a book from the local libary, and
it should have everything you need.
