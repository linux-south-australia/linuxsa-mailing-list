Re: just a thought about the smb_auth

"Freeman, Peter (ERHS)" wrote:
> 
> Footnote to last message.....
> Has anyone used the smb module for pam?  Pros/Cons etc
> If so, would it be possible/better to have Squid authenticate users through
> pam_smb?

Yes. I would use pam_ntdom by Luke Leigton (it's currently unmaintained
though), but it does some of the DCE/RPC stuff better and actually
creates a domain trust (the same as any Windows NT WKS or Member server.
You can grab an RPM (check out rpmfind.net) and I would advise this,
cause I've never managed to get the thing to compile. ;-)

You can then use auth_pam with squid to authenticate your proxy users.
We do the same here, but using a hacked version of pam_ldap. One thing
to be careful of is the use of spaces in a user's password. Squid uses
the space as a delimiter and you can therefore not authenticate a user
that has a space in their password (like me ;-)).

Hope it helps,
