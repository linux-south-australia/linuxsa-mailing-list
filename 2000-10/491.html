Re: Dum question

On Sat, 14 Oct 2000, Evan Bourlotos wrote:

> How do you cut the text from term window and paste it somewhere else?

I am assuming you are running X windows.  Do you have a three buttoned
mouse?  If so, you highlight the bit of text you want to copy by clicking
the left mouse button and draging.  Then you move the cursor to the window
you wish to paste in and click the middle button to paste.  (Make sure the
new window has the focus.)

If you have a two buttoned mouse, click the left and right button together
simultaneously to emulate a middle mouse click.

Hope this helps,

Mark.
