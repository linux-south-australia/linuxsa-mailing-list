Re: log messages

Alex Wilkinson wrote:

> Can anyone tell me how I could get logs for my account similar to
> /var/log/messages without being root.

You'd have to define "logs for your account".  Log messages are
generated by programs that aren't usually "account specific" (eg,
PAM, named).  You could give read access to everyone to .../messages,
but that opens up security considerations.

> Or is there any tool that a *user* could use to monitor there account?

Again, what is "monitoring their account"?  What _exactly_ are you
trying to achieve?
