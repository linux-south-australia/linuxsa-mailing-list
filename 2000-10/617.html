Re: ISDN cards to suit Telstra Onramp connection

"Freeman, Peter (ERHS)" wrote:
> 
> Is there a preferred ISDN card / terminal adaptor in use out there?
> This one will be connected to Telstra's Onramp2, so if anyone's been there
> done that, would love to hear from you.

I have some Digi datafire BRI cards that work fine with Onramp2, and
outperform most other cards (and routers) in throughput, since they have
their own motorola processor onboard. They also have the voice option.

The catch is that they don't have linux drivers, but they're great if
you use Windows, Novell, or Solaris OS's :) If anyone fancies themselves
as an ISDN driver hacker for linux or *BSD, and perhaps knows a bit
about the "xilink" programmable controller chips, they're welcome to
borrow one.
