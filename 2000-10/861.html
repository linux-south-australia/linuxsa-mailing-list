I've been using a D-Link Internet Server for a while to dial in to my
iPrimus account, in Adelaide and also in Brisbane, and I find that around
10-20% of my connections are simply "dead". One anomaly I've noticed is that
for maybe 50% of these "dead" connections, DNS appears to be work (even
though pings to the DNS servers will timeout).

We use these D-Link devices at work, primarily with SENet and DingoBlue
accounts, and I'm not aware of any inherent problems with them. I'm
therefore ready to believe that the iPrimus dial-in servers have some
peculiarities (dare I suggest that perhaps they're fine-tuned for accepting
connections from Windows clients? Apparently I do!).
