> The other problem was my decision to enable DMA during the last install

I don't know where you might find it, but under redhat there is a utility
called hdparm to enable and disable DMA modes. If its not compiled into
your kernel, it is generally off by default. Using this program in your
staretup scripts shoutl be able to disable this.
