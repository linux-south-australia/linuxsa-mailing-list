Re: make

> > When running make, what does the error "missing separator" mean ?
> 
> It means your Makefile isn't correct.


In actuality, you'll find that you've used spaces instead of tabs in the makefile... ex:

exeprogram: eceprog.o
<space><space>gcc ......

should be:

exeprogram: exeprog.o
<tab>gcc...

Make doesn't understand spaces; they generate errors.  You need the tab to tell it that the current commands belong under the current target.
