> > think is a little bit slow, but nether-the-less. MRTG is saying that the
> > maximum speed speed since lunchtime is 10 Kbytes/second. Just wondering why
> > it isn't reporting what I would have thought is higher?
> 
> I had a similar problem.. MRTG only reports about 1/5 the total link speed.  I can't see why, and > I have looked everywhere to find out how I can fix it.  Hopefully you will find an answer.

Sometimes it means that you're measuring the wrong interface, or the
interface itself doesn't fully support SNMP. For example, The SNMP
system on Cobalt servers only counts bytes in, and this then looks like
a 1/5 (or less) bytes out count when used with a default mrtg
cfgmaker-generated script.
