Re: RH 7 CDs

>I am about to download RH 7 from AARNet, but came across one smallish
>problem.
>
>The redhat-7.0 directory contains a few directories containing a few
>different
>ISOs. I would like to know which ones to download before I use 1200MB of
>Internet...

I got away with download just the first ISO, and as long as you choose the
out-of-the-box server or workstation configurations, everything you need is
on there. CD Number 2 has the extras on it that you might require if you do
a custom installation. There's usually an SRPM's CD and power tools as well,
most of which will be out of date by the time you find a use for them.

Also, try a traceroute to ftp.netcraft.com, it's nice and fast for any
SAIX-connected network. AARNET have faster servers, but I can still download
from netcraft faster than the CD-writer can burn it (although it's a little
clogged with RH7 activity at the moment), and it's a less expensive route.
