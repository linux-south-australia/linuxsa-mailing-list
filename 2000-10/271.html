This redirects the 'stderr' output to 'stdout' (so you get both).

Have a look at 'man bash' or 'man tcsh' for more information or even a
good unix book.

Summary: by default, every process opens three standard files,
standard in (stdin), standard out (stdout) and standard error
(stderr), with the unix file descriptors of 0, 1 and 2 respectfully.
