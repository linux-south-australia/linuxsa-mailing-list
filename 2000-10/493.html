Re: SendMail and Rebuilding Db's

David Lloyd wrote:

> I think that SendMail is automatically rebuilding the databases in
> /etc/mail but it only reports rebuilding the aliases.

You don't say which version of Sendmail, and what I'm about to say
definitely applies to 8.9.3 (ie, I don't know the story for later
versions).  So, with the caveats out of the way ...


>    access.db            Changed B.md5 UMtm UCtm.
>    domaintable.db       Changed B.md5 UMtm UCtm.
>    mailertable.db       Changed B.md5 UMtm UCtm.
>    virtusertable.db     Changed B.md5 UMtm UCtm.

> So who is rebuilding these ones?

Sendmail doesn't know how to build these files, unlike the /etc/aliases
database, and uses them in a "read-only" fashion.  So you're looking at
an external cause (most likely process).  I know RedHat ship a Makefile
that lives in the /etc/mail/ directory where these db's normally live
for rebuilding them; perhaps that's being run nightly?
