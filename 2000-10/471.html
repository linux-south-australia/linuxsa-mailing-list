Re: sorting in numeric order with Perl.

Daniel!

> I am having trouble sorting my perl script by numeric order.

That is difficult to interpret. I think you mean sorting the things your
script is sorting :-)

> I've looked at sort {$a <=> $b}
> but my problem doesn't lie here, well I don't think it does.

Do a perldoc -f sort. You'll find you can specify your own sort
functions...

> I have a generated list that
> shows an element on numbers and i want this to be displayed as list from greatest value to
> lowest value.  Where do I go from here?

I suspect your sorting on ASCII, that is PERL has decided that the
things you're sorting with are strings NOT numbers. Hence:

"20" is less than "3" (because 2 is ALWAYS less than 3, just as A is
always less than B)....

You could:

* force a numeric sort
 - possibly by adding 0 to it...or using some function that makes it
numeric

* write your own sort function
 - use the documentation....

Just a few thoughts...
