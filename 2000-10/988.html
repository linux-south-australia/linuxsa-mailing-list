Re: Smart Redial

Hi,

I have another plan for my setup, which will happen, in the fullness of
time, when I get around to it, when I move house, ...
(It will, really).

It has two parts:-

1. diald (I think) dials out whenever there's a request from the network for
an outside IP address, and then never conciously hangs up;

2. In the event that forces beyond my control boot me off, It doesn't redial
automatically. However (and this is the part I don't know how to do yet),
It, or summin't, listens on the modem for a ring in, and if one happens,
waits for it to finish, or picks up and hangs up (ideally it just listens
for rings and then waits for them to stop), and then dials up normally. This
means that if you suddenly find while away that you can't reach that file,
then you can ring the modem on your mobile, hang up, and 30s later it's
there again.

That's my grand plan, anyway. Anyone got any other ideas, or implementation
suggestions?
