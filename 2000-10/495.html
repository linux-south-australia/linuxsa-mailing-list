StarOffice source code

I haven't seen mention of it here, so I'll drop a pointer for those of
you who don't read slashdot (tip:  go read slashdot :-)

Sun has released the StarOffice source code.  It's available for 
download at www.openoffice.org, it requires a download of somewhere in
the order of 80 Mbytes, and (according to the docs) you'll need about
3 Gbytes of disk to build it.

For those of you who claim StarOffice is too bloated to use:  Judging
by those figures you're probably correct, but at least now you have the
source code and you can join efforts to prune the bits you think are
unnecessary or poorly optimized, leaving you with the fully-functional
office suite which fits on a floppy disk which you've always wanted.

By the way, disk space is cheap :-)
