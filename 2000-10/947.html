Re: log messages

> Hi all,
> 
> Can anyone tell me how I could get logs for my account similar to
> /var/log/messages without being root.

You can always pop the messages logs up on a console such as alt-f12
or alt-f10, (assuming you don't run 4 X servers).

Just as a line under your entry for /var/log/messages in
/etc/syslog.conf like this...

*.info;mail.none;authpriv.none         /var/log/messages
*.info;mail.none;authpriv.none         /dev/tty12

Restart sysklogd and your message will pop up on the 12th console.
