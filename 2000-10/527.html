Re: Newbie question.

Hi,

> How do I kill a terminal from another terminal? I.E terminal 1 has frozen on me and of coarse I
> don't want to restart is there a command like...
> 
> killall tty1... ?

You'll need to find the process number of your shell. To achieve this you
need to know approximatly when you logged in, and your shell (sh, ksh,
tcsh, bash etc).

Use the ps command to find your process number (ps ux) and look at the
start time.

then use the command kill -9 $PID supstitute the appropriate pid for $PID.

your shell and all process under it should quit.
