At best, you have made a category mistake by conflating the two. They use 
different explanations and are only united by the term 'evolution' itself.

At worst, it is an attempt to conflate the two so that one or the other 
can be rejected by applying arguments or conslusions from one area to 
another in an erroneous manner.

In addition, there is good experimental evidence for the big-bang, and 
theories of the evolution of the universe, as far as I am aware, deal with 
events from after the big-bang to current times.
