[ANNOUN/REMINDER] About Java: The July Monthly Meeting 9/7/2003 (Tomorrow)

The South Australian chapter of the Australian Unix User Group (AUUG)
formally invites you to attend their monthly meeting. Details are as
follows:
+----------------------------------------
| SA-AUUG Chapter Meeting
+--------+-------------------------------
| DATE   | 9th July 2003
| TIME   | 7pm Onwards
| VENUE  | Microbits
|        | 35 Magill Road
|        | STEPNEY
+--------+------------------------------
| AGENDA |
+--------+------------------------------

1. An Introduction to the Java Language and Platform
   by David Bullock.

David is a software engineer and will discuss what makes Java
different from other language/platform combinations, and
why formely held perceptions of Java being slow need
revision.

Attendees will be exposed to the basic Java
Development Kit tools, to give a quick-start into
making your first Java program.   Familiarity with
at least one language from the C-family of languages
(C, C++, C#) would assist comparison, but is not
required.

We will also take a quick look at Java in the browser,
and how it differs from JavaScript.

---------------------------------------
At the conclusion of the meeting many members of the group continue
to talk as they wine and dine at one of the fine local restaurant
establishments to which all are invited.

Hope to see you there.

Regards,
  SA-AUUG Secretary
