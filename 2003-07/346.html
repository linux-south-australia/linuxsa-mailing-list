ext2fs/ext3fs feature "reserved" space which is available only to root. This
is designed (at least in part) to prevent ordinary users from exhausting all
available disk space which can lead to difficulties in continued operation
of the system. This reserved space is not included in the df listing of
Available blocks and Use% and appears to typically default to 5% (as your
experience demonstrates, around 70M out of 1416M and 45M out of 904M).

As you're running very low on available space you probably will need to
consider doing a bit of cleaning up and/or pruning, especially if you're
wanting to compile.

See fs utilities, e.g. man tunefs
