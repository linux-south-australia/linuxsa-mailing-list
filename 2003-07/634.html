RE: [OT] WD Hard Drive Failures?

You should not be getting 7 or 8 drive failures in that sort of time. I
would be looking at your power supply. It is highly likely it is spiking (or
generally over voltage) your HDD. Pay some close attention to your 12V and
5V PSU rails. If they are significantly above or below spec or fluctuate
widey, this is most likely the problem.
