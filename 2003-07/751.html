Article: Indian slum kids using Internet

Kylie shared this with the ComputerBank list but some of you will find 
this a very interesting story.

Cheers,  Ian
 __________________________________________________________
 GO TO a slum district of Delhi, put a computer with an
 internet connection into a public wall and leave it on. Wait
 and watch through a remote video camera rigged up on a
 nearby tree. 

 Children who cannot read or write, with no English but
 blazing with curiosity, come along and start fiddling with
 the weird contraption that has surfaced out of nowhere. 

 Eight minutes later, children who do not know what a
 computer is are navigating the web. By evening more than 70
 are surfing the net. Days later they are playing games,
 creating folders, cutting and pasting, creating short cuts.
