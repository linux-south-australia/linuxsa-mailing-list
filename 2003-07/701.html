Re: Cisco IOS Bug - kinda Ugly

Glen Turner wrote:

> Rather that until Cisco release a packet signature for
> the problem-causing traffic, then the firewall will
> simply forward an exploit packet.


Cisco have identified the packets as having IP protocol types of 53, 55, 
77 or 103. /etc/protocols tells me these are SWIPE, MOBILE, SUN-ND and 
PIM. None of these protocols will work with NAT, and unless your 
firewall has the line 'allow ip any any' in it, it's unlikely that 
they're going to be able to traverse it.
