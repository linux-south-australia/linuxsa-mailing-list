Re: Secure Apache VirtualHost

In the immortal words of <regenerate@hush.com>...

> Surely there's some way to stop users in an apache 'virtual host' from
> being able to view other hosted site's files?

have you tried looking at open_basedir?

according to the PHP manual...

  Limit the files that can be opened by PHP to the specified
  directory-tree, including the file itself. This directive is NOT
  affected by whether Safe Mode is turned On or Off.

adding this to your virtualhost directives should do the trick

<Directory /docroot>  
	php_admin_value open_basedir /root/account/docroot
</Directory>

Unless my reading of the manual is in error.
