Re: RH9 Accessing files and printers on novell

Tony,
In terms of printer access, if you are using TCP/IP rather than IPX as your 
network protocol, then you could always use direct printing from the linux 
boxes to the printers. LPRNG provides security, and I also believe you can 
set up NDPS printers as unix printer queues so unix boxen can print to them 
too. 

In terms of greater functionality and file access, hold tight 6 months, as 
Novell is about to release a suite of products for Linux which should 
include all sorts of functionality.
