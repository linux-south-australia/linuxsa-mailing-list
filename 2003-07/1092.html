Re: IPCHAINS RESTART

jonathan soong wrote:

> Anyone got a script to reset ipchains rule at a given interval?


If you have more than one colocated box, you could run a null modem 
cable between the serial ports and set each one listening with getty. If 
you ever lock yourself out again [1] you can shutdown getty on the 
'good' system, and use minicom or similar to access the console on the 
system you're locked out of. Tuning firewall rules through a serial 
connection can be much more productive.

This is also handy in case you break sshd, or possibly in the event of 
some other kind of denial of service attack that makes it difficult to 
reach your system through it's network interface. Some datacentres have 
a facility where they can offer you such out-of-band access through 
their own terminal servers.

John Edwards

[1] The probability of locking yourself out ever again is greatly 
reduced after today's lesson :).
