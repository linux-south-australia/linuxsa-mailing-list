I beleive CGA and EGA used the DB9 connector, so this would probably be a
CGA/EGA to VGA cable, enabling you to use a VGA monitor on a CGA/EGA device.

DB9 doesn't automatically mean it's serial, just like DB25 doesn't
automatically mean it's parallel. You can run serial RS232 through almost
any connector -- Cisco routers, for eg, run it with an RJ45 plug. All you
need is at least 4 wires (I think -- I could easily be wrong there :)
