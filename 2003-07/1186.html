Re: Re: bsd- easier than you think

My problem in this debate is Mark's use of the word "hypocrite".
There is a way of looking at the two licences that rightly says that
GPL is less free than the BSD licence.  GPL imposes greater restrictions
on developers.  But it's just mean to say that people who develop under
GPL aren't providing a free gift.  They are.  What they give the world
is a gift, for which they ask nothing in return except the surety that
nobody will ever be denied that gift in the futre, even if the gift
becomes nothing more than the genesis of something greater.  I'm sure
many do hope for their gift to become the genesis of something greater.
That's not hypocrisy.
