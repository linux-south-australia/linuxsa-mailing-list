One of the best things about debian is apt-get. I currently use redhat
9.0 with apt-get from http://apt.freshrpms.net/, and its been pretty
good, but its also good to be familiar with the other major
distributions.

Luckily, trying out debian is as easy as getting Knoppix
http://www.knoppix.org/ and booting from CD. If you really like it,
there is a hd install script that does all the work for you.

Some months I like debian, others redhat, I tried mandrake 9.0, but it
put me off a bit, I've heard 9.1 is better, and I was quite a fan of
earlier mandrakes.
