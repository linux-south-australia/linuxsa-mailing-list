>   address that Adam Internet assigns me

http://www.billion.com.au/html/support_711ce.htm

You need to:

1) clear the username, password, and service name from the wan/ppp settings.
2) enable bridged mode in the pull down menu.
3) Change encapsulation to 1482 bridged ip.
4) setup a pppoe client on a PC.

Make sure you have PPPoE software and know your username and password 
*before* you disable the router's own PPP functionality :)
