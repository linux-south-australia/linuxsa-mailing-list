I have noticed some discussion about some 'hard to get' 802.11b cards and
using hostap, The Dlink DWL-520 works using hostap, but is discontinued.

Sakura IT has stock of some Sparklan WL-360F cards (the same card as the
DWL-520, its a 'homebrand' from gemtek), that are supported under
Linux/FreeBSD and work with hostap.

If you are interested in them , please visit http://www.sakura-it.com and
take a look under specials.

Regards,

Ben Wannan

P.S. Apologies if this is regarded as advertising, I would just like to help
those who are interested in hostap supported hardware.
