This has got to be stopped, or anyone that does not use Microsoft will 
be cut off from the rest of the world.  I also noticed that requiring MS 
word seems inadequate, now MS Office must be purchased to read emails.

Gates will try to sell this as a solution to spam and viruses, but the 
real motive may be a way to charge users by the email. He mentioned all 
of this in a recent interview.

I view stopping this as being of critical importance. Action has to be 
taken soon, weather by legislation to require open formats, or by more 
people taking an interest in alternative operating systems. But 
something has to be done before the momentum is unstoppable, and seeing 
that this is a beta program, the clock is ticking.
