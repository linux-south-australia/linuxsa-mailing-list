David,

The account I have with my ISP is their top rated plan. They guarantee
that I will not get kicked unless things get extremely busy. In the past
I have been connected for more than a week at a time. This saves me a
lot on phone calls and offsets the cost of the dial-up plan I am on. I
do not turn my server off at any time. It runs trouble free and as I am
away from home regularly this allows the family trouble free and secure
connection to the internet.
