Re: VNC - exporting current X11 screen

David Lloyd wrote:

>I wonder how well VNC's run intensive games like the UT's and CS's of the
>world...
>  
>

It's not likely to be workable, those games rely on the video card to do 
a lot of the rendering, which relies on AGP access to memory with a 
large amount of bandwidth. Try running any of those games in 'software' 
video mode rather than OpenGL and see how well they do. For a 
best-case-scenario emulation of a network, see if you can dig out an ISA 
video card and play on that :)
