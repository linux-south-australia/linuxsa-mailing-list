Online discussion areas

While talking about chat areas and sites and online discussion areas I 
spotted this reference.  Interesting.

"
 A group is its own worst enemy.

 An extremely interesting article about groups, and social
 software (software used by groups). I'm still reading (not
 even half-way) but wanted to share this.

 If you're running a forum, blog-with-comments, wiki, or some
 such, or thinking about doing that: read it. If you're just
 interested in what happens when groups get together (whether
 on or off the internet, there doesn't seem to be a
 difference), read it as well. The article quotes examples
 from the time of dial-up BBSs, so it's not about forums, etc
 - it's about what *happens* there.
