It sounds a bit odd to me. Digital is usually encrypted rather than
scrambled! The days of analogue encryption are over. Desktop computers
are typically fast enough to brute force the algorithms[1]. Plus, where
do you plug a scrambler in to a wireless card? Its not like they have an
audio line in or out on them! 

You certainly can encrypt the data with software over the wireless link
though, if your looking for tighter security. I used FreeSWAN once, but
I think it might be a bit out of date these days not sure if there is a
more prefered way.
