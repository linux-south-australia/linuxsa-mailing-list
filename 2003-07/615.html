The upgrade cycle that many distro's participate in now was not always the
norm. In years gone by stability was the go and Penguins were meticulous 
in keeping their systems up and running. They bragged about the huge number 
of hours in uptime they had achieved. Software that worked well was valued 
and kept in use for long periods. Time has moved on, software has improved 
and upgrading is more common. Maybe it will swing back again.
