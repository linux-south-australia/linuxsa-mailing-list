[OT] Google's "Easter egg"

Ok, some of you may have seen this, for those who haven't, enjoy.

An Easter Egg in a program is an out of the way routine included by the 
programmers as an "inside" joke. Sometimes they flash up messages, 
other times they carry out odd functions. The Easter Egg in Microsoft 
Excel used to start up a flight simulator!

So try out this little "Easter Egg" in Google before Google fixes its 
site. 

1) Go to http://www.google.com ; 
2) type in (but don't hit return): "weapons of mass destruction"; 
3) Hit the "I'm feeling lucky" button, instead of the normal "Google 
search" button; 

4) read what appears to be a normal error message carefully.


OK, it isn't a real programmer's Easter Egg but it is a funny site 
anyway.
