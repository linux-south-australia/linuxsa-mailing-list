Yes, evolution has holes, but then so does every other theory. However, 
the bulk of evidence does in fact fit the evolutionary model quite well 
(note I didn't say ALL the evidence), and as such, it's currently the 
best model we have.

Having said that, if we just accepted it as the only model, science 
would not progress. The way of science is to postulate a theory and at 
some later stage when more facts are known, change that theory or throw 
it out and postulate a new theory that better fits the known evidence.

However, the beauty of human existence is that we have free thought and 
the ability to choose what we believe. Thus, if some people want to 
believe we exist because a higher being thought it would be a good 
idea, good on them, but it doesn't mean that other people who want to 
believe something else are wrong, after all, there is no definitive 
proof either way.
