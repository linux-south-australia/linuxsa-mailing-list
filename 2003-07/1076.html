Re: Mutt mail questions

> 
> FYI, a nicer way of doing this is to add:
> 
> mime_lookup application/octet-stream
> 
> to your .muttrc.  That'll make mutt use the filename of the attachment to
> decide what type of file it is, rather than the mime type.
> 
> Then, you can type "v" to see the attachments, and hit enter to have mutt
> execute whatever program suits.
> 
> (I'm glad you asked - it's been bugging me for ages, and your question
> prompted me to actually look :-)
> 
> FYI, I'm using mutt 1.5.4i - the mime_lookup command might be a new one.
>
