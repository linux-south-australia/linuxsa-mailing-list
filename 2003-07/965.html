If you have xpdf installed and Midnight Commander add this to .muttmailcap

application/pdf; mcview %s

And this to mc.ext

# PDF
type/^PDF
        Open=(xpdf %f &)
        View=%view{ascii} pdftotext %f -
 
Midnight Commander uses pdf2text to convert and then displays
