RE: multiple HDDs

Hi Adam,

Just to nit pick on another post, it is 2 devices per channel, not 2 per
controller.
If you want lots of drives look at a products by a company called 3ware,
they make THE best IDE RAID controllers, they have models with up to 12 IDE
Channels (but they only let you run 1 device per channel) it is true
hardware RAID 5 (if you want it) it also has onboard cache for parity
bit/write caching.
You can then stick your CD-RW's etc on your standard IDE controllers, and
then all your HDD's onto the 3ware controller. You can then RAID 0 2 drives
for a squid cache (squid loves high I/O)

Sounds like fun.
