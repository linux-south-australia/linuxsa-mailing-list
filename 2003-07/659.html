> EGA to VGA connector maybe?

Not possible, the signal of both EGA and VGA differs too much.

You could, however, connect CGA monitors to EGA video cards - the signal was
simular, and EGA just offered (off memory, I haven't thought about this stuff
in years) more video memory, 16 colours at 320x200, and a new monochrome mode
(640x400?).
