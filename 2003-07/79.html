SCO vs Linux: SMH review

The Sydney Morning Herald reviews the SCO vs Linux story. In summary, 
they perceive that Linux will be unaffected by whatever scenario 
evolves out of the mess.

Cheers,  Ian

"SCO vs IBM: How does this affect Linux?
By Con Zymaris
July 2 2003

The topics to be covered in this article focused on the analysis and 
current positioning in the Linux vs IBM legal dispute. 

Firstly, while Linux is not under direct legal threat from SCO, SCO has 
decided to implicate it in terms of its contractual dispute with IBM. 
This is why Linux advocates need to be well informed of the current 
state of affairs. 

What will be covered is a synopsis of the key possible threats to 
Linux, and the likelihood of these threats. We will consider it from 
the perspective of SCO being proven right. We will show how/where this 
immediately affects Linux. We will show what the eventual overall 
effect this will have on Linux. 
..

This is an edited version of a talk given to the Linux Users of 
Victoria, a user group, on July 1. http://www.luv.asn.au/
