Re: BSD --> Bloody Semantics Discussion ?

Jason B. Standing <jason@jasonbstanding.com> wrote:
> How can you claim that a license imposes an exclusion on someone, and
> then claim that it doesn't force them into a course of action ?

"You are not permitted to reproduce my emails without acknowledging
the source by including my email address."  Does this force you to
reproduce my emails?  No.  Can you choose to reproduce them?  Yes;
and in which case you must include my email address.


> Your *choice* is whether or not to want to distribute your work so that
> other people can use it.
>
> Once you've decided that, it sounds like the GPL *forces* you to give away
> the source as well

It's not reproducing your work that's at question.  It's you reproducing
*my* work.  If you choose to do that you must abide by my conditions.
