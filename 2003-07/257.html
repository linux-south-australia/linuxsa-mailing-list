Hm, not sure exactly what you mean;  A lot of my wireless installs have been
sharing a PPPoE connection over a wireless connection (because location B, C,
etc can't get DSL).  The incoming connection on the wireless router (which is

difference to the wireless; if the ADSL drops out (which it sometimes does),
the wireless is fine.  It's been extremely successful.
