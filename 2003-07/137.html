> Anyone out there using a DLink 604+ ADSL 
> modem/router/NAT/DHCP/802.11b-AP ?  I'm looking for you experiences

They seem reasonable; definitely one of the better hardware AP's out there
(especially for the price!), but just like every other hardware AP I've seen,
they crash and burn and die if you do simultaneous fast, large file transfers
over it (or stream high bandwidth video).  I'm not sure whether its because it
can't handle the speed, or it can't handle all the SYN/ACK packets.
