http://ir.sco.com/ReleaseDetail.cfm?ReleaseID=114170

LINDON,  Utah, Jul 21, 2003

The SCO® Group (SCO)(Nasdaq: SCOX) today announced that it has received
U.S. copyright registrations for UNIX System V source code, a
jurisdictional pre-requisite to enforcement of its UNIX copyrights.  The
company also announced it will offer UnixWare® licenses tailored to
support run-time, binary use of Linux for all commercial users of Linux
based on kernel version 2.4.x and later.  SCO will hold harmless
commercial Linux customers that purchase a UnixWare license against any
past copyright violations, and for any future use of Linux in a run-only,
binary format.
