SCO opinion

After browsing this mornings diatribe from SCO I am
wondering if the time is approaching when the Open Source 
movement starts to go on the offensive. 
How long can this concerted attack from SCO be ignored ? 

The coordinated power of Linux users and Open Source 
movement is considerable. Maybe some of it should be
brought to bear on SCO. I am not sure what form this
should take. Others better qualified may have some
ideas. It would need to be carefully thought out.
World Wide protest action has been done before,
but it should take to form of something unique to Linux.
