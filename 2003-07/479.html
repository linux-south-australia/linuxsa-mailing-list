 > This is completely off-topic, but i thought some people here may have

 > often free speech. How do people feel about the recent censorship debate in
 > Australia? I'm thinking of three films in particular: "Baise Moi", "Ken

Ordinarily I'd be pretty narked by the whole thing, but I think in 
these cases the OFLC is pretty irrelevent.  Anyone in Australia who
wants to watch those movies can download them with eDonkey or Kazaa
whether the Australian Government approves or not.

"The Internet has made the concept of censorship obselete."  Just type
'"name of film" ed2k' into Google and go for your life.

As such, despite the abhorrence of the OFLC's position, I think they're
being pretty useful this month for the vivid illustration of just how
truly useless they really are.
