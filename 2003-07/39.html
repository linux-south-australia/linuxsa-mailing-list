Re: Linux knows too much

Andrew Galdes wrote:

> [andrew@test ~]$ echo "linux" | md5sum
> 5bb062356cddb5d2c0ef41eb2660cb06  -
> [andrew@test ~]$ echo "LiNuX" | md5sum
> c8d58815b7960cf9519f1e2cd59c04d6  -
> 
> They don't look similar to me :P

Bear in mind that is only partially how MD5 hashes are generated -
you're missing the salt, which helps prevent one 'LiNuX' password
from looking like someone elses 'LiNuX' password.

e.g.  given "$1$VeI1ftYi$yTyt9x/kYoiQs0JetXfJA/" the "$1$VeI1ftYi$"
portion is the salt, and is typically randomly generated at password
creation time.
