Re: FreeBSD - NVidia driver refresh rate problem

It's alright I solved it, I thought that the NVIDIA drivers used the EDID 
frequencies by default, but they don't. You have to set:

Option "UseEdidFreqs" "True"

And now I get perfect 85Hz :) 

Cheers,

Damien
