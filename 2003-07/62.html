Its never fair to construe a failure to treat a public mailing list 
as a sales channel to mean ANYTHING about what a company sells. They 
might just not have noticed your indirect request in lieu of simply

1300 788 233 during office hours :)

That said, since I -have- just noticed this message from you (I'm a 
busy person - I don't read LinuxSA all the time), the answer to your 
question is simple:

We have offered wireless Internet links for around four years now, at 
pricing substantially higher than ADSL (and pre-dating the existence 
of ADSL in this country), in the greater Adelaide metro area. We 
still do.

We are also in beta test on something explicitly called 'Internode 
Wireless DSL'. That beta test process is working very smoothly, and 
we do expect to release a product offering along those lines later 
this year.
