There is a small possibility that they are doing HTTP acceleration (i.e.
reverse caching) they may then be charging you for this cached data,
although it sounds highly dubious and unlikely if that is the case.  But
that would be some fantastic caching. Only ~5GB is registered on your box..
And the proxy would have to cache the remaining 7GB.......

Just another possibility.
