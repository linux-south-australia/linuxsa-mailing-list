You can use a tool such as "tar" to backup the contents of the folder(s)
containing your mail. There are two common mail formats : mbox and
maildir. I won't detail them both except to say that maildir is newer than
mbox and has some identifiable benefits over mbox.

This said, you'll find that older (i.e. less updated) mail readers may not
understand all of the mail formats. For example, Netscape 4.77's mail
reader won't understand maildir format. This isn't a problem because you
could use something like sylpheed to convert from one format to the other.

I hope this helps.
