> My atalk laserwriter has stopped working (I don't believe I've made any
> changes since it did work).
> 
> I can print using pap -p directly to the printer, but lpr -P lp (using the
> appropriate printcap) doesn't work, failing with:

> jobs queued, but cannot start daemon.
>

Is the lpd daemon running?

Otherwise 'strace' is your friend.  'strace lpr -P lp' will give you too much 
information, but it will tell you what fd it can't get a hold of.  Then you 
just need to work out how to restore that resource :)
