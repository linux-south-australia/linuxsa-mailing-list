Re: ASDL modems

> > To buy a ASDL modem from them they don't say that Linux will work with
> > a PCI or USB modem
> > but will with a Ethernet modem or router is this true

The DLINK DSL100 pci card and the Alcatel USB usb ADSL "modems" work with Linux,
however, I'd recommend getting the ethernet modems as they will:

a) work with pretty much anything that has ethernet
b) don't require YABD (yet another bloody driver)  

Or, if you look on whirlpool forums (http://www.whirlpool.net.au) you'll 
find a crowd selling an ethernet adsl router for around AUS$139.
