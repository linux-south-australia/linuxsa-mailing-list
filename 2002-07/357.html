Re: *STUPID* NT Workstation 4.0

Those numbers ring a bell.. 4GB was a limitation of earlier versions of
FAT and 8GB was tied to the 1024 cylinder issue associated with CHS bios
access. The one where you need an OS running to go past 1024 so it was 
the bootloaders that suffered most.
Its been a while since I played with this stuff, but I'd suggest you try
make a small boot partition at the front of the disk for NTldr and
hopefully you can create what ever partitions you like after that.
yeah - its a long shot! :)
