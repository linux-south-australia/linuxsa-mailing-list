Postgres and Perl::DBI

Hmmmm...

I am attempting to get a timestamp from Squid (the first field in the
access.log) into Postgres.

I have tried the following:

FIRST GO:

create table(
...
timestamp float;
)

...but DBD::Pg complains that:

"pg_atoi" fails because it can't conver the "integer" 

[but I'm not WANTING it to convert an integer]

THEN I DID:

$timestamp=~s/\.\d+$//;

...at which time it complains that:

"pg_atoi" fails because the integer is too large
