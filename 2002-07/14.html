I'm running RH7.3 and have a TNT2 installed. I've just managed to get the
nVidia open GL drivers working on my computer. Tux Racer works fine for me,
but I haven't played around with the settings. Quake 3 runs well in full
screen (I can only use it at 1024x768 as I've got more playing around to do)

quite cheap atm.
