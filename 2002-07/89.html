This might be a good tips for you to rescue physical crashed harddrives.

Some weeks ago we had a Harddrive that was crashed. The user wanted to retrieve some data from it. Although the data was not so important so the user wanted to pay $2500 to get it restored by ibas.no, anyway a colleague of me recalled a hint he was told some years before.

Put the HDD in the freezer for a couple of hours.

With doubt in my mind I did as he said. A couple of hours later i pluged in the HDD into the computer again and tested. And damn, I was suprised. The thing worked without any problems at all. We were able to retrieve every single byte from it.

Quite nice way to save bucks with. Although this might not work with all crashed HDD's but it's still worth a try before spending bucks on a expensive Data restore company.
