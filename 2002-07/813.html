Can't say, but in my experience Hewlett Packard printers are pretty well
supported under Linux (from way back, both my inkjet and laserjet
worked).  HP now have their own Linux driver project too, which is a
bonus.  AFIK, Canon are... um.. a$$holes when it comes to giving out the
tech specs on how to drive their hardware.

> 
> The overwhelming majority of my printing is black and white text on plain A4 paper, 

Have you thought about a laser? I rekon for just BW test on plain A4
they're the cheapest option (a little more expensive initially) with
toner cartridges lasting almost forever.  My inkjet printer costs me a
small fortune to run, while my laser (which does most of the printing)
costs me next to nothing.  Just hang onto your colour printer for the
few times you do print colour...

Just a few (hopefully) helpful suggestions.
