"Shonky laptops " was RE: Founder of "Enlightenment"

These el-cheapo laptops are ideal for quite a few users that want mobility
but don't need to use their laptops in remote places. There aren't many
locations that don't have a power outlet.

The promoters of these cheap laptops don't need to worry about the weight
and expense of batteries, neither do they have to worry about battery life
or processors that go into slow mode to save power.

I'm guessing that to make their price seem even better they are avoiding
paying M/S for an OEM license of Windows also which leads to a very
cheap/mobilish desktop replacement.
