well this bit's easy.  Is your login name mick or mcampbell?
Your "From" is one and your Cc the other.  Whichever one is
not your login name you need to add an alias for:
vi /etc/aliases
add (eg. "mcampbell: mick" if mick is your login name)
save and quit vi
run "newaliases"
reload or restart sendmail

Note: my reading of the above error you included implies the
message went to the destination ok but the Cc to yourself
bounced.
