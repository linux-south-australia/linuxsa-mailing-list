thumbnails, mogrify and large filesizes

Hi all,

I've been playing with automatically creating thumbnail images etc
for some photos I've got, so I found 'igal' that does the job
pretty well (once I added a small bit to the code to get round
some problem in mogrify).  So I've got thumnails and all seems ok....

But I looked at my webpage from someone else's place (via 56k modem)
the other day and it takes too long to load.  I noticed all
the thumbnails igal generated, using mogrify (part of ImageMagick),
are around 26k whereas some I created manually using xv and shrinking
the pic then saving at approx 60% quality are 5k - and look just as
good on the webpage.

No matter what options I pass to mogrify I can't get the thumbnails
smaller than 26k using mogrify - and I can't easily get xv to do
the shrinking in a script.  The results of my googling are that
everyone seems to use mogrify and no one has mentioned the large
image filesize.

What do all you people that create thumbnails do?  Got any ideas?

Dave.
