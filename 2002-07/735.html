Mick,

Not be be alarmist, but it looks like you've been "r00ted".
You'll find /sbin/nologin is a trojan that gives the user a shell (try
it - setup a new user with that as a shell and try to login, if you
get a shell rather than being dumped out, it's not a real nologin).

And yes, as for your mail problems, looks like someone is a) using
your machine as a relay or b) the cracker is using your machine as a
bot of some sort or c) both and more.


Best thing would be to save your important data, wipe the drive,
reinstall, patch and harden your machine and setup a firewall with a
default deny policy.
