Hmmm, that sounds like TWM or some such. Does it not give you a menu with 
some combination of left or middle or right mouse buttons on the root 
screen (the grey background)? How about ctrl and right/middle or shift and 
right/middle?

Failing that, there should be some files in /etc/sysconfig that control 
things like desktop, and if you look in ~/.xsession or stuff like that it 
may tell you what you window manager is. 

On my FreeBSD system with Gnome, I have, in ~/.gnome a file called 
default.wm which has WM=/usr/X11R6/bin/sawfish in it.

If you are using KDE you might find something similar. I am not sure.
