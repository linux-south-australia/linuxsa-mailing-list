"This bit" looks like it has 8 bit characters (certainly from this
end).  If you're running MD5 hashes on the passwords (most modern
Linux crypt implementations are), then you should have "$1$" as
the leading part of the password, and it should be 34 characters
long.  It looks like you're missing that initial $1$ and the salt
that follows it.
