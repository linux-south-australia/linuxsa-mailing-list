RE: Linux Hardware Drivers - How can *nux Improve?

James Leone wrote:

> So I already know that Linux has a lot of the little extra
> already, but when it comes to periferals, it seems to have
> "a little less" at least for me.

Part of the problem is that most drivers for Linux are written
by hobbyists with sometimes little information, whereas most
drivers for "other operating systems" are written by the
manufacturers themselves.  Traditionally most of the free
Unices have had this problem (Diamond Stealth video cards
from the mid 90's springs to mind).

> Wanting it to have "a little extra" I keep coming back to
> the idea of funneling money into Linux through not for
> profit organizations that would pay developers to create
> Free/Open Source software.

Typically the issue is getting the information out of the
vendors without signing non-disclosure agreements (which
don't work so well with GPL and the like).
