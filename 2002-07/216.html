Hi,
     Personally I have not had a problem with the VIA KT266A SDRAM and I
have a friend running the newer VIA KT333 DDR RAM the only issue he has
is the AC97 sound not being picked up.

We have had some issues with MS Xp on the VIA KT333 using DDR RAM not
shutting the system down properly and registering a RAM problem when
trying to reboot, The owner finally consented to trying linux (Mandrake
8.2 ) but try as we might we cannot reproduce the problem with linux.
  His AC97 sound card is recognised. His AGP geforce mx200 is recognised
his linmodem worked with patch, and with a few patches from the linux
games site his WarCraft is working (at a better frame rate than windows
gives him) Losing all her AOL and ICQ contacts but we transfered them
and she has one app (everbuddy)that lets her talk to both groups. and
her files and setting are seperate from his so her email doesn't flood
his mailbox anymore. Now he's pissed at the money he spent for XP when
it didn't work properly on his hardware (Can't "win" everything).
