Re: Getting losedow$ ttf fonts to work under linux gimp

James & Michel,

Thankyou for your helpful responses.  I have explored both suggestions and 
tried a number of options, the sum total of which brought me no closer to 
success.  Then I stumbled on the fonts option in the Mandrake Control Centre.
Oooooh I hate that. . . . right under my nose all the time.

The Path to finding the fonts options stuff can be fairly long and tortuous, 
but for anyone using Mandrake 8.2, and KDE, the path is:

K -> Configuration -> Other -> Mandrake Control Center -> System -> Fonts

Then just click "Get Windows Fonts"

The result; all fonts usable in Gimp.

I am assuming that this is all that was required and not any of the other 
things I tried before hitting the "Get Windows Fonts" button.  Just happy to 
have it going right now but if anyone else tries the above and fails to make 
those troublesome fonts available, e-mail me and I'll let you know what I did 
leading up to it.
