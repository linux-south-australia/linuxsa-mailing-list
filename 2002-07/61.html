Re: RH 7.1 IPTables? [was Re: Squid with two ports listening.]

The commands.....

chkconfig --level 345 ipchains off
chkconfig --level 35 iptables on
service ipchains stop ; rmmod ipchains
service iptables restart

...should fix your problems, but you should rerun your iptables script
or lokkit before restarting iptables. Try man chkconfig ; chkconfig
--help ; service --help

Of course this assumes your kernel has all the iptables modules support
it needs.

Alex
