Re: [OT] ISP anti-spam

On Thu, 4 Jul 2002, James Leone wrote:

> Seeing that there are costs for downloading, could that problem be
> solved by getting fetchmail to use spamassassin to not download messages
> it considers spam?

You can do this with more than one MTA... as each line of the message is
received it is checked, and if it is thought to be spam the sending MTA
receives an unconditional refusal to deliver that message. So the spam
never even enters your email system.
