In the immortal words of David Fitch <davidf@parachilna.com>...
> What do all you people that create thumbnails do?  Got any ideas?

I personally use netpbm and the jpeg libraries to create thumbnails or
just resize images in general.

/usr/local/bin/djpeg $filename | /usr/local/bin/pnmscale -xysize 150 150
| /usr/local/bin/cjpeg >
| /home/www/japancarimports.com.au/html/thumb/$imagename.jpg

but that's personal preference
