RE: Mail attachments (possibly) being corrupted

Is the sender using Outlook ?
If they are check whether the format is Rich Text format
If so change it to Plain text or at worst case HTML...cough...

Microsoft technet article talks about attachments and even emails going
missing if they are sent in RTF.

I dont have the article number here, however since removing RTF from PCs the
incidences of missing emails appears to have stopped.

BTW : Out mailserver is running Sendmail
