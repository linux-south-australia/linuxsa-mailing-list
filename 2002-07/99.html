Re: [OT] ISP anti-spam

Well, spam assasin is a program, not a person, and it still lets the
mail though, and just adds a header marking it as spam. Is this correct?
(I dont run it). So, that means that no human sees your mail, no stats
are held about you, and it just makes it easy for users to set up their
own spam filters, if they desire. If they dont, then they wont even
notice. As far as I can see, it looks like a brilliant idea, and doesnt
invade privacy.
