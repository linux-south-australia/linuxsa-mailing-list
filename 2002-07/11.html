> > What's really confusing me is that ping and traceroute -I are giving
> > different results... whyso?
> 
> ping uses ICMP ECHO_RESPONSE to solicit an ICMP ECHO_REQUEST,
> traceroute uses TTL on ICMP ECHO_REQUEST to solicit a
> ICMP TIME_EXCEEDED or *_UNREACHABLE as its response.  The machine
> you're pinging probably has ICMP ECHO_RESPONSE filtered.
