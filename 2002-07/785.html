>> I tried surfing with links and lynx but

>> constantly getting 80% packet loss still.  I really would like to get

>> ppp link  and got 0% packet loss.  Its got me beat.

Ping packets, by default, are a lot smaller than a stream of TCP bytes
coming back from the network, so that may not be an accurate test.  Try
using the -s option on ping to increase the payload size to something
more approximating your MTU size (you should be able to determine this
by "ifconfig ppp0"), and see if that generates any errors.  You may
have flow control issues between your modem and serial port that need
sorting out.
