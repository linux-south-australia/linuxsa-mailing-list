Re: Printers which will work with Linux

On Tue, 30 Jul 2002, Toby Corkindale wrote:

> Just make sure you get a decent printer though -- there are a few
> ultra-cheap laserprinters which are rather dumb and won't give you much
> joy. I think the cheap panasonics were one to avoid. Check for postscript
> capability -- if it has it, thats a good sign.

Postscript compatibility is typically expensive, and not a must, as long
as ghostscript can talk whatever your printer groks, you effectively get
a postscript printer.

PCL is OK, but don't think just because a printer does PCL it's OK -
what matters is the actual computer <=> interface, not the page
description language in use.
