Linux on the Desktop

If I were to develop a course entitled "Linux on the Desktop" which
encompassed subjects such as:

* Word processing
* Spreadsheet
* Basic drawing applications
* Presentations
* Sending and receiving e-mail

...would there be a desire for such a course? That is to say, a course
which taught the "Office Stable" but not with Microsoft Office...

I can say now it would be similarly priced to other courses (I do have
to eat), but if there were enough people asking for it, it's possible
you might get an accredited training authority [i.e. one that is
actually recognised such as the WEA, University Community Education
Programmes and such] willing to approve such a course.

My point is this: you might ASK for such a course in schools,
universities and other learning institutions, but are you, as a student,
willing to take the courses?
