Re: RedHat 7.3 logging out when unnattended?

Quoting Richard Russell <richard@yellowgoanna.com>:

> Hi all,
> 
> I have a confusing problem with two RH 7.3 desktop machines that I
> administer. When the users are not using them for more than an hour or
> so (I don't know if it's a regular amount of time, or random), they
> get logged out. AFAIK, there is no auto-logout facility that has been
> enabled...

Check to see if XScreensaver is running, especially the OpenGL screensavers. 
The GL screensavers may be kicking in, dying horribly, and bringing down the 
user's X session.

What sort of video card and which version of X are you running? Do you know
if DRI and AGPart are enabled/compiled in?

(note: If it's the screensavers causing the problem, it's more likely a bug
 in the X server or OpenGL driver code than the screensaver itself)
