> Why don't you just use IMAP?

Even with IMAP, annoyingly, evolution wants to keep a load of stuff in
the ~/evolution directory, including the content of all the messages.

I have evolution running from a linux box, which NFS mounts /home from
another server (which happens to also be the IMAP server, not that it
matters), and evolution can deal with this without problem.

But anyway, most anything will work fine with an NFS mounted home
directory, letting you back up your users stuff without you having to
worry much about individual machines.
