Re: strange ftp problems [solved]

Ok I finally got to the bottom of this. I hadnt posted all the info
about our TCP/IP configuration, but it all came down to a certain "tcp
redirect" command which works on most machines, but for some reason the
machine in question (client) was forgetting its newly discovered route
and sending its packets to the wrong place soon after establishing a
succesfull connection, and getting an icmp port filtered back from an
unrelated firewall, which it was ignoreing and consiquently masking the
real problem. Whew! 

Ant
