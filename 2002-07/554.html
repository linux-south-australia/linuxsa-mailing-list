Re: traceroute v tracert

Which one did you do first?

The first computer (linux or win95) has to wait for the DNS lookups to 
happen.  The second one has the advantage of the fact that they're cached.

If you do them without dns lookups: (traceroute -n on linux, tracert -d on 
win32) then you'll find they're pretty much line ball.
