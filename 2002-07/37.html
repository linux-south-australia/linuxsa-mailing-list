Re: tar - broken pipe

Wayne Simes wrote:

> This error happens when it is creating a new archive, I think it gets about
> 2/3 of the way through, which I think is about 5gb out of the 7 it has to
> do.

OK, it wasn't clear from your original email whether you were creating
or extracting.  You may be hitting a file size limit, which is either
two or 4 gig (I don't know off the top of my head, and can't test it
as I don't have that much free space currently).  Try executing
"dd if=/dev/zero of=/tmp/test.file bs=1m count=5k" (where /tmp is the
appropriate destination file system) and see if that runs to completion.
If it breaks at the 2 or 4 gig mark, then your file system can't handle
large files.
