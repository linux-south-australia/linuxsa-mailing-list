I'm guessing that it doesn't like sharing space with ipchains. rmmod ipchains 
first, then try running iptables commands.

There's a wrapper available which will convert ipchains commands to iptables 
format. I'm pretty sure that it's distributed with RedHat 7.1.

As it's a live server, your best bet is to muck about with iptables on another 
machine until you feel comfortable with it, and then start messing with 
firewalls on a production machine. You may want to do this sort of thing after 
hours or during a scheduled downtime period.
