Linux v Windows struggle.

With the caveat that this endeavor is not as sireous as the one taken by 
Kennedy and of course not in any way nationalistic.... 

I see a parallel between the 1960's space race (Soviets v Americans) and 
the 2000 desktop race (Windows v Linux).  Kennedy speaks of scientific 
advancement  - I see the progression of the Linux operating system. He 
proclaims - "We choose to go to the Moon!"  - and for some reason I feel 
that the answer "I choose to use Linux on my desktop!" is justified. He 
speaks of allocating resources towards the space program,  I see myself 
doing the same to learn Linux. I see the whole drama as Man's endeavor 
to explore, aquire knowledge and innovate - but most of all work on 
behalf for the betterment of mankind as a whole.
