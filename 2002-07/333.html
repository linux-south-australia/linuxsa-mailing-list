Re: [Kinda OT] Corporate Backup Systems

We went the cheap and easy route, so its probably not for you.  We 
acquired an HP Netserver LXE Pro 200 (c19/98-Dual Pentium Pro 512MB RAM) 
from an auction for $385US. It happens to have 18GB useable storage
plus 4 GB for the system, so we popped in Mandrake Linux and Samba ($40 
US) and copy the contents of one of our servers from a Windows 
workstation over night.
