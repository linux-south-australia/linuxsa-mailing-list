Looks very much like it.  You'll probably find it'll start making clunking
noises and freeze up your system again.  You might want to think about
replacing any other Quantum Fireball drives you have too (in the 6 to 13gig
range)

And if any of them are under warranty, be warned - they get shipped to
Singapore, where they are "refurbished" (by Maxtor, who bought out Quantum).
You'll receive a replacement drive in about 6-7 weeks which will die within
the first couple of hours of use.

Imagine my excitement when they sent me a 20gig drive to replace my 13.
It's been back twice now, though, and it still doesn't work.  We're working
on having Quantum drives replaced with new drives from Maxtor.
