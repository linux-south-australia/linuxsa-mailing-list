Re: SCSI

On Fri, Jul 05, 2002 at 01:03:42PM +0930, denver wrote:

> I have installed Linux and it has recognised my burner as a SCSI 
> instead of an IDE burner so does anyone know how i can make it 
> recognise my burner as an IDE type?

It's recognised as a SCSI device because of the ide-scsi module, which
provides SCSI emulation for IDE devices.

Programs like cdrecord depend on SCSI to work correctly.

Don't fret -- that's the way it's meant to be.
