Re: test

To manually start|stop|restart|status various services under redhat use
(as root) the service command. Like "service sendmail restart" or do
service --help for syntax and service --status-all for the full list.

You can see a list of which services are started at what run level with
chkconfig --list and man chkconfig will provide more useful information.
Read the top of /etc/inittab for an explanation of redhat's run levels.

Hope this helps.
