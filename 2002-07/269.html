Re: Server Config Report

On Tue, Jul 09, 2002 at 01:41:52PM +0930, Patrick Browne wrote:

> Is there a command that produces a server configuration report
> (hardware/software) and dumps it to a text file?
> 
> Something similar to the Netware "Load Config" that produces a 50+ page
> report.

Can't think of anything off the top of my head that does this all, but
I'd suggest a couple of things:

* dmesg(8)

* rpm(8)

* /proc

* freshmeat.net

dmesg will give you the hardware information, as will /proc, rpm can
give you the installed packages (search for each application's
configuration manually). freshmeat.net may have something to offer in
terms of an application that does everything.
