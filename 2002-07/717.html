David wrote:
> Has anyone seen any really weird behaviour with RealTek 8029 based

Not sure if this qualifies as "really weird", but mine would not work at all
until it had been on for about ten minutes, thereafter it'd work perfectly.

suspect a fault, and it diagnosed a faulty chip on the card. A replacement
card fixed the problem.
