AUUG SA needs *you*

The elections for the AUUG will be in a week's time, at IBM's facility
in Greenhill Road.  To remind you, there are five positions to be
elected: President, Secretary, Treasurer and two ordinary committee
members.  As of now, I have a total of four nominations, one each for
President, Secretary, Treasurer and ordinary committee member.

According to the statutes, if we don't get any further nominations,
all the candidates will be elected automatically, we won't need an
election, and we'll be one member short.  I don't like that.

Please consider nominating yourself for one of the positions.  To be
nominated or to vote, you must be an AUUG member.  See
http://www.auug.org.au/info/#join for further details.  You may join
on the evening if you wish.

I look forward to a good turnout next week.

Greg Lehey
Returning Officer, AUUG SA chapter
