Steve,
I have a port forward for a cvs server running on the local network, it was
a two part command, one to forward and one to allow the connection to the
port on the ext machine;

$IPTABLES -A FORWARD -i $INET_IFACE -o $LAN_IFACE -p tcp --dport 2401 -m
state --state NEW,ESTABLISHED,RELATED -j ACCEPT

$IPTABLES -t nat -A PREROUTING -p tcp -i $INET_IFACE --dport 2401 -j
DNAT --to-destination 192.168.1.24:2401


It took me a while to work it out and had similar problems to what you
describe (but thats due to my lack of knowledge at the time!)
see if you can change yours to similar to this.
