Linux and Ericsson R380

I've got an Ericsson R380 cellphone. It's quite a nice little beast
that does everything from WAP to calendaring and e-mail.

Rather than make rather expensive data calls to pull down my e-mail,
it's possible (I've done it) to sit the phone in the cradle and plug
it into your PC and pull e-mail out of your regular client.

The only problem is, your "regular client" must be Outlook. Not happy,
Jan.

It'd be really nice to be able to flag e-mail messages in Mutt and
then have an application periodically sync my phone and e-mail.

I've not been able to find anything on the Interweb so far that'd
suggest that someone's been able to do this (or something similar).

Does anyone know if this is achievable?
