Re: RH 7.3 and TrueType problem

On Mon, 2002-07-01 at 20:57, Michael Davies wrote:
> 
> If someone out there has a relatively virgin Red Hat 7.3, would you mind
> verifying a Red Hat 7.3 problem for me?
> 
> Can you cd into a directory with some TrueType fonts and try running
> "ttmkfdir -d . -o fonts.scale".  I get an "Aborted".  Can anyone confirm
> that they get the same?
 
After a little more investigation I found out that I didn't stuff up.  I
have several ttf fonts (10 out of the 810 fonts that follow me around)
that cause ttmkfdir (under Red Hat 7.3) to break.  Remove them and
everything is dandy.  Very strange - never had this problem before (on
other versions of Red Hat, other Linuxes and even other unices).

strace(1) is your friend in situations like this.
