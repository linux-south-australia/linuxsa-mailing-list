GNOME 2.0 initial thoughts

My initial thoughts on GNOME 2.0

Executive Summary:  Great step forward, but this release ain't for
everyone.

Detailed Stuff: I downloaded and compiled from source the GNOME 2.0
release.  Very easy to do, configure --prefix=/opt/gnome2; make; make
install.  The biggest problem is >70 packages.  First sign:  Nice new
gdm login, nice and configurable.  Nice.  Setting GDK_USE_XFT=1 in
~/.bash_profile gives me anti-aliased fonts everywhere on the desktop. 
Cool.  TTF fonts available everywhere on your desktop if your font
server can see them (see my post last week :-(  Consistency on the
dektop is really very nice.  Firing up the graphical desktop shell,
nautilus, is significantly quicker.  Nice new icons everywhere.  Tabbed
gnome-terminal - more usable than I'd have thought.
