Greetings,
I haven't tried the board under Linux, but I found running it under
OS/2, unless you loaded sound drivers, it seemed to want to use IRQ7 for
the sound card. I suspect this is what is happening unders Linux.
It is a CMI8330 chipset on these motherboards:- never believe the "SB16
compatability" claim.

Good Luck!
