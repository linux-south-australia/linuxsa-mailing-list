Re: Kernel for Beginners - VFAT???

Hi Big Fellow,

Sounds to me like you haven't actually told your machine to use the new kernel
(getting the kernel configured and compiled is the harder part ! - well done)

Section 4.4 of the Kernel HOWTO specifices how to tell the LILO boot loader to
use your new kernel.

To re-enforce the warning in section 4.4, ensure that you configure LILO to
give you the option of booting your old Kernel, just in case your new kernel
fails.

HTH,
Mark.
