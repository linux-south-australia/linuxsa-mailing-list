Star Office 5

Hi All,
    Just thought I'd let you know about star office 5.
It now reads word97, excel97 & access 97 stuff. Formatting of word 97
docts still isn't excat to ms standards but very good anyway. It is a
huge improvement over the last version. It now uses the glibc libraries
so on a redhat 5.X system it is MUCH more stable (I've not been able to
crash this version as yet). Anyone who wants it but isn't willing to
download 64MB can get it at the confiig/install fest when it happens.
