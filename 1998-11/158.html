Re: Extended or Logical? (fwd)

On Mon, 9 Nov 1998, Big Fellow wrote:

> I've got two harddrives:
> 
> 1. has three partition, all DOS, so that the last two are "extended
> partition"

Okay...
Your first hard drive is probably arranged as such:

/dev/hda1 :	Primary (First Dos Drive - prolly C: in win)
/dev/hda2 :	Extended
/dev/hda5 :	|    Logical Partition 1 (Second Dos drive - D:)
/dev/hda6 :     |    Logical Partition 2 (Third Dos drive - E:)
	  
The last two dos drives are inside the Extended Partition. They aren't
extended partitions themselves. As shown above, they're Logical
Partitions.

What you're most probably trying to do is mount the extended partition as
whole, which is A Bad Idea (tm). /dev/hda2 encompasses both /dev/hda5 and
/dev/hda6. Therefore, mount /dev/hda5 and /dev/hda6 instead. :)

Somebody shoot me if I'm wrong,
Michael.
