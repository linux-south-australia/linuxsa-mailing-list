Some SO50 statistics

For those interested. On my P233MMX system with 32MB of RAM, RedHat 5.2,
Star Office takes just over 50 seconds to startup (startup meaning
displays the StarOffice desktop thing).

Then double clicking to start a new word processer document takes
approximately another 40 seconds.

This is pretty bloated - but that's by far my major quibble with it so
far. Apart from that it seems pretty nice.
