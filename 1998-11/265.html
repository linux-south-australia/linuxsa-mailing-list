Take a look at the ISP-Hookup HOWTO.  Assuming you installed the HOWTOs

> I have followed the directions in the installation guide for setting
> up ppp, but somehow I think I may have stuffed things up or have
> missed something.

I expect the problem is that it's not using PAP to send your username
and password.  If you used Redhat's Network Configurator (I'm not sure
if they still use it in 5.2):
Click the Interfaces button at the top of the window
Select the ppp device (probably ppp0)
Click the Edit Button
Click the PAP button
Your username and password should be displayed in the box.  If they're
not, click the insert button, if that doesn't work, try the append
button.  If they were there, you already had it set up properly and
there's some other problem.
Type your username in the 'send username' box.
Type your username in the 'expect' box, and your password in the 'send'
box and click done.
