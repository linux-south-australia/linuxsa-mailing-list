RE: linuxSA business lunch?

Hi all,

This sounds like a great idea, but there is one small problem.

The SA Government, with the exception of Work Cover, have three
main forces in effect that would stop them from taking part.

1) SA government signed a 20 year Microsoft contract for all
workstations and servers.

2) SA government signed a Microsoft exclusive agreement that
expressly forbids the use of any products not from MS.
* There are some small exceptions to this.

3) EDS run the show and their official line is MS all the way.

This goes to the point of being unable to install netscape
without being sued! And yes MS do audit government machines
regularly.

I supported the only two Linux boxes in the government (that I am
aware of) with the help of NetCraft and let's just say...
Their days are numbered...

The government is committed to NT all the way and then some.

I would like to see them change but I don't think it is legally possible
until the MS and EDS contracts are removed and don't expect that
to happen in your working lifetime. :(

But don't worry their is a whole world of corporate opportunity out
their... And your catch phrase could be...
"The government doesn't use it so, it must be good." :)

*climbs off soap box*

Nick Stock
