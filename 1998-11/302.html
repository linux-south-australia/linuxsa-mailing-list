StarOffice mirror in Australia

Folks,
	A lot of people have been talking about mirrors of StarOffice.
I've just found out that mirror.aarnet have an official mirror of the
Linux version and are currently working to get the other operating system
releases mirrored (WinNT, Solaris sparc/intel and OS/2).

The Linux version is available from:
	ftp://mirror.aarnet.edu.au/pub/linux/staroffice/
	http://mirror.aarnet.edu.au/linux/staroffice/
as:
	so50_01.tar  (66017280 bytes, 62M file)

The administrators also said:
	Please note, StarOffice are pretty strict about having to be an
	official mirror of their software, and explicitly refuse people
	the right to `mirror' from downstream mirrors unless you have
	permission `in writing'.

regards,
