Microsoft serial mouse

I use a Microsoft serial mouse  (2.0A) in X Windows under RHL 5.1.
About once a week  the mouse freezes and I have to cold reboot  the
system. Sometimes I can get the system back, other times I need to do a
complete reinstall.

The RH manual indicates that the 2 button Microsoft Mouse of version
2.1A or greater is a "smart" mouse and speculates that when it doesn't
work it is looking for wakeup signals from Windows or it will not
respond to the system.

Notwithstanding the above version differences I gave one of the
suggested fixes a try viz.,  running gpm-1.13 as gpm -t  pnp -R and
configuring etc/X11/X86conf for MouseSystems Protocol with /dev/gpmdata
as the device. I didn't work.  My system crashed an I had to do a
complete reinstall of 5.1


If anyone has experienced a similar problem and managed a fix I would
surely like to hear from you to rectify this time consuming problem.
