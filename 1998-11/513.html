Re: video modes

On Tue, 24 Nov 1998, Darryl Ross wrote:

> Under dos you can change the default 25 lines a screen to 40 or 50. Is
> it possible to do that under RH5.2?

Add "vga=ask" to your lilo prompt and see what comes up. Once you know
what mode you like you can add "vga=NUM" to your lilo.conf.
