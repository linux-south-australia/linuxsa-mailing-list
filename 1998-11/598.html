Re: X Windows / monitor failure

Have you opened your box? The last time this happened to me, it was a
failure on the IDE0 cable (the one that connects the first harddrive to the
motherboard). If it happens only sometimes, chances are that either somthing
is on the blink or not plugged in properly...
