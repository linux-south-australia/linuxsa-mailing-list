> $ vi /etc/exports

It may be instructive to look at how exportfs does its job:

$ file `which exportfs`
/usr/sbin/exportfs: Bourne shell script text
$ cat /usr/sbin/exportfs
[...]
killall -HUP rpc.nfsd
killall -HUP rpc.mountd
