Oops, just re read it :). The patches take the form patch-VERSION where
the VERSION is the version to upgrade to. So you apply patch-2.0.36 to a
2.0.35 source tree. If you want kernel 2.0.36 you either download
linux-2.0.36 or patch-2.0.36, not both.
