Re: NT bashing

I disagree :-)

I wrote an article in response to a slashdot feature on this:
	Why easy Linux is a good thing
	http://www-personal.monash.edu.au/~nate/easylinux.html

My essential tenant is that using computers should _not_ be a skill.
It should be something I can do without understanding them at all.
I don't need to learn how my car processes fuel in order to drive, I
just turn the key, and go.  The fact that I'm using a computer should
be essentially transparent to me -- I am not using the computer for the
sake of it, I am using the computer to get a task done.  I am interested
in the task (the end) and not the means (the computer).

(This, of course, avoids the issue of geekery, which _likes_ to get
distracted with the means ;-)
