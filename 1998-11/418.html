Re: Book recommendation..

> As I'm a newbie to Linux, what book would you recommend me to get which
> would take me gently through Linux installation and setting up a small
> network?
> 
> Is there a preferred distribution to use?

RedHat is definetely your best choice if you're a newbie, and the
documents that come with the RH boxed set should get you on the right
track. If that's not enough, download and print the Installation and
Getting Started Guide, which is part of the Linux Documentation Project,
and can be found at http://sunsite.unc.edu/LDP.
