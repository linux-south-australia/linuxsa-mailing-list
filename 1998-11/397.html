I think you've all missed the point. I use Netscape for my mail, sometimes
under Linux and sometimes under Win95. Normally all my lines are wrapped,
however every now and then a message comes through that is not wrapped. I
haven't looked closely because I didn't care, but as so much energy has been
expended on this issue let me speculate...

know what Pine responds to but I'll bet it has a similar if not identical
problem.
