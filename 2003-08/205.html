[OT] Face The IT Media Forum

 IT JOURNO/Face The IT Media Forum

 The IT Journo Forum will bring together more than 50 of
 Australia's most influential IT journalists to listen to and
 question dozens of the industry's key vendors and service
 providers over two and a half days in an off-site conference
 atmosphere.

 When:  14 to 16 September
 Where:  Novotel Twin Waters Resort, Qld Sunshine Coast

 The aim of the Forum is to help companies generate media
 coverage and awareness of their positioning and strategies
 in key market segments.  The Forum will be run as a series
 of panel sessions, moderated by industry analysts and will
 cover:

 * Enterprise Computing - with sessions on Business Software
 (ERP/CRM etc), Web Services, Web Services, Enterprise
 Storage, Outsourcing

 * Personal Computing - with sessions on Broadband, Open
 Source Software, Next Generation Mobiles, Wireless, The
 Direction of Personal Computers

 If your company is positioning itself as a leading,
 innovative company in one of these fields and you are
 willing to go head to head with other market leaders within
 your field in front of the countries leading IT Journalists
 then this event might be of interest to you.

 The cost to attend the forum is $6,000, including travel and
 accommodation, a spot on the relevant panel, opportunity for
 one-on-one interviews with leading IT journalists and the
 ability to distribute materials to all attendee's.

 For more information, contact the event organisers - call
 Glen Myles from MediaConnect on 02 9439 8511.
