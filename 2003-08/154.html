"Propietary" isn't a useful word.

PDF is published, can be implemented by a third party,
but is still not a free software specification.

  "published"
    <http://partners.adobe.com/asn/tech/pdf/specifications.jsp>;

  "can be implemented by a third party"
    <http://partners.adobe.com/asn/developer/legalnotices.jsp>;
    "...Adobe desires to promote the use of PDF for information
    interchange among diverse products and applications.
    Accordingly, the following patents are licensed on a
    royalty-free, non-exclusive basis for the term of each
    patent and for the sole purpose of developing software
    that produces, consumes, and interprets PDF files that
    are compliant with the Specification..."

  "not a free software specification"
    You cannot take the PDF specification and alter it for
    your own purposes, as then the patents are not licensed
    to you.
