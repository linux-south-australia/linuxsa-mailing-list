Re: Dual monitors

On Friday, 29 August 2003 at 14:55:10 +0930, Simon Lindsay wrote:
>
> Hi All,
>
> I've just been mucking around with dual monitors, and got it going with
> the Geforce and a s3 trio 64 i had lying around.
>
> Trouble is, the trio can only do poxy resolutions ;-( I just cant handle
> 640x480x8 next to 1280x1024x16
>
> Is there some way to get the onboard and the geforce to work
> together?

As others said, no.  You can only have one AGP display, but you can
have multiple PCI displays.  You should be able to increase the
resolution of the S3 card without great difficulty, assuming it has
enough memory.  Take a look at http://www.lemis.com/grog/hardware.html
for some examples.
