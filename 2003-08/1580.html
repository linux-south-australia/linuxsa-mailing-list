ISP's running Jabber Servers

Hi,

Does anybody know of any ISP's that run a Jabber server as part of the service 
to their clients? The way I think about it is Jabber is an open transport 
protocol for Instant Messaging and should be encouraged as a standard, just 
as POP/SMTP is for email. I would love for my email address to double as my 
Jabber ID (bonus for spammers though :( but it's easy to block messages from 
people not on your contact list).
