Hi David,

I believe linux looks in /lib/modules/`uname -r` for modules for the
running kernel. You could probably change this in /etc/modules.conf or,
as you suggest, by changing the name of the running kernel. Of course
this won't help you if the modules were compiled using a different
kernel-source as the symbols might not match. But you'll soon find out
when 'depmod -a' starts complaining :-)
