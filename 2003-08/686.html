David,

     You have to put the following into your boot loader(lilo example):
append="rootflags=data=writeback"

I don't know how to do it with any other boot loader so if you use grub 
or something like that just duplicate that info into grub.  The problem 
is that you have to tell the kernel on boot what data option you are 
using.  The default is "data=ordered" and therefore until you tell it 
something different it won't do it.
