RE: mail not bouncing problem

OK, thanks to a number of people who helped off-list, I think I know what's
going on...

The domain is still delegated, so still has NS's assigned to it. People
sending mail to richard@yellowgoanna.com.au are finding that their mail
servers are querying a 150. address that I once had. This address may or may
not even exist any more, and if it does, won't be listening on port 53.
Packets are most likely being dropped into the drip tray. So, mail servers
are trying to deliver the mail, but for some reason that I don't understand,
are not timing out correctly, and are only eventually failing with an
undeliverable message. ...

Anyway, I'm asking Melbourne IT to undelegate, and we'll see if that
helps...
