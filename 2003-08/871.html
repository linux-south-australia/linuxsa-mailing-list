I'm guessing your Uni Inbox has been full too frequently, so the mailer
daemon is getting annoyed at not being able to send you. So it removes you
from the list.
Your UniSA exchange system would more than likey have a receive quota, a
send quota and a send/receive quota. Once you reach each quota you wont be
able to do those tasks. Without knowing much detail, I suggest you ensure
your mailbox always has a few MB free.
