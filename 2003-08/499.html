Why do it in a Red Hat 8 specific way? :-)

Under tcsh :

foreach i ( filename-pattern )
mv $i `echo $i | tr "[:upper:]" "[:lower:]"`
end

I'm sure you can adapt that for recursive directories, or for bash.
