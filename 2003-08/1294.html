Re: 57 ICMP/hour/IP-address - ideas on cause?

If you can get your hands on an old p200 (or even a p100) with around 32MB RAM 
and > 200MB disk space, you might wanna try running your box behind a 
dedicated firewall, especially if you're using windows. I recommend ipcop:

www.ipcop.org

It's a linux mini-distro that installs in about 5 minutes and can be 
configured in about 2 minutes, seriously (assuming it works on your 
hardware/network card, etc). I've downloaded the 3rd patch to 1.3.0 and it's 
so amazingly stable and reliable for me. It also means that you can boot into 
various OS's on your desktop computer without having to reconnect to the net.

Cheers,
