Kevin,

    Unless I misunderstand the question, all you have to do is change
your default route to the appropriate interface.  Whatever your
satellite interface is (eth0, eth1, eth2 ... etc), just make it the
default for your routing.

`man ifconfig` is another way to get what you want.
