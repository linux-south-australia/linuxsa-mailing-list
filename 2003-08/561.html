Re: hard drives previously linux-lilo or grub wont win 98

On Sat, Aug 09, 2003 at 04:53:12AM +0930, Luke Vanderfluit wrote:
> Hi,
> I have a couple of hard drives 1.5, 2.5, 4.3 GiG, I want to install win
> 98 on them for my kids' pc.
> Win 98 apparently finds it very hard to erase the entries that LILO or
> GRUB have made on the first sector of the hard drives.
> 
> Even after a BIOS low level format on all 3 drives, then formatting the
> drives with win 98 format.com no luck with a w98 install.
> 
> The drives check out OK with scandisk
> 
> Any clues on how to get virginalise the hard drives?

  dd if=/dev/zero of=/dev/hd(whatever) bs=512 count=1

Double-check the value of (whatever) to make sure you don't silently
trash the partition table on a wanted drive...
