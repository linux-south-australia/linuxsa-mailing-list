RE: Looking for Abit BP6 motherboard, possibly on loan

There should be 2 VRM (Voltage Regulator Modules) they keep the power levels
to the VTT accurate (1.10V or 3.1V off hand) . They are located amongst a
group of largish Capacitors below and to the left of the left CPU.
There are how-to's on the web to replace these components (very simple and
cheep cheep) the VTT voltage severly effects the stability of these boards,
although possibly not to the levels you describe.
It would be probably be worth the 30 mins it would take to do these 2 mods,
you have nothing to loose other than some of your time and a couple bucks
worth of bits.
When www.bp6.com is back up, they have excellent guides on how to do this.
