I suppose it would be possible to get NTLM only proxies, and I would 
presume they'd be a Microsoft offering, but the question lies, why 
would you want one ?

NTLM isn't really a particularly nice way to do authentication. Far 
better would be to use LDAP or RADIUS, modules for each exist for Squid 
and most commercial proxies also support this form of external 
authentication.
