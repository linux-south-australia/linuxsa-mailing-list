But you said, "updating ports without having CVSup looks horridly
complex". Which is irrelevant, since the answer is "don't do that, just 
install cvsup".

Some people might not want to care about keeping their ports up to date. 
Some people might want the box in a pure firewall role. In their case, 
they won't even have installed the ports collection at all.

sysinstall, or from the ports collection, or via pkg_add -r.
