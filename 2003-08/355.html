Black and White Printer Filter

I have a Richo AP3800C Color Laser attached to the network. It's a
rather nice beast, and I intend to use it to replace the existing
inkjet/laser combination, so I only have the one printer to deal with.

The beauty of having the two separate printers is that, from an
accounting point-of-view, it's easy to keep track of who's printing B&W
pages, and who's printing off in color.

It's a feature that I really don't want to lose. What I thought I could
do is setup a separate queue, and somehow running the incoming data
through some sort of filter program that converted the document into a
black-and-white equivalent.

Now, I initially thought that I'd just setup the second queue and set
(on the workstations) the printer to print the document in B&W, but that
is easily changed and worked around.

Does anyone know how I might go about doing this? Does anyone know of a
filter-type program that'd do this?
