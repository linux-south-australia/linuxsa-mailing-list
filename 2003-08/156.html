Re: Mounting Windows partition

On Mon, Aug 04, 2003 at 08:52:02AM +0930, Vern Wingard wrote:
> Hi, i would like to know how to mount my windows partition /dev/hda1  on 
> my Libranet 2.8 (Debian Sarge) partition which is /dev/hda3 ?

That's not quite how it works...

To get at your windoze partition from Linux you can mount it on /mnt
by using

  mount /dev/hda1 /mnt -t vfat
