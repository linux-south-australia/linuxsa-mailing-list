[NEWS] US Navy buys Linux on Apple kit

US Navy buys Linux on Apple kit

Apple Computer has scored its biggest ever sale of Xserve
 servers - thanks to Linux. 

 Terra Soft Solutions has announced that Lockheed Martin will
 buy 260 Xserve servers running its Yellow Dog Linux, with
 the eventual destination being the US Navy's submarines. On
 board clusters of the Apple rack server will be used for
 real-time image processing. ("Captain - it looks like a
 giant octopus") The deal is worth $1.9 million in hardware
 alone, Terra Soft founder Kai Staats told us today.
