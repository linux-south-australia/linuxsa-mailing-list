On Thu, Aug 28, 2003 at 10:59:06AM +0930, Justin Hawkins said:
> David Lloyd wrote:
> 
> >Define "grosser".
> 
> Although I've long come to realize that the FreeBSD ports system is far 
> superior to any other package management system I've used, it can 
> perhaps be nicely summed up by my experience last night.
> 
> I obtained a new digital camera. Support for this camera has only 
> recently become available in gphoto. On my freebsd system I pkg_deleted 
> my existing gphoto packages, and did a make install in 
> /usr/ports/graphics/gphoto2. (If I was more pro-active in keeping my 
> installed packages up to date then even this wouldn't have been necessary).

If you had installed portupgrade, you need only type "portupgrade gphoto"
and it does all that for you!  It also installs 'portinstall' which you can
use to install ports from anywhere on your system, eg from
/usr/local/share/swat/using_samba/gifs you could
type "portinstall gphoto" and away it goes.
