Is SCO MS/Sun backed?

Hi all,

Recently I've read that the SCO lawsuit is funded or backed not only by MS,
but also by Sun (http://www.theinquirer.net/?article=11168, among others)...
Now both MS and Sun are favourite targets for Linuxistas, being Evil
Megacorporations Plotting Against Linux, but is this accusation true? It
sounds to me a lot like the counter-accusation, that IBM is funding the ESRs
and RedHats and whoever else of the world. As far as I know, MS owns SCO
stock (I don't know how Sun is linked), but that's the extent of it.
Certainly sounds like less of an influence than IBM's billion dollar Linux
investment (not just the $, but the credibility). What confuses me further
is why Sun would be implicated in this -- aren't they trying to get in on
the Linux market these days?

Hmm...
