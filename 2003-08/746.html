Hi

For lots of things computer & Linux you'll find that www.google.com.au
is your friend. Enter the modem brand or model number and linux and
search. Hopefully you'll find something about someone having done
something with your modem and linux (try with all hardware). The
distribution webpages or package webpages will tell you about
compatibilities also.

Try to give as much info as you can when you post. If you get an error
message, find out what you can first ie search google, then ask the
list. Tell us what you've done yourself, what the error message is and
the piece of hardware/software it's referring to (if relevant).

Friday nights some meet at David Lloyd's (for food, drink and Linux) so
if you want to join us, ask David.

Robyn
