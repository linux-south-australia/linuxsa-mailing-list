> Jonathan is right, you'll need to open up more than just port 21.  Which

I haven't got a RedHat 8.0 system lying about but it's highly unlikely that
their firewall rules would kill a local connection. I would hazard a guess
that the firewall rules would go something along the lines of:

 * iptables -s localhost -j ACCEPT
 * iptables -i lo -j ACCEPT

(i.e. accept ANYTHING that happens to come from localhost and ANYTHING that
happens to come over the loopback interface).
