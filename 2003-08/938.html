US Power Outages and MSBlaster

Wow.

CNN reported that the power blackout may have been a result of
the latest worm doing the rounds.  The cynic in me quickly
poo-pooed that idea as media sensationalism.  Then someone on
Bugtraq reported that quite a few of the power stations in that
region use a product called OPC for SCADA control, which stands
for "OLE for Process Control", which uses DCOM, which runs on
Microsoft's RPC, which the aforementioned worm happens to attack.

Uh-oh.

This could be a tremendous black eye if this is substantiated.
