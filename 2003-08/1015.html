Re: How to refine "locate"

Hi Brian,

On Tue, 19 Aug 2003 06:30 am, Brian Marr wrote:
> Can anyone advise how to refine the "locate" command so that it
>
> a) leaves out the kernel source files from its search.
> b) leaves out the files on a neighboring hard drive.
>
did you want this for _ALL_ searches or a particular search?

updatedb --prunepaths ' /usr/src/ /mount/point/of/fs/ 
/anything/else/to/be/excluded/'

man updatedb for further instructions, you can also prune filesystems, define 
paths etc.

locate just searches within the database created

> My screen is full of search results I do not need.
>
> Brian Marr
