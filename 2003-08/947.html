At 2003/8/16 12:30+0930  Martin Sandiford writes:
> Does anyone here have any experience with Mini-ITX fanless motherboards
> and free O/S (I'm thinking Linux or *BSD here)?  I don't have any

I have the V5000 which is fanless. It only has one ethernet interface
so you'd have to add another network card. It runs Linux with no
problems. Don't expect great performance out of it, but its perfect
for situations where you need resonable performance and you want a
machine that makes no noise at all.
