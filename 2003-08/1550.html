Re: Dual monitors

Simon Lindsay <simon@dirtbike.ws> wrote on 08/29/2003 03:06:01 PM:

> Anybody care to offer opinions on a good value dual head card?

Have a look for a GeForce4 with DVI connector, just make sure it has a DVI
to VGA dongle in the box.   The GF4 cards have dual on board DAC so the
image quality from either port is the same.  I've had a couple of Matrox
cards but never been impressed with the quality of the Second port (450 &
550).  I've seen GF4 MX440's for around $99-$140....
