Re: Mounting Windows partition

My fstab has 2 entries for windows as I have a fat and a NTFS partition:

/dev/hda1 /mnt/nt ntfs iocharset=iso8859-1,ro,umask=0 0 0
/dev/hda5 /mnt/windows vfat iocharset=iso8859-1,codepage=850,umask=0 0 0

adapt to your /etc/fstab as you like.
