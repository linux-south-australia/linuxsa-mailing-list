The Privacy Act says what companies (i.e., people who aren't you!) can do
with respect to collecting, storing and distributing personal information.

Other Acts specify who can intercept and record telephone calls.

Then there are state laws associated with recording phone calls.

Last time I checked, in SA it was legal for a private individual to
record a telephone call provided that -one- of the parties to the call
knew it was being recorded.

That means you can record your own phone calls without telling the 
other party that you're recording them.

If you're planning to collect evidence, you really should consult
with a lawyer instead of the LinuxSA mailing list!
