I've just installed RedHat 9 on a nx9005 (not sure how similar this is),
and it's a bit of a pain, but nothing that can't be overcome with a bit
of hacking..

The biggest problem you'll have is that the keyboard disappears as soon
as you boot. You need to set the USB Legacy support to 'off' in the
BIOS.

After that, it's just drivers. I found most of the answers on
http://www.consultmatt.co.uk/nx9005.php
