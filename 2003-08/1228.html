    After having this argument with my ISP on numerous occasions (Telstra -
don't ask), I am repeatedly told that they are a content provider, and that

another ISP as I am really getting jack of this. Other ISPs WILL block this
stuff at the border, I even have it in writing from a couple, but Telstra -
No.

and so every time this happens, they make a packet. I am still getting

service than I am paying a premium for.
