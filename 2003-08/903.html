Re: mail/firewall gateway for extended power outages?

Few things to add:

if I was going to do this (and I have considered it) - I'd probably want 
to have the following features:
- at least 2 pcmcia or one pcmcia and usb [preferably 2 usb buses]
- this gives you a wireless card in one slot and extra ethernet or 
modem/adsl off a USB.  If you only have PCMCIA then you can have a wireless 
card and a usb pcmcia card.  These obviously use more power, but it enables 
you to have the whole lot selfpowered and no plug packs or reliance on a 
UPS.
