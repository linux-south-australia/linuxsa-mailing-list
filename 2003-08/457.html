Re: LinuxFromScratch.org

I ran it for the last 6 months or so. Done several builds and loved it.
Brilliant, bloody brilliant. Good for learning too. Highly recommended.

I have recently switched to Gentoo however, simply because I haven't got
the time anymore.

But if you have the time, I highly recommend it. Hell even if you don't,
build it on a seperate partition over as long as you like just for the
experience :)

Depending on how much you know beforehand, it will help quite a bit with
troubleshooting packages etc.

Also, the LFS IRC channel is full of people screaming RTFM at you, for
when you really need help :P

Seriously though, they're quite helpful, so long as you've searched the
mailing list archives, man's etc...

Best of luck
Daniel.
