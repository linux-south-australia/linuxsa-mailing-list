RE: Where to get cheap CAT5 in Adelaide

MIMP are on Richmond rd. If you are coming from city are you cross Marion
road and just keep on going. There building backs onto the airport fence.

I'd be getting CAT 5E rather than CAT 5. It's not much more. There are two
main types Solid core and Stranded.

Solid Core is what is used for main cable runs and terminates on patch
panels or female RJ45 connectors. It is not really that good for making "fly
leads"

Stranded cable is what you would use for fly leads and crimping male RJ45
connectors onto.
