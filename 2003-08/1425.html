To track Sid (Unstable) you'll download about 10-20Mb of data per day on
average. Some days you'll download less and other days more. I track Sid and
I rarely have to go find any later upstream sources.

I think Barney was being slightly sarcastic when he said that "apt-get
update" was painful. Having read parts of Greg Lehey's FreeBSD book (The
Complete FreeBSD), updating ports without having CVSup looks horridly
complex.

The packages are updated quite consistently ;-)
