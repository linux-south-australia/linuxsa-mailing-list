FLAG - Forensic and Log Analysis GUI (DSD)

Hi all,

just wanting to put the word out about a program I found the other day.
The DSD (Defense Signals Directorate) have a web based system analysis
and forensic tool called FLAG which is avaliable for public download and
has been released under the GPL.

FLAG was designed to simplify the process of log file analysis and
forensic investigations. Basically it analyses logs, packet traffic, dd
disk images and such.

Check out the link and be sure to look at the images page for more info.
http://www.dsd.gov.au/software/flag/
