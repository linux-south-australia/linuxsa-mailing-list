My immediate comment is that I'm sick of seeing ESR quoted as a 
representive, and have no interest in what he has to say. This is 
regardless of how insightful or intelligent his current comments are, 
as it's inevitably followed by loony shooting-self-in-foot bullshit.

The OS community is made up of many people, and continuing the media 
driven cult of personality by identifying a few select spokespeople 
does a great disservice to the collective.

It is appropriate for commercial endeavours since it is backed up by 
management structures, but it is inappropriate to have the same 
approach for group efforts.
