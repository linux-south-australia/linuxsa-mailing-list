RE: [OT] Dial-out accounts at ISPs

FWIW, it seems that no-one is prepared or able to offer this kind of service
cheaply. I had a few people mention that they could allow me to host a
server in their server rooms, with outgoing modems on it, but that really
defeats the purpose of what I want -- which is to be able to use modems
without having to pay for phone lines or bother with buying modems or
setting them up or anything. To be able to use them from an ssh session
anywhere, and to be able to pay purely on a per-call basis...

I guess not many people want a service like that (which is understandable,
as it's an odd kind of request), but that's ok... it was mostly just me
playing with ideas rather than expressing a definite need...
