Desktop idea

I think that you can't simply put a vanilla copy of Mandrake on a 
computer and expect people to want it.  Everything has to work correctly 
and its got to be packed with stuff people really want.  I think a 
company can succeed that does all of the following:

1. It sets up a computer with the best Linux distribution available at 
the time.

2. It provides its customers with Windows native applications on it 
preinstalled.

3. It provides additional hardware and guarantees the functionality of it.  

4. All of the hardware is of high quality

5. Allow for a high trade in value. So that when the hottest version of 
KDE comes out, or a distribution with a cool new feature comes out, the 
customer can receive the benefit without the fear of having to upgrade, 
or break existing configurations that work.

6. It guarantees the preservation of the customer's data.

7. The company has live demonstrations on the showroom floor. It will 
show Windows applications running and preinstalled.

8. Money back guarantee if not satisfied.


Thoughts?
