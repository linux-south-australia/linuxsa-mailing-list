In dieser Form ist der Kommentar erst in System V.4 enthalten und wanderte von

-------------------------------------------------
These comments [certain C comments with /* .... */] were first contained
in this form in System V.4, and migrated out of these to linux, as the Unix

question does not come out of the BSD development line, but rather was
written in 1986 by SGI and later licensed under the GPL. This code then
surfaced in Unix V.4 and was finally incorporated into the linux code
on 28 February 2002 by Marcelo W.Tosatti.
