The AUUG conferences are well worth going to, although I suggest
becoming a member - if you do the mathematics you'll save money AND be a
member of one of the world's oldest Unix user groups.

If you can't quite make it to AUUG, perhaps due to short notice, then
don't forget:

 http://lca2004.linux.org.au/

...if you look at the page very carefully registrations will be opening
in about a week and a bit's time. As one of its organisers I know that
we're well on track to meet that schedule.

And finally -- unashamed plug here -- check out:

 http://educationalinux.adam.com.au/

...if you can't afford the time to go the main conference then you're
welcome to attend the 2 day EducationaLinux 2004 conference for just
$66. Whilst we haven't worked out the full programme yet, there will be
a sponsored lunch, an on-site tour and education workers and students
from most parts of Australia.
