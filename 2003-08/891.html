ftp.gnu.org compromised

I havn't seen this come up before anywhere mainstream, but it might be 
of interest to the paranoid:

ftp://ftp.gnu.org/MISSING-FILES.README  

In summary, ftp.gnu.org was quietly root-compromised back in March and 
only fixed recently. Although there is no evidence of file tampering, 
the security conscious may want to check any installed software that may 
have originated from that site.
