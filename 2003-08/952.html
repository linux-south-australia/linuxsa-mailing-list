Re: Storing credit card numbers

Brendan,
Several things:

Talk to some banks.  You'll need a merchant account and ultimately 
you'll have to play along with them.  Most of them now have online 
transaction systems.  Camtech (where I used to work) offered an Ecommerce 
system to do this, not sure of the current state.

Second, WHY would you be storing the cc numbers?  Why wouldn't you be 
sending the cc straight to the bank (eg. Westpac, NAB, etc all have 
facilities for this) and then storing only an obscured pan (first 6, last 4 
digits) for verification?  

MMC
