Highly Concurrent Shared Storage (2000)
Khalil Amiri, Garth Gibson, Richard Golding
International Conference on Distributed Computing Systems

Search for citations on the paper as well.

At Panasas, we stripe files across 10 or more devices. Max file size goes 
up the more devices you can stripe across, as well as the max total 
bandwith and max bandwidth achievable per file.
