Everything.

If you'd like people to help, you need to ask nicely.  No-one is getting
paid to help people on the list, so if you want people to help, you need
to give them good reason to do so.
One way to make that happen is a) explain your problem fully, b) not
demand service, c) not use HTML email, d) check that your email isn't full
of typos and grammatical errors, e) search the list's archives to see if a
similar problem has been solved recently, and f) be nice and polite and
grateful.
You are likely to get a better success rate following these simple
guidelines.
