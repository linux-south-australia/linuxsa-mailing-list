Re: New Motherboard with onboard lan

Hi

I don't think you're supposed to put things in the /proc directory. I
have a downloads directory that I play with in /home/robyn/downloads and
until things are working they stay somewhere under there. In Linux I
temporarily put stuff I want to try in /etc/rc.local and you would put

insmod /directory where you put module/3c2000.o and after removing the
already loaded module try rerunning rc.local after this you could try
removing this if it works and putting a line in /etc/modules.conf

alias eth0 3c2000

when your Linux asks for eth0 the module 3c2000 will be loaded.

HTH

Robyn
