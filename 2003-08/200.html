This was the release that 'broke' things:

https://rhn.redhat.com/errata/RHSA-2003-222.html

The problem is partially mentioned here:

http://www.openssh.org/faq.html#3.1

However "PermitEmptyPasswords no" doesn't make any difference on most
systems.

There is also a bugzilla entry:

https://bugzilla.redhat.com/bugzilla/show_bug.cgi?id=101157
