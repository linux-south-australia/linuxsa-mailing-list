Oh yea and since you have RedHat 8.0

chkconfig --list  | grep ftp

Should give you something like.this

[root@computer root]# chkconfig --list  | grep ftp
        vsftpd: off
        mtftp:  off
        tftp:   off
        wu-ftpd:        on


If you have if all correctly turned on run

tail -f /var/log/messages

The next time you try it will probably give you more of an idea of whats
