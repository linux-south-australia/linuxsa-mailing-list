Re: Backup/Raid Solutions

On Mon, 4 Aug 2003, Mark Newton wrote:

> On Mon, Aug 04, 2003 at 03:41:28PM +0930, Benjamin Close wrote:
> 
>  >     I'm very quickly running out of room for a company's backups. I'm 
>  > just wondering where people get there raid solutions from. I'm after 
>  > hardware raid not software - shutting down the backup server to replace 
>  > a disk is not an option.
> 
> Bad implementations of hardware RAID can require you to shut-down to
> replace a disk.  Bad implementations of software RAID have similar
> requirements.
> 
> Good implementations of software RAID take advantage of hot-swap
> SCSI or SATA to allow you to replace a disk without shutting anything
> down.  Good implementations of hardware RAID offer similar benefits.

Better implementations do per-file raid. Makes a huge difference when you 
have to reconstruct because of a failed disk: you only have to reconstruct 
the files that had components on the failed drive ...
