Re: Does anyone know how Linux friendly a Compaq EVO nx9000 is?

Actually bought a HP NX9000 2 weeks ago.

Currently dual booted between xp and rh9.0.
So far, so good.

Haven't used the modem yet under linux but it is supported.
I'm not using power management either but there is support of some sort.

Video is the only problem.
Don't get me wrong. It works great under the generic vesa driver but
there is only experimental accelerated support for the ATI 340/RS200
chipset.
An absolute bucket load of lappies are now using this chipset so it will
only be a matter of time before a proper driver is available.

I suppose it all comes down to whether you play serious games under
linux or whether you use xp for that.

Any further questions, ask.
