Re: satellite

On Sunday, 17 August 2003 at 18:58:51 +1000, Kevin Waterson wrote:
> I wish to have all traffic download from a different
> interface than ppp0. I have a satellite connection with
> a different IP.
>
> How is this possible?

There are a couple of possibilities.  Assuming you have little control
over the addresses on the links, you need to find a way of sending
packets down the ppp link with a source address in the address range
of the satellite.  One way to do that would be to change the local end
point address of the PPP link, which should work (the address on the
link isn't important), but for some reason I've never been able to get
it to work.  The other is to enable routing on the PPP machine and
generate all traffic from another machine on the network with an IP
address in the satellite range.

Does that make sense?
