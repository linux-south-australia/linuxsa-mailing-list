Re: satellite

On Sun, Aug 17, 2003 at 06:58:51PM +1000, Kevin Waterson wrote:
> I wish to have all traffic download from a different
> interface than ppp0. I have a satellite connection with
> a different IP.
> 
> How is this possible?

Kevin,

Assuming that I've understood the question, it seems to me like you
need to spoof the source IP address on outgoing packets.

Suppose your IP addresses are 

ppp0 IP address	= IP_1

sat  IP address = IP_2

Then you should leave IP_1 as your default gateway IP address from
the point of view of your local network.
But you need to change the source IP address on all packets going
out through IP_1 to have the value IP_2.
Then all responses to your packets sent out on IP_1 will come back
to IP address IP_2 and will therefore come down through the sat link.

If you had an accomplice on the other end of the ppp0 link, you
could ask them to do redirection of packet destined for IP_1 to IP_2 instead.

The first method is completely under your local control though. So it's better.

Now the question of how to fake the outgoing source IP address...
I'm sure that iptables can do this for you. But I'm too busy to
look it up this evening.
