Sylpheed (Claws) Tip

-----BEGIN PGP SIGNED MESSAGE-----
Hash: SHA1


PROBLEM

Scanning all mail folders is I/O intensive and hangs Sylpheed totally whilst
it happens

SYMPTOMS

You are using Sylpheed and it suddenly stops doing ANYTHING (including
screen redraw) whilst it checks and scans all folders.

FIX

 1) Go to the main common preferences menu
 2) Click on receive tab
 3) Disable the Update All Local Folders on Incorporation

My work system's hard drives are fantastically slow and having Sylpheed
check mail and incorporate all the folders every three minutes made it
almost unusable.
