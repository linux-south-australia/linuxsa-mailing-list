RE: BIND refreshing

After some more hunting around, I have discovered that BIND does (as I
thought it might, but wasn't real sure) keep its cache in memory, and
has to rebuild it everytime it restarts... Then I hunted around further,
and experiemented by inserting certain records in the root hints file...
Which didn't work (one day, I will understand DNS fully, and then I
shall KNOW...)

Anyway, I managed to set it up as a slave, and that worked...

Woo....

(I realise that's not all that clear... There are two NS's involved --
my domain's, and another that I manage. I shifted mine without
shortening the TTLs, and now the one that I manage can't see my own
domain. Hence, I set the one I manage up as a slave of my own... And it
does an AXFR, and works...)
