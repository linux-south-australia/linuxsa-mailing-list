Re: display dies after using OpenGL app's

I have a NVidia GeForce2MX 32Mb and it works well with drivers from 
nvidia.com, currently 2313. I had no problems with it. Make sure that in the 
device section of xf86config-4 the driver name is nvidia and not nv.
