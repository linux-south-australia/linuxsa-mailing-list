RE: [OT] Telstra ADSL Price Increase

I'd just like to clarify for some of the Linux SA readers that there are two
(2) separate business ADSL plans from Telstra.

a) There is the highly reliable ( allegedly ) Bigpond Direct plan which is
what Simon is referring to as being comparable to the current Internode
business plan. This service is high grade and actually works ( LOL )but is
damn expensive. BPD has a usage meter that works also !!

b) there is the standard Bigpond broadband plan which is in reality the same
as that provided for the home users but which previously attracted a higher
rate if you were a business user. This service has extremely poor
performance/uptime.

I would not even consider entertaining the idea of running any sort of
business on the 2nd plan if your business relies on an always on connection.
