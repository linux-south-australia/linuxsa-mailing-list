Re: Sendmail Config

/etc/mail/access is your friend here

yourcomputer.yourdomain.com           RELAY
123.123.123.123  (your ip addr)        RELAY
123.123.123 (your whole subnet)       RELAY
spam.com                                         DISCARD
someoneyouhate@hotmail.com           REJECT

either restart sendmail after doing this or do (I think) "makemap hash
/etc/mail/access.db < /etc/mail/access" someone please correct this if its
wrong, I am not sure.


TCP Wrappers:

in /etc/hosts.allow

sendmail: all

in /etc/hosts.deny

all: all@all


hope this helps,
