As part of the original Dove 1900 tech support team I can attest to the
fact that the service was not economically viable. During the initial 3
month trial period, I averaged $1 per hour with extended periods of no
calls whatsoever. Since the users pay by the minute for 1900 help line,
once they get their phone bills they quickly find other ways to get
their support help for a much cheaper cost.

Providing tech support should not be seen as another small business
opportunity, but rather as necessary to reassure customers who are yet
provided with excellent quality of service, clear and precise
documentation, and still fail to resolve their difficulties without
additional assistance. Even the best managed and maintained networks
still encounter people for whom the telephone support lines are seen as
not only critical to their needs, but also their right.
