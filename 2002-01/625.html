> squid
>
> what should my routing table look like, i am using 192.168.0.* , my 
> gateway

Have you remembered to configure NAT/MASQ on your gateway?

Chances are your internal machines *are* getting out, but the return 
packets are coming back to you, as 192.168.0.x is a reserved "not to be 
routed" range.
