Re: Playing audio to a phone line

Hey Andrew,

Keep looking.  This is exactly what you want.  Most recent external
modems have the capability to send arbitrary sound (eg voice) down a
connected phone line.

Read the bit in the vm stuff about digital answering machines.  This is
pretty much what answering machines do ("I'm sorry we are not
available...").

The bit you want is the "-d" or "-t" option to vm, I think.

I've had this working with a DLink DFM-560E modem as a digital
answering/fax machine.

Martin
