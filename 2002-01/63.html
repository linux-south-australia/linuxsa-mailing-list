To cut a long story short, sendmail doesn't provide POP3 or IMAP
facilities - it's a Mail TRANSFER Agent (MTA), and you need to
provide a separate system to handle this.  You (current) problem
isn't with xinetd or ipchains (although it may be once you have
installed the appropriate package).
