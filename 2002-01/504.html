ext3 can't undelete

I recently rm -f'ed ~/mbox (it *was* empty, unbeknownst to be imapd
moved /var/spool/mail/mibus into it). So here I am, down several hundred
emails.
Hmmm...
I open up the Ext2-Undelete HOWTO.
Aha!
mibus: ~>debugfs /dev/hda7
debugfs 1.25 (20-Sep-2001)
# lsdel
<lists blocks>

Hmm, none deleted this year. How odd.

After a search on the web, it turns out that ext3 does something[0] that
stops the file from being recovered after it has been deleted.

Morals:
 Don't delete files, *ever* :)
 Ext3 cannot recover deleted files. You're stuck with "dd" and alot of
work for reasonably large files.

mibus
[0] It clears the number of blocks the inode uses, or something along
those lines.
