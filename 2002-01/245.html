Hi all,

Time for the LinuxSA January 2002 Meeting...

Here are the usual details:

  When:   7:00pm (doors open 6:45pm) on
          Tuesday, 15th January, 2002
  Where:  2nd Floor
          Compaq Computer
          139 Frome Street
          Adelaide SA
  Cost:   FREE
  Who:    Anyone and everyone.
          No pre-registration necessary.

Presentation:

  Geoffrey D. Bennett will be giving a talk on Linux Virtual Private
  Servers (vserver).  vserver allows multiple independent Linux
  (virtual) servers to run on one (physical) server, without any of
  the overhead of other common methods of doing the same (such as
  virtual machines).  This presentation will cover what vserver is,
  what it can do, and some of the uses to which it can be put.

Extras:

  As per usual, if you are having a problem with your Linux
  installation, feel free to bring your machine, but please warn
  organisers@linuxsa.org.au beforehand.
