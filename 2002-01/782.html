RE: ext3 vs. reiserfs

On Thu, 31 Jan 2002, Freeman, Peter (ERHS) wrote:
> Now that RH 7.2 has ext3 available by default the last 2 servers we set up
> used ext3 for the data shares, also no problems with those.  The other
> reason
> apart from ease of installation was obviously the backward compatibility as
> you mentioned.

I would say that full data journaling is a bonus for EXT3.
I understand that ReiserFS only journals the meta-data, where as EXT3 can
do either just the meta-data, or both the meta-data and the actual
file content data.
