>Does anyone have a copy of the Gentoo distro that I can get hold of (I
>believe that it is only a 16MB download), and has anyone played arround
>with it, it claims to have a port of the ports collection, which to me
>sounds like a cool feature.

You download the inital files (approx 9-12MB from memory) which then gets
your system running, grabs a net connection and then grabs the CVS archive
of the current build.   Drops about 20Mb of code on your system and starts
compiling.   Very LFS-ish to install, not as much polish as others.
Portage does not support code stored locally (you could fake a CVS archive
I suppose), so grab the first part but be prepared for the 20Mb online
download 8(
