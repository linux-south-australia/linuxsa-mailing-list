Re: book writing under Linux

On 2002.01.27 14:44 Stephen Donaldson wrote:
> A friend of mines writes short books and articles for interest and hopes
> to make a living from it one day.
> A question I got from him was what can Linux provide him with that would
> let him write books.

My advice would be to write in what the publisher wants.

O'Reilly has a strong preference for SGML. Macmillan insists
on Word, even for its Linux titles.

Publishers have huge numbers of people waiting for you to finish
writing each chapter, which is then copy-edited, illustrated,
laid-out, etc.

As an author you will want to change bits of that completed
work (eg: you want to change a bit in Chapter 2 as you find
that Chapter 5 assumes some knowledge).  You need to work in
the same format for that to have any hope of success.

Glen
