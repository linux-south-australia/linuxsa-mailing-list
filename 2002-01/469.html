intel 1000baseT NICs / NMI?

Hi,

I have a problem with some intel 1000baseT NICs..

Well, two problems, really. One is that they seem to generate about 17000
interrupts a second, and 23000 context switches (eep!) under load. This
tends to lower system performance just a bit!

You can throttle this with some options though, and get things to somewhat
more reasonably values. However, my second problem is that under load, the
machines get unstable or freeze.

One machine doesn't freeze, but does receive a NMI interrupt.. and after
this some, but not all, of the other interrupts stop working.

Can anyone help me out here -- What could be causing the NMI under heavy
interrupt load, and why does it trash everything else, and is there any
suggestions to fix it?
