RE: Internode dsl pricing

hell, i don't even care about a fixed IP... dynamic DNS is fine for me.
I don't use tech support; if i can't fix a problem, i doubt they'd be able
to.

All i want is low-latency (for ssh, gaming, internet radio) and either very
cheap per Mb rates, or else a reasonable download limit.. I find 3gb
restrictive since i live with 2 other people, but if i was the only user,
then 3 to 5 gb would satisfy my needs. I would prefer more, but wouldn't we
all? ;)

So, if anyone out there is interested, if someone was to offer a cable or
adsl plan that was along the line of $60 or so per month, for 3 to 5 Gb of
downloads, I'd swap over to it as soon as i could get out of my telstra
contract. Which apparently isn't very hard to do, given the constant AUP
and contract changes!

Also, I'd be happy to accept off-peak weighted downloads..

So, in a way, I guess i'm saying that the netspace and xis offerings are
good, but I'd like something thats about half the price, and offers half the
transfer quantities. I'm also willing to accept a dynamic IP, and
pay-per-incident for tech support. (For non-isp related problems.. ie. i
don't want ot have to pay $5/min just to tell them their router at
xx.xx.xx.xx is dropping packets or misconfigured or whatever..)

Toby
