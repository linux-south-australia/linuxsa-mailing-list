Strange home network problem.

Hi all...

(Im asking on behalf of a friend)

He has 3 PCs;
PC 1 - Windows XP
PC 2 - Linux (RH7.1)
PC 3 - Windows ME

All of the PCs have 100base NICs (all work ok), and connected with a tiny
5port switch. The Linux box has two NICs, eth0 for ADSL modem, eth1 for
the LAN. The Linux box has iptables flushed and default accept, and the
routing table is fine.
All of the PCs have 192.168.0.xx IPs.

PC1 can comunicate with PC3 perfectly (everything works).
