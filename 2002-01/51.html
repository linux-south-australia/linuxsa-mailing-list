Re: Multi List Address Book

In the immortal words of John Hall <jmh@senet.com.au>...

> Joseph Tan wrote:
> I am trying Sylpheed which is better than Kmail - it handles multiple
> lists and sub-lists in its address book, but you still have to select
> addresses manually (although Shift-click on the first and last name of
> a sub-list works).

Sylpheed works rather well, although I'm using the "bleeding edge" version sylpheed-claws.  Lots of very nifty stuff and definately better than the other mail clients I've tried (Balsa, Pine, Mutt, Evolution, Netscape, Mozilla, etc) for my purposes.  Especially the recent LDAP, GPG and Pspell additions.

One note, you can make your own mailing lists by defining an entry in the addressbook, and adding multiple email addresses to it.
