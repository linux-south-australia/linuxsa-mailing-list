RE: ReiserFS problems (long with details)

I don't know about the SuSE-specific kernel mods, but the standard kernels
had ReiserFS bugs right thru to about 2.4.8.. My advice would be to upgrade
to 2.4.17 (which is a decent release, so is 2.4.16), and also upgrade the
ReiserFS tools, if available, although your version seems to be past the bug
period.

Also, i'm suspicious about the using 3.5.x format, since its now 3.6.x..
There is an option to upgrade on next mount now, but i'd say thats probably
not a good idea until the filesystem is healthy.

-Toby
