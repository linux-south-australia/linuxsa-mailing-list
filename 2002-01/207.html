Re: Serial port cards + MS outlook-like software

Hi There,
    In answer to the first part of your query, in my
experience, the PCI standard has been around for more than
long enough so that all of the interface problems should be
gone by now. This is more than true with the serial standard
that has been around for maybe 40 years? I usually just buy
a vanilla 2 port serial card and it should just work. Brand
shouldn't be an issue. Dick Smith has suitable ones on the
shelf (but they are a little expensive - as much as $30
maybe). I have installed a few hundred Serial port cards
over the years, and as long as the jumpers are set correctly
(factory defaults don't always work) you will not have too
many problems. Hope this has helped.
    Cheers!
        Andrew.
