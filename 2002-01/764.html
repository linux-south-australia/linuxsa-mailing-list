Re: dead smb connections

it's peer to peer, there isn't really a server or client.

Anyway, point is, i'm doing exactly what you're talking about.. mounting a
share from one box on another.

I am then removing the share from the machine which WAS sharing..

ie. so there is a machine which still has something mounted.. but can't get
to it.

if i cd into that dir and ls or whatever, it pauses for a few *seconds*, not
hours, and then says
[root@asylum /mnt]# ls tjc

ls: tjc: Input/output error
[root@asylum /mnt]#


and thats it. No server hanging, no need to reset, etc.

I made a mistake when I said it automatically unmounts the directory - it
didn't. After i turn the share back on, on the source machine, and then
try to list the directory, it pauses for a while, and then lists things
again. Its back! ;)


So, umm, in conclusion, if you're having these problems, i reccommend you
update your software, or fix your configuration, cos it certainly work for
me, and part of the rest of the world.
