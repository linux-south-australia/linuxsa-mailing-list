Re: Sendmail Config

On Thu, 2002-01-10 at 17:18, James.Wilson@compsm.com.au wrote:

> What I really need is to allow access to one(or two) hosts.  By the look of
> things, the access table only serves the purpose for denying hosts as
> specified in the file, which is somehow opposite to what I'm trying to
> achieve.  But thanks anyway.

No, the access table doesn't just deny. You can achieve what you want
here by tagging the two hosts that you want to relay for with "RELAY" in
the access database. Sendmail's default relay policy (as of version 8.9)
is to deny.

If you'd had a look at Sendmail website, you'd have known that. Please
read the documentation.
