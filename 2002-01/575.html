Re: Linuxconf

As Steve Sloan said linuxconf is still available and must be selected 
manual using "individual packages" during install or load using the rpm 
later. My understanding is that the gnome-linuxconf requires the linuxconf 
to work and is only an X front-end for the package. Linuxconf has not been 
loaded with default configurations for the last two RedHat releases. 
Serviceconf only does a small portion of what linuxconf does and could not 
be seen as a replacement. Earlier versions of linuxconf used to cause all 
sorts of grief when configuring samba and there are probably better tools 
available to configure specific settings for daemons and services but 
linuxconf makes life easier for novices and people who have not achieved a 
high degree of understanding of linux.

Bill
