RE: Routing

You need to turn on IP Forwarding...

echo 1 > /proc/sys/net/ipv4/ip_forward

And you'll need to use NAT or IP masquerading (ipchains, iptables etc)
to get your 192.168 addresses to communicate with the outside world...
