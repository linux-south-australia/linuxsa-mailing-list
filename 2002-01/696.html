Is the monitor about the size of a wall? If so, it's probably normal. :)

Is everything else big? (keyboard? ... Nah -- pixels, fonts, etc... Does
the cursor look like it's of the order of tens of pixels high, or
hundreds or thousands?) If the cursor is not disproportionately large
compared to the pixels (and everythign else), then it's probably a
screen resolution thing -- increase the resolution, and everything will
get smaller. If it's just the cursor that's massive, and everything else
is normal, small, hi-res, then I have NFI off the top of my head... It's
probably a window manager thing though... Try the
Enlightenment/Sawfish/GNOME/KDE/twm/CDE/etc configs...
