Re: what gui language to learn ???

Hiya Paul,

The two main GUI toolkits under Linux is the GNU Project's GTK 
(http://www.gtk.org) and TrollTech's QT (http://www.trolltech.com). GTk
is the toolkit for the GNOME desktop, and QT is the toolkit for KDE. I
think both are equally suitable on UNIX, Windows, and Mac, but I'm not
completely sure about GTK - QT certainly is.

GTK is open source licensed under the LGPL.QT is a commercial product,
Windows and Mac evaluations are available, but you'd be expected to pay
for the full versions. Due to KDE's success, Trolltech allowed QT/X11 to
be open sourced too under QPL. 

http://www.trolltech.com/developer/download/qt-x11.html

Not being a GUI developer, I'm not sure of their relative pluses and
minuses, but my friends do a lot of QT development work under Linux/QT
and they give it their thumbs up.

Subjectively to me, TrollTech's website looks a hell of a lot better.
