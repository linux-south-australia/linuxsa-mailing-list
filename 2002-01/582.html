Re: [OT] Seagte Hard Drive Condom

Patrick Browne,

Does the rubber cladding cover the entire drive? The ones I have seen
are designed to cover the ends of the drive and the corners especially.
The reasons I read for this was to help the drive's survivability if the
device was dropped. The extra millimeter or so of cushioning helps
reduce the impact force if the device were to hit another object. This
is more of a concern for installations from the general public.
