Re: PPTP & satellites

Hi Tin

Work on getting your SM200d working first.
1. Log on to a PC that is not on your network and ping the sat ip address.
2. Run tcpdump the sm200d interface and see if the pings request are coming
in. If they are then you sm200d is working correctly.
3. Do a tcpdump on the out going link (ppp or Ethernet depending on you
config) if the replies are going out then the problem is with someone
upstream from you (ie your isp). If not then you have a problem with the
config on your Linux PC.
    A) check that ip forwarding is turned on (cat
/proc/sys/net/ipv4/ip_forward should be 1)
    B) do you have a firewall in the way (turn it of as a test)
    C) have you got ip spoofing turned on (cat cat
/proc/sys/net/ipv4/conf/sm200d/rp_filter should be 0)

Cheers
