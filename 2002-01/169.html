Have you tried different packet sizes with ping?

Realtek cards are notorious for losing packets for no good reason .. replace
them if you can.

To rule out the switch you could get a crossover adapter (I'm guessing the
inconvenient location rules out a cable) and plug it back to back with the
XP box at the d-link's physical location, using the existing cables.
