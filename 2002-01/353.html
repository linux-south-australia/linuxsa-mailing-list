Quoting Steven Ellison <sellison@pcsa.net.au>:
> Does anyone have a URL to any comparisons between the use of ext3 compared 
> to the Reiser journaling file system.  I would like to know the pros and 
> cons of theses two.

If you go to developerWorks from ibm.com there's a series of articles on linux 
filesystems there.  Basically (IIRC) they conclude:

o Choose Reiser for speed;
o Choose ext3 for ext2 compatability, RH install, and data integrity; and
o XFS for both speed & data integrity.
