Re: RE : PHP4 Installation

I just installed the rpms for php4 on my apache box which is I think
stock with rh 6.1 (apache 1.3.14). Note that there is a directory
contents disclosure vulnerability in older apaches that you may want to
upgrade to fix, but presumably you dont have security through obscurity,
and that seeing the directory contents wont make any difference.
Upgrading apache might break your config until you fix it, but depending
on your site configureation this might not occur, or might be trivial to
fix.
