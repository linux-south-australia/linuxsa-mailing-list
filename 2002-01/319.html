Re: auto mounting cd's

I know in Mandrake 8.1 there is a program called "supermount" that does what u r talking about.
If this is what is running then:

1. Remove the "supermount" info from that entry in your /etc/fstab OR
2. As root type: shell> supermount disable

Also,

Under gnome there is a daemon called "magicdev":
                 "the GNOME daemon for automatically mounting and playing CDs"
