I too have been with BPD for serval years and found them to very reliable
and professional.
The point that needs to be made hear is that as every ISP sales person
knows, once you have setup a client with their permanent connection, fix ip
on the email server etc. They are not going to move unless there is a very
good reason. I have one client that has a 2 meg link into Internode and has
a  large number of public ip no.s. To move this customer to another ISP
would involve a considerable amount of work and stress as the customer can't
stand being down for more then about 30sec.

The point here is that most business customers will tend to stay with the
ISP they started with unless something very bad happens.
