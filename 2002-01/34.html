What the error shows above is the recipient jack@4-vendor.com was not
relayed to due to the error shown.  For your information sendmail also
shows the source email address as shown.  In this case it was only one
email and it appears it did not get through.


> I had a look in /etc/sendmail.cf, /etc/sysconfig/sendmail,
