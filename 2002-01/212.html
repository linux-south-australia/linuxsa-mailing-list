RE: su ??

As root, 'visudo', then add the following line somewhere in the file...

yourusername ALL=(ALL) ALL

Alternatively, define a user alias, like "OPS" or "ROOTS" or "ADMINS" or
whatever...

User_Alias ADMINS=yourusername,someoneelse,etc

And then do the same as above, but with "ADMIN" rather than yourusername
(this should probably be below the above line, but it may not matter)...

Also see sudoers(5).
