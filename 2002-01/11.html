Re: Plotter

The Mutoh understands HPGL & HPGL2 protocol. The programs that you wish to 
use with the Plotter need this feature. Most Cad programs have drivers like 
the HP 7585 that will do this. Varicad does (A linux engineering CAD 
package). I have had a look at Cycas2 (linux architectural CAD package) but 
the free version has limited printing (A4 I think). There are other CAD 
packages under developement but the major vendors continue to avoiding Linux 
for some reason.

You could use Mandrake or Debian as a print server or a Samba server. This 
will enable you to print from Windows to you Linux box and on to the plotter. 
I hope this does not confuse you too much. 

There are some tablets supported under Linux  -Wacoms are.

If you get sick of the Mutoh, mine died a while back.

Brian Marr
