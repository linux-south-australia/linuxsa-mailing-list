To quote Mark Newton:

"Tying everything down is usually part of the problem.  If you leave
 /everything/ on your network on auto-negotiate, you'll rarely have
 any problem at all -- But as soon as you lock one of your devices to
 a given speed/duplex setting, that turns OFF the wire protocol used
 to make autonegotiation work correctly, so whatever is at the other
 end of the patchlead has problems."

That should clear up any fuzz ;-)
