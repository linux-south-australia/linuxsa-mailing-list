Re: Etex readers for Linux?

On 2002.01.29 21:57 Aristotle wrote:

> I am almost complete downloading the entire Project Gutenberg archive 
> and now need a good etext reader - any ideas?

They are just ASCII, so

    less cpbld11.txt

would work fine.

> One that can render with nice fonts and read zip files would be great!

Gutenbook meets these requirements.

There's also a Gutenberg to HTML converter called GutenMark.
I use this when I want something to read on the plane and I'd
recommend it.

If you need PDF (ie, you have an "Adobe e-text" reader) then you
can chain together GutenMark, html2ps and gs).
