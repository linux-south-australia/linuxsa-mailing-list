PC Authority: Linux as a Desktop is dead

Just browsing the newsagents and happened to be flicking through the 
latest issue of PC Authority. About page 20 something is an article 
discussing the likelihood of Linux ever coming up with a decent 
desktop. His suggestion is that people working on KDE and GNOME are 
wasting their time and should be better off working to make Linux a 
better server application. It will never be  able to come up with a 
decent desktop.

His comparison was that Linux would never be able to come up with a 
desktop equal to Windows 3 let alone any modern application desktop 
like 98 or XP.

Take a look at the article and see what you think about it.
