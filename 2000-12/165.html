Printer woes --- again!

Just when I thought I had solved my printer problems...

lpr started working (lprng).

But now I am having a problem in that after a while it stops working
again.  The only way I have found of fixing it, is to reboot, and then it
only works for a while.

When it stops working I get the output:

# lpr test.txt
