Re: Computerbank SA looking for help

Shaun Branden [shaun@pcuse.com] wrote:
> I am looking for some help with Computerbank South Australia. To get a
> background of this project please look at http://www.linunix.com/cbsa for our
> effort, or http://www.computerbank.org for an idea of where we are going.
> Basically, we simply salvage unwanted hardware, make systems out of it, add
> an open source OS and applications and give it to someone who could not
> normally afford it. We then help them to make the most of it.

Sounds like a good idea.  I've got another suggestion for you though.
In addition to giving away secondhand systems to those in need, why
not also sell secondhand bits and pieces cheaply?  What I am thinking
is a computer hardware version of a Goodwill shop etc.  Instead of
selling secondhand clothes etc, you could sell secondhand bits and
pieces of hardware.  You could use this money as a fundraiser for the
cause, and in addition it would raise your profile.  You could have a
"Web Front" shop instead of the normal charity store.  Just an idea
anyway.

Cheers,

Mark.
