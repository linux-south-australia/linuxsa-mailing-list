Jasmin is superb, but always very busy.
You might have difficulty getting a large enough booking.

Maharaja's is okay. 
You should be able to book in 30 people, just.

I've prepared this little list:

http://www.topology.org/rest.html

I prepared this for visitors from interstate who used to
always ask where best to eat in Adelaide.

I'd recommend for a linuxSA sort of evening with 30 people:

Stirling Golden Lake (I think they've changed their name recently)
-	in Stirling, excellent food, not as cheap as they used to be.
http://www.topology.org/rest/stirling.html

Rose Garden Thai
-	probably the most underrated restaurant in Adelaide
-	superb food, located on Churchill Road close to city
-	plenty of room
http://www.topology.org/rest/rose.html

Cafe Canton
-	very good food, very low prices, heaps of room
-	located in O'Connell Street, North Adelaide,
-	therefore some problems with parking sometimes.
http://www.topology.org/rest/canton.html

Jojo's pizzeria
-	heaps of room, very good food and prices
-	located on O'Connell Street
http://www.topology.org/rest/jojos.html
