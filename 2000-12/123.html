I recently read an article about logical volume managers, in which they said
that to expand the volume you used the lvm-whatever command and then ran
another command to extend the system areas of the ext2 filesystem.  I'm
afraid that I can't find the article so I cannot tell you which of the lvm's
it was referring to, nor (and this is the bit you really want to know) the
name of the program that extends what I suppose is the i-node table et al.

Otherwise you could use dump and restore.
