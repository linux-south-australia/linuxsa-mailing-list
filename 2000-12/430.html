Re: Sendmail Question

If you mean that *other* sendmails are recording you as
connecting as localhost.localdomain, then you are
probably running a dialup interface and also need to say:

  define(`confDONT_PROBE_INTERFACES',true)

for sendmail not to look at the interfaces that are up
on boot and select the "lo" interface's name.

Note that the configuration you posted is too incomplete
to make other suggestions.

If you mean that *your* sendmail is recording connections
from localhost, then this is exactly what you asked for when
you configured Netscape.  You can check this using "netstat".
