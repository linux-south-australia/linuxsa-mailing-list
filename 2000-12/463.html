Re: Samba + Automount

As has been said by others, Mandrake 7.1,2  and Redhat 7.0 have an automounting
function (named differently) They operate the in a similar fashion to a windoze
automount function.  If you dont have this on your system, follow the links from
Dan's previous mail and add this functionality.

I use Mandrake 7.1 in the fashion you are after and have no troubles from anyone
accessing  the cdrom without mounting it for every use, so it can be done quite
easily.

my FSTAB for zip =

/mnt/zip /mnt/zip supermount fs=vfat,dev/zip 0 0

Put a disk in and its readable, change it and it remounts the new one, ditto for
floppy, cdrom etc


Good luck


Steve
