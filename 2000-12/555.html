Linux/IBM super computer

IBM and Shell International Exploration & Production B.V will embark on a 
joint project to develop the world's largest Linux supercomputer. They will 
achive this by using 1024 IBM E-Server x330s in 32 racks, all running Linux.

With more than two teraflops of computing power the supercomputer will be 
the largest commercial Linux cluster for IBM. January is the set month for 
installation.
