Re: attack against sendmail - clues?

David Lloyd wrote:

> A vrfy will verify an address...
> 
> These are clearly trying to find out what accounts are on your system
> and what your system's topology (excuse the pun) is. It's easier to
> mount a brute force password attack if you can work out what your actual
> names are and so forth...

You can turn off VFRY and EXPN, and most sites do.

Edit /etc/sendmail.mc to say

  dnl Do not leak user names
  define(`confPRIVACY_FLAGS', `goaway')

and maybe also

  dnl Do not give away version info
  define(`confSMTP_LOGIN_MSG', `$j Mail; $b')

Then generate sendmail.cf with

  m4 sendmail.mc > sendmail.cf
