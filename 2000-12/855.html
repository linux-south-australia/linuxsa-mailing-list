> Martin Stacey wrote:

> > I need to install a tape drive onto a Red Hat Version 7 machine? What is a

> It depends a bit on your pocket.  DDS3, DLT or LTO from an estalished
> manufacturer like Compaq, HP, etc.

Don't go with DDS3. We have had consistent troubles at work getting these
things to read back reliably, even on the same tape drive they were
written on, let alone on other drives. Try DLT7000 if you have the money
(about $6k for a drive and $100 a tape). Apart from that, how about CD-R?
