The problem lies in your XFree86 4.0.x install.
I'm assuming that you removed your previous installation?

Mesa action.

Solution: get Mesa
Where: mesa3d.sourceforge.net  <-- Mesa3D libraries
And: reality.sgi.com/mjk_asd/glut3/glut3.html   <--- GLUT toolkit

Compile GLUT and then Mesa, install, and _bingo!_
