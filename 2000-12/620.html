Actually the passage above does not say it can be done, it says Microsoft

" a password-protected document cannot be recovered if the

Note the words "cannot be recovered".

MS's policy is a different issue.

Say I have a policy saying "I won't go to the moon this before I am 50
years  old".

Just because I say I won't doesn't mean I could if I chose too.

It just means I am not willing to even if I could.

Policy is will/desire, capability is a different thing.
