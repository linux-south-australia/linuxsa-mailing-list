Re: DAT Drive Wierdness

Hi

Look in the kernel boot messages - you should see a list of devices that
linux saw when it booted - also in /proc/scsi It is possible that adding
an extra drive has put everything out of sync - devices are assigned at
boot, so if you change the hardware it may be assigned to a differently
numbered device - /dev/st0 /dev/st1 etc.

Michael
