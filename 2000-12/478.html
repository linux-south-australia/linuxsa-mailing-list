Re: What's on my system?

man rpm

EXAMPLES:

rpm -qa | less

- tells you what packages are installed

rpm -qip package_name

- waffles on about the package

ls /usr/doc

- look at what the names of the directories; will give you an idea of
what's actually there

Get a script such as:

#! /bin/bash

for k in *
do
	if [-x $k]
	then
		echo "$k"
	fi
done

And pipe its contents to a log file...take a look at what turns up!

If you have the space, you could just install everything :-P
