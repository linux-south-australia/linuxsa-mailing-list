> Does any one know how to batch-convert truetype fonts into type 1 fonts?
> 

if you know how to convert 1 ttf to type1, then:

find -name '*,ttf' -exec ttf2type1 {} \;

where ttf2type1 is the name of the program that does one coversion.
basically:

find -name '*.ttf' <-- locates all .ttf & .TTF files
-exec ttf2type1 {} <-- executes conversion on '{}' which a filename from find
\;                 <-- is needed because...  ;)


Hope that helps,
