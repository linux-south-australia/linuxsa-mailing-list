Richard Russell wrote:
> 
> If you could post a summary of what you aim to do with this meeting, and
> perhaps an agenda, to the list, I'd be interested in thinking about it :)

The basic action is to develop a response to the committee's
report.

I suppose this consists of a number of steps:
 - summarising the recommendations
 - brainstorming the issues
 - making an argument for each issue that will appeal to
   the committee

We then need to agree on the contents of our response, appoint
someone to draft it, and meet again to have an edit session to
improve it.  We should leave enough time for other Linux UGs
to submit the document under their signature as well.  This
implies an early-January brainstorm meeting with a draft
circulating throught the Linux UGs by early Feb.

Further agenda items or refinements of those above are welcome.
