Look for things called "development" and "java". RedHat and Mandrake
seem to divide their development packages into "core development" stuff
and "peripheral" development stuff. The "core" stuff you really need;
the "peripheral" stuff you may not need. "core" would be things like the
compiler, libraries that are almost always needed (eg standard input
output libraries, terminal control stuff etc) whilst "peripheral" is
like "xlib - the X libraries" and so forth.
