>I'm fairly certain that you can use the IP address to specify the host when
>sending a message, but I don't know the exact syntax.  (I thought it was
>smbclient -M -I xx.xx.xx.xx, but I just tried it and it didn't work.)

>AFAIK, you cannot use the logon userid.

Since a Win9X and NT client registers <username><03> as well as
<computername><03>, you can use either for WinPopup messages.

Grab a trace of a user logging on some time, or look in WINS.DAT, and you
will see lots of usernames in there.

I think there may be an example of this in Special Edition, Using Samba.
