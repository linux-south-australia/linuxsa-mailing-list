If you ask me, it's more likely to be a dead or dirty fan.  That would
cause overheating, which could cause the other thing you alluded to.

Try removing the CPU and fan and see if the fan is clogged with dust.
If so, try cleaning the dust off; this might be enough to get it
moving again.  Otherwise you at least need a new fan.  You may *also*
need a new MB or CPU, but there's nothing in what you report which
would suggest it.
