Some information on 200.188.32.212: they were reported to spamcop.net,
and also listed on orbs.org, in September. (source: orbs.org) Seems like
they're not overly interested in securing their open relay. The SOA for
the zone gbl.com.br lists sysadm@gbl.com.br as a contact e-mail address
for this domain. Doing a whois search for 200.188.32.212 shows that it
is part of the Brazilian Research Network, and lists the co-ordinator's
e-mail address as gomide@nic.br. It may also be worth trying postmaster
and abuse addresses at the domain above. There's a few contact points
which you may want to try...
