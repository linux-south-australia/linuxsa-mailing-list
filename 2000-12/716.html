no...

running lilo will cause lilo to read /etc/lilo.conf and write to the MBR.
The MBR is all that is read at boot time -- booting will not real
lilo.conf... hence, you can change lilo.conf all you want, but until lilo
successfully (watch this one -- errors may make lilo fail to write) writes
the MBR, you will notice no difference...
