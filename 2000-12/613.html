Santa Claus is Coming to Town, prototyped in Unix

I found this one last week at http://www.davin.ottawa.on.ca/~matthew/humour/ -
since some one mentioned checking the naughty and nice list, I thought I'd
share this with the list:   

----------------------------------------
Santa Claus is Coming to Town, prototyped in Unix


better !pout !cry
better watch a.out
lpr why
santa claus < northpole > town

ypcat passwd > list
ncheck list
ncheck list
grep naughty < list > nogiftlist
grep nice < list > giftlist
santa claus < northpole > town

who | grep sleeping
who | grep awake
why | egrep "bad|good"
for i in goodness sake ;
do
        be good
done
