Resizing ext2fs

I have now found the article which describes increasing the size of a linux
filesystem.

Linux Magazine, Volume 2 / Issue 11, pp78-83, contains an article, "GURU
GUIDANCE, The Linux Logical Volume Manager."  It describes increasing the
size of a logical volume using e2fsadm, which aparently requires the
resize2fs utility originally provided by PowerQuest (as part of its
PartitionMagic product_, now available under the GPL at
http://e2fsprogs.sourceforge.net.
