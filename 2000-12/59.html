> System booted fine several times under windows.  Attempted to install

So your friend has a 2nd hand system, with 1 hadrdrive that 
boots fine under windows.   but after you installed a second hard drive 
and now it won't boot at all. 
 although you can boot from a bootable cdrom and a floppy boot disk.
OK .
  1) check to see if  both hard drives are set to the same [master/slave]
setting ( look for jumpers on the hard drive ) and they are connected on
 the same thick ribbon cable . The setting are usally printed on the hard drive
somewhere ?
 They refer to jumpers you may need to change.

If they are  then you have either 2 masters or 2 slaves together and 
that normally doesn't work.  :(

Try removing  the new hard drive, does it boot now ?
If so 
 change the jumper setting  on the new hard drive from slave --> master
                                                        or master--->slave   
Now it should boot !   :)
