Re: CUPS/mandrake 7.2

I have had cups running with no problems under Mduck7.2, I have a apple
postscript laser writer and there are two drivers, one specifically for the
apple and another generic postscript.  They both worked just fine ( i did
remove 7.2 for other reasons in favour of 7.1)
 Have you checked the margin settings for the driver you are using?  You can
do this by running printtool and reset the printer taking care off course to
check the settings.  It sounds to me (guessing though) that you have the wrong
printer driver installed.

As for printing from the term, did you pipe it through eg cat filexxx  |  lp
???? Or did you print through KDE terminal with the print button (I think
there was one but cant be sure) either way try both and check the results.
