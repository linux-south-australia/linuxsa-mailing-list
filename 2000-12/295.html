Re: kde2

Rather than installing the whole Mesa GL library have you considered:

Mesa-common-3.2.1-1-i386.rpm 
Files for Mesa (GLU and glut libs)

This may be what you need without the full library, but don't know
how that would work with the NVidia stuff, but it's another option. 
Check out this page:

http://rpmfind.net/linux/rpm2html/search.php?query=libGLU.so.1

or this one:

http://rpmfind.net/linux/rpm2html/search.php?query=libGLU

For packages containing the libGLU library, and good luck.

Damien
