This is something to do with STMP, the simple mail transport protocol.
EXPN says "exapand dog" and if you've set SendMail up to accept it, it
will reply:

expn lloy0076
250 David Lloyd <lloy0076@localhost.localdomain>

This is from my own SendMail.

A vrfy will verify an address...

These are clearly trying to find out what accounts are on your system
and what your system's topology (excuse the pun) is. It's easier to
mount a brute force password attack if you can work out what your actual
names are and so forth...
