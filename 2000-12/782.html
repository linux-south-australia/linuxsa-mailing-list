Re: Tape Backups/Backing Up

Chapters of the O'Reilly book on UNIX backup systems are
on line.

Also, I'd skip dump/restore and look at Amanda.  I run it
and I'm pleased with it.  It's tape handling is good and
it will also back up Windows machines (via Samba).

Whatever you do, check your disaster recovery strategy.
Most Linux distros don't have enough restore functions on
their CD-ROMs to get your disk back without a huge amount
of fuss.  You might want to cut a CD with your restore
program on it.
