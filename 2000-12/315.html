yes, 'twas Michael Neuling and I that were selling the 2.2r0 disks...

they are easy to get from EverythingLinux, and are not expensive...

r0 was not a beta, but a proper release... The betas were test cycles 1
through 3...

debian have now released r1 and r2 (see www.debian.org), but of course, you
can get to either of these from almost any version of debian connected to
the net by typing:

apt-get update
and
apt-get upgrade

(this will update you to the latest of everything...)

to put together deb packages, look into the deb-helper tools (a lot of work
has gone into this, as debian is maintained by a large number of people)
