Re: Help with glibc

David Lloyd wrote:
> 
> Martin!
> 
> > Can someone tell me what glibc is and how to determine what version I am
> > using. Thanks
> 
> In technical terms it's the GNU C Library. It's a library that I believe
> was developed originally by the GTK+ team to support the Gimp Toolkit+
> Widget set. You can find out about the GTK+ and a little about glibc at:
> 
> http://www.gtk.org/
> 
> Discovering the version should be easy. As a normal user (although it
> will run as root of course), do something like:
> 
> [lloy0076@localhost lloy0076]$ glib-config --version
> 1.2.8

Isn't that for glib, rather than glibc? try 'ls -l /lib/libc*'. Or if
you have an RPM-based distribution, try 'rpm -qa |grep glibc'

glib is a low-level graphics library used by gtk.
