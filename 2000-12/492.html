ppp off causes xringd to stop seeing phone rings.

Thanks to those people who put me on to xringd --- exactly what I needed!

Only one problem... disconnecting ppp seems to stop xringd from working
anymore (poff on a debian system) --- the daemon is still running, but two
of the modem lights which were on before, no longer are (these lights are
RS and TR).

Now if I run 
  /etc/init.d/xringd restart
the RS and TR modem lights come on again and everything works again.

I am guessing maybe that the poff script resets the modem which mucks up
xringd.

My solution is to run
  /etc/init.d/xringd restart
in my ip.down script, so that things are fixed up again on disconnect.

Does this seem like a sensible solution, or is there a better one?

Cheers,

Mark.
