Re: What's on my system?

Try:
ls /tmp 

Redhat always leaves a log in /tmp of what it installed -
sorry I can't remember what it is called as I renamed my copy to soemthing
else.

You could also I guess try:

rpm -qa 

which should list all the rpms that are isntalled.

You might want to pipe it into less or more as there will probably be a
few screens full eg:


rpm -qa|less
