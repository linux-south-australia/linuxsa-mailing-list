impressive equivalent of multitasking. TBBS could run 64 lines on a 286
(I think). Apparently the guys who wrote MBBS/Worldgroup had a 500-line
system running on an IBM XT with custom comms hardware, to do a dialup
MUD. Perhaps multiuser is more accurate as a description than
multitasking for these systems.
