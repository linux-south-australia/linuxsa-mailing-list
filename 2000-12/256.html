OnRamp 2 and OnRamp Home Highway are available just about everywhere.

The SFOA from Telstra also states that Telstra must install *one* OnRamp30
service anywhere in the country (they have differing time requirements 
based on the closeness of the requested location to a population centre,
but at the end of the day if you decide you need an OnRamp30 on a sheep
station 700km from Alice Springs, they must Make It Work.  They are allowed
to charge you "infrastructure" costs to get there, but we've been able to 
come to a "reasonable arrangement" (i.e.: not paying the costs) in each case
so far :-). 

There is no requirement for them to deliver more than one service to 
any given premises in remote locations, however.
