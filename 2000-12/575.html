Re: CUPS/mandrake 7.2 - workaround...

I give up. CUPS won't work for me with the hp 670c

I've gone back to lpd, tho that was tricky.

had to delete /etc/printcap

used linuxconf to set up the printer, print filters etc...

had to go to the sneaky directory:

/ect/alternatives/

in there are various links, like:

lpr -> /usr/bin/lpr-cups

had to change that to:

lpr -> /usr/bin/lpr-lpd

and I can print again. 

(oh, and I stopped cupsd from starting in /etc/rc3.d)

I don't think I have the same filter as I had with MD7.0, but it's working
okay, at least with general colour. Still not quite right for photos
maybe.

At least the side and bottom aren't arbitrarily cut off here and there and
the images on a page printed as big black blocks!

I'll leave cups til later, like MD8.x  :-)
