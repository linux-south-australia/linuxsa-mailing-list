I use LVM (2.4.0-test10) with ReiserFS and it can do exctly this... but only
to LVM managed partitions, not normal partitions.
As in, you cannot use the LVM resizing tool to resize a non LVM parition
(which i gather is what you where implying).

BTW... LVM is very very useful.
Try it if you havnt yet. Its more complicated to use (quite a bit more than
just simple fdisk), but it does make handling multiple drives and muliple
partitions a lot easier.
