I'm not sure about the "deliver sms to an ISP" requirement, but
several options are:

1)  Use a third party (such as Telstra or Redrock) to inject the
SMS message into the network on your behalf.

2)  Telstra offer TAP dialin ports; using something like qpage,
you dial into a modem and send messages directly to mobiles
without having to rely on email being up.  I think this may
be restricted to Telstra mobiles only.
