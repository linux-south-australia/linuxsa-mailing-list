Re: Reinstalling LILO RedHat 6.2

> >
> >If you've mounted your root partition on /mnt then lilo is probably
> >in /mnt/sbin.
> >
> >You *probably* want to do something like:
> >
> >/mnt/sbin/lilo -C /mnt/etc/lilo.conf
> 
> Thanks, Chris. I tried /mnt/sbin/lilo -C /mnt/etc/lilo.conf, but I still 
> get "No such file or directory". There is no /etc directory under /mnt, or 
> at least the /mnt I am looking at.

So /mnt/sbin/lilo exists but /mnt/etc/lilo.conf doesnt?

Well the lilo.conf file that was used could be in a different
directory. You could try doing a:

find /mnt -name lilo.conf -print

[It could be on a different partition as well so you might want to
mount the other ext2 ones and do a search on those as well]

Failing that, you could create one by hand (lilo.conf man page)

>   If I mount hdc1, wouldn't that be where I am looking? I can do a pwd but 
> it doesn't tell me which device I am looking at.

Doing a `df .' will tell you what device the current directory is on.
