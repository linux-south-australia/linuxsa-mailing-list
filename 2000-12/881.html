ok   Some motherboards have a over temp alarm set in the bios that 
will beep if it gets too hot .  (possibly your cpu fan has died)
was the system working ok but just beeping  or did it beep and not boot.? 
many bios's do a self check before booting and use beeps to alert you to
problems.  (such as memory errors video error etc)
If you post some more details of the symptoms  ie
how many beeps and when do they occur?
is there a video signal?
and what cards do you have plugged into the motherboard?
I might be able to help more.

If the cpu fan almost turns over then stops you may have a short across 
your power supply. 
try removing all your hardware and leave just the video card.  (  with the
power off , after earthing your self on the case of course ! 
( unplug floppy power, hdd power, and remove sound cards etc) 
 if it boots ok now then one of those was shorting your psu .  
add them back one by one and check to see if the problem returns.

goodluck and let me know what happens
