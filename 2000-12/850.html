Re: PROGRAMMERS! have an idea for you

Im not sure about the 2.2 kernels (havnt used them for a while), but the
2.4.0 test kernels have an option under "network devices" called "traffic
shaping". This looks like it might do what your after, but it looks like it
limits the flow of traffic per interface rather than by connection or client
addr.

As in the kernel help:
The traffic shaper is a virtual network device that allows you to limit the
rate of outgoing data flow over some other network device. The traffic that
you want to slow down can then be routed through these virtual devices.

May be of some use to you??
