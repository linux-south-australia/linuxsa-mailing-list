Ive found its always better to leave "Plug an Play aware OS" in the bios to
"no".
Linux prefers it this way and it also gives less headaches with conflicts in
Windows.
But if you set it to "no", windows might have a bit of a stress cos itll
prolly need to redetect all your PCI cards.
