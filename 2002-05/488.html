Re: Telstra NT1 Plus II (USB)

Hi Rhett,

Using the USB port on the NT1+2 will cost you $1/hour to most ISP's, or
$0.35/hour to Telstra or one of its defacto ISP's.

If you get a Netjet card (PCI) or ISDN Mouse (USB) you can connect to a
local ISP (Like Adam) for just 18c per call using DoV through the S-bus,
untimed.
