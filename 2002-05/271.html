Re: Seeking advice about employment paths

Hi Daniel

Looks like you're leaning more to the practical side of things. Make an
appointment with your local TAFE that teaches IT courses and complete
your high school education. You may be able to get credits for what you
already know in your TAFE courses. They call it Recognition of Current
Competancy, you show them you can do it and they give you credit.

Find some volunteer work, even go down to the local computer shop and
ask if you can help out on Saturdays, you may only get paid in computer
parts but don't knock the experience. If you can show TAFE that you know
your way around Windows and can install it etc that's a whole subject.

Good luck

Robyn
