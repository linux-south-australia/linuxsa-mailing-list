SOLVED: UK0023 NIC & SmoothWall 0.9.9se

Well... 3 NICs later I've got this box working.

My vendor supplied 3 extra NICs for me to test.
Only 1 worked.
Each exhibited the original fault described below.

Making no other changes, I tested each NIC in sequence, I even repeated
the test sequence just to make sure.

I did find out it is a regular EISA slot, but the only other PC I had
with EISA was my file server, which I really didn't want to
fiddle-with.
