Re: Knowledgebases

Take a look at phpWiki http://phpwiki.sourceforge.net/phpwiki/

It's very easy to setup (you have to guess a little with the mysql dsn
connection, but if you know mysql, then it won't be a drama).

Out of all of the Wiki clones, it's probably the simplest to use. 
However, it's not a very complicated clone and in my own experiences
with it, I've become very frustrated.  Probably the most frustrating
aspect is that I couldn't make it a virtual host.

I'm now looking at Twiki, which is a perl-based clone.  It's a lot more
feature full and complicated to setup to a usable point, but it does
handle being a virtual host and this is important for me...

We will be using Twiki to share ideas and the like so it's not quite a
knowledge base, but Twiki comes with example schemes to use it as such.
