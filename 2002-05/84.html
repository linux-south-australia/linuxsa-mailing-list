VNC success

Hi All,

I crashed my system trying to alter the VNC startup script (simply
adding kde & )  to get the desktop instead of the console. I  then
reinstalled with  Mondo backup and decided to try TightVNC in lieu of
the vanilla VNC. Voila ! it worked. When I inspected its startup script
it mirrored the KDE startup script which allowed me to access the
desktop.

Thank you to those who gave me their time and help
