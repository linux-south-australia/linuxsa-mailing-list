Out of the Linux & NT folks I know:

Linux Guys can:
Read & write documentation.
Problem solve.
Look at things from a system point of view:-
  Do we have spares?
  Is it consistent?
  How does it fit in our whole network?
  What will the user experience be, (user could be a sysadmin or
end-user)

NT Guys can:
Add-Up so they always have "the latest version".
Click-on icons that say "install."
Upgrade software or hardware.
Reboot.

I actually met a Linux-style NT Guy once, it was a pleasure working
with him.

getting rid-of many CowBoys.
