Hi Qwyn,
IPCop:
http://www.ipcop.org

SmoothWall:
http://www.smoothwall.org

SmoothWall is a better product, but has a really arrogant lead
programmer, so when you post to the listserv, expect to be intimidated
by Richard.

IPCop is a fork of SmoothWall, is slower & the web-interface is a
little ordinary, but support is friendly :)

Both are *really* easy to setup:- maximum 30min if all your hardware is
compatible.
