On Tue, May 07, 2002 at 06:37:17PM +0000, Dale wrote:

 > How do I close the above port???  Or can some one tell me how to find

It's your X server.  Kill off XFree86 and it'll go away :-)

Starting the server with "-nolisten tcp" command line arguments
will make it disappear for good (if you're using xdm, you can 
put those args into your XServers file in your xdm config directory)
