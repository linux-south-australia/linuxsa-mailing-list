Re: Maths anyone ?

Brian Marr wrote:

> 
> 
> Are there any Mathematics people on the list ? 
> 
> I have been using Lyx to lay out some maths problems. It does a terrific job 
> at this. Unfortunately amongst all of its symbols I cannot find anything that 
> prints the  "therefore" symbol. It used to be three dots in a triangle -if 
> you know what I mean. Is it still used ? Are there any substitutes ? 


 From memory what you want is the "\therefore" symbol.  However, to 
access it you may need to include some of the following:

\usepackage{amsfonts}
\usepackage{amssymb}
\usepackage{amsmath}
\usepackage{amsthm}

Ie, it comes as part of the ams-latex additions.
