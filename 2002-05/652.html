Hi DSL,
    Just FYI, concerning multipartitions
    I came accross this problem with the umpteen partition scheme just 
recently putting Mandrake 8.1onto someone elses system
I use

/
/boot
/usr
/home/user1 etc
swap
/var

as my basics and found that auto mount fell in a heap on this system 
because it(Mandrake) also mounts win partitions for ease of use as

/mnt/win_c
/mnt/win_d
..... etc

then add floppy,cdrom,cdburner system all goes together fine but when 
you put a cdrom in the system protests and will not mount the cd

After much searching, I finally found that automount will only take up 
to 9 items at once
ie 6 linux partitions and 4 winblows partition  allows no room for cd's 
to mount.
(Not a problem on my system (no winblows to access) ) but a pain for 
Newer users who want to access data of winblows disks

I don't know if this has or can be fixed, but it certainly makes me look 
first at what partitions I create and/or allow to mount in setting up 
Mandrake.
