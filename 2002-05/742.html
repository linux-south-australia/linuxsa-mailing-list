Re: Two questions: 1) VNC ; 2) Sendmail (?)

Shane Warner wrote:
> 1) I have VNC installed on my Mandrake 8.0 laptop.  How would I go 
> about having the Xvnc server started at boot time for a particular 
> user?  I have tried putting a su - <user> -c <startup script> in the 
> rc.local script, but that didn't work.  Anyone have any other 
> suggestions?

The first minus (or hyphen or dash, depending on what you call it) tells 
su that it's a login shell.  This doesn't seem to be what you want, so 
try removing the minus.

You could also try /etc/sysconfig/vncservers:
http://www.redhat.com/docs/manuals/linux/RHL-7.1-Manual/release-notes/s1-docs.html

This might be a Red Hat convention, but Mandrake may also do this.
