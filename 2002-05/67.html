To use docbook under RedHat:

1) write the docbook
2) type "docbook2pdf"

Try:

man jw
man docbook2pdf

Also try:

docbook2[tab]

(i.e. type docbook2 then press tab one or two times)

Goto:

* http://www.docbook.org/

..and download The Definitive Guide. It's a difficult book to learn
DocBook from but it's a start.

Docbook is a DTD (in XML or SGML).
