UK0023 NIC & SmoothWall 0.9.9se

Hi,
Has anyone got the UK0023 NIC chipset to work with SmoothWall 0.9.9se?

I've made a SmoothWall box on a system that has this NIC on an
proprietary EISA riser.
It needs to be a dual-homed box & its only got 1 slot for a generic
NIC, so I can't replace it :(

It "sort-of" works with:
ne io=0x300 irq=10.

Having the NIC defined on the Internet, (red) interface, I can surf,
however, once at certain Web-sites, clicking on links on the pages
time-out.

Defining the NIC on the intranet, (green), interface, times-out
regularly whilst trying to access the configuration page at:
hosts_ip_address:81

I've checked for irq & i/o conflicts, but there are none.
I have disabled PnP, defined irq & i/o using the setup.exe DOS utility.

Perhaps the NIC is stuffed...

Anyone had any experience with this chipset?
