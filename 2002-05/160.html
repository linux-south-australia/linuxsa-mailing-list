Re: [OT] DEC Tulip NICs

Hi.

My experience is that the genuine DEC tulip boards were very reliable, and
the 'clone' boards with genuine DEC tulip chips were pretty good too. Once
a clone chip came out things got a bit shakey. I don't know if the
situation has changed recently, I stay away from them now after having
been burnt.

I had some Netgear cards that wouldn't work reliably in any system, or os.

Anyway, if it's a genuine DEC chipset $20 is good. If it isn't, you might
as well go get a cheap Surecom card new for the same money, IMHO.

Regards,

Michael
