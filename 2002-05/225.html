RE: Seeking advice about employment paths

Hmm,

University is not primarily about learning facts, but about learning how 
to think.  

A University degree (arts, maths, engineering, whatever) is about learning 
the skills of thinking.  Taking a problem, breaking it down, analyzing it, 
understanding it, learning about it etc.  Sure, if you do a Maths degree you 
become better at understanding maths problems and if you do an arts degree 
you become better at analyzing literature etc.

Training your mind to think and analyze is vastly more important and will 
help you with your entire life.  Part of that training is the ability to 
remember certain facts and to be able to reason about new facts that you 
come across and to be able to put those facts into context.

Facts come and go.  Thinking is still the most important thing.

MMC
