Hi everybody,

Thanks for all of the support and help.  Things are much better.

learning process.

technical help, the kind a newbie like me needs.


Acknowledging others for improving the quality of the guide

I don't know how to acknowledge other people in the guide because on the 
one hand it could quite possibly end up not having been completed
