RE: Virus Scanning - Workplace LAN

I set up Amavis-milter with McAfee and sendmail a few weeks ago...  Took me
a couple of days to get it running (I had to recompile sendmail with milter
support, etc), but it works a treat.  We're using the milter version of
amavisd so that it scans all incoming, relayed and outgoing mail (we do some
secondary MX'ing stuff for clients).

Its increased the load on our server somewhat (as you'd expect), but our
Pentium 75 can still cope with our email load (500+ messages a day).
