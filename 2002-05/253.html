RE: Seeking advice about employment paths

> What I was hoping is for advice by people involved in this 
> area of employment as to what courses I should be looking at, 
> and really any useful information you may have.

Depending on what you really want to do, and what type of person you
are, I have two different peices of advice:

If you're an academic type, like the theory, or really want to be a
technical expert, go to Uni.

If you're a practical type, like doing things, and really want to do
things well, just do it. TAFE and other practical courses, as well as
books, will help, but doing it is better.

Basically, going to Uni is more like a Proffession, and TAFE, or
learning as you go, is more like a Trade. Both are equally respectable,
both have their advantages, both have potential for earning lots, but
you have to make sure that you do what you are suited to...
