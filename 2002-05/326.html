Re: Open Office 1.0 - was [OT] Bitchin about M$

I've been using OpenOffoce on Win2K at work for about 3 months now, 
unofficially of course as we're supposed to be in the M$ trap, and having 
OfficeXP installed as well it's been interesting to compare the results.
The only complaint I've really had about OO (and it's a minor one) has been 
the time to initiate the application, even on a 2 X 1Ghz P3 with 2Gig RAM HP 
CAD design station it takes about 7 seconds to start OO compared to roughly 2 
for M$Office.
The good part is I've received some badly formatted M$ word files and they've 
actually looked better on OpenOfFice than on M$ Office  :)  go figure
