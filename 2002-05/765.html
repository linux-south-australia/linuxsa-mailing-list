Re: Linux Software RAID

On Mon, 27 May 2002, Michael T. Cheshire wrote:
> When you setup Software RAID 1 on a linux system, is there any mechanism
> for notification or reporting if/when a disk fails?

You'll get a syslog message from the failed disk.

The /proc/mdstat will change from "U/U".

Unlike other RAID systems I've used there isn't an easy way to monitor the
RAID status from SNMP.  The format of /proc/mdstat makes it diffficult to
build such a mechanism.
