check http://www.tldp.org/HOWTO/mini/Linux+WinNT.html

I think all you need is:

other=<device>
label=win

or whatever label. I think you may be having trouble purely due to
having a long label... What error are you getting?

If the above link didn't help, try
http://www.tldp.org/HOWTO/mini/LILO.html for a more general document.

Alternatively, you could try using GRUB. I'm not really a fan of it --
the simplicity of LILO beats it hands down...

This could be part of your grub config file...

title Windows 2000
	rootnoverify (hd0,0)
	chainloader +1

but be careful about the hdd naming conventions -- (hd0,0) is equivalent
to hda1, IIRC...
