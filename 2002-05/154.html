Re: [OT] DEC Tulip NICs

>I've found a source of DEC "Tulip" chipset NICs for $20 each.
>
>Many manufacturers have used this chipset & I vaguely remember there
>were problems with some of the early implementations.
>
>Could people give some advice on boards to avoid?

Avoid the Netgear cards that claim to have a Tulip chipset or compatable.
Although some of the early ones had the real chipset, they started using a
clone (LiteOn I think) that didn't work as well under linux. They would
sometimes lock up for no good reason.
