The NetGear FA310TX PCI works with most Tulip drivers I've seen.  The
NetGear FA311TX PCI (also advertised as Tulip-compatible) is actually
based on a National Semiconductor chipset rather than the DEC Tulip
chipset.  Both cards are supported under recent versions of Linux and
FreeBSD, but you may need to download a driver for earlier versions of
said OSs.  I've also heard a rumor that the Windows drivers for the
FA311 are pretty bad, but I wouldn't know. :-)

Unfortunately, the FA310 is no longer in production, so you'll have
difficulties finding them.  Most NetGear PCI NICs claiming to be
Tulip-based or Tulip-compatible will be the FA311.
