Re: Video Editing

Mark,
>If you just want to cut-and-paste bits of it around, you can take

>advantage of the fact that MPEG is headerless and just use dd :-)

ER NO it certainly is not headerless there are a number of headers within
the mpeg stream 
you can certainly chop out bits but not seamlessly unless you know where
they begin and end. I was using cygnis (hex editor) to do this and
synthesise specific packet loss scenarios when transmitting mpeg, I was
able to do this by knowing the header codes and where they begin and end
rather than butchering an mpeg.

It was easier than learning dd :)
