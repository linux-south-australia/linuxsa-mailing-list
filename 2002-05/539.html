Hi John,

To make nice printed output on linux, and create portable documents at the
same time (I'm not a big fan of paper) I use pdflib (usually with PHP on a
web server). That way I can create a page for someone to print out on their
own printer that will look the same, or pipe the output into pdf2ps then
ghostscript (batch mode) for one-click printing.
