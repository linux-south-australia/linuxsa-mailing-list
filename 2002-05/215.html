I agree with the TAFE idea,
and if your lucky you may get into a trainee-ship, like two young men I have
working here,
both were doing year 12 the year before starting here.
one is finishing cert. #2 and the other is starting cert. #3,
both get a low weekly wage that increments each year,
but their tafe fees are taken care of by us (the employer) and they get paid
to study, rather than paying to study.

quickly.
