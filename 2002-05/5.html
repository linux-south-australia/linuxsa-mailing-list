Re: [OT] USB-DB9 Adapters and OSX

I use a Keyspan USA19PDA (http://www.keyspan.com/products/usb/pdaadapter/)

I use this on Win2k, but it _does_ work with MacOS _and_ linux.  (The 
website lists a new version - and it has winXX/MacOS/linux drivers for it on 
the home page!)

It's not the smallest, but it works (I basically only use it to connect to 
cisco kit and some other obscure bits and pieces) - it's reasonably small 
and light and has survived my abuse for about 9 months so far.

I got NextByte to order it in (as the distributor in Oz is a Mac 
distributor - so that'll give you and idea).
