Haha!  Yeah, I run into these all the time, admittedly when I stuff up 
a setcookie string; IE seems to have LOADS of cookie based bugs, 
especially 6, after they tried to introduce this other crap.

Generally speaking, to clear a cookie, you set one with a expiry time 
of negative value; something along the lines of :

function logoutuser() {
  setcookie("cookiename", "cookievalue", time()-3600, "/");
};

i.e.

setcookie("login", "BLAH BLAH BLAH", time()-3600, "/");
