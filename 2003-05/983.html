Correct.

I have a spellchicker, a very competent one. If you could see my messages 
before I post them you would think that the 100 monkeys have been tyoping up 
Shakespeare again ;-)

I don't make the mistakes deliberately, there are times that my finger control 
gets in the way of accuracy, there are times that my speech recognition gets 
words correct but in incorrect tense or else just stuffs it up completely. If 
I didn't read over my text before sending all sorts of errors would not be 
trapped.

NEVER rely on a Spellchucker, by all means use them to catch the most obvious 
of errors, then read it and use your own intelligence to make the corrections 
that the spellchucker will miss.

And for gawd sake, if you want to get into the IT Industry and you still make 
spelling errors then get some remedial classes in communications as it will be 
something that could make the difference between successful career or plodding 
boredom for the rest of your life.
