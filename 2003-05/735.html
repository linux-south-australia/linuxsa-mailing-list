I run a Toshiba 6100 P4 1Ghz, 512mb RAM & Nvidia GForce4 Go Video.

RedHat 9 & Mandrake both run well. (as do Win2k & WinXP withing their
specs)

Both distros deal with the cute 2Gb PCMCIA card I have in one of the
slots. (would be cool if I could boot off this, but I haven't really
looked into it.)
Inbuilt 10/100 Ethernet and Modem are picked up fine.
However I haven't tested the inbuilt Wireless with either distro.
