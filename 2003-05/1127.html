RE: Good source of MP3 players?

I know there are a few USB ones at www.auspcmarket.com.au under the USB
section. It's just a pity they don't make ones using the Vorbis codecs!
You'd probably want to make sure that they can play back all MP3
bitrates, not just 128kbit, as well as having a good history. I've had a
few friends who've had some trouble with some MP3 players. Usually you
can find 'real' reviews (not just product reviews) that people have done
about such devices, such as usability, problems, bugs, upgrades etc, on
the net somewhere.
I found these reviews of great use when I purchased an MD player online.
