RE: satellite connection

> Hi all,
> 
> I have a satellite connection and  it seems to be working but 
> I cannot recieve traffic through the interface.
> the interface is pentaval0 but all traffic is through ppp0.
> 
> How do I get traffic to download through pentaval0?

Normally, traffic returns to where it came from - if you make a HTTP
request from your ppp interface (say x.x.x.10), the response will come
back to that address, and, depending on your ISP and the IP addresses on
your machine, that is highly likely to be the same interface. It is
possible (dependant on your ISP) to set your satellite interface to the
same address, and have them route all incoming traffic to that address.
The other thing that is often done is to have a subnet (usually a small
one like a /30) that is routed from the internet to your satellite
address, but its default gateway is your ppp interface address.

No matter what though, you need your ISP to do half of the setup. It
would be best to contact them and make sure that your link is capable of
doing what you want.
