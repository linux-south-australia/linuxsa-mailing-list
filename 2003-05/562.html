Re: umount problem

Go easy killing processes, your linux box may have a cardiac arrest. A lot of 
these things are started in /etc/modules.conf. It may be a case of commenting 
them out in this file. Alternatively people on the list may be able to tell 
you more if they know which modules you are talking about. lsmod will list 
all of your running modules.
