Re: Laptop

I use a toshiba portege with Linux (Mandrake 9.0 soon to be 9.1). On the
whole I am pretty happy with it. The portege is VERY light - a great
advantage to a frequent traveller. It has a winmodem - but I am told
(though I haven't used this - I plug in a xircom ethernet/modem card) that
there is a linux driver for it - courtesy of Lucent.

One other virtue  is that  there is a 3 year warranty. I have had a new
mother board and processor and new brake hinges for it since I bought it.


As someone said earlier, BMS are pretty quick in servicing toshibas.

Bill Moran
