Re: Time server

Alan Kennington wrote:

> By the way, what is the relative load of a continuous NTP synchronization
> between two hosts compared with a 4-times-a-day sync with /usr/sbin/ntpdate
> (which is what I do in cron)? Do NTP server operators get peeved at
> the load on their servers? What about the traffic load?

CSIRO took down tictoc because a major ADSL modem manufacturer
had hardcoded tictoc's address into their firmware.  The
result was millions of users of the time service, increasing
by tens of thousands per month.

The latest firmware from the modem manufacturer picks on
some other stratum 1 NTP server (rather than doing the
right thing and running SNTP in broadcast mode).
