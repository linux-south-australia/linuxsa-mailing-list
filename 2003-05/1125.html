If you set your log level to 7   (kernel debug)
ie: $IPTABLES -A FORWARD -j LOG --log-level 7 --log-prefix "IPTABLES:: "

the following in syslog.conf
kern.=debug    /var/log/iptables

will make ALL kernel debug messages go to  /var/log/iptables

you may also want to change to the line that logs to /var/log/messsages
to something like this

*.info;mail.none;authpriv.none;cron.none  /var/log/messages

to stop the kernel debug messages from appearing in /var/log/messages
