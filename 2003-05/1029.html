Adam,

XDMCP is what you want. I don't use XDM at all here, so I can't check
much more than that. I do recall that you must make sure that the "other
machines" accept connections on the X port (actually, I think it's XDM
itself that does this on the other machines), and there is a config item
in XDM on your machine that should say "do XDMCP broadcasts", and
perhaps "show list of servers to connect to" or something similar.
