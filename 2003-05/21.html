When you get to the log in screen, does the command sax2 work? That 
should get you directly to the monitor setup.

I don't know much about the monitor model. If it is an older one, you 
coul try installing the older xfree compatability packages. These can be 
selected during setup.

>The installation seems to go normally until X is run -- and then the
>screen goes blank and the lights flash on the monitor.

Does a GUI come up during the installation?  If not, then it is not 
being detected. You need to find out what the specs on your monitor are 
and then enter them in sax manually.
