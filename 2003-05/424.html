Re: Creative 5.1

On Tue, 2003-05-13 at 07:52, peter osmond wrote:
>     
>     Hello,
>   I have just installed redhat 9 and the sound module will not load for the Creative 5.1 card that I have . Is there any known issues with this card in Redhat . The card works fine in Mandrake 9 .

RH9 still uses the OSS sound system as far as I'm aware. If you add the
following line to /etc/modules.conf:

  alias sound-slot-0 emu10k1

and reboot, you may have more luck. This line effectively tells the
module loader part of your Linux system that when an application tries
to access the device 'sound-slot-0' (ie, the first(0) sound card), to
load the module called emu10k1.

If that fails, try the following:

  lspci   # and look for the sblive card
  lsmod   # check to see if the emu10k1 module loaded OK
  dmesg   # and look for stuff relating to the emu10k1 module or the
sblive card
  cat `which ls` > /dev/dsp    # should make a nasty noise :-)

Hope it helps,
