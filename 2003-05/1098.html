Re: Mplayer

Hi Craig,

Have you tried different video ouput devices.

Type 'mplayer -vo help' on the command line for a list of those supported.
Then 'mplayer -vo <device> <filename>' & see if you still get freezage.

Have you checked /usr/share/doc for the documentation ?

There is a lively mplayer mailing list & on-line documentation on their
web-site, www.mplayerhq.hu
The web-site seems to be down at present. Traceroute falls over at one of
the main Austrian servers for me :/

That package versioning looks like an .rpm, & since you're using MDK I'll
assume that's the case.

Uninstalling the rpm & compiling mplayer from source may be something else
to try. The source also comes with the same on-line documentation.
Or there's always Xine as an alternative player.

Good luck.

Regards,
Rick
