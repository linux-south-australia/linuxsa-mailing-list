I have just been looking into this area myself,
i've found a couple of candidates that you may be interested in;


All these are Gigabit over copper;

 - Netgear GA302T - 32-bit PCI
 - Intel PRO/1000T - 32-bit PCI
 - Intel PRO/1000MT - 64-bit PCI
 - Intel PRO/1000MT - 64-bit PCI Dual
 - Intel PRO/1000MT - 64-bit PCI Quad

This one is Gigabit SX;
 - Netgear GA622 - 64-bit PCI

I don't have prices on the Intel Dual and Quad cards, but the NetGear
GA302T cards are about $90AU ... not too bad at all, but performance is
unkown to me as yet.

Dual port NICs are a little hard to come by and Quads are rare as, ATM.

the Intel PRO/1000 is supposedly well supported under Linux and FreeBSD,
IIRR. As is the GA62x series ... but im not sure about the GA302T yet ..

I'm about to take a leap of faith and buy some shortly... but I might
