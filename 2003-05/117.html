[OT] Worse than pop-up ads...

I have a WinXP machine, on which I installed KaZaa P2P filesharing
software. What I didn't realise was that it also installed some
annoyware called "savenow". It watches the sites you go to, and pops up
a window with ads in it, supposedly relevant to the sites you are
visiting. I managed to get rid of it because it, unlike most popup ads,
actually says where it come from, and that site says how you got it (it
comes with a bunch of different types of software), and how to get rid
of it (windows add/remove programs). In some sense, I guess they're more
"ethical" than your average popup ad purveyor, but somewhat more
insidiously annoying...

Anyway, I thought I'd let y'all know just in case clients and users (or
yourself) are getting this problem, and are wondering where it's coming
from...

http://www.whenu.com/faq_savenow.html
