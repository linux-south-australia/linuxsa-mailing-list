Re: [OT] Nokia 6610 Problems

Richard,

It sounds like the sim carrier is not quite correctly aligned on the
mother board.  I was getting similar probems on an 2210, which I fixed
by removing and resetting the carrier.  It could also be caused by
loose screws.  My 8210 was giving similar simptoms, too, and in that
case the screws which hold the unit together were loose.  I tightened
them and the problem went away.
