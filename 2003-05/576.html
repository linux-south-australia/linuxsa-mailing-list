It was the ide-scsi paramater that was causing the problemo. This was being 
passed to the hard drive (hdd) when it should have been the cdrom (hdc), by 
grub. Once reconfigured grub did its job correctly. New hard drives change 
the disk numbering hdd > hdc. This can create problems
