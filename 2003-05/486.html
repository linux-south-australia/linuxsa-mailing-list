> In Redhat I wish to have an icon on my desktop that I can access a
> samba share on another windows machine that may or may not be switched
> on at times, can I use fstab to mount a remote share to a folder in

Yes.

Look for the mount point type of "smb" or "samba". I think RedHat 9
might have a fstab configuration tool too...check your menus.
