> It seems that there are error logs in the Windows2k DC indicating

If it is happening every 30 seconds, it should not be too hard to grab a

> The error is a typical "Invalid password" error. It seems to happen more 
> regularly than people would use the Samba server. however, it's windows 
> 2k clients only on this network and who knows what they are doing in the 
> back ground.
> 
> On monday i discussed IPC$ on this list and found that "nobody" can be 
> used for IPC$ calls. Is this what the problem is? Is it that "nobody" in 
> not a valid user on the domain?

'nobody' is used on the Samba side if an anonymous login has been tried 
(no username or pass), or if the user failed to authenticate and IPC$ was 
the target share, or map to guest = <some cases> was set. Actually, the 
guest account is used, and nobody is the guest account by default.

So, in these cases, authentication does not fail, at least from the POV of 
the NT/2K boxes it doesn't.
