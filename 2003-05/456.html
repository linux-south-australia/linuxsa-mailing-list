Novell Certified Linux Engineer (Novell CLE)

Interesting bit of info I picked up from the Novell site.

About time too, considering most of the Novell folks I talk to don't
know jack about Linux, and NetWare 6 ships with Apache, and 6.5 will
ship with PHP & MySQL

Blurb from Novell's Website.

NEW Novell Certified Linux Engineer (Novell CLE)

We are pleased to announce the coming of a NEW Novell certification -
the Novell Certified Linux Engineer (Novell CLE). 

Announced at BrainShare US in April, the Novell CLE will focus on Linux
and the Novell Services that run on Linux. Those seeking to obtain this
new certification will be required to pass a practical exam that will
test your skill in Linux OS, Linux Networking and Novell Services for
Linux. Course and test will be available before the end of 2003.
