[ANNOUNCE] LinuxSA May 2003 Meeting

Hi all,

Time for the LinuxSA May Meeting.

Here are the usual details:

  When:   7:00pm (doors open 6:45pm) on
          Tuesday, 20th May, 2003
  Where:  2nd Floor
          Compaq Computer
          139 Frome Street
          Adelaide SA
  Cost:   FREE
  Who:    Anyone and everyone.
          No pre-registration necessary.

Presentation:

  This month we will be screening REVOLUTION OS, the inside story of
  the hackers and computer programmers who rebelled against Microsoft
  and the idea of proprietary software to create GNU, Linux & the Open
  Source Movement.  REVOLUTION OS features interviews with Linus
  Torvalds, Richard Stallman, Bruce Perens, and Eric Raymond.  See
  http://www.revolution-os.com/ for more info.

Extras:

  As per usual, if you are having a problem with your Linux
  installation, feel free to bring your machine.  Make sure to bring
  everything necessary to make your computer work (eg. monitor,
  keyboard, etc).

Pizza:

  After the meeting, please join us for pizza at San Giorgios, a short
  walk north along Frome Street to the corner of Rundle Street.

For more information:

  Email:        organisers@linuxsa.org.au
  Web Page:     http://www.linuxsa.org.au/
  Mailing List: linuxsa@linuxsa.org.au
