Kirsty,

> I don't have any specific advice as to what to get though...

One thing that we're missing, though, is that the after sales service
and what happens if something goes wrong is just as important. I know of
one person who was all set and ready to take a local supplier to the
ACCC except some liquid was accidentally poured over the offending
device before this occured.

So, I'd look for:

 1) Does the proposed laptop purportedly support Linux?
 2) Is there LOCAL hardware support?
    - local is important because you can end up arguing that the scratch
      was made by them transporting it rather than by you
    - if not, I'd go for a local supplier
 3) Do the guys you bought it from know anything about Linux?
