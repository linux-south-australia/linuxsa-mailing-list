> Where do I find out what tape robots are supported?

look under tape changers. an example would be the 6 slot DDS4 changer
made by HP/SONY/QUANTUM/... or the 8 slot SDLT made by HP/Quantum/..
but i've not used a multi-drive tape loader with amanda before,

> What does "differenctial" in "Ultra-Wide differential SCSI" mean?

differential scsi uses two cables instead of one.
its similar to the concept of extra cabling for redundancy, but
electrically its more complex, using +X volts on channel A and -X volts on
channel B. comparitors checking the signals are the same absolute
value... or think of it like parity. differential scsi also allows much
longer cables. its also plenty more expensive!
