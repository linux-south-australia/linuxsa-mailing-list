At home I use SpamProbe <http://spamprobe.sourceforge.net/>; very 
successfully (thanks to David Lloyd for help in setting it up), but at 
work (on windows) I just use the built in bayesian spam filter within 
Mozilla 1.3, I get a couple more false positives than with Spamprobe but 
I haven't been "training" it for as long either.
