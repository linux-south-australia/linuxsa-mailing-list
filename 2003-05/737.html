one tip that i wish i had gotten b4 i got my laptop - LOOK FOR DEAD PIXELS

most places say that up to 4 dead pixels are acceptable - its a 
manufacturing thing

my sony had 1 dead pixel when i got it (didn't notice in the shop) ..

so turn the machine on, get a black screen and look for unblack pixels
then get a white screen, and look for unwhite pixels
then get a green screen..
then a red..
then a blue..

well actually, maybe red/green/blue is all you have to test..
