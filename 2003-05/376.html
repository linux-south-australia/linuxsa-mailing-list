Acrobat reader under RHL9

Installed Acrobat Reader 5.06 from the Acrobat website,
using its ./INSTALL script.

   $ /usr/local/Acrobat5/bin/acroread
   Warning: charset "UTF-8" not supported, using "ISO8859-1".
   Aborted

Tracked down the fault to the environment varaible

   LANG=en_AU.UTF8

noting that

   LANG=en_AU.ISO8859-1

works.

So put small script in /usr/local/bin/acroread

   #!/bin/bash --
   export LANG=C
   exec /usr/local/acrobat5/bin/acroread ${*}
