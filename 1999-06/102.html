Conference in Melbourne

People,

As most of you know there is a Linux conferenec in Melbourne next
month.  It has a home page at...

	http://www.linux.org.au/projects/calu/

Other states are organising mini-busses (bussi :) to go over as a group.
Some people might even car pool.  

So if there are people who are going over there by car and can fit some
people in, make sure you let us know.  This may allow students and such
who may not be able to get over there otherwise.

If there are enough people without transport we might be able to look at
hiring a minibus or similar (if there is a suitable driver of course).

As an aside, the next meeting would be a great time to get your registrations
in.  Otherwise we will have to co-ordinate things outside of meetings.
There is always a post-office box that Paul clears regularly.

If you do want to register at the next meeting, 

	Please bring cheque/money order.  Cash is not prefered.

Hope to see lots of people coming to CALU 99 :).
