INTERNET CONNECTION

I recently reconnected with SeNet's new flat rate plan. The
phone number is 8210 1300 and the servers are apparently
different to the other servers used for other account types.

As you would all know my internet connection inexplicably
died. I discovered why quite by accident. This particular
server uses PAP authentication but SeNet forgot to tell me
(or don't know).

So if you change to that ISP number and your ppp suddenly
and inexplicably dies with a message in (/var/log) like
this:

  [dates] IPCP: timeout sending Config-Requests

Try setting up your connection with PAP authentication.
