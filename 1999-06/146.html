Re: Filtering mail ...

On Tue, 8 Jun 1999, Vypre wrote:

> Probibly a silly question but does anyone filter there mail and if so how do
> you filter out the 'linuxsa' mail.
> 
> Is there any way of adding a prefix to the subject of the mail (like
> 'Linuxsa:').
> 

As david recommended procmail works well. 

To filter the mail for linuxsa put the following in ~/.procmailrc

:0:
* ^X-Loop: linuxsa@linuxsa.org.au
mail/LinuxSA


This will make a LinuxSA folder appear in pine.
