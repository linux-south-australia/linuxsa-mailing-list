Re: How to tell when someone logs on

> > Hi All,
> >         I have a dialup machine. I provide a guest accound and a few user
> > accounts for my friends. They can access these when I'm online.
> > 
> > My problem is that they can logon and I am none the wiser. Hence you can
> > imagine thier frustration when I log off unknowingly.
> > 
> > Is there anyway I can get a beep/sound/visual effect to indicate that
> > someone has logged on so I don't hangup inadvertantly?

One other way is modify the ifdown/ppp-off script to check who is logged
in.  That is what you are trying to stop your self doing I would imagine.

Something as simple as who could be scripted to check to see if people are
on.  If they aren't drop the connection, if they are on it will email you
and abort the shutdown.

That would be the best place to do it IMHO.
