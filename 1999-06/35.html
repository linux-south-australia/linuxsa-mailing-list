Putting Pressure on ISPs...

For not joining "TIO's scheme" an ISP can face penalties
of up to $50,000 for individuals -or-
$10m for incorporated bodies.

I suppose TIO's scheme is one that (for the moment)
insures that Users' complaints will be taken seriously,
but could this be the first step towards enforcing the
"impossible" censorship bill?

Have a look (short formal direction notice for some
ISP[s] who've yet to join TIO's scheme...):
