Here is a good way of finding out information: Use 'grep' in the
man pages.  (Of course, Linux has attrocious documentation, or
rather it too often ignores the standard (c.f. man pages) in favour
of incompatible info and HOWTOs, still you can vary this advice
accordingly.)  To find out about zombies:

   $ grep -l -i zombie /var/man/*/*
   /var/man/man1/perlfunc.1
   /var/man/man1/perlipc.1
   /var/man/man1/ps.1
   /var/man/man1/screen.1
   /var/man/man1/top.1
   /var/man/man2/kill.2
   /var/man/man2/wait.2
   /var/man/man2/wait4.2
   /var/man/man5/proc.5
   /var/man/man8/fsck.minix.8

You will quickly discover that perlfunc, perlipc, ps, screen and
top are unhelpful.  You will equally quickly discover that kill,
wait and wait4 all are useful.  But please, don't take my word for
it.  I want YOU to try this for yourself.
