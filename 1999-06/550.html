For the historically inclined:

The LinuxSA mailing list was descended from the "SALUG" (South Australian
Linux Users' Group) mailing list, which was started by Chris Wood (who
departed this world in a car accident a number of years ago).

When the SALUG group was too small to actually have a mailing list, 
Chris and I spoke about the fact that since there were hardly any
Linux users in Adelaide at the time, it might be better off creating
a "free UNIX users' group" which included Linux, 386BSD and NetBSD.

Inertia then took over, and we never spoke of the topic again.  SALUG
was born, and SALUG begat LinuxSA.

If history had been just a little bit different (and if that upstart
Geoffrey Bennett character hadn't happened along :-), this mailing list
might have been "freenixsa".

Think of the flame wars we would have had then! :-)
