AGP cards are not a problem to linux/X at all, it will treat them as normal
PCI cards if no specific AGP drivers are used.

At the moment, there is no specific support for the TNT2 or V3 series.

The older TNT drivers included in XFree86 3.3.3 will probably work with the
TNT2.
NVidia are currently working on OGL support for Linux with thier cards.

I dont know about the G200... I imagine there is support for that as it is
an older card.

The best place to look for new drivers is a the SuSE web page.
