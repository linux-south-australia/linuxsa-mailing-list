> that the Turtle Beach cards are unbelievably good, but I haven't ran one under

I have a Turtle Beach Montego, which is a decent card, but it uses the
Aureal A3D Xstream chipset.  Though there are drivers for the TB
Multisound Classic, Tahiti, Monterey, Fiji and Pinnacle in the kernel,
there's no Montego support yet (or any A3D support for that matter). 
OSS has a beta driver which works OK now but looks a fair way from
completion.  It seems that Aureal aren't too willing to release the
specs for the A3D.

The point:  Beware cards that use the A3D chipset, as it could be a
while before drivers are included in the kernel, and even commercial
drivers aren't ready yet.
