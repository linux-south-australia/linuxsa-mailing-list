RE: Problem with internal networking

Sounds like you need to get tcp/ip working properly first. 
Try "ping 127.0.0.1" and see what happens, if it's negative look at tcp/ip.
Always try ping before telnet or anything else on tcp/ip problems.
I haven't had to mess around with it yet on my linux box so can't give you
more detailed help.
