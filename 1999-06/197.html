RE: linux and win98

Have you read the IP-Masq-HOWTO?  I'm not sure if it's a mini or not.. but
it's brilliant.  It helped me when I was down.

Just make sure you've got IP Masquerading compiled into the kernel.. and that
you've got the IPv4=TRUE line in the /etc/sysconfig/network  (I think that's
where it is..)..  then type the relevant ipfwadm commands..  there are two at
a minimum, you can find them in the IP-Masq-HOWTO.. and then set the windows98
box's gateway under network/properties/tcp-ip to the linux box's IP, and
you're cruising :)

Good luck.
