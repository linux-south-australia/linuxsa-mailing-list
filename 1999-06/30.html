Come on down to collect your prize. :)

Basically I got the card working by, turning off PnP
and setting the card to IRQ=10 Port=0x300.
Of course once I did this windows stopped working so
I turned PnP back on and the card stayed on its new
settings and both Linux & Windows worked.

Basically Linux didn't like it on IRQ=12 Port=0x210
and windows didn't like it when not in PnP mode.
