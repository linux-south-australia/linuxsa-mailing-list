Re: ls

Prasad Gunaratne wrote:
>
> Is there a way in linux to, list the just the directories with ls
> command ( or any other way)?


Try,

  find -maxdepth 1 -type d

Dont like the leading './'?

  find -maxdepth 1 -type d | cut -c 3-

They are out of order!

  find -maxdepth 1 -type d | cut -c 3- | sort

But I want it to look like 'ls -la'

  find -maxdepth 1 -type d -ls

What is all the crap at the front of the line?

find -maxdepth 1 -type d -ls | cut -f 3- -d ' '
