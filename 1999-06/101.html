Re: Optical Mouse without a mousemat

On Sun, 6 Jun 1999, Benjamin Close wrote:
> 	Anyone know where you can get an optical mouse (in SA) which does

Logitech make a 3 button optical trackball, called the Trackman Marble+.
It also has a "wheel", which is supported under Xfree86 3.3.2 and higher.

For info about how this works, check out:

http://www.inria.fr/koala/colas/mouse-wheel-scroll/index.html

.. there may be updated info built into RH6, but I haven't looked.
