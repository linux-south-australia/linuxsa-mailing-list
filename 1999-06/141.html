Re: Video4Linux

I use a cheapo BT848 video capture card, and it works perfectly under X,
using xawtv. You can capture video or stills from the commandline as
well, so you can automate stuff for webcameras, or for security
monitoring, or whatever.
xawtv was far better than the cheap freebie software that came with the
card in the first place for windows.

Toby

PS. One thing to note about BT848 Tv/Video viewing/capturing cards:
It doesn't matter what brand they are, because they all look and
function the same. So, buy the cheapest thing you can get - also, some
come with windows software, and charge more because that's included -
you won't be using it, so why pay for it?
