Re: help on modems for a mac

Hi,

>  I have a friend who has a mac and is wanting to get on the net. she has
> asked me to pick a modem for it but I am unsure which modems can be used
> on them. Do all modems work and just use a different serial cable or plug
> adapter, or are there 'mac' modems.
>  I know this is a linux list but you guys (sorry and girls) are helpful in
> everything else and I know there are ISP workers/owners on the list.
> 
> could anyone who has any idea on this please shed some light on the topic!

Most modems I know of work on both PC and Mac, and go through the Mac
Serial port on the back of the mac (Unless you have an iMac in which case
you don't need it), but you need an appropriate cable to plug into it.

Also reccommend you use FreePPP for Dialup.
