Having had the problem of checking nic ( or any other card) settings before,
I found that the easiest way was to use the dos utils that come with the
card ( or suck them off the net from the manufacturer's site). Just place
the utils on a dos bootable disk and do it the non-linux way.
Maybe one day someone will write a linux version but until then it may just
be easier this way.
