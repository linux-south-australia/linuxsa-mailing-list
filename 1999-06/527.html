> > Anyone know how to get this information out of the
> > whitepages server stings?
> 
> Two options
> 
>     1)  The hard way ;)
> 
> 	telnet to port 80 of your intended host
> 		type in the following.
> 
> 		HEAD / HTTP/1.0<CR>
> 		<CR>
> 
> 	Read the Server line (the <CR> is <the Enter key>)
> 
> 	Grab Queso and TCP fingerprint the host.  (Keep in
> 	mind that tranparent proxies break the validity of 
> 	this method.
> 
> 	That will give you a Server/OS pair.
> 
>      2) The easy way :)
>  
> 	Hit http://www.netcraft.com/Survey/ and hit the
> 	'What is that site running now'.
