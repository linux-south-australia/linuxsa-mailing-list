LOCAL: LinuxSA June 1999 Meeting (South Australia)

Apologies for the late announcement... Been a bit busy :).
==
Time for the LinuxSA June 1999 Meeting:

  When:   7:00pm on
          Tuesday, 15th June, 1999
  Where:  2nd Floor
          Compaq Computer
          139 Frome Street
          Adelaide SA
  Cost:   FREE
  Who:    Anyone and everyone.
          No pre-registration necessary.

      ** Be on time or early, it might be standing room only **

Presentation:

    This month we are having a double whammy.  

    Ramifications of the Online Content Bill
    ========================================

	Glen Turner will be discussing (from a personal standpoint)
	the government's new Online Content Bill which was recently
	passed through Parliament.  

    MP3s Under Linux
    ================

	Andreja Zivkovic will be discussing the use of MPEG Audio Level 3 
	(mp3) files under Linux.  

Extras:

    feel free to bring your machine.  Make sure you warn 
    organisers@linuxsa.org.au if you are bringing a machine.
