Re: zombies

>>>>> "Daniel" == Daniel  <daniel@webmedia.com.au> writes:

  Daniel> How do you kill zombies?

Grenades are usually the best, but if you've got plenty of rockets,
use your rocket-launcher.

Another answer: you don't kill them; they're already effectively dead.
From `man 2 kill':

        Errors
             ESRCH  The pid or process group does not exist.  Note
		    that an existing process might be a zombie, a
		    process which already committed termination, but
		    has not yet been wait()ed for.

Some other man pages that mention zombies:

top(1), killall(1), ps(1), perlfaq8(1p), wait(2), proc(5), fsck(minix.8)

RTFM.
