Hi All,
    Not only is it in Adelaide, it's GREAT. I highly
recommend buying it. It is a great game, and needs the Linux
community support. If more great games come out for Linux,
then the Game players will shift platform to a more stable
gaming environment and we will all benefit. Loki will also
be doing 2 other ports, one is MYST II apparently, and the
next is Railroad Tycoon II, and a new expansion. I don't
know much about MYST, but apparently it is pretty good, but
RRT is also a very good game. I will keep you up to date on
it if you like as I received E-Mail today offering me a
chance to Playtest RRT in the coming weeks, and if it is 1/2
as good as the PC version, then it will be a very superior
game.

    After all this, I bought my copy from Geoffrey, and he
had a number of copies on the shelf when I was there for
$79.95.
