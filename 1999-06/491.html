On Thu, 24 Jun 1999, Kevin Thomas wrote:
> Hello. When I create a program in C and compile it, I have to move the

What do you mean, "before it can be executed?"  Do you mean that it says,
"blah: command not found", or, "blah: Permission denied"?  The most likely
explanation is that you don't have /home/kevin in $PATH.  Assuming that
your program, blah, is in the current directory, try ./blah.
