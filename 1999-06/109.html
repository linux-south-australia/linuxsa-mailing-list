Re: Pop email server

On Mon, 7 Jun 1999, Steve Sloan wrote:
> I need to setup POP email server so the Windows client can access some local
> email on a private Lan. From my limit knowage of sendmail this will not do
> the job.

Correct.  Sendmail is used to transport email from one host to another.  What
you want is the pop server.  Look for the imap server; it probably also does
pop2 and pop3.  By the way, it also might have a (known) security vulnerability,
so if there's access from the Internet to your LAN it might be wise to deny
access to the pop2, pop3 and imap ports at your firewall.
