Re: tar and irritating pause

> When I am in the process of extracting files from a tar file, tar will
> extract a group of files and then pause for a couple of seconds and then
> continue with another group of files and repeat this group, pause,
> group, pause until it is finished. Has anyone else seen this? A little
> explanation of the hardware may be of some use:
> 3.2Gb Quantum HDD
> Intel 430TX Chipset using U/DMA
> Redhat 5.0 Distribution
> Kernel 2.0.33
> lots of other updates as well

What version of initscripts are you running (rpm -q initscripts)?  If
it's <3.32-1 then you should upgrade.  The Red Hat errata
(http://www.redhat.com/errata) says that the update on 30/Dec/1997
"fixes the extremely slow tar extractions", so this might be your
problem.
