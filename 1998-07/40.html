Just a quick note about your monitor - Linux is not DOS/Windows, and has
no silly 80x25 screen size heritage to live down to.

A nice little program called SVGATextMode will let you run your consoles
in all sorts of resolutions, 100x37, 116x40, you name it. Because of the
way video cards work you can also get MUCH higher refresh rates than you
would in X as well, so it's really easy on the eyes.

The greatest thing is that EVERY console program will work in these
'non-standard' modes.

Coding in emacs on a 15" monitor in 100x37 is great. :-)
