Hmmm, note that it is probably not a good idea to run anything less
than Red Hat 4.2, because 4.2 is the oldest version still supported by
Red Hat.  There are known problems with 3.0.3, and the official
solution (since at least Jan 1997) is to upgrade to a later version.
You shouldn't need to worry about 4.2 becoming unsupported in the near
future though.
