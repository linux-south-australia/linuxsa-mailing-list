Bay Networks and Netwave Technologies

Yet another Interesting Article,


Bay Networks will aquire Netwave Technologies, a developer of wireless LAN connectivity products, for approximately $US10 million. This will allow Bay's enterprise customers' employees to wirelessly connect to LANs with portable PCs. Netwave's AirSurfer technology - including IEEE 802.11 compliant LAN adapters which support Direct Sequence and Frequency Hopping spread spectrum - will be part of Bay's BayStack Workgroup.
