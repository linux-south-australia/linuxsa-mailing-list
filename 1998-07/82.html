Nortel and Bay Networks Merge

In case you didnt know .... Something I saw in a magazine yesterday :)


                 Nortel and Bay Networks Merge

 Nortel and Bay networks have agreed on an estimated $US9.1 billion merger. Bay shareholders will recieve a fixed exchange ratio of 0.60 of a Nortel Share for each share of Bay stock, while Nortel will issue approximately 134 million common shares in respect to Bay Networks' outstanding shares.

 Upon completion, Bay Networks will become a wholly-owned subsidiary of Nortel, taking over the latter's Enterprise Data Networks business. Chairman, Chief Executive and President of Bay Networks, david House, will become President of Nortel and be appointed to the Nortel Board of Directors.

 The Resulting $17.7 billion company will then turn its focus towards developing products for creating mission critiical IP networks. As for Nortel's existing partnership with Cabletron and Bay's relationships with Lucent and Ericsson, both companies have indicated that they expect these to continue despite the merger.
