Linux Databasen

According to http://www.infoworld.com, major database vendors have 
finally caved in to user demands and announced ports to Linux.

This is pretty amazing news, by the end of the year there will be NO
EXCUSE
for big corporates to deploy NT boxes as database servers anymore.

- ComputerAssociates is releasing _real_ ingres.
- Informix is releasing Informix
- Oracle is releasing Oracle8

We already have Interbase, PostgreSQL, mySQL, YARD, SOLID SQL, ancient
ingres, ADABAS, and half a dozen others as well. However the Oracle8
port is huge news, IMHO.
