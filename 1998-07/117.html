LOCAL: LinuxSA July Meeting (South Australia)

LinuxSA is having its July 1998 Meeting -

        Tuesday, 21 July, 1998 at 7:00pm
        Digital Equipment Corporation
        139 Frome St
        Adelaide

Presentation:

        Transaction Processing with Linux
        by Steve O'Connor

        Steve will be presenting a discussion of Transaction
        processing with a Postgres database backend.  He will
        also be discussing using transaction processing and
        the GTK+ GUI toolkit in an application that is being
        used in Pizza bars in Adelaide.

	** Steve hopefully won't have car problems this month! **
        
Schedule:

        7:00-7:15 Introduction and announcements
        7:15-     Presentation
        
After the formalish part of the meeting is over at about 9:00, people
are welcome to stay on for a while to chat with others.  You are also
welcome to bring along your computer if you need specific help from a
Linux expert.  The meeting will adjourn to a pizza place a couple of
minutes walk away when the guys from Digital decide they want to lock
up and go home.
