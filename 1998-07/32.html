Multiple hacking attempts

People,

There seems to be people probing machines in the .au
domain.  If your machine is network connected it would
be a good idea to ensure that you system is secure.

Make sure you have updates of the installed packages
on your machine.  And look for things like new user
accounts being created on your machine.

For more information on what to do to check to see if 
you have been hacked is available at 
	http://www.cert.org/nav/recovering.html

Cheers,
