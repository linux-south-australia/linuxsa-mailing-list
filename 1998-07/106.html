Re: PPP and IP Numbers

Hi Adam,

Most likely there are easier ways of doing it than this, but the following
will work in a shell script (at least on my system):

--snip--
/sbin/ifconfig ppp0 | sed -n "s/^.*inet addr: *\\([0-9.]*\\).*$/\\1/p"
--snip--

"ppp0" should be replaced with the name of your interface.  You may need to
tweak the regex near "inet addr:" if your ifconfig outputs in a different
format from mine.

Cheers,
Martin
