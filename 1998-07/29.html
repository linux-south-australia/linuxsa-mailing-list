Re: rpm Question

> Is there a convenient way to query a whole site or cdrom of rpm's to find
> out where a certain file is kept?
> 
> If you do something like...
> 
> rpm -qpl
> ftp://ftp.netcraft.com.au/pub/redhat/redhat-5.1/i386/RedHat/RPMS/*.rpm |
> grep in.identd
> 
> ...I assume you'll just get a dump of every single file. Bit to much data
> if you intend to grep it for one file.
> 
> Is there an easier way?

Provided that the package isn't installed (otherwise use -qf like
Steven said), run this from your RPMS directory:

rpm --qf '[%-25{=NAME} %{FILENAMES}\n]' -qp *.rpm | grep whatever

You can't do it from an FTP site easily in any bandwidth efficient
manner.  We do sell the CD's though!
