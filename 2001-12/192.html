> Is there a way of seeing where data is being sent through a modem,
> 
> i.e. a modem is sending out heaps of data to an unknown destination and I would
> like to find out where...
> 
> ... anyone got any ideas on how to find out.

You could use tcpdump, or ethereal.

tcpdump is quick and simple - ethereal has a gui and will give you more
pleasant information.

tcpdump is probably already installed, whereas you may need to find an
ethereal rpm (which will be easy :)
