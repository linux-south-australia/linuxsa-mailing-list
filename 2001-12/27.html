Thanks for your interest in Netspace Home ADSL

Unfortunately as you can expect the prices that we are offering down the
eastern seaboard are possible due to certain economies of scale.

 Although we do have pop's in both Adelaide and Perth and these pops have
been there for some time they are not at a level that would allow us to
provide a DSL service.

For example,  DSL requires a large amount of bandwidth to provide peak
throughput - which is not in place in these locations at this time and takes
some time to organise.

We would like to provide home DSL in Perth and Adelaide in the future but we
want to see how it goes on the eastern seaboard first.  We will re-evaluate
our decision in February.

Thanks again for your interest in Netspace Home ADSL - if we offer Home DSL
in Adelaide and Perth we will place a notice on our website, and if you have
given us your contact details we will mail/email you.
