Josh, 

Netwatch is useful for finding out which hosts and how much data.
It's a bit more concise then tcpdump and ethereal, which both give a
lot more info than is probably required for you case.  It's avaliable
at http://www.slctech.org/~mackay/netwatch.html or thru the netdiag
package under Debian.
