>With respect, that's not true. The good old unix 'vacation' program 
>gets this right, and it was written probably 10-15 years ago.
>
>Its the new crop of badly written, barely Internet standards aware 
>(and not really compliant) rubbish from Microsoft and Novell that 
>gets it wrong. Regularly.
>
>The program just has to make sure that it doesn't reply to messages 
>without your own mail address explicitly in the headers.  So the 
>other way to give yourself a quiet life (and save your co-respondents 
>on the list lots of unwanted duplicates, too) is to trim out all the 
>individual usernames and just leave 'linuxsa@linuxsa.org.au' in the 
>To: line when you respond to a post in the list.
>
>Otherwise you are very likely to trip an auto-responder for someone 
>you've explicitly listed.
