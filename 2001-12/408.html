Hi Joseph,

StarOffice 6 beta started out as OpenOffice 638c. From there they are 
different products. StarOffice 6b has additional programs that only Sun 
can include because of the licensing, things such as the Adabas 
database as one example. 

Whether OpenOffice 641b will be included into SO6 is something I have 
no knowledge of. Maybe Sun have included portions in the final release, 
maybe not.

My understanding is that the OpenOffice project will continue building 
and refining the code and Sun will regularly pick up a Build and refine 
that for commercial release. 

I have no knowledge of what is happening in the Netscape world.

Hope this helps, Ian
