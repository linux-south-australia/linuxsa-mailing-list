Re: Network Card IRQ problems

Dave et. al.,
	IRQ 12 is nowadays hard coded for the PS/2 mouse port. 
You'll have no end of trouble if you have a PS/2 mouse as well
as most mobos will hold on for dear life to IRQ 12.  If you
don't have a PS/2 mouse then you may be able to disable it's
interrupt in the BIOS.
Don.
