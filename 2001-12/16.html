Linux Counter

Hi Folks,

Some of you will know about the Linux Counter project at
http://counter.li.org and some may not.

The counter attempts to keep track of a percentage of Linux users
worldwide, and from this make an estimate of the total number of users.

The counter also counts Linux users by country, ranking countries by
user density (ie in Linux users per million population).  You can see
this at http://counter.li.org/reports/short.php

In the past, Australia has held quite a high position, with 11th being
the highest place reached from memory.  Unfortunately, this has slipped
somewhat in recent months, and now Australia is now languishing at 27th.

In order boost us back up the list and establish a high national score
on the nerd-o-meter, I urge you to register as a Linux user at
http://counter.li.org/enter-person.php if you have not done so already.

FYI, New Zealand is at 19, and we should be able to beat _them_ :)

Cheers,
Martin
