Re: email time stamp

Your time zone is set incorrectly.  It appears to be set to UTC -5 hrs,
which is the US East coast time zone (I think). Adelaide is UTC +10.5
hours.  The difference you should be seeing is 15.5 hours rather than
the 16 as described.

On redhat, you can change the timezone by becoming root and then running
timeconfig.

Select Australia/Adelaide (if this is where you are) from the list and
then select OK.
