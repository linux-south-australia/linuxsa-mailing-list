[OT] Linotype fonts on your server?

Linotype are getting heavy on finding copies of their fonts on some 
mirror sites as this report from The Register shows.
"German company Linotype Library GmbH is flexing its ATM font copyright 
muscles via 'cease and desist' letters with potential $30,000 legal 
tabs attached."
http://www.theregister.co.uk/content/4/23427.html

The fonts in question are Cascade, Flora, Frutiger, Helvetica, Isadora, 
Linotext, Linoscript, Optima, Palatino, Peignot, Present, Shelley and 
Univers. If you have them on any public servers it may be politic to 
remove them or at least clarify their status before you too get a 
letter for a "please pay us DM30,000".

Are any of these used within any Linux distros?
