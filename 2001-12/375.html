Alternatively, for those after a quick-fix arcade style experience,
download MAME (www.mame.net), get some ROMS (www.mame.dk) and start
playing.

For the full experience, and the cost of some CD's and postage, you can
get the ROMS for just about every arcade game released. The MAME burning
team (http://www.tombstones.org.uk/cgi-bin/burners.cgi) will copy them for
you if you provide the discs.

More fun than you can poke a (joy)stick at.
