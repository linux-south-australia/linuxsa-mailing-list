RE: Data destinations

Can I ask how you can use a different POP3 email password to dial up
password.

Most ISPs will force the username/password to be the same for dial up and
email. If someone was packet sniffing for instance on a local Cable segment
then they could get your username/password and use your time or MB
allowance.
