In general, it is best to have the swap partition as the last partition.
Doing so means that the data lies on the outermost part of the disk
where the transfer rates are greatest.  Remember that swap is usually
trying to emulate physical memory which has a 1000 times greater
transfer rate, so every little bit of performance is worth trying for.

Alternatively, buy lots of RAM so that you never have to swap.  :-P
