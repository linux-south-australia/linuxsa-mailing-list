Re: ASCII IMAP clients

"Richard Russell" <richard@yellowgoanna.com> writes:

> Quick survey -- what are people's preferred ASCII IMAP clients? I have been
> using mutt, but the 1.2 (stable) series doesn't seem to do IMAP well... or
> at all(http://mutt.sourceforge.net/imap/). Do alternatives exist? Do they
> suck?

Pine works very well with IMAP - some ad-hoc comparisons to MUTT found
it worked with IMAP a lot better. For instance, it seems to do a
better job of caching headers, so scrolling through lists of messages
is much less painful.

It also allows you to do neat things, such as storing your addressbook
or even entire pine configuration on the IMAP server.
