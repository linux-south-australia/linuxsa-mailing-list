Webmin and Sendmail

Hey Everyone,


As a relative newbie, i was introduced to webmin to set-up sendmail.  I find 
it to be the best way to do all my configs without having to really think too 
much about it (which we all know at the end of a long day can be a god-sent), 
i actually learned more by setting up the configs in webmin and then looking 
at the config files to see what it had done, kinda reverse engineering it, if 
you will.

So my advice is that if you are a newbie, get a hold of webmin 
hhtp://webmin.com , and learn how the config files get modified rather than 
guessing yourself.  Oh and remember to tar up the /etc directory, coz you can 
always get yourself in trouble when playing with config files.
