Re: ASCII IMAP clients

Richard Russell wrote:
> 
> Hi all,
> 
> Quick survey -- what are people's preferred ASCII IMAP clients? I have been
> using mutt, but the 1.2 (stable) series doesn't seem to do IMAP well... or
> at all(http://mutt.sourceforge.net/imap/). Do alternatives exist? Do they
> suck?

pine does IMAP.  The user interface is OK, the configuration
file sucks.  The lines in .pinerc you need are

inbox-path={imap.example.edu.au}INBOX
default-fcc={imap.example.edu.au}mail/Sent
postponed-folder={imap.example.edu.au}mail/Drafts
folder-collections=Example {imap.example.edu.au}mail/[]
rsh-open-timeout=0
