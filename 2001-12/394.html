Hoo boy, one of _those_ questions..

Personally, I'd use FreeBSD, but that's mainly because I prefer the way its
configuration and layout works compared to most common Linux distributions.

However, FreeBSD is a little more difficult to get the hang of than a
commercial linux distribution. If you're new to all this, I'd suggest RedHat,
Mandrake, or SuSE Linux. Buy the packaged, shrinkwrapped version rather than
a CD burn: you'll get lots of useful and distribution-specific documentation
that way. If you're not new to this, go whatever you feel most comfortable
with.
