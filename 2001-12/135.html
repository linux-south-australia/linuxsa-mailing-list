Re: no logins

> I upgraded a RedHat v6ish to RedHat v7.1 recently, in doing so I managed
to
> delete the contents of the directory /bin,(it was after a long, hard day).
> After recreating the contents of the directory including the links (by
> copying from another RedHat v7.1 system) I found that I could not log in
to
> the systems except as root.  The exact message is "No Shell: permission
> denied"
>
> How do I fix this?

find out what shell your user is trying to use (finger user, or look in
/etc/passwd -- it's the last field in the passwd record, and is probably
/bin/bash or something similar), and then check the permissions on that
file.
