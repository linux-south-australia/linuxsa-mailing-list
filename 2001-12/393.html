> We are about to start setting up some servers and Bandwidth to have our 
> webservers online 24/7....  Im thinkin of usin openBSD for the OS's, but 
> thought Id ask you guys what you would run....


I run a few FreeBSD servers and a few Red Hat Linux servers.

Both do the job quite well, so you should consider:
* what OS you know best
* what OS you have manuals and documentation for
* what OS your friends use (who you can ask for help)
* what OS you can get easily
* what OS will run on your hardware
* what license you prefer (if any)

If you're still undecided, I'd recommend FreeBSD over Red Hat Linux, as 
it seems to be better put together, and some would argue that it's 
faster and more stable.  Debian GNU/Linux would also be worth a look.
