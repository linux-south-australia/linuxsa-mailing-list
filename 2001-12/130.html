FW: FTP thru PPTP connection? and other ports too?

Hi Matthew,

I may be wrong - but one thing we had to do to allow FTP to "passthru"
was the following:

enable masquerading of ftp:
modprobe ip_masq_ftp

enable nat on your ftp connections:
modprobe ip_nat_ftp

enable connection tracking:
modprobe ip_conntrack_ftp

This enables us to let clients connect through the Linux box. We are
using RedHat 7.1/7.2 on those boxes!

Hope this helps!
