>I have a pentium 100 laptop with 40MB ram and 700MB HDD and 
>only a 3.5inch floppy drive. 

Does it have a network card by any chance?

If so, you can try one of the more mainstream distributions (Redhat,
Mandrake etc) by using the network boot disk supplied on the install
cd.  Most people on this list would be able to help you out with 
this type of install.

If you don't have a network card, u may need to try one of the small 
Linux distributions such as listed on this page, pay particular 
attention to the floppy-based & small disk sections:

http://lwn.net/Distributions/

(Unfortunately I haven't tried any of these myself, but hopefully
its pointed you in the right direction)

HTH
