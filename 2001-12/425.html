Mandrake Users' Club

Hiya All,

Firstly, I think all of us who attended the dinner had a good time. Other
than the fact that my lamb noodles dish was hotter than the surface of the
sun, it was an enjoyable meeting.

The Mandrake users club very recently opened (like today), it's a fee-based
service that allows access to commercial linux softare for those of us who
have never bought the retail package.

http://www.mandrakeclub.com/

As a bonus, previous monetary donators will be soon added to the members
list. This is good for me :)

Speaking to a few people tonight, it seems that many of us have never tried
the Mandrake distribution. I think one thing they set apart from most
companies is greater community participation, and I really think this club
is another example of that (as well as being a potential source of revenue).

Cheers,
