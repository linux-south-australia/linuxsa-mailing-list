Linux Rute User's Tutorial and Exposition

Hi All,
    For those of you looking for this great book, I got my
copy from Angus & Robertson. When I first went in they
looked it up on their system and couldn't find it either,
but luckily had it on the shelf. They recommended next time
to use the ISBN, so for those that missed it last time.....

"Linux - Rute User's Tutorial and Exposition" by Paul Scheer
ISBN 0-13-033351-4 (Prentice Hall)

    You can download the latest pdf and html version off the
net which is obviously a bit more up to date than the paper
copy, but I would still recommend getting a copy of the
book, even if you already have the electronic version. There
is nothing like being able to flick through pages to exactly
what you want in seconds than clicking away at links to find
what you want. Most people cannot do prep reading on the bus
or train with a Laptop easily (although some do)either. Hope
this helps.
    Cheers!
        Andrew.
