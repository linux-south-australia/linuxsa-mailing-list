Robyn,

normally you have to pass a switch or a package to up2date ie. to list the
packages which need updating on your system type "up2date -l" it'll list the
packages, then you can download them by doing

"up2date sendmail rpm tmpwatch bind"

the packages being sendmail rpm tmpwatch and bind. as an example
