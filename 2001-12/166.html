Re: [OT] dodo.com - any comments?

> Another thing we are looking for is somebody to host us so we can lock 
> in our email addresses even if we change suppliers.

Will your project be registering a domain name? It may well be worth the
cost, varying from $140 to $250. 
You could then create a number of email addresses 
eg ilox@ourlocalproject.net.au
   wardle@ourlocalproject.net.au

and if you change isp, your domain name and hence the email addresses
could move as well.

The small business a friend and I run has justed changed isp doing this
and it has worked so seamlessly that I have decided to abandon my
altavista.net forwarding address.
