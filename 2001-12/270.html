Re: Exit status codes

Mark <markc@trinity.sa.edu.au> wrote:
> does anyone know where I can find a list of exit status codes (using the
> bash shell).

No.  Nobody knows that.  Exit status codes are unique to every program,
with the caveat that zero means success and non-zero means failure.
The only way to find out what any particular exit code means is to RTFM
or UTSL.
