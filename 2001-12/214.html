go scsi, it's only slightly more and is much better.
I bought a plextor scsi (PX-412) one a couple of years ago, got 
because I wanted a Sun-bootable scsi cd drive as well as a burner 
and I can only recall ever creating one coaster with it.
They're not cheap though, value for money, get a ricoh, sony,
panasonic etc "bare drive" (scsi of course).
Check the howto (cd-writing I think) for supported models just to
be on the safe side.
