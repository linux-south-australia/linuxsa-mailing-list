I have found a simple way to convert mp3 to wav files is by using the 
following script:

#!/bin/bash
# mp32wav program
mpg123 -b 10000 -s "$1" | sox -t raw -r 44100 -s -w -c2 - "$2"

and to use:-

mp32wav file.mp3 file.wav
