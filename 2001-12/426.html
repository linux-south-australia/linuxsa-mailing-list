Re: Autoreplies?

Richard Russell wrote:

>>> However, it's generally pretty hard for autoreply software to tell
>>> if a message is from a mailing list or not...

>> Actually, it's pretty easy, unless you're running Exchange.

>> X-Mailing-List: would probably be a reasonable way of detecting
>> email from LinuxSA.

> true, but is that a "standard" or common accross mailing list
> software and platforms?

It's "a" standard, and other mailing lists use similar techniques
("Precedence: bulk" or "Precedence: list" comes to mind, of which
LinuxSA appears to use the latter).  However, as SMTP and RFC822
are open standards, guess how much chance you have of Exchange
natively supporting such a feature?  It's a snap with tools like
procmail.
