Re: Autoreplies?

The autoreplies generated by linuxsa are quaint when compared with some
more volumous mailing lists. Sending detail of a security exploit to
bugtraq generates enough autoreply email to DoS your inbox. 

Ironically it has also been the subject of much security discussion, as
an autoreply is like an invitation to blackhats to to nasty things to
your PC/Network/mail while you're away and unlikely to detect it.
