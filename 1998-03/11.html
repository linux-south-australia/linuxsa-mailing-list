mailing list information

Just a reminder:

Postings to the LinuxSA list are only accepted from your subscribed
address.  If you send a message to linuxsa@linuxsa.org.au from an
address that is not subscribed, your message will be ignored and will
not be bounced.  This is to reduce the likelyhood of spam making it on
to the list.

If you post a message and don't see it come back to you via the list,
then it is most likely that you sent it from an address that is not
subscribed.
