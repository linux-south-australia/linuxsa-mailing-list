LOCAL: LinuxSA March 1998 Meeting (South Australia)

LinuxSA is having its March 1998 Meeting:

        Tuesday, 17 March, 1998 at 7:00pm
        Digital Equipment Corporation
        139 Frome St
        Adelaide

Presentation:

        Linux and Security
        by Matthew Tippett

        Matthew will be giving a very brief overview of Computer
        Security.  Included in the talk will be risks, solutions and
        the possibility of a demo or two of actual attacks.

Schedule:

        7:00-7:15 Introduction and announcements
        7:15-8:00 Presentation part one
        8:00-8:15 Coffee break
        8:15-9:00 Presentation part two

After the formalish part of the meeting is over at about 9:00, people
are welcome to stay on for a while to chat with others.  You are also
welcome to bring along your computer if you need specific help from a
Linux expert.  The meeting will adjourn to a pizza place a couple of
minutes walk away when the guys from Digital decide they want to lock
up and go home.

For more information:

        Email:        organisers@linuxsa.org.au
        Web Page:     http://www.linuxsa.org.au
        Mailing List: linuxsa@linuxsa.org.au

        Mailing list details on the web page.

Hope to see you there!
