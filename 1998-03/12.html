Re: ifconfig

> At 13:18 2/03/98 +1030, you wrote:
> >The differences are:
> >Add :0 to the filename (the next ones are :1, :2, etc),
> >Add :0 to the DEVICE= line,
> >Change the IPADDR= line.
> 
> So if I leave this file in the /etc/sysconfig/network-scripts it will
> automatically get run at boot?

Yes, that is right.

You can also use the /sbin/ifup and /sbin/ifdown commands to bring
interfaces up and down manually.

Eg. "ifup ppp0" will start your ppp connection.  "ifdown eth0:1" will
bring down your second alias for the eth0 device.
