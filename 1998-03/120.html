Re: Ralph Nader and Microsoft

On Sat, 21 Mar 1998, mtippett wrote:

> In the quotes Mr Nader recommends PC manufacturers include
> the option of installing other operating systems such as
> Linux, BeOS, Rapsody, etc.

The really good thing about all this (i.e forthcoming relaxation
(forced) of M$ stranglehold on pre-installation of Win95) is that people
can get Linux pre-installed.  Linux developers can relax their
(considerable) efforts at making Linux easy to install - the installation
can be done by the "experts" at the shop.

An explosion in the number of Linux installations will surely follow,
given that, for many people, installation is too difficult or scary, or
both.
