Not necessary, can already do using:

"Daryl/LinuxSA/LinuxSA - IN"	Resent-From	linuxsa
"Daryl/LinuxSA/LinuxSA - IN"	Resent-To	linuxsa

This is the contents of sort.dat for Netscape Navigator 2.02 for OS/2.

It looks for the string "linuxsa" in both:
"Resent-From" &
"Resent-To"
 header fields & puts it in a folder:
 Daryl/LinusSA/LinuxSA - IN
