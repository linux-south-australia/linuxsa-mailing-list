Bleatings of a newbie

I only got onto this list 1 day ago, despite being a Linux addict since
24 Aug 1995.

I  have been quite impressed with the level of discussion, there must be
seething pit of hardcore developers in SA.

I have noticed one minor thing .. Everyone on the list seems to be working
at either BHP, or DSTO. This is really weird, as I came from a big Linux
site at Western Mining to work as a contractor for BHP, working on a
project at the DSTO site, and then get a phone call out the blue from a guy
asking me to join LinuxSA !

Linux does strange things to your life !
