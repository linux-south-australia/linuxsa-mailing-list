Hi David,

Here is an excerpt from comp.os.linux.announce circa Dec 97:

--snip--
There is an experimental release of pptp for Linux available. Some words
from the author ("C. Scott Ananian" <cananian@lcs.mit.edu>) (who is
available on the list) :

The code is at:

 http://www.pdos.lcs.mit.edu/~cananian/pptp-linux.tar.gz

You've been warned: it's very rough.  I wouldn't call it "experimental,"
though -- I spent time on the important parts, and the underlying protocol
and algorithms are sound and have been extensively tested.  I use this
stuff. But the user interface and packaging still leave much to be desired.

I'd appreciate if people drop me a line when they download it, just to let
me know how many copies are floating about.
--snip--

The whole article is available on the web at:
http://www.cs.helsinki.fi/~wirzeniu/linux/cola.archive/1997-12/mjr.1997-12-11.012
and probably other places too.  It also contains details of a mailing list
for folks who are implementing the pptp protocol.

Hope this helps.
