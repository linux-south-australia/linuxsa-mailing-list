Re: xdlx, dlxasm

On Mon, 23 Mar 1998, Alan Boldock wrote:

> I'm studying at Adelaide Uni and was wondering if any one 
> has a copy, or knows of a copy of xdlx and dlxasm for linux?

"xdlx" and "dlxasm" were mainly written by ppl here at Adelaide uni. You
should be able to get the source from ftp.cs.adelaide.edu.au. However
"xdlx" is extremely hard to compile. It uses an ancient version of libfwf
and won't compile with the newer ones. I tried to hunt the right version
of libfwf down but couldn't.

"dlxasm" compiles ok. There is a text version of "xdlx" called "sim" (or
"dlx" here at uni). I think it is in the same archive as "xdlx". It's not
great, but it is possible to work under it.
