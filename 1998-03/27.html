Metro-X is an X server, provided with the Red Hat Linux Official Boxed
Set as an alternative to XFree, but for most video cards, you will
find that XFree runs better than Metro-X.  The advantage of purchasing
Metro-X even if you don't use it is that Metro Link are big supporters
of the XFree project, so you help the development of XFree.  Metro
Link have already donated their dynamic loading technology and their
multi-head stuff to the XFree project.
