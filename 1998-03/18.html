However when doing a vgrep[0] of your mail file, especially when you don't
have access to mail sorting, the subject line is the only real choice.

I use pine (my only choice, and preference) which displays Date:, From:,
Subject.  I don't have access to the Resent-From: header until I am
reading the email.  I definately prefer to have mailing lists identify
their posts by prepending a tag to the subject line.  It makes it easier
to spot the posts that have been sent to me directly.
