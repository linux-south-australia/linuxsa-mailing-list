LinuxSA mailing list archives

Hi all,

It's not much, but I thought I'd let you all know that there are
archives of the LinuxSA list currently available at
http://www.linuxsa.org.au/mailing-list/

The messages go back to when the list was moved to
Elizabeth.netcraft.com.au (1486 of them so far), but they are all
lumped together and there is no search facility yet, although you can
read them sorted by thread, date, subject, or author.

Many thanks to Phil Nitschke for providing a list of programs that
will create HTML mail archives.  If anyone is interested, I'm
currently using wmf (Web Mail Folder).

My plan is to break the archives up by month, and add a search
facility.  I might have a look at hypermail and mhonarc too.
