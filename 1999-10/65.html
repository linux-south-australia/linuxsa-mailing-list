Hi Jim,

Well, in the absence of other replies, I'd suggest a search on the ibm
support site to verify the processor on your E20. Odds are that it is a
PowerPC, and if so it might be worth trying linuxppc on it. You may
even find info on supported hardware over at linucppc.com.

There is another PowerPC distribution called Yellow Dog, I haven't seen
it, but it might be worth a look also.
