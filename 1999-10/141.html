Phil's Helpful Hint#1455, for Newbies ;)
----------------------------------------
IF you type   help  on any linux system, you will get a somewhat
confusing & unhelpful screen containing numerous commands, command line
options etc etc. On SUSE Linux, the german word for help (hilfe) is a
valid command & links you to the SUSE-Help system. Its html & allows you
to view howto's, faqs, whatis stuff & a whole lot more!
So,If you run SUSE-6.2 (or earlier) do this...

ln -s /usr/bin/hilfe  /usr/bin/RTFM

NOW... when you have a problem, type  RTFM   and then hit enter! ;)
Now... you can say with total honesty that you did RTFM!
(AND, there is every chance that you WILL learn stuff & perhaps figure
out your problem on your own.)
