Re:Can't format ext2 partition in SuSE....

Sorry this answer is so late.... I just found out myself yesterday
installing SuSe6.2

The trick is to make the new partition _inside_ the extended DOS
partition.
That is, select the extended partition, then 'create' the new linux
partition with it's parameters. The bootable linux root partition must
still be inside the 1024 cylinder limit though. But YaST handles most of
the tricky parts, and tells you when it wont work.

BTW: SuSE is much nicer than Redhat... (imho)
