Concentrator Box

Hi,
I have bought second hand, 2 IBM Concentrator Boxes and have been unable to 
get connectivity with my Ethernet Interface cards.
Joining the PC's together (directly) with a crossover cable works fine, but 
different configurations using the Concentrator Box hasn't worked at all.

I was assured that these Concentrator boxes are equivalent to 10Mbs 
Ethernet Hubs.
Other than the RJ-45 connectors, there is nothing to suggest that they are 
Ethernet, a look around IBM's home page suggests they may be Token Ring, 
something I'm not familiar with.

The units did not come with any transformers, I supplied my own, which is a 
risky business, but all looks ok inside with the regulators outputting 
correct voltages. The only indicator, an amber LED, flickers briefly on 
power up.

Any advice is most welcome, model numbers are:
	16 port Concentrator Box FRU # 91F0817
	Suitable Transformers 53F3369, 70F9937, 70F9938, -39, -40

I used a 24V CT (12+12) secondary winding in lieu of the IBM transformer.
