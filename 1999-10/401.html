On Mon, 25 Oct 1999, Brian Astill wrote:
>At 12:52 PM 24/10/99 -0700, you wrote:
>>
>>It happily says "Loading win" and THAT is all. It boots my two other linux
>>partitions perfectly. Incidentally I did mean primary slave (master slave is
>>something to do with B & D).

>>DAVID

>Excuse my butting in, but did you say what is on the Primary master?
>SFAIK, Windoze expects to be given pride of place, whatever exists.  If
>Primary Master partition exists, THAT's where MS expects to find its boot
>sector.
>
>You CAN overcome this with Boot Magic (comes with Partition Magic) or some
>similar intervening device - but not Lilo.
>

Um, not quite true.
Try something like this: (this is an extract from my lilo.conf)
other=/dev/hdc1
        label=dross
        table=/dev/hdc
        map-drive=0x80
           to = 0x81
        map-drive=0x81
           to = 0x80

in /etc/lilo.conf.
It 'fools' windoze into thinking it is on the primary master.

(I have the same setup, i.e. linux on primary master, and windoze on secondary
master)
