AX25 in SuSE-6.2

Hi all, this message is directed to the Amateur Radio Operators who
frequent this list & who may be using the ax25 apps in SuSE-6.2

You may experience wierd probs when you use "call" when connecting to
other linux boxes  - standard prob is an "invalid command" error message
from the station u connect to - for some reason, call generates random
garbage characters periodically making it very difficult to stay
connected... dos ax25 apps seem more tolerant!

The fix is to d/load the latest ax25-apps & compile/install them -
default installation is to /usr/local/ so rm /usr/bin/call
/usr/bin/listen and mv /etc/ax25 or /usr/etc/ax25 to /usr/local/ax25

Cya, Phil
