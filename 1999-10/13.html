RE: Samba again..

To get it going at first it is often easiest to set

security=share 

and make your samba shares

guest ok = true
public = true

Ok you will have no security, but at least you will have contact. From here
you can then fiddle with other options to tighten up security and
authenticate users properly. 

You may wish to check the syntax as I have just jotted them down from
memory.
