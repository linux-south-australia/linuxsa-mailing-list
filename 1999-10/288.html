Re: Uninstalling Compiled Packages

lloy0076 wrote:

 > Does anyone know of any good programs which can automatically remove a
 > "make install"ed package? Every now and then I build something and it
 > just refuses to work or I just get rid of it and sniffing all the
 > installed packages manually is time consuming and prone to error.

I usually prepare for that before reaching the "make install" stage:
With GNU configure, you can say "./configure --prefix=/usr/local/progname"
to have it install into its own directory.  When you "make install"
it'll create /usr/local/progname/bin, /usr/local/progname/lib, 
/usr/local/progname/etc, and so on; you can later remove the entire
package by running "rm -rf /usr/local/progname"
