Re: protecting dialup lines

Justin Cannan wrote:

> Any ideas on how I can work out where they are calling from and who they
> might be trying to log in as?

You need to add the following lines to the syslog.conf:

daemon.debug	/var/log/daemon
local2.debug	/var/log/local2

In the options to setup ppp add the following:

debug
kdebug 7

In /var/log/daemon a number of LCP requests and various other matters
should appear when a negotiation occurs. Essentially, the basic thing to
do is to put full security on and full debug on and watch the log files
like a hawk...

man pppd will be your friend and man mgetty will be your friend to tell
you more than I have how to do this.
