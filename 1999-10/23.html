#linuxsa IRC channel on irc.linux.org.au

People,

To get things happening a bit more within LinuxSA, I have started lurking
and talking on the #linuxsa channel on irc.linux.org.au.  

Everyone can come down and have a chat and do things in real time, not mail
time.

So if you know what IRC is then hit irc.linux.org.au and /join #linuxsa.

If you don't have a look at http://www.irchelp.org/.  Some IRC clients
you can use under Linux are ircII, xchat and BitchX - all available from
the net and indexed at http://freshmeat.net/.  xchat does come with the
standard install of RH 6.0 (and there is a 1.0 update available on your
nearest RH mirror).
