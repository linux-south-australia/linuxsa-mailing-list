Well, this might not be the most elegant solution, but i have tested it
and it works:

call this rmspace or something, chmod+x and put in your path someplace,

Then rmspace /home/MWP/problemfiledir/  and your problemfiles are no
more with spaces replaced with underscores.

-------------------------------------------
#!/bin/sh
cd $1
for file in *; do
  mv  "$file" `echo $file | sed 's/ /_/g'`
done 
--------------------------------------------

that ` next to echo and at the end are backquotes, up under the tilde(~)

Michael
