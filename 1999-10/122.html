Re: LI-LO getting worse?

As you can see from below, Nick has done a few things to alleviate the
problem of being unable to boot Linux from hdb using lilo.

This has the smell of geometry problems, and while Nick has checked and it
looks like both drives are in LBA mode at the BIOS, when Linux is running
(using a boot floppy),

   cat /proc/ide/hda/geometry
   physical   3148/16/63
   logical    3148/16/63

While those for hdb show that the logical cylinders is less than 1024.

This seems to suggest that hda is not in LBA mode, while hdb is in LBA mode.

Now, I would not have thought that this matters, but Nick cannot boot.

Can anyone suggest anymore things to check?
