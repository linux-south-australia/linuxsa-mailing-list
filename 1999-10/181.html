Linux C Mailing List (ANNOUNCEMENT)

LINUX C MAILING LISTS
---------------------

There are now over 400 people subscribed to the Linux C Mailing List.
The Linux C Mailing Lists were created by David Lloyd and Chuck Mead
approximately two months ago. They were formed for the purpose of
supporting the devlopment of linux with C. David hopes that there will
be no question too difficult or too simple for the list to answer.

There's a very simple way to subscribe to the Linux C Mailing List. Send
an email with no subject or body to the following address:

mailto:linuxCprogramming@listbot.com

To find out a little more, visit our home pages at:

http://users.senet.com.au/~lloy0076/linux_c_programming/index.html

David has been quite surprised by the support for the Linux C Mailing
Lists. Please, check our home page out or join us if you want to find
out more about what we're about.
