RE: Qt

If your interested in finding what got installed and where in a Redhat system using rpm's you might use something like:

	rpm -qa | grep qt
which on my systems returns
qt-1.44-6
qt-devel-1.44-6
then:
	rpm -qil qt
or
	rpm -qil qt-devel
will give a short description of the package and list all of the files and where they were installed.
