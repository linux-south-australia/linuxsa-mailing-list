Hi!

On Sun, Oct 17, 1999 at 01:33:21PM +0930, lloy0076 wrote:

> I thought that this just dumped the object files so that make recompiled
> eerything but it didn't actually REMOVE the program.

Correct. Chances are there's no automatic method to remove the package
itself in the way that (I guess) you mean, similar to Microsoft's Add/Remove
Programs system or a rpm/dpkg remove operation. The only method is to manually
read through the Makefile and go forth into the filesystem, armed with your
trusty rm command. If it's a well behaved non-system package, it may have even
been nice and not scattered files all over the place, and you can remove it
with a bit of discerning directory pruning.
