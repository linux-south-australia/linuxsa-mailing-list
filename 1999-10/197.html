This is normal, phil:  The history is keyed by URL, and if a URL
starts magically working then that's the copy you'll end up with in
your history.

The real question is why MS's server reports that a page doesn't exist
when it quite manifestly *does*.  I suspect they have multiple servers
at the back-end somewhere, and the page is missing from some of them;
Depending on which server you hit, you'll either get what you're looking
for or a "page not found" message.
