>What do people think of Turbolinux? Is it a good distribution? I have

It is horses for courses. It did a very good job of configuring X on an AGP
card back when RedHat did not understand the same card.  That was TL 3.4.0
vs RH 5.2, but now Linux Mandrake does just as good a job, while TLW 4.0 is
having problems understanding a ViewTop Titan 4000 ...

TurboLinux has two packages:

  TurboLinux Workstation - A workstation package, is missing some server 
  				oriented RPMs

  TurboLinux Server      - A server package.  Will come with DB2 or something
				like that.

It also uses GLIBC2.0, not GLIBC2.1, which unfortunately means that RPMs
are often not portable between RH and TL.  This means that it is more for
the business environment, not the casual home user.

However, I use it, along with RH and Mandrake.
