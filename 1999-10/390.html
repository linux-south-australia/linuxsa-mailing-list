Re: Can't start X

Hi,

I just had this problem last week. Check your X11 config file at:
/etc/X11/XF86Config

    FontPath   "/usr/X11R6/lib/X11/fonts/TrueType/"
#    FontPath   "unix/:-1"

After I comment out above line, X back to normal again.
