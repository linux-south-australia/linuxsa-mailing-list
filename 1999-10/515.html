Re: lp0 strangeness on 2.2.12

> Has anyone had trouble with printing ...
> The system is RH 6.1, kernel 2.2.12-20 ...

According to Red Hat, printing is broken in Red Hat
6.1.  The documented fix is adding the line:
alias parport_lowlevel parport_pc
to /etc/conf.modules

I guess this is only if the parallel port device is
compiled as a module, tho.
