Re: lp0 strangeness on 2.2.12

Hi!

On Sun, Oct 31, 1999 at 04:34:53PM +1030, michael wrote:

> When I try to print something, the light on the printer blinks a few
> times, but nuffin prints. I've tried the EOF fix in the control panel, but
> there was no dif. nor did 'cat file >/dev/lp0' do anything but blink the
> light. 

I had similar problems; fixed them by playing with the lp module options and
forcing it to not do interrupt driven printing. i.e. in conf.modules I have:

options parport_pc io=0x278 irq=none
options lp parport=0

If you're not using modules, you'll have to force this at bootup with cmdline
options or your LILO config. Good luck. :)
