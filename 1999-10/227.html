cdrecord has all the power and flexbility of any windows cdr program,
except that it's command line driven, so you can script it.

Try a few mkisofs commands to build your preferred bootable iso image
from sources all over your disk, then just pipe it straight into
cdrecord. What could be easier!!

As mentioned there are some graphical front ends for cdrecord as well,
if you want to drop back on functionality and increase the prettyness.
