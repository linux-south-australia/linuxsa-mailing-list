Re: SBLive support in Linux....

I just installed a Creative Labs SB card and had all sorts of probs....
Until I read /usr/src/linux/Documentation/sound/VIBRA16 and followed the
advice there.
Assuming you have isapnp tools, just compile with these options only....

CONFIG_SOUND=m
CONFIG_SOUND_OSS=m
CONFIG_SOUND_SB=m

Edit isapnp.conf and reboot. Worked for me!  :)
