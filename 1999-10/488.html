Matrox Displays

There was talk on this list a while back of Matrox Orbital LCD displays.

Well, there is now another Australian Re-seller, based here in Adelaide.

The software that comes with it is quite cool. Straight out of the box,
it will display most things you would want to know about your server,
and with a bit of coding, you can make it display pretty much whatever
you want.
