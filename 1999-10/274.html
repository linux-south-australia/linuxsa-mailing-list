Hi,
    Try "make clean" in the directory you did the "make
install". This is supposed to clean up the mess you might
have made, ie delete all of the binaries and object files
etc, and also leave you with just the source. If this
doesn't work, then you have a very badly written make file,
and so you will have to print it out and manually delete all
of the files that were created in the make.

because even though it works all of your drivers are not
