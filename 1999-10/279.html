I don't know about  SCSI drives but do they suffer from the <1024 limit
as well? The kernel may not want to sit on the drive because it is being
asked to load onto a partition which extends past 1024. You might want
to look at man lilo (info lilo) and any of the lilo or booting howtos
under /usr/doc.
