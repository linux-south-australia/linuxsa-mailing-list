Re: HDD Problems

I have had this problem with a Western Digital 850Mb HDD a *long* time ago.
I was given the run around because it was "Intermittant" and the drive
returned to me unfixed with "dont come back until it is dead".   To fix my
problem I got out my trusty 9Vac 300mA plug pack, held one wire to the
chassis and the proceeded to run the other wire around every large IC I
could find.   The result was a completely dead drive which was then
replaced under warrenty, I would have loved to hear the goods returned
Engineers explanation of why that drive failed... Ha !.

But of course I cannot condone the same course of action.....   ahhh....
the things we do in our *younger* days....

Best advice is to take the drive back and take a firm stance that the drive
is intermittant and should be replaced under warenty.
