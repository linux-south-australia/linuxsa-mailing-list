Well, actually, not quite. Not really.

What the virus is doing, is taking random documents and emailing them
between addresses in the infected computer's address book (MS of course)

The viruses (virii?) I have seen in the last couple of days have had many
different documents and email attachments, some have been the same, but
probably because they came from the same infected computer. Many have had
attachments like documentxyz.doc.pif which hides the virus payload for our
unsuspecting outlook users.

By nature the content of the virus generated email will be different for
every infection.
