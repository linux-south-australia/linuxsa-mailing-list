Internode Email you when you hit 80% and at levels after that.

They will be offering no-excess plans which shape your traffic down to say 
64kbps when you go "over" a certain limit.  This means it'll go slower but 
you won't get a massive bill.

I haven't really gone over my limit.  But when you d/l something big (eg. a 
linux distro before they had free servers) it can be close.

ADmittedly, I'm not a massive d/ler nor do I play online games.
