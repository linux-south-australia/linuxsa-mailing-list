Linux.Conf.Au 2003 Sun Regional Delegate Programme

Hi all,

The linux.conf.au Sun Microsystems Regional Delegate Programme (RDP)
was announced this morning.  Thanks to Sun, the RDP provides funding
for one person from each state and territory in Australia to attend
linux.conf.au.  The package includes:

- Return economy air ticket from your closest major airport to Perth

- Accommodation at the on-campus lodgings (Currie Hall) for the
  duration of the main conference (excluding the mini-confs that may
  be organised)

- Professional Registration for Linux.conf.au 2003 

About the only thing not included is the food you eat (with the
exception of the conference dinner).

To be eligible for selection as the South Australian Delegate you must
be a resident of South Australia, and be able to demonstrate a
contribution (however small, and not necessarily in software) to the
Linux/Open Source community.  The successful applicant will be
expected to deliver a talk about their experience at the conference at
a future LinuxSA meeting.

Being involved with LinuxSA is not a requirement for eligibility.

For information on how to apply to be the South Australian Delegate,
see http://www.linuxsa.org.au/rdp.html

For more information about the RDP, see http://linux.conf.au/rdp.html

For more information about LCA, see http://linux.conf.au/

Have fun,
