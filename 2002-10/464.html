Re: Lycoris Desktop/XL on a 98 system?

I stuck it on a HP Omnibook 3000 (P233/48Mb/4.2Gb), to dual boot with Win 98
SE. The install is fantastic - you get to play solitaire while it installs.
:-) Unfortunately, I get as far as the desktop coming up then things go
pearshaped (i.e. stop dead). Given I haven't tried it on a faster machine,
it might either be: a) some weirdness to do with hardware compat b)the fact
that the machine is waayyyy too slow for it c)I'm an idiot (and should stick
to something I'm more familiar with, like FreeBSD).

My guess is the order of b, c and a: Its a slow machine period, I haven't
played with a Linux distro for yonks, it seemed to recognise the important
bits of hardware during the setup/first boot. OK - so trying to install it
on such a slow machine is a bit dumb.. gotta try these things.

I haven't had a chance to put it on something faster yet, or I might be of
more help. Have you checked out their website? My hopes of the system being
useful to me on anything seemed to drop when I went through their site. It
looks to be promising in the future though.. maybe.
