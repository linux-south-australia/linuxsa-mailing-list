Re: [OT] HELP!!! Can not find decent priced PCI ONLY wireless cards!

I think you'll find that the pcmcia interface is built in to the
chipset, so board manufacturers dont have much choice. While they are
selling anyway its not worth it for the chip manufacturers to put a PCI
interface on it either. When the pci ones come out, I suspect you'll see
its just a normal pcmcia chip, and a pcmcia bus on the same board, but
we'll see...
