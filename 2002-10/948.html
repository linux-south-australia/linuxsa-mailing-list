Yes, the semicolon (;) is required to terminate the exec augument.
To better understand what is happening, try something like
find /foo/bar -type d -exec echo {} \;
Which will just display the directoies that matches. Note: This is a bit pointless as find /foo/bar -type d -print appears to be identical.
or
find /foo/bar -type d -exec ls -l {} \;
