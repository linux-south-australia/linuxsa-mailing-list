Re: status of transmeta?

"Mcleod, Ian" <ian.mcleod@eds.com> asks:
> What's the status of Transmeta?  Does anyone know if they will eventually
> put out a pin compatible chip and cause a huge stir and lots of respective
> 'white papers' from Intel and AMD on why Transmeta sucks?

I'm not sure that they'll ever do that.  Transmeta doesn't produce an
Intel compatible chip.  Their Intel compatibility comes from binary
translation, which translates and caches IA-32 code to Transmeta code
when it is first needed.

Perhaps they could do IA-64, Alpha, and Sparc translation, too.
