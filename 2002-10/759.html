[OT] Community Software Distribution Network

Hi There,

As you'd all know I held a BBQ at my place last Saturday both as a
social function and to discuss what was then called the "Open Source
Distribution Network". I've renamed it because someone pointed out that
its acronym--OSDN--could be mistaken for the "Open Source Developers'
Network".

Two of the action items we made on Saturday were to:

1) Register an IRC Channel

 server: irc.openprojects.net
 channel: #csdn-sa

2) Setup a mailing list to discuss it

 subscribe: mail csdn-sa-request@freelists.org with subject of
'subscribe'
 
 NOTE: the e-mail address is csdn-sa-request <<- include the request or
you'll have to wait for me to subscribe you

We will meet again in the third week of November at my place to sort
this out and report on all of our other findings.

I'll take the discussion of this onto csdn-sa@freelists.org now but you
should be aware that we should be able to distribute copies of: FreeBSD 
4.7, RedHat 8.0, Mandrake 9.0, SuSe 8.0 and Open Office 1.0. I have
access to a few exotic distributions, such as Yellow Dog for PowerPC and
SuSe 6.0 for Sparcs as well.

The official web-site is yet to be announced, but a mockup site (with
false e-mail addresses except mine) can be found at:

* http://www.adam.com.au/csdn/
