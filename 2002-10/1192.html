Remedial CVS

After months of trying to figure out CVS, I was finally able to use it, 
with lots of training wheels and help from Pete.

I still need some training wheels. :-D

Anyway, I finally found a cvs application that lets me be really lazy 
while I use it. Click n' yawn.

Smart CVS, which really should be called _CVS for dummies_, is only 1 of 
2 cvs clients that I found I could use. It is intuitive, user friendly, etc.

Anyway, I wrote down everything I did to get it to work, included some 
pretty pictures, made it into a pdf file and uploaded to:

http://www.morton2001.org/cvs.pdf

I hope it will be helpful to someone.


James Leone
