Re: Network QOS and DL Limit management (Longish)

michael wrote:

> IPCop is currently on kernel 2.2.21, and my reading of tc/tc-ng tells me
> that 2.4.x is probably required for an effective QOS system.

Note that "an effective QOS system" is effective only on outbound traffic -
on the inbound side, it's too late once the traffic has already hit your
box (i.e., it needs to be placed on the router upstream from you, which
requires their consent).

> Any suggestions/comments on best practice, and methods of implementing the
> download limits?

If you're using HTTP and Squid, you'd be better off using the bandwidth
limiting features of that.
