Re: [OT] HELP!!! Can not find decent priced PCI ONLY wireless cards!

Mcleod, Ian wrote:
> I have looked EVERYWHERE - I can not find PCI ONLY wireless cards?  Surecom
> are bringing one out by Christmas but this *is not good enough*!
> 
> All I am finding are cheap PCMCIA cards which are still expensive with the
> require PCMCIA -> PCI adaptor.

There are no PCI-only cards.

This is a chipset issue (why design two), an economies of
scale issue (ie: you've got to put the receiver in a metal
box anyway, so why not a PCMCIA-sized metal box), and a
regulatory issue (you've got to pay to get the radio tested,
so why not test just one and use it for both laptops and PCs).

As a result, all "PCI only" cards are actually PCMCIA cards
mounted in a PCI carrier.  The only difference being if you
supplier the PCI carrier or if the vendor does.  You might
get more options (diversity antenna jacks, etc) on vendor-
supplied carriers.
