The Calgary office of Metso Automation SCADA are looking for people.

This "security" position is new to the company....


POSITION:               Technical Specialist, Security

DEPARTMENT:             Engineering

LOCATION:               Calgary

RESPONSIBILITIES:

The Technical Specialist, Security position provides demonstrated
excellence in the security and delivery of complex applications and
systems.  The successful candidate will start by working on the
ChevronTexaco project.  This position will provide leadership and interface
with the rest of the company (including Customer Service, ITM, RTD and
Engineering) for all security issues by building and implementing new
security procedures for setting up systems (both in UNIX and Windows) as
well as security issues regarding OASyS and business applications.  These
security issues may include:  internet/intranet, e-Business architecture,
website architecture, database, network, remote access, firewall, UNIX,
application level security issues and securing global access to systems.

An individual at this level may be involved in any aspect of the department
activities, on a technical level, or involved in the business of running a
project.   He/she will have a general familiarity with a broad field of
technology and will have demonstrated expertise in one or more technical
areas.

At this level an individual may work directly with our Customer's
Engineering and Operations staff to determine the requirements of a system.
This individual may be assigned a project role, which would involve them in
the technical leadership and planning activities of a project.  Problems
are solved using a combination of standard procedures and new approaches
when required.

Leadership responsibilities will include providing technical guidance to
other members of the department.  The Technical Specialist, Security may be
involved in the development of new applications and technologies within
his/her areas of expertise.



QUALIFICATIONS


     A Bachelor's Degree in Engineering, Computer Science or related
discipline such as Math, Physics or Geoscience, with 7+ years related
post-secondary experience, or
     A Technology diploma with 10+ years related post-secondary experience
(related post-secondary experience means software/systems development,
preferably in the SCADA industry).
     Security expertise working with Windows and UNIX in an IT type
environment;
     Systems skills with Win2000 Admin, DNS, DHCP, Active Directories;
     Experience in the entire life cycle, developing medium or large-scale
computer/software systems.
     Experience in the leadership and management of small teams of
engineering/software analyst staff;
     Experience in the management of requirements scope control;
     Experience in developing solutions of a highly technical nature for
real-time systems;
     Experience in providing accurate cost/effort estimates for solutions,
which have not yet been fully developed.

extension 2258 or email.  Please refer to file #C02-93.

CLOSING DATE:

November 8, 2002
