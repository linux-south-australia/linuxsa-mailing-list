RE: How many Unix applications are there?

I think this should be categorised.  I Perl script on Freshmeat to print
'hello world' in weird and wonderful ways is still an 'application'.

Perhaps this should include a category just for applications that are
regarded as 'applications' in the traditional sense - full blown projects or
applications like office and development applications.

I don't think fly-by-night GPL scripts on Freshmeat should be included in
any count to be presented to the non-Linux public.

It could also be misleading where 10 front ends exist for say 'cdrecord'
although it is arguable that this does not constitute 10 wholly seperate
'applications'.

I just think it would be easy to say 'yeah Linux has 10 million
applications' without really meaning anything.
