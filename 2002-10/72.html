Re: OT: Hub or Switch

On Wednesday,  2 October 2002 at  0:21:36 +0930, Ian Pilkington wrote:
> Looks like another computer is going to be added to my house's collection.
>
> Just wondering what people would recommend for the following
>
> A Linux box serving as an Internet Gateway serving 2 computers (Windoz
> boxes) with the possibility of adding "more" in the future.
>
> While I under stand the basic differences between a hub & switch and notice
> that switches are relatively cheap just wondering what would be the best
> idea and provide the best overall output.

A switch every time.  Even with only two computers, you get
full-duplex operation, though I don't know if Microsoft can take
advantage of it.  With more computers, you get higher throughput.  The
only possible advantage of a hub would be price.
