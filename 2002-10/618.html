RE: when do hard drives go to heaven?

Depends which bit breaks. Generally spinning them up is what stresses
them. Personally I diable power saving on my machines, to ensure the
disks dont stop spinning. I also have had a very good run with hard
disks. Every pre 500mb HD I have had has crashed, but none since <touch
wood>.
