Ian,

I use an application called guarddog for my firewall. You can find it at

http://www.simonzone.com/software/guarddog/

It is a graphical app. that is very easy to use.

For masquerade I use a complementary app called (strangely enough)
guidedog (available from the same site). This one configures irc and ftp
for you.

fyi: my machine functions as a gateway for my 'homenet' and my kids go
thru to the net via it (squid proxy). There have been no complaints
about access.
