Actually I was thinking something that, for instance, Internode could 
provide:

- just offer free access to download the ISOs via an access point
- just provide it, for instance, in Hindmarsh square (ie. next to their 
  offices).
- All you can do is download files from an FTP server.

Pretty easy.  Okay, so a 6-7Mbps shared link ain't idea but you should be 
able to download a few ISOs within a lunch hour.  All you need is a wireless 
card and a laptop.

he works from home, so he rode over with his laptop and just connected to my 
wireless lan)
