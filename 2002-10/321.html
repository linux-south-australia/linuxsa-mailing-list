Guilty. I use virge, and it works quite well for ~100 users. Most of our
desktops are Macintosh, so there's little point clogging their inbox with
.exe and .pif etc files anyhow.


> What's the best AV product or solution or whatever for a simple sendmail
> setup?  running linux of course.

Given the above situation, I haven't found a reasonable solution yet. The
AV companies want to license per user, even though I haven't seen a Mac
Virus for quite a while. It's cheaper to use a per-desktop Norton/Vet
solution for us. 20 Nortons is way cheaper than 100 AV licenses. Last time
I checked, even Win shops would find the licensed server based solutions
dear by comparison.
