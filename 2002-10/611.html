Re: nvidia causing lockups?

Hi Ryan,
I had the same problem with my SuSE 7.3 and 8.0 setup up.
I use the same CPU, motherboard and video card as you, just less HDD's :)
All I had to do was drop an entry into the 'append' line of  /etc/lilo.conf

append=disableapic
or  if that doesn't work try
append=noapic

I suppose I should recompile my kernel to disable APIC that way, but 
this is a quick and eaasy work around. I was having lockups every day or 
two, but since disableing apic I've had none for nearly 12 months.


Cheers,
