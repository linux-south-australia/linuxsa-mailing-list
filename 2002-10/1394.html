That sounds like the CD is corrupt or the HD is corrupt.

Do the following steps:

1) press ctrl+alt+F1 at the same time

2) Look

3) Press alt+F1, alt+F2, alt+F3, alt+F4

4) For each of these LOOK.

I'm looking for anything that says "seek error" or anything with error.

Write it down.

Post it to us.

5) Once you've finished that press "alt+F7"

...to get back to the installer. Try F8 or F9 if that doesn't work.
