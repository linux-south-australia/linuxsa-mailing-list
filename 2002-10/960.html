if you are on your work LAN, i'd say you are well and truly behind a
firewall.  Firwalling / NAT rules you can do anything, including blocking
everything except pinging (ICMP protocol) to force you to use a work proxy

and ask for a proxy server, your mail server, dns server you should be using
(sometimes a private on might be setup)

what?    two different kettles of fish.  if you have NAT at home, can you

TCP-IP basics?
