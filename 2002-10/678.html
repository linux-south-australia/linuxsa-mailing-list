Thanks for ppl who expressed help :) :)

it was actually the ipchains rules that were in place
(smoothwall)... it just seemed strange that one packet
would always make the return journey and no more..

so after i changed policys (to ACCEPT all) and flushed
the chains, ping was working fine..

i dunno if this is strange, but i'm glad its worked
out cos i've seen this a couple of other times and it
always annoyed me
