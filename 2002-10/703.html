Re: ne2k or ne2k-pci

Many ISA cards are supported in Linux. Try this to initiate the driver.
modprobe ne
Another alternative is to purchase a PCI ethernet card. They are inexpensive 
at $25 or so. Cards with the RealTek chip are widely available and supported.
