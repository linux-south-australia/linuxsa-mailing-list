The point here is that it was dropped from 100/110 to 80 because people 
were having accidents where they were driving to fast for the 
conditions, eg doing 70 around a corner that should be taken at 50, or 
driving too fast in the rain. Dropping the speed limit does nothing to 
force these people to drive better, but does force you to do 80 on 
straights that can safely be taken at 100. Laws should be used to 
enforce proper behaviour while still being acceptable to those who _do_ 
behave properly. Speed limits are upper limits, not recomendations 
(thats what the yellow signs are for).
