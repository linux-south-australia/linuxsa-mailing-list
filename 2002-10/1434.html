Re: [hardware] Tagged Command Queuing or Larger Cache ?

What's the problem you're solving (or at least what kind of problem)?

Buffering is more useful for large or predictable transfers.

Tagging is more useful for random access stuff (databases) as the drive can 
figure it out itself.

However, for performance, go SCSI, it does this stuff MUCH better and is why 
it's used in servers more ...
