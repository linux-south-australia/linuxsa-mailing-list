Usb storage device on MDK 9.0

Hello all,

First findings of MDK 9.0. I mention this on the list because i have just
been through the whole USB thing with redhat and mdk 9 pre release.

I have just seen MDK9.0 full release for the first time. I plugged in my usb
storage device and to my supprise, it added an icon to the KDE desktop. 

It mounted it ok but unfortunatly it's shortcut didn't actually locate the
files on the drive. (It went looking on the internet somewhere... strange.)
However: "#ls /mnt/removable" did display the files on that device. There
was no mounting or module loading required of the user. Very cool...  :)
