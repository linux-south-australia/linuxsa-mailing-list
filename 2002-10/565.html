Certainly not without *some* mucking around, but if you can figure out
how to set up Linux software RAID, you can figure out how to recover
from a dead drive in Linux software RAID - very straight forward.

I'd suggest that you play about with it by setting up RAID 1, taking a
drive out, formatting it, and then trying to get the RAID to re-sync the
original drive back. Once you know that you can do that, you're ready to go!
