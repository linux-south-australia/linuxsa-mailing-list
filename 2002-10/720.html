> 
> http://monmotha.mplug.org
> 
> and a reference guide for it is at....
> 
> http://www.mplug.org/phpwiki/index.php?MonMothaReferenceGuide

> It's very simple to setup and use. Just edit a few lines at the top of
> the script to set the variables like INET_IFACE and TCP_ALLOW
> etc...etc.... then chmod +x it and run it. Don't be fooled by the
> version numbers MonMotha uses, and his "development" code is quite
> stable for home lan's and even production boxes if you want. Just get
> the latest.
> 
> He even has a mailing list to answer your questions and resolve any
> problems you may have, with links on his page to subscribe. Or you can
> try asking him directly (when he's awake) by joining #linuxhelp on any
> EFNET irc server.
> 
> It's the simplest firewall script to use that I've found. It takes me
> about 3 minutes to setup and run. Then just do iptables-save or if
> you're using redhat/mandrake "service iptables save" to save the
> rulesets for future boots.
