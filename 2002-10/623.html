I use Ximian Evolution (Outlook clone) and it too doesn't issue the delete
commands until all messages are retrieved.  Also it doesn't display any
new messages until it's finished (which stops your screen going crazy while
it's downloading.)

This is probably not relevant but if you have a 'nix box at the site
where your Outlook client is, use fetchmail to grab the mail over the
slow link and outlook to grab the mail over the network.
