[semi-OT] Alternatives to PC-104 (was PC-104 suppliers)

Then checkout the range of Jumptec DIMM-PC Modules (http://www.jumptec.de).
They have 386's & 586's with onboard everything, about the size of a credit
card and plug into a 72-pin SIMM socket.   On the same site you will find
the range of National Geode products aswell, these are P133 class machines
with more grunt but now you are talking $700-900, where as the DIMM-PC
stuff starts around the $350-400.   The power requirements of either system
is very modest, less than 5W for the whole module (5V@1A).   They both
would make a pretty funky router.  The only downside is one serial, one
parallel and only one ethernet port :(

This is my current budget for my new IPCOP router, you should be able to
knock these out for 1/10th the equivalent Cisco ;-)
