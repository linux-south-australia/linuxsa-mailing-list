I may be wrong, but I don't think you can code any Linux 3D accel'd apps with "/no change/" to compile on Mac OS X.

As far as I'm aware, MacOSX native apps need to be coded in Cocoa or Carbon, and most likely if you're trying to compile something that originated from a UNIX platform, you'd be looking at Cocoa.  You need a bunch of Cocoa headers put in - its minimal porting, but it wont happen automatically.

If you're talking something like Mesa, that runs in XDarwin - afaik this is /not/ 3d accelerated (yet).  I could be wrong though - I'm not a huge coder myself :)
