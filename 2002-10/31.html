Re: [OT] hardware question

Most joystick ports are also midi devices. At least the sound blaster
ones used to be. This is probably still the case on a pci sound blaster.
Then you just need the little joystick-->midi box, which can be
purchased in kit form for places like dick smith, or probably purchased
elsewhere pre built.
