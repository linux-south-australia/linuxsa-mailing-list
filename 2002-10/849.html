No - I just wish everyone would be unanimous in agreeing that this sucks and
look at what can be done.  It does suck - we clearly all agree with that.

Internode put a submission in for the broadband inquiry which is excellent -
I suggest people read it.

I am not saying I am superior - I just loath the newbie trashing that is so
prevalent in the *nix community - it really has to stop.  For us it is easy
to see what is involved - for someone else - it is perfectly reasonable that
they don't read through 10 pages of fine print (even though I always do I
don't expect everyone to do this) - they just want it to work as it is
advertised on the television to do - and this is not happening.  There is a
clear gulf between marketing hype and reality that is clearly *not* targeted
at groups of users such as ourselves that know better.

Can LinuxSA submit a paper to the broadband inquiry?  We obviously have the
analytical and technical ability to do so.
