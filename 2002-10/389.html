Prism-based cards are currently supported by the orinoco_cs driver 
(included in the kernel) and the prism2_cs driver from the wlan-ng 
package (available at http://www.linux-wlan.com/linux-wlan/). Of the 
two, wlan-ng has better and more complete support for the Prism cards.

orinoco_cs supports the iw* tools for configuration, status, and AP
scanning. prism2_cs has limited support for the iw* tools, but
includes its own set of programs with similar functions.
