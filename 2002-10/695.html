RE: DNS and unknown host

Thanks David,
Its all fixed now. I followed your suggestion of using Ethereal and very
quickly noticed the address being requested was wrong.
It looked like 'a.mydomain.mynetr' I viewed resolv.conf with a hex editor
and saw it had line terminations of 0d 0a.
Once removing the offending 0d, everything worked fine.

Surprisingly, I found VI is adding 0d 0a terminations in the file
unsuspectingly. I have no doubt I once edited the file using a Microsoft
editor but am surprised VI continues the non-Unix practice.

One to watch.

thanks again,
David
