Lets pass the hat for Linux - about time..

I am willing to put down say $10 at the next meeting - I suggest we all do
the same.

My feeling of guilt for mindlessly downloading tonnes of free GPL'd software
is getting the best of me - yet I don't always have up to $200 to spare for
a new distro.

This could be a halfway point for those of us that still want to contribute
something.

Then perhaps we can split it - with say RH, Mandrake and the kernel
developers?

I'm sure every cent helps..

LinuxSA has no membership fee - we get free use of Compaq facilities, etc -
I am happy to start giving a little back - as small as that contribution may
be in the great scheme of things..
