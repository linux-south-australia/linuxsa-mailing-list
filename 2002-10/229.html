RE: Lets pass the hat for Linux - about time..

We *definitely* do not want to formalise this - then it will just become a
bunch of meetings and documents that fail to actually achieve anything IMHO

Local contributions is a great idea - do Mandrake / Red Hat have groups in
Australia that could benefit?

It's also a good idea because our farm / mining based economy has trashed
our dollar and has rendered our currency about as valued or respected as a
certain software vendor's promises of stability and security..

At least if we keep it in the country - what little we raise will still be
worth something - which it won't be the moment it leaves our shores - plus
it is nice to keep money in the country - especially today when all we
export is what we can find in the ground (maybe we can change that by
contributing to our local IT development).
