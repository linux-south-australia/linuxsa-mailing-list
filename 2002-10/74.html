Re: OT: Hub or Switch

Ian Pilkington wrote:

>
>While I under stand the basic differences between a hub & switch and notice
>that switches are relatively cheap just wondering what would be the best
>idea and provide the best overall output.
>
A switch is better for security, the performance advantages probably 
aren't all that important on a small network. If you have just one 
system on a hub network compromised, then the attacker can sniff traffic 
from all computers on the same lan, learn passwords and generally see 
what you're doing.

With a switched network this is much more difficult, as traffic is only 
sent to the port it's intended for.
