Re: status of transmeta?

Ian,
I'd focus on the cyrix stuff - Transmeta is unlikely to produce a pin 
compatiable chip as their chip requires a non-standard bios to work (to load 
the emulation sw into memory etc).

The VIA C3 stuff is low power and pin compatible with S370 stuff.

Goto www.via.com.tw and have a look.
