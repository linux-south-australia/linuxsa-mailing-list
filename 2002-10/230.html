RE: Lets pass the hat for Linux - about time..

Good points. So next time we get a distro, and feel like someone
deserves something, we should have a look at the software in the distro,
pick an Australian author of a good part of the distro, and send $50
there way. It'll suprise them when they get it. Still be $au, and wont
have cost more than the box set of the distro that nobody seems to want
anyway. It wouldnt take many people doing it to ensure that Australian
open source developers keep developing, and might get a few more in to
it as well.
