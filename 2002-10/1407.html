A MILLION THANK YOUS TO  TUUKKA TOIVONEN  WHO MADE THE DRIVER!!

:-)


This is what I did:

1. Obtained a Logitech Quickcam Express (White ball on triangular 
plastic stand, blue centerpiece)

2. Installed the kernel sources, make & the gnu c compiler (GCC)

3. Was using the Linux Kernel version 2.4.19 in SuSE 8.1 and Lindows 2.0

4. Downloaded the driver from: 
http://www.ee.oulu.fi/~tuukkat/quickcam/quickcam.html

5. Went into the shell

6. typed ----------->gzip -dc quickcam-tt-2002-10-20.tar.gz | tar xvf -

7. Waited for messages to scroll by

8. typed------------> cd quickcam-tt

9. typed -----------> make

10. Waited for messages to scroll by

11. typed-----------> ./quickcaqm.sh

12 .  Waited for messages to scroll by

13. typed-----------> xawtv -noxv

And it worked!

The picture quality is the best I have seen in a while.
