There's plenty of others in SA as well. I think the LPI.org site has a link
to the people that do the testing (can't remember whether it's Sylvan
Prometric or not), but they have a search engine thing that will show you all
of the places in Adelaide you can sit the exams.

The cost is roughly $150 per exam. To do LPI Level One, you need to do exams
101 and 102. You're given 90 minutes to do the exam (usually on a Windows
machine, but it's apparently possible to do it on paper). In case you haven't
yet seen it, there's sample questions and a list of stuff to know on the
lpi.org site.
