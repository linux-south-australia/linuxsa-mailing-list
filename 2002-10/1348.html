RE: Nvidia & MDK 9

sorry - in this case - I have to say RTFM

I run GeForce 4 on my Dell laptop - works a treat - just followed the
instructions.  I always install from .src.rpm files however - you will need
the kernel sources installed.  If you install for the wrong kernel version
that can mess things up.

Read the FAQ - you need to change two lines in your XConfig and that's it.

I don't know why people have problems with the Nvidia drivers - I have been
using them for years without problems - but then again I do read the
documentation.

You don't need the binary RPMs - you can always keep the source RPMs and
recompile them each time you upgrade your kernel - too easy!!!

Have you read the FAQ from the Nvidia site?
