> Hi Ian,
> 
> You could go to all this trouble.  Or alternatively, you'll
> find that SSH supports redirection of X clients on remote machines.
> 
>     local> ssh remote.example.edu.au
>     remote> xlogo &
> 
> The X Logo will appear on local's screen, under the control
> of local's window manager.
> 
> You can what you have running where with
> 
>     local> xlsclients
>     ...
>     remote.example.edu.au  xlogo
>     ...
> 
> Remember to close all X sessions before closing the
> ssh session.  There are no bad consequences of not doing
> this -- local's xterm screen just remains disconertingly
> blank until the last client on 'remote' closes.
>
