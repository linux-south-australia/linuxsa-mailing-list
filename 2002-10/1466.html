Re: Overcharging for local calls (Was: Regular phone number required to contact Helstra)

One other thing which often can 'help' to achieve results is, if they are a
member of the AHA... (the Australian Hotels Association) is to call up the
AHA in your state and have a chat with someone there about it.

The good thing with that is that if its a common problem the AHA will most
likely notify all hotels... ie. by means of a letter or a note in their
regular publications... (the local AHA publication is HotelSA) and that way
it will reach a lot wider audience... the AHA is a reasonably powerful
organisation within the hotel industry in Australia too.
