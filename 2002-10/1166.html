Re: [OT] List Format: Reply to...

On Mon, 2002-10-21 at 11:51, Matthew Western wrote:
> Is it just me or do other people find this list a little annoying?  if you
> reply, you get the person you sent it to and not the list, if you reply to
> all you cc every man and his dog.
> 
> Wouldn't it be simpler to have reply go straight back to the list?   because
> everyone is on the list anyway so there really is no need to cc anybody.
> and you can still send mail off list cos it's in the reply history.

Direct mails go through quicker than the list though, so it enables the
people involved in the conversation to more quickly have a discussion
without waiting on the list to catch up. If you don't want this, you can
just crop addresses before you hit send. I do this if the cc list is
getting ridiculously long and irrelevant!
