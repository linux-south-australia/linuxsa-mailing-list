Matthew,
	Partimage is an open source ghost like application. I have tried out 
archiving partitions but haven't tried restoring yet, but I have heard 
good things about it. It consists of 2 floppy images, that have a linux 
kernel and a selection of ethernet drivers etc. on it, so that you can 
store on a network. You might need to re-package it or have a driver on 
a separate disk if it doesn't support your card though.

I think another (open source) approach is Mindi/Mondo? but  I am not 
sure of the details of how it works.
