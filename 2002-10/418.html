Internode ADSL mirror

Sorry - where does 'Agile' fit in?  I thought Internode ran the mirror..

We are more referring to what's available - we know that of course it will
only be available to Internode customers - but I think people would like to
see more than the standard mirror that's updated only with a select one or
two standard distributions (normally Red Hat only) when they come out - so
the mirror is pretty much useless unless one requires a full distribution
they don't already have.

Updates, RPM / software collections, etc are all great too and would mean
the mirror is used by the customer more than every few months to get the
latest standard ISO (if they don't want Mandrake, etc instead of the default
Red Hat ISO images).

I just faxed off my ADSL form - I am hoping the 'minimum' 20 days does not
mean that it will sit unacknowledged with Telstra for a minimum of 20 days?

These talks of expanding mirrors for updates, etc is getting me a little
impatient to get connected..
