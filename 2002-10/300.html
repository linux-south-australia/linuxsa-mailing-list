RE: Text of virus infected email - Bugbear

I did for a little while, but that was with postfix on our server in
melbourne. Postfix supports body content filters and theres a reg-ex
around on the net that blocks all executable content extensions (it
contains a list of ORs as long as your arm!)

We dont do it here though, we just use the virus scanner. So far, so
good. It auto updated before bugbear, and we've had no infections at
all. (This was with sendmail/amavis/mcaffee/updating bash script on the
adelaide server).
