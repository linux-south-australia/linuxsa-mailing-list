VIA C3 and EPIA-M Mini-ITX

Has anyone tried Linux on a VIA C3 processor?

The new VIA EPIA-M Mini-ITX motherboard with a 933MHz C3-E CPU
is a contender for a new home PC.  It looks like a trade off
of less CPU grunt for silence and desk space, which sounds
fine to me.

http://www.viavpsd.com/product/epia_m_spec.jsp

The only downside seems to be the lack of Intel's SSIMD
instructions on the C3, and thus the need for i586 rather
than i686 compilation options.

I'm not too fussed about device support, it's basically
for running StarOffice.
