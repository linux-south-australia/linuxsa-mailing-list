Re: Spam assassin - any good?

IMHO, I think Spamassassin is so good, that it counts as a reason 
thatUnix should be used on the desktop.

When I set it up a few months ago, everyone here was just fantastic in 
helping me figure out how the thing works. I left a post to try to 
summarize earlier this year...you could look at that or write me and I 
will be glad to help as best I can. However, there are some real guru's 
here that are much better at mail issues than I am.

Anyway, spamassassin has saved me a ton of time, I get about 1,000 spam 
a week.
