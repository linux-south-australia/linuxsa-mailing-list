[NEWS] Linux for securing cyberspace?

Is Linux the Key to Securing Cyberspace?
~
Open source security options considered, but the feds say they won't 
play favorites.

Michelle Madigan, Medill News Service
Tuesday, October 29, 2002

WASHINGTON -- The debate over securing cyberspace collided with the 
rivalry between open source and proprietary technologies Tuesday--but 
the government still says it's not getting involved. 

A security summit here Tuesday explored how open source technologies 
can secure networks and computer systems, particularly in government 
agencies and offices. It was sponsored by Red Hat, the largest 
distributor of open source software, and Dell.
