I'm one of those Tel$tra customers that signed up as _soon_ as I could
get ADSL. Yes I was annoyed at the subsequent cap, and I joined the
Whirlpool lobby to voice my opinion. Not that I have ever come close to
the cap (well, once I DL'd all four FreeBsd iso's) but the principal
of signing a binding 18 month contract and then having the conditions
changed got my back up. It passed scrutiny by the TIO and the ACCC by
the fact that Tel$tra would release without penalty those who wanted
out. I thought those two bodies rolled over. I stayed because I got ADSL
for _speed_, cable had not rolled out (despite the hype from the Foxtel
goons) and Internode was not up and running yet.

adds to your traffic _and_ you don't bother to access your usage meter
