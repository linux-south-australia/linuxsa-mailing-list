Re: Regular phone number required to contact Helstra

The ACA describes the function and features of the 01983x number range
as:
 
"Prefix of data network access services: Call charge limited to low
charge.  The call is charged at an untimed local call fee in accordance
with the tariff plan provided by your service provider" 
The 'service provider' is the company that sells the hotel their local
phone calls which may not have been Telstra.

http://www.aca.gov.au/
