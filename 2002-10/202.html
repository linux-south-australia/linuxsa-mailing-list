RE: Contributing back to the open source community

On Thu, 3 Oct 2002, Brandon Johnson wrote:

> Just to add my $0.02 worth on this thread, another way to contribute is
> just to get involved in Opensource events. I have taken this approach by
> getting involved with this years Installfest. So all you gurus at
> installations register your support on the day at
> http://installfest.spyderweb.com.au/ as a helper and help out on the day.

This has to be the best way to contribute. On many projects we are simply 
lacking in people who can contribute both code and testing time.

For example, with Samba, we need people who can set up multiple Windows NT 
and Win2K servers and Samba and make sure it all works with things like 
LDAP etc. We also need more coders, but there is a steep learning curve.

And lastly, the problem with contributing is getting it to the people who 
could use it.
