Re: Which openssl did I use?

JoXo Campos wrote:

> I compiled UW imapd , and don't know which openssl was used. How can I
> find out?

ldd, if it's using openssl as a shared (rather than static) library.  To whit:

[dt@nipnyep dt]$ ldd /bin/ls
	libc.so.6 => /lib/libc.so.6 (0x40019000)
	/lib/ld-linux.so.2 => /lib/ld-linux.so.2 (0x40000000)
