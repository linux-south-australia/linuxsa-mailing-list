Hi Damien,

Sounds like a faulty HDD, I've had it happen a couple of times
with some 10GB Fujitsu HDDs.

With the Fujitsu drives it use to be random at first (always when
I was doing something important ), but then it became more regular,
until they failed completely.

I asked my supplier and they said that Fujitsu produced a bad
batch with faulty motors.
