Re: VMWare and mandrake 9

You might need to disable the APIC during boot.  I haven't had any
experience with this myself, but I've heard it mentioned before.

I don't know what Mandrake uses to boot, but with LILO you can use:

linux noapic

at the LILO: prompt.

With GRUB, it's pretty easy to edit boot time parameters to add this
also.
