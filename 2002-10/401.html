Re: Cloning HDD

On Sun, 2002-10-06 at 21:20, Daryl Pilkington wrote:
> Hi,
> I've got to clone 11 RH7.2 installs.
> Identical 10GB IBM IDE drives, Intel 815 chipset.
> 
> Is there any problem with:
> dd if=/dev/hda of=/dev/hdb bs=512
> 
> Is there any advantage increasing bs?
> It it limited by the HDD sector size or the like?

It can make small speed differences. Experiment to work out what works
best with your hardware. From my experience it doesnt make much
difference though. Perhaps use a different speed on each machine and
time it for next time? After the first few you might be able to
determine the most efficient setting for the last systems.
