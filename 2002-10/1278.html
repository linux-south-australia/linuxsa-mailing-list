RE: Linux powered water irrigation systems?

I will keep plugging away at it - I'm going down to Bunnings by next pay day
to get a small group of valves and relays and solenoids.  I will also get
another Ethernet or serial port lightning surge protector - I already have
one for my up and coming radio mast.

Could solenoids be powered by say 2 Metal Hydride AA cells charged either by
a clustered or individual solar cell?  A little UHF remote control kit could
then be a 'node' for a group of valves.

Ultimately we will need a radio or other link as this will be scaled up to a
small farm over several kilometres.

Ideally this would be deployed in a developing nation where a simple
automated solution could cut water use and increase plant yield, inject
fertiliser, control PH, etc.

We are going to see how many parts we can use from hard refuse night
scavenging missions and other spare parts to cobble this together to prove
it can be done simply and cheaply.
