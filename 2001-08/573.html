When your NIC enters promiscous mode, it captures (a copy of) all traffic
that goes by it, regardless of destination.  Normally, it only pays
attention to packets directed towards it.  You might want to check into
why its doing that, you could have a sniffer installed.  Or you might be
running Etherreal or something too, which is fine ;)

> what does this mean, and also if I have a 100 mps network card installed and

> is linux sending all information @ 100mps or is there a defaut mode which
> you need to change.
>
>
> Yellow =  10 mps
> Green = 100mps
>

Your card probably switches based on the connection it detects, as does
the hub.  Most new hardware autosenses.  If not, rtfm.
