Re: 20gb HDD 10gigs to linux 10gigs to fat32 how?

Hi Michael,
	The simplest way would be to create a 10GB partition for
Linux at install, reboot to Win2K once you've finished the Linux
install, and then partition and format the other 10GB to FAT32
from within Win2K.

Don't forget to setup LILO for booting to Windows AND Linux.

Don.
