> What is the quickest way to totally disable telnet on a RH 7.0/7.1 
> system?

Ummm, do you mean incoming, or outgoing, or both?

RH7.1 has a Firewall Setup that would disable this.

However, you can edit /etc/xinetd.d/telnet and change 'disabled' to yes.

You can 'rpm -e telnet; rpm -e telnet-server'.

Lastly, of course, you could do:

   rm -rf /

which makes the machine very secure, and the expense of ease-of-use issues.
