The GRUB FAQ seems confused ...

Hi,

It seems that the GRUB FAQ 
(http://www.gnu.org/software/grub/grub-faq.en.html) is a little confused 
when it comes to questions about cylinder 1024 and 8.4GB issues.

It was my understanding that supporting LBA was not enough for you to 
access anything beyond cylinder 1024 at boot time (via the BIOS).

I understood that you need EDD support, and that most motherboards since 
abouy 1998 have that.
