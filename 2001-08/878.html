Re: Gigabit Routing?

On 23 Aug 2001 19:31:51 +0930, Patrick Browne wrote:
> Most of the schools run 3Com switches for their switching needs and that's
> fine, but what is missing? Routing! We are killing each other with each
> others broadcast traffic.

Switches usually have the option of setting broadcast ranges on each
port. If each campus has a defined IP ranges, then that should be your
solution right there. Just set the IP ranges on the ports and broadcasts
won't be sent anywhere else anymore.

I don't think you would need a router for the scenario you describe. If
you do, I'd suggest also checking out Enterasys's SSR-8000 (ex Yago, ex
Digital, ex Compaq). They're rock solid and work flawlessly.
