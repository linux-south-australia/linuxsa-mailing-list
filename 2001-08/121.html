Re: HELP! with umounting

From: <wlsimes@tell.net.au>
> When I try and unmount the home directory I get
> umount: /home: device is busy
>
> I get the same when I try and force it.
>
> What should I try next ?

lsof will be useful to you:

apropos lsof
lsof                 (8)  - list open files

running something like "lsof | grep /home" will probably give you the
information you need.

if it's not already installed on your system, Red Hat provides it in the
lsof package.


hope this helps.
