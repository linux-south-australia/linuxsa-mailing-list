Re: procmailrc re-send recipes (which fallback to a mailboxfileif userunknown)

I wouldnt use procmail so much, its going to be hard to read, slower,
log heaps of crap, and become very unreadable real fast. For a small
amount of users you could just use the aliases file. eg (presuming
sendmail):

NSW.ant@whatever.com: ant@isp1.com
VIC.fred@whatever.com: office@isp2.com

etc,etc. Since "they" are not adhearing to conventions that would make
anything generic work, this is probably the simplest. If they plan to
expand, I would tell them they need some better conventions. Maybe even
in the domain part of the addresses :) eg ant@nsw.whatever.com
