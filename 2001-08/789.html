> Couple of points in general for MDK 8.0 and Fonts.
> 
> 1. If you have Windows on the machine, use drakfont to utilise the 
> fontsin MDK.
> 
> 2. Check your font directories, they should all have a 'fonts.dir' 
> file in
> them - many of the fonts used by X vis xfs are in
> /usr/X11R6/lib/X11/fonts/ each directory under there is named 
> after the
> font style/type, and should have a fonts.dir in it. If not, there 
> is a
> utility called mkfontdir that builds the fonts/dir for you.
> 
> I had awful font problems in MDK until I discovered these messed 
> up font
> directories.
