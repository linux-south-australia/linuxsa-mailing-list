RE: Redhat 7.1 and Win2k

I'm tri-booting my system.

I have win2000 installed on hda1
hda5 and hda6 are file storage areas to share files between all 3 OS's.
I've got WinME installed on hdb1
and RedHat linux 7.1 on hdc1 (linux swap on hdc2).

I didn't have to put anything special in lilo.conf except for two
map-drives, to get winme working. The redhat installer didn't figure out
that there's an OS installed on hda1, so I had to add an entry into
lilo.conf, and it made 'dos' pointing to hda5 and I had to hance the drive,
partition and add the map-drives.

Anyway, my point is I didn't have to do anything special to get Win2000 to
work with RedHat 7.1 except change lilo.conf and run lilo. Also, lilo spat a
heap of warnings because i've trying to boot onto hdc and some BIOS's can't
do this, but my computer works fine :)

Good luck,
