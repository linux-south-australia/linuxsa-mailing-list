Andy!

> the monitor's menu and looking at the 'status' section. When resizing
> windows, the size of the window is displayed in the centre of the screen. Am
> I correct that the size should be displaying the number of pixels? When I
> resize a window such that it's taking up the whole screen (including going
> over that panel at the bottom of the screen) it says it's about 716x444.

Take a look at /etc/X11/Xf86Config-4 and look for your screen
definition(s). There may be something obvious in there that could answer
your question...don't forget that man X will help you figure things out.

I'm running an nVidia on a generic 17" monitor at 1024x768 without any
problems.

You might also want to ferret around the options in your Window Manager
as well. I've noticed things like size and position options for Sawfish.
