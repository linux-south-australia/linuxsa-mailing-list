Re: RADIUS Accounting with SQUID

Andrew Reid wrote:
> 
> Following some thinking coupled with a chat with a person or too, I've
> become slightly interested in the posibility of a SQUID plugin that sent
> a RADIUS accounting packet whenever a request is returned.
> 
> I've looked around and have managed to find RADIUS Authentication, but
> not much in the way of RADIUS Accounting.
> 
> Has anyone heard of such a thing? Any links or information would be
> greatly appreciated.

You might want to have a look at the Cisco Netflow spec. Once you start
using radius to account for discrete usage at a high rate, Netflow works
similarly and is more efficient. There are also more tools available for
receiving data at high rates, unlike radius which isn't really geared
for dealing with many packets each second.
