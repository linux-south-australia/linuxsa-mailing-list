Port forwarding & NAT.
Eg:
Packet hits firewall (203.x.x.x) destined for port 80 on ip "A", and gets
redirected to an internal box (192.168.x.x)
Packet hits firewall destined for port 80 on ip "B", and gets redirected to
an internal box (192.168.x.y).

Quite a common deal for people with lots of servers, I believe.
