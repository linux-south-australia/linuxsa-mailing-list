I use Keytronic (formerly Honeywell) keyboards, since they're good
quality, and relatively inexpensive (about 50 $ for a SpaceMate model),
but if you want a _real_ keyboard (no Windows keys, no numpad), have a
look at the Happy Hacking Keyboard
http://www.pfuca.com/products/hhkb/hhkbindex.html.  You can get one from
everythinglinux.com.au or ridge.com.au, but be warned that they're not
cheap.
