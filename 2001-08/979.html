Since the diagrams are otherwise quite clear, my bet is that your problem is
identifying pin 1 on the connectors. Try making up a cable with the
connections the opposite way to what you've been doing.

Note that the connectors that they show the diagrams of are the "jacks",
i.e. the sockets, and that if you interpret those diagrams as being for the
"plugs", i.e. with the cable entry away from you and the latching lever at
the bottom, then the numbering is reversed to what it should be.

My "memory technique" for remembering it is that if you hold a cable with an
attached connector in your hand with the cable pointing down and the
contacts facing you, then pin 1 is on the left. Simple, eh?
