Re: Central Scotland Police choose StarOffice

Apparently the Northern Territory education dept. have chosen Star Office 
as the "mandated" office suite.
They have said that MS Office can be used but that they won't pay for it.

Trinity College use it on our SUNRAY appliances and it is installed, along 
with MS Office, on our Windows machines.
It really is quite good.  I understand Star Office 6 won't require that all 
applications be open at once (chewing large amounts of memory up), you can 
open the application you want.
