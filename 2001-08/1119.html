Re: Quiet PCs...

>Most of the noise that I can determine is the fan(s). Hence, is Richard
>asking about the motherboard or the power supply fan (which is by far
>the greatest noise maker)?

Most CPU/GPU noise can be muffled by the addition of dampening material on
the inside of the case, usually found on the inside of good quality
speakers.

Power supply fan noise can be minimised with the construction of a 'snorkel'
for it to pull air through, possibly lined with the same dampening material
mentioned above.
