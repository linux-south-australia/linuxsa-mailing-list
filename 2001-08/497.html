Bynari Insight groupware released

Bynari have recently released their Insight groupware software
for those interested in Exchange replacements running on Linux.

http://eltoday.com/article.php3?ltsn=2001-08-10-001-14-PS

www.bynari.net

Quote from press release:
"Bynari's Groupware Solution includes their Insight Server which 
provides global directories and contact lists, IMAP and POP3 
email accounts, calendaring, meeting management, free-busy 
facilities and high performance SMTP. The Insight client uses 
all the server's facilities with shared calendars, tasks and events."
