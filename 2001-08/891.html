Re: Telnet Disable Help

David.Major wrote:

> What is the quickest way to totally disable telnet on a
> RH 7.0/7.1 system?

To disable the telnet server on a Red Hat 7.x system, you
would edit /etc/xinetd.d/telnet in your favorite text editor,
and change "disable = no" to "disable = yes".

The more permanent method would be to remove the telnet server
files form your system completely by doing something like
"rpm -e telnet-server".

By the way: many members of this list dislike HTML (sometimes
called "rich text") mail.  I would suggest you disable this
option, and ensure that you are sending only plain text.

Part of the reason for this, is sending the same mail as a
text-only mail would have made the mail 108 characters long,
however your mail client has turned this into a mail 880
characters long - more than eight times larger!

Another reason is that many persons do not have a mail client
which will read HTML messages, so your message starts looking
like this:
