> system("/usr/sbin/ipchains -A forward -j MASQ -s
> $ipaddress/255.255.255.255 -d ! 172.21.0.0/255.255.0.0");

Error checking works for me.  At least you will know if
the system command succeeded or not.

$result = system("/usr/sbin/ipchains -A forward -j MASQ -s
$ipaddress/255.255.255.255 -d ! 172.21.0.0/255.255.0.0");
die "system: $result" if $result;
