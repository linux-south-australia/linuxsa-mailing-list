Re: PPP and Ozemail

This sounds very familiar, I believe there is an option in KPPP to "use peer
DNS" or something like that, which asks your ISP to send the IP's of their
Domain Name Servers. These IP's should be in a file called /etc/resolv.conf.
You should see something like this in /var/log/messages:-

Aug 13 22:11:08 Gromit pppd[216]: local  IP address 144.134.133.101
Aug 13 22:11:08 Gromit pppd[216]: remote IP address 139.134.59.228
Aug 13 22:11:08 Gromit pppd[216]: primary   DNS address 139.134.5.51
Aug 13 22:11:08 Gromit pppd[216]: secondary DNS address 139.134.2.190

and something like this in /etc/resolv.conf

nameserver 139.134.5.51
nameserver 139.134.2.190

Alternatively ask your ISP for their DNS server IP's (may be on their
website) and put them in /etc/resolv.conf as above.
