> whatis ls

> appropos bison
bison                (1) - GNU Project parser generator (yacc replacement)

And that's with me not running "makewhatis" manually, but relying on 
/etc/cron.[daily|weekly]/makewhatis.cron

Note that these two scripts use "makewhatis -u -w" not straight "makewhatis".  
The "-u" option says "update database" and "-w" says "use the manpath optioned 
from man --path".  I think you may want both of these :-)
