Re: xmms plays too fast

I saw something like this on my work box, during the 2.4.0-test series for a
couple of revisions.

Basically, the codec seems to be locked to 48khz. Playing 44khz stuff on it
is going to sound a bit too fast. Newer sound drivers identify this, and
everything works fine.

Try bypassing esd, just to see what happens.
Next, try forcing esd to use 48khz output.

Regardless, try upgrading your kernel and sound driver ;)

Toby
