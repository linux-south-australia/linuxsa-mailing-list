I think it's great that you make an active choice about which linux 
distribution you'd like to run, but I don't really like your use of "RedHat 
linux monoculture".  Why?  Red Hat have improved the useability of linux for

community off (unlike Caldera et al with their closed-source extensions) and so 
IMHO don't deserve bad press.  They're the good guys!  And sure, feel free to 
use "XYZ Linux" if you like, but I'll stick with RedHat which is well-behaved 
member of the community which produces a darn fine distro.
