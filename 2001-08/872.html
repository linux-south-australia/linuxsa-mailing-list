Re: Macintosh and Linux

I can't help you with (1), but there may be a solution to (2) and (3)
depending on what your requirements are.

MacOS 9 and below can't access SMB shares out of the box.  There are
expensive proprietary add ons that can, though.

However, if what you really want to do is to access the files on your
linux box from your MacOS box, then you can use netatalk.  This will let
your linux box talk AppleTalk over IP.

I'm pretty sure it's in the packages for debian, and it's available on
the powertools CD for redhat.  Home page is http://netatalk.sf.net/
