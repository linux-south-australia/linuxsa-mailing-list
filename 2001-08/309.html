Re: Stripmime.pl

The default config for most distros seems to setup PINE in the same way..
ie. it will display HTML emails fine.
(I believe it passes it via lynx, actually, but this is configurable)

It's also disable-able at the hit of a key, incase you want to check those
URLs or something before you hit them ;)

Because its lynx or other text-mode browsers, there is no javascript, no
automatically loaded doubleclick-style webbug gifs, or other nasties, no
stupid backgrounds, blah blah blah.

However, to strip HTML, procmail will do the trick.
You can also do it from inside of PINE, by configuring a viewer command for
the mime-type text/html, among othe ways.
