Re: 256k ISDN with 2 NetJet Cards

Jim O'Halloran wrote:

> We're currently running 128k ISDN through a Linux box with a single Traverse
> Technologies NetJet card.  At some stage we may need to increase capacity to
> 256k or beyond.  The traverse site suggests that you can use two cards in
> the same machine to achieve 240k (not quite 256k).  Has anyone tried this?
> Any comments or experiences, etc would be welcome.

I've done it with 2x digi datafire cards in NT on a few occasions, and
there's no technical reason it won't work with the traverse cards. You
can test it out by grabbing a second traverse card and multilinking 1
channel on each card from your existing Onramp.

The thing to watch out for is the DMA access used by the netjet cards,
if you have some other device using DMA (like an ATA66 controller) you
might run into a hardware conflict that gives weird results.
