What you say!

Brian, although my previous answer may have looked like rubbish (All 
your data are belong to us), you have no option but to conclude that 
they have taken over your machine. They could have copied anything they 
wanted to, as well as trojaned any binaries on the system, and it will 
be very hard to detect.

If they used a root kit that installs loadable modules, you may well 
never be able to detect the problems without a lot more skill than you 
seem to have.

So, do as the second suggestion says. Backup, reinstall, set up a good 
firewall (about 1cm of air is very good :-), and be happy.

For great justice! All your base.
