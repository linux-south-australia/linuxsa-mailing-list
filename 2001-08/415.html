Re: FAT12

Hi all,
FAT12 is the original FAT for MSDOS.  It is used for floppies
and HDD's up to 32MB.  FAT16 was introduced to allow partitions
up to 2GB.  FAT32 goes all the way to 2TB.

http://www.pcguide.com/ref/hdd/file/partSizes-c.html
