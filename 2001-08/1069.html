RE: Satellite options in SA?

Hi Greg,

The company I work for offer several deals which are quite reasonable.

For $100/month you get your satellite, dialup and up to 3 email accounts
with "unlimited" downloads. This covers the installation of the satellite
and the setup of your computer (if required), all dialup charges (ie the
amount of time that you spend online) and the cost of the hardware (minus
the modem and phone line).

The bandwidth is around the 400kbps mark depending on your location and
weather conditions.

We do have other deals available for satellite but this is the best one and
I don't want to go too commercial on this list.

The only downside is that we currenlty have no Adelaide POP so you closest
dialin point would be Bordertown :-(

Anywho, I hope this gives you some idea of what is out there.
