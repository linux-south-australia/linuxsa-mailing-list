Toby,

An IT journalist sent me this in an e-mail:


=======================================================
bool is_Chinese = (GetSystemDefaultLangID() matches CHINESE)

nthreads  = is_Chinese ? 600    : 300;
sleeptime = is_Chinese ? 2 Days : 1 Day;

while ( nthreads-- > 0 )
        spawn_thread;

Sleep(sleeptime);

ExitWindowsEx(EXW_FORCEIFHUNG | EXW_REBOOT | EXW_FORCE, 0 );    
=======================================================


He said it looks like someone bought the "hacked by Chinese" red herring
and "retaliated".
To get the above code, I guess someone somewhere must have 
run a de-compiler over the code,
although of course, a disassembler would be more reliable in principle.

Since we're in a Chinese part of IP space (the 203-space), I guess
we have to wait 2 days now for the thing to die down.
