> Hi all,
>
> I have just upgraded to RH7.1. I skipped the installation of LILO
> forgetting that this will ommit installing /etc/lilo.conf. Can
> somebody please send me their lilo.conf so I can reconstruct mine.
>

Well, here is mine, although you will have to update parts.

############
boot=/dev/hda5
map=/boot/map
install=/boot/boot.b
prompt
timeout=5
#message=/boot/message
lba32

image=/boot/vmlinuz-2.4.9
        label=linux-2.4.9
        read-only
        root=/dev/hda5
        append="idebus=66 acpi=no-idle"

image=/boot/vmlinuz-2.4.2-2
	label=old
	read-only
	root=/dev/hda5

############
