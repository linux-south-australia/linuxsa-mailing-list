Re: hda trouble

I think the problem is with your hard drive has so many bad sectors.  Linux or
UNix has reserved some spaces for swapping bad sectors and I think It used them
all and there is no more space to swap  or redirect the bad blocks.
It's a time to change to new hard drive. ( you can reformat bad hard drive and
use it for windows . As  times go by, it will be gone! )
