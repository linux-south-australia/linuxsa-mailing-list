RE: Which Email Client?

G'day Nick,

How about not limiting ourselves?  If one of you had said that
Netscape may not be the best Email agent to use when you have so many
other alternatives available, I'd agree, but there's more to Linux and 
Unix than Pine and Elm.  While I'm a big fan of both programs and have 
them available for our users, by choice I use Xemacs/Vm. I have it set 
up so that if I'm in the console, I can also use Pine. Someone else 
might use another Xwindow based Email client. 

What I like best about Linux is that it gives me freedom of choice for 
many of the different things I need to do on a daily basis. I hate to
see that freedom limited to two Email clients. 

This isn't intended as a flame, just a casual comment.
