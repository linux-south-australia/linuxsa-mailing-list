Using & Managing PPP

The Bookshelf in town (on Friday 25 August) had two copies of Using &
Managing PPP by Andrew Sun (pub: O'Reilly). They are $29.95 each; RRP is
$75 and http://www.everythinglinux.com.au sells it for $60+p&h. It
covers pppd 2.3.7 and would certainly help anyone thinking of setting up
a dialup connection, such as my next experiment with Linux will be.
