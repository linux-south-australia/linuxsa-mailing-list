Re: boot up problems - debian ver 2

I don't use it often...I have LILO installed on the MBR as well, but here's my
loadlin line.  You do need the kernel (vmlinuz) on the DOS partition as well I
believe.

c:\loadlin\loadlin  c:\loadlin\vmlinuz  root=/dev/hda2  ro  vga=3  hdd=cdrom

regards ... Don.
