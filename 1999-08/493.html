Hi, havent heard of the modem but it sounds nasty!

Just stick an external on /dev/ttyS1 (or S3) OR an internal (u can set
them up for /tty/S2 or S3 with irq 5 or 7 too usually) ... you may need
to modify /etc/rc.d/rc.serial [or its equiv depending on your distro]
(in the manual configuration section) and then run ./rc.serial or
./serial start etc etc, if you use /dev/ttyS2 or S3... before attmepting
to use kppp.

It wont cause any probs (unless you have other devices attempting to
access same io or irq's) I do stuff like that all the time... I have a
33k internal that ocvcassionally locks up so to avoind being offline for
too long Ihave a 14k4 internal on /dev/ttyS3 irq 7
