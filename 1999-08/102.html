Re: kernel boot/system startup help

that sounds like a DNS problem, as all 3 daemons need/use some sort of dns
lookup for their names.... so maybe your localhost is not setup
properly...

Have a look at these, as sendmail's hang is 99% of the time caused by a
DNS problem with localhost, or your hostname failing to convert to an ip
address.. I don't have much knowledge in this area, but when something
like that happens, I would 1st start looking at a DNS problem, although I
odn't know how making a kernel would cause that problem.....
