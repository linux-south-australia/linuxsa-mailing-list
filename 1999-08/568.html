RE: LILO stops at LI

   >Problem: LILO stops at LI
   >Question: Why should this happen when one of
   >my mutli-boot machine's drives is replaced by an
   >unformatted disk?

Am I missing something here? Forgive me if somebody has already suggested
this solution to you.
Since you have effectively removed LINUX from your system (and the files
LILO was using) you just need to complete the job in 15 seconds:

1. Grab DOS boot disk, put fdisk.exe on it from c:\windows\command
2. Boot with it and type: fdisk /MBR

This tells DOS fdisk to clean out the LILO infested MBR with a new fresh
one, returning you machine to a bootable state.

Done.
