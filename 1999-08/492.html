Re: kppp

On Mon, 23 Aug 1999, Gary Ewing wrote:
> I have recently installed RedHat 6.0 and tried to get kppp to work. I can
> get it to dial and hook up to my isp (work) using a script, but it times
> out when trying to raise the ppp interface.

It's something to do with stty modes for the serial port.  I discovered that
kppp works fine after I have used the port using cu.  I have a work around,
which is particularly hacky, and I promise to do something neater when the
effort of the work around exceeds the effort of doing something neater.   The
work around is this:

1.  Find out what stty modes work:
    a. cu somewhere
    b. quit cu
    c. stty -g < /dev/cua1 (or whatever port you use)
    d. save the output into a file.

2.  Before you use kppp, perhaps at boot time, restore the
    output from stty:

      stty `cat stty.output` < /dev/cua1

I find this works for me.
