Matt,

You are on the right track.  init is respawning because getty keeps
hanging up.  In situations like this it is useful to fix one thing
at a time: run getty from the command line and experiment with the
options until it works, then paste the result into inittab.

Checking the man page shows that getty recognises the option -L
which is used for local lines as it ignores the carrier detect.  
Its my guess that this will fix the problem. If not, use stty -a to 
show the settings that are in place when you print characters to
the screen, then do your best to make getty use those.  In 
particular the hand shaking protocols.
