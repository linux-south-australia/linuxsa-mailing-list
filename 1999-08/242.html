Re: Finding Duplicate Records

Hi Nick,

In this case sort and uniq are your friends. If the lines you seek to
remove are _exact_ copies, sort filename | uniq will give you a list of
the lines which are not duplicated.
You could then redirect to a file, and presto! a unique set of lines.
