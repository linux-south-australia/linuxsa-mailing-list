> > combination can be setup to automatically mount a cd/disk if it is not
> > already mounted and will automatically unmount it after a period of not

> Does this use the silly idea of MS by continuously polling the drive?

No it doesn't.
The cd is only mounted upon first access. Since it has some parts in
the kernel the timeout is taken from the last inode (like a file) request.
Hence it's really just a count down timer. (did you really think Linux
would do something sloppy :)
