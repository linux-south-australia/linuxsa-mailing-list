Re: What linux should I get?

MIG:

> 
> I wanted to get a copy of linux but since I am new to linux not sure what
> should I get. Getting RH6.0 seems kinda scary cause many problems posted
> here are about "I had RH5.2 and it worked and when I installed RH6.0 I got
> .... error"

This is quite a normal glitch. RH 6.0 is a newere dotzero operating system which
hasn't quite gotten all the bugs ironed out. Nonetheless, I believe that RH 6.0
is a good operating system and that with a bit of tweaking it can be better.

If you want to spend approximately $100, go get Mandrake. It is basically RH 6.0
with KDE, a later kernel and less bugs.

You can also purchase RH from www.netcraft.com.au for much less than $100. I
believe it floats around $15 to $20.

RedHat 5.2 or 6.0 will do what you wish. You will need to get another word
processor, though. I'd suggest you get StarOffice from www.stardivision.com but
work out how to rebuild the kernel before you run it or it will appear to be
fantastically slow.
