RedHat Linux & Setting up PPP

I have decided to write an easy-to-understand HOWTO for PPP setup with
RedHat Linux. I acknowledge that a number of HOWTOs exist, however they
ignore tools such as linuxconf and netcfg completely. The existing
HOWTOs would show a Unix guru how to setup PPP; however they could
easily confuse a newcomer to linux (such as they did with me).

I expect to be able to complete this within about 36 hours...if anyone
would care for me to copy the completed document to them (it's shaping
up to be about 300kB at least - I have screen shots of linuxconf and
netcfg embedded) I would be happy to. I intend to email a copy of the
completed version to RedHat ITSELF as well as to the Linux Documentation
Project.
