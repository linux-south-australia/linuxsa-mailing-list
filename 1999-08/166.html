The "hack" solution to running pgaccess for those who don't
want to recompile it is to point LD_PRELOAD to the crypt
library, with something like -

    alias pgaccess='LD_PRELOAD=/usr/lib/libcrypt.so pgaccess'

Voila!  Instant viola ...
