Can you see the difference in these two lines??

In /etc/sysconfig/network you will find a couple of lines that read:

GATEWAY=0.0.0.0
GATEWAYDEV=eth0

change these to:

GATEWAY=
GATEWAYDEV=

And next time you reboot the computer, there shouldn't be a problem.
