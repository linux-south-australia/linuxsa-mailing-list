>>>>> "BA" == Brian Astill <brian.astill@flinders.edu.au> writes:

  BA> I need to use some stats programs eg SPSS, Wesvar, HLM, Streams.
  BA> Are there Linux alternatives or ports available?

Check out SAL.  From their web site:

   SAL (Scientific Applications on Linux) is a collection of information
   and links to software that will be of interest to scientists and
   engineers. The broad coverage of Linux applications will also benefit
   the whole Linux/Unix community. There are currently 2,397 entries in
   SAL.

http://sal.rising.com.au/index.shtml

Choose Category Mathematics and sub-category Statistics, and you get a
few dozen hits.  Debian GNU/Linux offers PSPP (SPSS clone) in its main
distribution, but I have not tried it.
