Re: Setting up RH6

 Does the monitor still turn off when you go into 800x600 or higher?

 If it does, then its prolly a problem with your monitor settings rather
than
 your video card settings (or the driver).
 It will be your monitors refresh rates that you have set up wrong. Again,
 recheck XF86Config, or re-run Xconfigurator to re setup the monitor.
