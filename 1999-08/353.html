hi there fellow Netscape Mailer Users!

the problem was some malformed mail-header information.

I have filters sorting out incoming mail into seperate folders. After
watching Nestacep die 54 times in a row on the same message, I
investigated. The Folders are stored as text files, much the same was as
mail is stored on a Unix machine. I went into this folder using a text
editor, and tidied up the headers for that message. No probs. Netscape
survived and the message was intact.
