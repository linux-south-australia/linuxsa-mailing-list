Don't bother :-) TCP/IP is badly implemented on all the netware versions up 
to 5, it apparently encapsulates tcp/ip inside IPX packets. The 
encapsulation is done by the netware packet drivers, as a cheap hack to get 
dos tcp/ip software to work.

As a much better way to do things, compile your kernel with IPX support, 
and grab the ncputils packages, as they will allow you to remotely mount 
netware volumes on linux. The mars-nwe software will even let you setup is 
a netware 3.11 compatible server, but as someone who worked as a techy for 
Tafe not long ago, EDS get extremely upset at extra servers popping up on 
their network. :-)
