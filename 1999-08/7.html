Hi David,

I realise that this is somewhat tangential to your original request, but
here are a couple of ideas that may help with news reading:

1.  Have you considered using Gnus ( http://www.gnus.org )?  It is an
excellent news reader, and has good facilities for mail reading also,
esp. from mailing lists.

2.  Have you considered using suck ( http://home.att.net/~bobyetman/ )
to retrieve news, and sending the news to yourself with sendmail
(procmail, rcvstore etc)?  This is pretty easy to set up, but has the
problem that posting can become a bit of a pain.
