Re: network

on your linux box, in /usr/doc/HOWTO/ check out
Networking-Overview-HOWTO, NET-3-HOWTO and SMB-HOWTO, they realy are
worth the read. I would suggest reading them fairly closely at the
start, then skim the rest. That way you will know the basics, and will
know where to look for more info.
