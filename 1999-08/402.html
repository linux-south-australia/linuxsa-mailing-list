Not sure what you are trying to achive, but I was able to do following:

I created a file with first lines:
From: Name@host
Subject: Something silly

and piped that into mail

cat joke.txt | mail target@email.address

and guess what? when recipient opens it he/she sees it as from Name@host!

you can even add Date: field
