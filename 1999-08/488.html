Hi all..... have had the "
LI "  problem often when creating multi-boot pc;s - quickest fix I found
is to boot from a boot type floppy eg/  linux mount=/dev/hda1 (or
whatever hdd partition ur linux is on) and  once u r "in" just run lilo
again & the problem is fixed...actually can't recall ever having to do
anything other than that... has happened with all sizes of hdd's.
