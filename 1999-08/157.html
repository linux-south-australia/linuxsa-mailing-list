Re: postgresql-data

> Is it just me, or has pgsql-data been phased out? RH6.0 doesn't have it,
> so I checked their site, and it doesn't appear to be in the distro
> anymore, which is fine, but postmaster, as one would expect, whinges
> about no data in /var/lib/pgsql, and won't start!
> 
> If someone has set it up from the (Netcraft) RH6.0 CD, please tell me
> the crucial step I have overlooked, unless the CD distro is broken (I
> doubt!)

It's not broken; you just need to run 'initdb' instead.  Do
'su - postgres' as root, then:
initdb --pglib=/usr/lib/pgsql --pgdata=/var/lib/pgsql
