Binary to Decimal Converter

Last night whilst reading my new book, "Using & Managing PPP", I
discovered I lacked a simple CLI binary to decimal converter.
Sepcifically I was presented with a decimal IP number which I wanted to
compare to a binary number within the book. As I had plenty of time on
my hands, I set out to write my own using flex, the GPL version of
At&T's lexical scanner.

Should anyone wish to obtain a copy of it (tar gzipped approx 28kB)
please email me directly and I shall send you one. As soon as I redesign
my website (within about 2 weeks) I will also post it there.
