Re: Floppy Drives

On Sat, 14 Aug 1999, Robyn Manning wrote:

> 
> To read a floppy disk I have to type in mount -t vfat /dev/fd0 /mnt/floppy
> the problem is that I have to unmount and mount again every time I change
> floppies. A similar problem with the cdrom, I can't open it without umount
> first.
> 

As pointed out by others, this is normal.

If you want to make things easier have a look at automount/autofs. This
combination can be setup to automatically mount a cd/disk if it is not
already mounted and will automatically unmount it after a period of not
being used.
