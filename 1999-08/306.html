Often the best way to learn is to look at source code; pick your favourite
open source project (suggest you pick something simple, learning C from the
linux kernel, sendmail or other large complicated projects is probably not
recommended :) ) and read through it. Piece it together. C is not unlike Java,
certainly in terms of syntax. I think there are probably some decent textbooks
in the Barr Smith Library at Adelaide Uni, too; saves you having to buy
anything, anyway.
