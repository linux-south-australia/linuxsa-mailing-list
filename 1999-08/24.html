> what files do i need for the lastest xfree86 with support for matrox g100,

That card uses the SVGA server.
So the files you need (assuming rpm format - let me know otherwise)

XFree86-SVGA-[latestverionsnumber].rpm (Where lvn = 3.3 or greater)
XFree86-libs-[lvn].rpm
XFree86-[lvn].rpm
XFree86-75dpi-fonts-[lvn].rpm

Others I recommend are:

XFree86-devel-[lvn].rpm
XFree86-100dpi-fonts-[lvn].rpm

You can search http://filewatcher.org for them as well put XFree86*3.*.rpm
in the search box.
