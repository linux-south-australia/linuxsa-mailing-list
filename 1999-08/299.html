Re: Titan 4000 S3 AGP Graphics adapter and XFree86

On Tue, 17 Aug 1999, Richard Sharpe wrote:

> 
> Hi,
> 
> I had a rather awkward situation today with a Titan 4000 AGP Graphics
> Adapter that claims to be an S3 Trio3D card with a PCI ID of 8904 with 4MB
> on board.
> 
> XFree86 would not recognize it and the best we could do was to configure it
> in 640 by 480 mode with about 8 bpp.
> 
> Has anyone seen these devices?  Can anyone suggest an XF86config file to use?

Searching through the Known cards file: /usr/X11R6/lib/X11/Cards 
I find:

NAME S3 86C365 (Trio3D)
SEE Unsupported VGA compatible

NAME S3 86C391 (Savage3D)
SEE Unsupported VGA compatible

NAME S3 Trio3D
SEE Unsupported VGA compatible

This is with version: XFree86-3.3.3.1-52

Have a look at the Xfree website (www.xfree86.org) I know that a beta of
Xfree 3.9 is available, this may support your card. Check the supported
adapters section.
