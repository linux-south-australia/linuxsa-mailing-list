Warning about breaking Telstra Contract and data charges

I just got off the phone to a rather rude ( to be expected ) Telstra rep to
whom I asked the question.
"If I Downloaded 3 gig in the first 2 weeks of this month and then cancel
the T$ contract what happens ?"

I was told if you cancel mid term your gig allowance for that month is Pro
rata, you will be up for $285 or just a tad more for excess usage charges
for February.

Something to be aware of if you are thinking of dumping the big T$
