Re: [Linux] Linux Drawing Programs - Visio replacements

Dale Long wrote:
> 
> Are there any drawing programs similar to Visio that people recommend for
> use under Linux? 

I've looked at a couple, but because drawings are always required
yesterday, I tend to fall back on the drawing program in Star Office. It
has generic shapes, and a line-mode that links objects together with
arrows etc, which does the job nicely for quick diagrams and flowcharts.

It's save-to-ps option makes it easy to generate pdf's instead of
converting to a chunky bitmap format.
