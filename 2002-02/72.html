Re: [OT] best public IMAP mail account or similar

runbox.com used to provide pretty decent services, though they are now
no longer free. This could be an advantage though if you can pay the
price, as they used to have periods of time where you couldn't login
(they had such a good services that too many people joined up. maybe now
not so many people are members, or at least the money they get can
support them). A good free pop provider I have found is gmx.net although
they are a german site but when you just use a mail program, who cares?
They only thing with them though is that they delete mail after 30 days,
as long as you check it before then though it is on your computer
anyway.
