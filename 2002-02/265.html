Re: Internet connection

I'd suggest you put it behind a firewall (of your choice) and use a VPN 
client to allow remote machines to connect in.  Use PPTP (which is 
supported by Linux and many commercial firewall vendors) and Windoze comes 
with the client to use it.    I think people here tend to use things like
Smoothwall which should support it out of the box.

MMC

Any other way and you're really asking for trouble.
