Hi All,
	Here's the details of the next AUUG-SA meeting happening next

------
Date : Wednesday 13th February 2002
Time : 7:00 PM
Venue: Marcellina Pizza Bar Restaurant
       273 Hindley Street
       ADELAIDE
Who  : AUUG-SA members, SAGE-AU members, prospective members,
