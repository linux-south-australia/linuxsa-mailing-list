The piece I missed initially was making it optional. We've fixed that

For those who elect to use the filter when we provide it, we'll be

Its really more about being up front with our rotation rate, unlike 
other ADSL providers who don't even disclose how long they'll hold an 
address up for.

Since you're obviously savvy on dynamic dns frobs etc, you will 
obviously just set up a script to reconnect/reconfigure things when 
needed, so you're not going to even notice the 18 hour rotation of IP 
address.

These things are partially there to protect less educated people than 
you, for whom its far safer to have their computer disconnect from 
the 'net if they go on holiday for a week and leave their computer 
on, than it is to leave it online indefinitely.

Remember we're trying to cater to a community far wider than just LinuxSA here.
