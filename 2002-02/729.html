Re: Fax modems & ADSL

Hi Shane,
No problems whatsoever, I run a fax server on an ADSL line & get clean
14k4 faxes, so does one of my clients :)

The ADSL low-pass filter *does* work. Depending on the fax-modem, you
can usually get-away without the ADSL filter, but don't blame me when
you fax-modem doesn't negotiate properly :)

           |   |                  1 Wall jack
1-------- 2| 4 |                  2
     +----3|   |                  3 RJ11 double adapter
     |     +---+       +---+      4 ADSL modem
     |     +---+       |   |      5 ADSL low-pass filter
     +-----| 5 |-------| 6 |      6 fax/modem
           +---+       |   |
                       +---+
