Re: data transmission - which way does it go?

Shan!


> Can someone tell me, when I am on my LAN, if I have my pop client 
> looking at my server using it's fully qualified domain name, if 
> anything is brought down from that server, is it going to go out to 
> the internet and back into the workstation, or is it going to realise 
> that we are really talking about another computer on the LAN and take 
> the short way?


It depends on how you've setup your DNS and routing.

If you've sudo access (i.e. admin privileges) you could test this very 
easily by running tcpdump [read its man pages, I'd say you'd be looking 
to filter on port pop and using the -w and -w options] or by running 
ethereal.

Obviously that will tell you what's going where and what's coming in...
