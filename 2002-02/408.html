If you're using PPPoE, see 

   http://www.freebsd.org/doc/en_US.ISO8859-1/books/handbook/pppoe.html

which is the PPPoE section in the handbook.

(essentially speaking, you use the same "ppp" utility you use for 
running PPP over a modem, but you say your device is PPPoE:something
instead of /dev/cuaa0, where "something" is the name of your ethernet
interface)
