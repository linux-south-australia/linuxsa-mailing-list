> Hi -

> I'm looking for a laptop fast enough to run win4lin, so that 
> I can finally be completely windows-free. The best deal I've 
> seen so far is a P3-1Ghz, 20gb, 256mb with modem/net/dvd for 
> $2200. The thing is, it's a 'Highlander'. Has anybody used 
> this brand of laptop? Does linux run well on them?

Three people here at work use Highlanders and considering they're not a
'well known' brand such as Dell or Toshiba, the laptops seem to run very
well.  They are very competetive for their pricing, considering they
don't generally use proprietary hardware (such as RAM), but I have not
seen any of them running Linux.  The three guys here with Highlanders
are all running some form of Winblows.

I don't think they'd hesitate in recommending them as a laptop for
affordability, but I'd probably do some double-double checking to make
sure their hardware is compliant.
