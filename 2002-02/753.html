Re: Upgrading to Raid 1

With 2 new drives in a 3 drive RAID-5 system, you should be able to
extend the concept to do this.

In theory you could create an incomplete 3 disk RAID-5 stripe set with
the two new drives, format this, and then copy the old data over.  Once
copying is complete the old drive could be added to the now complete
stripe set.
