Re: spam attack - please help!

Hi Mike,

Your problem is not your firewall as you must have port 25 open if you want
SMTP to work but you do have on open relay. What you need to do is limit who
has access to mail relaying. The easiest way I find to do this is limit
access to the localhost or to the private subset you are running behind the
firewall. In RH7.2 you can edit /etc/mail/access and add an entry like
"192.168.0    relay" and then compile the db or cheat like I do and use
Webmin which does all the work for you.

I am getting a least one spamer a day trying to use my box at the moment.
