[Article] Sun to charge for next StarOffice

This article popped up not long ago....

"
Sun to charge for next StarOffice

By Stephen Shankland
Staff Writer, CNET News.com
February 25, 2002, 3:50 PM PT

In a move that could undercut its anti-Microsoft campaign, Sun Microsystems 
plans to start charging for the next version of its StarOffice software, a 
suite of programs that competes with Microsoft Office but runs on Linux and 
Solaris as well as on Windows.

Sun has been offering StarOffice as a free download since acquiring the 
German company Star Division in 1999. But Sun plans to start charging for 
version 6.0, due to arrive in the second half of May, a source familiar 
with the plan said.
