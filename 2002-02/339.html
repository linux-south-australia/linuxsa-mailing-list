Rumoured ISP's to charge for E-MAIL

I heard this "rumour" this morning and float it for comment.

Some if not all ISP's will soon start to charge a fee per E-mail sent based either on size or destination in addition to the current monthly fee.

As Simon is a regular contributor to this list he may be able to deal with this early in the day.

I don't send a large amount of E-mail but an additional charge on top of my ISP cost would see me very seriously consider whether this "luxury" is needed.

It would have a serious impact on lists such as this.
