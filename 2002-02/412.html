Re: Postgres vs Mysql

Lots of simultaneous transactions? Use Postgres.
Need online backup? Use Postgres.

Very few simultaneous connections? Use Mysql.
Want really low overheads? Use Mysql.

In general, both work pretty well, and reliably. I'd rather use Postgres
than Oracle, any day. In fact, I'd go out of my way to avoid Oracle. ICK!
