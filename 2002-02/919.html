RE: Squid Tuning

For my dialup link, i use:

refresh_pattern -i \.(jpg|jpeg|gif|png|mov|avi)$ 10000 95% 99000 ignore-reload override-expire override-lastmod
refresh_pattern -i \.(mpg|mpeg|mid|wav|mp3)$  10000 95% 99000 ignore-reload override-expire override-lastmod
refresh_pattern -i . 300 15% 3600

Works very well.
I get good hit rates on often visited sites and i very rarely get old
content.
