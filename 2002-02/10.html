Re: WebCams

Matthew,

I am currently going through the process at the moment of trying to get 
my Creative Web Cam working under Linux. Before you make any purchases 
you will need to research whether there is a currently supported driver 
you can check out http://www.exploits.org/v4l/ for more information.

In my case even though a number of Creative Web Cams are supported (see 
http://alpha.dyndns.org/ov511/) some using a later omnivision chip 
aren't yet (although work is under way to implement support). It is 
worthwhile checking out what sensor and bridge the Web Cam you are 
intending to use has prior to purchase.

Best of luck... 

Kym
