I used to (in a previous job) have a Compaq Armada laptop - it was a bit 
chunky, but linux went on pretty easily and ran quite happily.

One good thing I found about the Compaqs is that there is a Compaq repair 
place in the Compaq building on Frome St (where the LSA meetings are) and 
they'll repair things pretty quickly and to be honest I think that's very 
important with laptops (my new laptop is barely 6months old and covered in 
scratches etc already)
