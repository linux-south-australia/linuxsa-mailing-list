What do you mean by "custom" NEC motherboard? 

At startup, make sure you have ALL things that require interrupts on
different IRQ's. Make sure you have the IDE drives either on separate
buses or CABLED correctly. To cable an IDE drive correctly you put the
master on the END of the cable and the slave on the MIDDLE of the cable.
Older Seagate drives will act really strangely if you cable them wrong
no matter what you do with their jumper settings.
