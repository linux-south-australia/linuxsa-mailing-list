Re: kernel/module build help

Anthony Symons wrote:
> Sounds like your getting your versions mixed up.

If you're trying to build two versions of the same kernel, and want to 
keep your modules separate easily (usually a good idea!), I think 
there's a variable something like MINOR_VERSION in the top-level 
Makefile.  Just add a number, or the date, or your initials, or 
something similar so you can distinguish between yours and the original, 
then go and do the whole "make dep && make clean..." thing.
