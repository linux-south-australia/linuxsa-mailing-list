Re: Driver - Philips 150P2

Would it perhaps be useful to try xvidtune?

I don't think a driver is what you need.  xvidtune, on a CRT display
anyway, can change the horizontal and vertical sync rates just
slightly.  You can then use the settings in your /etc/X11/XF86Config-4
(look for the Modeline lines) and X will come up with these values every
time...

eg...

        Modeline "1400x1050"  129   1400 1436 1628 1868
                              1050 1051 1054 1072 +HSync +VSync
