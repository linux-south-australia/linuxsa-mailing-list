[OT] Youth Project seeks offers of work experience

Hi,

The Salisbury North Youth & IT Project is providing training and 
experience to a group of young people who wish to gain employment in 
the IT industry in South Australia.

At present they have completed requirements for the "Train Small 
Groups" qualification and they are working their way through the 
requirements for the International Computer Drivers License.  By May 
they will also have completed awareness sessions on basic Computer 
Maintenance and Web Design.

After the formal training is completed they will be required to 
complete 2 weeks of work experience in the IT industry.

If there is anybody willing to consider some of these young people for 
work experience would they please contact the Project Coordinator, 
Saara, on 8406 8566 business hours or email to 
spiiroinen@salisbury.sa.gov.au and she will discuss their requirements 
and suitability with you.

Any offers to help would be greatly appreciated.

Cheers,  Ian Loxton
