Hi There,
    You need a book called "Operating Systems:Design &
Implementation" by Andrew Tanenbaum and Albert Woodhull.
ISBN 0-13-630195-9

    This book is by the guy who wrote minix (and taught
Linus BTW). It comes with a copy of minix and all the tools
you need to do whatever with it.

    As for the modules bit for Linux, try any of the Linux
Programming books out there. Hope this has helped.
    Cheers!
