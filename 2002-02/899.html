i think by "dynamic content" andrew was referring to content that is
generated server-side by some kind of scripting (like getting product
catalogues from a database), rather than flash/dhtml/etc. that is,
content that is not just a file on a server, but is generated based on

without the parameters changing and without the file's timestamp
changing (such as, adding a product to the database).
