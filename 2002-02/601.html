RE: [OT] How Modern CPUs Deal with Heat Managment

Have two Transmeta 800MHz Crusoes on the bench at home on PC104 arcitecture
motherboards.   The chips are standard BGA form factor (JDEC standard) but
the major desktop manufactuers are not supporting them yet.   Still feels
like an Intel/AMD agreement lurking somewhere.  I have these two boards as
a result of an industrial control project that I have been working on in my
spare time.   Transmeta have targeted OEM suppliers.manufacturers for their
initial release, hence industrial control/remote management applications
are their main focus.   Desktops maybe in the future, I cannot wait as they
are *bloody* fast for the power consumption !

Get your hands on a Fujitsu notebook and you will find one there aswell.

http://www.fujitsupc.com/www/products_notebooks.shtml?products/notebooks/p_series

The Midori Linux port is not bad either, few quirks but useable.
