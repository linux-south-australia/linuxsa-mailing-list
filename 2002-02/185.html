Better ADSL pricing coming soon

Hi,

I've just been informed by a Telstra rep that fantastic new ADSL
wholesale pricing will be available in 2-3 weeks time. This means that
by the end of March, every ISP in the country will be able to offer
great deals on ADSL, but no-one will be able to give you an exact price
until *after* the Bigpond February 14th cut-off-contract-without-penalty
date.

Telstra make no apologies for this poor and convenient (for them)
timing, so if you want a good deal long term, it might be worth enduring
the pain of dialup just for a month or two until ADSL becomes
affordable.
