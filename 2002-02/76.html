Re: ADSL Feedback

Generally speaking the more bandwidth people have available to them the more
MB they chunk through in a month. Not simply because they download more,but
also because it loads faster so they look at more, faster. So for 90% of
people they will find that they end up using more on a DSL connection than
they would on a modem connection. IMHO

-Michael
