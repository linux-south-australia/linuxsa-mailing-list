Re: [Article] Sun to charge for next StarOffice

This is my take:

1) Star Office

I'll find someone or somehow get to use (read pirate) Star Office 6 and
see if it's worth buying. If it's worth buying then I'd be willing to
buy it. I will buy good software; I'm just not prepared to buy crap.

2) Open Office

As my part of "Open Office" development I'll continue to use it and to
find out what it does. I don't have the skills or time to actually fix
bugs or create enhancements but I do know how to use bugzilla. Whether I
buy Star Office or not won't stop me from helping the Open Office team
by just using it, making suggestions and pointing out bugs when I can.


I personally don't believe we can complain about Sun making Star Office
a paid for product, especially if they can demonstrably prove
compatibility with other companies' products. If they can't prove that,
or their product is worse than Open Office then I'll just cope with Open
Office.

Linux is about choice. I can choose to pay for a crappy product if I
want to...
