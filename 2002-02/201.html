Re: Dynamic DNS.

I use magenet's dyn host services and have found them 100% reliable and
best of all, totally free! They provide you with a very simple perl
auto-updater script which can be invoked by your ip-up.local scripts or
from the command line. You can sign up at
http://summit.magenet.net/mpis/

They also have web mail and lots of other free facilities. Very nice!
Very friendly!
