I have used Dia quite a bit and I highly recommend it. It has never 
crashed on me, I have never lost my drawing. It is easy to use, easy to 
learn and has all the features I need. Symbols, grouping, great text 
labels, etc. I export my diagrams into png, and they look great.
I've used Dia under Windows, Solaris & Linux. Its extremely handy to be 
able to work on your diagrams no matter where you are.

Only two small things,
- I haven't worked out how to get two lines to dock with each other
- it would be nice if it shared a format with Vi$io, but this isn't
  Dia's fault.
