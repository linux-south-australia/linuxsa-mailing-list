RE: Linux-supported hardware

>I expect to install Mandrake Linux (as well as, temporarily I 
>hope, Windows 98) and assume I should check on 
>'www.linux-mandrake.com' for supported hardware.  Could 
>somebody please tell me the bits and pieces I should check for 
>Linux compatibility (eg sound card, video card, motherboard, etc).

As a general rule of thumb the base components are supported
'out-of-the-box' 
(ie: motherboard, CPU, video/sound cards, cd-writers, dvdrom etc)

Its the peripheral hardware you'll need to take more notice of, things like
printers, scanners, cameras, many are supported, but not all (esp. USB), so
do your homework for these.

http://www.linuxdoc.org/HOWTO/Hardware-HOWTO/

www.linuxhardware.org

www.linuxharware.net

www.linux-usb.org (check the working devices list)

www.lhd.org

http://www.redhat.com/support/hardware/

HTH
