Re: ATA RAID for Linux

Daniel Cumming wrote:

> I intend to use IDE RAID 5 on an upcoming Linux Server. What cards are good
> and what should i stay away from? I have been looking at Promise and 3ware
> cards at the moment, but i dont want one with poor linux support.


Okay, personal experience and opinion speaking here - don't.

I've got a Promise IDE Raid controller that I was running as (*ahem* 
hardware) RAID 1. A drive died. I replaced the drive, and told the 
controller to re-build the array. It killed all the data on the good drive.

I now run software RAID 1 (which I've tested, and I know works).

</b*tch about Promise controller>
