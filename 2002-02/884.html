Re: Flash drives

Might be worth looking at cramfs and/or romfs also.

One technique I have used is to store everything on the flash disk
(using cramfs) that doesn't change (ie /bin, /sbin, /usr etc), and also
store tar files for the stuff that needs to change (/etc /var), and
untar these into a freshly mounted ram disk at boot time.
