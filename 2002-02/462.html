RE: squid

>Is there a way in squid to limit the amount of logging that it 
>does or am I better off just rotating the logs every week or so?

You're probably better off rotating the logs every week or so.

We do it daily, weekly & monthly, mainly for the HTML reports using SARG.

The squid log is appended to separate files (D,W &M) every night, then SARG
is run 
over these files using cron jobs to generate the D, W & M reports.  
Then the files are cleared in the same script, keeps it nice and clean.

This way you only have a max of one day's traffic in your main squid log,
and you have html reports for the rest.  

Of course whether this is the absolute ideal is open for comment, but it
works
very well for us.
