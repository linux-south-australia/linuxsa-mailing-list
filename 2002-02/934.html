RE: SCSI Tape problems

Also try changing the parity settings on the tape and/or the scsi bus.
How long is your scsi cable? Do you have an active or passive
terminator? (hint: the passives look normal, the actives often have an
LED on them, or say "LVD" or have some other indicator). Long cables
with passive terminators can create intermittent problems. If this is
the case an active terminator might fix them. 

Also, speaking of LVD, Im guessing that it is not an LVD device. If this
is the case, make sure you dont have it plugged in to an LVD scsi plug.
I once mixed the two and got a hdd that I could only access the first
50mb or so of the disk and then it'd lock up. Sounds very similar...
