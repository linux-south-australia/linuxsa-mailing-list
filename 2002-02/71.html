Re: ADSL Feedback

One of the problems for me (if I could get dsl.. in the hills, yeah
right) is the cap would be reached far too quickly. When we were on
bigpond dialup we reached the 300mb cap in like a week (if we made it
last the month, it would be avg 10mb a day which we use so quickly). Now
with the much faster download rate, even a cap of 500mb would go so
quick you wouldn't know what has happened.

Granted, different people use the net differently. I think if the price
was reduced more customers who aren't heavy net users would sign up,
justifying a bigger cap for the few that do need/want it. Then again,
this might just attract people to scab up all they can and lose you
money. It's a hard one.
