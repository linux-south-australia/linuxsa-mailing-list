Re: [OT]: best way to redelegate without interuption

Change the TTL and similar values to something way low on the telstra
nameserver first. Then wait for the duration of the old TTL so that the
new ones have propagated. Then when your ready, do the swap, all
nameservers will have updated in the short time period, and when your
satisfied there was no errors turn the TTLs up again.
