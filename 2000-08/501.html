This is a nice little script that I call "kn" for kill netscape:

--script begin

#! /bin/bash
killall -9 netscape-communicator; rm -f ~/.netscape/lock

--script end

Use it as the user who owned the netscape that is misbehaving. I put it
in /usr/bin/ because my netscape crashes so often it's not at all
funny...
