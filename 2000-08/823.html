Re: Swap space

Nick Stoic wrote:
> 
> I have two swap spaces:
> 
> /dev/hdb6       which is 64MB
> /tmp/swapfile   which is 128MB
> 
> Now I've been advised that swap won't help much to speed up the system. 
> My question is, could I reclaim in a safe manner the /tmp/swapfile swap
> space to recover the 128MB of the HDD?

As root:
1. swapoff /tmp/swapfile
2. Edit /etc/fstab removing the entry for /tmp/swapfile
3. rm /tmp/swapfile

Dada!
