Re: xman query (newbie question)

Paul Malcolm Bailey wrote:

> Worse, if I type "xman &", I get:
> 
> [1] 23567
> Error: Can't open display:
> 
> ...and *no* prompt until I hit Enter.

No, you would have got a prompt; it's just mixed in with the output
of the above, because two concurrent processes are writing to the
same terminal.  When you hit Enter you're getting a brand new, virgin
white lemon fresh prompt.  Ergo, when I do this on my machine here:


[root@nipnyep /root]# xman &
[1] 7139
[root@nipnyep /root]# Error: Can't open display: 

Hoopla - embedded prompt.
