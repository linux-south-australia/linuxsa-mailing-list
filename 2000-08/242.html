Painful personal experience has taught me to very carefully check the S3
setup options. There are several S3 servers for XFree86 (and in version 4
they are diferent). Carefully check your model against the list.

If your monitor is not in the list, do a custom setup on the monitor, and
place the closest setting in you can. Try for lesser rather than greator
settings in any case.
