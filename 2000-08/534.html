Re: ippp0

On Tue, 15 Aug 2000, Frank W. Holden Jr. wrote:

> Howdy all. I have a fresh install of Mandrake 7.1 and at boot time I
> keep getting it is trying to load ippp0 module and it can't find it. I
> don't use ISDN I have ADSL and have determined that ISDN is what this
> module is for or atleast taken a good guess. Can anyone please tell me
> where this is being called up or tell me HOW to turn it off??? It is
> starting to drive me crazy and I don't have the whole box configured
> yet!

I don't know where it's being called but you should be able to turn it off
by putting something like:

alias ippp0 off

in /etc/conf.modules (or /etc/modules.conf for 2.3+ kernels)
