An aside:  There used to be an OS floating around called keyos
(the principal developer having moved on to www.eros-os.org).
It used to save snapshots of its processes state to disk on a
regular basis, so much so that you could actually pull the power
from it and have the entire system bootstrap itself back to the
point where it was at before the power was removed.  Neat trick.
Something I planned to look at when I had plenty of time up my
tight-fitting sleeves ...
