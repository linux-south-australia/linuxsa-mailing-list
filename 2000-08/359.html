Karl, I had the same problem with a KTX monitor and Cirrus Logic card, on 
both Suse and RH.
First, there are specific instructions concerning the video card (Cirrus 
HOW_TO ?) which you should seek out and read.  Second, in RH 6.2 install 
the x-config sequence allows you to try out your selection before 
saving.  I chose a monitor which looked like the KTX one (Samsung 15E), 
which worked just fine on one monitor, but ACER (something) worked better 
in the other KTX monitor.
I now use Stealth Diamond 3D 2000 Pro 4Mb on one monitor and an i740 8Mb on 
the other.  Both KTX monitors are driven at 1024x768 without any problem.
Hope this helps.
