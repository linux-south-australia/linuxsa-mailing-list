Configuring my NT-Linux LAN: Something must be wrong, but what?

I know it must be easy, but it just don't work!

I'm trying for days to configure a local LAN
between a NT client and a Slackware 3.5 server.
Configuring the server was a joke, but the NT don't
see it!

The problem seems to be on the NT side,
because I have Red Hat 5.1 on /dev/hda2 on the same
computer and the network works just fine from it
(with the same hardware & IP settings).
