Re: Trouble installing Linux

On Thu, 17 Aug 2000, Matthew Mills wrote:
> John , It may be that you are installing on a HDD greater than 8.4Gb, I believe
> the version of Lilo in Redhat 6.2 does not support booting from HDD's greater
> than 8.4Gb  (please everyone correct me if I am wrong)  Newer linux
> distributions such as Mandrake 7.1 have a newer version of Lilo which supports
> these larger HDD's

This seems to be the case. On versions of Mandrake before 7.1 and Redhat
before 6.2, the kickstart auto-allocation of partitions and the Disk Druid
allocation (with a mind of its own) seem to put the root (and boot)
partition(s) where it likes, which can be above the 1024 limit for the
older LILO. In some instances I think there were issues with the swap
partition(s) as well.
