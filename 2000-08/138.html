IP Accounting

Hi Folks,

I'm after some leads on IP accounting for Linux.

What I really need is the equivalent of the facility on a cisco
where you get a list of the source, destination, packet count
and byte count for all traffic exiting a particular interface:

    Source           Destination    Packets    Bytes
    111.222.10.20    10.20.30.40         72     6912
    222.111.20.10    10.20.30.50          9      565

The approaches I've seen so far are either interface level byte
and packet counts, or approaches that exist on top of ipchains
which (if I understand them correctly) only report counts against
a particular rule, not the individual IP addresses.

Cheers, Dieter.
