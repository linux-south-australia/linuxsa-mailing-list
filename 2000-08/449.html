Debian 2.2 CDROMs

Hi all,

Sick of RPM based distributions?  Sick of having to manually check for
package upgrades?  Sick of not being able to upgrade your distribution
with one command?  Want your distribution run by a community rather
than a company?  Well this post might just be for you.

Debian 2.2 should be released any day now (in fact ftp.debian.org has
been updated already).

If you are interested in upgrading or switching to Debian, we are
offering a once off CD burning service for this new release.

There are 3 CDROMs in the distribution (main, contrib and non-us)
and the fee will be $10 for the set.  This is higher than the cost of
the CDRs so to cover any drink coasters made.  Any profits left over
will be donated to the Debian project.

If you are interested then email me (mneuling@radlogic.com.au) or
Richard (rrussell@deh.sa.gov.au) off the list or talk to either of us
at tonights meeting.  CDs will be able to be picked up at the
September meeting or from my work (Fullarton Rd in Eastwood).
