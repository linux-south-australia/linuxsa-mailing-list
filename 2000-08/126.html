Re: IRC Chat (newbie question)

Ricardo!

> What is the best IRC Chat service that i can install in my Red Hat 6.2?

I presume you mean a client and not a server. I use xchat with the gnome
environment; it has almost all the features of mIrc but it's for the
gnome environment. You don't need to use gnome to use it though. There's
also one called blade, bitchx and of course, irc (the command line one)
itself...

Why don't you do an "irc" lookup at http://freshmeat.net and take a look
at some of the home pages? A lot of them have screen shots and
descriptions of features. You should be aware that you don't need to
"only" use one client all the time; you could for example decide to use
xchat one day, kchat (I think) the next and irc the next or for that
matter, all of them at once :-)

DL

(incidentally if you installed X, xchat is probably already there...)
