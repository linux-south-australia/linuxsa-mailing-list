Well, technically, he is right. The version of LILO on RH 6.2 does not
support booting vmlinuz on any parition that spans or is above 8.4GB.

The fact that a standard RH 6.2 install creates a /boot partition, and thus
works around this restriction, does not matter. His claim, as I see it, is
correct.

RH 7.0 Beta uses LILO 21.4.4, and can, with the correct BIOS, boot from
anywhere on a large HD.
