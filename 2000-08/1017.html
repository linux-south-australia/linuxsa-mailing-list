RE: modem speaker not working

I would suggest you add "M1L1" to your Init2 lines.  Obviously without the
quotes.

This should turn the speaker on during dialling (M1) and set the volume to 1
(L1) you can play with the volume if you need..... I believe it can range
from 1 to 4.
