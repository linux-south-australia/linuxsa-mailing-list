At 04:56 PM 8/20/00 +0930, Nick Stoic wrote:
>Hi gurus,

On the assumption that you have damaged your swap area on /dev/hdb6, use
the following command to make it usable:

  mkswap /dev/hdb6

and then:

  swapon /dev/hdb6

After that, you should be able to do:

  swapon -a

to see your swap areas.
