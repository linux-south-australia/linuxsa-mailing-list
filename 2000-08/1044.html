I'm using a Lucent winmodem in a laptop, and I found that setting l1m1
did nothing untill I got sound working, then I discovered a whole lot of
extra channels, and lo and behold, on of the sliders on my mixer
controls the volume of the modem speaker. Weird huh.

Well no, not really. I took the laptop apart (about 5 seconds after it
arrived) and found that I think the modem and the sound card are the
same card. (the laptop I'm sugin using has a mini-PCI bus. same bus,
smaller footprint).

So it makes sense that the soundcard would control the modem speaker,
and in fact, the modem doesn't *have* a speaker, but uses the sound
card' speaker. and also my line out, which means I can amplify my modem
noises. Yay!
