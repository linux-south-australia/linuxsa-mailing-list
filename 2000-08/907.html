Re: Samba - web based administration program

Why dont u just use webin?

You can customize webmin for a new user specifiying just what processes they can
actually do.  Literally you can allow them to control one process or all of
them.  You just allow access to relevant processes and grant more as they become
familiar with the program.

Why would you let someone who  is so "nervous" about commands in linx GUI even
have access to the system?
Murphy's law indicates you will be "granted the wonderfull opportunity to learn
more about the inner workings of linux" rather than the comforts of having a
newbie take some of the workload off your shoulders. JMHO
