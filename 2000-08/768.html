Re: Cabling Rules

> Do these new rules apply to running pieces of cable between a few pc's and
> making a small lan.

Hi Robyn,

We currently have two of our techs going through AUSTEL training at the
moment and I quizzed them on this a little while ago.

Theoretically, if you are using your LAN for Internet connection/phone line
sharing then the cabling has to be done by an AUSTEL guy/gal because your
LAN is being connected to the phone line which belongs to AUSTEL

If your are going to either staple the cable to the walls or run it through
the walls then everything cabling wise has to be done by AUSTEL but if you
are running the cable across the floor then you can place it yourself but
the cables still have to be made by an AUSTEL person.

If the LAN is just private then it doesn't matter.

If you ask me I think that the whole thing (from a SOHO point of view) is
just ridiculous.....
