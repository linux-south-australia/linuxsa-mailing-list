I forget if you've mentioned what your machine is? I've only been skimming
most of the threads lately. What programs are running? Running 'ps axf' will
give you a list of processes, for all users, in a nice tree format that
shows you which processes are subprocesses of another process. If you also
do a 'top' you can find out which processes are using the most CPU.
