a perfect day

Had an interesting day at work today Linux-wise, thought you might be
interested.

Sat through a demo of a security key mechanism (iKey) that you plug into
the USB port of a PC. yadda yadda yadda (looked interesting though).
There was discussion about downloading the software drivers from the
system providers, and how secure their system is. Very secure, we were
told. Passed a heavy audit. When pushed for details, yup, it's a Linux
box. Nice one, one point for the good guys.

Later sat through a talk about Gov't Land Administration, what is
happening interstate. Victoria are pushing ahead down the road of online
transactions, and are to propose soon a development model to
create/deploy a framework for secure, internet based land transfer type
transactions. They are proposing an open source development model to
develop the softawre and data frameworks (XML based). Very nice, two
points for the good guys.

All in all, a most pleasant and informative day. It must be sun spots,
or am I still asleep and dreaming all this...

Bruce
