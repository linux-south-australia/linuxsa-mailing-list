Linux UPS support

I must say though that I made an inquiry to Sola Australia only yesterday about Linux support for their UPS's.

Today I received the best response from any company yet.

They went into a lot of detail about Unix support and even pointed me to the source code should I need to recompile for my particular operating system.  They also advised that should I still need help, I can forward a query to them and their programmers will look at it.

That's the sort of attitude I like to see, so check 'em, out!  www.solaaust.com.au
