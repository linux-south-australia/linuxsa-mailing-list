Re: speed problems over ppp link

On Wed, Aug 30, 2000 at 04:15:43PM +0930, David Lloyd wrote:

 > Is there a way to determine what the maximum MTU for a particular link
 > is? [i.e. one that is relatively easy and not too arcane]

There's very little point, because whether or not fragmentation is
used depends on the smallest MTU between you and each-and-every endpoint,
not on your own MTU.

Path MTU Discovery is the best thing to use here (which is why it's
so mind bogglingly silly to block ICMP).  There was a discussion on 
this about six months ago on the lists, so check the archives for more
detail.
