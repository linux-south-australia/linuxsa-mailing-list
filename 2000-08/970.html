speed problems over ppp link

Hello, all:

I've got an internet gateway connecting about 30 users to the internet for
fairly basic smtp/http connectivity. It's a Mandrake 7.1 install running
postfix and squid. Since its inception, it really hasn't been anywhere near
the speed that I had expected, after replacing an old RedHat 5.2 box for
similar services. Apart from recently replacing the old gateway, it has also
been set up to dial in to a different isp to the old server, which is now
connect.com.

If I ping ifconfig's P-t-P address, I get around 50% packet loss and an
average 172ms response. Given that I have all services turned off while
pinging, that's not what I expected of a 56k modem link.

Hunting through /var/log/messages, I don't see the connect speed (only
"CONNECT<cr> -- got it". Looking at /proc/tty//driver/serial I see that on
ttyS1 I have a 16550A uart at a baud of 115200. "stty -a" tells me that I'm
running at 9600. Is there a way for me to see what speed I'm connected at?
Does anyone have any other tips to offer regarding crappy ppp speeds?

Much appreciated,
