That's just linux trying to be helpful and informative. It says, hey, the
guy who wrote this man page stuffed up some bits, here they are, and now
I'll display the page anyway. (and hopefully the error isn't too bad)

because windows doesn't tell you these things. The help file or app just
doesn't look right or work right. It doesn't tell you why :-)
