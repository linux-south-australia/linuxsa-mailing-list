Linux support by companies (was Austar Broadband)

Tell me about it.

I am trying to get information out of my ISP (Primus) regarding the configuration of their NT RAS server (which they tell me that they use) so I can forward this information on to discussion groups, Linux vendors, you name it.

The told me that MS is not enforcing a monopoly by forcing people to use MS operating systems.  I replied that this is not the issue, that here they have people willing to do all the research and hard work for them and they still don't care.
