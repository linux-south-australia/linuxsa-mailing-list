Modem sharing

After reading the "Linux Modem sharing mini-HOWTO"

We have our LAN users sharing a generic v90 modem hanging off com2 on the
server.
Total setup time about 10mins COOL!!!! (make sure you have cu installed
first BTW)

My question is more for the client side.

The HOWTO pointed me to called "DialOut/IP" see www.tactical-sw.com
Now this runs on our Win2k clients fine and maps the server:port to a com
port so windoze can assign a modem to it.
Again COOL!!! I works and works well the first time!! (doesn't do a great
job of hanging up the modem BTW)

But in the vein of "if it a'int broke don't fix it" does anyone know of, or
use, something else on the client side?
