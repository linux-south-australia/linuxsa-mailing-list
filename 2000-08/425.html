Re: Login Warning!

On Mon, 14 Aug 2000, Patrick Browne wrote:

> Can I display a warning message BEFORE the login/password prompt?
> i.e. "This is a private systems, you are subject to prosecution if you ...
> etc etc"
<SNIP>

Have a look at /etc/issue and /etc/issue.net. These will do what you are
after. issue.net is for remote logins via say telnet, issue is for local
console logins. Be careful though as redhat has a nasty habit of
overwriting these files at boot up. /etc/rc.d/rc.local is the culprit
here.
