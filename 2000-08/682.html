The Web Excel modems are a generic modem packaged by a company called
"Discovery". They are imported by a group up in Sydney, and normally sold
locally by Leader Computers. These modems average around the $80 - $90 and
are really worth buying over a Netcomm or 3Com / US Robotics etc because
they use exactly the SAME ROCKWELL chipset as the $100 - $300 modems and do
not perform any differnetly.

Altough the 15 k/sec ... I find that a little hard to belive as a 56k modem
is not even capable of that speed ... let alone Australian PSTN phone lines
