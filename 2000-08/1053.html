News article: Hardware giants back Linux lab

Hardware giants back Linux lab

Hewlett-Packard, IBM, Intel and NEC intend to accelerate development of
Linux applications for the enterprise environment through the joint
creation of a Linux lab. Announced overnight in the US, the new initiative
will be known as the Open Source Development Lab and will be based in
Oregon, US. The four companies plan to provide equipment and funding to
the lab over the next several years. They will work closely with Linux
vendors including Caldera, Linuxcare, LynuxWorks, Red Hat, SGI, SuSE,
TurboLinux and VA Linux.
