Re: NOT impressed

To telstra or not to telstra!

I have a number of friends in the east who have cable. A few for various
reasons got stuck with BPA. For the last 6 months or so there has been an
ongoing complaint that their connection was worse than with modems. The
people on Optus @ Home are laughing all the way to ping heaven. 

An interesting site is www.whirlpool.net.au which shows current n/w stats
for each it also talks about ADSL options. Telstra clearly have a track
record of 
1. being first with an expensive service
2. locking one down to it to maintain revenue to avoid churning b/w providers
3. oversubscribing their services to the point of non-functionality.

Some people go so far as to say telcos no jack about networking and as such
should stay out of it or bring in the expertise to administer it. "They see
wires with phones on the end"

I am personally going to wait, I have lived with modem for so long now, a
few more months till the competition is up isnt going to kill me, but when
it is and the dust settles I expect there will be a few disgruntled telstra
customers (AGAIN).
