Re: 56k Modem

> Can anyone recommend a good 56k v90 Modem for linux? I
> just upgraded my computer and I can't get my old ISA
> internal 56k Modem to work under linux or win98(It
> worked before on my previous linux box). Strangely the
> modem works under win2k.

Um, I'd recommend a Netcomm Roadster.. I have one and it is the best
modem that I have had the fortune of using.  I get consistend high speed
and good performance from it.

I'd also recommend working out why your old modem doesn't work.  I don't
think that it would be a winmodem because it is ISA, but it may well
be.  I think that fixing the problem with the modem you have would be
both more cost effective, and also help you to learn a lot.
