Re: Scrolling in xterm

> My xterm is version 4.0.1b(104). I have the scrollbar showing up on
> the left side of my window.


Congratulations on installing this considerable upgrade.  You will
find the new scrollbar a considerable improvement for the following
reasons:

  +	logically located on the left hand side for left-to-right
	languages such as all the Latin1 encodings, where it is close
	to the head of the line rather than across an expanse of
	emptiness;

  +	makes use of all three mouse buttons;

  +	allows small incremental up and down motions without requiring
	the user make distracting placements of the mouse pointer in
	the small buttons at either end of the scrollbar; and

  +	allows intuitive control over the granularity of the scroll
	movement.


But wait, that's not all.  You also get the standard <Shift><Page Up>
and <Shift><Page Down> bindings that dispense with the need for using
the mouse at al!
