Riva TNT cards are not 3DFX, but based on the Nvidia chipset. Nvidia have
their own X drivers on their web site www.nvidia.com. You can also use the
default SVGA driver (i think. The TNT works, but I don't know about the
TNT2), although it probably won't give you much if any hardware
acceleration.
