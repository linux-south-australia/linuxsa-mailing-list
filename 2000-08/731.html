>Hi Matt,

>I realised my computer being very slow. After a pretty long time, I came

>Then I read the e-mails sent by Michael, then your own, and I thought it
>would be a good idea to increase the swap space. I have a swap space of

>using fdisk. The problem is I have the feeling that is inaccesible.

>Do you guys think I can have two swap spaces, such as /dev/hdb6 and

Yes, of course you can have two swap spaces ... Just enter them both in
/etc/fstab (see 'man fstab' for info on the format of the fstab file).
