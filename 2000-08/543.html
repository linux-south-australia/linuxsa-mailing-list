Re: email

Attila Kertesz wrote:
> I would like to automatically CC all my outgoing messages to a specific
> email account. Can I do it?
> 
> For example, if I send a mail, I only send it to myfriend@hiscompany.com ,
> but in the meantime the mail is also sent to archive@mycompany.com as if I
> gave this address as CC.

Most decent e-mail packages have an "options" or "preference" menu that
would contain such a setting, allowing you to define a CC: or BCC:
(blind carbon copy - it will not show up in the recipient list) which
will automatically apply to all outgoing mail.
In Netscape, for example, you can access this setting under:
   ==>Edit==>Preferences==>Mail&Newsgroups==>Copies&Folders

What e-mail client are you using?
