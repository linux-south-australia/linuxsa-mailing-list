>Do you have access to a CD Burner and a Bootable CD ROM Drive ?

The simplest approach is usually to use the boot disk in the following way:

   At the boot prompt, type:

   linux initrd= root=/dev/<your-root-part> single

And then to rerun lilo, perhaps after fixing /etc/lilo.conf.
