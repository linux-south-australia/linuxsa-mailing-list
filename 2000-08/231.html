>Richard/Sean: is it possible to start and stop ethereal save the output and
>gzip it to another filename via a cron job?

Yes, you can do that, but why not use tethereal ... But what you have to do
is to tell it how many packets to capture ... There is no way to tell it to
stop when it sees a certain packet or after a certain time.

Actually, what you could do is run tcpdump -i eth0 -s 1500 -w file.cap
and fork that and then sleep for the period you want to capture for, and
then kill tcpdump.
