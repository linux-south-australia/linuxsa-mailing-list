rec.1 is part of the sox package, as is play.1.  The symbolic link 
included in the package just points to the wrong place.

[man1]$ ls -al rec.1
lrwxrwxrwx   1 root     root           37 Jun 22 12:15 rec.1 -> 
 /var/tmp/sox-root/usr/man/man1/play.1

[man1]$ file rec.1
rec.1: broken symbolic link to /var/tmp/sox-root/usr/man/man1/play.1

[man1]$ rpm -q -f rec.1
sox-12.16-3

[man1]$ rpm -q -l sox
...
/usr/man/man1/play.1
/usr/man/man1/rec.1
/usr/man/man1/sox.1

I have reported it to RedHat:
http://bugzilla.redhat.com/bugzilla/show_bug.cgi?id=15602
