Re: Trouble installing Linux

John , It may be that you are installing on a HDD greater than 8.4Gb, I believe
the version of Lilo in Redhat 6.2 does not support booting from HDD's greater
than 8.4Gb  (please everyone correct me if I am wrong)  Newer linux
distributions such as Mandrake 7.1 have a newer version of Lilo which supports
these larger HDD's
