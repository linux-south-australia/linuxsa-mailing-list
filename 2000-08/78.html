RE: modems & linux

Hi Peter, 
I have an el-cheapo deluxo, isa internal. it's a web excel, not sure if you
can get them, but they are flashable and do either flex or v90. But in
order of choice I'd prob go for
Swansmart external or ISA internal I dont need a modem chewing up valuable
cpu cycles doing compression, when it could be delivering me higher frame
rates ;-)
Banksia, Avtek or flagship netcomm all externals to avoid annoying hassles
of winmodems.

I normally do a half hearted check with ISP as to their recommendations.
But found the more discerning people (online gamers) want every ounce of
juice out of theirs so it may be worth checking with on some message boards
etc.
