Just briefly

>I can see where you're coming from, but why do you use qmail (restrictive)
>as opposed to smail (free; GPL'ed)?

I didn't really go into smail...I don't know why, but I suspect it wouldn't
do some of the things I needed when I decided on Qmail.


I set up a server for an organisation that needed easily created mailing
lists, that could be established by technically challenged users, and which
archived to the Web without too much system admin.

I chose Qmail for its support in this area and was impressed by the
performance. I have stuck with it since to make the admin of multiple
servers/sites easier for me to manage.
