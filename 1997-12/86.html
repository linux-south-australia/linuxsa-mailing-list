Re: Long filenames

> I have been using netscape to browse the cd's which come with a
> popular magazine, (APC), and have found that some of the images don't
> load bacause their filenames contain upper case, which the browser
> can't find on the cd.
> 
> After mounting the cd, using iso9660 filesystem, the filenames I see
> all appear to be dos 8.3 format, with no upper case present.  However,
> when I mount for instance the Slackware linux cd, long filenames
> including upper case are there in all their glory.
> 
> Does this mean that the filesystem on the APC cd is not fully
> iso9660/RockRidge compatible?

Most likely, the APC CD is ISO9660/Joliet instead of
ISO9660/RockRidge.  A kernel patch for Joliet and FAT32 support is
available at
http://www-plateau.cs.berkeley.edu/people/chaffee/fat32.html
