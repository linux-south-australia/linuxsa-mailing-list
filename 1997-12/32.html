Re: Multiple ethernet cards

Joshua Cole wrote:
> 
> Hi guys
> 
> Is there any trick to installing more than one ethernet card?  I have two
> identical NE2000 clones (KTX I think) and the machine is an Intel 386.
> Each card is configured to a different IRQ and base address.  Each works
> when the other card is not installed.  But when both are installed
> together, the kernel only notices one of them.  Subsequent ifconfig eth1
> up's fail because eth1 is claimed not to exist.  What gives?  I expect I'm
> overlooking something obvious, but I can't think what.

Try this at the LILO boot-prompt.

     ether=0,0,eth1

Also, read the BootPrompt-HOWTO and the Ethernet-HOWTO.  There might be
some other caveats to watch for.

Matt
