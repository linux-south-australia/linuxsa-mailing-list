> I went ahead and installed RH4.2 on to a 100MB partition and it was
> unsuccessful.  While it claims that the packages I selected would require
> 41MB of space, I suspect it requires some disk overhead for package
> decompression.  The install scripts failed for things like db, and
> e2fsprog.  Finally unpacking the kernel rpm failed and then ld.so.  Then

No (or at least very little) disk space is required for package
decompression; certainly not 60MB.  During the install, use 'df' on
the second virtual console to watch what it is doing as it installs.

Try selecting no extra components, then installing what you need
after the proper installation.
