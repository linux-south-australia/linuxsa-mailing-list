As an addendum to my previous post, sometimes you may 
have problems with the NE2000 autoprobe so you can specify
the ports and IRQ's on the LILO command line.

So in that case try...
	ether=5,0x300,eth0 ether=15,0x280,eth1

Or something similar for your particular setting.  
(The format is ether=IRQ,port,interface)
