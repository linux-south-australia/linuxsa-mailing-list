Hi John,

I have very little experience with Netware, and I've got no experience
with Lantastic, but I'm sure Samba compares favourably with them as
there are some large Samba installations where the comment was that
Samba was the only thing that would do the job.  For example,
Hewlett-Packard in the US use around 1,000 Samba servers to support
over 100,000 users.  Cisco Systems use Linux and Samba to serve about
1,600 printers worldwide for Unix, PCs, and Macintoshes.  The Samba
docs say that a single Samba server is capable of supporting over
2,000 simultaneous clients.
