On Sat, 13 Dec 1997, Ultimate Man wrote:

> I'm afraid that this is going to be a question that you'll all laugh at,
> but how do I set up a modem? I have RH 4.1, and all the usual stuff, but I
> can't find a modem HOWTO anywhere -- can anyone direct me to it, or send
> it to me? Thanks!

ttyS0 = COM1:
ttyS1 = COM2:
ttyS2 = COM3:
ttyS3 = COM4:

Also, you'll need to setup in your init.d scripts, the setserial command.

For 33.6k,
/sbin/setserial spd_vhi
For 28.8k & 14.4k,
/sbin/setserial spd_hi

Then go to the PPP Howto.
