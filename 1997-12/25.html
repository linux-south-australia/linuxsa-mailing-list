RE: SAMBA and/or other net software

Just before this thread dies... 8^)

I cannot comment on Netware, however, from my (now old) experiences with
Lantastic it was a good (read stable and reasonably featured) network
solution in its day.  However, it was of the LanManager style with you
directly building the protocol stack and was thus reasonably difficult
to configure from scratch.  I have found Samba much easier in this
regard.

Lantastic (at least the DOS version I got going) allowed the sharing of
disks and printers, and also (surprisingly) modems and the like.  It may
have had simple messaging facilities in there as well. I also had a
Windows (3.?) version but I think this was really just a GUI interface
to the original deal.

Anyway, hope this is of use...

Chris
