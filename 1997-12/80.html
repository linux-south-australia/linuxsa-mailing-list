Have you tried running minicom -s to see if the modem responds?  You
should
be able to get a dial tone by issuing atdt at the minicom prompt.

Also are you setting the modem up on the right port ?  Remember that
under
linux cua0 is port 1, cua1 is port 2 etc.

If this is ok, then may have something wrong with the ppp part.

Hope this sort of helps you get going.
