NFS (linux to solaris server)

Has anyone experienced slow NFS writes when going from
Linux to Solaris NFS (2.4 & 2.6)

Linux to Solaris NFS   - slow, by factor of 8 to 10 slower 
Solaris to Solaris NFS - OK
Linux to Linux NFS     - OK
Solaris to Linux NFS   - OK

Rates of about 800 kb/sec are expected (100BaseT & Cisco Switch)
