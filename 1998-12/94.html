Re: Does LINUX get into (the ISP) EISA.net.au for anybody here?

At 10:31  4/12/98 +1030, you wrote:
>
> I've just watched a newly installed LINUX box
> attempt to dial-up EISA (c.f. www.eisa.net.au)
>
> The ISP answers and (apparently) the two agree
> on a baud-rate... then, after maybe 3-5 min's,
> the box hangs up...
>
This might be the old-Telstra-timed-local-calls-trick, try turning off
call-waiting before you use your modem to dial your ISP by either manually
dialing #43#  or include it in your dial-up script with about a 20sec wait
before the ATD isp-number command.
Hope this helps.
