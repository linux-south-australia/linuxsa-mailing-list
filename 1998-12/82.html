Yes, i agree. It's also a little annoying for people who have seen it many
many times before. The Cookie Story is over 10 years old now. It even has
it's own Yahoo.com category. Unfortunatley we still get these things in our

http://kumite.com/myths/myths/ They have almost 200 different hoaxes listed!
