Unless I'm very much mistaken (and I rarely am), OSF1 does use sendmail
as the MTA, in which case you do have a .forward file.  Consequently you
also have filtering which is limited only by your own imagination...

   $ cat .forward
   "| /home/me/mail-filter"
   $ cat mail-filter
   #! /bin/sh
   `tee /tmp/$$ |
      sed -n '1,/^$/p' |
      tr '[A-Z]' '[a-z]' |
      fgrep '^to:
   ^cc:'`
   mbox=/var/spool/mail/me
   echo $headers | grep linuxsa > /dev/null && mbox=/home/me/linuxsa

   # lock $mbox
   cat /tmp/$$ >> $mbox
   # unlock $mbox
   rm /tmp/$$
