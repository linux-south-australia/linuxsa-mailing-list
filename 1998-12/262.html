NOT *Another* BSOD (Win NT)!!!

 Don't want to spend $15 for a LINUX Pocketbook
 for your friends?  (E.g. can't find one...)

 Then, go download the BSOD Screen Saver (by
 whatever name) from www.osr.com

 (Last time I looked, it was Cost-Free!)

 It won't make BSOD's any less frequent...
 but it might just help reduce your friends'
 blood pressure, eventually...

 I have occasion to work with a Win NT box
 which is equipped with such a screen saver

 And - after a number of "false starts" I'm
 happy to be able to report that my blood-
 pressure doesn't rise & I don't hear others
 (at this same Win NT box) streaming quite
 as often when the screen goes blue...

 It doesn't seem to matter whether there
 is a *real* BSOD failure-indicator -or-
 a *phoney* (screen-saver) image there.

 People just seem to have gotten used to
 it...  Who knows?  This may be just the
 right present for those friends you'd
 like to see live a few years longer...!

 BTW the error messages changes every time!
