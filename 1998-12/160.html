> Problems with Nettscape, sometimes I get a message box saying Plugin

I had that problem too. I do not what causes it, but I upgraded to
Communicator 4.5 and the problem disappeared.

> The other is no sound is played for those pages which have sound, my
> Netscape has no plug ins what and where do I get what's needed.

I also had the plugin problem with Communicator. It was there
no matter what version I used. The thing is, it only happened with
the Redhat RPMs of Netscape. Downloading the .tar.gz file from the
Netscape official web site allowed me to use most of the Plugins
without hassle. You also need to get onto the netscape page and
have a look at the Linux plugins. There is one called UMP which
handles MIDI files and then on sunsite
http://metalab.unc.edu/pub/Linux/apps/www/plugins/!INDEX.html
called mimeplugin. This allows playing of wav and au files.

Hope this answers your question.
