OK, here is what I get when I do a 'man ipfwadm'

-F = firewall forwarding policy
-p = default policy
-a = append new rule to policies
m = masquerade packets on the way out and demasquerade on the way out
-S = source
-D = destination
-P = protocol

BTW, ipfwadm-2.3-0.6 is installed
