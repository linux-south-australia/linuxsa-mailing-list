Re: Any speakers from the LinuxSA group available??

For those who've asked, info about SDCUCI can be found at:
http://videocam.net.au/sdcuci/  

It's a fairly small group, with about 20-30 members. The majority of the
members use Windows, although there's a few DOS users and a handful of
Commodore C64/128 folks (some of which do use other computers as well). 
There are quite a few who use 386 and possibly even 286 machines.

A good proportion of the members are retired, and I'd say it's definitely a
hobbyist group. Very nice people.
