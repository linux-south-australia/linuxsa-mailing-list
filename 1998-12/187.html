Re: scsi controllers

For a cheap, good, PCI SCSI card, the Advansys cards are worth a look.
I try to use Adaptec cards myself, but that's just because I've been lucky
and had good ones (eg. 1542, 2940, etc.) that have good kernel support,
rather than the more obscure/budget ones that aren't supported well (1502,
1505, etc.)
Symbios cards have decent support under linux as well, although I got the
feeling that the low end symbios clones were extremely cheaply made cards.
But hey, if it works, it works.
Toby
