Kirsty,

Don't get your hopes up too high.  The December 98 issue of
Linux Journal has an article on kernel 2.2 by Joseph
Pranevich, apparently a kernel developer.  In it he says:

	"... the bad news concerns an ongoing effort to 
	support USB (universal serial bus) and USB devices; 
	so far, any progress made in this area has not been
	included in a Linux 2.1 release.  While this could
	change before the official 2.2 release, it is
	unlikely that such a large feature would be included
	this close to release.  "

On the other hand, if I had a USB device, I would not hesitate
to download a developmental version of the kernel.  Maybe 
someone will respond favourably.
