Re: POP accounts for my students.

Easy. You can also put them into a group called "students". Before you add
any students, give the command:

groupadd students

to create the group. If you don't want each to have an individual home
directory, then you can make all the students share one directory. For each
student:

useradd -s /bin/false -g students -d /home/students USERNAME

Obviously change USERNAME for the name you want to give the student.

Hope that's what you were after.
