Pocket Book + Gnome

Hi ya all,

I have just had a play with a work mates Linux system.
He installed the entire RH5.2 distribution off the
APC Pocket Book and then tried to install Gnome.

It gave the errors.

... cannot install rpm failed dependencies ...
gtk+ needed by gtk+-devel
imlib needed by imlib-devel

which seems logical enough till I did a:
rpm -qa | grep xxx

and found that...

gtk+-X.X.X
gtk+-devel-X.X.X
imlib-X.X.X
ibmlib-devel-X.X.X

were already installed.

I decided to remove the development package of each
and then tried to install it again and it worked
perfectly and in the process added the development
packages back again.

My question is... Who, What, When, Where, Why and How?

It doesn't seem to make sense.

Nick Stock.
