/sbin/ipfwadm -A -lx will give output similar to  

  147495   11813054 out all  anywhere             anywhere             n/a
    2339    1327074 out all  lsi.ticons.com.au    anywhere             n/a
  149416   56256149 in  all  anywhere             anywhere             n/a
    2755     302031 in  all  anywhere             lsi.ticons.com.au    n/a
    3405     186295 in  all  anywhere             www.ticons.com.au    n/a

Which will give you usage of your link.  This coupled with MRTG will
give you some very useful statistics in graph form.  The URL for MRTG 
eludes me at the moment however.

For the above example the accounting rules only account for packets on 
the ppp link, giving in/out values.
