Re: Fw: 'The Emperor's New Clothers' on a HowTo doc't?

Darryl Ross wrote:

   [ resolv.conf "search" directive ]
 > Doesn't that tell it to seach everything through the nameserver?

No, it tells it the order in which it should append domains to
resolve non-fully-qualified names.

 > My /etc/resolv.conf is:
 > 
 > search bachblue.com.au
 > nameserver 192.168.1.1
 > nameserver 210.8.138.7
 > nameserver 210.8.138.4

Because you only want your system to look at one domain name, you 
might as well say "domain bachblue.com.au" instead of "search ...".
"search" is used if you want to look at more than one domain name,
e.g.:  "search cs.adelaide.edu.au itd.adelaide.edu.au maths.adelaide.edu.au"
would say, "If I type 'telnet foo', try foo.cs.adelaide.edu.au first,
then foo.itd if that didn't work, then foo.maths if that didn't work, 
then foo by itself (as a fully qualified name)"

"search ." is meaningless.  I'm surprised it'd work at all.
