>> Yeah? I can get a C64 for about $5 from a garage sale, maybe another $50

I write about computers and seldom get into net discussions. Just to say 
here, somebody got it right. Had a few people phone me to get a computer 
for their child. How old? Oh, six! Go to Cash Converters and get them a 
C64. If the kids wants to play games buy a used Nintendo or something 
and rent the games from the video store... they will want a new game 
every three days.

Another trick. If all you want is email get a shell account somewhere 
and run a VT-100 emulator on an XT. All up (with 14K modem)... less than 
$100. Works fine. If something breaks ('puter, monitor or modem) throw 
it in the wheelie bin and buy another one.
