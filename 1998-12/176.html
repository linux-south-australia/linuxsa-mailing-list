You can also remotely get ESMTP servers to run their queue...

Connect to your MX secondaries, issue the following... (Drop the
<> though!)

	EHLO <your.domain>
	
The system should present you with a list of ESMTP commands  included
should be ETRN.

So to get a queue to run use

	ETRN <your.domain>

And it should start processing all mail destined for your domain.

I haven't looked too closely at the complete ins and outs of this method,
but that is what is their for..
