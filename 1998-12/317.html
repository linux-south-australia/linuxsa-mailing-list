Re: Installing?

Just two thoughts that I have here:

1. change your harddrives around to make the 540MB primary and 120MB
secondary, this will give you the full 540MB on the big drive.

2. I'd stick with the basic structure and set up the first harddrive as
Linux Native (540MB should be just comfy).
The second I'd slice in two, ie. 60MB swap and 60MB as a second native Linux
to use as a backup for your primary drive.
