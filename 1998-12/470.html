Gigabyte AGP Card under linux not working

Hi,

I installed Linux on a friend's machine last night,
but couldn't get X working.  The video card is a
Gigabyte GA-600 series 3D AGP card.  I have no
idea as to the chipset used.   

I'm about to hunt around the net for some info, but
would appreciate if anybody has any info if they could
pass it on to me (or to the list).   I have read of similar
problems in the archives with another card.
