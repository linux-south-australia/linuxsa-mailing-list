Alan I heartily agree.  I started looking at Linux after the Apcmag article of some
months ago.  I purchased RedHat 5.1 and have spent the rest of the time trying to
work things out.  I have purchased some of those "600 page" books ie QUE: Using
Linux; Linux Unleashed 3rd ed.; A Practical Guide to Linux, Mark Sobell;  SAMS: Linux
in 24 hrs and even O'Reilly: Linux in a Nutshell.  However despite the quality of
information given by these books none of them have been as practical nor encouraging
as the Apcmag Pocketbook.  It wont replace the aforementioned tomes but it is an
encouraging introduction that produces results and is worthwhile as an evangelistic
tool in drawing others into the Linux using community. Bravo Apc
