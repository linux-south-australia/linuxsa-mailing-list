Stampede 0.89 is out

FYI...  for all of those optimisers out there. :-)

Europa-0.89 released.


     Wed Dec 9 21:33:16 MST 1998 Matthew S. Wood

     The Stampede Linux Development Team has announced the release of
Stampede Linux, version 0.89
     (Europa). This is a major release, featuring splat (the Stampede
Package Listing & Administration Tool),
     the latest 2.0.7 release of the GNU C Library, enhanced security,
optional SysV style color init scripts,
     and a host of bug fixes. More information is available at
http://www.stampede.org. 

     The distribution can be obtained via FTP, at ftp://ftp.stampede.org, or
at one of the Stampede Linux
     mirror sites (a list is available at
http://www.stampede.org/mirrors.html). 

     On a side note, beta testing has begun for the Stampede/Alpha port.
Interested persons should contact
     minotaur@beer.stampede.org for more information. 

Regards,
Brandon Johnson
