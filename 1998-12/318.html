Re: [SLUG] HP 1100A Printer (scanner & copier) combination

As far as I remember, that's a GDI (no processor) printer, ie. Good Luck,
and let me know how you went with it. (I assume that there is a work around
for GDI printers, as Linux cannot print directly to them).

Just for all the others here, most of the new copier/printer combos that are
to be released are GDI devices (ie. all from Sharp, Canon, Xerox, Toshiba,
Konica and HP - not sure about the others).

Also, most of them will scan at only 400dpi and print at 600dpi, so if you
need a higher resolution printer, go for a laser printer. (600dpi is enough
for the average user, though).
