Re: scsi controllers

I've always used the Adaptec cards and currently have a 2940. Works fine for
me. Adaptec used to go out of their way to not support linux but they have
changed their mind about that a little while ago.

If it is a bit too expensive, most scsi card will work and you can probably
find a card at http://metalab.unc.edu/LDP/HOWTO/Hardware-HOWTO.html or
redhat's http://www.redhat.com/support/docs/hardware.html
