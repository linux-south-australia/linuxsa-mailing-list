Re: Linux and memory usage.

Linux should automaticly reduce the size of the cache if a program need's more memory. I'd check how big your netscape memory cache is, just in case it's trying to allocate 50mb or something :-)

On the other hand, have a look at /usr/src/linux/Documentation/memory-tuning.txt if you've got  the linux kernel src installed, as it gives details on how to easily modify a the swap thresholds (thrahholds :-> -sorry)

What does 'free' say after you've loaded netscape?
