Lea,
	I have installed RedHat 5.1 & 5.2 on machines running win95
and win98 and there were no problems for me on either and
Lilo will setup dual boot in mbr which can allow you to
either automatically boot into windoze or linux as well as
manually choose instead to open your alternative OS. Linux
install will however recognise win 95/98 as dos but it
doesn't seem to affect anything.  
The problem with NT apparently (accordoing to the HowTo) is
that NT wants to control the mbr and therefore conflicts
with Linux, this is not a problem with win95/98 as far as my
experience goes.
