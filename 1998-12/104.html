Following on from the ComputerBank posting that was sent.  The original
author of the email has indicated that she wanted to respond.  There was
a good deal of personal information that she did not want forwarded.

At this point I think it would be useful to remind the general 
netiquette guideline of treating private email as private and not
forwarding information to a public forum without
prior consent.  If you still feel that you should post something
without prior consent (although i can see only rare occasions where
this would be appropriate) at least include the original author 
in the CC list.
