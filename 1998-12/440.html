Re: pppd goes mad!

The traceroute lists which hosts the packets you send travel through on the
way to the destination, in the order of leaving your machine, to getting to
the other end. For example:

> 1  gw.way7.Adelaide.telstra.net (139.130.140.1)  1925.882 ms *  4279.715
ms

1 is the hop number
gw.way7.Adelaide.telstra.net is the host
139.130.140.1 is the IP address of the host

If you want to find out where the delays are, what you can do is start at
the top of the list and ping that host. If there does not seem to be much
delay there, move down to the next host, and repeat until you get to the
host that is giving heaps of delay.

That should help you track the problem down a bit more.
