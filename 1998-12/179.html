Can't speak for the others you mentioned, but as owner of amy (aka 
*.narp.apana.org.au), I can say that I had no end of trouble setting up
PPP to the APANA hub.  In the end I went with SLIP instead, which has
been rock-solid for the last couple of years.  As I understand it, there
was a problem with the Linux (kernel mode) PPP driver talking to the *BSD
(user mode) PPP driver.  This may have been fixed by now, but as I'm not
using it, I can't confirm that.
