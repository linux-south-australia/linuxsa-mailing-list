You don't need X if you just need to do the equivalent of modemtool.
Simply type one of the following commands:

ln -sf ttyS0 /dev/modem    <- for COM1
ln -sf ttyS1 /dev/modem    <- for COM2
ln -sf ttyS2 /dev/modem    <- for COM3
ln -sf ttyS3 /dev/modem    <- for COM4
