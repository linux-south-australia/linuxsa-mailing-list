Re: PDP-11's (was labelled The death of John Lions)

Lots of people involved in High Tech have a
hobby on the side... in which they enjoy get-
ting their hands dirty with some classic
technology or other...

Classic Cars, Morse Code Radiotelegraphy,
Restoring Antiques (e.g. valve radio sets),
Sailing (motorless craft), etc.

So, why should it be *so* surprising that
there are people using PDP-11 (emulators)
or keeping alive other Classic Computers?

There is an elegance (and/or simplicity)
inherent in lots of classic relics from
previous generations...

Just because Telstra *closed* the Tele-
communications Museum (and no gov't can
find money to create a new generation
replacement) doesn't mean people don't
have every right to take pleasure in
quaint old systems in this field or
another...

"Past is Prologue" - who's words?
