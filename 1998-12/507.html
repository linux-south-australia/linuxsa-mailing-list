Re: Canon BJC255SP Bubblejet printer converter for linux

Jason Nunn wrote:

 > ofcourse, ghostscript can convert gif,jpg,raster data (etc)  to other
 > native printer languages (in Epson, HP), but my 3.33 aladdin distribution
 > just doesn't support 255SP.  .. hence my problem ;). 

Try one of the other bubblejet drivers:  bj10e, bj200, bjc600 and bjc800
are all supported by Aladdin Ghostscript 5.10, which came out just
over a year ago.

I think most of the bubblejets talk more or less the same language,
so you might have success with drivers for one of the 255-SP's
relatives.
