No fault, just that people should send postings to
linuxsa@linuxsa.org.au, not linuxsa@Elizabeth.netcraft.com.au.  Either
address will work at the moment, but one of them isn't guaranteed to
work if I move the list to a different machine.

Filtering based on the 'To:' address isn't a great idea though; as you
noticed, not everyone sends mail to exactly the same address.  Also,
if someone Bcc'd the list, your filter wouldn't catch that.  The best
header line to filter on is:

X-Loop: linuxsa@linuxsa.org.au

If you're using procmail and mh-style mailboxes, you can put this in
your .procmailrc:

:0 w
* ^X-Loop: linuxsa@linuxsa.org.au
| /usr/lib/mh/rcvstore +linuxsa -create
