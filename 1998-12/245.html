Survey: Which License Do YOU Put on Your [Open Source | Free] Software?

 After reading a bit of the debate on issues
 surrounding a developer's choice of license
 to be applied to the open source or "free"-
 software that you write, I wonder what YOUR
 practice in this area is...

 The Question is:  Which license do you use?

 If your answer refers to a "standard" lic-
 ense, e.g. one written by some open source
 organisation or other, kindly list:

 - the name and version of the license used
 - the URL of the organisation's license page

 If your license is a specially composed one
 (& you don't mind disclosing it to the list)
 please include a copy in your response.

 Any comments on why/how you chose/wrote the
 license you use would also be of interest.
