Register Now & Save for LinuxWorld

Come One, Come All ... Register Now & Save -- LinuxWorld Conference & Expo
                 '99; Register Online and Learn More About
           Today's Hottest and Fastest Growing Operating System


                  Gain Access to Exhibit Halls, Keynotes,
                     Conference Sessions and Tutorials


FRAMINGHAM, Mass.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Dec. 18, 1998--  IDG World Expo
announced today that registration is now available for Linux enthusiasts,
press and analysts to attend the upcoming LinuxWorld Conference & Expo,
March 1 - 4, 1999 at the San Jose Convention Center in San Jose, CA.
Register for the conference online now to receive advance information on
new products and services, parties and events and all other important
show-related information.

With over 40 conference sessions and 13 tutorials to choose from,
LinuxWorld Conference & Expo offers the corporate developer community the
most comprehensive showcase of real-world applications and technologies
using Linux. LinuxWorld Conference & Expo will feature Linus Torvalds,
Creator of Linux, Dr. Michael Cowpland, President and CEO, Corel
Corporation and Mark Jarvis, Senior Vice President of World Wide Marketing,
Oracle as keynote speakers who will present current and upcoming trends in
the Linux industry. The show will highlight exhibitors and users who will
provide the most up-to-date technological and marketplace information about
the industry. Visit the show's official site at
http://www.linuxworldexpo.com and explore conference sessions like GNOME,
Beowulf, Perl, along with a session on the Continuing Revolution presented
by Eric Raymond, just to name a few.

"Linux is one of the hottest technologies emerging from the industry
today," says Charles Greco, president, IDG World Expo. "We have launched
this information-rich Web site to deliver Linux news prior to the
conference and exhibition."

How to Register

To be eligible for registration, please complete the form available on the
Web site, in its entirety. Early registration is encouraged, as space is
limited. Save money and receive a T-shirt by registering for the conference
program before January 25th. Members of the press and analyst communities,
please register by February 22nd with the required credentials specified on
the Web site.  IDG's LinuxWorld Conference & Expo, sponsored by LinuxWorld
Magazine, Linux Journal, Earthweb's Developer.com, InfoWorld, Macworld,
Network World and Sys Admin is expected to draw over 8,000 independent and
corporate Linux developers and business professionals.

For exhibit availability and other promotional opportunities please contact
Jim Donovan via email at jim_donovan@idg.com or call 508-424-4814 (EST). To
be added to our mailing list, please email your name and mailing address to
linuxinfo@arcadiap.com.

About IDG World Expo

IDG World Expo, a business unit of IDG, the world's leading IT media,
research and exposition company. IDG publishes more than 290 computer
magazines and newspapers and 700 book titles and offers online users the
largest network of technology-specific sites around the world through
IDG.net, which comprises more than 240 targeted Web sites in 55 countries.
IDG is also a leading producer of 168 computer-related expositions
worldwide, and provides IT market analysis through 49 offices in 41
countries worldwide. For more information, please visit IDG's Web site at
www.idg.com.
