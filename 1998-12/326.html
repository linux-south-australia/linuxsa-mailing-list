Note that this is one very important contribution that new users can make
to linux.  When you come across outdated, or incorrect information in a
document, you should send an email to the maintainer.  Naturally a patch
to the documentation would be prefered, but even just a quick note to
inform the maintainer that a passage is out of date is useful.
