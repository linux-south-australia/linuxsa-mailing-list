Distributed.net switches to linux

Heyas - I don't know how many of you are aware of distributed.net's efforts
(see http://distributed.net if you dont), but have a look at their latest
plans for the new statsbox...It has been running on NT4 with MS SQL since
it's inception, but it is continually failling to cope with the large amount
of work it needs to do these days. As well as crashing, for various reasons,
it's taking about 1/4 of a 24hr day to generate the stats, which are then
available for the remainder of the day.

The new statsbox will probably be running Linux with Sybase ASE. For
reasons, comparisions, etc. see this url.

http://nodezero.distributed.net/cgi/dnet-finger.cgi?user=nugget
