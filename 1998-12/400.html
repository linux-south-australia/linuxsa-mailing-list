Re: Disk Space

On Mon, 21 Dec 1998, Darryl Ross wrote:

> Heyas All,
> 
> I just got a quick, and probably really easy, question for you. I want to
> know how I can find out how much disk space a certain directory and it's
> subdirectories takes up. Eg, a users home directory.

du -s <directory-name>

If you don't have read permission to everything under that directory, the
figures will be wrong.  "man du" for further information.
