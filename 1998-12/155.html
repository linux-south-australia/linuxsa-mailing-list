Remember UNIX is not the answer, it's only a stop on the way.  It doesn't
matter if in 10 years time we're all using HURD, Plan9 or some other
wonderful new OS.  The important characteristics of UNIX will always
be with us (flexibility, power, ease of use, stability) or we will
never leave the current implementation.  But that doesn't mean it will
always stay the same!  Unix now is much better than it was 10 years ago --
in 10 years more, we may not recognize it as Unix again :-)

And, of course, writing a Windoze haters handbook would be a little
redundant, really, since everyone hates it, they just don't know there
is a better alternative :-)
