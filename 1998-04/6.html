Proposal for Policy on Commercial Posts

Okay,

Well that got a bit of comments going.

The following is only a proposal, just something a bit more
concrete than my previous post.

In general, the LinuxSA policy will be along the lines of

====

Any commercial post will:

	o have COMMERCIAL: as the first word in the title
	o be pre-approved by Geoffrey or Myself
	o have a strong bent towards Linux, or at least
		Unix, or be regarding a benefit that a 
		company is offering LinuxSA members.

====

That should cover about all the concerns that people probably have.

Remember it will never be set in concrete.  And hence will be 
flexible to a certian extent.

Comments welcome.
