I downloaded it about a week ago and installed it installed on RedHat 5.0
without any problems. I had already  upgraded the libc5 libraries to

loading up though (486DX4-100, 32Mb).
I don't think that there were any local sites listed on the download page
but the site I downloaded it from in the US was quite fast. I don't
remember which one it was though.
