Re: Linux startup files ?

I did a talk in August 1997 on the init system under Linux.

That might give you some help.

The URL for it is

	http://www.linuxsa.org.au/meetings/1997-07/init/

You might also want to do the following to find out what else
is associated with the startup scripts (assuming rpm-based system).

	rpm -qf /etc/rc.d/rc

gives

	initscripts-3.25-1

And then a 

	rpm -ql initscripts 
	
gives

	*a whole pile of files that are part of the initscripts package.*

This procedure is quite useful in finding out where config files
are hiding for a particular program.
