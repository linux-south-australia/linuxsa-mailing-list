Re: F3 dies under X

On Tue, 14 Apr 1998, Benjamin Close wrote:

> Hi All,
>     I have a rather curious problem. When ever I'm in X I cannot switch
> back to tty3. I.e. ALT+CTRL+F3 does nothing. All the over virtual
> terminals work and if I switch back to say tty1 and then ALT+F3 to tty3
> it works. I had this problem from a direct installation in both 5 and
> 4.2. F3 works fine in other apps in X
> 
> Not urgent to fix but one of the things on the list :)
> 
Your window manager is probably catching the ALT+CTRL sequence.  This is
almost always configured in the .*wmrc file.  Have a look and if you still
can't fix it give us some more information about your X setup -- and/or
post your .*wmrc file somewhere so we can have a look at it (just don't
spam everyone's mailbox by posting the whole thing to the list).

Good Luck
