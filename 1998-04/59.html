FYI: OEM Alpha Motherboards in Australia

Hello LUG coordinators (except Canberra, they've already seen it). Here's 
what might be a useful bit of information regarding Alpha motherboards 
for PC cases. I have nothing whatsoever to do with this, except for the 
fact that I'd love to have one :-)

Regards,

Dan Shearer
Dan.Shearer@unisa.edu.au

Contrary to popular rumour OEM Alpha motherboards are available in volume
in Australia right now. Samsung manufacture Alpha chips and are involved
in developing the technology further. All motherboards run NT and Linux,
in fact their top-end product mentions Linux in the glossy brochure.  Have
a look at their product line at http://www.samsungsemi.com. They are
looking for representatives but they are also willing to deliver small
quantities. Pricing seems to be well below Digital Alpha but definitely
above Pentium 333. Their fastest motherboard is faster than Digital's. 
Throughput is very high on these boards - but check the specs yourself.
