LinuxSA Logo

Hello ppl.

If people are bored and are graphically gifted (or at least can use gimp)
maybe people can do a LinuxSA logo.

Just something that looks good, and will be noticeable, and can be used on
top of meeting announcements, etc.

Some conditions,

	Use red and blue as the primary colors.  
	Be 150x50 pixels.
	Be indexed color friendly.
	Be a gif.

	Have an equivalent B&W image that looks good too.
	
It doesn't have to use Tux, but can have a funny looking logo shape.

I can't promise that it will be used heavily, but you will be acknowledged for
it.

Cheers.
