If this pause occurs after the sendmail starting message, it may be a
problem with the format of your host.domain name in /etc/hosts. This was a
problem for me in the past.  This format for host.domain worked for me
when I had redhat installed.

#                hostname       host.domain
127.0.0.1        localhost      localhost.localdomain
192.168.1.1      triffic        triffic.deadgood
