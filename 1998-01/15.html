Just to add my 2c worth.  K&R did make an ANSI version also, it is used
(now) as the Unix textbook in the Uni. of South Aust. and should be
available through UniBooks etc.

I used Peter Norton's C++ book to learn C++.  It may be what you are
looking for but I would consider it pretty basic.  Good to start with
but I would expect more from a reference.
