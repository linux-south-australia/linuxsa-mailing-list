Linux and 'Titanic'

Here is a notch on the belt for Linux.

Apparently Digital Domain, the company that did the graphics for
Titanic, used a render-farm of about 160 Alpha based computers running
Linux to do the bulk of the rendering for the movie.

The suppliers of the hardware, a company called Carrera, have a press
release at http://www.carrera.com/pr3.html.

There is an article in the Feb 98 Linux Journal.
