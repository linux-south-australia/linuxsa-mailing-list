> I want to copy 500+MB from one hd to the other, what's the quickest and
> safest way to do this. I want to verify the files, if this is possible.

Presuming that the stuff you want to copy is in the directory /source
and that you want to copy it to /destination, you could do this (as
root, of course):

cd /source
tar cf - . | tar xvpCf /destination -

To verify:
diff -r /source /destination
(no output means that they are the same)
