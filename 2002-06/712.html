from my experience no sa.edu.au delegation has ever gone near aunic in any
sense.

whilst edu.au is still governed by aunic (at least until jul 1)

see: http://www.aunic.net/tded.html

sa.edu.au is administered directly via hostmaster.tafe.sa.edu.au under the
auspices of the minister for education.

fact.

where you'll need to register a matching business name just to get that
