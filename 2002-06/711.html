>I'm trying to do a redelogation for an sa.edu.au domain. No one I know
knows anything about how to do an sa.edu.au properly. Every lead I have
looked at the person either has left the job or has retired.

whois.aunic.net contains data for all of the sa.edu.au domains, and they're
registered with normal aunic XX-AU NIC handles.

My memory is hazy on this, and it's quite possibly wrong, but I think last
time I needed to redelegate an sa.edu.au, I used (or told someone else to
use..) the .edu.au form on aunic to do so..

Don't believe that just because there's a '.' in part of a hostname it must
recurse through the DNS to work.. there used to be a few xxx.yyy.net.au
domains in Australia that didn't require 'yyy.net.au' to exist, the .net.au
name server looked after it.
