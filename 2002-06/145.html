RE: Laptop Purchase

Personally, I like Toshibas -- well built, they use the nipple rather
than the pad (I like nipples), and they are generally well-supported.
However, the keyboard repeat problem sucks. I still get occasional
repeated letter pairs. Also, no matter what laptop you get, be very
careful to check all the model numbers of every major component (eg
graphics, network, etc), and make sure it's suppported under the version
of the OS you want to use. Mine has a Trident CyberBladeXP ALi, which
isn't supported under X, whereas CyberBladeXPm (for eg) is,
apparently...
