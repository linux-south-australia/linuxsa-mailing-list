I beleive (from what I recall) , that I had similar problems with a promise RAID card.

The problem is that Linux picks up the individual IDE controlers rather than the "RAID" part of the controler.

See www.promise.com under "linux support".

I beleive the supplied driver is only for Redhat though not Mandrake.  Insnit Mandrake basically Redhat with extra optimisations and a different installer though?
