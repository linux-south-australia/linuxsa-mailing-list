Re: Probing external SCSI devices

On Fri, May 03, 2002 at 10:26:18AM +0930, Andrew Reid wrote:

> Under 2.2, how does one probe external SCSI devices? I plugged my AIT
> drive in, and it didn't detect it automagically.
> 
> For some reason, I was expecting the kernel to magically see it, as I
> seem to recall that kind of thing happening in other servers in the
> past. Perhaps I'm imagining things :-)

It would seem that I've found the solution to my problem (!). I found
a little script on the Interweb which seems to cause the kernel to
scan all SCSI buses for devices.

It worked flawlessly, and I was immediately able to write to the
tape.

For those that are interested, I've put a copy on my
website. http://www.plug.cx/rescan-scsi-bus.sh. Credits to Kurt
Garloff <kurt@garloff.de> for the script.
