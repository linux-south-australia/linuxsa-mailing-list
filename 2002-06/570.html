*We* all know, the internet is a hostile place, but perhaps countless
other Australians don't understand that.  Call them naive; call them
innocent; either way, the Government seems to be trying to help.

but it does sound like a good idea in principle.  I'd say it's a geniuine
attempt at pro bono publico.
