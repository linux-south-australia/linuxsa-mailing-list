It doesn't sound like you're really qualified to ask that I "...not
mention Compaq again..." -- you're basing your opinion on heresay.

I've worked with *lots* of laptops including Compaq, and I'd grant you
that:

* They're expensive to repair.

* Compaq's turn-around times for parts are shocking.

However,

* They're built like brick shithouses (less so when compared to IBM,
  but still good).

* They generally use good quality components.

* Geeze, they just work

They're not quite perfect (by a long way), but they're certainly
better than you give them credit for.
