On the first bit, we have also cooked a couple 10k SCSI drives due
to lack of ventilation.  We had 4 of them setup in RAID 5 and 1 died,
strangely enough before we could get a replacement a second one went
AWOL as well............must be something in the air........caused all
sorts of hassles because at the time it was our main file storage box
and quite a number of users lost data from anything they had open at the
time.  We ended up replacing it with a single IDE drive as a "temporary"
measure, temporary being a little over 12 months and counting :), I'm 
about to replace it with a software RAID setup using IDE drives rather
than SCSI.
