As it currently stands, you need an ABN, ACN or a RBN (Registered
Business Name). So, you don't have to have an ABN as a sole trader, so
long as you've registered a business name. One would assume that if you
are doing the trading as your self (eg. Andrew Hill) and not as
something else (eg. Andrew Hill trading as Foo Bar) then you would need
to register your name as an RBN.
