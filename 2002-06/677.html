Re: [OT] SCSI Drives, how hot is too hot?

Most HDD Manufacturers will give you specs on the max temp their
drives will run at -- I've generally heard temps in the range of 50-60
degrees is ok, but you may want to check that for your drive. Of
course, 10K will be hotter than 7.2K, and more platters will also be
hotter...
