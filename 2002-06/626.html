Re: OT anyone know of a sourse for Galaxy Pay TV antenna?

>I'm looking for a source of antenna that Galaxy Pay TV used to use.
Anyone know where I can get a couple of these babies?
>Want to set up a wireless link between buildings.

Hills Industries supplied all of the Galaxy antenna's.   When Galaxy went
bust they redesigned the feed antenna and now sell them over the counter in
Edwardstown for WLAN work.

You will be lucky to find the aluminium grid packs that Galaxy originally
rolled out, there are not that many around.  The powder coated grid packs
have an integrated LNA/Downconverter up front which makes them difficult to
modify.  So unless you are a 2.4Ghz antenna guru you are better off paying
the $$$ and buying one new from Hills.
