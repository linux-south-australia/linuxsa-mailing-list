Re: [OT] SCSI Drives, how hot is too hot?

On Wed, Jun 26, 2002 at 01:14:50PM +0930, Patrick Browne wrote:

 > Anyway the two SCSI drives run very hot, almost too hot to touch, is this
 > normal (Quantam drives BTW).
 > Also a replacement IBM SCSI drive runs hot (but not as hot).
 > Is this normal for 10000rpm SCSI drives?

Yes.  Most hard disks these days are designed to run pretty hot.  That
doesn't mean that you shouldn't give them adequate cooling air, but it
does mean that you don't need to worry if your cooling is ok but the 
drive is hot anyway.
