Lindows - subtle disclaimer <G>

Just got a great newsletter from the folks at Lindows.

In line with the recent court case (for those who came in late, MS lost 
the case against Lindows over naming rights and also put the Windows 
trademark into question) the disclaimer from Lindows is very subtle and 
tongue-in-cheek.

is a registered trademark of Linus Torvalds. Microsoft® Windows® 
operating system is a registered trademark or service mark of the 
Microsoft Corporation."

"Trademark or servicemark"? Nice subtle one there <G>
