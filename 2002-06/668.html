Use of OSS in Peru

Came across this on LinuxToday this morning, thought it is a superbly
written letter in response to a letter from the GM of Microsoft in Peru
disagreeing with a Bill in the Peruvian government recommending public 
institutions be mandated to use open source software.

The interesting sideline is that the author of the letter is in fact
a Peruvian pollie, why don't we have such people in government here,
instead of the uninformed decision makers we currently put up with...

Original letter by MS GM in Peru:
http://www.pimientolinux.com/peru2ms/ms_to_villanueva.html

Response letter (lengthy but worth the read...):
http://linuxtoday.com/news_story.php3?ltsn=2002-05-06-012-26-OS-SM-LL
