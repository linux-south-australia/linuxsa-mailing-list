Hi Andrew,

The answer to your question depends upon the distribution you use. Most 
distro will have a control center that allows you to specify which 
services to start up at boot up. It is usually in a GUI.  In Mandrake go 
into control center, system and then services. In SuSE, go into Yast 
control center, system, run level editor, and run level properties. You 
will have to know what the run level numbers mean - I think that 5 is 
GUI.  I am not sure about Red Hat, its been a long time since I used it.
