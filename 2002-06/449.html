Re: apt-get from behind a firewall

David Creed wrote:

> Can someone please give me a few pointers as I can get stuff all
> help from MIS and I wont be able to get them to change anything
> without excellent justification as they both make and work to
> 'corporate standards' and we're just a fly in the ointment.

It sounds like they may have opted for NTLM authentication on the MS
proxy.

If so, you'll need to find something to create this information (I've
seen some interesting hacks for cache systems that involve running a
daemon on a windows box just to generate the auth credentials for a unix
system) or get the MIS guys to turn on 'basic' authentication.

If you're really lucky, the samba guys may have worked out a way around
this.
