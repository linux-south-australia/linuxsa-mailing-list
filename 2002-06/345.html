> one he is given netspace.

> servers via the BPA link
> and the remainder via the netspace link.
> 
> He wants to funnel all (free) BPA traffic which could be to multiple IP

> [lan ]--------[Linux router natted ?]/

> 
> I read up on route but I admit I didnt understand it too well.
> He asked me I said it was possible but I am not sure how (conceptually I
> understand I think) but practically I dont.

'route' only tells the traffic where to go, it doesn't tell it how to
come back.

You probably need to read up on iptables etc to make sure that a routing
decision is made BEFORE NAT takes place, and there is a seperate NAT
rule for each outbound interface, then you'll probably be ok.

Definately keep a close eye on the data usage once you think you have it
working :)
