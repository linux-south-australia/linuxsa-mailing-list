None of the above. If I have understood the problem, it is most likely a 
Linux/Unix SysV problem.

When files are created under Linux, they inherit their owner and group 
owner from the UID and primary GID of the creator. When you create files, 
Samba changes its UID/GID (vector) to your credentials. (Note, FreeBSD is 
different. The GID of newly created files is inherited from the containing 
directory.)

There are a couple of ways around this:

1. Set the SETGID bit on the directory. Under Linux and SysV this 
specifies that the GID is inherited from the containing directory.

2. Specify 'force group ...' as David suggests below.
