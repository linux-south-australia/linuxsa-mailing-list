from http://www.auda.org.au/docs/auda-policy-rules.pdf, page 6:

1. To be eligible for a domain name in the com.au 2LD, registrants must
be:
a) an Australian registered company; or
b) trading under a registered business name in any Australian State or
Territory; or
c) an Australian partnership or sole trader;
d) a foreign company licensed to trade in Australia; or
e) an owner of an Australian Registered Trade Mark; or
f) an applicant for an Australian Registered Trade Mark1; or
g) an association incorporated in any Australian State or Territory; or
h) an Australian commercial statutory body.

so i'm assuming part (c) is the relevant bit. do you need to register
for an ABN as a sole trader for this to apply, or can anyone call
themselves a "sole trader" without any external reference/registration?
