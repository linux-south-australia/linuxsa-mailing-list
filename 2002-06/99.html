Yes, many. LinuxSA is one of the more active ones though.

good starting points for finding mailing lists: 
www.linux.org.au
www.linux.org

Usually, look under User Groups, and you'll find plenty of lists. Also,
look at the web page for the product you're trying to use, or do a
google search for subject-specific mailing lists.

A word to the wise: Never send HTML email to Linux mailing lists :)

Look into iptables (or ipchains if you run a 2.2 kernel).

Start by closing everything, then open the ports you need for the
traffic you want.

Know if you need NAT (masquerading) or not.
