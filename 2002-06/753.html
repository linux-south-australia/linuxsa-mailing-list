Linux Usage In Australia, Particularly South Australia

Hi all,

I am new to this list and I was wondering if I could draw on the experience of 
the local Linux community. I am curious to know what the state of Linux usage 
is in South Australia and Australia in general.

That is, do you know of schools, businesses, government departments, 
libraries, etc that run Linux ? 

I am particularly interested in schools as I think an open source environment 
is very important in education from a number of angles: better learning 
experience (ie learn programming, administration, or just generally be a 
better computer user), more affordable for schools, students can take home a 
copy so there's no pressure to pay for software just to have what your school 
has, more disadvantaged communities can be supported due to low cost.

Also, are there any major initiatives to lobby government in South Australia 
or Australia in general? Such as the attempts made by the Open Source Society 
in New Zealand. Or grass roots initiatives like the schools project in 
Portland, USA?

Thanks,

Damien.
