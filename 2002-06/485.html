Hello Simon,

I had the same problem using rp3 on Redhat 7.2. What it boils down to 
is that your "user" doesn't have the permission to access some of the 
PPP configuration files. You need to find those (I don't recall them of 
the top of my head and I'm in front of my work Win 2000 box) and change 
the permissions so your user can access them.
