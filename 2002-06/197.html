Re: GPG

If your key has the string "adam" in its name or email address fields you
could do:

    gpg --export adam > adam.key

to create a binary file with a public key that you could attach to an email.

Or you could ASCII armour the key with the "-a" option and put the resultant
ASCII text in the body of your email.

    gpg -a --export adam > adam.key

Cheers.
