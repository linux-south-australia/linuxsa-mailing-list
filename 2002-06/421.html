>Has anyone managed to get RADIUS to do their
>accounting in a similar situation? What tools do people use to do
>accounting with squid?

I'm unfamiliar with authenticating squid against RADIUS, we do it against
either NT or Samba PDCs, in which case the username is recorded in squid's
access log instead of the ip address, I would presume RADIUS authentication
would give you the username in the log also.

We use SARG (formerly sqmgrlog) for reporting, it pulls out some nice html 
reports with most of the info you might need, ie: bytes in, % of data in 
cache/out of cache, and with a little basic scripting (verrrry basic), you 
can get it to generate daily/weekly/monthly reports, depending on
preference.

http://web.onda.com.br/orso/sarg.html
