Netscape Email Pita

Although I thought I figured out what was causing the "recycled email" I
think that I was wrong.

Instead, I think that Netscape 6 will mark email as deleted or read in a
different way than pine or fetchmail will.

Netscape 6's deletions and markings as read are for Netscape 6 only!

When I read or delete my local mail in Netscape 6, it will be marked as
read.

However, if I close Netscape 6 and go into Pine (I am using that now) the
message will still be there or marked as unread.

I tested this theory by going back into Pine and marking the messages as
read whilst in Pine. When I went back into Netscape 6, lo and
behold...they were markd as read.

I will talk about my gripes and frustrations in my next email.
