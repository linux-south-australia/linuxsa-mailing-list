Re: Multiple default routes

Sam Silvester <sam@myunlimited.net> wrote:
> Can I put in two default routes

Think about it: The default route is where data is sent in the absence of
any explicit route.  Do you really want the data to go via *two* different
links?  I think not.  In any case, no, you can't have two default routes.

I think what you want is explicit routes that data to some destinations
go through one interface, and data to other destinations go through
the other.  Perhaps you prefer to have these routes be automatically
determined, in which case you need to exchange route information with
your upstream providers.

Look at BGP or Gated.
