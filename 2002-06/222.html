At 5:06 PM +0930 05/06/2002, Wayne Simes wrote:
>What advice would you give to someone (like me) who knows very little about
>intrusion detection software.

time), and to base your beliefs about system integrity on knowledge 
of whether ANYTHING has changed that you don't expect - at all - and 
to work out why, in such circumstances.
