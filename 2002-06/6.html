Re: JDK for PowerPC linux

Jason Tan wrote:
> 
> I know about blackdown.org, but am curious if there are others.
> 

If you are looking for a port of Sun's JDK to Linux/PPC, then AFAIK, 
Blackdown is the only place you'll find one. Unfortunately, there aren't 
any large companies in PowerPC-land who are interested in Linux with 
Java, and everyone else just uses the Blackdown port.

I know Debian packages exist for GCJ on PPC, so I assume GCJ runs to 
some degree on PPCs. Provided it implements enough of the APIs that your 
project(s) use, it may be useful for you.
