Now I understand a little more what you mean.

Here is what I see for login:

***
Redhat Linux 7.1(Seawolf)
Linux linux 2.4.18-SGI_XFS_1.1 i686 unknown

login:
***

Which part of the above would you like to change?

The first two lines are contained in /etc/issue and /etc/issue.net. The
"login:" is provided by the /bin/login program and so it might be a
little more difficult to change.

Be aware that in some versions of Linux, eg RedHat 7.1 and earlier,
/etc/issue is created dynamically with the script in /etc/rc.d/rc.local.
