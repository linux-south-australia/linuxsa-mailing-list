Re: [OT] anyone know of any second hand ADSL modems? wireless gatewayfor ADSL?

> Yes you can use a wireless network card and a ADSL card in the same
> machine as a router. You need to get a card that uses the Prism chipset.
> You then reflash the card to allow the card to support Access Point.
> Note: make sure you firewall your router... Restrict DHCP if you use it,
> etc..
> 

You don't _have_ to reflash the card.  You can run it in ADHOC mode as well.  
(this is essentially peer-to-peer rather than via an AP).

But the statements about firewalling are valid.  Also, at home, I suggest a 
metal fence around your house - it kills wireless connections outside your 
boundary stone dead (well, I ended up with a metal fence through shear luck 
and I can't connect or even see any of my machines on the otherside of the 
fence - this does mean I can't surf from the park just behind my house ..)
