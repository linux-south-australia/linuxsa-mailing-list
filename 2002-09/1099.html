The above mentioned cards do work on Mandrake Beta9, default kernel. The
"usb-storage" module does recognise them and runs them as a SCSI disk
emulation. In Mandrake, chaning "storage=no" in /etc/sysconfig/usb got
the device recognised without having to do any manual module probing.
