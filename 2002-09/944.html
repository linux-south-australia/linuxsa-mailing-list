ClarkConnect is built on Redhat 7.2 so in my experience anything ( including
RPMs ) that are available for Redhat 7.2 will also work in ClarkConnect.

Most utilities and log analysers can produce graphical output in HTML
format, so you just need to direct that output to your inbuilt webserver and
you're off.
