Recent advances in the Samba area :-)

Hi,

For those who are interested in migrating NT and non-AD Win2K servers, the 
recently developed 'net rpc samsync' (internally known as 'net rpc 
vampire') allows you to retrieve the SAM from an NT server and stick it 
onto Samba. Along with the 'net setlocalsid S-1-5-21-x-y-z' command or the 
'net rpc getdomainsid' or some such, a Samba server can pretty much 
completely take over the role of an NT server. Even profiles work!

Secondly, if you have had problems when changing the name of your Samba 
PDC (workstations that have joined the domain have to rejoin [because 
Samba regenerates its SID when you change its name]), you can fix that 
with 'net getlocalsid <old-name>' and 'net setlocalsid S-1-...'.

You need samba-head, but all this functionality should be in Samba 
3.0.0alpha30, and 3.0.0 will be come maintained soon.
