Luke,

Do you have any sessions open on your system which is in the directory mount
point, eg if at sometime you did a "cd  /mnt/floppy" and left your terminal
in this location.

If you do, this may be blocking your unmount request, can you close the
session or change to another filesystem location, eg "cd  /root".
