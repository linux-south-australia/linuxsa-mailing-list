It's probably much easier to bag outsourcing when on the outside - but that
is the nature of today's economy.

Outsourcing - if managed well - like any market system - can work
beautifully.  But it requires the willingness of the customer and their
support people to be a little constructive, to work as a team with the
outsourcer rather than just bag the outsourcer without putting too much
thought or research in to it.
