Re: [OT] Adelaide Uni - "The Microsoft Way"

All,

A little knowledge and a touch of gossip adds up to pure crap.

Firstly i am not affiliated with ITS ( the central university It service ) 
I work for a department.

This is absolutely rubbish. Yes some servers are being moved to ITS. But 
they are remaining the same platform as they are now. ie MS re remaining MS 
and Linux etc is remaining Linux etc.

Yes we have some MS servers ( not me personally - rather chew my own arm 
off ) but this is because the programs needed for course work is only 
available  under a MS platform. eg the language labs, and the business school.

As for the Cs Dept there has been no change at all. There is at least half 
a dozen different platforms for students to work from. Not to mention some 
very large clusters ( one of which is ours ) ALL RUNNING *NIX

So lets not listen to rumours.

Cheers
