What really sucks is the situation when you apply for ADSL
and are _NOT_ told that you can not get this service because
you have been pair gained.

When my neighbour decided to get ADSL and was refused service
on _two_ separate lines I recalled him complaining about 14400 max
connections. Since one of his lines had the same prefix as mine
and I have ADSL I told him to challenge Tel$tra (armed with a
Kate Lundy is your friend article) regarding "pair sharing"
(their term). It turns out that he was pair gained, Tel$tra
took it off and now he can get ADSL.

I think that is b@#$dy disgusting!
