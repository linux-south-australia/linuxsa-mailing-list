Re: X-ray machines and high-tech hardware

>Does anyone have solid evidence about what this does to your hardware?

I have undertaken quite a few trips to the US & China over the last 15
months.   I have not had problems with the Dell laptop other than M$
induced.  Watchout for mobile phones & X-ray machines.   I have found simm
cards do not like X-ray's and will break.  My record is one for one through
out the US when I forgot to take it out of the phone and put it in my
pocket.

If you want fun put a handheld transmitter, electronic prototypes, cro,
pliers, cutters, gas powered soldering iron etc in a briefcase as carry on
luggage.   In the US I was bailed up continually by the customs people for
over an hour explaining why a Electronic Design Engineer was carrying such
equipment.   Then again it was only 5 days after 11/9 (or 9/11 if you are
backwards) :)

My 0.022 (inc GST) worth.
