Re: Modem install HELP! :)

On Sun, 2002-09-08 at 03:38, Anthony Stapells wrote:
> Am I wrong in assuming that a 16650A device is a modem?  I admit that I am
> blindly guessing that from the instructions on the Redhat site.  I am dimly
> starting to suspect that it might actually just be a serial device...ie my
> mouse.

yes it's just a serial port.
/dev/ttyS0 is the first serial ("com") port
/dev/ttyS1 is the second one

sounds like maybe your mouse is in the second port?
(where does the /dev/mouse link point to?)

it's an internal PCI modem is it?
I've never used them, but maybe try ttyS0 or ttyS2...

BTW for setserial you use the serial device name (ie. /dev/ttyS0)
not the memory address, but it shouldn't be necessary anyway.

BTW also, 'cat /proc/pci' should list all your pci devices as
the kernel sees them.
