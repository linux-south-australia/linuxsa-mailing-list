Re: mini mice

Hi.

I have an benq mini for my laptop.

It really is mini - if you have a large hand, it might be too mini. I find
the buttons a bit awkward, but it's fine for travelling and occasional
use. It comes in a little bag and takes up very little space (which is why
I bought it). It's a usb device, comes with the usual ps2 adapter if your
laptop doesn't have usb.

Stacking it up against the desktop's intellimouse, I'd say it is 2/3 the
size, and about half the volume. A normal mouse I hold in my hand, the
mini I hold with my fingers.

Michael
