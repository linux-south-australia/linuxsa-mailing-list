Daniel,

You could either use another partition as your new swap... or backup the neighboring partition and resize the swap partition... (I think you are required to delete and re-create a ext2 or ext3 partition).

Otherwise use a swap file.... But swap files are slower than a swap partition...

Better, idea could be to increase the amount of RAM in this machine...
