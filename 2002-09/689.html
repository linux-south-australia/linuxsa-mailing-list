Re: APC's Linux

The October Issue covers Linux in a Feature - Linux - the next wave. They
look at Mandrake 9.0 beta 3, Lycoris Desktop / LX (which is on the CD) and
the OEone HomeBase Desktop.

The Linux Workshop talks about "Fixing Linux glitches" including how to fix
a broken network link, fix an ailing CD-ROM drive and enabling 32Bit DMA to
speed up your drives. They also have a page devoted to rolling your own
Kernel.
