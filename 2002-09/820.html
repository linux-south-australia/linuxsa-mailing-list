Ian,
My personal (and professional) experience is with Cisco and Enterasys.

The Enterasys Roamabout cards can be had for around $60 through "The 
Incredible Bulk" (http://wireless.org.au/tib/). 

I personally use the Enterasys cards at home because:

- they work
- they're cheap
- they're well supported on most OSes (Win, Linux etc)

At work we use Cisco Aironet (we sell _lots_ of Cisco stuff so we sell their
wireless stuff too). The cisco stuff has alsorts of neato features but is
not cheap.  It too is well supported.

Alot of the cards are the same, but rebadged. (eg. the Enterasys ones and 
the Lucent ones look very very similar ...!)

MMC
