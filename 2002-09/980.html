Re: [OT] Adelaide Uni - "The Microsoft Way"

As someone who is employed by ITS, I can tell you Ian that you couldn't 
be more wrong. There are far more Linux, Solaris and Tru64 servers in 
ITS than Windows (there is less than 5 Windows servers in total, while 
we have over 40 Solaris/Tru64/Linux servers). All of the University's 
major central infrastructure bar file and print sharing (where we use 
Netware, not Windows) is running on Unix/Linux. ITS even has several 
Linux-certified staff (LPIC-1), including myself. And I'm typing this up 
on my Linux desktop, which apparently according to you I'm not allowed 
to be using. That's news to me.

It's always interesting however to see the kinds of misinformation 
spread around the place, masquerading as fact.

Regards,
Lachlan
