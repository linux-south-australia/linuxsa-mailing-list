Re: mandrake 9.0/gcc3.2 vs. gcc 2.9x

Ben,

> so, is moving to a mdk9.0 system perhaps a little premature at this
> point, until more people/companies start compiling things with gcc3.x?
> good old chicken-and-egg problems!

It depends on what you want to do and what you are running on your
system. I tend to be ultra-paranoid because I've seen what installing
new stuff can do to systems. For example, when I installed RedHat 7.2 +
2.4.2 we fell over VM problems that we didn't detect in our testing. The
VM coped quite well under stress with multiple connections but the
system would almost crash at about 8:30 and just after lunch finished.
The VM fell over when lots of connections were being made
simultaneously.

It's a chicken and egg problem.

"obstropolous" can be defined as being deliberately difficult.
