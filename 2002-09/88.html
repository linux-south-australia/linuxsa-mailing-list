Something needs to be said, for better or worse.  Basically, the quality of 
the help & guidance that can be contributed by the members of this discussion 
group is pretty much a reflection of the work & focus you put into your 
question.

Few are perfect in their grammar, etc, and nobody expects perfection 
in that regard.  Nevertheless, there's no excuse for not making a reasonable 
effort to try to phrase a question in a way that can be understood.  In fact, 
it is probably a bit rude and presumptious to expect responses from questions 
that haven't been thought through, just a bit.  The poster of incoherent 
questions should anticipate some degree of annoyance from the group, or worse 
still no response at all.
