Re: Wireless PCI cards as cheap access points?

Yep.

I do exactly this.

I have:

PII233mhz with 2 ethernet cards and a PCI PCMCIA adapter with an Enterasys 
card running AdHoc mode.

One ethernet goes to the ADSL device (Alcatel SpeedTouch) and one goes to my 
internal network.

Everything happily uses my Internode Business ADSL link. Although there 
would be no problem running it over an PPPoE based link.
