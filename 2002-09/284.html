Re: Modem install HELP! :)

Hi Tony,

you have having the clasic IRQ conflicts. You said you have a serial mouse, which I take is on COM1 (/dev/ttyS0). Here is what you should do:

Disable COM2 in the bios of your MB.
Change the modem jumpers to COM2.

That should fix your problem, and you should see the modem on /dev/ttyS1.
If you dont see the modem, but the serial port does appear, then I would sugest that that scrounged part is no good !

Cheers,

Ben.
