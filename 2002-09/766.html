Re: Squid + firewall on old machines

On Wed, Sep 18, 2002 at 04:49:51PM +1000, Mcleod, Ian wrote:
> Will an old Pentium 133 with 32MB RAM work okay with Squid and firewall
> software (console only?) - caching with large files and so on?

Depends on the bandwidth and the number of users. You can tweak squid to run
quite well on the above hardware as long as you're not trying to serve 1500
users over a 100Mbit pipe. Have a look at www.squid-cache.org. I think they've
got some performance tuning documentation there somewhere.
