RE: best firewall - proxy software / reporting tools?

>Is there a good distribution for this?  Would Gentoo suffice?

Look at Linux from Scratch (LFS 3.0) http://www.linuxfromscratch.org/
You can download the packages yourself, follow the "step-by-step"
instructions and build your own minimal linux distro.   After you have done
this configuring wireless LAN and IPTables will be a snap.   This is the
best way of learning the fundamentals of Linux, the LFS doco is excellent,
if you have been using Mandrake (console mode) then you will find LFS
easier as they both have SysV filesystems.
