Re: Mouse configuration problems

its probably /dev/ttyS0 and /dev/mouse is more likely just pointing at 
the serial port /dev/ttyS0 = 'com1'  /ttys1 = 'com2' and so on..

check which port its physically connected to and that they are setup
'setserial -a /dev/ttySx'

is this a gui login?
then maybe Xserver needs configuring in here;
/etc/X11/XF86config

or maybe run the binary (it should be in the path so just type it);
Xconfigurator

otherwise 'man FX86config' is your friend

HTH
nik [tm]
