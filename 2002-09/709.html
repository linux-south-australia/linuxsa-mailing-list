Try just running gdmflexiserver from a prompt (or you may have a menu
item for it). On my system, it opens a new X session in the next
available virtual console, and prompts for a username and password. 

Once there, you should be able to switch between the two with
impunity.

This assumes you are running gnome. Though it'll probably work even
from within KDE.
