Windows managers aren't a required component for an X-Windows desktop.  I think 
we've covered that point enough.  

VMWare is an emulator - it can't run quicker than "on the raw hardware".  What 
it does give you (amongst other things) is the ability to run off a virtual 
hard disk, so you don't need to reinstall Windows and all your apps when 
everything gets corrupted - you just copy your backup disk back on top and away 
you go again.
