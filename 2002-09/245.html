Re: Equipment and airlines

> Apparently a lot of Boots have steel parts within them for durability.

Many have a steel rod down the midline of the sole.  Stops
them flexing when you step on a rock or whatever with the
arch of your foot.  The more expensive the boot, the higher
the odds.

Just give up on boots for the foreseeable future.  They're
as much worried about them containing explosives ever since
that idiot tried to light the fuse to his shoe bomb.

You'll be occassionally asked to remove sneakers, but you
need only flex these to show they aren't lethal.  But boots
don't flex and thus come in for heavy scrutiny.
