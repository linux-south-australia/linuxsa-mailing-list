Apache Open SSL Worm vulnerability

For those who may not have seen this yet

http://securityresponse.symantec.com/avcenter/security/Content/2002.09.13.ht
ml

"The worm targets Apache Web server installations on a variety of Linux
systems, including those from Red Hat, SuSE, Debian, Mandrake and Slackware.
By exploiting a security hole in the Apache OpenSSL module that enables a
widely used encrypted communications service known as the secure socket
layer, the worm can copy itself to new servers. "
