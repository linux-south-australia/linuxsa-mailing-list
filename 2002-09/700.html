XFS finally in!

Ok, so it's on slashdot and you'll all read it there, but I'll still let you 
all know that XFS[1] has finally made it to the official linux kernel at 
version 2.5.36.

It seems that Dan was right last night, it was coming very soon :)

Woohoo![2]



[1] The journalled filesystem by SGI.  That brings the tally to 5 journelling 
filesystems (ext3, ReiserFS, JFS, JFFS[3] and now XFS) in the mainline kernel.
[2] Now I just want 2.6 to come out and be included as standard in mainline 
distros - don't want much really :)
[3] ...for flash devices mainly.
