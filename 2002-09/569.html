Stupid Computer Tricks - VNC in GotomyPC

Hi list,

Well, the boss is away this weekend and needs remote access to two 
applications on that runs on his computer in Windows, the other that 
runs off of my desktop in Linux. We're on a local network and he is not 
computer savy by any means. He needs to work on this from out of town as 
the job is due on Monday at 11:59 PM, and it is Saturday morning.

Oh yeah, and by default, SuSE Linux will only display the bash shell 
unless the vnc settings are modified.

Mission: Impossible

Strike the match....

First, I was able to change the behavior of vnc with the help of this 
guide. (Thanks to thelinuxgurus.org!)

http://www.thelinuxgurus.org/vnc.sht

Gotomypc http://www.gotomypc.com will act as the necessary conduit to 
allow two computers, with local ip numbers to share their desktops even 
though both computers have local ip numbers. Unfortunately only for 
Windows...but there were hints at Unix clients under development as well.

Anyway here is a snapshot of my stupid computer trick.

http://www.morton2001.org/VNCINGOTOMYPC.HTML
