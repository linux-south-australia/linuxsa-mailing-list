Re: Tar problems.

On Mon, Sep 09, 2002 at 12:16:07PM +0930, Adam Smith wrote:
> The script begins to tar the appropriate directories and seems to go
> fine until.. BAM.
> 
> tar: Error exit delayed from previous errors

Tar usually gives this error message once it's finished archiving the rest of the stuff. Loose translation: "Some bad stuff happened before, but it wasn't fatal". To find the error message run your script and redirect the output to a file, so that you can look at it later:

  tar -zcvf /wherever/file.tgz * > /tmp/tar.output.log 2>&1

This grabs the stuff sent to stderr as well as stdout (there's simpler syntax, but that's less portable). You can then look through tar.output.log and find out whereabouts the problem lies.
