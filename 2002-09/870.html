Thanks for the hints guys - no, the disk isn't full. as for the 
installation procedure, i (gasp) followed the instructions on the 
mandrake website relating to 3.0 
(http://www.linux-mandrake.com/en/mkd-kde3.0.php3) - that is, download 
all the RPMS and execute "urpmi *".

seemed to be a broken symlink (doing a 'ls -aFlh' in an xterm showed it 
with that flashing red background). i deleted this link and re-created 
it linking to the right .DCOP_* file.
