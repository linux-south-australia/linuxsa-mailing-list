On Thu, Sep 19, 2002 at 11:42:37AM +1000, Mcleod, Ian wrote:

> A self compiling, self updating firewall box would be *cool*

Assuming you trust the QA team that creates the updated packages. It might
cause a few hassles if it updated itself and installed a version of package X
that had an obscure bug in a kernel module somewhere that managed to bring
the system to it's knees at seemingly random times.

A lot of companies are starting to do this sort of thing internally. They do
the testing of packages on a development/test box (that's configured
similarly to the others) and when they're happy, the copy the package to a
central place and all of the other servers update themselves. It's not just
an Open Source thing either....
