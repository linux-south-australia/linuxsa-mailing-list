I think mbufs are FreeBSD's Network Memory Buffers, in which case the tuning man page may not provide any help (I'm assuming you've already checked that). I think some of the kernel parameters that affect the mbufs and mbufclusters are:

  * maxusers
  * NMBCLUSTERS

You could try reading up on those and recompiling your kernel to better suit your situation.

I've just found an article / paper that kind of says something similar:
