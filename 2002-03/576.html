Re: How can I kill the connection

Well, tty1 is your console. So you probably left it plugged in and
turned off or disconnected the screen? Do a:

 ps -ax |grep tty1 

and check the process number of that particular shell then kill it:

kill <process id>

That should do it.

Ant
