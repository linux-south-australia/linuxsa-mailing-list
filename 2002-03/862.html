Re: POP3 Delays on checking email (using Outlook etc)

>The major problem comes when checking your account for emails using POP3
>- I have noticed that all my other email accounts check for mail, login,
>download (if needed) and close the like almost instantly, my local linux
>box takes around 30 seconds to do this.
>
>I have read a few documents suggesting to change the /etc/sendmail.cf
>file's Timeout.identd to 0s (i.e disabling it) - well it was only set to
>5 seconds anyway - I have set it to 0 seconds, with no change.

Sounds like the POP3d is trying to do a reverse lookup on your DNS, and
timing out before continuing.

Either disable reverse lookups, or make an entry in your /etc/hosts for each
local IP (10.0.0.3?) that will be connecting to it.
