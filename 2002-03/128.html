telnet. Its the charsets. This is what Ive figured so far:

Linux can support 2 charsets at once - G0 and G1. This is a kernel
limitation. Im trying to change to G1 in a term, and use the old
extended charset that has chars that get used to make borders etc. EG
the "terminal" font in windows.

I can do things such as:

was it?) extended chars?

Am I barking up the wrong tree?
