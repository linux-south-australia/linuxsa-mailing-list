Just to follow up myself - I have been unable to get fetchmail to
co-operate.  I've hung out on "fetchmail-friends" to no avail. 
Fetchmail just can't do it AFAICS.

So instead I'm staying with a solution using both fetchmail and
procmail.  I suck my email from my ISP using fetchmail, which then hands
it to local delivery (sendmail).  Procmail gets a go automatically,
looks at the headers and puts things in /var/spool/mail for the
appropriate users with a very basic procmailrc.

My MUA then sucks using movemail.

Not perfect, but it works.  A cronjob to start ppp, fetchmail, then drop
the connection has me almost emulating UUCP.  Amazing how slow things
have moved over time.
