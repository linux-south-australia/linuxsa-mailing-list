FreeBSD -- 2 identical drives, different geometries...

Hi,

I have two identical drives (Seagate Barr IV 20G -- both detected as
ST320011A in BIOS), bought at the same time, detected with the same
geometry in BIOS, but when I use fdisk or disklabel on them in freebsd,
they show different geometries...

CHS numbers as reported by fdisk are:
/dev/ad0: 2434/255/63
/dev/ad2: 38792/16/63

and in BIOS, they are both 38792/255/63 ... although, the POST shows the
Prim Master as being LBA, and the Sec Master as being CHS... this is
depsite the modes both being set to "auto" in the bios, and both
detecting the same CHS geometry... And if I change the settings there
both to "LBA", FreeBSD still reports them as above...

Have I done something at some stage to make FreeBSD think the first has
different geometry? I don't recall doing anything ... And why is my BIOS
apparently treating these disks differently when set to Auto?
