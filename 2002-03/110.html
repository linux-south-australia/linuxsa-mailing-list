[JOB] Wanted: UN*X systems administrator

Guys,

My employers are looking for someone to fill the following position, 
please have a look if you are interested or feel free to pass it on to 
anyone you know who may be. Thanks!

--------------------------------------------
-- System Administrator Fulltime Position --
--------------------------------------------

Recall Design Pty Ltd has a permanent, fulltime vacancy for a junior to 
mid-level candidate with a minimum of 1-2 years commercial Unix system 
administration experience.

Duties include deploying new systems, supporting and maintaining 
existing company systems, software and network infrastructure. 
Additional tasks will cover improving current systems and practices to 
provide better services for staff and clients.

You must have the following skills:
----------------------------------
- Regularly use or maintain least one of: FreeBSD, Linux, Solaris.
- Familiar with at least two of: FreeBSD, Linux, Solaris.
- Programming:
   . sh/bash required,
   . Perl, C, Java, C++ and PHP are bonuses.
- Can setup and support Linux, Windows and Mac OS 9/X workstations.
- Must be proficient with TCP/IP over local area networks.
   Additional TCP/IP knowledge (IPSec, tunneling, etc) will
   be highly regarded.

We are looking for experience in the following areas:
--------------------------------------------------
- Standard Internet services (including DNS, SMTP, IMAP, HTTP, FTP)
- Workstation file serving (SMB, AppleshareIP, Appletalk, NFS)
- UN*X backup (Amanda)
- Cisco networking hardware
- UN*X directory services (LDAP, PAM, NSS)

We need a person with great communication skills, who is highly 
resourceful, committed systems administrator with a genuine interest in 
running and improving UN*X based systems.

Recall Design is an established, growing IT company that offers a 
challenging and enjoyable work environment. For more details visit our 
website at <http://www.recalldesign.com/>;
