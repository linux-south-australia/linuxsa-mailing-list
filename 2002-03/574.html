RE: Virtual Mail Domains w/ sendmail

>We will be shifting approximately 35,000 email accounts for our domain
>plus about 400 hosted domains with that number steadily
>rising.
>
>Another question, can sendmail handle this on one box or are we going to
>have to look at several boxes for this?

You may have been better starting this thread with these questions first!! 

I've seen low end boxes ( ie. Pentium 200 ) handling > 2000 mail accounts
with sendmail/pop3d. So you should be able to get a reasonable setup with
some decent hardware. As for whether it will handle 35k + users I really
can't answer. I suspect it might bog down a bit. In general I have found
qmail to run better with larger installations.

Your choice of MTA is a pretty complex thing, and should include other
things such as integration with open relay databases, virus checking,
integration with various webmail interfaces, and so on. For a large
installation as you are suggesting, you may even be better looking at some
of the commercial offerings around.
