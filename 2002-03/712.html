Re: More Linux and TV

Nearly all TV tuner cards are based on the brooktree 848 chipset, which
is well supported. Where I used to work sold that flyvideo card and some
other one and Im pretty sure they were bt848. Thats the question to ask,
and if the sales person doesnt know try to get a card to have a look at
to try and see for yourself.
