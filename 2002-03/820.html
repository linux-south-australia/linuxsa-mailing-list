Just found this

NOTE:
    The BIOS on some older Compaq machines reports 16M of DRAM when they
really have more. For 2.2 kernels that was overcome by appending "mem=64M"
(for example) to the kernel boot parameters. For 2.4 kernels the syntax has
changed such that you specify the size and address of the extra memory
rather than total size. So, "mem=64M" under 2.2 becomes "mem=48M@16M"
(assuming the BIOS reports 16M when you really have 64M).
