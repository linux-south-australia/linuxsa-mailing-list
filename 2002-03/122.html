[OT] xDSL IP address change

howdy all,

i'm interested to find out from people who use xDSL how often their IP
address changes. i'm investigating how practical it would be to
implement a login system (on a website) that checks the remote IP as
well as the other details to maintain a session. for dial-up users,
it'll be obvious to them if they've been disconnected from their ISP,
and they'll be forced to login again. but for DSL, i'm assuming any drop
in connection would be silent, and thus the session ending would be
unexplained (or at least non-obvious) to the user. we're talking about
non-computer-savvy clients here, generally. i'm thinking, if DSL IPs
tend to change once every 3 months or something, it'd be fine, but if
they change every few hours, then it'd be no good.

and for the linux-ness of the post, the website runs on linux/php/apache
;-)
