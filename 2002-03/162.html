Well although not strictly a bank, Savings & Loans gets top marks here
for me.  I've found ZERO problems with their Internet banking under
Netscape/Linux - there are a few wierd points (can't use menus so can
only check balances basically) when using Opera (my primary browser)
however.  Other than setting proxy settings, I've had to do nothing to
get it working from any platform I've tried (Linux, IRIX, Win32
various).  Quick as anything too...

The speed and usability under Linux was one of the major reasons for
switching from BankSA - a disaster zone on the Internet side.
