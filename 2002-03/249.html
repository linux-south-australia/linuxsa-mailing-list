Re: [OT] Hard Drive for Refurbished Computer

Like Ian, we at Computerbank Australia Inc refurbish donated hardware.

However, we actually donate these systems, with Linux (naturally) to 
recipients. As a non profit group, we are always after parts, whole systems, 
volunteers, any help we can get! 

At the moment, we are also experiencing a critical shortage of hard drives - 
with an ever growing waiting list of disadvantaged and low income applicants, 
we would be glad of 500mb or up!

At the moment, as part of the national body, we are preparing a submission to 
the NSW & Federal Gvts about receiving their outgoing hardware. We are up 
against Microsoft backed groups, who are offering 'low cost' systems, 
naturally with Microsoft included! it has become yet another Linux vs Goliath 
battle.....

For anyone interested about Computerbank Australia, visit:
http://www.linunix.com/cbsa/

Thanks,Romana:)
