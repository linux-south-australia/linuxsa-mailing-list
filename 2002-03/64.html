RE: [OT] Hard Drive Recommendations?

Depends what you want them for...

If quietness is an issue, then you may want to look at Seagate U6 or
Barracuda IV, and I think the latest Maxtor...

If you want ultimate speed, you probably want the 15K RPM U160 Seagates,
or if you want cheapness, pretty much any 5200RPM ATA66 drive would
do... 

IMHO, Glen's right -- it's more the individual model... but in most
situations, I think it has a lot more to do with luck (and heat and
power quality) than anything else... esp if you are buying one HDD... If
you buy squadrons of them, then other things are more important, as the
law of averages is more likely to apply to you (on average...)
