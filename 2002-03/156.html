[ANNOUNCE] March AUUG Meeting

Hi There!

The local chapter of AUUG (http://www.sa.auug.org.au/) is holding its
monthly meeting this Wednesday, 13th of January. Details are as follows:

Date:	Wednesday 13 January
Venue:	Marcellina's Pizza Bar (Hindley St, Adelaide)
Time:	7:00 PM

There will be short presentation about wireless networking by Andrew
Rutherford (or a replacement just in case he gets called overseas for
work) followed by a demonstration of an actual wireless network (1).
Some people have kindly offered to bring along spare wireless cards so
if you're lucky you may be able to participate in this demonstration!

Marcellina's is both licenced and able to cater for us so food won't be
a worry.

Hope to see you there!


David Lloyd
Secretary, AUUG-SA
