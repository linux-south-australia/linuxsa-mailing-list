RE: [OT] Cheap vid card

Try the "vesa" driver...

----
Section "Device"

	Identifier "yourdevice"
	Driver "vesa"
	Option "shadowfb"

EndSection
----

works with most cards (on XFree 4 -- I run this on RedHat 7.2, FreeBSD
4.4 and Solaris 8...)
