Re: Diskless workstations

On Tue, Mar 19, 2002 at 11:14:59PM +1030, David Fitch wrote:

> you mean dumb xterminals?  (I love the reinventing of buzzwords
> for technology that been around for decades).

"Thin Client" refers to the concept in general, not just X terminals.

> Personally I quite like SunRays for this type of thing, they work well
> but require a beefy server (depending on number of users of course) and
> network.

It really depends on what you're trying to do. The SunRays come with a
whole bunch of other neat-o stuff (like SmartCard support, for
example) that isn't necessarily required in all situations.

SunRays become more economical when you're buying lots (i.e., 100+).

Depending on the magnitude of the roll-out, you'll probably be better
off putting together some bits (or buying one of the pre-built
terminals, as displayed at InstallFest 2001) and building something
LTSP-ish[1].
