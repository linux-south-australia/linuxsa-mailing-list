Re: Community based wireless LAN in Adelaide - interest?

bjohns44@csc.com.au wrote:

> What would your recomendation be for people who cant access ADSL for
> improved bandwidth?  Especially in more remote areas like Virginia/Two
> Wells area?

If you would like DOV ISDN in two wells, and also have an analogue line
at the same location, you can choose the 'wide area calling plan' for
the analogue line, and switch call forwarding on when you want to dial
DOV to Adelaide. Voice calls forwarded by easycall under the wide area
calling plan are charged under the calling plan in the bills I've seen,
at 25c.
