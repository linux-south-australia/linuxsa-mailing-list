Re: DNS troubles

Thankyou to everyone that helped me with this. As it turns out, my
firewall was allowing incoming connections with a source UDP port of 53,
rather than as a destination. Security through obscurity isn't a
solution, but I'm glad that this wasn't an advertised fact... 

Lesson for the day: check thy firewall script. Here endeth the lesson.
