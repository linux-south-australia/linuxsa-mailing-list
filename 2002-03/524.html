[ANNOUNCE] LinuxSA March 2002 Meeting

Hi all,

Time for the LinuxSA March 2002 Meeting...

Here are the usual details:

  When:   7:00pm (doors open 6:45pm) on
          Tuesday, 19th March, 2002
  Where:  2nd Floor
          Compaq Computer
          139 Frome Street
          Adelaide SA
  Cost:   FREE
  Who:    Anyone and everyone.
          No pre-registration necessary.

Presentation:

  Geoffrey D. Bennett will be giving three mini-talks:
  - adding AI to Tetrinet (or: Having Fun with Free Software)
  - tweaking XFree86 modelines (or: Getting in Bed with David Lloyd)
  - backups without tapes (or: Preserving Purged Penguins)

Extras:

  As per usual, if you are having a problem with your Linux
  installation, feel free to bring your machine, but please warn
  organisers@linuxsa.org.au beforehand.
