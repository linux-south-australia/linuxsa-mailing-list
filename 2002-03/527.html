Re: lex

Hi James (and All),
    In answer to your question, you will probably find that
the GNU version of lex (called Flex) is already installed.
See David Lloyd's answer to your mail on how to check. The
next thing you will need to do is tell make to use flex
instead of lex. This is fairly straight forward, just change
the compile command. "man make" will tell you how to do
this, as the exact syntax of the command escapes me at
present, but it is under the section about using other
libraries. It should take you about 20 mins to read
everything and make the necessary changes, and you should
then be back on track. Hope this helped.
    Cheers!
        Andrew.
