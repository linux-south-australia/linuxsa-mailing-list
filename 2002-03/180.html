I currently bank with Australian Central, and while their net banking
facilities re good, and should run on Linux with no dramas (the current one
looks like straight HTML), unless you keep a lot of money in your account,
or have been a member for a very long time, their fees are horrific.  85c
for EFTPOS transactions, $1.85 for Australian Central ATM's, $2.85 for other
ATM's and 25c for Online bpay transactions is a but pricey for me.
