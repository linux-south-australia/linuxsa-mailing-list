DOWNLOADS
www.freshmeat.net
www.icewalkers.com
www.tucows.com
www.planetmirror.com
mirror.aarnet.edu.au

www.linuxiso.org (when it works)

INFORMATION
www.linuxdoc.org
www.redhat.com

SEARCHING
www.google.com/linux

NEWS
slashdot.org
newsforge.com
www.theregister.co.uk

I'm sure there's some more links of interest; perhaps there's a web site
somewhere with links to all these sorts of things.

The manuals that came with your copy of Linux (or the online versions on
www.redhat.com) will come in very handy.  It's usually expected that
you'll try to find the answer to your problem in the manuals or on the
web before asking on mailing lists such as this one, however the people
here are mostly friendly. :-)
