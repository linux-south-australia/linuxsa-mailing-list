Re: Laptop as X term - minimum configuration?

I do this on a toshiba satelite pro (pentium 166/ 80mb ram/ 2gb HDD),
but the bottleneck in my case is my 2mbit wireless card. It runs
evolution usably, but not what i'd call fast. Opera is faster running
locally than over the wireless. A faster wireless card would do wonders
I'd expect. Unfortulatly X seems to use more ram for the graphics than
the programs use themselves though, so second to a decent wireless card
you still need a decent amount of ram.
