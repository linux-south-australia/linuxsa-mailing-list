[OT] Alcatel SpeedTouch Home to Pro

Hi,
Found this tidbit whilst roaming the web.  It tells you how to turn your 
Alcatel SpeedTouch Home into a SpeedTouch Pro which will do NAT, Routing, 
basic (I mean BASIC) firewalling:

http://www.mail-archive.com/linux-biu@linux.org.il/msg00099.html

Also, it explains how to get into expert mode, which is interesting because 
it allows you to look at several things under "golden" like acheivable 
line rates, SNR etc:

eg for me:

golden>operational_data

Operational data report : near end (Downstream)
-----------------------------------------------

Attainable Atm rate       :  2688 kbit/sec
