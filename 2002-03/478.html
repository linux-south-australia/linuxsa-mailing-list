Re: Public Whinge

WinXP hasn't really improved..
I installed it on a new machine (all with fairly standard and
qualified-to-work-with-XP), and after a couple of reboots, hooked it up to a
network and ran MS Update to suck down the various patches.

Required a reboot.. Did so... Machine boots back up to a blank screen with a
polite dialog box saying it couldn't find msvcrt.dll.

Fixing the machine involved finding a linux boot disk that grokked the ms
filesystem, and searching for msvcrt.dll. 3 copies were found under the
windows subdirectory, although a wierd error came up trying to less one of
them. So, copied another one over the top. Rebooted back to windows, and it
was fine again.

NFI what happened there, but there are more stories about windows doing
wierd things than there are stories about UFO sightings.
It's probably the reason why you and I are subscribed to this list, instead
of gunning for our MSVP certificates on the iLoveW~1 mailling list ;)
