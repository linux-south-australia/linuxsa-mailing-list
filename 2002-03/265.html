[REMINDER] AUUG Meeting (Wireless Networks)

Hi There:

Just a friendly reminder that AUUG-SA is meeting at:

Date: Wednesday 13 March (i.e. this Wednesday)
Venue: Marcellina's Pizza Bar on Hindley St, Adelaide
Time: 7 PM

We'll have a talk about wireless networking and how to ensure the
security of your communications. A number of people have promised to
bring some equipment with wireless cards so hopefully we'll be able to
have a small "hand-on" demonstration as well. David Bullock (President)
has suggested that we spend some part of the meeting to kick start a
community based wireless network.

Marcellina's is licenced and we normally enjoy pizza or any of the other
dishes they can make around 7:45-8:00 PM.

Anyone can attend, however at some meetings we have promotions or
discounts only available to AUUG members :-)
