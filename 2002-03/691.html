Re: LDAP web gateway?

michael wrote:
> 
> Does anyone know of a trivial way to get an LDAP/Web gateway going? Most
> of the stuff on freshmeat seems to be too much rocket science for my
> liking.

LDAPExplorer <http://igloo.its.unimelb.edu.au/LDAPExplorer/>; is trivial 
to setup, and works nicely if you are looking for a basic, functional 
interface.

If you're looking for something pretty and polished, then you probably 
won't find a trivial solution because whatever you use will need to get 
customized for the schema and structure you are implementing.
