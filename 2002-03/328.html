Re: DCOP communication error / Kpanel (kicker) disappeared

Hi,

The usual fix for that is to rename the .kde directory in your home directory 
and delete the .DCOPxxx files in your home directory. You can also try 
deleting the contents of your /tmp directory and restarting kde.
