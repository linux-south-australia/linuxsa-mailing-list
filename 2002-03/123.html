Hi There,
    I seem to remember that many years ago Telix was THE
software to use when dialling up in dos to a BBS, and was
also run by most BBSs on the other end as well. I also seem
to remember that it was a copy of Minicom or a variation of
it ported to dos. (This may not be true but Minicom
certainly "feels" like Telix did.)

    Minicom should be available in most distributions of
Linux, and I seem to remember that you can set up the
terminal windows to most of the popular ones (ie ANSI,
VT100, etc). Give it a try and see how you go. Hope this
helps.
    Cheers!
        Andrew.
