Hi Sue

You need to include them like this

Section "Monitor"
        Identifier "Sony GDM-17E10"
        VendorName "Unknown"
        ModelName  "Unknown"
        HorizSync 30.0-82.0
        VertRefresh 50.0-150.0
        Option "dpms"
        Modeline "1152x900" 94 1152 1160 1260 1416 900 904 920 940
EndSection

Hope this helps
