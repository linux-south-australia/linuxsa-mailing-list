Re: iMacs

Hi.

For iMac linux compatability I'd be poking around the distro sites: Yellow
Dog, PPC Linux, Mandrake etc. You should be able to find compatibility
information there.

As far as value is concerned, I'd be expecting to pay well under $1k for
that box. We bought a few 6 month old iMacs late last year for $1300 each.

You're right about the US sites. Macs seem to depreciate a whole lot
faster in the states than here, you can't really use their prices as a
guide. The iMacs have had the same model numbers worldwide though, as far
as I've seen.
