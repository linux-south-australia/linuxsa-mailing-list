REDHAT-6.0

Hi All...... after my last msg I really did install the RH-6.0 off the
APC Pocketbook!!! :)

I am sure that I am not the only person to notice that 99% of all Linux
apps on the APC Mags & the APC CDWEEK thing, are all basically
Redhat-Friendly... it has really been pissing me off so decided to SEE
if this RH-6 delivered what it claimed on the cover..."UPDATED
REDHAT-6.0 with latest fixes & upgrades" [emphasis on FIXES!]

I am actually more than vaguely impressed ;)   Gnome is still ugly &
IMHO glitchy (and also highly favoured by Redhat given the RH stuff in
it- while there is stuffall in KDE-1.1.1) but overall, this RH-6.0
appears to work & I can certainly compile damn near anything (and have
been for hours now :) )  This RH-6.0 still has stuff in "the wrong
places" :) BUT I can live with that for now til I move stuff to where it
SHOULD be & symlink it so RH doesnt crash/die on me!  This RH-6.0 has
replaced the CALDERA on my Pentium-75 testbox .  At least now I can
compile stuff from APC mag disks without spending an hour figuring out
what it wants and where it thinks it should be, when its doing a
./configure !!!!!

OK...its just finished compiling SSH-2 and LICQ so off to "play" for a
while.

Cya, Phil
