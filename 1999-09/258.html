As a learning experience, setting up your own network at home is a good way
to get your head into things like TCP/IP, samba etc etc. However, you really
can't expect people to just give you a step by step run-down: you really
wouldn't pick much up that way, and we'd probably be inundated with mail from
you everytime something broke.

If you plan to do it yourself, then DIY. Otherwise, you might want to start
offering some $$$$$$ as what you're asking for is someone to set up a LAN
for you. This is not necessarily an elementary task.
