Telstra is protected against the nature of carried traffic by
the Telcommunications Act (search www.austlii.edu.au if you
need the section).

Oddly, non-telco ISPs have no legislative protection (read
that carefully: they made have judical protection, but there
is a lack of precedent, so you wouldn't want to stake too
much on it).
