Caldera Open Linux is slick

Hi,

I have just installed Caldera OpenLinux 2.2 off of the Linux PocketBook CD,
and apart from it hanging on one of my machines, it is very impressive.

Even lets you play Tetris during the install process while the rest of it
goes on in the background.

Much better installation process than that clunky Windows install process.

Even lets you try out X setups etc.

Of course, the Samba setup is very different :-(
