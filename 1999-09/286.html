I reckon that you should give the method out, as anyone who is really
interested in security would already be taking measures to prevent access to
the actual server. I can't remember who said it, but I remember hearing
something along the lines of "If someone can gain physical access to the
computer, then they will be able to get all the access they want."This is
fairly true (even with Linux it would seem!)
Giving the method out will probably serve to better illustrate how important
things like physical security are, as I think that many people just don't
realise how important it is to the overall security of a computer.
