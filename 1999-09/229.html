Re: how hacked

Just a thought - but if it had "no security set on the relay" then while
it could be used to relay emails, that is still a far cry from hacking
the machine.
How would you know the machine was being used for spamming, if you
couldn't access it at all, as dredd claims?
Pulling the network plug on the machine would have solved the problem of
spamming until you could call a professional to come and fix the
machine. I suppose some people wouldn't think of that though - not
everyone knows which plug IS the network (or modem) plug anyway. But
even failling that, why not just hit the power switch?

As a side note, someone might want to make a "Hacked-HOWTO" that
explains the first steps to do when you believe your machine has been
hacked into..
