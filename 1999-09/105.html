Re: Linux and Apple Computers (Comments)

Recently, perhaps it was 2/9/1999 11:40 PM +0930, David Lloyd wrote

>If anyone has had any experience working with Linux and an Apple
>computer, would it be possible to make any comments regarding speed,
>reliability, useability, portability or any other pertinent comments?

Fast, reliable, usable. Been ported to iMacs, PowerBooks and PCI Power 
Macs. Should be ported to iBooks (incl. wireless networking), and the new 
G4 "desktop supercomputers" some time or other.

See www.linuxppc.com for starters. There are several distros (search the 
list archives at lists.linuxppc.com).

Commercial apps are not as easy to find (as x86 Linux), although there 
have been commitments made by various developers to port to PPC.
