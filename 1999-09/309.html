Re: Squid

> I  am trying to set up my proxy server (squid) on a linux box, (Red Hat 5.2),
 to serve a few computers web access. I have got this working through the ISP s
enet, who automatically redirects requests on port 80 through their proxy serve
r. However, I need to get the set up to work through Camtech,  who dosn't autom
atically redirect. 
> However, I would still like to use squid, instead of providing full net acces
s to the client machines on the network. How can I tell squid to go though Camt
ech's cache?  

You need a line in your config something like:

    cache_peer proxy.camtech.net.au parent 80 3130 no-query

This is for 2.* squid only.  1.* is different
