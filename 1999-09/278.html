Re: Pusle dialing

On Fri, 3 Sep 1999, Adam Crawford wrote:
> I do bealive there is a service ... called MOLDS (modem online
> diagnostic service)

The details are a little different to what Adam said.  You need to be
registered to use MOLDS, which requires you only to ask for it and to come
up with a reason for needing it.  The obvious reason, "having trouble with
modems," is appropriate.  You are only registered for a brief time, I think
a month, and the results are displayed on your screen as they are collected.
Telstra give minimal assistance interpreting the data collected, which is
the weakest aspect to the system, but hardly surprising.  You all know that
Telstra only guarantee 2400bps on a voice line?  Note the careful use of
"bps", instead of "baud".
