My guesses would be that the internal quality of the PABX they are using
is really low. The PABX where I went to school was really bad, to the
point of not being able to hear the person you were talking to.

Another possible reason could be that if they are having to use pulse
dialling because of the exchange not the PABX, then the exchange might not
have the bandwidth, or the quality, of a modern digital exchange.
