Someone asked a question about network games

Someone had asked a question about Linux and online games on here
whether or not it could or would connect up to game sites. Well, I
found this handy little proggie! I will let its description call the
shots:

Tipxd is an IPX protocol tunneling daemon, running IPX over TCP/IP
between many hosts and unpacking them on the other side. This is
especially useful for people with masqueraded hosts who want to
communicate IPX traffic to others with masqueraded hosts. Now, why
would you ever want to do this, you ask? GAMES! GAMES! GAMES! 

 http://www.norritt.org/Projects/tipxd/ 

I really hope this answers their question on what can be used with
Linux and some of these online games...
