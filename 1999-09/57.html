Hello all,
Telstra have launched a trial of a new product called "Internet Call Waiting"
which is really a virtual second telephone line for Internet users. The
trial is free.

Unfortunately they are offering only a Windows95 client. They are taking
feedback though. If you'd like to see a Linux client for this product
then *now* is an excellent time to let Telstra know that you are out
there and that Linux is a genuine market for its products.

You can find information about the trial at:

    http://call.telstra.com.au/icw/VSLOverview.htm

The feedback form is available from the "For More Information" button at
the top of the page, or directly at:

    http://call.telstra.com.au/servlet/VSLFeedback

I know from personal experience that Telstra are beginning to notice Linux
and are in a position to be swayed if we can demonstrate demand.
