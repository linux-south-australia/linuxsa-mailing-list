 Hi...
 
 Yeh... I use chariot and have no probs at all.
 
 Turn on the debug option for pppd and the watch the log (tail -f
 /var/log/messages) to see what happens.
 
 The "DEBUG=" line is found in /etc/sysconfig/network-scripts/ifcfg-pppx...
 set it to =yes.
 (this is the case for RH5.2 and RH6 anyway)
