RE: Internal Modems

Really the only advantages of an external modem over internal "proper" modem
are

1) can be added/removed while PC up
2) can be reset whilst on
3) Heat Dissapation can be better
4) Generally Easier to configure

Disadvantages are

1) Extra Power Supply 
2) Extra room (hardly an issue ever)
