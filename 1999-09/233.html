Re: Pusle dialing

> 	Some of our dialup users when staying in Motels have to use pulse
> dialing to dial up the server.. They report poor performance and low access
> speeds. Does anyone know the technical detail behind this if so I would love
> to know the reasons for this..

Pulse (or better termed Decadic Dialing) is just an older way of getting
the number to the exchange.  Once the phone has connected, the dialing
technique is inconsequential.

I would probably say that the phone system they are using doesn't have
the bandwidth that is needed to connect at high speeds.  Some hotels have
special phone points for people wanting to use modems.

Does anybody know of a Telstra service where you connect with a modem and it
will tell you the quality of the telephone connection?
