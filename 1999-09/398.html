Re: permissions problem

Okay, for those of you who may be interested, I've solved the problem. 
Apparently the attributes (not the permissions) on the file were changed
so it couldn't be removed/etc by someone who managed to get into the
system.  

More Info:
the file's permissions weree changed to +i by a cracker and has now been
rectified.

chattr -i <filename> fixed the problem.

It's amazing what you can find out by doing some serious (can you wear
out a search engine?) on the web...
