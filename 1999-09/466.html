Internal Modems

UNASKED FOR ADVICE
------------------

I am no expert at linux but I am on virtually every mailing list in
Australia. I receive about 400 emails per day. Here is my observation:

1) If an external modem isn't working either the hardware is literally
faulty or something else is

2) If an internal modem isn't working it's normally the internal modem
at fault.

The advice? DO NOT EVEN THINK OF BUYING AN INTERNAL MODEM AND EXPECT TO
BE ABLE TO PUT IT IN A LINUX BOX AND MAKE IT WORK. The bandwidth that
I've seen taken up by this subject is quite amazing. This doesn't mean
that they don't work or they're not good, it simply means that they may
need a little more work to get them working.

DAVID L.
