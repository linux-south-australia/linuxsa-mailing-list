Re: Internal Modems

I must be "lucky phil" ;)

I have 6 internal modems in 4 linux & 2 freebsd boxes ...1 x 14k, 1 x
28k & the rest are 33k6. All worked perfectly "out of the box" with
linux & freebsd (altho I did rejumper all of them to io 0x2e8 & irq5 -
allows me to use the 2nd comport & for FreeBSD you MUST change the IRQ
for coms3 & 4 anyway!) All these internals were ISA!!!

In my experience, most (all???) pci internal modems are winmodems and
most (all???) ISA are NOT winmodems! I assume "they" do make 56K ISA
internal modems??? ;) My 56K is an external !
