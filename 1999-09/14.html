Re: Which box

Hi.... start small and safe if you are a newbie :)  use a non-critical
box...the dx266 is fine.... most of my linux boxes are dx266's with
between 16 & 32Mb ram & 500Mb to 4Gb hdd;s.

Main reason for suggesting the dedicated box is that you are quite
likely to screw things up while you learn..which just, at worst, means
reinstalling linux...as opposed to losing possibly valuable windoze 98
stuff and having to reinstall 98 plus a truckload of apps etc etc as
well!!!!

When you have a handle on linux & realise that its the way to go and/or
you feel comfortable attacking the big box..then just blank the 10Gb hdd
& install LINUX on the whole thing :))

Cheers, Phil
