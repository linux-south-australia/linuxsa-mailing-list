Presentation:

    Developing Software in Qantas Club Lounges by Richard Sharpe
    ==========================================
		or Why does Ethereal print those strange things?

	A significant portion of my contribuition to Ethereal has 
	been done in Qantas Club lounges (and on Qantas flights).

	This talk will look at what Ethereal is, what its functionality 
	is, and where it seems to be heading.  It will also try to answer 
	the question: Has any of that alcohol had any effect on the code?

	In addition, I will talk about recent work on automatically 
	generating decode routines for Ethereal.
