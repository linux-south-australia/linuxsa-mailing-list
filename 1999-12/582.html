Re: NIC help

At the risk of saying too much on this subject, I wanted to mention the
following.

An important issue when you have two NIC cards of the same type is the
order that they will come up in.

With PCI cards, I have observed that they will come up in slot order, with
the lowest slot being closest to the processor.,

In a PnP situation, with two ISA NIC cards of the same type, what order
will they come up in?
