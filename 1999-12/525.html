> > Ive had EPP working at up to 1.2Mb/s.

> Is the theory the same for serial and parallel just on different
> devices?

No... its not.
Serial COM ports are very slow, and theres not much that can be done to fix
that.
Printer Ports always had enough free lines (and partly used ones) that it
could be expanded and remain backwards compatible.
Youll find most newer printers and other devices that use the printer port
will support EPP and maybe ECP.
BTW ECP was designed to be used with multiple devices simultanously (it
includes a kinda address bus).
