Re: unix security !!!! BIG PROBLEMS- HELP

Um, without sounding too harsh.  You don't have a security policy,
do you?

Let's see:
 - you don't run ssh
 - you use the root password from somewhere other than the console
 - you don't run tripwire and can't tell what files have changed
 - you don't limit promiscuous use of the ethernet interface by
   using an ethernet switch 
 - you haven't recieved adequate training on security
 - you do no personal professional development
 - and you don't have a company privacy and security policy that
   would lead to the dismissal of you coworker

I'm not surpised that you have problems -- I am surprised
that they are not worse.

Considering that this is the Linux South Australia list, you
would be doing us all a great favour by letting us know what
company you work for.  Lack of a security policy and procedures
is usually indicative of a more widespread lack of capable
management.
