JOB NETWORK POSITIONS










  

    


    NETWORK AND SYSTEMS

    
ADMINISTRATOR

    

  








Service to Youth Council Incorporated (SYC) is seeking to appoint a highly motivated
professional to fill the position of Network and Systems Administrator.



The primary responsibility will be to ensure the availability and operations of a
real-time on-line system critical to business operations. 



You will install, implement, maintain and manage a Linux server with approximately 40
Windows 95 workstations across a WAN (5 separate sites). E-mail, intranet and internet
access together with a server/client Employment Services database represents our current
and planned systems architecture.



Persons with strong skills in the implementation and use of Linux interfacing with Windows
95 will be highly regarded. Good communication and problem solving skills, a strong
customer focus and the ability to work autonomously are essential requirements.



An attractive remuneration package will be negotiated with the successful candidate.
