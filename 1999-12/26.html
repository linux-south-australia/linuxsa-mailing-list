Re: 1st time modem configuration

Hi Nic,
it sounds like you have a win-modem. If your modem is plugged into a PCI
slot then you can be about 99% sure that it is one of these beasts.
I would recommend that you purchase an external modem. There are two
good reason for this, the first being it easier to upgrade modem as
nessacary and secondly if your phone line gets hit by lightning then you
whole computer wont get zapped.
