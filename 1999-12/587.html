Re: NIC help

On Thu, 30 Dec 1999, Richard Sharpe wrote:
> At the risk of saying too much on this subject, I wanted to mention the
> following.
 
> In a PnP situation, with two ISA NIC cards of the same type, what order
> will they come up in?

they are electronically enabled in the order they appear in /etc/isapnp.conf

they are loaded, in the order they appear in rc.modules

this generally means a script lying in /etc/modules/<version>/dot default (if
invoked at boot time)

because of a scripting bug it is necessary to state the paramaters in both
/etc/modules.conf (conf.modules) and
/etc/modules/<version>/options

the assignment of which is what, occurs in /etc/(conf.)modules.(conf)

the actual location of the /etc/modules/<version> depends on distribution and
whether or not you have done a (re)compile

this blurb^H^H^H^H^H applies only to 'at bootime'
