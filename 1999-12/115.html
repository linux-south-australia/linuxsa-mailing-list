Ethereal 0.7.9 and beyond

Just a short note to let you know that Ethereal 0.7.9 has been released
with even more protocol decodes, and it now decodes more of the SMB protocols.

The next version will contain a little tool, editcap, which can convert
captures from one format to another.  The number of output formats is
limited at the moment, but more will be added over time.  This tool is very
useful in those cases where the capture you have is not suppoted by the
tool you want to analyse the captured data with.
