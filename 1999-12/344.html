Re: ISP Recommendations

Alex,

I agree that SE Net is a good choice of ISP,
on the basis of both price and the fact that
they have a bunch of people there who
have good technical knowledge etc. etc.

But....  there are two sources which might be
useful for seeking an ISP in Adelaide: my own

http://www.topology.org/isp.html

collection of ISP links, and

http://www.cynosure.com.au/isp/

the Kim Davies list of ISPs.

Good though SE Net may be, it does not have
international reach. If you want to be on the
net anywhere in the world with a laptop, you
might prefer connect.com.au, which is the only
ISP I could find which could connect me by PPP
to a linux laptop throughout the world.
