Re: APC UPS

Geoff Russell wrote:

> Why waste a serial port? 


1. Certainty -- a tape drive can be powered down for any
   number of reasons, one being drive-initiated shut down
   from overheating due to a tape jam.

2. Obviousness -- it's not expected that turning off the tape
   drive shuts down the computer.

3. Resource usage -- a daemon blocked on waiting for
   DCD can be totally swapped out.

4. Price -- low speed serial ports are cheap, you can pick
   up a superceeded multi I/O card for $2 and jumper off all
   the unused functions.

5. Maintenance -- you can use standard software.
