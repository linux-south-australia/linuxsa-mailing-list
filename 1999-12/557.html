Re: NIC help

Hi,

At 08:42 PM 12/29/99 +1030, you wrote:
>RH 6.1
>
>I had to take a PCI NIC from my Linux box the other day and have now
>replaced it with an NE2000 ISA card.
>This is not detected - the message I get is 'ne2k-pci-c - No usable cards
>found!'
>How do I add a driver for the ISA card and set it up as eth0?

if [ `modprobe ne io=0x300` ] 
then
	echo Found NE2000 at 0x300 ...
elsif [ `modprobe ne io=0x280` ]
       echo Found NE2000 at 0x280
elsif [ `modprobe ne io=0x320` ]
	echo Found NE2000 at 0x320
elsif [ `modprobe ne io=0x340` ]
	echo Found NE2000 at 0x340
else
	echo You are hosed
fi

should do the job ...
