Re: Mirroring Config....

If you're talking about RAID style Mirroring, Just compile it into the
kernel. It's under block devices (in make xconfig or make menuconfig) and you
want to enable multiple device support. When you enable this option hit 'h'
for help and it will tell you where the RAID mini HOWTO is located. It will
also tell you that you need to get the raid-utils.
