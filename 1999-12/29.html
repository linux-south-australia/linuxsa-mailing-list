Re: Video card question

RH6.1 should come with a version of XFree-86 that is new enuf to include
support the NVidia TNT cards. Use XConfigurator to select any TNT card (CL
TNT, Diamond Viper V550, etc)

If you still cant get it working, go dowload the linux X drivers from
www.nvidia.com (try ftp://ftp1.detonator.nvidia.com/ first). Follow the
instructions.
