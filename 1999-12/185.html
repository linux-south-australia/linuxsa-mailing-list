Re: SEC: U Settings up RedHat 6.1 box as mail server for Windoze PC's

look at the sendmail FAQ pages.

if the ISP is queuing your mail - and not delivering it to a POP3
mailbox:

How do I get all my queued mail delivered to my Unix box from my ISP? 
http://www.sendmail.org/faq/section3.html#3.23

and you may find this one handy:
How can I batch remote mail to be sent using my ISP while delivering
local mail immediately? 
http://www.sendmail.org/faq/section4.html#4.12


if you are just collecting mail for some POP3 accounts, like
fred@isp.com.au
mary@isp.com.au

then probably fetchmail is what you want.

but if your users are sharing one account, like fred_and_mary@isp.com.au
then you have a rougher time ahead:

How can I add a header specifying the actual recipient when having
multiple users in a virtual domain go to a single mailbox? 
http://www.sendmail.org/faq/section3.html#3.29
