Re: Hardware Problem - Power supply

I would find a new power supply asap if I was you - AND I wouldnt turn
it on again -  in my experience there are not to many things that cause
a PSU to NOT turn on **EXCEPT** when the PSU is rooted! IF its shooting
out wrong voltages/spikes in the nanoseconds of life it now has, it can
still scramble your motherboard & things attached to it - presuming it
didnt do so when it first died! :(
Good Luck!!
