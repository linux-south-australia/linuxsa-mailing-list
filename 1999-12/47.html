Hi,

The ISC DHCPd has a similar problem, their problem and workaround is
documented on their web page at:
  http://www.isc.org/products/DHCP/v2-readme.html#5.1.3

You could try doing what they do, adding a static route to the broadcast
address pointing to your ethernet card, eg:

route add -host 192.168.0.255 dev eth0

This may not work, I have not tried it.
