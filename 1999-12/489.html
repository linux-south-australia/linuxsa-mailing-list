Argh!

I have had more $20 network cards DIE on me than I care to remember.
They simply stop working.

It is WELL worth it to spend an extra few $'s and buy a decent quality
network card such as a 3Com.

I am speaking from horrible, painful experience here.  Don't buy cheap
network cards unless you're willing to buy another.  And another.  And
another...

I no longer buy cheap no-brand hardware.. it just isn't worth the heart-
ache.

That's my 2c!
