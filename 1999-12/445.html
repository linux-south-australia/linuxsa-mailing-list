Hi, This is slightly off the topic but:

Why waste a serial port? Just find a device not plugged into the UPS
and write a program to watch it. When it is dead, send the users a
message and shutdown.

In my case I have an external tape drive which I open at 30 second
intervals and see if it is busy, offline, or turned off.  You could do
the same with any external device (zip drive, modem etc).
