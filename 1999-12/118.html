When I have installed Linux on my laptop (RedHat 5.x and 6.0), I have had a similar problem. Try running tail -f /var/log/messages
and insert the network card. This should show you what syslog is told the problem is. If you get a message about and unknown card,
you will need to edit the /etc/pcmcia/* (I think) files. The PCMCIA-HOWTO is a good place to find out more.....
