Re: KDE on an i486 (RFC)

Hi David.... yep, got 4 x 486dx2/66's running SuSE-6.2 & KDE - 2 of
those have only got 16Mb RAM, the other 2 have 24 & 32Mb. Runs fine with
16Mb... abt the only downer is that Netscrape takes ages to load up
otherwise everything else is fine - dont expect supersonic speed but its
more than functional!. have also got SuSE & KDE running on a 486dx40
with 12Mb RAM - a tad slow but quite functional.
Something worth noting - all the above boxes previously ran SUSE-5.3 &
the previous KDE version & it was very slow - the new KDE is a lot
quicker so best to run 1.1 rather than old version.

Cya, Phil
