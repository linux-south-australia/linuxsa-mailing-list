Re: second ethernet card not communicating

What are the IP addresses / Subnet masks? You might find that it is a
problem with subnetting / default gateways....

That is assuming there are no hardware problems with Cables and Hub ports
and the like.

Also check your route table for entries that aren't what they should be
(Unfortunately, apart for the TCP/IP Administration Handbook from O'Reilly,
I am unsure of the whereabouts of some GOOD documentation on this).
Although most times you will get a message about destination host / network
unreachable.
