Hi everyone!

wide range of OS flavours - but not, as yet, Linux.
Head honcho at http://www.elebbs.com (Maarten Bekers) says he is willng to
provide a port to Linux, but will need help from experienced Linux people.

If you would like to see the BBS in another flavour before offering your
services, I have most of them and will send your choice to you as an email
file attach on request.
