second ethernet card not communicating

I have a Pentium box with RedHat 6.1 loaded with 2 ethernet cards.  Both
cards are recognized and show up as configured with ifconfig.  Eth0
communicates on my external network just fine.  Eth1 can ping itself but is
unable to ping anything on the internal network.  In ifconfig the tx and rx
stats are 0 all the way across.  I can take eth1 up and down with ifup
(ifconfig).  I have changed eth1 cards with an identical card, I have
swapped cables and hubs with no success.  Any ideas??

eth0 is an Intel EtherExpress PCI 100 using eepro100.o for the driver.
RedHat used plug and play for IRQ and address.
eth1 is an Intel ISA EtherExpressPro10/10+ using the eepro.o driver.  I used
the DOS IRQ and Address settings for this device.
