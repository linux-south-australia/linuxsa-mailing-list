If you edit your /etc/inittab, you can delete the lines with mingetty on them
(these are the virtual consoles / login screens). However DON'T DO IT. I would
personally leave one enabled, so that you can still log in if the network dies.
Without any virtual consoles, it's a bit difficult to do stuff. You'd have to
hit Ctrl-Alt-Del and boot into Single User mode.
