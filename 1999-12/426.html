RH 5.2 perl no good.

Users of Redhat 5.2:

If you still have machines running this OS, and you
haven't upgraded your Perl yet, I think you should.

I've just wasted quite some time and stress trying to
install "majordomo" on a redhat 5.2 machine, and finally
found in an FAQ that the completely incomprehensible
messages our of "sendmail" etc. were caused by the
buggy version (5.00404) of Perl on RH 5.2.
I upgraded to 5.00503 and it all works fine now.

I had the same problem some months back with Perl/Tk.
I wasted huge gobs of time/stress/anguish on this, until
the Perl/Tk author told me that I should complain to redhat
about their buggy Perl. Once again, upgrading Perl
fixed the problem.

Cheers,
Alan Kennington.
