> Sometimes though, it will seem to switch on (ie the fans start, and the

> power supplies 5 volt rail) still fails to light, and nothing happens on
> the motherboard (the network card lights stay off etc)

Your thinking of an ATX	power supply  [switchable via a lead to M/B]
in a 486 I would bet  you have an AT power supply [switched by a 240v
line and switch , often at the front of the case].   Either way the PSU
is no good,  buy a new one  straight away [their not that expensive]
or as a temp measure rip one out of an unused 486,  386, etc you have
lying around. Just be carefull with the 240 line and switch.
