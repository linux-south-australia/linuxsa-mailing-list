Re: Email Service Monitoring...

man date.


date can do some funky sh*t, like:

date --date '3 days ago' +%d:%m:%Y
gives 12:12:1999

date +%s gives
945221140 (seconds since epoch)

so one solution could be to compare the seconds value of both dates....
