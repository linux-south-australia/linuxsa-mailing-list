Re: APC UPS

Well, there are some reasons. Our UPS lasts for a while, often longer
than a short blackout. By using the serial port to communicate with the
UPS, you can check remaining battery power, and hold off shutting down
until the battery is down to 10%, for instance.
Our UPS can signal that the battery in it needs replacing, or if it
fails a self-test, etc.

Still, the idea of checking, say, the tape drive, is a novel idea. Pity
if you were trying to do a tape backup though.

Toby
