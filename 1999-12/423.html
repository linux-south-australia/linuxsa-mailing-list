Thanks Re;New Linux Mandrake Setup

My Thanks to All Those that replied ,

I am sending this via my linux installation, for those that are
interested I solved the modem problem via a combination of the
suggestions proffered and a whole heap of time. I'll take another look
at the Sound Card later tonight.

Basically, I confirmed the Isa 56Kps Dynalink Modem was Hardware
Apparently this is true for all Rockwell ACF chipsets while the HCF
chipset is the dreaded WinModem.
I disabled the Plug and Pray, Set the Modem for Com3 IRQ 7 (as someone
pointed out irq isn't required for the printer) I disabled the printer
irq in W98 (no problems) to avoid conflicts there.
I cleared all the files I created with my earlier dabling, and used
setserial (/dev/ttyS2 irq 7) to set the serial and modemtool  to set the

link from /dev/modem to /dev/ttyS2, Changed to my user level
Loaded my ISP settings via Kppp and here I am using Netscape
Communicator.
Simple when you know How ;-)

Apart from the connection seeming a little slow everything is running
fine.

Thanks again     dcd   (David)
