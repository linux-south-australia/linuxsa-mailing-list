Re: APC UPS

If it's anything like the Sola UPS I've got here, the pin allocations are as
follows (for DB9 on the UPS

Pin1 Rx
Pin2 Tx
Pin3 N/Open, closes (to common) 15 seconds after UPS switches to battery
Pin4 Common sig gnd
Pin5 N/Open, closes (to common) on Low Battery condition
Pin6 P&P sense for Windoze9x
Pin7 Remote shutdown (Shuts UPS down 120 seconds after holding down to common
for 5 seconds)
Pin8 N/Closed, opens (from common) 15 seconds after switching to battery.
Pin9 Unused

Hope this is of interest or use.
