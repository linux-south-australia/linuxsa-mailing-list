RE: incorrectly reported RAM

> I had the exact same problem with one of my installations, although it
> never reported any more then 64Mb.
> 
> I got around it by inserting the line:
> 
>      append="mem=128M"
> 
> into my lilo.conf file.
> 
> I am assuming that the board I had had a buggy BIOS or something, as
> the BIOS reported 128Mb but Linux would only ever detect 64Mb.  The
> above line fixed the problem and it has been working fine for about 6
> months now.
> 
> Regards,
> Steve Tee.
