Unless support is compiled into the kernel this will do nothing.
Redhat, by default, has almost everything as a module, so you need
to tell it to load the module relevent to your card.

eg, on a machine with an ne2000 NIC
 modprobe ne  io=0x300 irq=10 eth1
ifconfig  eth1 203.123.345.678 netmask 255.255.255.0 up
route add -default gw <gateway IP>
