Re: ISP Recommendations

Matt,

About permanent modem connections, I looked into this
about a year ago. My results at that time are at:

http://www.topology.org/isp_perm.html

My findings then were that everybody offering a
permanent modem connection in Adelaide does so at
a _much_ higher price that Big Pond.
Therefore I had no choice but to get the BigPond
connection, and after 12 months, I'm still sure
I made the best choice -- particularly on price.

On the other hand, I guess that with BigPond,
you have to learn how to do everything yourself,
although their staff are very helpful (especially
the guy in Tasmania who runs BSD on his own
personal ISDN connection).

AT a very low MB/day, BigPond compares very well with
other permanent modem ISPs, but I can't comment on
high volume usage. Maybe there could be advantages
in other ISPs there.

Cheers,
Alan Kennington.
