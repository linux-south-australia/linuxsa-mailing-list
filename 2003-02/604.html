Sudheesh,

My guess is that when you compiled the kernel, you did not include
support for your network card.

Check the config under Network Device Selection.

Just quickly - if you want netfilter (iptables) you should be able to do
this with the stock Redhat 7.2 kernel (modprobe iptables), you just cant
have ipchains loaded at the same time - they are mutually exclusive.

M.
