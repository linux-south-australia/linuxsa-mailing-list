Microsoft, Bill Gates and Free Software

So,
Ever wondered why Bill Gates (and hence Microsoft) hate free software so 
much?

Basically it comes down to the first thing Bill Gates and Paul Allen wrote 
which was Basic for the Altair computer.  They wanted to sell and make money 
from it.  But, all the various people around the place copied it.  He wrote 
the following "open letter" in 1976: 

http://www.blinkenlights.com/classiccmp/gateswhine.html

If you're interested in a bit of the history read:

"Hackers: Heroes of the Computer Revolution" by Steven Levy.
