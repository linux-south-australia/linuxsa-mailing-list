On Monday, February 24, 2003, at 10:58 AM, David Drury wrote:
> It is my job to update the DNS, and I remember at one stage when 
> dealing with Digital Unix boxes, that you could use one name and it'd 
> load balance the hits to the service across both of them.

Round robin DNS - certainly can be done with bind.  Just add two (or 
more) A/CNAME records for the same domain.

i.e.:

irc.openrejects.net.    in      a       217.158.195.23
irc.openrejects.net.    in      a       62.30.120.26
irc.openrejects.net.    in      a       216.158.96.68
irc.openrejects.net.    in      a       212.126.148.7
