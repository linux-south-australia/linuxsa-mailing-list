[invitation] Air-Stream Wireless Open Day

Just a reminder that  Air-Stream Wireless Incorporated
(http://www.air-stream.org/) are holding their second open day.

  Date:    Saturday, February 22
  Time:    11:00am to 04:00pm
  Venue:   Park Side Primary School, Glen Osmond Rd, Parkside

A BBQ and drinks will be provided catering for meat eaters and
vegetarians as well. Lunch will be around 01:00 PM.

For those who don't know, Air-Stream's primary goal is to establish a
support structure for a community wireless network in South Australia.
In addition it is likely that Air-Stream will provide the backbone for
the distribution of all of the Community Software (Distribution)
Network's open source material.

The programme for the day will start with some general talk about
Air-Stream and wireless basics, followed by lunch and then some more
technical stuff.

Demonstrations of wireless applications, such as VOIP and gaming [eg
CounterStrike], will start from about 12:30 and run throughout the day.
