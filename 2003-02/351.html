Re: hacked

Do a file search on your machine for files with a creation date after a
known "non hacked" date, ie over the last 7 days.   You should be able to
pick which files have been modified, you will find ps, ls, top and a few
others are likely candidates.    Replace all of these off your RH7.1 CD.
Anything that you dont recognise do a search on google, it will help to
identify which r00t kit you were hit with.

Some pointers to keep you out of trouble in the future.... ;)

- Grab yourself the "Hardening a Linux Box" document off the CERT site.
- Learn how to configure tripwire
- Get a new packet filter script
- Switch off every daemon you dont need, especially things like http, ftp
etc... or at least ensure they are firewalled from the internet.
- Patch every service presented to the internet and keep on top of updates.

Cheers

Matthew
