linux.conf.au - call for helpers!

Hi LinuxSA,

At tonight's meeting (18/2) I made a plea for people willing to be
helpers for Australia's premier technical Linux conference to be held in
Adelaide in January 2004.

If you are willing to be pragmatic and volunteer to do stuff to help
out, you are most welcome to subscribe to our lca2004-helpers mailing
list at:

  http://www.netcraft.com.au/mailman/listinfo/lca2004-helpers

it's not going to work, and I get to die from a heart attack.  This is
your chance to be part of history, to help make this great event a
success in Adelaide in 2004!
