Jim,

I would consider SOPHOS, http://www.sophos.com

We have used it under linux a fiar bit, its fast and works well.

Keep in mind as far as I am aware there are no "realtime" scanners for
linux which scan files as they are open and closed - the operating
system does not support this as far as I am aware - you just have to
setup a cron job to run every now and again to scan the shares.
