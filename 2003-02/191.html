bigpond are spammers?

I've been gradually implementing a bunch of anti-spam measures,
the latest of which is some dns rbl's.  Unfortunately it
appears at least one of bigpond's MTAs is listed on 
spam.dnsrbl.net:

Feb  5 17:06:57 gw postfix/smtpd[18948]: reject: RCPT from
mta03ps.bigpond.com[144.135.25.135]: 554 Service unavailable;
[144.135.25.135] blocked using spam.dnsrbl.net;
from=<rnmscott@bigpond.com> to=<davidf@parachilna.com>
Feb  5 19:44:42 gw postfix/smtpd[20191]: reject: RCPT from
mta04ps.bigpond.com[144.135.25.136]: 554 Service unavailable;
[144.135.25.136] blocked using spam.dnsrbl.net;
from=<Daryl.Tester@iocane.com.au> to=<davidf@parachilna.com>

The dnsrbl mob say it's a "known/proven SPAM site".

For the moment I have removed spam.dnsrbl.net from the list of
rbl's to check, but what do other people do about this type
of thing?  Blocking all of bigpond is not really practical
(however much I might like to).
