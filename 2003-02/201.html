This seems to be the case - see man 3 getopt

Programs that parse arguments using getopt(3) are sometimes more
flexible than the man page suggests - the man page documents the
author's personal preference for the argument format, but the
alternative formats provided by getopt(3) also work.

I remember being intrigued by the lack of need for a - with ps on a
VAX 11/780 in 1987. Think it was running SysV.
