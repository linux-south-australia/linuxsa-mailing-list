Heya,

I posted a few weeks ago complaining about the quality of my sb live card
(emu10k1) in Linux compared to Windows.

I've just installed alsa 0.9rc6 on my debian installation, and it appears to
be running fine - only problem is that it doesn't seem to sound much
different.

High pitched noises hiss, low end bass sounds dodge.  I've tried
playing around with various volumes (main volume, pcm, wave), the pitch
controls, disabling the EQ in XMMS (I've tried both the OSS, ESD and ALSA
output plugins) and I just can't get it sounding as great as it does on
Windows.

Don't get me wrong, the sound's acceptable, its just nowhere as near as
clear, and the bass/treble is nowhere as near as defined as it is using the
latest Creative drivers under XP.

Anybody here got any clues?

I think I'll stick with ALSA in any case though - 0.9's far improved over
0.5, the mixer is crazy, check this screenshot:
