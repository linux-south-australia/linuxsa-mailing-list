filted to separate mailbox, checked periodically and usually
deleted.  Auto-replying is bad (IMO) and a waste of time, you
end up either replying to a non-existant address or a valid but
innocent address or worse still replying to the spammer and
therefore letting them know your address is valid.

However if I see it came from someone at an Australian ISP or
company/individual I send a complaint email to their ISP and
upstream provider and anyone else relevent I can find (but not
the spammer themselves).
