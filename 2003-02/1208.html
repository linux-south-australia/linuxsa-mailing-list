Fetchmail + Debian Sarge + Spamassassin + Procmail + Exim

I have the following setup:

 1) fetchmail gets the mail from gw [i.e. gateway]
 2) exim gets the mail from fetchmail
 3) exim delivers via procmail
 4) procmail filters it through spamassassin
 5) eventually mail gets appended to /var/spool/mail/lloy0076
 6) then I get it through uw-pop

..all the above, except (1) occurs on the localhost.

This morning, one of the above tools--I'd guess either procmail or
spamassassin--delivers the very top line of the mail in
/var/spool/mail/lloy0076 as:

rom: dfjakj;jdl;das

Naturally this causes my pop server to go "huh" and not get any mail.

Packages are:

ii  fetchmail      5.9.11-7       POP3, APOP, IMAP mail gatherer/forwarder
(cr ii  fetchmail-comm 5.9.11-7       POP3, APOP, IMAP mail
gatherer/forwarder (co ii  exim           3.36-3         An MTA (Mail
Transport Agent) ii  procmail       3.22-4         Versatile e-mail
processor.

..on Debian Sarge.

Anyone experienced this problem? It only seems to happen when I download a
_lot_ of e-mail at once.
