I'm not sure what exactly could be causing this, but you could try the 
following:

In your home dir:

mv .xinitrc xinitrc-backup
mv .xsession xsession-backup
mv .gnome gnome-backup

Do this from a command line boot (init 3) not from within X.

Go to run level 5 (or reboot) and see what happens.

(Note, this will toast some of your X preferences).
