"BAN PHON KHAM, Laos (AP) -- Villagers in this remote jungle hamlet have lived 
for years without electricity or telephones, relying on occasional visitors 
and a sluggish postal system for news of the outside world.

their way onto the Information Superhighway. 
Custom-built computers -- running on bicycle-powered generators -- will
