On Wed, 2003-02-05 at 11:35, David Lloyd wrote:
> 
> James,
> 
> > I'm trying to get my linux box to talk to a digital camera and a USB 
> > memory stick. For the memory stick the kernel seems to load the drivers 
> > OK but no /dev entries are created. I am using devfs. Is there something
> > 
> > I have to do to have the /dev entries created?
> 
> Maybe, and maybe not. It might appear as a SCSI emulated storage device.
> See whether anything appears on:
> 
>  * /dev/sd[a|b|c|d][1|2|3|4]

The commands:

cdrecord -scanbus
 and
dmesg
 and
lsusb

are very handy for finding out which devices (if any) are found. Poking
around in /proc is also a fantastic way to find out similar info.
