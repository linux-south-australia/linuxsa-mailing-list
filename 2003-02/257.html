Recall of APC Uninterrupted Power Supplies CS 350 & 500 VA

FYI
---


 APC have issued a recall on some of their power supplies manufactured
 between November 2000 and December 2002 due to overheating and
 potential fire hazard. If you are using any, please check them to see
 if they are the recalled units.

 The only models being recalled are Back-UPS CS350 & 500 CA with the
 first six characters of the serial number in the ranges shown below:

 Models: BK350EI and BK500EI
 First six characters of serial:
 	AB0048 thru AB0251
 	BB0104 thru BB0251
 	JB0125 thru JB0251
 Note: Any units with an "R" at the end of the serial numbers are not
 part of the recall.

 What to do: Stop using the device immediately and contact APC for
 replacement.

 Phone: 1800 652 725 or visit http://www.apc.com/rely
