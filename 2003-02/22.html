Re: More sound card fun...

Out of curiosity I conducted a test with the default win2k drivers and the 
alsa v0.9 drivers (the ver that comes with mandrake 9.0), and I honestly 
could not tell the difference. I played the same song at approximately the 
same volume under both systems, and I couldn't tell. I might get my friend 
who claims to have very good hearing to come have a listen...

The sound card I have is SB Live value, and it is connected to a logitech 
Z560. There are no tweaters on the Z560 so I guess high frequencies wouldnt 
be very hi-fi, but it has a 188W RMS subwoofer and the bass comes through 
beatifully under both systems... I must admit, the ALSA drivers sound better 
than the OSS ones. I may have to try the official live drivers (from the live 
cd) under 2k and see if there is a difference.
