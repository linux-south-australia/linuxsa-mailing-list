Re: Commentary: The Greatest Flaw In Linux

The greatest flaw with Linux is the same as it's greatest strength.... 
the open source nature of it. It can't be stolen but it can't be sold 
either. With computers as with everything else people don't really buy 
what they need or want they buy what someone tells them they need or 
want. Nobody is going to market Linux like Microsoft or even Apple. The 
people that are smart enough to find out for themselves probably 
allready have. Just look at Mac OS X.......... how many people run 
that??? It's all in the hard sell...

2.5 cents
Daniel.
