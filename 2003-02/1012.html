Re: Mandrake Linux

I too only recently used both Md and RH guis to install.
They were both appealing for different reasons.
The one point which I saw that killed it for 'noobies' was the section
about disk partitioning. I wouldn't touch it without the feature but MS
assume they will install on the complete drive and assume a ntfs/fatxx
partition with a DRIVE letter.

I'd hate to suggest RH/MD/(other) to someone and have it clobber their
partitions due to lack of knowledge on their part.
