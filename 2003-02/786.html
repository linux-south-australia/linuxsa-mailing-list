Re: Gentoo was: (Commentary: The Greatest Flaw In Linux)

>i am so fed up with distros, I'm going the other way (more work)
>to hopefully have less work.  I'm going gentoo.

Unless you have a seriously quick internet connection, a fairly grunty
processor, lots of spare time and *really* enjoy upgrading & having your
linux box broken then Gentoo is not for you.   Gentoo is not called a
"Bleeding Edge distro" for nothing.   When it is time to upgrade it will
want to upgrade *everything*, you will find it not trivial to stop it from
doing this either.

Spend the time getting familiar with Linux from Scratch, it is less
painless in the long run than Gentoo if you just want a stable box to use.
