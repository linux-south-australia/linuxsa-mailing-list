IIT Training will be holding their Hands On Linux course in a couple of
weeks time.  I attended this one a couple of years ago and found it quite
beneficial.  Although the course content was excellent, I probably gained
more from spending a week doing nothing but linux in the same room as a 
bunch of tech heads all doing the same.  That as well as having
Richard Sharpe as our lecturer kind of helped the learning process....

Details are on their website: http://www.iit-training.com.au

The other thing I would recommend is getting to know other people who run
Linux, this list is certainly a good place to start, as are many of the web
resources you'll find, Google is your friend.
