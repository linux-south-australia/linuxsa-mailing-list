Hi.

Given that the dictionary attack is one of the easiest, maybe you don't need a
password? I would advise your users to pick passwords that are not based on
dictionary words.

However, If you really want dictionary words as passwords, you can set them as
root on most systems (haven't tried RH 7.3) and you will get a warning, but it
will let you set them. [open a terminal, su to root, then passwd username and
follow the prompts]
