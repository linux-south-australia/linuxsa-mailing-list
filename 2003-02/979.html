URGENT : Special General Meeting of CAISA called

Over the last few months, as we have reported regularly on the lists, we have
been pursuing premises and WFTD funding. Now, after a lengthy meeting today to
discuss the final budgets, we would
like to call a Special General Meeting of CAISA to determine the
future given the number of heavy fiscal decisions we are faced with, and
realisations of the amount of member support and involvement that will be
required to guarantee a successful future for CAISA.

Without sufficient attendance and input, it is highly likely CAISA will be
forced not only to have to reappraise the viability of the WFTD and
premises we are trying to secure, but in all probability the future of CAISA
will need to be seriously looked at.

We will be negotiating to hold this meeting at The Hut in the upcoming
fortnight. Please contact either of us in the next few days as to suitable
dates and times, and we will simply the pick the most popular option
available.

We strongly urge ALL to make the effort to attend this meeting, as it is vital
to the future existence of CAISA.
