Re: Commentary: The Greatest Flaw In Linux

This is an interesting thread. I have been pondering why SO many corporate
people are gainst Linux and or open source. I face the problem at work when
I advocate investigating Mysql on linux (or even NT) I get glazed looks.
Openoffice gets the same reaction. I have yet to suggest anything which
will light managements eyes up. I tried the money thing but even still they
seem reluctant.

Digression:
I was going to seek some information about visting an existing site which
uses this stuff in production. Not some micky mouse setup but a real
commercial environment in a similar operation to us. ie large DB lots of
users and internally developed robust (not mine of course :P) GUI apps. As
yet I have fully compiled my thoughts on this but when the licensing of SQL
2k etc comes in at 15k it may make sense to look at different options.

Evan
