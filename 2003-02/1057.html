Digital or not

Anyone else care to comment on this?

http://slashdot.org/articles/03/02/23/1610250.shtml?tid=141

I guess those poor souls using solid state amps would have less to worry
about given that they're used to a nice flat sound, but my Jackson PS4
or my 7 string Ibanez running through my Marshall couldn't be
replicated.

There's a comment in there somewhere about the signal over the CAT 5
connection being less susceptible to interference, but I think I also
read that the sampling rate is only 48kHz, which is pretty poor. Yes, I
understand that 48kHz covers everything the human ear can pick up, but
what about the stuff that it can't pick up that affects the sound you're
listening to?
