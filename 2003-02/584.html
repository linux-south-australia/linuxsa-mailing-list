At 10:31 12/02/03 +1030, Don Collins wrote:
>Mike!
>When you land at the DOS prompt - type this in:
>
>   fdisk /mbr
>
>If fdisk is not on the boot floppy then try:
>
>    c:\windows\command\fdisk /mbr
>
>This will replace your GRUB MBR with a stock standard DOS/Windows one.  All
>fixed.
