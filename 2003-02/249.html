Re: Printing problems

Martin,

> Printing (I have an Epson 440, which RedHat found). I've installed CUPS
> but can't get openoffice or mozilla to print to it. CUPS test page
> works, and the RedHat test page works, but can't find any details on
> getting openoffice or mozilla to print. I am assuming it is a simple
> setting somewhere.

In Mozilla:

1) File->Print
 -> properties

In the CUPS setup you would have called your printer something. It's
normally just called "lp" or "lp0".

Change the line to:

lpr -Plp

..substitution lp for whatever it's actually called.

To test this from the command line:

% echo "TESTING" | lpr -Plp

..should print "TESTING". Use the same piece of paper or scrap paper ;-P
