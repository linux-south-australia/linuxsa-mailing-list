Re: Commentary: The Greatest Flaw In Linux

The resistance to Linux and/or Open Source that I've seen is based on  
two invalid assumptions:

1. You can't get anything decent for nothing
2. Any Operating System/Software without support is worthless.

In my experience I'd suggest that virtually the complete opposite can  
be the case.

Damian.
