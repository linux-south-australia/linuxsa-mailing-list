Re: change file permissions

On Friday, February 7, 2003, at 03:13 PM, Don Collins wrote:
>    chmod -R 755 *

That sets files and directories, so I usually do the following:

   find . -type d | xargs chmod 755
   find . -type f | xargs chmod 644

to set general access to files.
