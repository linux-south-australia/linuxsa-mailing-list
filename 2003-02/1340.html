"Matthew Western" <mwestern@affairs.net.au> wrote:

> we've been thought this before several times.  I'd be in favour of changing
> the default reply to go back to just the list.

I'm on lists that do just that.  Two of the biggest problems are:

1)  Automated replies go straight back to the list - out of
office attendants and braindead spam/virus blockers.
2)  Mail loops caused by point 1.

One such mail loop resulted in over a hundred emails during
the course of a day before the list administrators could be
contacted to stop it (if it wasn't such a high value list for me
personally I would unsubscribe from it, because I don't like
the practice).  It's a bad idea.
