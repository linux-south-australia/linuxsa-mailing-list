In this case, fa is my gateway, then the next hop is the other side of
my modem at my provider. I suspect in your case you'll only get 1 or no
hops as you probably have a local problem, but you can use this as an
indication of where the fault lies. If its your provider, you should
call them, and if its beyond that then its usually a larger scale
problem where theres not much more you can do, and your probably not the
only one who's lost services. Check news sites and verify this. If you
dont even get to your provider, check your local network configuration.
