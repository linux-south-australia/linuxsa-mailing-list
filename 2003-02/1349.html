Re: to reply personally or not...

> we've been thought this before several times.  I'd be in favour of
> changing the default reply to go back to just the list.

I think it really does depend on the purpose of the list. I am on a couple
of role-play lists (I even run a listserv for them on my own machine)
where the reply-to is set to the list address. Very rarely in that
situation do you need to just reply to just one person. Everything goes to
the list so it makes sense to make that as simple as possible. I think
I've only had to reply to one person perhaps a handful of times in the
last 12 months.

However, LinuxSA, being a question-and-answer type list, the number of
times you just want to reply to the poster is much higher. In that
situation it makes sense to make it easier to do the reverse.

My 2c
