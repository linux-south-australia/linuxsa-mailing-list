RE: fetchmail setup problem

Smells as if fetchmail is trying to deliver mail to your localhost's
mailserver on port 25. Perhaps you need to set fetchmail up to deliver
to a mailbox rather than a SMTP server?

note: I've rarely ever used fetchmail, didn't do any research, and
haven't tested this at all. It could be a complete red herring. I
suspect, however, that I'm right. ;)
