Drain and flush the fuel system with clean fuel, change the oil, make
sure there's no corrosion inside the bore, give it a dribble of oil
into the pot before starting it.. oh yeah make sure it's not full of
spiders.

If it won't start make sure you've got good compression and check that
the injector is giving you a good spray pattern.
