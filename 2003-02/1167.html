Why?

I've been very impressed with the SA Housing Trust developments I've seen
of late.  The town houses at Walkerville, near the start of the O-Bahn
track, seem quite pretty and are certainly situated in a very nice
area; and the flats near the Kensington pool, while perhaps a little
too gaudy for my taste, are also very well positioned.  I consider it
most undesirable to place public housing in ghettos, and I applaud the
Housing Trust for building in good locations.
