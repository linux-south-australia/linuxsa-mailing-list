The Debian stable pcmcia was very out of date so don't expect easy answers 
there.  I think Dan got a very new pcmcia from sourceforge (sf.net) and compiled 
it up himself.  Not trivial.  RH7.2 will be out in the not too distant future - 
wait for that :-)

Or have a look in /usr/share/doc/kernel-pcmcia-cs-2.2.16/SUPPORTED.CARDS or 
similar (whatever version number for your kernel) to see what is actually 
supported on your system.  According to this file on a random RH machine I just 
telnet'ted to, the following cards aren't supported in this version of pcmcia:
  Xircom CEM II Ethernet/Modem
  Xircom CE-10BT Ethernet [ but try xircce_cs contrib driver ]
  Xircom CBE-10/100 CardBus

Of course like I said, Dan got his card working, so try contacting him and see 
if he has any ideas.  If you get it working please post back here for the rest 
of us.
