FreeBSD and VIA Chipsets (was: Linux and VIA Chipsets)

On Wednesday,  3 October 2001 at 17:44:30 +0930, Dale Long wrote:
> What experiences have people had with running Linux on VIA chipset
> motherboards? In particular, the VIA 694X & 686B chips, and kernel
> 2.2 and kernel 2.4.

Well, I haven't had any :-) But there was a problem reported with the
686B recently, and a patch to the FreeBSD IDE driver has been released
as a result.  It seems that there were data integrity problems running
both more than one IDE controller at a time.  I'm hazy on details, but
the indication was that the bug was generally fixed in the BIOS.  I
have a box here, and I'm going to try to reproduce the problem in my
copious spare time, and to confirm that the patch fixes it.
