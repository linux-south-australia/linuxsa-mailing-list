Re: Dial In Servers

On 02 Oct 2001 11:17:18 +0930, Richard Russell wrote:

> yeah, so that sets up one link between two computers. He's still got
> his LAN and their LAN to worry about. Sure, a Dial in server would
> normally allow him to see their LAN, but not from his LAN unless he
> sets up routing between then, and even then, he needs to be carefull
> not to duplicate IPs. So no matter what, he still needs to think about
> how he wants them to talk once he's got the PPP connection.

Choose a different subnet. Use 172.16.0.0/30 for the dialin link, then
either add a default route (assuming this box doesn't have any other
connections) or a network route for the other LAN.

I fail to see what's so difficult about it.
