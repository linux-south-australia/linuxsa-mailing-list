RE: Linux and VIA Chipsets

I have had no problems running linux on a VIA chipset however I had a
similar problem to Brian in that when I upgraded to a TNT2 M64, my Aureal
soundcard ceased to work. This was not an issue with my old 3dfx card. After
playing with it for a while inlcuding BIOS updates and driver updates, I
replaced the board with an MSI with an Intel chipset and everything works
beautifully.

I would be surprised if you cant get an Intel based board in Adelaide as
there are plenty around and even if you cant get one in stock, most
suppliers should be able to order you one.
