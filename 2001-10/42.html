What version of Samba? If it is RH7.1, then it is probably Samba 2.0.7-xx.


> Even using SWAT offers no help, even though it looks much easier to use and
> understand.


Well, that is because SWAT is a pretty-boy front end to the smb.conf 
file, and it does not offer any help with debugging problems.

>             My experience is with RedHat 7.1, Mandrake 8.0 and 8.1 on a
> mixture of Windows 98SE, 2000, and XP machines.


The guest account setup is very important here. All my recent experience is with Samba 2.2.x and Win95 and Win2K, and it all works fine.


However, if you want to send me a packet trace of when the problems are 
occurring, I can look at them and suggest solutions.

To take a packet trace that will be useful, do:


tcpdump -i eth0 -s 1500 -w smb.cap

And then, after the problem has occurred on the PC, type ^C on the 
console where you are taking the trace, and send it to me.
