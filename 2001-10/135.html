RE: Linux and VIA Chipsets

We use mostly VIA chipset motherboards, we will normally use the VIA chipset
in about 5 to 10 systems each week. We use the 694D chipset boards often in
entry level servers which require dual processors as the Intel 815 only
allows 512MB ram, whereas the VIA is a little more flexible. We have had
troubles with the Epox 8KTA3's and 8KTA3L's but I'd but that down to Epox
having bad AMD mainboards, rather than the VIA chipset.
