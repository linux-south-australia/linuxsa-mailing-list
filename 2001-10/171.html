> > probably be via a /dev/ide/host0/bus0/target1/ kind of location.

Well, the idea is that the you get a heaps specific location depending
on bus, controller and all that, then you set up devfsd.conf with
something like:

LOOKUP          ^cdrom$         CFUNCTION GLOBAL symlink cdroms/cdrom0
$devpath
REGISTER        ^cdroms/cdrom0$ CFUNCTION GLOBAL symlink cdroms/cdrom0
cdrom
UNREGISTER      ^cdroms/cdrom0$ CFUNCTION GLOBAL unlink cdrom

Which creates your normal devices in the normal places.

It also has some degree of backward compatibility.

I needed to access my dvd as a raw device because I was dropping frames
in xine, but the docs were all for a normal device. So I decided just to
try and mknod the devices as per normal, which worked. Then I rebooted
adn they were still there. I was expecting it to fail, then I was going
to RTFM but I didnt need to in the end ;)
