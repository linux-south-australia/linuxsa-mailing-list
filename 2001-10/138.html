This machine is loving life as an email virus scanner. Its on a 2 mbit
link soon to be upgraded to a 1gbit, but having 2 processors seems to
really help when some tasks (like generating reports). The cron job 
starts hammering one processor greping through huge gzipped logs, the
other can still handle the scanning fine. As for stability, that depends
what your doing. We plugged this box in, and it worked 1st try, and has
never has a problem since (about 1 month ago). Its scanning about 20,000
emails per week. (mandrake 8.0, amavisd, mcaffee, kernel 2.4.9). I'd say
stability under MP depends on your apps, so I wouldnt write off an MP
server out of principal.
