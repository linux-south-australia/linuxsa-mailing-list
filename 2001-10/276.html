custom firewall distributions

Hi.


This week I came across a Linux distribution known as SmoothWall GPL.  
It looks like this distribution was mentioned on-list earlier this 
year, but I thought I'd bring it to your attention.

It seems to be a very good firewall distribution, and comes 
"out-of-the-box" with support for IP masquerading, firewalling, VPN,  
proxying, dial-on-demand, dynamic DNS (such as dyndns.org) and supports 
most standard devices such as modem, ISDN, and ADSL.

After a very rapid base installation, further configuration is 
primarily done using a web-based frontend, which is very impressive.  

I am using this at home as an internet-sharing device at home, and it 
seems to be very good at doing this.

The entire distribution is a 20 MB download from
<URL:http://www.smoothwall.org/>;.
