Hi All

Well after a lot of fun, I worked out that the version of fdisk that comes
the current stable Debian does not understand drives larger than 8Gig :(

So I used a program called cfdisk which had no problems.

So now I have a 75Gig drive doing 34M/sec in hdparm test:)
