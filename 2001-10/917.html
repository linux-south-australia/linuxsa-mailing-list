Speaking of Solaris

Is anyone interested in starting a Solaris Users Group? The main
reason for this is to have a legal "organisation" within which to
distribute Solaris binary CDs...

refer to:

http://www.sun.com/software/solaris/binaries/faq.html#9

and

http://www.sun.com/software/solaris/binaries/faq.html#10

for why we can't just distribute it anyway...

I would suggest that "you can use the Solaris 8 software anywhere
inside your organization" should be interpreted as anyone who joins an
incorporated club... if anyone has an informed opinion as to why this
is not the case, please feel free to speak up.

I would suggest a ~$20/yr or something like that fee per person, which
would cover the intel and SPARC binary media kits every few months,
and duplication costs... as well as the cost of maintaining a record
of every machine it's installed on, to keep us legal...

Could anyone interested in joining this rumoured club drop me an
off-line email? ta...
