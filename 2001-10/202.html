Re: My SAMBA Quest...

Richard,

OK, here is my smb.conf file. The computer come up in NN today.

Theory:

My problems over the past few months seemed to originate from the
Bastille Firewall I configured. It was blocking the relevent NetBIOS
ports, and there doesn't appear to be an easy way of getting SAMBA
through it. Where are the IPTables settings stored again?

(My) Solution:
I eventually come across FireStarter (URL:
http://firestarter.sourceforge.net/ ), that does the job of a firewall
configurator. Its a a script generator for IPTables, and is very
understandable.

Cheers,
Joseph Tan
