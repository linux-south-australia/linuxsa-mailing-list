Re: Help with emacs binding

> I was wondering if anyone could tell me how I could bind various emacs
> shortcuts to a new key. For example I would like to change the "Mx goto-line
> XXX" to a more manageable shortcut :)

Put the following in your $HOME/.emacs

  (global-set-key <key binding> 'goto-line)

where <key binding> is replaced with the key binding you want.  Eg

  (global-set-key "\C-xg" 'goto-line)

will bind to Control-x g.  Or

 (global-set-key "\M-g" 'goto-line)

will bind to ESC-g or ALT-g.  Both will prompt you for a line number
after you run them.
