Re: [OT] Should I be worried...

> > but don't you really want the so called "high speed" serial ports
> > for such an application?  (16550 or whatever?)
> > didn't they not come until the first pentiums?

We still use 'high speed' 16450 UARTS in some applications. There wasn't
anything wrong with the 16450, but the PC architecture was/is such that
it required too many interupts to be generated to transmit data. By
having the slower chips on a better system that standard XT com ports
(ie: a decent multiport serial card) they could quite happily run to
57600.
