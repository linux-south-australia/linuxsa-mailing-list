Re: bsd v gpl licensing

Daryl Tester wrote:
> 
> And that's the issue that I have with Microsoft doing this - they
> publically abuse the OpenSource system while benefitting from it
> _AND_ doing their damnest not to acknowledge the fact.

You've missed Mark's point about the differing mindsets.

The BSD license comes from a DARPA-funded project at a
university.  The developers were doing PhDs so the
release of the source code fit the academic tradition
of making PhD theses available to the public.

The commercialisation of the code wasn't an issue.  DARPA,
the sponsors of major parts of the project, would see
commercialisation of the research as a desirable outcome.

This mindset is miles away from the issues that occupied
Richard Stallman at the start of the GNU project.
