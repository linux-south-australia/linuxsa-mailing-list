DDC stands for "Display Data Channel" and is the mechinism for a screen
to tell the video card/X what it is capable off. There are (too many)
things out there that support it, but misreport there features so that X
thinks all your mode lines are invalid, deletes them, then fails to
start X due to no valid modes. I get this with my projector :(
