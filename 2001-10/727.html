STM Laptop bags

Just a quick note that might interest those of us who lug laptops to and
from work each day.

I saw an article in the "Computers" section of the Sunday Mail (or was
it the Saturday Advertiser? I don't remember) talking about these STM
bags. According to the article, they're pretty ergonomic as well as not
looking like a laptop bag.

I went down to NextByte on Glen Osmond Road. I liked them so much, I
bought one.

It seems to hold everything nicely. It looks like your average backpack,
except with a heap of really cumfy padding everywhere.

The one I purchased looks like
http://www.standardtm.com.au/images/sports_sand.jpg

The website for the (Australian) company that makes these bags is
http://www.standardtm.com.au/

At $85-00, they're not too bad (when compared to other laptop bags).
