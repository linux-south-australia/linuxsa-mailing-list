Yeah, we get that too.

It's just some multicast packets being dropped by your firewall. I don't
know about the source of them, but i think they might actually be from
hellstra, not someone else's machine. Maybe.

I reccommend you set your iptables to DROP the packets, rather than REJECT,
since REJECT sends back an ICMP packet, thus creating traffic.

I don't know what the multicast packets are for, but it doesn't hurt to
ignore them ;)

Does anyone else out there know?
