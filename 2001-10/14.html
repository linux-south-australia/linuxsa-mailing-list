For the curious, Microsoft's original XP announcement email went out in 
at least two batches of around 1000 addresses each, with all of the 
addresses in the header of each messages. There may only be two batches, 
as I've recieved several copies of each list from various people, but if 
there are more, I'm happy to add them to the list. (-:

The email versions were sent out slowly, personalised, and with a 
different message ID on each message.

     http://cyberknights.com.au/MS-OEM-list-email.html

Before we get the distro flamewars cooking, I chose to promote Mandrake 
NOT necessarily because I think it is the best distro ever (for what?) 
but for the following very specific reasons:

   * It was released at just the right time

   * It gets rave reviews in the press for ease-of-use

   * At least one person has claimed that XP derived some features from
     it

   * It is shipped installed by a major manufacturer (HP)

   * I use it often and so can answer sticky questions about it
