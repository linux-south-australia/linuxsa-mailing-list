Welcome,

When your installation locks up press Alt F1 and read the text there 
preferably the last few lines and send us a copy of anything there that may 
be of consequence ie error loading, fatal etc. Do the same for the other key 
combinations that David mentioned.

On a side issue if a message is sent in HTML it's not always understood by 
everyones email programs and therefore doesn't get read. HTML email is also 
atleast twice as large as text messages. I'm not sure where in Outlook you 
select plain text for the message type but it is there somewhere.

Good Luck

Robyn
