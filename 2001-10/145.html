Actually the LS-120 FDD is an IDE device, and as a result appears to 
linux as:
/dev/hdX - IDE device X (where is is: a b c d e f g (depending on #of 
controllers).

fdX is only applicable for devices sitting on the floppy controller 
(720K-2.88MB devices).
