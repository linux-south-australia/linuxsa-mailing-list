XFree86 + TekTronix 119 3916 00 + Titan Graphic Reactor

Requires:

XFree86 4.0.X (preferable 4.0.3 but it works with 4.0.1)

And the following under monitor:

Section "Monitor"
   Identifier "My Monitor"
   VendorName "Unknown"
   ModelName  "Unknown"  
   HorizSync 64.3
   VertRefresh 50-90
   Gamma 2 2 2
   Option "dpms"
   
Modeline "640x480" 55 640 664 776 856 480 503 509 1066 -hsync -vsync
Modeline "800x600" 118 800 1136 1360 1840 600 813 816 1053 -hsync -vsync
Modeline "1024x768" 118 1024 1240 1448 1840 768 894 897 1054 -hsync
-vsync
Modeline "1152x864" 118 1152 1320 1528 1840 864 939 942 1054 -hsync
-vsync
Modeline "1280x1024" 108.00   1280 1284 1396 1696   1024 1025 1028 1066
+hsync +vsync

EndSection

The last line was made by xvidtune because my monitor's settings are
quite out and I needed to shift the image to the right. All of these
won't crash X and the rest of the system.

The gamma is because my monitor is old and needs a bit of highlighting.
Other than this, the quality of the colour is very good.

You also need to have the following:

Section "Device"
   Identifier "GLINT"
   Driver "glint"
   BoardName "Unknown"
   option "NoDDC"
EndSection

I don't know what the NoDDC means but it is apparently required.
