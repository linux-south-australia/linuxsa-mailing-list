Re: Mandrake 8.1

Mandrake 8.1 is running with DevFS

DevFS is cool.. its the "new" way to handle /dev/ entries.

When you load a driver for something, the appropriate /dev/ entries are
created. When that driver is removed, the entries disappear.

You could previously access your LS-120 on /dev/hdb, which was the old dev
entry for 'entire disk on the primary slave'

IDE devices can now be found under /dev/ide/

Since you are trying to mount a LS-120, I suspect there are two ways to
access it.
One, will be via a specific /dev/ls120-specific-name and the other would
probably be via a /dev/ide/host0/bus0/target1/ kind of location.

Give that a whirl perhaps?

-Toby
