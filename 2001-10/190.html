Re: AMD Optimised Compiler for Linux

Davis,

I'm not a compiler expert, but looking at multimedia players like Xine
and MPlayer on Linux, it would appear that GCC 2.96 and 3.01 support the
Athlon's 3DNow, 3DNow+ extensions. Otherwise it's just a standard x686
CPU.

While on the topic, it would appear that the Athlons do have a slight
performance lead over Pentium IIIs under Linux just as under Windows. I
get ~2000 Bogomips on my cheap-arse 1GHz overclocked Duron PC-133 gaming
box which is about the same as the Pentium 1Ghz Coppermine PC-133
machine. A full Athlon would edge the PIII out, but I haven't had a
chance to play with the new Pentium III "~Tualatines" yet (these ship
with double the cache over the Coppermines).

Cheers,
Joseph Tan
