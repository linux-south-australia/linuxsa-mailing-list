Jacky,

Why are you typing "emacs | emacs" in the first place?  This directs the output
from the first emacs into the second one, which should hang the second
instance, but let you use the first one.  You use the pipe to direct output
from one program to another, like "ls -al | lp".  If you are trying to open two
copies of emacs at once, you should use "emacs & emacs" (not "emacs && emacs"
as this will run the two after each other).

Cheers,

Jarrod.
