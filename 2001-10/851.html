>can anyone recommend a good ftpd? one without known current

i've found proftp to be fairly easy to setup/configure

it's the default ftp daemon in Mandrake, as opposed to wu-ftpd which is 
fairly notorious for holes

the config is modeled on Apache with the html-style parameters, so if 
you're in any way familiar with httpd.conf, proftpd.conf will be pretty
straightforward, its quite well documented also
