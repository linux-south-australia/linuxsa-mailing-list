Mandrake 8.1

First, a little plug - I rang Linux Systems Labs in Melbourne about
lunch time on Monday and ordered a 4-CD set of Mandrake 8.1 for
delivery by post in Adelaide.  I was very pleasantly surprised to find
it on my front doorstep when I got up at 8am on Wednesday.

Now for my problem.  Installation went without a hitch as far as I
could tell and I spent an hour or so playing around before settling
down to restore my home directory from a tar backup on floppy disc. 
Here the trouble started - I could not mount the floppy drive.  I
tried to mount it via the icon on the desktop and with the mount
command from a terminal (mount -t auto /dev/hdb /mnt/floppy),
with the same message both times:
	Could not mount device.
	The reported error was:
	mount: special device /dev/hdb does not exist.

I looked through various files in the /var/log directory and have
appended parts of what might be relevant below.  Can somebody please
point me in the right direction to fix this (for the time being, I
