RE: WTB Low-end Pentium

Hi Glen,

Try Evans & Clark Auctions http://www.evansclarke.com.au/. I regularly pick
up complete Pentium boxes for under $150 incl. Monitor. The latest one was a
Fujitsu Pentium 166 MHZ incl. 15'' Monitor for $90. They make great little
Linux servers and routers. Hang around till the end. Most people would have
left by now and you are applying the laws of supply and demand. The
auctioneers are prepared go really low on their price. They have order to
dispose the gear.

Tip: check all HW before you start bidding for it. There is no refund and of
course no warranty.

However, you can pick up good gear for a very good price. You got to know
what you are bidding for! Arrive at least one hour ahead of auction time and
inspect ALL gear you are interest bidding for.

Cheers,

Stefan.
