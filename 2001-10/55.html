On Tue, Oct 02, 2001 at 10:45:47AM +0930, John Edwards wrote:
> Richard Russell wrote:
> 
> > I've been puzzling over this for some time now -- how does Squid do
> > DNS lookups, and why does it ignore my resolv.conf and nsswitch.conf
> > files?
> 
> squid uses it's own resolver routines, so it can cache the lookups in
> it's own ipcache, unless you build it with --disable-internal-dns.
> 
> The quick fix to your problem (resolving local hosts without FQDN's) is
> the following in squid.conf:
> 
> append_domain .yourdomain.com
