Internode DSL expansion into rural South Australia

Hi All,

I thought folk here might be interested to know that we've decided to 
expand our ADSL and SDSL coverage into rural SA. Orders accepted now, 
first deliveries in mid to late November.

New areas are: Port Augusta, Port Pirie, Port Lincoln, Whyalla, Mt 
Gambier, Penola, Naracoorte, Millicent (most online in November, some 
in December).

I think this is important stuff, as the notion of greater equity into 
rural areas is good for everyone. Its a strong passion of mine, and 
this has driven the decision to take the plunge in this manner.

I've been talking to a number of folks on this list privately, who 
are in these areas, who have been asking me to do this - so I'm 
really glad to be able to give you some good news at last - I know 
its been a long time coming :)

Regards,

   Simon Hackett
