RE: [OT] Re: Asia Online gone into liquidation!!

Apparently, the VA gets "first pick" on whatever money is left in the bank before paying creditors but after paying employees thier
entitlements.

The administrator can choose not to receive any payment at all if this impacts on the employess wages ie if there is $100 left in
the bank, the VA would simply walk in, chose to wind up the company after a quick meeting with the creditors and walk out, hoping to
cover their loss on the next suck^H^H^H^H client.

They do not take on ownership, however they do put their names on the bank accounts, bills etc as they become liable for any refunds
etc after the day they take over.

Once a Deed of Company Arrangement is in place, the company can continue to trade without the adminsitrator and they can leave with
whatever fee they see fit (within reason).
