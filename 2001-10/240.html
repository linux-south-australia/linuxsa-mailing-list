Re: Firewalls

> I lost two machines to various hardware faults about a week ago.  In 
> the process of getting back up on the air, I upgraded Red Hat from 
> 6.0 to 7.1.
> 
> With 7.1 and pppoe, you get some default firewall scripts using 
> ipchains.
> 
> Can anyone tell me what ports I need to open up to allow MTA's to 
> deliver mail to my host?  I have opened up what I thought I needed to
> and a few more besides, and yet anything going to it from the outside
> world still seems to get bounced.

In Redhat 7.x sendmail only listens on 127.0.0.1 be default. You need to edit the 
sendmail.mc file, removing the line about binding address (it is well commented 
and hard to miss) and recreate your sendmail.cf
