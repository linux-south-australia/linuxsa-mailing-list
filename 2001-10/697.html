Re: Mobile phone plans for remote monitoring...

FYI, A few years back SA Hams had a connection of around 30km using two 
garage door openers running in microwave frequencies. Once set up (that 
took lots of time) they were able to plug the link in as an Ethernet 
connection and get good transmission across the network.

You need to be a Ham to play with those frequencies and you need really 
good line-of-sight. It can be done. A fun exercise.

Cheers, Ian
