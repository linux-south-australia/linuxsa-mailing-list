Roll-your-own Linux distro

With all the research that I have been doing to find the right distro 
for this community project this message turned up in the CompuServe 
Linux Forum for me the other day. 

I would love to hear if anyone has tried this.


" Take a look at

 www.linuxfromscratch.org

 It's got a cookbook for building your own linux
 configuration from the sources. It might be just what
 you need for working with your "Battlestar Galactica"(*)
 hardware.

 I found the LFS book in an internet search for Linux sys
 admin documentation. I'm using some of the information
 in a current project. I'm also considering building a
 couple of toy systems from components I've got lying
 around and think LFS might be the ideal vehicle for that
 endeavor (when I get a round tuit). I downloaded the
 HTML version from

 http://www.linuxdoc.org/LDP/lfs/LFS.html.tar.gz"

BTW, the CompuServe Linux Forum can be found at
