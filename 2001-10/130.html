On Tue, Oct 02, 2001 at 03:30:48PM +0930, James Brooks wrote:
> Hey Everyone,
>     I'm having great difficulty geting my Xircom CE-10/A Ethernet Adapter
> (PCMCIA) working in my laptop (Running RedHat 7.1). Could anyone help?
> 
> Cheers,
> James

PCMCIA support is still a bit iffy in the kernel. David Hinds, the author,
recommends using his pcmcia package available from
http://pcmcia-cs.sourceforge.net/
It's simple to config, compile and install and is still needed for the
pcmcia utilities even if you do try kernel support.
If you are using kernel 2.4.9 or later you will need pcmcia 3.1.29
(latest release) as earlier ones will not compile.
