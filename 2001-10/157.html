> entry level servers which require dual processors

An odd specification :)

We avoid multiprocessor x86 servers as much as we can, the reliability
tradeoffs for perceived extra speed (MP can be slower in some
applications) aren't worth it. Just buy a bigger single processor. If
you have enough multitasking going on to demand dual processors, why not
put them in seperate machines, and seperate the tasks so that each
server can do one job well?
