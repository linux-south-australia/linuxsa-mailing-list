CPU Load VS Fan Speed :-)

Hi,

Oh how I yearn for the old days, when computers were computers. They 
filled whole rooms, and had lots of flashing lights, and you could tell 
roughly what a machine was doing by the pattern of lights on the front.

These days, machines are so whimpy looking it is positively revolting.

Anyway, I have discovered over the last few days that I can hear when my 
BP6-based system is under load. When I am running a long job, the fans 
change speed and spin faster to push more air through the heat-sink.

When the job finishes, there is a definite audible difference.

Now, that is more like a real computer!
