I don't know about 4-channel, but you might want to try out the Sound Blaster Live! DE 5.1. It's a bit pricey (a little over $200, I think), but the sound quality is well worth it. As an added bonus, it has a built-in Dolby Digital ecoder. The Linux drivers can be found at: http://opensource.creative.com/

These drivers also allow you to modify what sounds get directed to which speakers, so you could probably get some really funky effects going.
