It could be WINS. It could be a browsing conflict. It could be an
authentication problem or password encryption. It could be all sorts of
other problems.

Have you checked your log.smb file and perhaps log.nmb. If you are using a
prepackaged distribution such as comes with RedHat Linux, have a look in
the files log.[CLIENTNAME] where [CLIENTNAME] is the netbios name of the
machine that is trying to make a connection to the Samba box.

Hope it helps,
