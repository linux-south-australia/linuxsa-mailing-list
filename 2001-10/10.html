Re: Mac clients for linux services:

Skip Telnet. Go straight to SSH, find a program called 'Nifty Telnet', the
current incarnation is a good SSH client. (Does telnet as well if you
must)

Not sure about Fetch, but it should work. I have used Anarchie
successfully for FTP.

Is your iMac's IP in /etc/hosts.allow ?

Michael
