Re: bsd v gpl licensing

On Tue, 2001-10-30 at 13:00, Anthony Symons wrote:

> I really dont like seeing microsoft ripping BSD code, and adding a
> little pissy footnote somewhere that most people wont see with bsd
> credits. Then, they charge a fortune for it, the non-bsd bits are still
> dodgy and people make money out of it who, in my mind, dont deserve it.

They say that imitation is the most sincere form of flattery :-)

When I first found out about the Microsoft using BSD code issue, I felt
quite the same as you seem to. At closer inspection, I felt that it
proved that the OpenSource community *does* produce better software than
that made by the commercial software giant, Microsoft.

Microsoft isn't really taking that much away from BSD by using the code.
If anything, they're giving them lots of free publicity. It's basically
Microsoft saying "If you can't beat 'em, join 'em".

Personally, I find the whole issue quite funny, particularly in the
light of recent comments by Microsoft big-shots on OpenSource.
