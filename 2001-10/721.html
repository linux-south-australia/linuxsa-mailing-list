I've tried it and actually use a 2.2 kernel-based distribution for a
routery/firewally/gateway type box between a couple of networks at
home. It's fairly straightforward, but I doubt that it would be the way to
go for your project. There is a fair bit involved and it does require a
fair amount of CPU time (it takes days to build on the 166MHz Pentium I
build it on at home :-)), even without X (let alone Gnome or KDE).
