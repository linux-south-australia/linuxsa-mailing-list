RE: Boot CDRom Creation - Dual Boot XP / RH7.2

>
>Um... I Installed XP first then RH7.2
>
>However I chose GRUB as the bootloader and now the only menu I 
>get is boot to RH7.2
>Thinking back I'm not sure where GRub offered to put the bootloader. It
>should be in the / mount point I presume and not on the 
>Primary partition.

Hmm, couple of options.

1. If you created a bootdisk during the RH install, you could boot with that
and add an entry into the grub loader to boot the XP partition.
Check the docs on Grub on how to do this, I'm not very familiar with it 
as I normally use LILO :o)

2. You could install LILO as your bootloader :o)
(or you could just reinstall redhat & choose lilo as your bootloader, but
that's
a windows-like solution :o)

Probably the first option is the preferred, and quickest too.

Also, if you install the bootloader on the / mountpoint, you still need some
sort of bootloader to boot into Linux, either that or you will need to boot
with a bootdisk everytime you want to use your Linux install, as Windows
won't
give you the option of booting anything but itself.

I'm not sure about RH7.2, but Mandrake has a nice boot config wizard which
allows
you to point & click your way to installing grub or lilo with access to your
windows
partitions as well.  Maybe theres a similar tool in Redhat, thats of course
presuming
that you're more comfortable with a GUI than the CL ;o)

HTH
