Two parts = Two things that can go wrong. Besides, PCMCIA cards are usually
much more expensive than PCI cards due to the miniturisation that they
feature. Just looking at network cards, you can pick up a 10/100 PCI for
under $50, but to get a PCMCIA card without any external dongles, you're
looking at over $300.

It's fair enough if they don't exist, but I would have hoped that a nice big
bulky cheap and good wireless card is on the market. :]
