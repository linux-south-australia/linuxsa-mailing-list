Ok firstly RH does not have to support SMP(which is the multi-processor
threading) as it is a kernel level thing. secondly there is an advantage,
the usage is spread across 2 CPUs increasing speed. you need to uncomment
a line in the Makefile of the kernel source then recompile the kernel. I
have never had to do SMP but one of my friends has a Dual PPro 200 and
does it all the time, I belive the line is appropriatly commented and all
so it shouldn't be hard to find.
