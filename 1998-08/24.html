HDD partitioning

Fellow Linuxers,

Thank you to the people who replied to my request for opinions on partitioning
my new HDD. I am pleased to say that the cut-over went very smoothly, much
easier than anticipated. I expected some grief with /proc and lilo, but it went
across without a single hiccup.

I eventually decided on a minimalist approach with only 4 partitions:
/ = 150 (includes /home)
swap = 64
/data = 500
/usr = the rest

Given my user base and my usage, this is ample compartmentalisation. I learned
from your advice, and appreciate that. Now if only I could afford a new.....

:)))

Cheers,
