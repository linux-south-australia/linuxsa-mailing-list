>What I have noticed is a very high call dropout rate since switching
>from 2.2.0f to 2.3.5.  I haven't yet found out why.  Has anyone else

Absolutely! We have been having hell with PPP 2.3.5 on our
dialup servers. Once it dies, no-one can establish a stable
ppp connection (it also can make PAP auth really dodgey when it
goes like that). Worst of all, we actually need 2.3.5's auth-up/auth-down
capability for our bandwidth logging (so we can't just downgrade
until it is stable).

The most stable incarnation we have found so far was a
hybrid of 2.3.3 and 2.3.5:

On Slackware Linux 2.0.30 we used:
- the ppp.c from 2.3.3 for the /usr/src/linux/drivers/net/ppp.c

- for he rest of the package we used 2.3.5

But if anyone has a better workaround, we'd love to hear
about it.
