Re: Epson Printers

The epson stylus colour, and then colour II, came out long before the 
numbered models (400,600)
I have one of these old ones, several years old now, working fine (and in 
colour) under a standard install of redhat 4.2

The old epson stylus colour printers use a protocol epson calls esc-p2, 
and AFAIK all the newer models use a superset of this protocol.

If you are really desperate, there is are some epson 24-pin colour 
printer driviers listed in the control panel that work with mine, that 
will probably work with yours. (Although at a lower resolution)

Leigh
