As it might be of interest, and as suggested by the subject of the
message, it relates to getting ppp-2.3.5 running under linux.  The
person who I quoted said that "make kernel" didn't install ppp.c.
I didn't find that, your mileage may vary.  He also said that the
FREE_SKB macro in ppp.c doesn't work on all versions of the kernel.
I'm running 2.0.30, for which I found the following necessary:

--- linux/ppp.c Wed Mar 25 10:24:59 1998
+++ /usr/src/linux/drivers/net/ppp.c    Sun Aug  9 17:04:58 1998
@@ -3078,3 +3078,3 @@
  */
-#if LINUX_VERSION_CODE < VERSION(2,1,86)
+#if 0 // LINUX_VERSION_CODE < VERSION(2,1,86)
 #define FREE_SKB(skb)  dev_kfree_skb(skb)
