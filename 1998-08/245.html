My ppp-on equivalent is attached as /usr/local/bin/pap-pick.
My /etc/ppp/pap-on-dialer is attached also.
My /etc/ppp/pap-secrets file contains a line like:
my-user-name-on-picknowl	*	my-picknowl-password
substitute your username and password obviously.

inconsequential to the problem.
