hi all,

	I have received permission from SDC (who do the Word Perfect
Unix ports) to establish a mirror site to provide the newly-released
Word Perfect 8 pre-release.

The software is now available from ftp://vic.auug.org.au/pub/wp8

Thanks to the Australian Unix Users Group for the use of their
server.

One gotcha in installing it is to ensure that the tar files
are in the install directory, ie tar xf ./fn   
not  tar xf /another-directory/fn
This is because the install process, for whatever reason, needs
the tar files and extracted files all together.
