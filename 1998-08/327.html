Re: Significance of MX numbers

Steven Bolbot wrote:

 > What I am curious about is the actual significance of the MX numbers. I
 > know that the lowest number should be the local machine and so on, but
 > would there be any difference if I used "2" and "1" instead of 20 and 10?

No difference.  SMTP software (such as sendmail) tries the lowest numbers
first, but it doesn't really matter how much "lower" they are.
