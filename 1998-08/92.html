Re: Panasonic Laser Printer

On Tue, 11 Aug 1998, Patrick Ryan wrote:

> Hi,
> 
> Is there any magical way of getting a Panasonic Laser Printer KX-P6100 to
> operate using Linux.  I do accept that the printer is designed for Windows.
> 
> Thanks in advance

This printer is (as far as I know) a horrible GDI beast and will never
work under Linux. It might if you can get the PCL4 emulation going, but
this usually works via a DOS TSR.

If:

echo Hello > /dev/lp0

Doesn't produce some output, you're stuck (though with a laser you might
need to press a button on the printer to get the 'Hello' output, since
they don't usually print till a full page of data comes in).
