Re: pop client

On 19 Aug, Ben Kramer wrote:
> I am looking for a mail client to use in X other than netscape.
> Can anybody recommend anything?

I use a client called postilion.  It's written in Tcl/Tk with a C back
end (called TkRat).  It has its peculiarities, but it supports full POP3
and IMAP rev4 (which was my main reason for choosing it), and is very
useable.  As it's written in Tcl its also not that hard to hack around
with it if you don't like something.

Have a look at http://www.postilion.org
