InstallFest Details

Hi everyone,

Thank you to those helpers and installees who have registered to
attending the InstallFest.  If you are planning on coming, please
register now at the InstallFest page so the organisers have some idea
of numbers:

http://www.linuxsa.org.au/meetings/1998-08/

If you have not heard about the InstallFest, here is some information
(more is available at the above web page):

> When and Where
> 
> The InstallFest will be held on Saturday, 15th August 1998, 10am to
> 4pm at SA Water, 65 Broad St, Marden.
> 
> What is an InstallFest?
> 
> If you want to run Linux but don't know how to install it, then an
> InstallFest will help you. You can bring along your computer and
> have an experienced Linux user guide you through the installation.
> 
> What will be happening?
> 
> We will have an area where people can have Linux installed, an area
> where vendors can peddle their wares, and also an area where users
> can show off the fun things that they do with Linux. An
> ``Introduction to Linux'' talk will be given at about 11am for
> people who are new to Linux.

If you have any questions not covered by the above or at the
InstallFest page, please contact the organisers by email to
organisers@linuxsa.org.au or you can phone me on 8370 3650.
