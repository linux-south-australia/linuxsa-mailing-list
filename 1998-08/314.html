Job Vacancy: Software Engineer with Integra Australia

Anyone interested in working as a Software Engineer
with Integra Australia (http://www.integra.com.au )
at Technology Park might like to 
to check out the job advertisement in this Saturday's 
Advertiser (Saturday 29 September). 

Part of the work would involve C++/GUI programming
under SunOS assisting myself on a radar simulation
project at the Electronic Warfare Division (EWD) at 
DSTO Salisbury.

A working knowledge of C and Unix (or Linux) is essential.
Familiarity with C++ would be a definite advantage.
Knowledge of VME, transputers, Occam, VxWorks or YACC
would be a bonus but not essential. An ability to 
follow just how various types of computer and
RF hardware work together would also be looked upon
very favourably.

Working at EWD would require obtaining a Defence
Security Clearance to Secret level. This would be
done by Integra and is generally much easier for
Australia Citizens.

To apply, please send your job resume
to trevor@integra.com.au 
or contact Trevor Ambrose on 8260 8948 for
further information.
