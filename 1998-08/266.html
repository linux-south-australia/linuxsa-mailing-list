Re: messages

> Can I delete /var/logs/messages ?

Best to do it like this:
cd /var/log
mv messages messages.0
killall -HUP syslogd

This should be done automatically if you leave your system on
overnight.

> i am getting sick of going through 3 months of logs to get to the end?

Try the 'tail' command.  Eg.:

tail messages      - shows the last 10 lines of messages
tail -20 messages  - shows the last 20 lines of messages
tail -f messages   - shows the last 10 lines, then loops forever
                     displaying anything extra that appears in that
                     file (very useful for watching what is happening
                     on your system)
tail -20f messages - you can guess this one
