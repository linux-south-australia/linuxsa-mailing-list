InstallFest Update

Hi all,

Thank you to everyone who has registered so far for the InstallFest.
We currently have 17 helpers, 25 users, and 3 curious people
registered.  If you have registered, but your name is not listed
below, please let me know; I'm planning to print name tags for
everyone who has registered.

Even if you can't/don't want to help, or don't need Linux installed,
you're welcome to just turn up on the day.  It should be an ideal
opportunity for people who are interested in Linux to find out more
about it, so don't forget to tell anyone you know if you think they
might be interested.
