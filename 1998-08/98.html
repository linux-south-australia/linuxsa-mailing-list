Re: CDE display and usernet

> It now seems that the source of the problem is usernet. Prior to using
> usernet I could open screen based applications without any trouble,
> however after using usernet I then encountered this problem. In addition
> usernet is behaving oddly, with erractic behaviour and I am not able to
> quit my ppp session without switching off the modem.

I've noticed that if you "start" a PPP connection while one is
running, it will forget that the first one was up and then you won't
be able to shut it down without killing the pppd process manually.  As
a guess, double-clicking on the button in usernet might do this?

> Has anyone had similar problems or can you offer advice? I guess one
> solution would be to avoid using userner altogether.

I like:
/sbin/ifup ppp0
/sbin/ifdown ppp0

If you put /sbin in your path, it's not many more keystrokes than
"usernet", and you don't need to touch the mouse.
