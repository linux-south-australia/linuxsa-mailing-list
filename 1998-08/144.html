InstallFest

Thankyou to all who helped organise the Installfest. I had a very good
time, learnt a few things and actually helped one fellow. He was a bit
miffed because he stuffed up his boot record and couldn't boot his PC at
all. Had to re-install Win 95. It is worth noting that if you do stuff
things up try this. Boot your PC using the Windows startup disk. Type
fdisk/MBR then sys c:
This clears the boot record and writes the system files onto the hard
drive. It has worked for me on several occassions. This will get your Win
95 working again, then you need to have a second attempt at Linux and lilo.
This info assumes one hard drive partitioned for DOS and Linux.
Cheers and beers,
