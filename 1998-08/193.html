> hi,
> problem: two identical ne-2000 compatable network cards.
>          i486 and i586, both have soundcards, 486 has int. modem.
>          redhat5.0
> 
> setup doesn't find either network card, either at first (nfs install)
> or later, when it asks about setting up a n/work card.
> 
> same problem on each pc.
> 
> what am i missing?

ISA NE2000's can't be auto-detected by Red Hat Linux 5.0.  You need to
specify the IO address (as eg. "0x300" -- don't forget the "0x") and
IRQ manually.
