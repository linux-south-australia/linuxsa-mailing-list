Hi again,

PPP works the same as in Slackware; chat and pppd are still used.  The
PPP-HOWTO has info on how to set it up.

If you have X, then netcfg is the easiest way to get things running.
Just pick interfaces, add, ppp, type in your phone number, username,
password, turn on "Use PAP", turn on "allow any user to (de)activate
interface", turn off "restart PPP when connection fails", click Done,
Save, and that's it.  You can then use "/sbin/ifup ppp0" and
"/sbin/ifdown ppp0" at a shell prompt.
