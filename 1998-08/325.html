Re: ejecting CD-ROM when umounting

> Hi
> Does anyone know a way I can automatically eject a CD-ROM when I umount it
> under Redhat 5.1? I've heard that the slackware umount program does this,
> but the Redhat one doesn't.

Hi Steven,

Check out the eject program (it does come with Red Hat; it's in the
eject package).  You can:
- eject a CD now
- umount and eject a CD (doesn't work for me, but umount followed by
  eject does :-)
- turn on auto-eject mode (ejects the disk when you unmount it)
