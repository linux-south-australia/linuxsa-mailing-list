Hi Allan.
It would appear that there was a problem when the install tried to 
install LILO onto your HDD MBR.
If you can get in to Linux, try typing /sbin/lilo. If that fails I 
suspect you have Virus Protection switched on in the BIOS to protect 
(it never does...just creates more hassles) the MBR.  Go into your 
BIOS/CMOS at boot and switch it off then try running /sbin/lilo 
again, as root.

regards..Don
