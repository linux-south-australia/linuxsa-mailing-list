Re: ssh with public and private key

Hi,

Thanks to everyone who contributed to this thread.

I did have the 'authorized_keys' with a 'z',
the problem lay in the permissions for the files in '.ssh' and the
permissions for the directory itself. Thanks in particular to David
Lloyd, who alerted me to this. Directory has to be 700, files 600.
