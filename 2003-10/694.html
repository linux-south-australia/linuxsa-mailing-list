Re: ssh with public and private key

In the immortal words of John Edwards <isplist@adam.com.au>...
> For systems with multiple administrators, forced non-root logins can 
> provide a better audit trail and a more fine-grained control of
> access. It's by no means foolproof, but like most security measures
> it's a good way of keeping honest people honest.

Also, if you use FreeBSD (not sure about the other BSD's) you also need
to be a member of the wheel group to su to root, making it just that
little bit harder, and as we all know, every little bit helps.
