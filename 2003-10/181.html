Alternatives to sourceforge?

Hiya,

Sourceforge offer an amazing service, and their (portal?) software beats
anything else out there; I'm involved with a few projects hosted at sf, and
what they offer is brilliant.

Recently though, presumably due to the massive loads on their servers, things
often (more and more often) are down then not, and more importantly CVS is
both incredibly slow and at least 24 hours out of date, which isn't that great
when you're actively trying to develop things.  I did a checkout of one
project last night and landed up with something a month old; something sure
isn't right.  They are looking into it, but there's no guarantee things will
be fixed.

I notice there's a whole heap of sourceforce-like sites around the place now;
both using their own sourceforge-like portals, and those actually using
sourceforge's commercial product.

Has anybody here used any, and can compare them to sourceforge, especially in
regards to features/reliability?
