Hi,

lftp does exactly what I intended.
Thanks to everyone who contributed :-)

######################
lftp -u username,password host.whereever.com <<EOF
cd remoteDir
lcd localDir
mirror -R
quit
EOF
######################

this also is a good contender, I think.
