bluetooth network doesn't work any more

Hi all,

I used to have a tcp/ip network with the ipaq I'm playing around with 
but this time it doesn't want to connect anymore and honestly I have no 
clue why.

I use a Belking USB dongle F8T003 and do the following to load the stuff 
on my SuSE 8.2 laptop

modules:
  bluez
  bnep
  l2cap
  hci_usb
  hci_uart
  hci_vhci
  rfcomm sco

then
/etc/init.d/bluetooth start
hciconfig hci0 up
pand --listen --role GN

I can connect now with the ipaq to my laptop by choosing "join a 
personal network"

then I bring bnep0 up with
ifconfig bnep0 192.168.5.1 netmask 255.255.255.0 broadcast 192.168.5.255
ifconfig bnep0 up

and till today this worked well. I could ping the ipaq and do everything 
else.

now I get simply
 From 192.168.5.1: icmp_seq=1 Destination Host Unreachable
when I try to ping the ipaq.

The questions are:
1) shouldn't I get a timeout when I can't ping the ipaq instead of a 
"unreachable"
2) what can cause that problems? The last thing I configured was some 
simple routing for testing but my iptables are flushed and empty. 
/proc/sys/net/ipv4/ip_forward is 0

I'm very thankful for any hints!

Peter
