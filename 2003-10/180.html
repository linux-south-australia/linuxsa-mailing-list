Re: Xinetd services and a delay with no DNS?

Matthew Western wrote:

>so, i'm appealing to the xinet gurus out there to help me out.    i also
>have a firewall on the machine which i read may be making things much slower
>to do these ident lookup things, but i wouldn't have thought so.
>  
>

If you set your firewall to 'reject' rather than 'deny' incoming ident 
connections, you won't see that pause. This is because the 
ident-checking routine can see immediately that the service doesn't 
exist, rather than waiting for a response that inevitably times out.
