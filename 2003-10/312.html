Promise IDE controller problems.

Hi all,

Im having problems with my new Promise Ultra133 TX2 IDE controller card.
It has connected to it two WD 120Gb drives (one per cable).

It seems that the kernel isnt liking this card too much even though it
has specific support for it (the PCD20269 chip).
Almost as soon as either of the HDDs that are connected to it are used,
the kernel has a panic attack and hangs.

I have tried kernels 2.4.22 and 2.6.0-test7, both of which give the same
problem.
I have also tried many hardware configs (diff ram, cpu, diff PCI slots,
etc), which all point to the card being the problem.

Is anyone else using this card successfully?

Thanks for any help.
