Re: WINE and/or WINEX - howto

Hi Corey,

My first question is why are you trying to run RTCW in Wine/Winex, when
it is supported natively under Linux ?

ftp://ftp.idsoftware.com/idstuff/wolf/linux/wolf-linux-1.41-3.x86.run


As far as getting other apps to work, I've had more success with Wine
than Winex. Apart from some DirectX & Installshield support, Winex
always seems to be approx. 2 Wine releases behind in what works.
Speaking from experience only.

It also greatly depends on what the application or game is, as each
(usually) needs to be configured with the right *.dlls (preferably from
a working windows install), via the ~/.wine/config file.

http://www.frankscorner.org/ is THE definitive Wine site to visit for
getting things working. It has some nice Howtos for a lot of popular
apps/games, & the forums are very good also.

Hope it helps,


Regards,
Rick
