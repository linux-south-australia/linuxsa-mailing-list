I am pleased to announce that Adelaide Institute of TAFE has now officially
registered the domain edulug.org edulug.org will be the host for new
initiatives by Adelaide TAFE IT Studies Dept and will be the portal for OSS
training and information.

The site will be managed by students, for students, with support form staff
and hopefully with expert input from Linuxsa contributors.

As a supporting role, we have started a TAFE linux  users group (although we
will cover all aspects of OSS) and we will encourage self development,
student presentations and peer support across schools, TAFE's and VET
sectors.

We are developing monthly meetings/learning-workshops be based on core
Linux/OSS/BSD topics, eg installation, configuration, X, security, DHCP,
DNS, SAMBA, MAIL, and so on, etc etc.

We will try to keep it related to real-world skills needed by organisations
to integrate and take advantage of OSS and last but not least it will ALL be
practical based, e.g. each monthly meeting/workshop will be a hands on
affair so that topics covered will be done with people's hands on the
keyboards! Not only will you have access to knowledgeable people, you will
be able to try, break it and fix it.

Needless to say all comments about our direction, monthly workshops and
offers of assistance are warmly welcomed.

Further news about our progress will be made available to LinuxSA as we go.
