Re: 19 inch monitor quality

I've got one of those benq 19" monitors (earlier version when they were called 
acer - it's the g991).. and I'm pretty happy with it... However, my friend's 
monitor has lost quite a bit of colour in one of the corners of the screen. 
Personally I'm fed up with acer/benq stuff (my acer burner just died).

I have a  friend who has a Mitsubishi monitor (21" I think)... he's had it for 
years and it's never failed him. It squeals at some refresh rates though.

We have the diamond view monitors at Uni, and they seem to be ok. It looks and 
works exactly like my acer monitor at home, so they might actually be the 
same monitor with different branding.

I haven't tried the LG ones, so I don't know.

Damien
