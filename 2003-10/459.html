Re: 19 inch monitor quality

well that depends on a couple of things:

Do you want more desktop space, or every thing bigger ?
If you want more space, and can read things that are small then probably
the LG, but then that also depends on the refresh rate at 2048x1536.
The BenQ 78hz@1600x1200 is quite acceptable, and a good price.
The TFT probably only runs 1280x1024, so no good for desktop space. But
great for reduction of eye strain as there are no refresh rate issues.
Forget the first Bits-are-missing, but what are the specs on the second
one ?
