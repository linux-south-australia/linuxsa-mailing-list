Attend linux.conf.au for FREE :-)

Hi all,

Last year, Sun Microsystems sponsored the Linux.Conf.Au Regional
Delegate Programme (RDP) which provided funding for one person from
each state and territory in Australia to attend Linux.Conf.Au.

This year, Sun are doing it again!

The package includes:

- Free Professional Registration for linux.conf.au 2004 (this
  includes, amongst other things, a ticket to the Conference Dinner,
  and a ticket to the Professional Networking Session)

- Return economy air ticket from your closest major airport to
  Adelaide (for applicants that live outside of Adelaide)

- Accommodation at the on-campus lodgings (St. Marks College) for the
  duration of the main conference (again, for applicants that live
  outside of Adelaide) 

To be eligible for selection as the South Australian Delegate you must
be a resident of South Australia, and be able to demonstrate a
contribution (however small, and not necessarily in software) to the
Linux/Open Source community.

You don't need to be involved with LinuxSA to apply, so please pass
this message onto anyone else who you think should put their name
forward.

Applications close 31/Oct/2003, so hurry!

For information on how to apply to be the South Australian Delegate,
see http://www.linuxsa.org.au/rdp.html

For more information about the RDP, see
http://conf.linux.org.au/rdp.cgi

For more information about LCA, see http://conf.linux.org.au/

Have fun,
