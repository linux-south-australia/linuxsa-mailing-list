Children's & Youth Software Awards

Children's & Youth Software Awards

The Premier's Children's and Youth Educational Software 
Awards announces an exciting new initiative of the South 
Australian Government.  

The Awards provide Australian software developers, including 
students, with an opportunity to put their multimedia skills to 
the test. 

Prizes will be awarded by an independent panel for software 
developed within the previous two years in two categories:

Category 1 - Children's software; Software intended for 
early/primary years.   Focus for 2004 - enhancing literacy and 
numeracy learning 
Category 2 - Youth Software; Software intended for middle/
senior years.  Focus for 2004 - enhancing literacies and 
numeracies in lifelong learning. 

Funding will be available in each category for up to 3 awards 
of between $5,000 and $20,000. 

Australian software developers, school and tertiary students 
are encouraged to apply. 

Applications close on 28th November 2003. 

Visit www.tsof.edu.au/Events/Premier/ for all the details
