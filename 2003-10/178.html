It's worth noting that doing this with devels is normally ok, but you
shouldn't be a forcing non-devel packages.

Just so it's easier for you next time to find out what package has the
file you just deleted, you could have done a: "rpm -qf
/usr/lib/oafConf.sh", and if you think you could have damaged an installed
package, but not sure, get rpm to tell you via "rpm -V packagename".  And
while I'm at it, instead of "--force"ing, you should consider
"--replacepkgs" which is a slightly smaller hammer.

Hope this helps,
