> I'm in the market for a new monitor.  So far I have been offered:
> Which would you choose?

My two cents:

I can't go past LCD monitors.  I have two and would never* go back.  Yes
they do cost considerably more and if you want more than 1280x1024 it does
get a tad expensive, but the sharpness, convergence, clarity, and lack of
annoyingly low refresh rates can't be beaten (my eyes only feel comfortable
at 85Hz or higher).  I have intelligent font antialiasing (ClearType in
Windows XP) switched on which makes text a joy to read.

* The only real reason not to buy LCD is colour matching.  Trinitron CRTs
can't be beaten for doing design work.
