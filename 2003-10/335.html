Well I realize that this might be bad timing seeing how the recent "Linux Vs

secure either.  Even though I would (and do) use Linux over anything else,
IMO the majority of the software for Linux is still in the minority for it's
class of software.  In the circles that matter... who cares if you got the
minority, because the more havoc you cause the more "respect" you get...
</2c>
