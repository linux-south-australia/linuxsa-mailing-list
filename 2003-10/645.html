Damien:  I'm really doubtful about this.  Binary packages for *all* Linux'es
will never, ever work IMO - I mean, the amount of different kernels,
libraries, etc out there mean that the same application's not going to work in
every enviroment unless you pull all sorts of duplicate dependencies over,
i.e. version 1.3 and 1.4 of x library, not to mention all sorts of other fun
things. This ain't Windows - it's the land of choice, and we've sure got
plenty of it.

Then of course you've got more then one architecture; not just 32-bit x86
code, but PPC, etc.  Good luck getting a x86 package running on a G4.
