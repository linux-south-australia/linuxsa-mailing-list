Where is the hosts: 0 coming up?

Most of the SCSI cards I have seen interrupt the boot sequence (well sort
of) and say something to the effect of:

 "Press CTRL-S to enter SCSI BIOS"

...yours of course will be different but you get the drift. If you do this
there should be a way of getting the SCSI card to tell you what it thinks
is connected to it. Obviously if you can't find the tape drive then that
is part of the problem.

I'd double check whether termination is OK, change the cable if you have a
spare one and checking the card in another motherboard and even another
OS.

Also, do a google search for:

 "SME linux AHA-1505"

...or similar. If that doesn't turn up an answer, try substiting the
kernel version for SME.
