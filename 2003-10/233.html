I too recently discovered Sodipodi and agree it is easy to use and
stable. Sodipodi works well hand-in-hand with The Gimp as it exports to
PNG files which can be dropped into Gimp projects. Individual shapes can
be selected in Sodipodi and exported for Gimping. This page provides a
really good introduction to using Sodipodi:

http://hawthorn.csse.monash.edu.au/~njh/programming/drawing-packages/sodipodi/
