Re: gdk_pixbuf errors when compiling gtk+-2.2.4

Hi Chris,

This is a common problem not for just gtk+, but other apps as well.
Usually fixed by upgrading binutils & libtool packages.
http://ftp.gnu.org/gnu/binutils/
http://www.gnu.org/software/libtool/libtool.html

After upgrading, re-extract the gtk+ archive & re-compile.

Hope it helps.

Regards,
Rick
