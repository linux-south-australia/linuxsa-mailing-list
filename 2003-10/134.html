.mailfilterrc misunderstanding|explanation

I said that the following line in my ~/mailfilterrc file didn't work, 
but it does:

"DENY<>^(To|Cc):.*(naota|anithen|cnixon3)@tampabay\.rr\.com"

I thought "DENY<>^(To|Cc):.*[naota|anithen|cnixon3]@tampabay\.rr\.com" 
worked, but it does not.  I failed to look at my ALLOW list when testing 
my POP filters.  The address that I was sending from was in my ALLOW 
list, so the 
"DENY<>^(To|Cc):.*(naota|anithen|cnixon3)@tampabay\.rr\.com" didn't 
affect the sent mail, making for spoiled test results.

...found out that the filter rule didn't work when I sent from an address
that wasn't on my ALLOW list.

Anyway, I'm tired, hence the rambling, so let me get to the point:

REG_TYPE = basic

The above is why 
"DENY<>^(To|Cc):.*(naota|anithen|cnixon3)@tampabay\.rr\.com" didn't work.
 
REG_TYPE = extended

The above allows 
"DENY<>^(To|Cc):.*(naota|anithen|cnixon3)@tampabay\.rr\.com" to function 
as it should.

I apologize for the error, and thank you.
