Re: Secondary DNS services

Brett,
I use secondary.org to ackup 3 domains under my controll. I've never had a 
problem that I've noticed, but I've only ever had my primary DNS down twice 
for any length of time. In both cased, secondary.org worked fine. 

I think the question you have to ask is what is it worth to you ? If this is 
a business critical decision, a free service has no financial recourse if it 
goes down. If a paid service, particularly of the likes of Internode, goes 
down, you know its in their business interest to return their service ASAP, 
or they may have to refund you money, or pay you compensation for lost 
service. 

For my personal domains, and domains of organisations I'm involved with, 
this isn't so much of an issue.
