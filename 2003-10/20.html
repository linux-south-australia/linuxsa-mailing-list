Re: Printing in Open Office

Brian,

> I cannot see any suitable drivers
> (Epson EPL-4000) in ./spadmin so I have not gone down the path of simply
> adding a printer.

I would:

 * ensure that CUPS is configured or the packages are installed that allow
   it to look like an LPR style printing system

 * then convince OpenOffice to simply print post script to the relevant LPR
   driver

I do this with my LaserWriter at home. Basically, I have setup CUPs to use
it. Then I share it via the LPR emulation facilities as "lp" and then print
to it using:

 lpr -Plp < something.ps

..although I don't need the -P because without it lpr assumes the printer
is called "lp".

In Debian the packages I mention are called cupsys-bsd.
