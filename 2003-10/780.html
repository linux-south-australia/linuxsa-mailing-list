Re: Major NAT headaches

Is it possible that Explicit Congestion Notification (ECN) is enabled on
the router, and the Windows machines don't understand the ecn bit in the
packets they are receiving?

Perhaps try disabling ecn:
echo "0" > /proc/sys/net/ipv4/tcp_ecn



Hope this helps =)

~Rhett
