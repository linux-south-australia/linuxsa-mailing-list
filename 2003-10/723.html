[OT] KAZ It Traineeships

KAZ It Traineeships

Do you know anyone in Year 12 seeking a career in IT?

KAZ has been offering IT traineeship placements to young 
school leavers since 1999. This highly successful program will 
continue in 2004 & we are seeking students who have completed 
Year 12 & who have a practical interest in computing. A 1-year 
traineeship, commencing in February 2004 with KAZ will offer a 
young person a chance to join the IT workforce and achieve a 
nationally recognised qualification.  Each year the traineeship 
offers a scholarship to a trainee, which ensures entry into KAZ's 
Graduate Program at the completion of their university studies. 
KAZ is also able to offer paid holiday employment during 
university semesters. 

All trainees in the history of the program have been gainfully 
employed at the end of the 12-month program.
