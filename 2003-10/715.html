RE: [OT] "Personal" UPS?

I had a 1000VA UPS made by PowerCel Power Excel or something similar. It's
batteries died after 6-9months. Survived a few power outages and looked
after a computer (400W PSU with 8 disks), 400W amp and 1000 Lumen projector,
it would keep it running for a good 5+mins (with amp not powering the
speakers). I basically bought it as a glorified power filter. :)
Single PC would keep it up for over 30mins.
It was very cheap, around the mid $200's. But I spoze you get what you pay
for. I wouldn't recommend it. Batteries should last longer than that.
Management cable/software is an additional $60
