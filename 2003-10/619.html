Free Linux Art

As much of it as you want to print, actually :-)

At http://lca2004.linux.org.au/art/ you'll find assorted promotional
material to go on walls, websites and wherever a postcard will fit.

If you want to add your company's logo or slogan just ask the LCA 
committee, who are unlikely to refuse reasonable requests. Courtesy
the hard work of Ryan Verner, thanks Ryan!

(And if anyone wishes to contribute their own creations they'll be given
fair consideration, break out The Gimp!)
