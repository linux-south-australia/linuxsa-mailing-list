Re: .mailfilterrc help

Curtis wrote:

> Also, the full stop at the end of the third line is because that is the
> end of the sentence.  In the .mailfilterrc file, it's not there.

That's bad practice - you should post your config files *verbatim*,
not modify them in the interests of grammatically correct sentences
(which they aren't).

> I took your advice, and tried 
> DENY<>^(To|Cc):.*(naota|anithen|cnixon3)@tampabay\.rr\.com, but
> that just deletes all three of the accounts' messages, treating
> (naota|anithen|cnixon3) like a user name.  The good news is that your
> advice caused me to try
> "DENY<>^(To|Cc):.*[naota|anithen|cnixon3]@tampabay\.rr\.com"

Interesting - how is it that (To|Cc) works correctly, where as
(naota|anithen|cnixon3) doesn't?  I suspect there may be a case
sensitivity issue, as your name is posted Capitalised, but you're
comparing lower case.  Does mailfilter treat regular expressions
differently on the right hand side of a colon? (in fact, as an RE,
[naota|anithen|cnixon3] is just a single character class, if
mailfiter is using REs).
