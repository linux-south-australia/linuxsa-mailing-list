Just a thought that raced across my mind as I was reading this, you
could use cron to run scripts at the appropriate times to modify
iptables/ipchains firewall rules to block certain IP's getting to the
SMB ports on the server.  Simple and easy to implement and it would give
you very fine control over ip ranges or specific IP's to block.  I
haven't tried this myself but it was just an idea.
