It's still a "test" kernel - it's pre-release quality.  You might expect
there to still be issues (afterall, 2.4 took months to stabilise even
_after_ release).  Why not report the problem to the appropriate place and
see if it is "just you" or others as well?

I haven't looked at the preemptive stuff, but it may not be recommended
for desktop users.  There's plenty of stuff in the kernel targetted at
very big or very small systems that are not enabled by default for just
that reason.  But I think it's more likely to be other issues, like
misconfiguration of your kernel.
