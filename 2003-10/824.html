Heya Jordan, 
     I suppose you could always find out what and where the variables
are by runnig something as simple as this. 
------- 
#!/your/path/2/bash 
echo "Content-Type: text/html" 
echo 
echo "<html><head> </head> <body>" 
echo "<pre>set stuff:" 
set 
echo "</pre>" 
echo "<pre>env stuff:" 
env 
echo "</pre>" 
echo "</body> </html>" 
------- 
and by passing get (http://URL/bash.script?stuff=nothing) stuff to it,
it should tell you what variables the info is stored in.  I would take a
guess that everything past a "?" would all be in the $1 variable
respectively, so you would have to `awk` it or something.
