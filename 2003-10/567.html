[ANNOUNCE] LinuxSA October 2003 Meeting

Hi all,

Time for the LinuxSA October Meeting (it's tomorrow).

Here are the usual details:

  When:   7:00pm (doors open 6:45pm) on
          Tuesday, 21st October, 2003
  Where:  2nd Floor
          Compaq Computer
          139 Frome Street
          Adelaide SA
  Cost:   FREE
  Who:    Anyone and everyone.
          No pre-registration necessary.

Presentation:

  Sean Burford and Seth Simons will be talking about "Linux
  Entertainment Systems":

  Seth and Sean will be talking about building a Home Theatre PC.
  Drawing on their experiences, they will discuss both the hardware
  and software necessary to create a variety of media
  station/entertainment center computers suitable for use in the
  loungeroom or the car.

  Seth has built a couple of in car entertainment computers with LCD
  displays, Freevo, etc, and Sean has built a media centre for his
  loungeroom using a system based around an low power EPIA motherboard
  and MythTV.  Seth's old web page with some details of his first such
  system is at http://www.gurukuli.co.uk/carmp3.html

Extras:

  As per usual, if you are having a problem with your Linux
  installation, feel free to bring your machine.  Make sure to bring
  everything necessary to make your computer work (eg. monitor,
  keyboard, etc).

Pizza:

  After the meeting, please join us for pizza at San Giorgios, a short
  walk north along Frome Street to the corner of Rundle Street.

For more information:
