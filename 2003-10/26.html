And if you haven't tried, be aware that the usb implementation on the
camera's is invariably slower than a cheap USB memory card reader, and
as a bonus, it doesn't drain your camera batteries to use one.

If you really want to fly, grab a USB 2.0, or FireWire card reader. Even
the best DSLR's have a hard time keeping up with the speed of these.

The card readers come up as usb mass storage devices, and if you take a
lot of images, they work out to be very convenient compared to tinkering
with the dinky micro connectors on the cameras.

heh. You notice this stuff when you have ~500mb of images to upload :)
