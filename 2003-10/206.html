Re: Netscape Outgoing Mail Warning

James,

> ... Netscape will say the message was properly delivered, but it will 
> never arive at the recipient's email box.

But it very well may have been "properly" delivered. For example, if your
mail goes like this:

 James' Computer

 - sends an SMTP e-mail to foobar@nonexistent.com.01 message to James'
   sendmail server-

 Sendmail Server

 - attempts delivery to foobar@nonexistent.com.01 -

 ...which then complains

Now, James' Computer could very well get a "Mail got to me OK" by the
Sendmail server depending on its configuration. The mail DID get there
correctly. The bounce will come when the Sendmail server gets an error.
