yes the latest CVS version of WineX still does this.

Can someone please point me in the direction of a proven link or give me
some guidance because I'd really like to get this happening. The system I'm
running this on is virtually a vanilla RH9, AMD Athlon XP 2200, 512Mb RAM,
etc.
