RE: monitoring a computers network traffice from a switch

Jon,

Mirror the port on the switch (which basically replicates the data on the
port in question to another port), then do with the data as you like, ie to
another logging PC. This is if you want to know WHAT the data is as well as
the amount.

This is then transparent to the monitored PC.

Nice way to get all those POP3 and telnet passwords. :) j/k
