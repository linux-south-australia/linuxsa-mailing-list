Indeed, it isn't a huge issue for you.
Also, the full stop at the end of the third line is because that is the 
end of the sentence.  In the .mailfilterrc file, it's not there.  

I took your advice, and tried 
DENY<>^(To|Cc):.*(naota|anithen|cnixon3)@tampabay\.rr\.com, but that 
just deletes all three of the accounts' messages, treating 
(naota|anithen|cnixon3) like a user name.  The good news is that your 
advice caused me to try 
"DENY<>^(To|Cc):.*[naota|anithen|cnixon3]@tampabay\.rr\.com"

It's fine now!  Thanks a bunch.
