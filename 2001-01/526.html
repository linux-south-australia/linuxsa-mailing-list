Computer Education in SA. A Microsoft Entrenchment Tool

Hi,

You might have noticed an article on computer education entering the
Curriculum in SA in a big way.

I suspect that this is nothing more than a Microsoft entrenchment tool.

The article suggested that there would be an official curriculum from
Reception to year 10, and would include skills in wordprocessing, graphics,
etc.

I think that a great deal of scrutiny should be applied to the proposed
curriculum to ensure that it does not become nothing more that a means to
entrench MS in the minds of millions of school leavers.
