Re: Mylex Raid and RH7.0

At 08:58 PM 1/9/01 +1030, Steve Sloan wrote:
>Hi all
>
>Has any tried to get the Mylex raid 150 U2 controller going under RH7.0

Hmmm, I haven't tried this combination, but I do have Mylex RAID
controllers running under 6.2, and have been using them since 5.1 days.

My first suggestion is, don't use RH7.0. It is a crock. Use RH 6.2 and wait
for RH 7.1.

>I am looking at setting one up and would appreciate any feedback.

Even 6.2 understands the Mylex series of RAID controllers, I believe. It
should find it when you boot the CD, and if it doesn't, you can stick the
driver on a floppy, and mount the floppy at a strategic point during the
install, and then insmod the driver off the floppy.
