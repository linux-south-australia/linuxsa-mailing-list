Hi Andrew

Looks like you may have a problem with your Win partition. Reboot computer 
and try and go into Win. If you can't get into Win then try something like 
Nortons Disk Doctor and try and repair the drive (? dud partition table, ? 
virus). You may lose everything on your Win partition so be prepared.

I'm not sure if there are any utilities out there that can reconstruct a 
partition table, if you do have Nortons installed you could try Image.
