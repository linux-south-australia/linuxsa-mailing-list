List Etiquette???

Perhaps we should instate some guidelines:

1)

Don't post in HTML deliberately; occasional mistakes will be forgiven -
especially for new comers to the list

2)

Don't even mention politics, even if it is a means to promote our open
source efforts

3)

Speak only proper, formal English

4)

Don't say anything that may require an emotional mark such as :-), :-P
or ;-) - you may accidentally forget to include the mark and you're
meaning will be misunderstood

5)

Don't ask any questions that could - even remotely - be contained in a
man page, info page, HOWTO or found at http://www.google.com/

6)

Tone down your message and deliver it in the most culturally sensitive,
politically correct manner possible; any other methods - especially
sarcasm - will almost certainly be misinterpreted

The only problem is, section (5) basically makes the list go silent.
Section (6) kills any interesting discussions...
