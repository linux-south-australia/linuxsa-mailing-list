RE: Free Hard Drive space

A little frobbing around with the `man -k` (or `apropos`) command, with
likely keywords will gve you:
---------------------------------------
$ man -k "disk space"
df (1)               - report filesystem disk space usage
---------------------------------------

(you could also try things like `man -k disk |grep space` and so on)
