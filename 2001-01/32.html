Re: Essentials for Rh7.0

Hi,

> Well I am slowly going to infiltrate work with Linux. So I am aiming for 
> rh7. I mean to download it over our connection and was wondering the best 
> way of doing so and which files are which. aarnet lists the following sadly 
> no index file so I'm asking for info.
> Some are obvious but my last RH 6.1 only had 2 CDs and it was complete so I 
> am confused atm.

For posterity, even tho alternate methods have been mentioned.

> 
>   7.0-i386-docs-8bit.iso  23-Sep-2000 03:39   620M

Docs. Relevent pages can be downloaded from the Web. Useful for offline
help.

>   7.0-i386-powertools.iso 14-Sep-2000 02:45   586M

Powertools: useful utilities, most of which you won't need. Useful ones
can be downloaded from mirror.aarnet in individual package form.

>   7.0-i386-respin-SRPM..> 12-Oct-2000 00:35   647M

Source RPMs. Are you planning on re-compiling most of your system, if not
you don't need this.

>   7.0-i386-respin-disc-1.iso 12-Oct-2000 00:51   644M
>   7.0-i386-respin-disc-2.iso 12-Oct-2000 00:55   646M

Depending on whether you do a base or custom install, you can just get by
with disk one. Disk 2 contains more utilities and custom/full installs
need them.

>   7.0-i386-upgrade.iso    12-Oct-2000 00:31   256k

Needed to patch RH to a stable version, but there should already be an
updated ISO there, dated later than the rest.

Hope this helps.
