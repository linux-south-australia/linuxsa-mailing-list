Re: Linuxbox with RedHat 6.2 and Windows98

It's easy enough... Put your reattach your second HDD to be first for
installation procedures. As installation will be complete swap it back.
Start your linuxbox and add following lines to your /etc/lilo.conf :

--- cut here ---
other=/dev/hdb1
    label = windows
    map-drive = 0x80
        to = 0x81
    map-drive = 0x81
        to = 0x80
--- cut here ---

then run

lilo -v

and check that everything went OK. Now reboot and on lilo: prompt enter
windows
That's all! :)

Note: this will work if you have second HDD as slave on primary IDE
interface
