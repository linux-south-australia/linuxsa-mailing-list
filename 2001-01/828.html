NIS (or NIS+, which isn't available on Linux, AFAIK) would be a good
starting point. I'm sure there's some other way as well, but I have no idea
what it is. Of course, you can always roll your own -- remove access to all
passwd programs, and write your own little program that will perform the
change on all servers at once for you... (a friend of mine wrote a web page
to do that for synchronising Squid, SAMBA and shell account logons)...
