> of pouring money into (lappies preferring instead to get twice the

I've done this with exactly the same setup.  If you use the laptop as an
X terminal, then the performance will be adequate (as opposed to
exceptional).  The slowness of the network can be mitigated by tidying
up "busy" desktops to a large extent.  If you have enough RAM in the
laptop, then you can even organise for its hard disk to turn off after a
period of time, so that it doesn't stick to your lap (you will be
wearing shorts and sipping daquiris, yes?).
