This is a strange one. I running POSTFIX on my server (POP3 incoming mail
and SMTP outgoing mail) and a mix of OUTLOOK EXPRESS and OUTLOOK 2000 on my
client PC's.

Someone at my place of employment forwarded 5 JPEG files to me (they use
Outlook Express). However my Outlook 2000 couldn't display them. By display,
I mean as icons in the attachment area of an email. Outlook 2000 decided to
display these JPEG files as text (shows the code that makes up these files).

However, if we saved these files to their hard drive and then created a new
email and attached these 5 JPEG files, my Outlook displays them properly.

There are only a few of us who are using Outlook 2000, so I forwarded the
original email to another user using Outlook 2000. The email arrived
perfectly.

Has anyone seen/heard of a problem like this? Can anyone help?
