For most purposes this would be fine. Most people go into their office
several times a week if not every day, and downloads to laptops or
borrowing CDs etc can keep the cost down. I have worked in situations in
the past where there was a bare end-to-end ISDN connection and people did
keep it flat chat 24x7 running mirroring applications. But careful
application of rsync reduced this to a trickle.
