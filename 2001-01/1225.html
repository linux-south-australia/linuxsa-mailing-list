Success in resurecting a dead drive ...

Hi,

Sometime ago I enquired about resurecting a dead drive, and today I can
report success. A little longer that three days, I know, but I am not a
miracle worker :-)

Had a dead 425MB WD that would not show up under the BIOS, and someone
really wanted the data.

I had another 425MB WD of the same type, and it was fine.

I simply swapped the electronics. Four screws (TORX or something), and two
connectors, one on the edge and fixes and the other flexible and connected
to the motor.

Did it all back up and stuck it on a machine, and it was recognized and I
copied all the data on it and checked out a WORD file, and could read it
with Word 97 ... 

Yah. It works.
