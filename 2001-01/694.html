Re: Linuxbox with RedHat 6.2 and Windows98

BTW: you can have just
--- cut here ---
other=/dev/hdb1
    label = windows
--- cut here ---
But it will be good only for Windows 95 & 98... If you will install Windows
NT, 2000, ME it will not work until you will have map-drive statements.

Linux get drive mappings directly from controller and it couldn't be tricked
with such statements but Windows (any kind) get drive mapping information
from BIOS where LILO can make some modifications on start. So LILO have
chance to trick Windows by changing of drive mappings.
