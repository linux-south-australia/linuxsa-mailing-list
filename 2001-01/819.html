RE: NIC Problem

Being a Pnp card (i presume), its probably fighting with another card for an
irq.
i had this problem with an NIC the other day

do a 'cat /proc/interrupts' & see whats being used, might give you an idea
whats using the same int. (usually 10 or 5)
also check in your BIOS & disable anything that you wont have a use for (ie.
com2, usb, onboard sound/midi etc)
this might free up another irq that the NIC can pinch

HTH
