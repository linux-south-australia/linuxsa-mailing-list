>how to replace a number in a field with another number.

I'm not entirely sure what you're asking, but you could do something like:

UPDATE Table SET veiws = veiws+1 WHERE RowID=$ID

Which would let you increment a value in the field "veiws" for the row
having the field RowID equal to $ID.
