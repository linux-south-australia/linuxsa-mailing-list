RE: Computer Education in SA. A Microsoft Entrenchment Tool**YES*

I'd like to suggest that schools should discourage the use of computers.
Particularly word processors with spell checkers. The spell checker was one
of the worst things to happen to writing. Schools should focus on giving
students the skills (for want of a better word) that they will need for
life -- thinking, reasoning, expressing, understanding, learning, inventing.
They can then apply these to the subject of computers, or use computers as
tools to enhance their abilities in these areas...

Seems to me that the entire educational system has lost sight of these core
goals, and has been distracted by the short-term goals of vocational
education... Indeed, many people in computer land have forgotten that
computers are only tools to get something else done better or faster.

That's my LinuxSA email for the day...
