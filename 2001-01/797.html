I can't comment on a P166.

The wireless cards are good. They are fast and cheaper than
cabling and you can use an old PC as your dialup server
and masquerade onto your wireless subnet.  Alternatively,
most manufacturers now have a "out of the box" machine
that does the same in a nice form factor.

If you have a multistory house with concrete floors, don't
expect inter-floor coverage without some antenna design.
