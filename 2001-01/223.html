as a user of both, I can say that I prefer the compactflash cards. I
have 3 devices that take CF cards, one of them an adapter for a PCMCIA
slot.

1) take pictures
2) take CF card out of camera
3) put CF card into PCMCIA adapter
4) insert said PC card into laptop
5) wait for beep
6) mount /dev/hdc1 /mnt/cf -t vfat
7) cp /mnt/cf/dcim/canon100/* /data/raw/
8) umount /mnt/cf

voila. faster than USB :)
