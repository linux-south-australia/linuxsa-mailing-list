Re: PPP0 - dialup connection

Mark as a general rule MTU:1500 is fine if the hops are through main
backbones etc as it's a pretty common figure.

580 errors of 70000 is a low error rate < 1% again it's not unreasonable. 

Your deterioration of upload speed maybe time of day, load on your
ISP,industry in the area if the modem is retraining to minimise error rates.

These are my guesses from limited experience.
