Re: Coupla Questions

I like RFS its stable and is a little more robust if the system goes down, ie
power outage.  Note though for servers that could or do require users to have
limited space on the HDD, RFS cannot do this yet and is one of the reasons RH
has not included it in its distro, so far.

Other than that I would say RFS is the way to go on home systems.
