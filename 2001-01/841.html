Matthew!

> definition for me, but the length parameter is set to 9830. Does anyone
> know if this matters or not? Is it worth bumping that figure up to
> something closer to the truth?

The length parameter does help AMANDA estimate how much it thinks will
fit on the tape, however it will write until it gets an error and/or an
EOF...I'd leave it at what tapetype told you if you're just starting. If
you were to adjust it you may find that AMANDA won't send something to
tape even though it could have, or it might attempt to stack too much
onto the tape...
