RE: Experience with DAT or DLT vs Travan?

> We are considering a move to DAT or DLT.  DLT is more expensive than DAT,
> but has twice the capacity: 40GB uncompressed vs 20GB uncompressed (DDS4),
> but is somewhat more expensive (not quite twice ...).
>
> What have been the experiences with these devices out there, especially
> where they are used (written to) each day?

Our experience with DLT Drives has been that they are very fragile -- We use
Sun E series servers and the DLT drives that Sun provide, and they are very
sensitive to how they are treated... We replaced one mechanism about three
times in as many months, and had multiple firmware upgrades and so on. The
tapes are also delicate... If the tape header (the little metal hook) is
bent at all, then the drive mechanism reaches out to get it, and pulls it in
to the winder, and then doesn't ever come out, as it depends on the tape to
pull it back out again... Great drives otherwise though...
