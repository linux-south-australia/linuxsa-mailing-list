Re: config problems with a DX4-100

At 06:17 PM 1/7/01 +1030, Michael Kratz wrote:
>Hi all,
>
>i am trying to configure a DX4-100 with 24M of RAM as a webserver,
>I am having a couple of problems however:
>
>1. it can't find the network card. all I know is that the network card is a 
>10M isa card, it has a BNC and a RJ45 connector on it. how do I get Red Hat 
>6.2 to find it??

Well, you will have to know the type of card and the IO address it is
located at and the IRQ possibly also, although it is possible to probe the
card.

Assume it is an NE2000, then try the following:

  modprobe ne io=0x300

if that fails, try

  modprobe ne io=0x340

and walk through a few of the usual addresses for NE2000 cards on the ISA bus.

Once you have found the values, add the following lines to /etc/conf.modules:

   alias eth0 ne
   options ne io=<what you found above>

eg

   alias eth0 ne
   options ne io=0x340
