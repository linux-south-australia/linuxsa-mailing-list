Hi Richard,
    I have been away for a few days and only just checked my
mail so this may now be irrelevant, but. Try
Universal-Meyer. I think they are on the corner of Grand
Junction Rd. and Churchill Rd. They used to have an outlet
in the city, but I don't know if it is still there. They
specialise in all kinds of bolts and screws and that kind of
stuff, and they also used to carry some of those unusual
tools for dealing with them. The only thing I will warn you
about however is that while they are great as a supplier and
usually have heaps of stuff, they are not cheap. Failing
that I would try RadioSpares, they also carry alot of tools.
Hope this helps.
