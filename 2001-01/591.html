There's a project called SANE which adds support for scanners natively
in Linux. If you've got you're scanner setup then programs such as the
GIMP can speak to it in a similar fashion that Adobe Photoshop and other
professional imaging programs for other operating systems do...

Web Page: http://www.mostang.com/sane/

one, although I believe some parallel scanners do now work.
