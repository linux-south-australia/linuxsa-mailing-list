Re: mgetty & auto answer

On Tue, 9 Jan 2001, w0Ja wrote:

> Hi,
> I just installed mgetty, as i have agetty installed on my box, and i read up
> that mgetty is better to use for ppp connections?
> 
> One problem i havent been able to overcome is how to get the modem to pick
> up the call. I have looked through the modem-HOWTO and the serial-HOWTO and
> have only been able to find out that the command you set for your modem to
> auto answer after X dials is S0=X. I have no idea how to put this command to
> use though!

You don't want to do this. One of the points of mgetty is that it's
intelligent and it picks up the call itself. If the modem is set to
auto-answer and your computer isn't ready for the call, the caller isn't
going to get very far.

You might want to see if mgetty is saying something interesting in it's
logs when a call comes in. The most likely location for these is
/var/log/mgetty.*, where '*' is the name of your modem device.
