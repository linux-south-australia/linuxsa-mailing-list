Re: Forwarding email

Sam Silvester wrote:
> > Is it possible to forward the email destined for an address to another
> > email address, whilst still leaving a copy in the original account?

David Drury wrote:
> With sendmail and a .forward file

Also, with qmail, look in your ".qmail" file, and add:

  &user@domain.com

(Don't forget to leave what's there to ensure delivery to your local
mbox or Maildir still happens.)

See http://www.qmail.org/man/man5/dot-qmail.html for more info.
