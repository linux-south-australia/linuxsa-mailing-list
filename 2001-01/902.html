Re: AOL? or best ISP

People has this topic gone askew somewhat?

I think most have or know of people working within ISP's. I have a number 
and they all say the unlimited plans do not make them money if they are 
raped to the limit. But as it turns out I think 10% of them are and the 
remainder are reasonable and they at least do not lose money on them.

The mindset (in my case) is that I do not want the restrictions of time or 
download for the off chance I will need it perhaps once or twice a year. 
Time I will for sure data though is really kept within 600 mb a month. I 
think that's reasonable for most average consumers.

The mp3 heads who constantly steam stuff down get up into the multi-gb but 
across the whole it averages them back to under a gb in most cases.

I think an unlimited only plan from an ISP doesnt give them sufficient 
buffer money wise so they have their other plans for the simple I'll check 
my email once a day type stuff.
