readline-4.1-5.is86.rpm has libreadline.so.4.

libreadline.so.3 is in readline2.2.1-2.2.1-2.i386.rpm for RH7.0

This makes no sense to me either.  My (admittedly basic) math skills
lead me to believe that 2 != 3.

http://rpmfind.net can be a useful source of information on this stuff,
as it will let you type in a filename that you are missing (say
libreadline.so.3) and tell you the RPMs that you can find it in.
