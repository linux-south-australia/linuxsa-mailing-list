Upgrading to 2.4.0

Hi!

It looks like it the time to take systems from 2.2.X to 2.4.0 is getting
closer. I think LinuxSA should:

* produce a paper on the important things that will break if you do this
and how to fix it
  - important things are things that either cause the system to become
dysfunctional (eg: /bin/login fails) and/or present security problems
(eg: ipchains for 2.2.X doesn't work any more)

* put together a talk/presentation on this subject

Whilst I have two systems to `crash' I think others would like to know
what caveats and other hurdles might snag them if they try to test this
new beast out...
