Re: determining IP address of client

netstat -npt | grep ':22 ' | grep ESTABLISHED | awk {'print $5'}

will give you list of IPs (with ports) which currently connected to tcp port
22 on your box

--------------------------

netstat -npta | grep ':80 ' | grep LISTEN | awk {'print $7'}

will give you list PID/name of program which listening for packets on your
tcp port 80

netstat can show alot of things about ports, IPs and processes which using
them. Unfortunately netstat will not make some coffee! :)
