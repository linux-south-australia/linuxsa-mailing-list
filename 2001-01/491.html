RE: Disabling login on ttyS*

Friday, January 12, 2001, 10:46:32 AM, Peter wrote:

> Hi all
> I'm trying to set up a Sola ups & am having problems setting up comms to it.
> Both the Sola software & net ups tools wont talk to it.

I found I had to recompile the *nix Sola UPS software in Debug mode to
get  it  to  work  (note:  this prints all it's status messages to the
console,  BUT  you  can  avoid  this  by  starting it nohup'd and then
exiting that session).

> Sola's documentation mentions disabling logins on the port u have the ups
> connected to.
> How do i do this?

That  depends  on which distribution your using. You could take a peek
in  /etc/inittab  or  if  you  have one /etc/gettydefs (I think - SuSE
doesn't use this file)

Hope that helps...
