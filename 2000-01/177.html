Nick Morrison wrote:

> Subject: Y2K bug! Woohoo!

I had a low end Pentium acting as firewall/router that rolled
over correctly (as it would, being the system clock) but the
year went screwy on reboot.  Turns out the BIOS couldn't store
dates correctly outside the range 94-99.  "Aha!", thoughts I,
easy fix: patch hwclock with an offset to the year.  But as I
was reading the CHANGES file, I noticed that someone had already
come across the problem for the exact same BIOS (Award v4.50g).
I hate it when I'm pre-emptively out-thought like that ... :-)
