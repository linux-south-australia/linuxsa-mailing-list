Timothy Aslat wrote:

> Sam Silvester wrote:
> > I have a couple of old Olivetti M4's (486DX/2) that I had planned on
> > putting linux on to run as servers for dhcp and samba, but I can't work

Try holding down a bunch 'o' keys while it's doing the POST. It should
complain loudly about a keyboard error and *most* machines will allow you to
access the CMOS.
