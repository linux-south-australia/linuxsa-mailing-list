"A few other small applications" that add up to 300M?  I wonder what a big
application is.  :-)

Note that you need 300M of *virtual* memory.  Big deal.  Virtual sets
are generally much larger than working sets; the amount of virtual memory
needed is generally much larger than the amount that needs to be in RAM
at any one time.
