Re: SENDMAIL BROKE

Hi,

> All of a sudden, Sendmail has decided that "we don't relay". Netscape Mail under Linux works fine, but Eudora and Netscape mail on Windows don't. Telnetting to port 25 and trying to send mail by giving the server direct SMTP commands doesn't work either.
> 
> Anybody got any ideas? I remember looking for it in the Bat book a couple of months ago, but I don't think there was anything there.
> 

Ok, first if you are running redhat, and have used linuxconf to generate
your sendmail.cf file, scrap that, and go to www.sendmail.org, and read
how to create the m4 files, and use the sendmail-cf rpm to generate the cf
file.

If not, look at your sendmail.cf file and find out what file restricts
relaying, and look at the enteries in that file. Add in appropriate IPs,
hostnames or domains.
