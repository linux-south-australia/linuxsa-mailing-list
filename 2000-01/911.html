The motherboard is definantly your problem !

The MSI-Athlon board (in particular the AMD chipset) is very dodgy with
video cards. I had the exact same problems with Riva TNT-2's on this same
board in windows. In the end I gave up on using TNT-2's on this board. The
other cards which are really dodgy on these boards are S3 Savage based
boards. All 3DFX cards run beatifully on the board however !
