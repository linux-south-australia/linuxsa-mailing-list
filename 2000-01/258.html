is it just me, or is there IP address information missing from this.....

once you know the IP of your wireless interface, set the winXX box to
have an address in the same network then ping it.

ie., lets say your wired LAN in 10.0.0.x, and your ethernet IF is
10.0.0.1. Make your wireless IF 10.0.0.2, and your winXX box 10.0.0.3.
(netmask is 255.0.0.0) Once this is done, ping 10.0.0.2 from your winXX
box.

linux box from the winXX box, and other things like samba (winXX
filesharing etc)
