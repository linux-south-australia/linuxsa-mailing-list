Doh !!! This argument is getting boring.....

It's a very simple concept, and is not dependent on a year zero.  When you
were born you began living in the first year of your life, in your first
decade, in your first century, etc.....  When you reached your first
aniversary you were 1 year old - then straight away you began living in your
2nd year, etc...

Thus, on 01/01/1999 we started to live in the 2,000 year (forgetting any
additions/subtractions from the calendar in previous times) the final year
of the 20th century and the 2nd millenium.  At 01/01/2000 and one second we
began to live in the 2,001 year and thus the first year of the 3rd millenium
and the first year of the 21st century.
