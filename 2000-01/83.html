Re: Printing problems. "lpr:lp:unknown printer"

Hi Michael

You could try starting linuxconf as root. Then goto Control, Control
Panel and Control Service Activity and make sure 'lpd' is activated.
This will start the daemon on startup and restart linux.

Hope this helps
