open (e.g. 80 for HTTP), it says that this is
a serious vulnerability as hackers can you attack
you at this point.

It just has a look at ports 21, 25, 79, 80,
110, 143 adn that sort of stuff.

If you've got linux, this set of tests
is a big yawn.
