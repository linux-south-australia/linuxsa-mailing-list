On Sun, 23 Jan 2000, David Drury wrote:
> Hi,
> 
> Can someone tell me how to find which RPM identd is in under redhat. It
> isn't installed, and I want to install it.
> 
> There isn't an identd rpm BTW :)

netkit-base-0.11-5.i386.rpm  is what you are looking for.

in general, this is how you find out what rpm package is required inetd is
given as an example;

"locate inetd"

this will give you a list of various inetd names such as

/etc/inetd.conf     
/usr/sbin/inetd

type in

"rpm -qf  /usr/sbin/inetd" # (or any other of above)

netkit-base-0.11-5
