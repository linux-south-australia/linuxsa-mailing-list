Ok, your linux gateway needs to provide two functions to your wintel box.
A means to transfer packets from the PPP interface to the ethernet
interface, and trranslate IPs

Something to resolve names to IPs both inside and outside your network.

It looks like you've done DNS instide your network with a hosts file. This
is fine on a small network, but unworkable on a large.

You have two options.

Place the same enteries in the HOSTS file (located in the windows
directory), or setup a name server. For the second you'll require a
caching name server for external, and make it a primary for your local
network (the DNS HOWTO will tell you how to do this).

You simply then set your DNS entry in TCP/IP setup in windows to be your
linux box, not SENET.
