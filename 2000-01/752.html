Re: Self help

There is a whole group of computer users out there that have no interest in the
technology and typically use application tools such a wordprocessors and drawing
packages (such as an Art teacher and an Architect I know).  These people
typically opt for a turnkey Mac solution, since there focus is on the
application, not the technology for getting there.  If the needs of these
legitimate users are not met under Linux, then they will stay with there existing
solutions.
