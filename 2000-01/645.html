Re: refresh problems : black stuff

On Wed, Jan 19, 2000 at 09:52:48AM -0500, Daniel Moore wrote:

 > I installed RH 6.0 on a friends computer, everything is cool except the
 > Xwindows display seems to be having trouble refreshing. Where there
 > should be menu items there sometimes appears black boxes, or blank
 > rectangles (this occurs with gnome and e)...

This is a semi-common problem which usually indicates that one or more
hardware acceleration features X is trying to use aren't actually 
supported by your card.

The first place to start is to disable all hardware acceleration in your
XF86Config.  If that works, progressively turn-on acceleration features
until you've found the one that wrecks your display.  Consult the 
manpage for your particular server to find out what they are, and
read the release notes in /usr/X11R6/lib/X11/doc to find out if there
are any known problems/workarounds for your card.
