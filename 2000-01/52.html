Re: Strange kppp/rp3 differences

Hi - the Kppp in the latest KDE tells you to remove the lock in
/etc/ppp/options when you first boot it up - previous versions didnt -
it was in one of the readme's . Kppp does its own locking, as needed, so
it wont work properly with "lock" already in the options file.
