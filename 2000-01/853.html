Some Computer Associates products for Linux

Products of interest to be ported RSN are their popular
ARCserve network backup utility and their virus checker.

Both are a big win for people planning to replace their
WinNT or Novell file servers with Linux.

CA haven't caught the Linux/Open Source religion (like say
IBM or SGI).  This is reassuring in a sense: CA are porting
their products because they are the Linux market is now big
enough for them to ake money.
