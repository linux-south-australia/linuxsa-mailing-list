is one of those things that turns christianity into a religion. God
hates religion. Apologetics creates more religiousity. People debate
from their religious positions (Catholic, Protestant, Atheistic,
Scientific, Pure Logic, Agnostic, Linux, etc) and attack other religious
positions, hoping to shore up their own positions through their efforts.

validity of the Bible, and a million other trivialities. If you're a
Christian, do yourself and others a favour -- get off your religious
high horse, forget the debates, the theology, the intellectual
christianity, and get on with relating to the living God. If you're not
a Christian, do yourself a favour -- get off your religious high horse,
forget the debates, the theology, the intellectual christianity, and get
on with relating to the living God.
