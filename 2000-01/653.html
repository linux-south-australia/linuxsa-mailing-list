Nick,

You could try looking at http://www.tucows.com <http://www.tucows.com>;  in
the Windows 95/98 section. They have a section
covering server tools. In this section there are X-Windows servers. They
have three to choose from.

1)	MI/X which gets a four cow rating
2)	X-Win32 which also gets a four cow rating
3)	X-WinPro which gets a massive five cow rating

You can download them from this site and try them out.
