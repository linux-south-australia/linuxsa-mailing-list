Generally if there are no jumpers on the card, they should be auto sense,
however it is possible on some cards to turn off autosense, through the
Software configuration program. Sometimes there are also driver options.

If you no longer have the driver disk for the card try the 3com site (that
looks like a 3com driver), or other apropriate driver site if it isn't a 3
com card (www.windrivers.com contains links to all network card driver
sites on the web).
