Hi James

Which version of 'X' are you running?

These are my settings for XF86Config-4

Section "InputDevice"
	Identifier	"Mouse0"
	Driver		"mouse"
	Option		"Device"		"/dev/mouse"
	Option		"Protocol"		"IMPS/2"
	Option		"Emulate3Buttons"	"no"
	Option		"ZAxisMapping"		"4 5"
EndSection

With 'X' version 4 and above you don't need IMWheel.

Robyn
