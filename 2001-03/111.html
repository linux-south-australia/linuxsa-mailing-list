If you are looking for a linux friendly UPS I would suggest the APC range of
products. The APC products are a known brand with great reliability and they
offer linux software to perform an unattended shutdown and monitoring.

Try the APC site at http://www.apcc.com follow the links to the Australian
region and find your local stockists.
