interesting NT utils

Hi all

A few weeks back I posted regarding outputting the NT rights & permissions
on directories & files, no-one knew of any utils, but a few wanted me to let

them know if i found a solution
Being the pain in the rear that NT is, I copied the permissions with 
the time-honoured pen & paper method
Murphy's law being what it is, guess what i stumble across weeks after I
need
them, a utility that does just that

DumpSec dumps your file & directory securities to a file + there's also
some other utils which look to be very handy for dumping stuff from NT into 
files and/or databases if need be.

My next NT -> Samba migration is not going to be as time consuming :-)
