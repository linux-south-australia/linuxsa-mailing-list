Richard knows enough about computers to realise when the BIOS isn't
detecting the hard drive. He even says so in the sentence immediately above
yours.

> Maybe, if that doesn't work, you could install /boot + boot record on to
> either another partition (at the start of the disk) or put a small
> (100MB) hard drive in to hold the /boot partition and boot off of that.

This would probably be the quickest and simplest answer. Wouldn't even need
to be 100Mb, and old 20Mb one (IF you can find a reliable one!) would
suffice.

> > Has anyone seen this? Does anyone know of any BIOS updates for Award
