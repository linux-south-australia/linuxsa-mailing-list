Re: ADSL (residential) - why waste one's time or money?

Jason,

Please take this offline, as Simon suggested.

Simon has done a great job explaining the economics of
being an ISP.  If you don't get it, it's not because
Simon isn't being clear.

Basically a 'real' unlimited pipe to the fast ethernet
port on the back of your PC is going to set you back
about $100 per hour.

The Internet works because people are willing to
accept a small drop in response time to gain a level
of link sharing that will allow about 50,000 other
people to share that 100Mbps and split the cost
with you.

Unless an "unlimited" ISP is asking more than $70,000
per month you can be assured that there will always
be some method that requires you to coexist with the
other 49,999 users.
