I have a Miro PCTV (which is BT848) and it works nicely.  Is your
sound card working?  Try plugging something else into the line input
of your card.  Take a look in your audio mixer (or try xmixer) and
make sure that your line input is enabled and turned up.  And finally,
make sure that the audio is enabled in your TV software.

If all else fails you could always connect the output from your TV
card directly to your speakers...

HTH.
