Also try looking up LVMs (Logical Volume Managers) on your favourite search
engine.

As Richard suggests Logical Volume Management is designed for this, you can
shrink and grow volumes very easily and it works well. To use it you need
LVM support in your kernel (or as modules) and the LVM toolset from
http://linux.msede.com/lvm/
Make sure you use the toolset version that matches your kernel LVM version,
e.g kernel 2.4.2 has lvm_0.9.1_beta2 so you must use the beta2 toolset.
