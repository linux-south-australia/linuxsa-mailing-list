RE: Modem init strings

Andrew,

On every modem that I have ever seen the 'Set to answer on x Rings' 
command is ATS0=x where x is the number of rings before answering.
Set it to 0 to turn it off. But depending on your modem you mileage
may vary (but I seriously doubt it).
