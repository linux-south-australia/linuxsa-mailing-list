A month or so ago I received a port scan on the Ramen ports (21, 111,
515).  Just out of interest I pointed my web browser at the offending IP
number, and was greeted by a page substituted by the worm (that computer
had been infected).

So that's a possible way to check if it definitely was a Ramen scan on
your machine.
