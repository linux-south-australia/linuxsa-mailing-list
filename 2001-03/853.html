At 09:13 PM 3/20/01 +1030, Richard Russell wrote:
>Someone's looking for Linux trainers in Singapore... Maybe ESL too :)

Having just come back from LinuxWorld Singapore, and having conducted an
email conversation with the person in question and asked about these
matters in Singapore, I feel that there are a number of problems.

You will note that the mail message does not provide an email address,
which is significant, and the feedback I received in Singapore suggests
that this person might not be in a position to offer training contracts to
anyone.

My suggestion would be that you ask to see a contract and that you ensure
that any such contract has actual dates on which courses are to be
delivered and that there are penalty clauses in it (that compensate you for
any time and expenses incurred in preparing for a course that does not
eventuate).

Feel free to contact me for further details ...
