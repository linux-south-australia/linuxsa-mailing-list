A location following program

Hi,

It is relatively cheap to get a GPS and connection for a PC. You can even
get devices that are built into a PCMCIA card, I am told.

That being the case, it would be interesting to put together an application
that can locate where you are on a map.  Such applications exist for
Windows I am told.

The key to this application would be in getting a source of maps.

I am told that the South Australian has a DCDB that provides lots of
information, and it might be possible to generate the required info from
the DCDB.

Does anyone know if the DCDB is available and if it has the required
information?
