Xircom PCMCIA Card

Hey All,

I've got a Xircom Ethernet/Modem card. Seems to work fine -- PCMCIA
picks it up on boot with no worries. /dev/modem is successfully linked
to /dev/ttyS1 and the modem works like a charm.

The ethernet part also is picked up, seemingly with no dramas.

--- dmesg snip ---

xirc2ps_cs.c 1.31 1998/12/09 19:32:55 (dd9jn+kvh)
eth0: Xircom: port 0x3e0, irq 3, hwaddr 00:80:C7:7E:3F:DD
tty01 at 0x13f8 (irq = 3) is a 16450

--- end snip ---

However, when I setup the eth0 device I cannot get any data over the
link. ifconfig shows the device as up and running, but no data.

--- ifconfig snip ---

eth0      Link encap:Ethernet  HWaddr 00:80:C7:7E:3F:DD  
          inet addr:192.168.1.9  Bcast:192.168.1.255  Mask:255.255.255.0
          UP BROADCAST RUNNING MULTICAST  MTU:1500  Metric:1
          RX packets:0 errors:0 dropped:0 overruns:0 frame:0
          TX packets:11 errors:0 dropped:0 overruns:0 carrier:0
          collisions:0 txqueuelen:100 
          Interrupt:3 Base address:0x3e0 

--- end snip ---

Pinging doesn't work....

--- ping snip ---

[root@percible andrew]# ping -n 192.168.1.5
PING 192.168.1.5 (192.168.1.5) from 192.168.1.9 : 56(84) bytes of data.

--- 192.168.1.5 ping statistics ---
3 packets transmitted, 0 packets received, 100% packet loss

--- end snip ---

I'm using a 3Com in the mean time (which works perfectly :-), but I'd
like to get the Xircom working.

When it's in a Windows box, if I do some stuff like change it to memory
mapped mode it works without issue.

If anyone knows how to fix this I'd love to hear from you.
