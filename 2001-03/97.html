Ximian Evolution...

Just a quick note to anyone who is using Ximian's Evolution as their
mail client.

Yes, I know... It's still in development and has eaten my mail twice,
but I just love the look of it. And to be fair, I've not had much
problems since upgrading to the latest `Developer Preview'.

Being a big fan of what Ximian are doing, I downloaded and installed a
preview version of Red Carpet (Red Carpet is a Gnome-Based Package
Updated etc from Ximian).

After I installed it, I was going about my merry way with Evolution when
I tried to compose a message. I got an error message saying that `the
composer window could not be created'. After a bit of digging and
talking to someone at Ximian, I found out that the version of GTK-HTML
that Red Carpet installs is newer than the Evolution Snapshot version.
This meant that it Evolution couldn't create the composer window...

After removing Red Carpet and its version of GTK-HTML and reinstalling
the slightly older Evolution-Snapshot version, things are working again.

I'm not sure how many (if any) of you guys use Evolution, but I thought
I'd share this with you anyway in case you ran into a similar problem.
