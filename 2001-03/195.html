Yes.  But any reasonable judge would conclude that by posting it to a
public mailing list you're granting the public (or, at least, the members

It always has been.  There's actually nothing new here other than the
offense provision -- Copyright has *always* (for all intents and purposes)
been owned by the creator of an original work.

someone attempts to sue (or prosecute), so in the overwhelming majority
of cases it's a moot point.
