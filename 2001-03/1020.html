Re: Award BIOS from 1998 and 15GB drive problems

On 27 Mar 2001 09:14:17 +0900, Richard Sharpe wrote:
> I could actually install Linux on the 15GB drive, it is just that the BIOS
> could not detect it, so I could not boot Linux.

Isn't that something to do with not having lilo installed after cylinder
1024? I would have thought that as long as the BIOS could see the boot
sector, it should be fine.

Maybe, if that doesn't work, you could install /boot + boot record on to
either another partition (at the start of the disk) or put a small
(100MB) hard drive in to hold the /boot partition and boot off of that.

> Has anyone seen this? Does anyone know of any BIOS updates for Award that
> take it over the 8.4GB limit? 

A lot of the BIOS manufacturers don't actually distribute the BIOS
updates from their site. They generally have a message saying "Go to the
motherboard manufacturer's website and download it there". If it's not a
reputable (recognised brand) they often don't have websites which makes
downloading BIOS updates a tad hard.
