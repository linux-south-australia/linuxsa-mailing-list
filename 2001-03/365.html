[Article] New digital Internet/TV has "Linux inside"

Find below a link to an article on a new model 27-inch (68cm) digital TV, 
the Sylvania Model SPC2700iHD Internet/TV, which internally contains "a 
single-chip PC ... running on embedded Linux operating system".

Some of the key reasons quoted for selecting Linux were "...that its 
per-unit cost is less than with any other operating system, its strong 
support for Internet access functions, the fact that multimedia plug-ins 
are readily available, and ... frankly, (the) customers wanted it."

The full article is at:

http://netscape.zdnet.com/zdnn/stories/news/0,4586,2694183,00.html
