Re: Porno Sites

On Sun, Mar 25, 2001 at 03:34:43PM +0930, Mick Collins wrote:

 > Hello. I am currently setting up a internet cafe and am looking
 > for a command or something to limit or deny all access to porn sites.

If you really think this is possible, I can only suggest that you've
been reading the wrong minister's press releases.

 > I have currently read that you need some kind of file to read from
 > which sites it allows and deny's. I would be muchly appriciated if
 > someone could help me .

There is no way to help you;  There is no technological way to prevent
people from looking at porn.  No matter what list you install, they'll
still be able to view porn.  You simply will not be able to find all
the porn sites -- Not even the professionals at Censorware companies
know how to do that, so don't delude yourself into thinking that you'll
be able to make any meaningful difference.

If you really care about this (and think hard about whether or not you
do!), you should just angle the screens in your cafe so that the 
person behind the sign-in desk can see them.  That's the closest you're
going to get to anything resembling content control.
