It's doing the automated data completion.  You
can turn that off in Tools | Options | Text document |
Tables | Insert in tables | Number recognition.

You should also be pressing Shift+Enter to force a line
break,  Plain Enter is a paragraph end mark, which is
an entirely different beast.

There is an excellent Star Office book:

  M Koch and S Murray with W Roth
  Special Edition Using StarOffice
  Que (Macmillan), Indianapolis, USA.  1999.

Worth every cent.

I have found Star Office to be an OK word processor,
certainly the best for Linux.  The flaws that annoy
me also tend to exist in Microsoft Word.  Word and
Star Office are different products and some things,
like paragraph numbering, work entirely differently.
In these cases the book is really useful.
