[ANNOUNCE] LinuxSA March Meeting

Hi all,

The LinuxSA March 2001 Meeting is on next Tuesday...

Here are the usual details:

  When:   7:00pm (doors open 6:45pm) on
          Tuesday, 20th March, 2001
  Where:  2nd Floor
          Compaq Computer
          139 Frome Street
          Adelaide SA
  Cost:   FREE
  Who:    Anyone and everyone.
          No pre-registration necessary.

Presentation:

  Mel Boyce will be giving a talk on the GIMP, covering:
  - Can the GIMP replace commercial tools?
  - Comparing the GIMP with commercial equivalents.
  - Scripting, and batchmode in the GIMP.
  - Basic tools and filters.
  (more detail at http://www.linuxsa.org.au/meetings/)

Extras:

  As per usual, if you are having a problem with your Linux
  installation, feel free to bring your machine, but please warn
  organisers@linuxsa.org.au beforehand.

For more information:

  Email:        organisers@linuxsa.org.au
  Web Page:     http://www.linuxsa.org.au/
  Mailing List: linuxsa@linuxsa.org.au
