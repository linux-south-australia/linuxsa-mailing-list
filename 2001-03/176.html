Re: copyrighted email

Jason Tan wrote:
> Perhaps to make it all explicit the list owner might add soem sort of
> discllaimer to the footer?

Best tactic is simply to put the terms in the e-mail received
from the "SUBSCRIBE" message.  Mention the archive, suggest
that they grant unlimited rights of reproduction in electronic
form.  Invite them to unsubscribe if they cannot agree to the
conditions.

Note that LinuxSA doesn't want to own the copyright, this
would cause other problems :-)

Nick Morrison asked:
> When I post to the LinuxSA mailing list, do I still own the copyright on
> the message?

Yes.

> Does LinuxSA own it?

No, incorporated or otherwise.

The members of the mailing list have some rights because they
are the addressees.

Everyone else has some fair use rights (short quotes, criticism,
review, etc).

> The contents of the archive... who owns it all?

Each poster owns their message.  This *is* a problem for an archivist,
and the changed Act has some new rules making their life easier.
