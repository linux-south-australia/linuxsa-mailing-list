Re: Squid port problems

Phil Hutton wrote:

> I have a squid cache that dies regularly (almost every day).

> Mar 12 20:40:40 proxy squid[676]: Squid Parent: child process 679 exited due to signal 11
> Mar 12 20:40:40 proxy kernel: Unable to handle kernel NULL pointer dereference at virtual address 00000004

Signal 11 is usually a hardware-related fault, often to do with a
faulty/overclocked CPU, or bad ram. It usually only occurs during a
processor-intensive task like compiling the kernel. There were a series
of AMD K6 CPU's recalled because of a CPU fault that exhibit this
behaviour.
