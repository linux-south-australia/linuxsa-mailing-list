IMHO, yes, there is a (smallish) market for such a product. The fact that
Dell & friends are selling this product demopnstrates this market. I think

installation... Of course, the market decides these things, but if a small
retainler was to be able to install Linux, or perhaps dual boot PCs without
much more effort than a Windows install, I would think they would get _some_
sales...
