Thanks, you hit it on the head that I'm after something for a 1RU
application, there are lots of servers out there with 2 ethernet ports
on the motherboard (cobalt, sun, hp, intel) but plain motherboards with
this feature are scarce.

As for the D-link card, it's worth looking into VLAN's if the goal is to
get more ethernet interfaces on a linux box. By having a single ethernet
card act as a vlan trunk, you can make each port on a vlan-capable
switch look like an ethernet interface to the linux box. Handy for
accounting for usage when you don't/can't trust IP addresses, or need
all data to pass via a central point to reach any other system.
