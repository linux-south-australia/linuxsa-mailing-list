Re: tar '.*' files

update:
just tested it and the full command that I found worked was,

tar -czvf test.tar.gz .* * --exclude .. --exclude . --exclude test.tar.gz

the last exclude is only needed if the resulting archive is in the same 
directory, otherwise it gets into a loop.
