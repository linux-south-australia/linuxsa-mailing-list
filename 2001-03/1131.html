NDS Authentication, SQUID Quota

Hey All,

We've written and been running our own NDS Authentication system for
Linux (PAM Module and Associated programs) and also Internet Quota
Support for SQUID (SQL Database based + Web frontends, etc).

We've been running it for about 12 months now without too many hiccups,
and I've finally got around to cleaning up the code tree(s) and putting
it all into CVS.

It's all OpenSource (except a NLM that is closed-source Freeware) and
we'd encourage people who are interested to have a look at it.

I know that there are others out there in Education, who like us, may
benefit from this. I've setup a mailing list for anyone who is
interested in finding out more about it and how it's working.

Email me off-list for details for Mailing list and CVS.
