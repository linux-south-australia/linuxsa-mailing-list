Re: mailing from within a perl script

#!/usr/bin/perl

#open a pipe to a program, you can then just print whatever to stdin of
#that program
open (MAILER,"|/bin/mail -s");

print MAILER "$your_message";

I think that should do it.

man perlfunc look for "open" and "print", for more details.
