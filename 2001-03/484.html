Re: monitor-mounted keyboard holders

If you just want out of the way storage, you could use self adhesive
velcro.

Velcro is my friend.
Just put velcro onthe appropiriate pllaces and then keep the keyboard in
top of the monitor.

A quick rip and you can pull it down for use.

Also great for cable mess, instead of cable ties, particularly where you
are constantly adding to a removing from looms.

Alternativley you get get one of those lovely junction box thiongos where
one mointor and keyboard can be used to service several machines.
