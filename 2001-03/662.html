RE: Canon Digital Camera

I have a Canon S10. It has Serial, USB, and I have a USB card reader, and a
PCMCIA card reader for my notebook.

The connection from the camera to the computer didn't work with Gphoto last
time I tried, but the serial did. I never have tried the USB card reader,
but I would be inclined to say it would work under a 2.4 kernel. I have the
PCMCIA card reader working beautifully under 2.2 and 2.4 kernels. I just
mount it as a VFAT partition and is fantastic !

IXUS is pretty much the same as the Sx0 Series cameras, only i don't think
it comes with the serial interface cable.... and of course it's about half
the size.
