As far as I can tell you have your hard drive (ide) set to lba ? yes? Can you 
boot from the floppy created during installation? If you don't have one make 
one. In /etc/lilo.conf change the line that says 'linear' to 'lba32' (without 
the '''s) as root and run lilo again and reboot.
