Re: ADSL (residential) - why waste one's time or money?

David,

Look - don't get me wrong.  I have seen your set up and I am not in any way criticising ISPs which I view as victims - not perpetrators.  If I offended you - I apologise without reserve.  I did state - perhaps with not enough emphasis - that it is not smaller ISPs or even larger ones I am upset with - it is the backbone maintainer/s.  It is not Connect's fault they have to charge this.  Whose fault is it?  I think we all know.

It is the government and the backbone providers I am disappointed with.

With record profits and ADSL coming out in the early 1990s - Telstra decided to pump up its share price rather than invest in infrastructure and now they are only just starting to take their copper network seriously.

The government - for economic mis-management.  Our dollar is so pathetically low - that I am amazed any ISP can afford the equipment to set up at all!!

Our dollar is low because we are a third world commodity based economy with first world living styles. No one can tell me that the government never saw this coming - never considered a cut in R&D concessions a mistake - never considered wheat and other farming subsidies over communications infrastructure a mistake.  They were winning the vote and to hell with the country.

Our businesses and consumers pay ridiculous prices for hardware and software that never even works properly (Unix variants excepted of course).

Yeah I know that it as not as bad as it seems - I should be more optimistic - I should be more sympathetic and I am willing to stand corrected on everything that I am mistaken about.

But I am very disappointed that - for whatever reason - Australia is being ripped off - and it never had to be this way.

We have subsidies for an industry that is a hundred years old - why not subsidies or even tax concessions for a totally new industry - ISPs?

Perhaps we need a National Techonology Foundation to lobby against the National Farmers Federation for subsidies and tax concessions?

No one can tell me that we should just sit back and accept what is being thrown to us.  There must be something that can be done.
