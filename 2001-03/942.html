Re: AMD vs Intel

"Freeman, Peter (ERHS)" wrote:
> 
> > Don't the Athlons run hotter than an equivalent Intel Processor? That
> > alone is a pretty good reason to use Intel in a server.
> 
> They do, marginally, but thats what heat sinks & fans are for :-)

Moving parts are the most likely thing to break and cause server
downtime. Generally speaking, devices with no moving parts are more
reliable than those that do have them. Adding more fans is rarely
desireable in a mission-critical environment when they can be avoided.

IO throughput is often more important than CPU grunt in many server
applications, so the extra speed of the Athlon isn't a big issue. I've
got some older AMD processors in a few servers, at the time I think they
had less heat issues than the Intel offerings.
