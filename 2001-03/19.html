I was under the impression that it was actually a worm and not a virus.
That Ramen actually broke into machines the same way a script kiddie
would and used that machine to break into others. It exploits various
known problems on Unix (in particular Linux) systems. The easiest way of
not being attacked by such software is to (a) turn off all unnecessary
services and (b) update software whenever a problem is found (doesn't
take long and doesn't happen very often ;-)).
