Red Hat -Open Source Develop Deploy Manage- Seminar Series March 2001

I'm not sure that most people here fit into the category of "curious
about the hype around Linux", but this might be interesting to some...

-----8<-----8<-----8<-----8<-----8<-----8<-----8<-----8<-----8<-----8<-----
* Are you curious about the hype around Linux?

* Are you interested in open source software in commercial
  environments?

* Do you want to reduce your IT costs?

If your answer to any of the above questions is "yes", then attend

        Red Hat's Asia-Pacific Seminar Series
        OPEN SOURCE: DEVELOP, DEPLOY & MANAGE

        Brisbane        Tuesday 6th March 2001
        Sydney          Wednesday 7th March 2001
        Melbourne       Thursday 15th March 2001
        Darwin          Tuesday 3rd April 2001
        Perth           Wednesday 4th April 2001
        Adelaide        Wednesday 5th April 2001

        All seminars run from 7:30am - 10:30am.

By attending this seminar, you will receive an overview of Red Hat's
open source solutions - including the multi-award winning Red Hat
Linux.  Also, you will gain insights from Red Hat technical staff
about how to successfully implement open source solutions to power
your IT and e-commerce infrastructure.

Seminar highlights include:

- The open source development & support methodologies

- How Red Hat provides services & support throughout the region

- Case studies of successful "real-world" deployments of open source
  solutions

- Insights into the Red Hat product range: Red Hat Network, embedded
  technologies, and Red Hat Linux

- How to gain the skills and support to ensure a successful deployment

You will also get the chance to meet and ask questions of Red Hat
staff and partners.


WHO SHOULD ATTEND

This seminar is ideal for IT managers, system administrators, system
integrators and software developers who want to take advantage of the
tremendous benefits provided by open source solutions.
