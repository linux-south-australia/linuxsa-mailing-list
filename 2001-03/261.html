Same problem here.

I purchased a Sola 325 SOLELY because they promised time and time again that they support Linux / Unix.  What a load of crap.  The cable did not work and they advised me to pull out the plug and play pin as "oh yeah - we have had some trouble with that".  I would not have minded had they included information with the UPS or told me first - it is their responsibility - not mine - to ensure that people are aware of this.

I have also inquired about using the advanced features of the UPS - mains line information and battery time, UPS state, etc, being read by Linux - they never got back to me.

Why are Linux users regarded as second class citizens?

On a side note - I work for a large multinational corporation (EDS).  I asked around and it turns out that EDS will not use Sola owing to software issues - they much prefer APC owing to better Unix support.
