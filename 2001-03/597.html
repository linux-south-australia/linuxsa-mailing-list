Pay per MB (was: Unlimited Traffic (was Re: iPrimus ADSL))

On a slightly different topic;
It is cost effective for all ISPs to offer only a pay per megabyte
account?
This would stop income based on those who pay by the hour and chat by the
hour using a lot of time but little bandwidth. Do those people offset the
cost of the download leaches? Would the pay per MB effectively cover
investment in equipment in the long term as well as support costs? Or
would an effective model be cost per MB plus an extra cent per MB?

Are there any usage patterns of users that can give a view into the
average usage of bandwidth for all users?

Note, I aggree that unlimited bandwidth is silly. It would be like going
to an all-you-can-eat restraunt with a bottomless pit for a stomach. They
would not stay in business.
