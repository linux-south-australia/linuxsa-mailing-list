Re: DNS Question (Kind Of)

Changing the DNS won't help with the redirection of
www.original.com/website to www.anotherdomain.com

If you want say,
www.redhat.com/linux to take you to www.linux.org
but www.redhat.com to keep you within the redhat.com domain then you can't
acheive what you are trying to do by modifying DNS entries, and will have
to use some of server/software solution.
