Re: Hard drive optimisation

Ian!

> Does it make any difference if I have the hard drive
> on secondary master rather than primary?

Apparently sticking a UDMA 33 drive on the same channel as a UDMA 66
drive will drop ALL drives to UDMA 33. Lookup "Greg Lehy UDMA" at the
list archives.
 
> I still have Windows which I use about every 6 months
> when I feel like playing a game on the primary hard
> drive.  Can't I relegate it to a secondary master?

You can however you need to fiddle with lilo.conf (or GRUB). Here's what
I've done:

 
other=/dev/hdc1
   label=win
   table=/dev/hdc
   map-drive=0x81
      to=0x80
   map-drive=0x80
      to=0x81

Essentially that swaps the two channels over.

As for your log files:

* turn on UDMA
 - it's highly unlikely to "bugger" up on a modern system
 - I turn mine on in /etc/rc.d/init.d/rc.local

* try turning on unmaskirq
 - man hdparm
 - I think the option is -u or -U

* investigate I/O support
 - I don't know enough about it to make a recommendation, but someone
else might like to comment on whether changing the default form 16-bit
to 32-bit would make a difference.

Hope this helps :-)
