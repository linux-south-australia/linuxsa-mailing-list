Hi there,
    I would recommend contacting Pacific Dunlop Batteries or
there is also a place that was on Pulteney St in Adelaide
who refurbished all kinds of batteries. These places can do
all sorts of things to help you and they are reasonably
cheap and reliable. Pacific Dunlop used to be able to "deep
cycle" a rechargeable battery that was dead or dying for
about $10 and resurrect it. If it didn't work, it was only
$10, if it did, it saved you about $80 for a brand new one.

    Both these places repack batteries and may turn 2 into 1
for you at minimal cost. Hope this helps.
    Cheers!
