Re: OFF TOPIC: Telstra ADSL problems?

Telstra's ADSL dose seem to be having problems, but they don't seem to be
international networking issues.If I do a traceroute from my ADSL connection
and my Telstra Bigpond Direct connection, they use the same international
link. the modem's traceroute gets to slashdot, but the ADSL connection keeps
getting "request timed out" after the second international hop. Like I said,
they use the same internation link (203.50.113.18 -> 166.49.254.141). Also,
looking through bigpond's newsgroups, a lot of people are complaining about
the problems, some cable users saying they're not effected (some are, i
think), and they say people using optus aren't effected.

Anyway, if you make your browser use a proxy (i'm using
adelaide.cache.telstra.net:3128) everything will work (or at least it has
for me and several others).
