ADSL (residential) - why waste one's time or money?

I received their terms and conditions document today.

250Mbyte limit?  Are these people SERIOUS?!

Get stuffed!  I don't give a damn what people say about business models and so on.  There is no way I am paying for something that is fast - yes - but only clocks up 18c / 10 seconds or something when I want to listen to online radio or whatever.

This is just complete bullshit.  No wonder Australia is such a backward country.

Primus now has a 1000Mbyte limit.  I am so glad I did not sign up - as then they would have changed the conditions on me and I would have been stuck with a crappy contract for 12 months.

I have just had it with the whole home retail Internet industry in Australia.

The whole idea of having ADSL at home is to listen to radio and download films, etc.  Otherwise why should I bother upgrading from a dialup connection?

ADSL as it just stands is just completely pointless unless you are a milionaire who can afford to clock up the equivalent of a phone call to Ghana peak time!

It is not a FINITE resource - it is a resource that is only limited by bandwidth and infrastructure costs.  And at the moment - bandwidth and infrastructure costs are too high to justify ADSL at home.

Get real - I guess I will stick with my trust modem until ADSL is truly obsolete and something better comes along - perhaps when I am retired and over the whole thing anyway.
