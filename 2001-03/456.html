I said that I was after a server, not a hobby :)

With all due credit to the guys who put it together, some 486 systems out
there are now 10 years old and at the end of the CPU's life .. that is if
the CMOS battery hasn't leaked all over the motherboard yet. As such, they
have no place in an application that requires reliable servers. I also
wouldn't recommend production servers based on hand-soldered components and
cut-to-size circuit boards to anyone either.

The Sun Netra X1 servers look like the best bet for 1RU at the moment .. at
$AU2050 in the Sun catalogue they're an excellent deal for the supplied
features, although they might lock you into an upgrade path that costs an
order of magnitude more.
