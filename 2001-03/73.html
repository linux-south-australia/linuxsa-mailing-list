RE: LinuxSA, Organisers and incorporation

I still think that there is space and justification for us incorporating
separately. We can link in with AUUG as much as we want, but IMHO, we won't
compete, we will complement. I see no disadvantage to doing it ourselves,
particularly if we can arrange special membership of AUUG for our members,
and plenty of advantage to the continuity and independence in incorporating
separately.
