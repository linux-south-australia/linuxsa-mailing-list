This is where I can relate this flame war to Linux - this is something we should ALL care about and few of us seem to be paying serious attention to this.  Why are public institutions investing so much money in to propriatry infrastructure when there are cheaper and more reliable alernatives such as Linux - is it a cost or marketing issue?

For example - if I want to convince a business that an x-server server based desktop solution is the way to go to avoid upgrading obsolete desktop hardware - what is the correct approach - is this technically and financially feasible?
