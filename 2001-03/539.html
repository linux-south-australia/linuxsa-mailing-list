Until recently, I've been using Redhat and Mandrake Linux distros
on a Cyrix MII-233 and MII-300 for about 3 years, no problems.

I also had similar problems at various times with hangs during
installation, or straight out crashes. Turned out to be dodgy
RAM chips - ran perfectly as soon as I replaced them.

crap, and trying to do anything complex (playing MP3s, encoding
MP3s, etc...) would bring the machine to a stand still. Well, made
it rather tedious to use anyway.
