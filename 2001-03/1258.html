> I have a dilemma. I want:

> * http://www.originaldomain.com/website/

> To redirect to:
> 
> * http://www.anotherdomain.com/website/
> 
> It's web pages so I can use a header redirect, however I have no control
> on the "originaldomain.com" except to say that I have ftp access. It's

Do you have control over the domain? ie, Can you edit the zone records?
If you can, you could just edit them so that x.x.x.x (IP of
www.originaldomain.com) resolves to the IP of www.anotherdomain.com.
Failing that you can use META tags and Header redirects to send them to
another page.
