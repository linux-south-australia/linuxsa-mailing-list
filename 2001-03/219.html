Re: iPrimus ping packet loss

Hi Mike!
We discussed your friend's link over the phone whilst I was still in
Adelaide.

iPrimus' intranet seems fine.
If I dialup to iPrimus in Adelaide & ping my ADSL host in Sydney, it is
quite good.
But connecting from outside their intranet, e.g. from Telstra Bigpond
cable, its garbage.

Would you agree that:
1)
20-30% packet loss is unacceptable for a broadband connection.
2)
This much packet loss is likely to cause non-cacheable web-pages to be
unreliable downloading?
Such pages would include on-line forms such as found on web-based email
accounts.
3)
It is not unreasonable to expect iPrimus to rectify this problem
considering equivalent ISPs can achieve 0% packet loss under the same
conditions?
