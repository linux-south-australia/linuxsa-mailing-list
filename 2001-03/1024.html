Re: Award BIOS from 1998 and 15GB drive problems

Richard Sharpe wrote:
> 
> Hi,
> 
> I have a machine that I just put back in use, an AMD K6-2/400 with an Award
> BIOS from 1998.
> 
> It could not probe the 16GB IDE disk I put in it, but was fine with an
> 8.4GB drive.
> 
> I could actually install Linux on the 15GB drive, it is just that the BIOS
> could not detect it, so I could not boot Linux.
> 
> Has anyone seen this? Does anyone know of any BIOS updates for Award that

I don't know the exact BIOS that you refer to, but I believe some of the
late versions of LILO (and possibly GRUB) can boot past the 8.4GB limit.
Depending on your situation, you may also be able to partition the disk
to keep the kernel below the 8.4GB mark.
