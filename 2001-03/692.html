RE: CF Card Readers for Linux (Was Re: Canon Digital Camera)

On 20 Mar 2001 19:06:29 +1100, Adam W wrote:

> You can always go out and purchase one of those CF to IDE interfaces... and
> i'm guessing it would just mount as a hard drive??

That's what this little USB CF Reader allowed me to do, mount it as a
hard disk-

insmod usb-storage
mount -t msdos /dev/sda1 /mnt/cf

When you insmod usb-storage, it comes up with the SCSI device listing
thing that Linux does, telling you what the SCSI device is. I assume
this would change to appropriately if you already have a sda1.

> simple.. dont know how much it costs though..

Yep! Very. It makes getting images off a camera a lot faster. I used to
use a serial cable for the Kodak DC210+Zoom camera that I used, which
took *ages*. The USB CF reader took about 3 seconds to copy off 3
images. That makes it a lot more barable.
