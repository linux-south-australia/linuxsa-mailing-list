Matthew Geddes wrote:
> 
> Does anyone who followed this link
> (http://www.it.fairfax.com.au/e-commerce/20010226/A25226-2001Feb26.html)

Look for the

  Copyright Amendment (Digital Agenda) Bill 2000

and its explanatory memoranda.  It amends the Copyright
Act 1968 (Cth).

All of which are available from both Parliament House

 http://www.aph.gov.au/

and the Attorney-General's Department law database

 http://scaleplus.law.gov.au/

(click on Popular Acts | Copyright and you'll find
that this copy of the Act is already marked up with
the amendment).
