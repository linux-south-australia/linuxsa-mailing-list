I found a USB card reader attached to a Win98 box and attached it to my
RH-7 Development server. Magically, it works! It comes up as a SCSI
device (sda1).

All I had to do was:

insmod usb-storage
mount -t msdos /dev/sda1 /mnt/camera

Voilla! The files were nice and quick to transfer. A lot faster than
that dodgy serial cable that I used to use.

For anyone that's interested, it worke for me. The card reader was made
by SanDisk (http://www.sandisk.com/cons/imagemate-USB-main.htm).
