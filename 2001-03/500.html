There are two CD's that contain the binary distribution, and you need
the files from both.  I'm afraid you'll have to copy the hierarchy of
files from both CD's to the same directory on your disk.  Alternatively
install 2 CD drives and union mount them both (Probably only the BSD's
offer union mounts.)
