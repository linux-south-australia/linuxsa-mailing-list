in rc.local (or whatever)
--- cut here ---
/usr/sbin/pppd call isp
reconnectscript &
--- cut here ---

pppd detaches from control tty and thus allows to rc.local run further but
reconnectscript don't and your rc.local waits for reconnectscript
termination. As I see from script itself it will take too long to terminate
(or even it will not terminate at all if your pppd will reconnect
sucessfully) so you need to detach it manually by applying & at the end of
the command.
