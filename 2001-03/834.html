HORRAY - Connection Speed in PPPD

Horray,

After ages buggering around and searching for solutions I finally found out 
how to get the damn DCE speed from the modem to be displayed by PPPD

for those that want to know how to do it, add a "w1" into your modems init 
string...

example:
Mar 24 02:08:35 <hostname> chat[31310]: send (atm1w1dt0812345678^M)
Mar 24 02:08:35 <hostname> chat[31310]: expect (CONNECT)
Mar 24 02:08:35 <hostname> chat[31310]: ^M
Mar 24 02:09:01 <hostname> chat[31310]: atm1w1dt0812345678^M^M
Mar 24 02:09:01 <hostname> chat[31310]: CARRIER 45333^M  <---[HERE]---<-
Mar 24 02:09:02 <hostname> chat[31310]: ^M
Mar 24 02:09:02 <hostname> chat[31310]: PROTOCOL: LAP-M^M
Mar 24 02:09:02 <hostname> chat[31310]: ^M
Mar 24 02:09:02 <hostname> chat[31310]: CONNECT
Mar 24 02:09:02 <hostname> chat[31310]:  -- got it
Mar 24 02:09:02 <hostname> chat[31310]: send (^M)
Mar 24 02:09:02 <hostname> pppd[31309]: Serial connection established.
Mar 24 02:09:02 <hostname> pppd[31309]: Using interface ppp0
Mar 24 02:09:02 <hostname> pppd[31309]: Connect: ppp0 <--> /dev/ttyS1
Mar 24 02:09:07 <hostname> kernel: PPP BSD Compression module registered
Mar 24 02:09:07 <hostname> kernel: PPP Deflate Compression module registered
Mar 24 02:09:07 <hostname> pppd[31309]: Unsupported protocol 0x8207 received
Mar 24 02:09:07 <hostname> pppd[31309]: local  IP address 0.0.0.0
Mar 24 02:09:07 <hostname> pppd[31309]: remote IP address 0.0.0.0

BTW does anyone happen to know what "Unsupported protocol 0x8207" means??
