RE: Dual ethernet cards and Firewalling (I think)

I am currently using the dlink DI-704 cable/dsl gateway, and its a great
solution for the average home user who wants a simple, secure way to share
a connection.  It can share a modem or ethernet connection, and also
supports PPPoE.  I have trouble though when it comes time for me to
initiate a connection from my machine (eg, DCC chat/send on irc).  And
whlie my connection itself is pretty flaky, the gateway seems to crash on
its own periodically, and I usually end up cycling the power.  IT also can
only forward 10 ports to internal machines.  Not nearly enough IMO.

Thats why i built a new dedicated linux box and bought a switch.  Now I
hafta find time to config it... :/
