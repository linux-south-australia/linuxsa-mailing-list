Will Linux do well in a recession

Richard, that is complex many faceted question!

I think it will be a great thing!  All the hype surrounding IT will die off and people will become a little more realistic.  Costs will need to be saved and people will pay less attention to the worlds greatest marketing firm - Micro$oft - and look to cheaper more reliable alternatives such as Linux.

May Linux companies will die off - but the ones that remain will have proven business models which we can all learn from.

It will be hard - although there is reason for optimism.
