[OT]: Spacewalk today (where to watch)

Hi all, this is off topic but I thought I would post it anyway, today as 
everyone knows, Andy Thomas, goes into space. Those that want to watch his 
spacewalk live it can be seen through NASA TV on the web.

For general info and reports go to:
http://spaceflight.nasa.gov

for NASA TV from NASA (windows media player) go to:
http://spaceflight.nasa.gov/realdata/nasatv/index.html (28.8k & 56k)

for NASA TV from U.S.A. (real player) go to:
http://www.unitedspacealliance.com/live/NASATV.htm (28.8k & 56k)

and finally the best source IMHO
from broadcast.com (windows media player) go to:
http://www.broadcast.com/learning_and_education/science/space/nasa/nasa_television/ 
(28.8k, 56k, 100k & 300k) very nice with broadband at 300k

Yes I know that most of these can only be watched though windows media 
player and there are bound to be people that are unhappy about this but 
please don't blame me, I just thought that some of the people on this list 
might have the time to see this milestone today, the first Australian and 
the first South Australian in space.


Regards,
