Ron,

Like I said before I a flamed to death - I am not angry at smaller or even the larger ones such as Primus and Connect.  I am a little more understanding that I once was.

However - I still don't understand why out of my pay check every month - some of my income goes to subsidise farming and mining somewhere.  Not that it makes any difference any way because the steel comes back as cars, etc, etc.

I don't understand why there is not more pressure to subsidise or at the very least give tax breaks to this emerging and still oh so fragile industry.

Why is there not more lobying?  Not more letters to the papers?  I see plenty about the plight of farmers and the occasional one about our right to online privacy - but zilch on subsidies and tax concessions for ISPs and communication infrastructure expenditure.  Especially with our dollar so low.
