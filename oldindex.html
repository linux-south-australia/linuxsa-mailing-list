General Information

The LinuxSA Mailing List is the main method of communication between LinuxSA members. Meeting announcements, Linux announcements, Linux help, and general chit-chat can usually be found here.

Subscribing

To subscribe to the LinuxSA mailing list, send a message to linuxsa-request@linuxsa.org.au with a subject of "subscribe". The body of the message is ignored.

Unsubscribing

To unsubscribe from the LinuxSA mailing list, send a message to linuxsa-request@linuxsa.org.au with a subject of "unsubscribe". The body of the message is ignored.

Posting

To post to the LinuxSA mailing list, send your message to linuxsa@linuxsa.org.au.

Important Notes

  • Do not send subscription requests or posts from a root account (even if you change the From line in your header). The listserver will ignore any requests from root and will not bounce the message in any way.

  • If more than three messages sent to your account bounce, you will be automatically removed from the list and you will have to resubscribe.

  • Postings to the mailing list are only accepted from your subscribed address. This is to reduce the likelyhood of spam making it on to the list.

Digest List

A digest version of the list is available. To subscribe or unsubscribe, follow the instructions above but use the request address of linuxsa-digest-request@linuxsa.org.au instead of linuxsa-request@linuxsa.org.au. You use the same address to post to the list.
