When you go to Helix GNOME I'd seriously consider dropping enlightenment for
sawfish (the GNOME window manager).  Sawfish is quite configurable, but with a
small footprint while still being very nice.  I use sawfish exclusively on the
desktop with Helix GNOME now.  I've weaned myself off enlightenment altogether
and saved myself some RAM.
