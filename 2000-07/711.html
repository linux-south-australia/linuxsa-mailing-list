Installfest: The last 1%

All the LinuxSA people and Installfest organisers did a good job. No need
to state the obvious, perhaps :-) But to get the most benefit out of all
this excellent work, a tiny little bit more needs to be done.

All the material needs to be in one place on the LinuxSA site - brochures,
event pictures, howtos, the lot. Even the cgi scripts used for registering
and submitting other information. Mark has put his photos up, but everyone
else should contribute theirs to.

To reasons for this:

  1) I did some research for David Newall before the event, and I know 
     that there is material here which would easily make the best 
     installfest information site anywhere. In fact combined with the
     existing two or three installfest HOWTOs it would be a real 
     example.

  2) It will greatly assist the people covering the event in the media,
     which could attract more help in any future events that all you
     hardworking LinuxSA organisers might arrange

Regards

Dan
