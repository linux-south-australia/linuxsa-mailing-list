RE: Shared task list - Exchange server replacement?

Exchange server has the option of allowing a web
access frontend.  

We've got it working here at FMC.  You can access all
your mail and calendar and tasks (or most of it I
think) and such like through your browser.  

The only problem we've been having here is with some
people not being able to access mail attachments, they
were on the server, just not accessable to the clients
(the problem was with IE4, a change to IE 5 fixed it).


I'm not sure how well it works with Netscape (never
mind Linux Netscape) but you could give it a go.

Good Luck. (P.S. I haven't actually used it myself but
the tech support guy used it while he was here)

Damien.
