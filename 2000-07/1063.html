Re: Database Query

Perhaps you might like to look at Interbase? (www.interbase.com)

It's not open source, but will be "real soon now" (The open source release
of IB6 is meant to happen next week, I believe)

It's easy to install, is ANSI92 SQL compliant, and has a great developer
community (mailing lists at www.mers.com)

There are also versions available for other OSes, including Windows.
