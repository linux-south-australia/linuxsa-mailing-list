Have you tried asking help from the debian-user@lists.org.au mailing
list?  In my experience they are generally pretty helpful?  Indeed, it
was one of the things which attracted me to Debian.

Warning --- the list has a lot of traffic, so if you subscribe, make
sure you are willing to cope with large volume.  Perhaps the thing to
do at this stage would be just to email to the above address without
subscribing.  Explain in your message that you aren't actually
subscribed; that you haven't used Debian before and are trying to get
your ethernet card recognised.  Hopefully you will get some useful
feedback.  (Occasionally I need to send a repeat email because it gets
lost in all the volume.)  Anyway, in general they are a pretty helpful
lot --- the Debian user community.
