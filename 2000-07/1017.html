Win4lin has been described as Linux with training wheels. It operates from
within the dos environment. This makes it ideal for windows refugees needing to
test out what all the fuss is about. For anyone who's so much as 6 months into
Linux proper it has nothing to offer, but, as said, it's a perfectly good (and
creative) introduction into Linux for the refugee.

Redhat 6.2 has as one of it's options, a very similar approach where you can
installl linux within the dos partition.
