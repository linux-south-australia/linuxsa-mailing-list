Re: Newbie alert!

I agree..  It is too easy to point someone to manuals and How-To's, etc.  I 
often resort to IRC and lists after reading the manuals or because it is 
much easier.  I work on a help desk which holds 'docco' to be paramount, yet 
human nature means we more often stick our heads over our cubicles and ask 
our co-workers.  Besides, it builds a 'community' which I would like to 
encourage as something FUNDAMENTAL to Linux.  This is what attracted me to 
Linux above, is the community that I find no where else.

Being a newbie myself (although a fairly experienced one now) who started 
from scratch all on his lonesome, I am happy to re-translate documentation 
in accessible, non esoterical ways, to make it short and sweet for the 
newbie.

I think something just as important as a FAQ, command list or how-to, is a 
GLOSSARY.  I don't mind writing one up that is as non-technical as possible, 
an entry level glossary.

Ian
