IDG offering to pay for installfest coverage

Want to be a journalist?

Rodney Gedda <rodney@linuxworld.com.au> is offering 40c per published word
for a story about the LinuxSA installfest tomorrow, with the rights being
retained by IDG. You can email him or ph (02) 9902 2728. You'll probably
need to present some sort of evidence that you can string words together,
but apart from that anyone who is familiar with free software basics and
who has a small flair for conversational writing will be able to do a good
job.

I will be willing to go through a first draft if someone is nervous about
being published for the first time. My advice to the successful applicant
is to get it done as close to possible to on the day. I'd also suggest
that if more than one person does this that there will be plenty of scope
for getting it aired, if not at these pay rates.
