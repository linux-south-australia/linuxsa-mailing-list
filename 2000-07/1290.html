Adding X Headers (or other headers)

Actually, how would one add an "X Loop" to one's headers, or for that
matter a "HEADER X" to one's headers. I can think of two ways:

        cat ${tmpdir}/receivedmail |\
            ${formail} -f \
                       -A "${X_Header_String}" \
        | ${deliver} "$@"

(where ${deliver}=/usr/sbin/sendmail and the arguments are arguments to
sendmail) or alternatively fiddling with a sendmail.cf configuration.
Does this appear to be a sensible way to go?
