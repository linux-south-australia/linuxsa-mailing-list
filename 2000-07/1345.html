Ummm, since you mention DECstations, you need to check whether or not they
are MIPS boxen or Alpha boxen.

Secondly, I am familiar with much of the DEC gear that went into the Uni
:-), and I think that almost all of it had TurboChannel, rather than PCI.

I do not think that there is any support for TurboChannel under Linux,
although NetBSD supports it for DECstations, so you might be able to port
the code.

This is, however, a serious undertaking, and it is definitely going to be
cheaper to get an old 200MHz pentium or whatever. Faster too, both from the
POV of time, and the speed of programs you will eventually run.
