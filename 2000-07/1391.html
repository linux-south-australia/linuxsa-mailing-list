I am quite happily using dual head (nVidia TNT2 + i810 card).  I have a
two button mouse, which is configured with this option.  (I am hoping to 
get a 5 button scrolly mouse which should give me a total of 7 buttons :)

Section "InputDevice"
        Identifier  "Mouse0"
        Driver      "mouse"
        Option      "Protocol" "PS/2"
        Option      "Device" "/dev/psaux"
        Option      "Emulate3Buttons" "true"
EndSection

So looks like you where close, but not quite there.
