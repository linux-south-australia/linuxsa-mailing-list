Re: Forcing a 10/100 NIC into 10mb mode ... conf.modules?

Ian,

> Does anyone know the A)
> Switches and B) best place to look for conf.modules information...?

Finding module parameters is always fun.  Probably best to look is in
the kernel source code .... really, linux is user friendly and well
documented :-)

Check out eepro100.c and tulip.c in drivers/net/ in your linux source.
Search for "MODULE_PARM".

There doesn't look like there is anything to slow it down to 10Mbs but
you may want to play with TX/RX FIFO lengths and Full duplex options.
