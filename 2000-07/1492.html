Re: I810 video with linux mandrake 7.1

> Hi there.
> 
> Does anyone know whether or not i810 video cards can be set to a
> decent colour depth and resolution under MK7.1.
> 
> We can't get better than 1024x768 with 8 bit colour.
> 
> Any advice would be welcome.  (We've tried both XFree86 3.3.6 & 4.0).

At work I use an i810 and nVidia TNT2 dual head with XFree86 4.0.1  The
trick to get it working is that you do need the agpgart backport module to
make the video card available.

I don't know about MK, but you should be able to find kernel contrib packages
(if not Mandrake ones).  rpmfind.net is nice that way :P.

But anyway, with XF86 4.0.1 the i810 worked a treat.  The agpgart isn't
quite stable.  The i810 that I have doesn't seem to like starting twice
without removing the agpgart module and letting XF86 probe it again.
