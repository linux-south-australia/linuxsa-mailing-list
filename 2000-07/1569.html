Re: what version of linux for a crusty mac laptop?

MkLinux stands for  Micro Kernel Linux - I believe.

It is CMU Mach based. And most pople all Mach a Micro Kernel.

I think Linus was quoted at oen stage as sayign that MkLinux was nto a
pure Linux (or migth have said it was nto aLinux at all), so it is not my
conclusion, but soemone I thik has the right to make the distinction's
conclusion.

Personally though I would have to agree as Mach does provide some
functionality that is normally provided by Linux (eg primitive memory
managmanet , some process like abstractions and some IPC - from my very
very basic understanding of Mach and Linux).

However from a (non kernel)programmer's/user's perspective it is Linux or 
at least looks a hell of a lot like Linux.
