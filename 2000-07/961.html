Re: Directory listings

Paul Malcolm Bailey wrote:

> I'm a Linux newbie and need some advice on how to list directories --
> *only directories* -- in any directory in my filesystem.

A couple of methods:

"du" (normally used for disk usage; still shows you tree structure).

"find . -type d -ls"  (the -ls is a GNUism, and not available on all
finds.  You can replace this with "-exec ls -ld {} \;", or just a
straight "-print" if you're not interested in any of the stats).
