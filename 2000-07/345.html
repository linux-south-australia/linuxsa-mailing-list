Re: Which modem??

V90/K56 are not interfaces, they are modem protocols.

You should get which ever your ISP supports.

These days that is generally v90 as that is the standard (k56Flex and
x2 were both pre v90 protocols that various alliances of companies
created
because v90 ahd nto been standardised at the time), however it is possible
that some ISPs might not support v90.

K56flex is still supported by some ISPs as well as v90, but v90 is the way
to go.

As for interface, I dont know how well supported USB is, but David is
likely correct, so so better to be safe and go for a serial modem I would
think.
