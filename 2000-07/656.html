Re: best way to write a large document?

In that case Shaun, I would recomend LyX or KLyX.

Both of these are essentially frontends to the TeTeX
(or is it LaTeX) typesetting systems and can output
into either DVI or PS printable formats.

I have not personally used it for extreemly long
documents but I believe that that is exactly what the
system is for.

Find out more at:
http://www.lyx.org
http://ettrich.priv.no/klyx/klyx.html

Hope that's closer to what you're looking for.

Damien.
