Re: Plural of Computer Virus

Hi All,
    It was my understanding that words ending in 'us' in the
English Language were pluralised by substituting 'i' or
sometimes 'ii', but I believe the former is correct. As an
example that I know to be correct - the plural of Octopus is
Octopi. However with the Bastardisation of the English
Language from influential sources, ie the Internet, some
words, and in particular computer related words, have been
changed because it is "easier" to remember them. Virus
appears to be one of them as every reference I can find
related to it gives the plural "viruses". Unfortunately as
time goes by and more people are exposed to these
influential sources, the incorrect versions will become the
correct terms and words and the correct words will fall into
disuse.
    Cheers!
        Andrew.
