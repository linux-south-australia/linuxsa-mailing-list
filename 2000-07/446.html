Squid and IE5 SSL - solution

Ok, now Ive asked this before but im posting a solution.

I have overcome my problems, by adding the following lines to my
squid.conf

anonymize_headers deny Content-Length

Can anyone for see any problems with this? 
It means now that SSL under IE5 is finally working using my proxy, so
hopefully content-length isnt anything to critical.
