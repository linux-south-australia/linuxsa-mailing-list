Re: Scanner - how to?

Hi Andrew,

You need to check out the Linux usb project at http://www.linux-usb.org 
and check to see if your scanner is supported and then to download the 
backport for the 2.2.x Kernel. After you've downloaded it follow the 
instructions about patching a kernel and then rebuild the kernel.

Hope this helps, please feel free to ask questions.
