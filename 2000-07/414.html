Re: Please suggest a Linux friendly network card

If your after somthing thats cheap but will do the job well, pop into Jaycar
electronics on Wright St and grab thier 100baseT card (they only sell 1
type).
Its a no-name card but is based on the well known Realtek 8139 chipset.
The 2.2.xx kernel has support for it but its speed is rather slow (i get
2.5Mb/s with xover cable to a win98 box using the same card).
The preformance of the card will increase with the 2.4.0 kernel as new
drivers are being included.
