>Hi List,

>i ve just put a second ethernet card in a redhat 6.2 box, and cant seem to
>get it to work. Having run netcfg, the device delays initalisation.

This typically means that the driver did not load. Perhaps you have not set
up /etc/conf.modules to alias eth1 to the correct device driver module.

You probably should load the driver manually first to ensure that you have
everything OK.

An ISA card will require more TLC, but a PCI card should just work. Ie, if
your card was an RTL8029, then 'modprobe ne' should do it, and so on.
