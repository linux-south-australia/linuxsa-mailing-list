The most frequent question I was asked was, "which is better: TurboLinux
or Red Hat?"  Obviously one has a picture of a very elegant red hat on the
CD, and the other has a neat stylised penguin; this is the most important
difference.  (There are other differences but they are not so important.)

The second most frequent question was, "what can you do with the Linuxcare
bootable business card?"  (It boots into Linux and since it can mount FAT12,
FAT16, FAT32, NTFS and various unix filesystems, the disk is great for making
almost any wintel pc unusable.  Take it to somebody who you don't really like
and invent new Saturday night games.)
