Gnome/enlightenment is a popular combination for looks and functionality.
I still don't like gnome that much - simply because the panel crashes
occasionally and complex themes make the system sluggish (to coin a
word) on my k6-2/350 with a tnt2 and I think the foot thing is ugly - but
hey.  Lots of people like it so.

I like icewm *grin*  Have I said that enough here yet?  I guess because
it's one of the only window managers I've used that runs as fast and as
smoothly as the Microsoft Windows 9x interface.

I'm looking forward to the Berlin window manager..  it has *real*
translucency and scaling and rotation and all sorts of really really
funky features - it's in development but you can play with it :)
http://berlin.sourceforge.net/screenshots.html

There's a recent slashdot post sorta related to this too..
http://slashdot.org/askslashdot/00/06/21/0046236.shtml

I'm still waiting for the ultimate desktop of all desktops.  Full 3d,
completely new way of thinking (I am soooo bored of WIMP), AI, and
whatever else.  Someone'll make it...
