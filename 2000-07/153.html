Bolshoi; there are several ways to skin this cat.  I've two-line
hacked[1] (TM) ruleset 0 to deliver mail to other domains to the
mailer vdeliver, which is the adequate pop daemon used by netconf.
Something like (ripped out of sendmail.mc):

FV/etc/mail/vdomain
S0
R$*<@$=V>		$#virtual $@ $2 $: $1
R$*<@$=V.>		$#virtual $@ $2 $: $1

Virtual domains for receiving are listed in /etc/mail/vdomain, and
to allow relaying are stuck where appropriate.  Too easy.
