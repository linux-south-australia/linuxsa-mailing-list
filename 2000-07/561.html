Re: Accidentally deleted lost+found. Does it matter?

At 05:02 PM 7/13/00 +0930, Mark Phillips wrote:
>I accidentally deleted the lost+found directory on one of my
>partitions.  Does it matter, and if so, how should I recreate it?  Any
>special way, or just "mkdir lost+found"?

Well it does matter actually, and you have to recreate it in a special way:

   mkdir "found lost"
   mv "found lost" lost+found

If you don't do it that way you will be all fscked up :-)
