The Enlightenment bundled with Gnome on RedHat systems is quite
old.  (0.15.something?)  0.16.4 is the current release, and is
much more stable.  I normally use Enlightenment 0.16.4 by
itself, and am quite happy.

To be fair to Gnome, if you upgrade the version supplied with
RedHat 6.2 to that distributed by Helix (www.helixcode.com),
you'll find Gnome is *much* improved.  Stability is
wonderful, many annoying bugs fixed, and the interface is
more consistent.

Hence, I'd recommend either Enlightenment 0.16.4 or instead
use HelixGnome with Sawmill.  Many people swear by KDE, but
I've not used it much.  But, as someone already said in this
thread, choice is good.  Choose what works best for you.
