Even if it were only that, it would be an excellent example of an intuitive
interface vs an obscure command set. However, you can drag an image onto (or
into) an image editor. It will instantly open up and display an enlarged
editing view, and a thumbnail icon. alter the image, you alter the thumbnail.
That's a pretty good visual example of a pipe.

Open your head up David to what guis can do instead of looking for false
examples of what they aren't or can't be. It's self defeating. It's like
arguing that because horseless carriages don't mow grass, can't provide manure,
and cannot be eaten, they aren't as good. You're right. In the meantime the
world moves on and leaves you behind.
