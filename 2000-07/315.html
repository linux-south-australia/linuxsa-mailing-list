RE: Query - security at Installfest

Excellent idea.

I think that should be adequate, as only modems and perhaps a few other 
pieces of hardware would fit in a bag, the most expensive peices would be 
too large to not make it obvious.  Even so, we should not be too complacent.

I have been to far to many parties, fests, raves of all description to 
recall, where everyone was part of some 'community' and yet someone would 
always manage to 'lose' something.

There should only be one entrance / exit.  This is very important, and there 
should be at least two people at the door at all times.

It will not suprise me if my 'paranoia' elicts a few giggles in the 
audience, but I have seen it happen all my life since the first events like 
this that I went to when I was a teenager.  Sad but a fact of life.

Ian
