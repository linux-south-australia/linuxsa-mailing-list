Re: best way to write a large document?

You live in an idealistic world.

Try working in a corporate environment where standards (and not quality or 
price) are the main motivator.

I need to know that my girlfriend can type up her resume and have it come 
out on any printer, anywhere, any time.

I also need to know that I can read and edit documents written in a standard 
(however good or bad) that most of the world uses.

Even so, adopt standards and migration happens naturally.  People like what 
they are used to, not all people embrace rapid and dramatic change.  But if 
the interface is the same, they would not care about what is under the hood 
and think "why am I paying $$$ for that shit when this is soooooo much 
better and cheaper!!"
