Hi,

The NIC's I use are 3COM 3C905C-TX-M. It's a 10/100MB Fast Etherlink III XL
PCI card.
I have two of these in my Redhat 6.2 server and one in one of my RH6.2
workstations.
They were very easy to setup and work very well.
The only problem I have with them is that I don't know how to get the
Wake-On-Lan
working under linux.
The other network cards I use are 3COM 3C509's. These are 10Mbit ISA cards
which
are also Linux friendly.
