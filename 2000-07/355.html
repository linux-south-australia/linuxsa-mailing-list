At 04:37 PM 7/10/00 +0930, Freeman, Peter (ERHS) wrote:
>Check out the Mylex/Buslogic cards, we have a Mylex Acceleraid 150 card with
>4 x 9gb SCSI HD in a Raid 5 configuration.
>All your Raid configuration is done using the card Bios setup.
>We're running RH 6.2 on the server, it picked up the Raid device without any
>hassles at all.
>HTH

The support for RAID has been getting better and better. When we first put
a Mylex DAC960s in at one site, we have to compile the driver into the
kernel and fly by the seat of our pants, but now RH6.2 and other distros
have modules that will work with the raid controllers without any problems.

I have seen Adaptec raid controllers being supported too (as in, I have
heard of RH providing modular drivers, but not used them).
