While I don't recommend this, you can try editing your /etc/XF86Config file.
If that file is not there, try looking in the /etc/X11/ directory.

Now look under
Section "Screen".
 There is a configuration option you should change at
DefaultColorDepth.
Change it from 8 bit to 24 bit. (24 bit is the maximum in Windows 9x for
i810 video so I don't recommend trying for 32 bit)
Then restart your X-Server.

Ray

PS Colors will look weird as linux/kde doesn't support 24 bit color too
well. It seems that either 16 or 32 bit color work well.
