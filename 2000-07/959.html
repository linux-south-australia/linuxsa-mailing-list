Re: FTP problem

Bryan Wetton wrote:

> I can ping between 2 Linux RH 6.2 boxes but I can't ftp to one of them. As
> ftp is supposedly set up to default to accept anonymous I must have changed
> something. I've looked for a FTP HOW-TO but all assume anonymous is
> accepted.

You do have the ftp package installed, right?  RedHat have a habit of
leaving an entry in /etc/inetd.conf for ftp, but neglecting to put
put in the binaries in for ftpd (so you don't even get a "connection
refused", but a quick connect/disconnect instead).  It _should_ be in
/usr/sbin/in.ftpd (well, it is for 6.0 and 6.1, anyway).
