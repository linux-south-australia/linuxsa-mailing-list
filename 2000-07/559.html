Re: Accidentally deleted lost+found. Does it matter?

Hi,

> I accidentally deleted the lost+found directory on one of my
> partitions.  Does it matter, and if so, how should I recreate it?  Any
> special way, or just "mkdir lost+found"?

Warning, this knowledge is not based on hard fact, just my
understanding. People with more knowledge can correct me.

Lost+found is a result of running fsck over your file system. It is
automatically created by the program, and not requirted for the running of
the day-to-day system. If you have corruption for which inodes are lost,
they'll end up in that directory.
