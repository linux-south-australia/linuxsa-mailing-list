Re: moving /var/spool

I've forgotten the syntax (I'm at work, win95 and
Access/VBA, eugh), but an alternative to using copy
(and I think a generally more reliable way of moving
larger bodies of files) is to use "tar".  Someone else
might remember how to do it properly, but the gist of
it is (non sytaxly[sic]):

use tar to bundle up the whole lot recursively, then
pipe it back to tar with a command to untar to the
target location.  You use a period or something as
part of the taring command.

Hope that helps (read the tar documentation)
