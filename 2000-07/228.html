> certain what "frame" refers to (i.e. I'll have to RTFM about it).

Characters were received when a framing byte was expected.

All errors should be under 1 in 10^6 for all media, you are running
at 1 in 10^3.

Check that the modem handshaking, carrier detection, etc is being done
correctly.  You'll need the modem manual for this and will need to
change the modem's initialisation string.  You want CTS/RTS handshaking,
with DCD set to follow the line condition.

If you lose handshaking to the modem, then a lot of the modem-modem
communications has to be re-established, thus the pause.  Check that
the "framing" count increments when you have the event.

You will get massive delays if you are unfortunate enough to drop a DNS
packet.  [BTW, you should also be running BIND configured as a DNS forwarder
on the machine.]
