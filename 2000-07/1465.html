 in fact, it's not just the combination of CDRW media and certain
drives/players that don't work -- I've been told that certain combinations
of CD-R media, CD-R burners and CD players just don't get along... most CD
drives will read most CD-R, but even there, they sometimes don't like random
combinations of media and burner -- find something that works, and stick
with it...

Many "normal" CD players don't grok CD-RW media.  There are subtle
differences between the format of a "normal" audio CD and the format
of the data deposited by a CD-RW drive.

Some CD players don't care, others care a lot.  YMMV.
