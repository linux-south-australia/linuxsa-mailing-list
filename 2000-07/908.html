Re: XFree86 4.0.1 RPMs? - You bet!!

Yep, found it just for you.  There is one in the
Rawhide directory at redhat's ftp site:

ftp://ftp.redhat.com/pub/rawhide/i386/RedHat/RPMS/

or you might prefer to get it from the aarnet mirror:

http://mirror.aarnet.edu.au/pub/linux/redhat/rawhide/i386/RedHat/RPMS/

the file you want is XFree86-4.0.1-0.30.i386.rpm in
both sites.

Although being in the rawhide collection they may not
be entirly tested (I only know that rawhide is the
redhat prerelease distro).  Good luck.
