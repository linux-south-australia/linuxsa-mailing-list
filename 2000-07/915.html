Re: *OT* Netgear Print Server

Hi Martin,
The PS105 & PS110 do work well as lpd servers. You'll probably need to
use the Windows software to configure them & turn-off all other
protocols. Do not try & configure them on a large LAN, if you have
access to a x-over network cable use that or make sure there is nothing
else on the LAN, they are really *crappy* to configure, but they do work
once configured.

Don't even think of using them in any other mode unless your clients are
Windoze.
