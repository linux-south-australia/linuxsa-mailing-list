different 'views' of your mailbox. You look at the linux 'folder' using a

*drumroll*

"X-Loop:"!

It still doesn't change the fact that issues with mail clients are not
solved by changing the mail list subject field.

I'll just say it again as some people don't seem to be groking this
concept.

The Subject: field is for...   The subject.

The X-Mailing-List: field is for...	The mailing list.

The X-Loop: field is for...	Filtering.

You have the information you need so use it as you see fit to get the
results that you need. If you can't get your mail client to do what
you need it to do with these mail headers then get_a_new_mailclient();
