Re: Email delivery & filtering

John Kennison wrote:

> I'm looking for some advice as to where to look in regards to how to handle my
> current email situation. I need to recieve email from a pop3 account (thinking
> fetchmail) & then I need to filter it by the "To:" field.

As the recent debates of here (only a week ago) and elsewhere (including
fetchmail documentation) have indicated, filtering by the "To:" field is
bad.  If you do pursue it, expect to have some email that will not be
correctly filterable, and expect to live with it as a problem.  You'll
probably want a (suggestion only) combination of fetchmail+an MTA of your
choice (eg, exim, qmail, sendmail in no particular order other than
lexicographic)+procmail+Cyrus(IMAP).

> Any suggestions, ideas on what manuals to read, or advice would be greatly
> appreciated...

fetchmail's documentation is probably the best start.
