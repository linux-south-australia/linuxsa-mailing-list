RE: Gui vs Cli

"is" is correct.

"a maximum" is singular, and "is" is the singular form.

People get confused because they attach "is" to the "16,000" icons, but this
is incorrect. It would be correct to say "There are 16,000 icons", or "There
is a maximum of 16,000 icons"... Of course, these don't mean the same, but
they illustrate the point.

Of course, the English we see used around us (by colleagues both verbally
and textually, in the media, in emails, and in every day life) is so
consistently bad that correct usage can sound wrong. As Winston Churchill
said when someone criticised him for a dangling participle: "This is the
sort of English up with which I will not put."
