[ANNOUNCE] LinuxSA July 2000 Meeting

Hi all,

Time for the LinuxSA July 2000 Meeting:

  When:   7:00pm on
          Tuesday, 18th July, 2000
  Where:  2nd Floor
          Compaq Computer
          139 Frome Street
          Adelaide SA
  Cost:   FREE
  Who:    Anyone and everyone.
          No pre-registration necessary.

Presentation:

  In lieu of the the usual presentation, this month we will be having
  a mini-InstallFest for those people who were unable to have their
  machines attended to due to the great demand we saw on Saturday.
  Richard Russell will also be giving a post-InstallFest report if I
  can convince him to :-).

  People planning to bring a machine should email
  organisers@linuxsa.org.au beforehand so we can get an idea of
  demand.

  Could people willing to help with installs please bring powerboards
  and CDs, as those are the items we are mostly likely to be short of
  on Tuesday.

For more information:
