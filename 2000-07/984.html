Argh!  E on a 40mhz box!  I don't know how you manage.  If you ever feel
like moving to something a little quicker - less resource and screen-space
intensive, give blackbox (http://blackbox.alug.org) a try.  This wm is not
nomrally packaged with distributions - but I can almost guarentee it'll
compile cleanly on most boxes.  It's not GNOME complient (it does,
however, support the new WM-spec that GNOME 2.0 and KDE 2.0 will use), and
its interface may take a little while to get used to, but I find it a
really comfortable and quick wm.

If you do give it a try, don't let the term "beta" of the 0.60.x series
fool you - I have never had a crash with it, and the 0.60.x series is a
lot better than the old 0.51.x ones.

Just my 2c
