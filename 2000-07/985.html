Re: SCSI Scanner

At 11:30 AM 7/19/00 +0930, Robert Acres wrote:
>Has anybody ever succeeded in getting an Acer SCSI scanner to work under
>Linux using the cards that come bundled with the scanners?
>Anybody who has any suggestions or has been able to achieve this, your input
>would be appreciated,

Well, I had another look at this, and had to undertake the following steps:

   pnpdump 0x203

This gave me a protype isapnp.conf that had info in it about the SYM53C416
in it.

Then I enabled it by specifying the Interrupt that Windows gave me (10),
but not the IO port location:

   isapnp sym.conf

Then I loaded the driver:

   modprobe sym53c416 sym53c416=0x280

And the driver loaded ... Now to see what I have to do to get the Gimp to
scan with the scanner ...
