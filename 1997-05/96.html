Survey

Well, I suspect that no-one is going to ask me to start this, so I better
get off my bum and just do it :)

*Firstly* don't send me any results yet!! The account don't even exist,
it's a waste of your time.

Proposal:

E-Mail survey results to ossurvey@tardis.apana.org.au

Send at most one entry per ______? (timeframe)

No subject line.

Body of message is as follows:

	1 line per operating system
	each OS line contains:

	OS,percentage:task,percentage:[task,percentage]...

Clear as mud ay? A solid example:

linux,70:wp,20:games,5:inet:40,play:35
win95,25:games:80,wp:20
dos,5:games:100

This means that:

I use Linux 70% of the time. When I'm using Linux, 5% of the time I play
games, 20% of the time I do word-processing, 40% of the time I'm on the
internet and 35% of the time I'm just fooling around with it.

Another 25% of the time I'm using Windows 95. I play games 80% of the time
with that, a bit of word processing the rest.

Lastly, I use DOS occasionally, just for games..

Notes:

Only count the time you are actually using your computer. If you leave
your computer on 24hrs (like me), only include those actually spent at the
keyboard. It would slew the results a bit if you leave your computer on
running Linux, but don't actually use it that much, but you boot Win95 for
a game of Sega Rally every now and then.
