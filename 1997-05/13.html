Try deleting the hidden files belonging to Norton Utilities (if you have any).  You may need to change your Windows settings to see them or "dir /a" will display all of them; attrib "*.*" will also show which files are hidden etc.
These include "sd.ini" "image.*".  Most of these are in the root directory of the drive.  If you have used PC-Tools
or the DOS 5.0+ Mirror program you may have some hidden files from these as well.  Once again most of these are in the root directory of the drive.  The drive image files are almost always at the end of the drive.
