Re: MyFastModem Problems.

Paul,

Is this a COM1/COM3 or COM2/COM4 problem?  That is, are you 
trying to use COM1 and COM3 both at the same time e.g. 
mouse + modem or somthing similar?  If this is so, you 
should be aware that this is unreliable on most serial 
cards due to level based interrupt sharing on the PC.  (In 
fact stupid design of the IO cards.)

If this the case, try using COM1 and COM2 or some other 
combination avoiding the 'odds and evens' pairing..

Chris
