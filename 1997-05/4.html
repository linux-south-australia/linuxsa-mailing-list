Re: Screensaver

> In my previous Linux installs I had beaut screensavers in x. With
> Red Hat all I have is the menu item, no screensavers!!! How do I get
> them back?

Try installing it by mounting your Red Hat CD and running this
command:
rpm -ivh /mnt/cdrom/RedHat/RPMS/xlockmore-*
(I think that is the one run by the menu option).

For a real screensaver (activate on idle, instead of activate on menu
option), install xscreensaver:
rpm -ivh /mnt/cdrom/RedHat/RPMS/xscreensaver-*
