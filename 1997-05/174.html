Re: mounting floppy and enabling hpdj500 printer

to mount your floppy drive a: type at the console:

mount -t msdos /dev/fd0 <mount_point>

where <mount_point> is where you want the floppy disks contents to
be mounted. (If you change to this directory - cd <dir>, you will see
whats on the disk). You can add this to your /etc/fstab file, and
then you will only have to type mount <mount_point>. type 'man
fstab' for more info.
