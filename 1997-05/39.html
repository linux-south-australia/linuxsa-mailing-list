There have been some modems recently, that have had all the Error
correction, and Compression
routines in a windows driver that does it for you.

Do you have a "modem driver" for win95?

I haven't done much work with these modems so I can't help you if it is.

Another possible gotchya is the interrupts/ioports.

If you have a look at the output from dmesg you should find a few lines
about the Serial driver.

That will display the ioports and irqs for each of the serial ports
(including the modem).

My guess is that you are sharing either IRQ4 or IRQ3 and the other
serial card is grabbing most
of the interrupts.
