Internode mirror server available at LCA

Internode is a sponsor of LCA2004.

As a service to conference attendees, I've just opened up access to
ftp://mirror.internode.on.net to the wireless network used on Adelaide
Uni campus.

There are several Linux distros online, along with lots of other stuff.

Normally this is only available to Internode customers, but if you're
at LCA you're part of the family, right?  Get it while it's hot, and
pass the word around to any conferencegoers who aren't subscribed to
LinuxSA.
