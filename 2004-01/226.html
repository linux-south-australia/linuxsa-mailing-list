>  -a high performance sound card (I must be able to specify this, as I

You won't get a built in sound card that doesn't suck. It'll be an
after-market job. You can pick up USB and Cardbus/PCMCIA audio devices
at many different levels. RME make a nice (apparently, I don't have one
:-( ) cardbus card that can do 24 channels of input. That will set you
back around the same amount as your laptop.

Have a look at the linux laptops site (something like linux-laptops.org
or .net or something - google knows) - there's a heap of information
there about specific models.
