Interesting concept for govt oss development

For those who attended the OCG sessions you may recall some discussions
about development and use/reuse of code within government and also between 
it and its external providers.  This sounds like an interesting way of
solving some
of those issues.

http://www.infoworld.com/article/04/01/12/HNblackduck_1.html

    "A small software startup will make its debut at LinuxWorld next week by
rolling out a package it believes can help developers manage and mitigate
the intellectual property risks of reusing and mixing both open source and
proprietary software code.

    "Black Duck Software will introduce its Enterprise Edition, a management
tool that integrates the development cycle starting from the initial
concept, through code development and distribution, and finally provides an
audit trail that tracks what technical contributions were made by each
individual developer..."
