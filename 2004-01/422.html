Re: Reliable P4 system power supplies?

On Sat, 2004-01-10 at 23:36, Evan Bourlotos wrote:
> At 09:52 PM 10/01/2004, steve wrote:
> 
> >Cheap Codegen 300W or greater from DIY IT supplies.
> 
> With the NEW video cards they need their own power feed anything other than 
> a 400 watt PS is asking for trouble (come to the OCAU forums and find out 
> how people get caught out like this).

Yes and no, just get a reputable power supply with honest output.

I have a P4 2.8Ghz with a ATI 9800 Pro running happily with only a Antec
TruePower 330W powersupply. So far no problems under load.

Now I did pay over $100 for the powersupply alone if I remember
correctly but it well worth it. I have 7 fans in my Antec case (Two in
the powersupply, two case etc.) and it is quieter then my old Celeron
800 which only had 3 (And no it did not have dodgy bearings.).
