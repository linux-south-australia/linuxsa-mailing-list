Ecasound: 
http://www.wakkanet.fi/~kaiv/ecasound/Documentation/examples.html

"Mixing

      Here's a few real-life mixdown examples.

   1. ecasound -c \
            -a:1 -i drums.wav \
            -a:2 -i synth-background.wav \
            -a:3 -i bass-guitar_take-2.ewf \
            -a:4 -i brass-house-lead.wav \
            -a:all -o /dev/dsp 

      First of all, interactive-mode is selected with '-c'. Then four
inputs (all stereo) are added. All four chains are then assigned to one
output, which this time is the soundcard (/dev/dsp). That's all."

Ecasound is at http://www.wakkanet.fi/~kaiv/ecasound/welcome.html
