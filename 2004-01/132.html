Okay, Telstra do this wierd thing when people try to authenticate too many 
times.  They block the MAC address of your ethernet card.  You'll need to 
talk to your ISP to get them to talk to Telstra.  (Or goto a different ISP 
with a more stable infrastructure who doesn't do the crazy things they've 
done to you!!)

The easiest option is to change your ethernet cards around or change the MAC 
address of your card on the external port (ifconfig eth0 hw ether <mac 
addr>) should change it (from memory only)
