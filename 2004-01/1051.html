Adrian Visser wrote:
> I am having probs getting my Mandrake 9.1 system to run in any form of nice.
> Here are my issues: firstly I managed to get nVidia's 4496 installed. 
 > My second Prob is that now and then X stops for no reason no mouse no
 > keys nothing. shot of going in init 2
 > and restarting x nothing works.
 > And finally I can not seen to get the system to accept hardware
 > accelerated graphics

I have a Geforce 2 on Mandrake 9.1.  I upgraded XFree86 to v4.3-8.2mdk 
(using Mandrake's packages of course), then installed the NVidia drivers 
again.  Then I made sure that my display driver was set to nvidia (not 
nv), and I've been running fine ever since.  If you have a Geforce 2 MX 
(like I do), you'll need to get a fan for your graphics card... mine has 
had lockup problems since the start of summer.

Also check in your X configuration that you're loading Mesa and glx modules.
