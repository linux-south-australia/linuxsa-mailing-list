RE: Hating meeces to pieces.

I've had excellent success with middle-button/wheel mice on X under
Debian and on Windows where I use it with Unigraphics. Easy to set up,
nice to use, feels a bit strange under the middle finger for a while,
but you soon get used to it.
If you've got the option of going optical -- do it!
You won't look back, you'll never have to clean those sticky little
mouse furr balls off the contact rollers of your mouse again!


Cheers,
