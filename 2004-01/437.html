linux.conf.au 2004 in Adelaide

So it's near the end of the first day of linux.conf.au's mini 
conference. There's a good vibe here - relaxed yet interesting. The 
conference packs are very well done, and I'm particularly impressed 
with Dan Shearer's instanta-demo CD. :)

I noticed something curious... nearly all the seminars I find 
interesting are South Australian speakers. Either we have a lot of 
them, or we're a very interesting bunch of people. :)

So far I've seen a couple of people I know... Romana Challans, and the 
elusive David Lloyd disappearing around a corner. I'm sure I'll meet 
more people (for that 2 seconds and 3d image!) as the main conference 
kicks in.

Wish you were here, etc etc.
