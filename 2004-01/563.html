Not quite true :)

Just a small story. I had a top of the range netcomm modem
quite some years ago. It didn't work. I rang support. Support was
brilliant. Determined the firmware problem, determined the upgrade
required, posted the new modem --- it still didn't work (properly - with
hardware flow control). Rang support. Again brilliant. Always prompt,
courteous and knowledgable. Next firmware upgrade arrived. Still didn't
work.

As it happened I had another machine needing a modem, I plugged in the
netcomm and it worked fine. Then I went and bought a mymodem for half the
price. It worked flawlessly for years on a variety of systems including
on the box that had caused the netcomm so much trouble. I never needed
support, so I have no idea if it was any good.
