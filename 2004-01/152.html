Re: Acrobat 5 support

I am of the thought that any proprietary format being accepted as
standard is going to cause headaches for all.

I strongly dislike PDF as an online format.

It requires that the whole document be downloaded before even Page 1 can
be viewed, & the layout is really setup for printing, not viewing.

Just the other day I wanted to print out a .pdf, but the font was way
too large.
So I set about trying to change it, & found that it couldn't be done 
because the font type/size was locked in the document. And of course you
can't even cut/paste the text from it into a word processor.

The fact that it is a proprietary format is the icing on the cake.
Let it die a quick death!


Rick
