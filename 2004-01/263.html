Actually, latest cvs is a good thing, as it seems the ipv6 patches are 
now in a nice branch off HEAD in the 2.5 stream.  So I didn't have to 
apply patches at all, just grab the latest 2.5.x squid as per these 
instructions:

http://www.bieringer.de/linux/IPv6/IPv6-HOWTO/IPv6-HOWTO-4.html#squid

I then needed to hack the Makefile.am file to remove VERSION=1.5   (as 
automake-1.6 on SuSE 8.1 doesn't like that) and hack the configure.in 
to make it more automake-1.6 friendly.

It's now going through the configure stage, which I'm hoping will 
complete without errors...
