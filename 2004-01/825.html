You'd be better off not buying the license.  Buying the
license establishes a contract between yourself and SCO.
This in turn gives SCO more leverage to further pursue its
claims, should SCO wish to do so.

For example, look at the situation of US firms which currently
hold SCO source licenses.

Note that the media release carefully avoids *requiring*
you to purchase a SCO license, as that would certainly
bring the attention of the ACCC.  We'll see how interested
they are in SCO's current offer.
