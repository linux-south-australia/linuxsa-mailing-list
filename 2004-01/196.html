My advice is to get an ISDN home service installed, this will give you a
few options. 

1. You can use this for normal dialup and you will find that your dialup
connections will pickup to around 50K. 

2. You can use an ISP that supports ISDN DOV calls, and pay only a local
call per 64k connection.

3. Join an ISP that has an 0198 number and pay Telstra the $16.50/mo for
capped calls on ISDN dialup.

At home I'm 8km from our exchange, I had an ISDN home service installed
and it's the only way to go for us poor people in the dongas :) 

The Telstra tech whinged about all the work he had to do to get the
service running, took him 6 hours of back and forward finding lines and
pits and going backwards and forward to the exchange.
