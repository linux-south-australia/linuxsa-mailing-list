I've found that a set of 4 P4 systems with a moderately aggressive
nVidia card are unreliable, with the video card showing corruption on
the initial boot screen, locking up during operation, etc.

There's a reasonable suspicion that the power supplies are to blame
(350W power supply, but a fairly generic brand rather than a
high-profile manufacturer).

I would like to source some reliable power supplies and swap over the

	- Antec.
