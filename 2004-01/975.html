Re: XFree86 Tripple Head - Xinerama

I don't think you'll find those options in the XFree 'mga' driver (man
mga).

I do know for a fact that the 'MergedFB' option is only present in
matrox's own binary driver found here ->
ftp://ftp.matrox.com/pub/mga/archive/linux/2003/mgadrivers-3.0.tgz
This is what gives you 3D acceleration on both heads of the card (good
luck getting it on the 3rd S3Trio head at the same time).

MetaModes describes the combination of resolution modes to use on each
monitor.

The 'Monitor2..,' options are self-explanatory.

Hope this helps, I don't actually own a matrox card.

Regards,
Rick
