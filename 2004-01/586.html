Re: Firewall filtering by application

Evan Bourlotos <evan@cs.adelaide.edu.au> wrote:
> ZA simply blocks ports or sniffs them the concept is no different to any
> 'normal' firewall

I'm sure that's not right.  ZoneAlarm knows which application is trying
to use which ports, and whether that use is by way of a listening socket
or a connecting socket.  An external firewall can't do that.  It can't
know what ports some other host that sits behind it is listening to.
