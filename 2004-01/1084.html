As someone who works with Internet all the time, one of my pet peeves is 
when I tell someone to use a perfectly good hostname in a web browser 
and through some kind of subconcious brain process they prepend 'www.' 
to it. These days I find myself adding 'www' cnames to DNS in advance 
rather than correcting people. 'UseCanonicalName' in Apache can also be 
a subtle way to make the point :)

Given that the majority of domain names are used for just a web site and 
email, don't you find the 'www' somewhat superfluous?
