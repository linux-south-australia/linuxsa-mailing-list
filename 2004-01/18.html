I'm not sure where what I'm about to write fits in to this
discussion, but it's vaguely related to the above quote.

I was part of the organising collective for the 2003 Students of
Sustainability conference (and the 2004 one now, too), held at
Flinders University (sort of) back in July.

Anyway, we did this awesome, intense, 5/6 day conference of about
500 people.

Registration costs were $120 unwaged and $150 waged (and we did not
require presentation of concession cards etc), and people could
apply for further concessions if they needed it.

The registration fee was inclusive of campground accommodation and
all meals. Some field trips were extra.
