Well, dialin uucp worked pretty well for _years_ for us. We changed in
the end (last year) because we were the last customer using it, and the
ISP in question was in a rush to destroy their business.

It makes sense to reduce the number of supported protocols though.
Probably most uucp customers were on dialup, but big users on smtp over
ISDN or broadband will always see more throughput (bigger pipe). I
wonder if anyone's tried running uucp over tcp, I can't imagine it would
be horribly inefficient.
