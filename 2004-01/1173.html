Once upon a time Brian Astill tapped upon the keyboard...

> FWIW I think this is an issue.  You want to compile on
> PC_superfast and produce a system optimised for PC_superslow. 
> That isn't totally straightforward

This is straightforward with Gentoo, at least if you are compiling
for the same architecture. 

For example, for a 486 all that'd be required is to set (in
/etc/make.conf):
CHOST="i486-pc-linux-gnu"
and
CFLAGS="-march=i486" (+whatever else you want in cflags).

You configure your own kernel anyway.
