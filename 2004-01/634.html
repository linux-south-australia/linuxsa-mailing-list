A bunch of people from OSV, SLPWA, OSBN-SA and other states have all agreed
that a single, national and non-technical voice of business needs to be
created to:

 1) Interface with big business
 2) Interface with the government

...on their own terms.

This was agreed at a meeting held at The Left Bank Cafe last Thursday
evening and was eventually supported by all present, including Con Zymaris
(OSV leader), Leon Brooks (Perth representative and committee member of
SLPWA) and myself (OSBN-SA).

Such an organisation also has the tacit support of the other important open
source organisations in Australia as well.
