Daryl,
I've not used packet writing in Linux, though I've heard it can be done
now.
Was some limitation in the kernel that has now been worked around
apparently.
I use UDF filesystem support just to be able to mount DVDs. Not all
require it, but it's there in case they do (/etc/fstab entry for
'fstype' on the dvd device is set to 'auto').
Notably, some commercial DVDs of mine won't mount until UDF support is
there.
