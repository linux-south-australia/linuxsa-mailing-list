What does your procmailrc look like?

Mine looks somewhat like this, for Maildir.

----------------------------------------------------
MAILDIR=$HOME/Maildir/   # Must have slash at end.
DEFAULT=$MAILDIR
 
:0
* ^X-Loop:.*linuxsa@linuxsa.org.au
.LinuxSA/
----------------------------------------------------

You're probably just missing the dot in the procmail rule.

As for copying the messages between them, if it were just my mail and there
wasn't too much of it, I'd point mutt at the directory and copy the messages
using mutt.  Mutt understands Maildir, so it shouldn't have any trouble.
