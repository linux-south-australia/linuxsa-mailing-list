Re: Multiple Desktop Quirkiness - Pager - SOLVED!

Hello All,

Seems that the Desktop Switching might not have been Pager.  Had a look at 
"Window Behaviour" (Config->KDE->Loon'nFeel->Window Behaviour) and under the 
'Advanced' submenu, there's a thing called "Active Desktop Borders".  It 
seems that Disabling this does the trick quite nicely. . . . well there's 
been no switching for the past 30 min anyway.   Here's hoping . . . . .

Thanks for the help.
