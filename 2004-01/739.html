Let's do this another way:

Scenario A:

I'm not a malicious person, I'm not going to break into your house.  
Tell me your address so I can come and observe the security of your house 
and have a look around.  I'll do this at 3am and possibly 4am.  You may or 
may not be aware of me doing this.

Scenario B:

I'm not a malicious person, I'm not going to break into your computer. Tell
me your ip address so I can come and observe the security of your computer
and have a look around.  I'll do this at 3am and possibly 4am.  You may or
may not be aware of me doing this.

Your comments lead me to believe that you're not okay with Scenario A but 
you are okay with Scenario B.  

Why?  Because scenario B doesn't pose a possible physical threat?  Because 
you don't necessarily value the cost of what I could damage if I was 
malicious in Scenario B?
