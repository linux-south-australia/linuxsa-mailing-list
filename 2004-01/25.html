for those who cannot afford to come.

I do want to clarify and simply say that this event is non-profit, the
organisers aren't getting paid to put it on, and we're only charging what
it's costing to put it on (you know how much it costs to fly someone from
Boston to Adelaide, put them up in a hotel and fly them home again?  Then
multiply that for all international speakers :-)

Some of the organising team have gone mostly without paid emplyment for
months so they could devote themselves to making LCA2004 rock.  (Wow).

Any money left over ("profit") goes to Linux Australia, which is only made
up of unpaid volunteers, for use in furthering Linux and Open-Source in
Australia.  If you're doing something worthwhile in OSS, consider applying
for a grant from them next year :-)
