Re: [OT] Un-invited LinuxSA 'Guests'

Years ago there was a site that supposedly could test your system and 
check if you were secure. Sure enough it found your system wasn't 
secure as it could display on a web page your whole system and file 
structure.

Gee, you thought, I better get this system and stay secure. Turns out 
that it was just a routine to build a local display of your files. It 
never left your computer. Not too long after the scam was reported that 
site dropped the pretence of doing a security check.

It sure sounded to me like this was a new take on an old joke but I 
didn't want to speak up till others had picked up on al the other 
possibilities. Well it seems that all the expected exploits have been 
spoken for so perhaps the 'joke' routine is al that explains the 
reported site breakin.

I could be wrong but funnier things have happened.
