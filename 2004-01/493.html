assuming just one o/s:

hda is your primary hard drive. so, its asking if you want to instal on
that hard drivve. as you only have one hard drive, its kind of
redundant, but allows for multiple hard drives.
examples:
hda1 is partition one on your first hard drive.
hdc2 second partition on another hard drive.

hdd is my cdrom. so, it goes by bios - hda, hdb, hdc, hdd. primary
master, primary slave, secondary master, secondary slave.
