Credit to all LCA organisers

Hi,

I was really impressed with the LCA conference. The content of the
presentations was high level (sometimes too high for my non-geek mind).
There was lots of diversity in subject matter, which I believe would
have catered for most tastes (mine included). I was much entertained by
the jovial quips of geek homour that left my mind running in circles and
trying to catch up (good exercise). The wireless network was a huge
bonus not to mention the apple notebooks. I found people to be very
friendly and non-pressured. Of course the presence of Linus was,
well...., let's just say I got his autograph (astounding: the fact that
I could just walk up and have a conversation with the eponymous king of
geeks).
Personally, eyeopeners were:
-audio conference
-Python miniconf
-perl 6 presentation

I'd like to congratulate all those involved in making it happen,
Credit to you all :-)

a happy conference goer,

Luke
