You might want to double-check that the hosting you're paying for is 
actually located within Australia.

Their own site appears to be in Melbourne, which is an improvement on 
many 'Australian' web hosts. Their 'about us' page states that they use 
'a network consisting of nine Gigabit Ethernet connections to the 
Internet plus one DS-3 and one OC-3'. Aside from DS-3 and OC-3 being 
uncommon in Australia, there probably aren't 9 providers in the country 
that can honestly supply a gigabit link. Even MCI-Worldcom refuse to 
sell that kind of bandwidth.
