I think your issue is that you're pgp signing your messages.  That
requires MIME (look at this message, also signed, for comparison).
Mutt tells me:

-- Mutt: Attachments
  I     1 <no description>                           [text/plain, quoted, us-ascii, 0.4K]
  I     2 This is a digitally signed message part    [applica/pgp-signat, 7bit, 0.2K]

I've heard of Microsoft having problems with this kind of message,
especially Outlook Express.  Maybe it's too secure for its liking.
