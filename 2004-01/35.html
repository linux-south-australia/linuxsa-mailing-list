I don't think that's what Ryan's asking for.  If he was wanting
qualified broadcast standard operators, he probably wouldn't have

With due respect to the other comments on this thread about LCA not
being cheap at $275, firewire cameras are (relatively) cheap consumer

the sort of equipment that a "qualified broadcast standard operator"
would be using.

- would be coming (or would like to come) to LCA anyway
- wouldn't mind paying less than what they would otherwise pay to
  attend the conference
- happen to own a firewire camera
- wouldn't mind pointing that camera at a speaker for a few hours each
  day

Sounds like a good deal to me.
