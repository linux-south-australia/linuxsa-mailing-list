Hi,
One of the best tests for a product is how good the support is when 
something does go wrong.

The 1st BIPAC-740 GE I purchased from Mega PC:
http://www.megapc.com.au

Was Dead-On-Arrival, (DOA), it happens sometime, every manufacturer 
sometimes allows a dud to get through.
I telephoned the Billion support number on the box & went through some 
basic problem-determination with the useful support staff. He agreed 
also it was DOA.
So I emailed Mega PC, explaining Billion support also thought it was 
DOA, they then promptly airbagged a replacement & airbag to return the 
DOA unit.

No fuss, no hassle, the replacement unit has been running for months 
now. Didn't even have to pay for the freight back to Mega PC!
