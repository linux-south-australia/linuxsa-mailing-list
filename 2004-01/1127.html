Re: [OT] Un-invited LinuxSA 'Guests'

Hmm, I guess the first and most obvious question is, have you changed the
default password on the device?  All have admin/admin or admin/1234.
Secondly, you should block off all incoming connection attempts on ports 21,
23, and 80 (probably only 80 is required, but the others don't hurt).  That
should block all external administration capabilities.

Billion's products have a decent reputation.  I think it's much more likely
that someone gained access to your computer through a misconfigured router
rather than a security hole in the router itself.
