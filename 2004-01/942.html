Open source survey

I believe all South Australian-based open-source companies should 
complete this survey to ensure our views are taken into account to 
define an 'industry view' on open source.

"This survey has been developed and is being distributed by the IT 
Council of South Australia in conjunction with Government ICTS 
(Information and Communication Technology Services). This is the 
business unit in the Department of Administration and Information 
Services responsible for planning, implementation, management and 
review of best practice information and communication technologies for 
the state public sector. 

The IT Council is the collaborative voice of the ICT Community in South 
Australia and has a role to ensure that a balanced view on issues such 
as Open Source Software occurs and that the views and requirements of 
local industry are considered by Government when developing policy. The 
Council is therefore pleased to participate in this initiative on 
defining industry views on Open Source Software and urges interested 
ICT companies to respond.
 "

http://www.intra.sa.gov.au/os_skills/
