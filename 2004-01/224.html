Re: Laptop purchase

>  -a high performance sound card (I must be able to specify this, as I
> will be using it to record music)

Buy an external USB pod for this.  Don't rely on Laptops having any kind of 
sound quality - typically there is a LOT of noise happening inside the box.

For USB devices that do sound try:

http://www.creative.com/products/welcome.asp?cat=2

or

http://www.xitel.com/

Note: I'm not a music recording expert and there are many more people who 
know more - but my limited experiences have shown this to be true.

If you do some searching you'll no doubt find better information regarding 
recording music.

In terms of laptops:

I normally recommend brand name laptops as they are usually easier to get 
repaired.  Dell seem to offer some decent value:

http://www1.ap.dell.com/content/products/features.aspx/featured_notebook1?c=au&l=en&s=dhs

Is $2098 plus $299 for the burner.  

However, recently a number of people have lead me to believe that the ASUS 
ones are good value.

I've always used Compaq laptops (usually midrange to high end) and had 
success with those.
