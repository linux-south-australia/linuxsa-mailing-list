Microsoft Ads Called Bogus

By Amit Asaravala

09:03 AM Jan. 07, 2004 PT

Open-source advocates are crying foul over a new ad campaign from Microsoft 
that claims that Windows-based systems cost less in the long run than 
Linux-based systems.

Ads for the campaign, which were unveiled Monday and will appear in online 
and print publications, refer potential customers to a section of the 
Microsoft website where they can "get the facts" on Windows and Linux. 
There, visitors can view what Microsoft calls "independent analyses" of the 
two operating systems.
