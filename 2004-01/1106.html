> >LVD = Low Voltage Differential.
> >
> >Differential signalling is where there are two wires for each "bit" and the
> >difference between the voltage on the cables is used to determine what the
> >value is for that "bit".  Differential signalling is more reliable and can
> >go longer distance.
> >
> >Normal SCSI doesn't use Differential signalling, you need to specifically
> >either a) enable it for the entire bus (if the controller supports it) b)
> >get an adapter for the drive.
