[News] Giants back Linux fighting fund

Giants back Linux fighting fund
Matthew Fordahl in San Jose
JANUARY 13, 2004

TECH giants Intel and IBM are among the contributors to a legal fund that 
will help companies running Linux software defray the cost of defending 
themselves against lawsuits.

The fund, which has so far received $US3 million ($3.9 million) in pledges 
and is seeking $US7 million more, was announced Monday by Open Source 
Development Labs (OSDL), an industry group that aims to accelerate Linux's 
adoption by businesses.
