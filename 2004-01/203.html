Ryan, David,

Okay, so you are both in Athelstone???

I might have a bit of good news for you then, just maybe...

Telstra have plans to upgrade the RIM in Athelstone with an ADSL MINIMUX.

This means that those people connected to that RIM can then get ADSL
(provided you aren't pair-gained also*).

Telstra have this upgrade slated for the 20th of February.

So.... even if you are pair-gained(*) as well as being on the RIM...try
applying for ADSL through your chosen provider on around the 20th of Feb
this year, if it fails, try again, and keep trying a few times. As it may
take Telstra a while to update their records to indicate that theres a
minimux installed there. (this was the case with Oakden late last year).

Once this goes ahead, you should be in for a fighting chance to get ADSL...
make sure you get in early, because the minimuxes only have limited ports
available on them.

(*) Even if you are pair-gained, you may still be able to get ADSL because
Telstra may be able to 'transpose' your line to straight copper to the RIM.
