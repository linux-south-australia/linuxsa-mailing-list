Re: changing to maildir with imap

On 08/01/2004, at 2:00 AM, David de Groot wrote:
> Well, encouraged by all the useful tips on this thread, I converted my 
> mail from mailbox to Maildir tonight, changing from UW-IMAP/POP3 and 
> dovecot (IMAPS) to Courier-IMAP (imap, pop3 and their ssl equiv).

Just fyi as you're an OS X user, darwin ports (which plays quite nicely 
with a fink install too, although you may get duplicate installed 
dependencies, but no biggie) has courier-imap in it which I'm using as 
a local server.  Panther has postfix as its MTA, and enabling it (+ 
telling it to store in Maildir) is easy as hell.

Having a local IMAP server, I've found, is incredibly bloody useful.  
You just use an imap sync tool to sync it with your real server, and 
not only do you always have your mail no matter the network connection 
status, but you don't get duplicate emails, lost emails, and such.
