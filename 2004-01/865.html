The old space still exists but depending on whether you use `mv` command or
the `cp` command to move the contents of the old device to the new device
will determine if the data is still there as well as the new place.

> mount -t umsdos /dev/hdb1 /mnt/router/pkg/home


This will work just fine considering you actually have a directory called
/mnt/router/pkg/home and you have at least copied what you wanted from hda1.
Although you will have to update your /etc/fstab to reflect this change upon
successive reboots.
