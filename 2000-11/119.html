Re: controlled download via Squid

Steve Sloan wrote:
> 
> I am trying to find a way to control downloading in a school. All traffic is
> via a linux box running ipchains and Squid. I can control FTP easy enough
> but a lot of the download pages are on http sites and disabling ftp has no
> effect. Is there a way I can control the downloading via squid? Or maybe put
> a quota system in place on a per user basis, I seem to remember something
> along these lines not long ago but don't remember reading any conclusive
> solutions to the problem.


We use proxy auth here and every so often pick the top ten downloaders
and pull them to one side and have a word. Word gets around pretty
quick. The log files are very simple text files, so a perl script could
extract the info you need (In fact, I could probably find one for you
somewhere). Check out the squid site. I believe they have a heap of
contrib utils for that kind of thing....
