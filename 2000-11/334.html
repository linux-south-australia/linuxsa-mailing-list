Re: more scripting questions

"Freeman, Peter (ERHS)" wrote:

> Is there an easy way of using two variables in the following, one for
> username & one for uid:
> 
> For f in `cut -f1 -d: smbpasswd`
> Do
> Useradd $f -d /dev/null -s /bin/false
> Done

No.  But here's something I once posted to Ian H.:

#!/bin/sh

awk -F: '
{
    # Build useradd option line.
    opt = "-p \"" $2 "\" -u " $3 " -g " $4 " -c \"" $5 "\" -d " $6
    opt = opt " -s " $7 " " $1
    # Fire that puuppy off.
    system("useradd " opt)
}' /etc/passwd


Flavour to taste.  "Donuts^Wawk - is there anything they can't do?"
