Corel O/S

OK we all hate the sudden influx of corporations
putting there hands into our Linux, but I just have to
say one thing. Corel Linux second edition is the best
distribution I've seen without doubt.

They have taken everything good from Debian, made it
easy to install and have made it pretty damn good.
It's good to see a Distribution evolve from crap to
something great, unlike other un-named (RedHat and
Mandrake *8)) distro's who update the software and
call it a new release with little testing and big time
security holes, who just have to be the first with a
new release. This is not a troll, do not respond with
flames until you have tried Corel OS out.

I got it from the latest Linux Format Mag, of which
our little LUG is named *8)
