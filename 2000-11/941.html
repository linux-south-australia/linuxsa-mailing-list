cd-r... In this case 700meg cd-r rather than 650meg.

the looks of the 700 meg cd-r. Okay, it might be they are crappy cheap
disks, but my son bought them to make a backup copy of a game disk for him
(really, just to save the original from being damaged by the other kids
while they were playing). The image is 665 meg, hence the 80 minute
cd-r...
