I've heard a few people say it was 'very bad'. Not for critical use, pain
in the bum to compile stuff, etc.

Probably wouldn't worry me as a system to play with, but doubt I'd use it
for the main box - I like to wait for .2 releases :-)

BTW I install RH6.2 on a box for another person the other day and I must

unaware... Or at least it would take a lot more setting up to get 'right'. 

So I'm getting MAndrake 7.2 next... They are all good and have their good
points, and I started on Slack, but I just want something quick and easy
for the main working machine...
