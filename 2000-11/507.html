RE: sendmail antivirus scanners

Try AMaViS. If you look at the content headers of my Email message, you'll
probably find an "X-AntiVirus: scanned for viruses by AMaViS 0.2.1
(http://amavis.org/)" tag. ;]

Works almost specifically for sendmail, but it's way cool for postfix, qmail
and others. And it's free. All you need in addition is a scanner itself, and
it tells you where to go to get them.

http://www.amavis.org/
