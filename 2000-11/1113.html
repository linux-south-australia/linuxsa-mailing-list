Re: Telnet Not Working under RH 6.2

On Mon, Nov 27, 2000 at 06:07:55PM +1030, David Lloyd wrote:

 > > I am unable to telnet to the linux box. Message received is Connection
 > > Refused. What have I overlooked in getting this working ?
 > 
 > Are you logging in as root? By default, the superuser (root) cannot
 > login via telnet. Try logging in as a user.
 
That'll say "login incorrect", not "connection refused".

The problem is one of the following:

    - inetd is not running

    - inetd is running but the telnet service is not enabled

    - inetd is running and the telnet service is enabled, but telnetd
      isn't actually installed.

    - inetd is running and the telnet service is enabled, telnetd is
      installed, but /etc/services has the telnet service somewhere other
      than port 23

    - inetd is running and the telnet service is enabled, telnetd is
      installed, /etc/services on the machine running inetd is correct,
      but /etc/services on the machine running telnet is wrong ("Hi, Tim!")

Check /etc/inetd.conf, check "ps ax | grep inetd", check /etc/services.
The answer will be revealed.
