In the US there's a trend of making fault reports private.

Just to give everyone fair notice, in Australia AARNet will
never sign a contract containing such a provision, as it makes
informing our thronging multitude of users of what happened
far too difficult.

We have the entire set of AARNet, Telstra, Optus and Connect outage
reports, since Day One.  We added Southern Cross Network last week.
We'll be making these available on our re-vamped web site, complete
with a search engine.  Since this is a valuable resource, once that
web site is up I'd be happy to add the outage notices of other ISPs.
