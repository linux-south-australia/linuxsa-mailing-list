That fax is only as secure as the handling of the document at both ends -
plenty of cases of credit card fraud start with a discarded charge
document, and don't forget the opportunities in your own waste basket for
fraudulent use of your credit card details.

I once paid for some fax software with a downloaded app that encrypted the
details into a heap of gobbledegook that was deciphered at the other end -
not a bad idea for email, depending on the strength of the encryption I
guess..
