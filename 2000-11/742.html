It has been my experience that in order to save a few cents to dollard
most manufacturers will put crap fans on any piece of equipment. This is
usually fine for a home desktop pc on a couple of hours a day. On a work
desktop or server class system this is completely inadequate. I've lost
count of the number of cooling fans I've replaced on Power supplys, and
CPUs on systems I maintain. Mos failures occurr on systems that don't have
Bearing Fans installed. A couple of extra dollars will buy you one, and
I've never had a bearing fan fail.
