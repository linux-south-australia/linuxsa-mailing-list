Re: RH6.2 Telnet

Ryan Verner wrote:
> I've noticed on quite a few RH6.2 installations (including my own) that
> sometimes when the box is telnetted into, there is somewhat of a delay until
> the telnet server spits back the login screen.
> 
> I can see from syslog that it does connect instantly, but I am sometimes
> waiting 10 seconds - 10 minutes for the login screen to come up.
> 
> (login screen being: Red Hat Linux release 6.2 (Zoot); Kernel 2.2.14-5.0 on
> an i486; login:)
> 
> What could be causing this (This is even on default installations) and how
> could it be remedied?

The most common cause is the redhat box is trying towork out who is
connecting vis a hosts file entry or DNS.  You could try placing all
relevant hosts into the /etc/hosts file and see if that makes a
difference.  It's also a good idea to do this on any client machines as
well.
