Re: Smart Redial

Hi Ryan,
Better late than never, maybe.

If you're happy (sort of ) to redial every 4 hours, just not every 2 - 20
minutes, then would you be satisfied to be only disconnected every 4 hours?

I think your ISP is at fault here since I had a similar (well, exactly the
same) problem. At a guess I would think that your ISP is disconnecting you
after a period of inactivity for whatever reason (maybe they've run out of
modems and kick off the user with longest period of inactivity , or whatever)

I ended up writing a simple script that pinged an IP address (their's actually)
every few minutes with a single ping, and since then I have not been
disconnected before time once! The number of calls to my ISP has dropped by
2/3.
