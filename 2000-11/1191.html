RE: Some info on speed of connection

When telstra install it, they normally do a speed test where they ftp a 1
and or 10 meg file from and to a server on the ADSL network, they then
record the speed on the installation work sheet. They use a machine on the
"local" network so that other traffic doesnt degrade the the speed. You
could get your friend to try downloading a 10 meg file from somewhere like
http://mirror.aarnet.edu.au
I normally download 10 megs of a linux iso when I want to test my
connection.
