The other effective way of combating HTML mail on lists is to prefix all
of your posts with:

<BODY BGCOLOR=PINK TEXT=YELLOW>
<FONT FACE="wingdings">

Particulary anti-html list users may also want to add a "Content-Type:
text/html" to their mail headers, to ensure that anyone who even has a
program compatable with html will want to turn it off.

When people start complaining, you can have the same HTML-email-is-bad
thread in reverse :)
"What's that, someone posted something that you can't easily read, Mr
HTML Poster?"
