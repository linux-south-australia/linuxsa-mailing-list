ADSL Enabled Exchange Areas: Updated 20/11/2000

G'day folks,

South Australia now has 15 exchange areas enabled for ADSL:

BRIGHTON
EDWARDSTOWN
FLINDERS
GLENELG
HENLEY BEACH
LONSDALE
MODBURY
PARADISE
PROSPECT
REYNELLA
ST MARYS
UNLEY
WAYMOUTH
WOODVILLE
SALISBURY

To see if your phone line is capable of ADSL, visit Telstra's
ADSL query site at:

	http://www.telstra.com.au/adsl/ordering/search.cfm

and enter your phone number, select "Home User" and submit.

If you receive a positive (Congratulations!) result, and you
are interested in broadband internet access in your home and/or
your office, feel free to register your interest in Internode's
new ADSL offerings that are about to be released by contacting
Chris Merchant at Internode via email: chrism@internode.com.au
