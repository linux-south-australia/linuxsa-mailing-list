G'day Tim,

    These comments more probably belong in a review on the Toshiba Tecra
8100.  I have both loaded Red Hat 7.0 de novo and upgraded it from Red Hat
6.2.  You need to use text installation.  Installation de novo works just
fine.  However when you upgrade, the system hangs halfway through rebooting.
I haven't had time to investigate why as yet.

    I have also read that the old glib libraries are not loaded on the disc
and therefore WordPerfect will not work.  Again, I haven't had time to
investigate this properly.  I won't go into the other wrinkles on sound,
video and modem.

    Since I need a word processor, I will probably put Red Hat 6.2 back

Cheers,  Rick McQueen-Thomson
