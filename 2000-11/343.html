Re: Proxy Speed.

tafe.sa.edu.au is called "barnnie" and is the main gate for ALL of TAFE SA.  It
is not unusual for activities being carried out particularly on evenings.  It
was recently upgraded due to extreme overload, but from what I hear the upgrade
did little to improve things.  I use the educational side of things every day
and to be honest if they were a private ISP they would be out of business by now
(EDS control all the servers for TAFE SA) I believe there is not a great deal we
can do about it because we no longer control what happens with the equipment we
use.
If you think Regency is bad go to Port Adelaide and see what its really like.

Also we use  WebCT online training programs  and its a big drain on resources.
So if you are unlucky to use the connection during a heavy WebCT night its just
you luck :-)
