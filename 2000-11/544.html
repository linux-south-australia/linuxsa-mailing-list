AFAIK it's a specialised Sun related part, and I'm not aware of anyone in
Adelaide who handles Sun gear.  I can recommend Craig Dewick at Sun Ripened
Kernels http://www.sunrk.com.au/srk_main.html  but it's still going to be
around the $90 mark.  I've got a cable similar to this which I've tried on a
couple of Sun monitors, but beware that many Sun monitors will NOT work with
this cable / adaptor, as I've found.  There's a list on the net somewhere
which lists which Sun monitors do work, but I don't have the url handy at
the moment.  From memory most older model Sun monitors won't work.  Good
luck!
