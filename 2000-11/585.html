Volunteer Req: Community Aid Abroad

Hi all,

Community Aid Abroad is looking for some volunteers to provide
occasional help and advice with their Linux server in Adelaide.

For further information, see the links at the bottom of this message
or contact Robert Tune <robertt@adelaide.caa.org.au>.

Regards,
Geoffrey.

> REQUEST FOR VOLUNTEER LINUX EXPERTISE AT
> COMMUNITY AID ABROAD OXFAM AUSTRALIA
> 
> Community Aid Abroad Oxfam Australia's Adelaide branch office is
> seeking some volunteer expertise to provide occasional help and
> advice in the care and feeding of its Linux / Samba based office
> network.
> 
> We currently use the e-smith 4.0 distribution on our server, a kind
> of cut down Redhat 6.1 server "appliance" which provides file, web,
> proxy and mail services to (currently) nine Win 98 clients running
> Office 97, Pegasus e-mail and Netscape. Even aid organizations must
> commune with the dark side to some extent...
> 
> Most of the admin work is done remotely from national head office in
> Melbourne so a volunteer's duties mostly involve monitoring
> volunteer and IT oriented mailing lists and putting up your hand for
> tasks that need doing in the office when and where able.
> 
> The first step in getting involved is to subscribe to the S.A
> volunteers mailing list. Send mail to savol@scribe.caa.org.au,
> introducing yourself and giving an indication of your skills and
> interests. One of the IT people in Melbourne will get in touch with
> you and give you a run down of the current state of play and
> instructions for subscribing to the list if you so desire. From
> there it is up to you how involved you wish to be. Whatever help you
> can offer, CAA will be grateful. You can find out more about
> volunteering at CAA and our IT oriented work from the following
> sites.
> 
> 1. http://www.caa.org.au/groups/IT/index.html       :IT at CAA page
> 
> 2. http://www.caa.org.au/groups/IT/dili/index.html  :Linux in Dili
> 
> 3. http://www.caa.org.au/helping/work/volunteer/index.html
> 
>    This last one above is a general page on volunteering at CAA.
