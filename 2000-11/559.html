> Interactive 3D Visualisation of Modification of Facial Features
> 
> This project aims to develop user interface metaphors and tools for
> the manipulation of facial features depicted by 3D graphical models
> of human features.  The outcomes of the project are three systems
> for different users.  Planners design changes to the facial
> features, orthodontist. Recipients use the changed facial features,
> patients.  Evaluators retrospectively appraise the actual changes to
> those predicted by the Planners, researchers and educators.  Each of
> these users requires different forms of visualisation to enable them
> to perform their tasks.  The significance of the project is the
> non-invasive nature of the system, new metaphors, and the innovative
> visualisation techniques.
>
