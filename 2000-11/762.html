> You're better off using http://html2pdf.linuxave.net/

The General Theory of Emacs implies that there is some way 
of doing this from Emacs, so if you get sufficiently desperate
you might have a look at w3 (which will generate LaTeX) and
pdftex.

I've been sufficiently desperate in the past, and the results
are excellent for pages that are mainly text but dreadful for
your average GIF-driven page.

The advantage is that Emacs *is* an editor, so you can delete
all the navigation stuff and just print the content.
