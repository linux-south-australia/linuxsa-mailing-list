AUUG - Open source symposium - Blatant Plug if ever i'v seen one

Just a reminder that tomorrow is the AUUG Open Source Symposium.  It is
going to be held at the University of South Australia (city west campus).

It will be lots of fun and you will regret it if miss the chance to attend
this fine symposium.

And yes I am directly affiliated with AUUG!  However AUUG (the Australian
Unix User Group) is not about money.  It is about Unix enthusiasts doing
cool stuff.  In order to run events like AOSS in Adelaide, we really need
the support of the locals, otherwise Sydney and Melbourne will get all the
cool stuff and that would just plain suck.

Registration is at 8:30am Saturday (in the Barbara Hanraham Building), for
full details go to http://www.auug.org.au/aoss
