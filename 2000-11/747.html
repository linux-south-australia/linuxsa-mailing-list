> If I manually down ppp0 and manually bring the IF up the modem
> connects OK.  I have tried different ISP's with the same problem.

In /etc/sysconfig/netconfig-scripts/ifcfg-ppp0 (or whatever the device
is called), turn debuggin on by saying "yes" (as opposed to "no" or add
a DEBUG=YES line). In /etc/ppp/options add a "debug 4 [newline] kdebug
4". You'll get a pile of debuggin in /var/log/messages.

Secondly, to stop it automatically redialing, turn persist to "no" and
redial to "no" in that same file. Remember to turn them back to "yes"
once you've worked out what's wrong.
