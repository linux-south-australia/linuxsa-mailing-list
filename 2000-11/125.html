Re: WinModems and Their Legality

This link
http://www.austel.gov.au/publications/industry/Telecommbook.pdf  is
to Austel's sexy document RE this (2M download).  The question is
whether a driver constitutes "equipment" under the Telecommunications
Act (I'm not sure:
http://law.agps.gov.au/html/pasteact/2/3021/0/PA000240.htm).  It
would seem that it is an offence to hook up an uncertified device
(http://law.agps.gov.au/html/pasteact/2/3021/0/PA003690.htm), and
that if you developed such a driver you would proabably have to get it
certified by Austel before distributing it (subject to it being
"equipment") (This is a really interesting question).  You would
probably also breach any waranties that came with the modem (subject to
the Trade Practices Act).

I doubt that a computer is equipment under the Act (but that's a matter
of interpretation really).
