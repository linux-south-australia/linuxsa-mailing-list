Re: runing apps in the background

Jeremy Ervine wrote:

> Is it possible to start an application with some sort of flag so that it will
> start and immediately go into the background.

"&" as supported by most reasonable shells.  "foo &" (sans quotes)
runs foo in the background.  You may or may not have problems with
it being associated with a process group and controlling tty (I
can't remember if it is a problem with Linux, but can be with other
Unices).
