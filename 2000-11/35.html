The DUL is different from the RBL - that's the Dial Up List (or Dialup
User List?), and is basically a list of IP addresses of known dialup
points.  The reasoning is that dialup IP addresses should have no reason
for connecting to their SMTP ports (unless you are one of their customers,
in which case it's a misconfiguration on their part).
