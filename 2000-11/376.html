finally got it to work!

Awk, thanks to all those who replied to my queries re: creating accounts
from smbpasswd.
Its taken me almost as long as it would have if I created them all manually,
but the sense of achievement is much greater!
Here's the working script for those who are interested. 


#!/bin/sh

awk -F : '
{
	# Substitute field 1 in smbpasswd for username & field 2 for uid
	system("useradd -n -d /dev/null -s /bin/false -u " $2 " " $1)
}' /etc/smbpasswd
