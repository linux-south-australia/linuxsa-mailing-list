Re: IRC

Probably the closest in appearance and UI to mIRC would be XChat
(found at http://xchat.org/).  Most likely it should have come with
your distribution (most of the mainstream distros include it).

Another one with a slick GUI is KVirc.  This pretty much requires
KDE2 to run, so unless you've already installed it (for example if
you're distribution comes with it; like Mandrake 7.2) or you're
planning to upgrade to it anyway, you might want to stick to XChat
(although KDE2 is very good, especially for migrating from windows).
