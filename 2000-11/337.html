Australian Open Source Symposium in Adelaide

AUUG are holding the second Australian Open Source Symposium here in
Adelaide this month, on November 25.  Cost is only $60 for AUUG members and
$100 for non-members.  A special student price of $50 ($30 if you are an
AUUG student member) makes it very affordable for everyone.  You should
attend this event if you are interested in open source.  Full details on the
web at http://www.auug.org.au/aoss/.  Hope you can make it.
