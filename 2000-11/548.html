Re: Apache personal homepages and FTP

> I am trying to setup apache with personal webpages ie. /~username. Found out
> that putting a dir public_html under the users home dir got everything
> working.
> Now I set the users home dir to /home/username/public_html which allows them
> to ftp straight to the web page, which all works just great except how do I
> keep them going back up the tree with there FTP client. ie as the user I
> could go right back to / and then into any where I liked.
> Can I make them no see anything else or do I have make sure they are locked
> out of everything else.

Hi Steve,

You can do it with the 'Guest FTP' feature of wu-ftpd, which works
like a cross between anonymous & regular FTP.  There's a bit of info
about it in the FTP article I wrote for APC (look for 'FTP Server with
Linux' amongst the headings):
http://apcmag.com/apcmag.nsf/HTML/Workshop/linux?OpenView

ftpaccess(5) is also worth reading (search for 'guest').
