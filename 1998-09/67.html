Re: Xdefaults and xmailbox

>>>>> "AH" == Adam Hawes <adam@hilite.dyn.ml.org> writes:

  AH> Hello all.  I have a question that is probably really basic but
  AH> I am having trouble configuring xmailbox using the ~/.Xdefaults
  AH> file.

Try adding either of these lines to your .Xdefaults file and then
running xrdb -merge on it:

*xmailbox*mailsndcomm:  /usr/bin/play %s
*xmailbox*mailSndComm:  cat %s > /dev/audio
