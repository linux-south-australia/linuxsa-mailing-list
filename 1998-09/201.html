Commercial: Job Vacancy: C++ X-Windows/Motif Developer

Anyone interested in working as a Software Engineer
at Integra Australia (http://www.integra.com.au )
of Technology Park, S.A. might like to 
check the job advertisement in the 
Linux Australia's Jobs Database (http://www.linux.org.au/jobs/)
( job advertisement was lodged on Friday, 25 September 
  and so should  be on database by Sunday, 27 September. )
 
The present vacancy calls for a C++ X-Windows/Motif Developer
to work in a software development team role on a project to do 
with high performance graphics in OpenGL. They will need:
1. Strong C++ skills, preferably 3 or more years;
2. Good X-Windows/Motif skills;
3. Experience with development under Unix on a Sun platform
        preferably on a Sun UltraSPARC.

The applicant will preferably have:
1. Working knowledge of OpenGL 
        (need to use an existing OpenGL toolkit);
2. Australian Defence Security clearance 
                      to confidential or secret level
                      or otherwise eligible for such clearance
                      i.e. need to be an Australian citizen;
3. Java programming skills.

This placement will be for 12 months but might be extended
up to 18 months or two years duration.

To apply, please send your job resume
to trevor@integra.com.au by Wednesday 30 September 
or as soon as possible thereafter.
Contact Trevor Ambrose on (08) 8260 8948 for further information.
