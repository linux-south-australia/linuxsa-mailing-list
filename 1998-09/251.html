RE: Minor LaTeX problem

Hi everyone,

OK, I came across a similar problem a while back and I think you will
find it is the same problem.

I found I was getting about 1" of extra white space at the top of the
page. This was due to a bug in GhostScript that only affected some
HP LaserJet printer such as the 5L.
The only options are get a new printer. :)
or upgrade to a newer version of GhostScript. I think the problem
exists in V3 & V4 but was fixed in version 5. I know that my copy
of RedHat 5 comes with the buggy version but I just downloaded
the latest RPMs. Since it is a ghostscript problem you should
find it affects anything that uses it.

Nick Stock
