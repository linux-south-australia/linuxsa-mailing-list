LinuxSA extra meeting for September

Hi all,

The next LinuxSA meeting will be held (as usual) on the third Tuesday
of this month -- the 15th/September.  Matthew Tippett will be giving a
talk on Linux & Cryptography (or similar).

On the 24th/September, there will be an extra LinuxSA meeting, with
special guest Richard Stallman giving a talk on the history of the GNU
project and the GNU system.  If you only attend one meeting this year,
this is the one to be at.

For those who don't immediately recognise the name Richard Stallman
(aka RMS), Richard is the founder of the GNU project, the principle
author of the GNU C Compiler, the GNU debugger, GNU Emacs, and various
other GNU programs.  The majority of the software that makes Linux
tick is GNU software.

Richard is giving a talk at AUUG this year, and we are lucky to be
able to have him come to Adelaide to speak to us.

The reason for this email is to find out how many people would be
interested in coming along to the meeting on the 24th.  If there are
too many people, we won't fit into the room at Digital.  If you will
probably be attending this meeting (no definates required; I'm just
trying to gauge interest), please let me know by email to
geoffrey@netcraft.com.au

Thanks,
