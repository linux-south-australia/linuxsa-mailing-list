Linux Powered badges

Probably a bit late to add an opinion, but I think that Isaac's designs on
http://www.lynx.net.au/cybernet/linux.html are best. The font is what does
it for me... I also like the simplicity of them. My preference is for the
third option with the text, and the forth for the penguin (ie make the
penguin bigger on the third one.... Possibly even keep the big Yellow 'L'
and put 'linux' up on the vertical bit, in the sans serif font, and
'powered' along the bottom. OR have the L backwards, and etc.

I think what I really like about Isaac's design is the Font, the shadow
behind the penguin, and the 2D yellow bit -- I don't like overuse of 3D...
(ie make the big 'L' 2D -- it gives more focus to the words and the
penguin...)


As I said, probably a bit late, but I only saw Isaac's designs just now...
