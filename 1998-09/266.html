I got it on my machine.

	rpm -qf /usr/include/gtktty/gtktty.h

yields 

	gnome-libs-devel-0.20-1

So make sure that you have gnome-libs-devel installed on
your system.

Remember that if you don't get any of the -devel packages
you probably won't be able to compile against gnome.

Other than that, gnome 0.30 is quite a lot more stable and
a lot tidier on install..
