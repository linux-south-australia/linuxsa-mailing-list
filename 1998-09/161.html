Of course it does.  SRPMS aren't managed by the RPM database.  When
you do an rpm -i on an SRPM, you just get the source dropped into the
SOURCES and SPECS directories.  You can verify this by doing 'ls -la
/var/lib/rpm', 'rpm -ivh whatever.src.rpm', 'ls -la /var/lib/rpm' and
noting that the timestamps haven't changed.
