Re: linux access problem

The broadcast address is the same as the subnet mask, just replace the 0's
with 255's, ie 192.168.0.0 with 192.168.255.255 this tells the machine how
to talk to all the machines on the sub network, make sure this is
configured, it's pretty important. Man ipconfig should give you some more
information or have a look at the Net-3 HOWTO (may also have a 4 in it now
days!).
