HTTP, although being a simple protocol, does have lots of options.
My guess would be to look at what is actually travelling over the 
wire.

Your browser does *a lot* more than just do a GET / HTTP/1.0 when
talking to the server.  My guess, is that there is a funny setting
for one of the browsers.

To do this, have a look at 'ethereal'.  Of particular value is the
'Trace TCP Stream' option which will let you see what has travelled
over the wire.
