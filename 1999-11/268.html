Hi...

I had exactly the same problem and found it was caused by a wrong setting in
the kernel.
It was a while ago now, but im sure it was to do with the "Unix98 PTY
support" setting, i found that turning it "OFF" fixed the telnet problem.

Did you recompiled your kernel around the same time this problem poped up??
