On Fri, Nov 05, 1999 at 07:09:33PM +1030, Alan Kennington wrote:

> Question: What does UDP port 752 do?

Wouldn't have a clue - haven't seen it before. However, you can get the process
that owns it by running the 'lsof' command, as follows;

lsof -i udp:752

If it's not already installed, you can grab a package. Lsof (list-open-files)
is invaluable for this sort of thing.
