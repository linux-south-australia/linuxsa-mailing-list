Re: Off Topic: Modem question...

Andrew Williss wrote:

 > What exactly is the difference between a modem on a IBM PC
 > and an MAC? Is it just the cable?!!!

Yup!

 > does the mac require a specific driver for the modem or is general
 > support incorporated in to the O/S...

Support to drive the modem is always part of the base OS;  In almost
any case worth worrying about, basic TCP/IP software and a web browser
is included as an add-on to your OS too.  The Mac modem will come
with comms and fax software for a mac, but that's often exactly the
same CD as the PC version anyway.

 > Why is a mac modem generally more expensive than one for an IBM pc...

'cos Mac people are accustomed to paying more for hardware, and any
company with half a brain will charge as much as they can get away with
for any given widget or service.  

The power of the open-source movement is that it changes peoples' 
expectations about how much things are worth.
