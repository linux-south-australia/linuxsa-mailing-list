Re: Linux/Apache problem

just a quick thing

in access.conf, you need

<direcroty /home/fred/public_html/scripts>
Option ExecCGI
</directory>

and ls -lad /home/fred/public_html
should show:
drwxrwxr-x   2 fred  fred    1024 Nov 26 13:17 public_html


and ls -lad /home/fred/public_html/scripts
should show:
drwxrwxr-x   2 fred  fred    1024 Nov 26 13:17 scripts

notice the 'x' bits.

and restart httpd:
/etc/rc.d/init/httpd restart
