Re: Interested in Linux .....

Jeremy Ervine wrote:

 > I am asking if anyone on the Linux users group is able to assist in
 > helping me out with learning the Linux environment or have an tips
 > on how to get familiar with it faster so I can move my business and
 > my clients away from MS and into Linux.

Use it on your home computer *instead* of Windows.  Grab a Linux
office suite, some Linux games (Civilization: Call to Power, Quake-2,
etc) and play around with the programming tools; if you don't have a
Windows machine around and have to run Linux instead you'll gain
familiarity with it much faster.

Come to the LinuxSA meetings (you just missed one on Tuesday, so you
have another month 'til the next one) and ask (well-researched) 
questions on this mailing list.

It's challenging, but not difficult.

Good luck,
