Re: Telnet Problems

Hi Michael... I believe you are missing a period at the end of WIN in
your example. This would allow ANY machine called
win.something.something.mroe.more.more to log into the machine... OR,
which I think is best, use your network name here as in this example:

in.tftpd: LOCAL, .my.machine.net.au
or
ALL:LOCAL, .my.machine.net.au

This will allow ONLY the machines residing in the my.machine.net.au
network to use the facilities...

Please correct me if I am wrong guys-n-gals!

Good luck...
Frank
