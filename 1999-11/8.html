Re: Redhat 6.0 Install & SCSI Adaptec-1520B

Hi,

> While we're on the subject, what are some pro's and con's of partitioning
> the drive into separate /tmp /usr /var / partitions, or just making one
> big / partition?

I dislike partitioning if I can help it. It imposes a real physical limit
on a drive that is dificult to get around (even with partition re-sizers,
new disks, and directory linking).

Having said that, it is useful havin /var, and /tmp and /home on separate
partitions (particularly on multiuser systems), as it protects the system
from drive filling up (eg a large influx of mail, or a user filling up
their home directory)
