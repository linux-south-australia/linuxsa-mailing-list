Hi - Try (for testing) just having the directory name only in
/etc/exports

 eg/  just put /home  or /cdrom   with nothing else (meaning anyone can
access it)  and see if that works

Although I have RH-6.0 it only lasted abt 2 days before a number of
probs it has caused me to consign it to /dev/null where it belongs - so
I have no idea what wierdness it has in relation to nfs.

Doing the nfs "restart" manually may produce better results:
make sure portmap, mountd, nfsd are all killed (-HUP where appropriate)
and then restart them.
