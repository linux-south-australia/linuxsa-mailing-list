I doubt it.  I very muchly doubt it.  What if more than one screen displayed
a message?  Possibly a better idea is to use syslogd over a network and have
all the boxes logging to one log server.  Just make sure your network gear
is UPS'd :)

something like "The System Log File is Full".  That didn't mean that the
