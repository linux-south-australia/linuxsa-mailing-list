Yeah, the picture files are stored in /usr/share/Eterm/pix
Add more pictures to that directory.
You then have to update the text file :
/usr/share/Eterm/pix/pixmaps.list
Just add them like you see the others. Hack around a bit.
You can change the drop-down menu from /usr/share/Eter/themes/MAIN to
incorperate your new pictures. You can also change the font size,
colour, and other properties there... 
Of course I found all this out by hacking through the files, there may
be another way you are meant to do it for multiple users... (I'm sure
there is, but I don't care).
