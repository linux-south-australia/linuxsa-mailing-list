Re: Two quick questions about bash

Josh,

On Tue, 13 Apr 1999, Joshua Cole wrote:
> 1. Firstly, with a for loop can you use arithmetic substitution for
> the loop index?

Try:

   i=1
   while [ $i -lt 4 ]; do
     # whatever
     i=`expr $i + 1`
   done

> 2. Secondly, can you simulate arrays by using substitution in variable
> names?

Try:

  eval A$i=whatever

be careful with quoting.
