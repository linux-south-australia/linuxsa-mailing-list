Progress on Ethereal

Hi,

for those who might be interested, there has been some progress on Ethereal.

In our goal to make it the best packet monitoring package available, new
protocols have been added:

   TFTP
   FTP
   POP

The next protocol that I am looking at adding is SMB, which will hopefully
include SMB over NetBT, SMB over NetBEUI, and SMB over NBIPX. After that,
Telnet and SMTP will be added, and then anything I can think of ...

I don't know when the next release will be, but I imagine it will contain
at least the above three protocols plus the IPv6 support added by others.
