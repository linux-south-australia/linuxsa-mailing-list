Abobe Acrobat Reader for Linux - Sys Reqt's

I don't recall reading any posts on Adobe's
free Acrobat Reader for Linux, but happened
to find the System Req'ts today for the same:

http://www.adobe.com/prodindex/acrobat/acrrwhatsnew.html

In short, it says (re: pre-release ver. of Acrobat Reader 4.0):

"LINUX System Requirements
   Red Hat Linux 5.1 or later or Slackware Linux 2.0 or later
   32 MB of RAM
   12 MB of available hard-disk space"

Apparently, one can download the pre-release version now.
