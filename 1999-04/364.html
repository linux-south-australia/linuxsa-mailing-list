Re: Slow disk access speeds.

Hi Paul,

I've had a problem like this and it was because the CD burner didnt have the
power terminator jumper closed, I donno if its your problem but it might be
worth checking to see if you have termination at both ends of the SCSI bus
or if you have a Terminator pack on the back of the CD burner's case that
the power terminator jumper is closed.
- Dale Butterworth
