Re: ifup ppp0

I must agree with Alex on the "play as yourself and not root concept". I
have seen some Unix boxes (or what is left of them) after people have played
as root. The worst problem was causing the root file system to become
corrupt by running certain DiskSuite commands (okay this is Solaris, but the
point is the same for Linux, NT, VMS, or whatever OS you are using with the
notion of a super user), which play with mirrors.

Remember fsck is just 5 keystrokes away from destroying your installation...
