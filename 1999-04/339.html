Re: modem-I/O error

This could be a irq/ioport error....

I had this error pop up when I accidently put my network card and
card on the same io port in the isapnp.conf file as the com2 for my
modem...

so when I tried to dial my modem, it gave me an io error...

look in /proc/ioports and /proc/interrupts and /proc/ioports

to see what resources they are taking up
