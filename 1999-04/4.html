Melissa ID creator?

We've seen a bit of hysteria about the Melissa word macro virus lately,
but here's an article I've seen which talks about using another 'hidden
feature' in Word, the global identifier, which is based on your ethernet
MAC address. (Supposedly completely unique.)
Unless the author of the original document spoofed his identifier, the
FBI would be able to find the given netcard that was in the PC at the
time the document was first saved.
(I'd be interested to know the web site that it WAS originally posted
on, and how these people found the website, given the millions of them
around. That might be more interesting than the story of just extracting
the ID number)

Toby

numbers.
