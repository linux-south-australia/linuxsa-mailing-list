Re: MIME File extensions - URGENT NEED HELP

> what are MIME types for    .pl  and  .cgi

Those extensions generally don't have particular mime types.  .pl &
.cgi are usually perl or other scripts which are executed by a web
server, and then their output includes the mime type of the document
they create.
