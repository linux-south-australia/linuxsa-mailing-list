Just an update on the situation. Still nothing definite, but looks good.

Sounds like people are interested, so I spoke to our IT guy in
Melbourne. He said that the preferred option was for them to go into the
big green bin out the back. When I told him that people from LinuxSA
were interested in them, he liked the idea that they would go to someone
