Re: IP Fowarding

Is it a standard Redhat 5.2 installation? As far as I can remember, it is,
but you have to enable it. Do this my changing the line:

FORWARD_IPV4=false

in the /etc/sysconfig/network file to:

FORWARD_IPV4=true

And hopefully that will work.
