Hi Steve,

Well Eagle only make the boards not the actual chipset.
You need to find out what chip is on the board.
I would say it is a Trident 9750 but I don't see any
support in XFree86 for any Trident above the ProVidia9685
so it may be a very new card that is not supported, so you
may be in a spot of bother.

What's wrong with the good old S3 Virge DX (375). Dam fast
and as stable and well supported as they come. :)

As far as AGP goes, the official word from the XFree team...

Q.F14- What about AGP?
At this point most AGP cards with supported chipsets are known to work.

When in doubt RTFM :)
http://www.xfree86.org/FAQ/
http://www.xfree86.org/FAQ/3.3.3.1/index.html

Oh... and make sure that you have the latest version.
