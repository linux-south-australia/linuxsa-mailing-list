Time for the LinuxSA April 1999 Meeting:

  When:   7:00pm on
          Tuesday, 20th April, 1999
  Where:  2nd Floor
          Compaq Computer
          139 Frome Street
          Adelaide SA
  Cost:   FREE
  Who:    Anyone and everyone.
          No pre-registration necessary.

      ** Be on time or early, it might be standing room only **

Presentation:

    This month we are having a double whammy.  

    Venture Capital in South Australia
    ==================================

	Firstly we will have Grant Warnes from the Playford Centre is going 
	to be giving a talk on the availability of Venture Capital within 
	South Australia.

	More information is available on the Plaford Centre's web site at
	http://www.playford.com.au/

    Commercial Opportunities with Open Source Software
    ==================================================

	After the Venture Capital Presentation and questions, we will be having 
	a panel to discuss what real commercial opportunities there are with
	Open Source Software.  Not just the development of the software, but the
	peripheral industries outside of development.  

	Feel free to ask to join the panel, or just sit in the crowd to ask the
	curly questions.
