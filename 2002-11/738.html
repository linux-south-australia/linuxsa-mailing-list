Re: LPR, CUPS, LPRNG

The cups interface is good. You get a browser window with good menus for 
monitoring printers. More importantly, cups printer drivers are very 
extensive and are being improved all the time. Gimp print  drivers show in 
the cups printer config - so for graphics you have extensive choice.
