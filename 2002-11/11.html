Wireless conns between buildings, pros, cons?

Here's the scenario:  
I have a primary LAN at a small site which needs a small satellite
office (3 PCs) to be connected in some way to the primary LAN.  At
first we looked at fibre which is what we have used in other sites to
connect satellite offices (talking <500m distances here).  However 
Tel$tra aren't allowing the use of their cable run so we have to look
at above ground options and I'm thinking wireless may be the way to
go.  The satellite office is approx. 700m away with LOS to the main 
building.

My initial thinking is to use a <insert whatever> connection between the
two LANs and then run a VPN over the top of the connection for security
reasons.  Is this over the top or the status quo?  Is the builtin security
in some of the wireless routers sufficient here?  We're talking fairly
sensitive data so security needs to be considered.

Comments, suggestions anyone??
