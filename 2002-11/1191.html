Not sure if this is just for HP SureStore or all generic DAT devices,
but hey...

----
How to check or turn on/off compression
To check current status of compression: 

mt -f /dev/st0 datcompression

To turn on compression: 

mt -f /dev/st0 datcompression on

To turn off compression: 

mt -f /dev/st0 datcomppression off
----
