Re: Bluetooth was [ADV] PCMCIA Wireless Network Card

bluetooth is for connecting small devices together such as your laptop to your mobile phone 
/ printer / microwave oven etcetera.  802.11b (wifi) is wireless ethernet for file sharing, 
internet access etc.

if you have more than one 'puter and want to network them use wifi, if you want to search 
endlessly for bluetooth compatable devices then that's the go. ;-)

i wouldn't 'invest' in bluetooth hardware, although 802.11b is worth the money (imho).  
bluetooth is yet to be proven as much more than a technology companies want us to want.

hope this helps.
