As of now, I feel that, if the Linux you own was made in the last 6 
months, than I feel that, more
often than not, that keyboards, monitors, printers floppy drives, cdroms 
and mice will work properly
after a fresh Linux installation.

I say 'safe bet' because things pop up now and then with regard to 
monitors, printers and mice,
like the mouse issue on topic.

Now if you want a webcam, PDA, sound, cdrom burning, or applications 
unique to Windows

things AFAIK.
