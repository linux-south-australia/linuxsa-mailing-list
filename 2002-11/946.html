Peter,

I upgraded to Evolution 1.2 from Ximian's RPM package for Mandrake 9.0.
Upgrading was seemless, I just left my Evolution directory alone and
didn't have to perform any importing of my Evolution 1.0.8 data.

One weird thing was that my contacts didn't show up. I quit Evolution
typed killev, restarted and everything was OK.

Evolution 1.2 is a lot faster for indexing operations. The hard disc
doesn't churn nearly as much as compared to Evolution 10.8 when
receiving new mail.

Cheers,
Joseph Tan
