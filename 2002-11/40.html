(solution) Flash and sound lockups while Sound device active

Hey All,

Sorry if someone has already posted something like this, but I didn't
see anything after a quick search through the archives.

Normally, I have XMMS playing songs continuously, however, if I went to
a webpage with Flash, the plugin would lockup Mozilla until the sound
device became available.

To fix this problem, try getting the Flash Player 6 beta:

http://www.macromedia.com/software/flashplayer/special/beta/


I hope this solves this annoyance for a few people =)
