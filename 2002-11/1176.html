Quite a few of the Geforce 2 MX Cards only had Heatsinks and no Fans, so I'd
guess that you shouldn't have too much of a problem without one. Quite a lot
of the Fans I've seen of GF2MX cards are fairly woeful when it comes to
airflow anyway. The hard core overclockers usually replaced the standard fan
with something else anyway.
