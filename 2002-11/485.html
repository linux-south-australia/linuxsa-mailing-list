Re: CD Burning under Lycoris

I happen to like cdbakeoven.

There is on trick to it, and that is if you want to burn cd's as root, 
you have to issue this command:

*optional----> which cdrecord   (Tells you where cdrecord is)
chown root.root /usr/bin/cdrecord
chmod 4111 /usr/bin/cdrecord  (Assumes which cdrecord responded with 
/usr/bin/cdrecord.)

Here is the reference:

http://wt.xpilot.org/publications/linux/howtos/cd-writing/html/CD-Writing-4.html#ss4.22
