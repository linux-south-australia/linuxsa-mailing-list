Re: Samba printers

On Tue, Nov 12, 2002 at 04:28:18PM +1030, Matthew Western wrote:
> err, a workstation sharing a printer?  you mean you have parallel printers
> as shared printers on your network?  we have 10/100 base T ones...    then
> you don't need any workstations with reserved addresses  :)_

Using either WINS or Dynamic DNS as your name resolution protocol gives you
the kind of dynamic name resolution you're talking about. You can leave your
workstations set to use DHCP, but still call them by their name and therefore
"point" a print client at it (such as the SMB/CIFS or LPR/LPD clients).

Hope it helps,
