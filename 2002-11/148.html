To answer your questions.  Registered RAM will be advertised
as registered RAM as it is more expensive and slower.  It's not
yet a retail line, you'll need to order from a specialist supplier
(such as a local retailer with a big memory range or a specialist
mail order memory company).  There are no indutry-wide chip markings
for registered RAM, so you'd need to know the manufacturer's part
numbers.  The registers are on the chip (that's the point), so they
aren't externally visible.
