Re: Fan less power supplies

On Thu, Nov 28, 2002 at 09:36:43AM +1030, Benjamin Close wrote:
> Hi All,
>     I'm in the process of building a 'quiet' computer and are looking 
> around for a fan less power supply. It only has to supply 55W.
> 
> Anyone know where one can be purchased in SA?

I've looked into this previously, and one of the best resources for
quiet PCs is the SilentPC mailing list. Subscribe or read archives at:
http://groups.yahoo.com/group/Silent-PC/ ...

I don't think there were many fanless PSUs around, but you could
always get something that is bigger and well-built, and risk removing
the fan... The other thing you can try is a silent PSU, preferably
one with a variable fan speed -- Enermax are supposed to be OK...
