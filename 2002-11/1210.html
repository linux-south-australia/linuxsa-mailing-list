Problems with video card refresh rate

Hi,

My video card is a Trident Providia 9685 (detected as
a Trident TGUI 9660 by RedHat Linux 8.0). I cannot get
into graphical installation mode niether start up X
system because of a disagree between my video card and

although refresh rate is detected absolutely correct
