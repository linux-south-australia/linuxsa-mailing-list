> I think a lot of you are missing the original point.

Missing it? More like mutating it.

> ...why? 

Because text isn't enough. Perhaps a limited markup language that 
allowed tables, perhaps colours, fonts, and the ability to embed objects 
like pictures (you can send pictures attached to text mails too). People 
want you to read what they compose, they want pictures to appear in a 
certain order, with captions. It's not possible with text.

This limited language would be format preserving, unlike html.

If you are receiving a lot of emails that you don't want with a lot of 
images you don't want, that sucks, but it's not a technology problem, 
it's a spam problem. They have different solutions...
