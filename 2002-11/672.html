Right, a page with a phpinfo() call will overwhelm you with facts, but it will 
tell you the command line options used in the configure and what has been 
compiled in - that will tell you if GD support is available and therefore the 
story on imagecreate()

Do a search in a phpinfo() page for "gd".  Or to avoid serving this up over 
HTTP, do a 'echo "<?php phpinfo(); ?>" | php | less' and search there.
