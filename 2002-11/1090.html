Re: UPS's

And while we're on the topic, what's with the power connectors on
UPS's?  They all seem to have the male computer power connectors
(don't know the real name for that style of three-prong), which means
you can't plug just any old device into them, you have to have one
that takes the right type of cable. Why don't they just have the
normal Australian-style connectors? Can you get adapters, so I can
plug (say) a Modem PSU into it? Is there any reason why you shouldn't?
I mean, seriously, who has oodles of male-female computer three-prong
power cords lying around anyway? </rant>
