If you want to graph ping responses, it's rather difficult to go past 
'smokeping' from the author of MRTG.

It uses rrdtool as the backend, and shows packet loss, ping time 
distribution (the 'smoke') and now has added background colouring based 
on link uptime. Extremely handy for tracking those 'intermittent' 
network faults, spotting noise on wireless links, and getting a feel for 
performance over time.

http://people.ee.ethz.ch/~oetiker/webtools/smokeping/
