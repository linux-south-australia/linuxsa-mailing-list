linuxmanagers

Hi,

I've recently come across the sunmanagers mailing list
(http://www.sunmanagers.org/), which is a fantastic resource for
running a Sun server. The idea is that you hcan post a decent problem
description to the list, and people on the list reply individually to
you, so you can solve your problem, and then you post a summary of how
you solved it. The traffic is low -- a few messages a day -- and it's
all tech, no discussion. They have strict rules that there be no
discussion of anything on that forum, which makes it extraordinarily
useful. Anyone know of anything similar for Linux? There was once a
linuxmanagers, but I can't find it anymore...
