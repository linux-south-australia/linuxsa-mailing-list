Re: UPS's

On Tue, Nov 26, 2002 at 03:23:21PM +1030, Richard Russell wrote:
> Any other comments re UPSes?

they're pretty much compulsory if you value your computer.
the quality of the power supply in SA is incredible.
my UPS has saved my machines many times I'm sure (see my
next post) anyway....

I've got a sola 520 I bought second hand for $150 (a while
ago) from some mob in Melb, some kind of PC recyclers, I try
to remember to find their name tonight.  It works great, true 
sine wave output, got 2 normal power point sockets on the back
(I agree the PC type connectors are a pain in the arse) 
into which I plug a power board.  It keeps 2 PCs going for about 
20-30 mins (I think it's 750VA).  I have yet to get the 
auto-shutdown thing happening (supposedly I just have to configure
'nut' and it works), which has become less of a concern now both
those machines are ext3.
