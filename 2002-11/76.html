Incidently, if you use a minus after the "hereis" operator, you can indent
your text (with tabs only).

cat <<-EOF
	blah blah blah
EOF

(and of course, those reading this email with Outhouse & Friends won't
see the tab character at all, as it seems to strip them, innovative
mail readers that they are).
