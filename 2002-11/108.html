Netwinders available

The ARM distributor for Australasia is making a bulk order of Netwinder
StrongARM systems and is willing to tack on extra individual orders
provided they are in to helen@bluewatersys.com by the end of the week.
Price is USD470 FOB Christchurch, and shipping from there to .au is cheap.

For those weren't around at the time: these systems were sold as low-cost
Linux office gateways. They company went broke and then came back again
with a Transmeta product, but they still have old stock. See
http://www.netwinder.net/2100. It runs Linux but nothing else (someone
started a BSD port but it never got finished.)

My interest in these is that they look and behave like a normal
computer (you can plug a screen/kbd/mouse into them and they have a PCI
bus) but they are binary compatible with hundreds of kinds of devices that
run ARM Linux. However I am leaning towards an Itsy motherboard instead
because it runs a range of operating systems.
