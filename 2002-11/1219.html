Re: Problems with video card refresh rate

Ok, well I read the bit about the "graphical installation" :) I had a
problem with my projector reporting incorrect values. I got around it by
waiting untill it says probing for monitor type, then pulling out the
VGA cable and waiting for 20 seconds before plugging it in. It just used
nice sensible default values. Having said that, your other option is
just to install in text mode. Eg, when the the cd first boots enter
"linux text" and hit enter.
