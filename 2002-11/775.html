Re: [OT] Eclipse Madness

On Sunday, 17 November 2002 at 13:06:26 +1030, Richard Russell wrote:
> Hi all,
>
> Is anyone planning on going to see the total solar eclipse on December
> 4th? Good places to be near include Ceduna, Roxby Downs, and Leigh
> Creek. Each is between 600 and 800km away from Adelaide, but it could
> be worth the effort. Of course, it's at about 8pm a Wednesday night,
> so it's not a weekend or anything...

In particular, it's just before sunset, so it's not going to be nearly
as spectacular as it would be in the middle of the day.  That's also
the time of day when you can expect a few clouds.

For the same reason, Ceduna is a better choice than Roxby Downs or
wherever; it'll get there a few minutes earlier when the sun isn't
quite so low in the sky.

Because of these considerations, I've decided to cancel my plans to
go.  I'll get up on the hills behind my house and watch a partial
eclipse instead.
