uPNP traffic? port 1900?

I have a machine running RH 7.3 with Ximian desktop (192.168.0.40).  I
ran the Ximian "update everything" feature the other day (about 80 megs
worth), which updated all sorts of packages.  Since then, we've noticed
a "buzz" (averaging about 500bps) on our internet traffic graph that
wasn't there before (and disappears when this machine is shut off) - and
I get these 11 packets appearing almost exactly every 25 seconds

The machine's default gateway is 192.168.0.5, which is a uPNP capable
D-Link NAT/Firewall box.  I have no idea what 239.255.255.250 is.

I've looked around, and it appears to be something to do with uPNP NAT
but I don't know much about it - does anyone have a quick answer?
