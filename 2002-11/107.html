RedHat 8.0 vs Compaq EVO N1000c

Greetings all,

I have a query regarding running RedHat 8.0 on a new Compaq Evo N1000c
Notebook machine. We have successfully run RedHat 8.0 on this notebook's
predecessor, a Compaq ARMADA E500.

The problem I am having is a strange one. It appears that the X
configuration is correct, in that the mouse and background images seem to
display correctly, but any text or icons appear with a bizarre stripe-type
effect rendering them almost unreadable.

I've messed with numerous vsync and hsync settings and even dropping to a
lower resolution (640x480), with the same outcome. All the time,
background-type images (even title bars on gnome windows) appear fine.

Needless to say, the Compaq website has been less than helpful.

I won't dump an ugly XF86Config file here, I guess mainly I'm just wondering
