Re: Configure a cron job not to email me?

On Fri, Nov 22, 2002 at 11:30:12AM +1030, Adam Smith wrote:
> I have a cron job that executes extremely frequently on FreeBSD, and I'd
> prefer it not to email its stdout/stderr to me.  How do I go about
> formatting the cron job correctly so that its output diverts to
> /dev/null?

you can also try the "MAILTO" variable in the crontab file
(man 5 crontab)
trouble is it applies to all cron jobs, maybe you can do:

MAILTO=""
*/2 * * * * /your/troublesome/job
MAILTO="root"
1 * * * * /normal/job1
1 * * * * /normal/job2
and so on

dunno if that'll work, you'd have to give it a go.
