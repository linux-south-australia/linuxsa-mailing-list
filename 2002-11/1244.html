Hi all,

Yet another question from ask-o-matic Richard.

In /etc/init.d/iptables on my Debian woody (3.0) system, the iptables
script has comments in it (which aren't accessible to me right at this
point in time) that say something to the effect of "don't use this
script under any circumstances", but doesn't say why. The script
