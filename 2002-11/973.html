Re: Spam

On Fri, 2002-11-22 at 11:44, Michael Campbell wrote:
> Can someone quickly tell me how to sumbit spam to the ORDB or SPEWS?

ORDB doesn't list spammers, it lists open mail relays.  I believe there
is a "test address" facility on their web, and I would assume that if
the address you give is an open relay then it will be added to their
data base.

SPEWS web explains, "SPEWS does not run a request or nomination based
list, entries in the list come from the knowledge and experience of the
people who set up and use the SPEWS lists."
