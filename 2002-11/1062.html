> If the other suggestions from people on the list don't help you much (as
> I dont think they will), you can try hand editing the
> /etc/sysconfig/desktop file so that DESKTOP="KDE" becomes
> DESKTOP="GNOME" or vice versa. All the gui's just try to edit this file
> and also play about with complex arrangements of .xinitrc.switchdesk
> files and .Xclients-default files etc.....
> You can run around for a long time trying to understand all those
> dotfiles so the quick solution is the /etc/sysconfig/desktop file. Just
> be aware that dotfiles in $HOME/ can sometimes lead to confusion.
