Hmmm... according to the SCSI HOWTO, the devices nst0, nst0a, nst0l,
ast0m have got to do with what mode you access the device in, where no
trailer is mode 0, l = mode 1, m = mode 2, a = mode 3.
As you already know, the n on the front designated no-rewind.

I'll have a bit more of a dig around and see if I can't find what the
modes mean.
