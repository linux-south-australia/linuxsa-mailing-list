Re: Samba printers

On Tue, Nov 12, 2002 at 11:35:52AM +1030, Andrew Galdes wrote:
> Hello all,
> 
> I am having trouble getting the "auto download printer drivers to the
> windows system" feature of samba working. The trouble that i am having is
> finding the drivers to use. I know what kind i want but i don't know where
> or how to get them. 

A lot of people have had success in the past using Adobe's Generic Postscript
driver (from www.adobe.com) on the Windows side (therefore one printer driver
for all printers) and doing the Postscript->Printer filtering (where
necessary) on the Linux box.

If you're looking for the drivers for your specific printer, I'd check the CD
(they usually come with Windows drivers) or their web site.

If you're using Windows 2000 or "greater", Novell 6 or greater, Mac OS 10.x
and/or any flavour of UNIX, IPP might be a better printing protocol choice.
A UNIX implementation can be found at www.cups.org. It's also capable of
using other (legacy :-P) protocols such as SMB/CIFS, JetDirect and LPR/LPD.


Hope it helps,
