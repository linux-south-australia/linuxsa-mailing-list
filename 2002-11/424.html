RE: Mouse problems day 2

Hi all,

Just a few thoughts:

1.  GPM (the console mouse program) can play tricks with some mice and
X.  I have had no end of trouble with GPM doing a thing where my USB IM
Explorer would eventually not reach the left of the screen - the pointer
would stop about 100 pixels right of it, and that was the left. 
Disabling GPM permanently and rebooting fixed that.

2. X needs the protocol IMPS/2 if you're using the USB to PS2 converter,
otherwise there is another protocol to use.

3. Look in /usr/X11R6/lib/X11/doc/README.mouse and read it carefully. 
There's lots of information in there about what is and what isn't
supported.

Cheers,
Adam
