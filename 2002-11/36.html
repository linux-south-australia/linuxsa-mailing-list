Re: Linux Games

http://www.transgaming.com/sims.php is probably the best way to get sims
working under Linux.

There is a reference to NHL 98, 2001, 2002 and 2003.  98 appears to be
the only one that works under winex - "Joystick not tested, but works as
it is supposed to with keyborad and mouse. No problems with graphics or
sound."

NFS3 - "Users have reported it working in winex 1.0.2. The only problems
seem to be slow graphics and non-perfect textures, aswell as no sound."

The *only* problems???  I can't think of much else that could go
wrong...
