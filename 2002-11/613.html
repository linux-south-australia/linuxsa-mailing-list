RE: Samba printers

>You could always try using a protocol other than SMB/CIFS. 
>Windows NT has
>*very* basic support for LPR/LPD through the 'Microsoft TCP/IP 
>Printing'
>service that you can install through the network control 
>panel. That will do
>what you want (by the sounds of things) 

This is how we setup all of our printers.  The primary reason for
this is the ease of management over SMB/CIFS.  All our sites are 
DHCP, and its much easier to reserve an address for a workstation
sharing a printer than messing about with 6 different share names
and getting confused about what is where and how....ok I might be
exaggerating a little, but you get the picture :)

(It also simplifies things if you have a mixture of network printers
and workstation attached printers, if you're using LPR printing they're
all treated the same way by the print server)
