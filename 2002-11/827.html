One point of view says that LinuxSA does not exist at all legally. The
other point of view, which I tend to agree with, is that LinuxSA is an
unincorporated group of individuals who have a reasonably well-defined
purpose and membership criteria [which incidentally opens another set of
considerations].

Glen Turner actually got some real legal advice about what risks would
occur if someone (eg, Geoffrey Bennet) made a statement on behalf of
"LinuxSA" for some definition of "LinuxSA". That advice in summary was
that it was a tricky and perhaps dangerous path to lead.
