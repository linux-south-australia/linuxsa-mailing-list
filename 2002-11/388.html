Re: Html Emails

On Fri, Nov 08, 2002 at 01:23:28PM +1030, Nick Morrison wrote:

> If you use mutt, you can make it invoke lynx to render the HTML to
> relatively friendly-looking text.
> 
> Put this in your ~/.mailcap:
> 
>   text/html; /usr/local/bin/lynx -force_html -dump %s; copiousoutput
> 
> 
> and this in your ~/.muttrc:
> 
>   auto_view text/html
> 

Also, you can control whether to show the text/plain or text/html part of an
email using:

    alternative_order type[/subtype] [ ... ]
          This command permits you to define an order of preference  which
          is  used by mutt to determine which part of a multipart/alterna-
          tive body to display.  A subtype of "*" matches any subtype,  as
          does an empty subtype.

e.g.  alternative_order text/plain text/html
