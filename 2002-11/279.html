Re: [OT] ADSL Offer

Hi.

First, I'd like to say that I believe that both of these ISP's are good
quality long-term players in the adelaide market, so don't take these
comments the wrong way...

If you do the math, you will see that there is very little difference in
the rack rates of $119 and $135. Why? because Adam charge on a 4 week
cycle, which gives 13 months against Internode's 12. So that's $1547 vs
$1620 per year, not $1428 vs $1620. You also have to consider the risk of
blowing the download limit metered on a weekly basis against a monthly
one.

As usual, feel free to corrct me if I'm wrong, but that's how I see it.

Michael
