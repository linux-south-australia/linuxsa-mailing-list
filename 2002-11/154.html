[ANNOUNCE] LinuxSA November 2002 Meeting

Hi all,

Time for the LinuxSA November Meeting...

Here are the usual details:

  When:   7:00pm (doors open 6:45pm) on
          Tuesday, 19th November, 2002
  Where:  2nd Floor
          Compaq Computer
          139 Frome Street
          Adelaide SA
  Cost:   FREE
  Who:    Anyone and everyone.
          No pre-registration necessary.

Presentation:

David Lloyd will be giving a talk on the Squid Proxy Server. The talk
will cover:

- What proxy servers do, what their uses are, and how they work

- Obtaining, installing, configuring, and testing Squid as a regular,
  transparent, and reverse proxy

- Understanding and analysing Squid logs 

Extras:

  As per usual, if you are having a problem with your Linux
  installation, feel free to bring your machine.  Make sure to bring
  everything necessary to make your computer work (eg. monitor,
  keyboard, etc).

For more information:
