Re: Fan less power supplies

On Thu, 28 Nov 2002 09:36:43 +1030, Benjamin Close
<cisbjc@cs.unisa.edu.au> wrote:

>Hi All,
>    I'm in the process of building a 'quiet' computer and are looking 
>around for a fan less power supply. It only has to supply 55W.
>
>Anyone know where one can be purchased in SA?
>
>Cheers,
>    Benjamin

If you connect a suitable resistor in series with the fan of a normal
PSU you can slow it down until it is inaudible but will still cool a
PSU running at reduced load. Try around 180 ohms 0.5W.

If 55W is all you need, you might even be able to get away with simply
disconnecting the fan altogether. Run the computer on the bench for a
while, with the lid unscrewed from the PSU but still in place. Then
***UNPLUG IT FROM THE MAINS***, lift off the PSU lid and feel the
heatsinks. If you can keep your finger on them comfortably it's
probably OK.
