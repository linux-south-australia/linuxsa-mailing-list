I can't but one piece of advice: Get another hard disk and do a dd (man
dd) copy of the contents of the dead hard drive onto it. And work on the
copy.

DO NOT ATTEMPT TO RECOVER ANYTHING WITHOUT THIS COPY

Recovering data is likely to damage the file system a couple of times
before you get it right.
