I've configured nagios at work. It is quite complex to setup initially
and you have to enter each node into your config yourself (i.e. it
doesn't auto-discover), but once setup, adding new nodes isn't too hard
and it gives you a LOT of flexibility on what you want to monitor.

You say you only want ping times now, but will you want to expand on
that later ? If so, it's probably worth the 20 hours or so to setup
nagios now, rather than get locked into something that doesn't expand
later.

Just my 2 cents worth anyway.
