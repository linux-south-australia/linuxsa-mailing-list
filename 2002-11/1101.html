Re: UPS's

I picked up a dead APC Back UPS Pro 650VA at a computer auction for 20bucks
($22 with GST).  Threw a new UPS battery in it ($80) and works great.  Of
course your milage may vary but most IT department throw equipment rather
than simply fix so computer auctions are a great place to pick up cheap
items that with a simple fix will serve you well for years (Large workgroup
laser printers come to mind here).

Would not live without my UPS.
