This suggests to me that the suse computer is not forwarding anything from
the clients through to the internet. Consider reconfiguring your suse
route/firewall to allow ip masquerading. Use the GUI tool if need be. 

some things to try:
-ping both the mail server and the clients from the suse machine. this will
tell you if your routes are correct on suse. The command to show this is
"#route -n".
