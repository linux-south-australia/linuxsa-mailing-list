I've used MS Mail, MS Outlook and MS Outlook Express and they all can
display a mail message in a window with no unreasonable adornment.
It sounds like you were using Outlook, and viewing the message in the
preview pane.

If the problem was merely an excessively long subject line there were
other alternatives, too, for example dragging the mouse through the
subject to scroll it, or cut-n-paste the subject to a different window.
I'm not saying these are nice things to have to do, but they are
quite reasonable for the pathalogical case you seem to be describing.
A standard 80 character window could have had the same sorts of problems
with that message, so you seem to have described a situation where HTML
email probably was, or would have been, an improvement.
