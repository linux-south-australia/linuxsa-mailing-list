Andrew Lord wrote:

> G'day all,
> 
> I've been attempting to get uudecode to decode a base64 encoded attachment
> that came with an e-mail to me, but so far without success.

That's "base64 encoded", not "uuencoded" - you should be using "base64 -d".
If you don't have base64 (mine is part of the openssl suite), then "uudecode
-m" may work, according to uudecode's man page.
