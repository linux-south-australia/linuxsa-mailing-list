Linux.Conf.Au

Hi LinuxSA'ers,

In 11 weeks and 20 hours, 27 minutes, and 41 seconds,  Linux.Conf.Au starts in 
Perth.  This is going to be one kick-butt open-source conference!  It follows 
in the tradition of l.c.a.'s that have been held in Brisbane, Sydney and 
Melbourne.  See http://conf.linux.org.au for more details.

If you are free in January, and you can make your way to Perth you should come 
and immerse yourself in the power of the source for 4 days.  The confirmed list 
of speakers (and some accomplishments) includes:

Andrew Tridgell (samba, rsync)       Paul "Rusty" Russell (kernel hacker)
Telsa Gwynne (GNOME docs)            Alan Cox (kernel hacker, GNOME)
Rasmus Lerdorf (php)                 Bdale Garbee (Debian proj leader)
H. Peter Anvin (kernel hacker)       Jeff "Hemos" Bates (/.)
Geoffrey Bennett (LinuxSA superstar) Alexander Reeder (kernel hacker)
David Glance (Linux in education)    Martin Pool (librsync, distcc)
Martin Schwenke (???)                Greg KH (pci,usb kernel hacker)
Rhys Weatherley (DOTGNU)             Malcolm Tredinnick (GNOME)
Brad Hards (USB CDC Eth driver)      Philip Hart (???)
Matthew Wilcox (PA-RISC Linux)       Conrad Parker (Sweep)
Wayne Piekarski (ARQuake)            Adrian Chadd (Squid)
Gavin Sherry (PostgreSQL)            James Henstridge (GNOME)
Jason Bainbridge (KDE Usability)     Jeremy Kerr (LVS)
Jeremy Malcolm (OSS Licencing)       Jeff Waugh (GNOME Release Mgr)
Suresh Kodati (networking)           Thomas Dreibholz (High Availability)

Why am I saying this?  I've been before and these conferences are very cool, 
you get to meet famous people and you learn a lot.  What will it cost?  $AUS250 
for hobbyists, $AUS99 for students and $AUS550 for people whose companies are 
paying.

So get out those pennies, register and pay now, and come along with other 
LinuxSA'ers and have a fantastic time in Perth in just over 11 weeks time!
(And who knows, you might just catch the bug and want to help realise my dream 
of having l.c.a. in Adelaide sometime!)

Cheers,

Michael...
