Hi,

XP has a nasty habit of "phoning home" whenever it sees a network connection
active.

If you are using a "less that legitimate" copy of XP, I highly suggest that
you format your drive with a sledge hammer. The only way that I have found
stops this activity is to block the data being sent out on you linux box
using iptables.

You will still get internal traffic happening however you are less likely to
have a Microsoft BlackOps team drop in on you in the middle of the night .
