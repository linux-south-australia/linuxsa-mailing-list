RE: Ext 3 partition imaging

Ive seen kernel panic with ghost 6 and ext 2 with later redhats. The
problem was ghost 6.03 didnt support the disklabeling, which redhat use
to locate the root partition. Workaround was boot with a rescue cd and
use e2label to label the partitions. You may be hitting this problem.

Ant.
