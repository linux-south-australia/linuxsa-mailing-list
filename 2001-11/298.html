Re: Firewalls

Other ppl have already responded to the other parts, but:

For the Data logging by MAC address, you'll need to use either ipchains or
iptables. I strongly reccommend iptables.

For the netmeeting-friendly part you *will need* iptables.

Smoothwall is a nice setup, but i believe its currently limited to using
ipchains, thus preventing you from setting up the more complex firewalling
required to handle the forward-and-reverse tcp connection masquerading.

(port forwarding is a PITA in ipchains.. sort-of-works but you're
causing yourself more grief than its worth)
