Hey - lay off EDS unless you have your facts straight.

An article in The Australian was VEHEMENTLY reputed by DAIS - the one 
that claimed EDS was overcharging - that was called MARKET PRICE 
ADJUSTMENT on mainframe services as costs in that area have dropped so 
fast in the previous years.  EDS is bound by it's contract to promote 
local businesses - something that people conveniently forget sometimes 
when they trash EDS.  EDS has no choice but to use local businesses as 
hardware suppliers for the SAG contract which leave prices slightly more 
expensive than if purchased through the manufacturer directly.  The 
other thing is that EDS must remain ISO-9001 certified or they lose 
their contract - bang gone.  How many other SA businesses are audited 
EVERY YEAR for ISO certification?  Does anyone here even know what ISO 
certification entails?  It certainly does not leave much room for 
stuffing around - let's put it that way.

EDS is not the problem - it is this insane partnership with Microsoft 
and the brainwashing that they have conducted so effectively.

We should know our enemy - sure - but let's also remain sure of who our 
enemy is.

For your information - EDS has experimented with open source solutions - 
but the government was not remotely interested.  Why don't you blame 
them instead?  EDS only supplies what the customer asks for.
