Re: webcam support in linux

>Can anyone reccomend a cheap webcam that works with linux? I wish to put a
>camera on my house, and am worried about possible theft and/or weather
>damage so I dont want to use my good firewire cam. Also it seems usb cable
>is cheaper than co-ax for a ccd cam, and should be good for the 60m run I
>need.
>
>Any Ideas?

If you use a Capture card (Miro PCTV - ~$150, works well with linux) and
coax, you can also wire the camera to an ordinary television. That might be
useful if the cam is positioned near your front door and you're lazy, or
plain cool if your TV has picture-in-picture :)
