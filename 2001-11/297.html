Re: Firewalls

> I am looking to replace my existing firewall with a Linux box.

Have a look at http://www.smoothwall.org

> I need:
> HTTP cache
YES -- has squid or similar

> DNS
PARTIAL -- uses dnrd which is mainly a caching DNS service for internet
hosts

> DHCP server
YES

> Data logging by MAC address
PROBABLY -- some (IP) accounting facilities exist, but I haven't used
them

> NetMeeting-friendly
UNSURE -- uses the standard Linux modules for RealAudio, Quake, IRC,
etc.. there may be something suitable there

> I'm thinking SQUID would be good
>
> Will it do all of the above?
I don't think squid provides DNS or DHCP.  Maybe you want to make a box
with squid and bind and dhcpd.

> Could people give me their experiences.
I've been running a SmoothWall firewall for about two months now, and
it's been really good.
