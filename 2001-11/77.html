Re: Mac OS X on a PC?

Well... you _can_ run the Darwin kernel on a x86, albeit with only 440BX
(afaik) chipset support.

There are projects on sourceforge looking at porting Alpha and other goodies
to Darwin on x86 :

http://sourceforge.net/projects/winmac
http://sourceforge.net/projects/darwin-x

There's also a project looking at bringing ports and other goodies to Mac OS
X if you're interested... although its called "GNU DARWN"... and seeing as
Darwin has a BSD license.. hrm.  Yessss.
