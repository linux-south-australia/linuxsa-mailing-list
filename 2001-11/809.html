You might like to ask Tel$stra if they can install 'Onramp Home 
Highway' at your place. Even just using that to plug your modem into 
will give you high 50k's per second connect speed in most cases, at 
the same (untimed local call) rate as you get on your analogue line. 
Plus being able to use Data-Over-Voice if you want to go faster later 
on.

Costs more per month than an analog phone line, but it gives you 
-two- digital re-inventions of an analog line, and gets rid of that 
nasty RIM thingamagig from your circuit.

Simon
