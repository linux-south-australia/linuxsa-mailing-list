Hi Grey

I have access to the equipment which would allow you to do this, the but the
costs a in my option a little high. The major problems is hardening of the
box for the weather and provision the power.

I have access to some single board computer and you could use the compact
flash port and a compact flash 802.11 like
http://www.dlink.com/products/DigitalHome/Mobile/dcf650w/

The advantage of a SBC is that they are low power normal require no fan and
can be easily mounted in a small weathered sealed box. Another advantage of
a SBC is that most have I/O wires which you could use to trigger the gate.

I'd think with a battery and a solar panel of the right size you could run
this thing with no external power wiring.
