Re: FreeBSD 3.2 ports...

Look under /usr/local .. you'll probably see a /usr/local/etc/rc.d for
anything you install from ports in there.  init.d style :-)  Ports rhawk.

Under /etc you'll probably want to look at /etc/rc.conf for startup-style
stuff, check out /etc/defaults/rc.conf for a template to work from.
