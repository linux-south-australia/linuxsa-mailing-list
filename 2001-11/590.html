> I know this has been kicked about a few times but I'm after folks
> opinion specifically in regards to education institutions.

Here's the schemes I'm aware of:
* University of Adelaide
firstname.lastname
(aliased to ua<studentnumber>)

* University of South Australia
<first 3 letters of last name><first letter of first name>
<first letter of middle name><3 digits>

* LaTrobe University
<first letter of first name><first letter of middle name>
<last name><some digits>
(or similar)

* University of Melbourne
<first initial>.<last name>

Other common schemes:
* <first initial><middle initial><last initial>
* <first initial><max. 7 letters of last name>
* <max. 7 letters of last name><first initial>
* lots more!

Of these, I think the UniSA method is very good, as the
length is not > 8 characters, and there is sufficient allowance
for persons to have similar names.  A potential disadvantage
is that the address can not be automatically deduced from
the person's name.
