Re: Wireless Networking

Wayne, 

We have been using Planet (www.planet.com.tw) Wireless Equipment here in Laos.
They also sell pci and pcmcia cards.

The WAP-1900 or WAP-1950 would fit your bill... Unfortunately, I don't not
have the retail prices. They are external units that plug into your
hub/switch.

We have been using two WAP-1000 (now superceded) for more than 15 months
without a problem. We had an external  power supply die but that is due to the
poorly regulated electricity grid. 

The only thing that I don't know is if they can be used in Australia. The
frequency range at 2.4Hz allows them to be used in just about every other
country see below.

N. America/FCC: 2412~2.462 GHz (11 channels )
Europe CE/ETSI: 2.412~2.472 GHz (13 channels)
Japan: 2.412~2.484 GHz (14 channels)
France: 2.457~2.472 GHz (4 channels)
Spain: 2.457~2.462 GHz (2 channels)

Please feel free to contact me off the list for further questions.
