Hi Greg

He's talking about the distribution call LinuxPPC not the version of the
kernel for the PPC hardware. The LinuxPPC distribution have not been updated
in for ever and is basically dead.

The Q4 release was nothing new and have very few updates in it, all the main
low level stuff had not been updated. e.g. glibc and others

I recommend using the YellowDog or Debian distributions for PPC hardware. If
you do not have time to play around then YellowDog is the way to go.
