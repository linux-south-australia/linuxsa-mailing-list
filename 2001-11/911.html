On Thu, Nov 29, 2001 at 03:54:32PM +1030, nik [tm] wrote:

 > I thought Internode and Netspace were working together for the ADSL...
 > isnt that what was posted to this list about July?

Yes, that's correct.

If you're an Internode customer and you want ADSL on the Eastern 
seaboard, we provision it through Netspace.

if you're a Netspace customer and you want ADSL in SA, they provision
it through us.

This means we can both be "one stop shops" for broadband connectivity 
for most of Australia;  It represents a short-cut around the fact that
to do ADSL in a new region you need to pay Telstra a very large 5-figure
sum just to get into the game (before you've even signed up any customers).
Internode has paid that five-figure sum in Adelaide, Netspace has paid
it in their service areas, so we resell each others' services instead
of paying more money to Telstra.
