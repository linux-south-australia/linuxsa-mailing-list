Re: Digital Video anyone?

Hi.

My understanding is that the authors of broadcast2000 realised that the
software was being used in some big name video editing houses and got
scared that there was a potential liability that they could not afford to
fight if something was found horribly lacking with the software.

It's a David and Goliath story where Goliath wins. A big studio with a
fighting fund could make a mess of them. I guess you can't blame the
authors, but it is a pity for the rest of us. You have to wonder what sort
of moronic lawyers would go after the authors of GPL code where there is
no representation of fit-for-purpose, and no consideration in the
contract.

If you have a look, the source is on the PCWorld linux minibook cd's.

Michael
