Hi Dave (hmmm, flashback to 2001 - ASO).

http://developer.apple.com/qa/qa2001/qa1013.html

According to this Apple Technical Q&A article the root in OS X is disabled by 
default as "an intentional security feature to limit the support problems 
that could arise from casual use of root".

The article goes on to talk about using sudo and how to re-enable the account.
