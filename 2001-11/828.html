Re: Red Hat 7.1 and Gnome

On Thu, Nov 22, 2001 at 09:43:52AM +1030, Martin Stacey wrote:
> Why does my screen go blank/power down after a period of no activity. No
> screen saver is setup and power management is turned off. How can I stop
> this from happening?
> 
> Martin Stacey
> IT Support Manager
> Safcol Australia Pty Ltd

If this happens in X then have a look at xset, the default is to blank after
10 minutes inactivity.
If it's the console then check the setterm command which has lots of options
for blanking and powersaving and is probably set in one of the /etc/rc.d
files.
If you run "xset s off" this will stop X blanking the screen but won't stop
setterm doing it so "setterm -blank 0 -powersave off -powerdown 0" should.
