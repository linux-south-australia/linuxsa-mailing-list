I use exactly the same setup with iHug and I have no problems, except
that the iHug links seem to get real busy between 8:00pm and midnight 
most nights. With local sites like aarnet.edu.au I normally average
about 4kb - 4.5kb per sec, but overseas sites get can get real slow.
Trying the same sites again in the morning often gives *much* better 
results. Could be iHug, could be their bandwidth providers, so I'm
not casting stones at anyone ;-)

Use hdparm to check if you're using DMA to access your hard disk, and
whether or not interrupts have been unmasked (-u flag). Setting DMA
mode and unmasking interrupts can make a huge difference to serial
port throughput on slower systems.
