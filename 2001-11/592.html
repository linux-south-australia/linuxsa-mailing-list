Re: telnetd

> How can I find out how telnetd is started by default in RedHat 7.1

Services like this are handled by xinetd.
"man xinetd"

chkconfig is also a very useful tool, and can manipulate xinetd.
"man chkconfig"  :-)

Note that under a default installation of Red Hat 7.1, some
firewalling options are enabled, so you may be unable to telnet
to a box without disabling this.

If you haven't yet done so, have a look at secure shell (ssh).
