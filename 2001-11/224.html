Re: webcam support in linux

I have an IBM Xircam webcam and that seems to work fine.  It is even 
infra-red sensitive!  Mandrake comes with module pre-compiled - I just 
modprobed ibm-cam and ran xawtv and viola!

It is also VERY sensitive to low light.  It is USB BTW, but it works 
like a charm.
