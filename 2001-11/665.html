Hi martin,
I had a similar problem with my own box. Turned out not to
be the OS or any related software. The mother board was at
fault.

It was a socket 7 (pc player was the brand off tyhe top of
my head). The board had onboard soundbut that was it no
on-board video. THe vid card was an S3 trio 64v. THe mobo
would just decide randomly mid-typing a sentence to
power-down the monitor and that was that. I also checked the
power settings in the BIOS and the OS (the box was however
running win98se). I re-installed the os twice it didn't fix
the problem. I ended up taking the board back to leader and
they replaced it for me, not a problem.

hope that helps
