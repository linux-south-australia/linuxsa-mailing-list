I also had a thought as to the best way of getting this to Sen.
Alton...

options:

* Web page with form for adding name -- digital petition.

* paper petition (print the text and a page of lines, and get
people to sign the lines, then compile it and send it in)

* email barrage -- form emails, either manually or via a web page
(enter your address here, and it sends email with the text from you)

* paper barrage -- print this, sign it, mail it from yourself to Sen
Alston

No matter what, we would have to have some sort of contact details on
it, probably the individual sender/signee, and a central one. Perhaps
someone could make a page (I could) that has the text, and a few
details on it. We could also try to /. that page ;)

Personally, I like the idea of a paper barrage -- it gets a lot of
attention, and while it requires more effort from each of us, it
generally has a good return for the effort...
