I remember from high school physics that the way it heats the contents
of microwave is actually heating the water molecules only.  It is
because the microwaves resonate at the same frequency as water.  Most
foods have water (of some degree) in it so it is perfect to heat
things.  If you try to put something that is completely without water in
it, you will find that it should not increase in heat. (Assuming there
is no other molecules that happen to resonate at the same frequency).
