not already a business customer of theirs based in one of the Eastern

prices.  These would not be the same as the prices you've seen at 
http://www.netspace.net.au/products/adsl/home/plans.shtml - They are

on their website).  Those business prices would be calculated by Netspace
on the basis that they want to make money from them.
