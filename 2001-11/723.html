The only place I've seen this done is on w2k server. There is a 'virtual'
adapter created that manages the actual adapters, with a choice of
failover fault tolerance or 'teaming' to increase the throughput. (FEC
mode) I've never investigated what happens on the arp side of it.

I do know that there is great potential for confusion. We had one of these
boxes that was misconfigured, and it was answering on two ip addresses on
the same network to simple pings...
