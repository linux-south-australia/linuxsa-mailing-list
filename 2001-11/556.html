[OT] Some blackbox trivia

Hi all,

Blackbox users:
You may or may not know this, but you can add the date to the toolbar of
blackbox.
In the .blackboxrc file is a setting for a time string. This uses the same
symbols as the 'date' command.
Just add the bits of the date you want, where you like.

Oh well, a bit more trivia for you.
