WTS Client for linux

Hi.

Came across this today, and I'm surprised I haven't heard of it before:

 http://www.rdesktop.org/ 

It's a RDP protocol client that connects to Windows Terminal Services.
Works very well (on the basis of about an hour's use) over our wan.
Compiled on Mdk 8.0 with XFree86-devel installed with no problems. Seems
faster to connect and display login than WTS on W2k (but that might be my
bias showing) I am using the 1.1 source.
