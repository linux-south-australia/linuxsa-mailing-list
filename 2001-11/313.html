RE: MP3's

Check out www.audiogalaxy.com !

It has a linux client.

You create yourself a account on the website then install the client on
your linux box, you can then search for tracks using the web, and the
client will download them for you.  Very nifty little client too,
automatically resumes when you reconnect etc etc. MUCH better than
napster ever was :)
