Alfred mailing list, LinuxEDU mailing list

Two things I'd like to cover briefly -- a new Alfred mailing list I've
just setup, and the LinuxEDU mailing list that's been in some form of
existance for a while.

Here's the interesting bits from a message I sent to a few other
individuals that are interested in Alfred stuff:

    e-mail message to majordomo@plug.cx with "subscribe alfred" in the
    body of the message. Of course, it goes without saying that the
    quotes don't get put into the subscription request.
    
    The purpose of the list is to provide support to those that are
    trying to setup and work with Alfred, as well as providing a
    developer forum for those that are interested in the growth and
    enhancement of Alfred.
