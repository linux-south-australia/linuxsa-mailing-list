Re: [OT]-ish wireless networking card for 802.11b

I'm running a d-link wireless bridge at home plugged into my ADSL 
router. Cheapest thing I could find from a company that I still felt 
likely to deliver a working product (which is d-link in a nutshell 
really)

Seems to work great, including to my various mobile Macintosh systems.

The only annoying thing is that it needs a windows pc to do initial 
configuration of the unit. But after that, just turn it on and have a 
nice life.
