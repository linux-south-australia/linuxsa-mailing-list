Hi Brenton,
It appears you don't have DNS resolution working for you.  Try
creating/changing your /etc/resolv.conf as follows:

# ------- /etc/resolv.conf ----------------------
search  camtech.net.au
nameserver 203.5.73.2
nameserver 203.5.73.1
# ------- /etc/resolv.conf ----------------------

regards .. Don
