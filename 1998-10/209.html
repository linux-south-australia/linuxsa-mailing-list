Host scanning ports (possible hackers)

Hi Everyone,
  Just a word of warning.
Over the last week I have had the following hosts scanning ports on my
system.  I recieve a message from a daemon that has replaced my IMAP port
(and other unused ports) each time this happens.  That daemon instantly
puts a full incoming packet block on so they can not go any ferther.  They
also recieve a text message that they can contact me via the supplied
e-mail address if they think they are inocent.  I have never recieved a
mail message!  Port scans with scripts are common.  I see them every day
so be warned, keep an eye on your logs ALWAYS.
