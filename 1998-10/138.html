SB-AWE / sndconfig RedHat 5.1 FIX



If you are having trouble with SB sndconfig and RedHat 5.2 check the kernel
version you have installed.

kernel-3.0.35-1.i396.rpm  did not allow sndconfig to work.

kernel-2.0.35-2.i386..rpm  worked. (fetch from netcraft ftp site)

Sndconfig and all sound works for me without any of the advanced hacking
suggested in other emails.
