What I did, which may not be the best way to do it, but hey!
it is still at 0.30 :).

Get 

gnome-utils-0.30-1
gnome-media-0.30-1
gnome-libs-0.30-1
gnome-guile-0.20-1
gnome-admin-0.30-1
gnome-games-0.30-3
gnome-core-0.30-1

Then do `rpm -i --test gnome*.rpm`

This will generate a list of failed dependencies.

Then grab the files as needed from 

	ftp://mirror.aarnet.edu.au/pub/gnome/redhat/latest/i386

until you have only the libjpeg62 error.

Then just force the install (or find a working libjpeg rpm).
