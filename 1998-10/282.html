Re: Signature sizes.

On Thu, 29 Oct 1998, Matthew Tippett wrote:

[about signature files in email]

> Also it is generally considered nice to put a '--' on the line
> preceding the signature.

<PEDANTIC>

Actually, the preferred format is '-- ' (that's DASH DASH SPACE) on
a line by itself.  Newsreaders particularly, but also many mail
programs, look for this and will automatically prune this special
marker line and anything below it for replies (and news follow-ups).

</PEDANTIC>

> Now more historical than anything else
> it still does seperate things nicely.

Hardly historical.  As I said, many news and mail programs will do
"the right thing" with a proper signature marker.  A lot of people
don't realise this, becuase the proper marker (with the space on the
end) is becoming less common.  A lower proportion of people now on
the net know about it.
 
> Also in replies cut out extra signatures, we don't need multiple
> signatures on each email.

Agreed.  Other sigs snipped in this reply ;-)
