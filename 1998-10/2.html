Re: Advice on 56k modems.

Hi Dave,
My employer, Binary Logic, can sell you a WebExcel internal for $129.

Remember to turn-off hardware flow control with internals. There is no
real RTS/DTS lines with an internal modem.

Emperically, we have found these work well, flash-upgradable to v90,
distinctive ring, detect all Australian PSTN tones, plug & pray can be
turned-off, configuration as com1,2,3,4 with a 16550 UART talking at
115200 baud, no probs. I use one at home under NT, OS/2 & Linux.
