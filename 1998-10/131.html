Hi there, I think you may find that your problem is related to the location
of your config files. I beleive that there were some changes in the dist in
RH 5.X so make sure that httpd is finding the httpd.conf, srm.conf and
access.conf (also look out for mime types - it's in /etc)

Also DO NOT use config files from older version in your new version. Take
the new config files and add to them any extra options you may need from
the old files, being careful to follow the syntax described. I find that
doing things that way leaves less room for error.
