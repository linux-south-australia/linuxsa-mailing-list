Position Available - Network Admin

Network Operations Manager (rate $NEG)

A leading SA ISP requires a technically competent person to head Network
Operations. The position will be initially the only full time network
operations position in the company with dedicated network operations staff
growing as the need rises.

Duties:

Implementing a network management system that will allow network 
operations to manage all aspects of a growing network, to forecast
network operations budgets for the following month, to analyse previous
budget over/under runs and on a monthly basis, to implement a system for
growing and expanding the network as per management policy,  maintaining
internal and external databases and registries such as routing registries,
and level 3 customer technical support (for customers with large kilobit
connections). 

Requirements:

o  knowledge of IOS routers including 5200, 7200, 3600 and others 
   preferable; 

o  experience NON MICROSOFT/NOVELL TCP/IP networks required; 

o  experience in running network management platforms - preferably in 
   implementing TCL/TK/scotty/tkined based network management utilities
   or any other tools used to manage networks; 

o  programming knowledge in Perl, C or shell; and 

o  Experience with Linux, FreeBSD or any UNXI variant and software such as
   gated, squid and named.
