a small libc6 anomaly

hello,

i've spotted a small (annoying) problem with clib6 (that comes with rh
5.1).

some of the definations defined in /usr/include/linux/socket.h are
duplicated in /usr/include/socketbits.h

/usr/include/linux/socket.h includes /usr/include/asm/socket.h and
/usr/include/socketbits.h


i get problems when i use /usr/include/linux/if_ppp.h in any of my
programs, because if_ppp.h includes /usr/include/linux/socket.h..

.. the result is double definations, and the compiler aborts.

anybody got any comments?. Has anybody discovered anything similar?.
