Re: LILO

Sam Silvester wrote:
> 
> Hello Everyone!
> I am very new to Linux, having just bought Redhat 5.1 and successfully
> installed it on my
> computer. What I would like to know is how to uninstall (yes, you heard
> right! Uninstall!)
> LILO from the MBR of my hard disk drive. I realise that I will need a
> boot disk to boot
> my system if I do remove LILO somehow.


Under dos type fdisk /mbr
This will make dos rewrite the master boot record for you. It's an
undocumented fdisk command.
