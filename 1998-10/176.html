Re: What's wrong with my network configuration.?

> I have been persevering for some considerable time now trying to setup
> internet services with Redhat 5.1 with only partial success. The problem
> is that I am able to send and receive mail and to access newsgroups but
> otherwise unable to see past my service provider's home page
> (camtech.net.au).

Like Camtech suggest, I'd check your proxy settings in Netscape.
If you go Edit->Preferences, on the left there'll be a window that
looks like:

   Category
v  Appearance
     Fonts
     Colors
>  Navigator
   Identity
>  Advanced

If you click on the ">" next to Advanced, it'll change to:

v  Advanced
     Cache
     Proxies

Click Proxies, then configure it as per Camtech's instructions.
