Try getting away from the zip archivers.
Try using RAR (winrar or dos) or WinACE.
RAR and ACE both achieve far better compression than ZIP (with ACE being
better than RAR).
A long time ago when i used RAR to archive a large number of files it had no
problems and made the archive rather quickly.
