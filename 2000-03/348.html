Hi all,
    From many days of DOS/Windows I thoroughly recommend ARJ and JAR from

        http://www.arjsoft.com

ARJ is very powerful and extremely flexible.  JAR gives fantastic compression
ratios.
You've probably got the Linux version of the `unarj` utility on your Linux install
for decompressing ARJ files.
