Sendmail with Virus Scanner

Some of you may be interested in a patch for Sendmail 8.9.3 which implements
virus scanning on incoming and outgoing email messages. The URL is:

http://www.decros.cz/~reho/check_virus/

I have used this patch in a couple of places now, in conjunction with the
"AntiViral Pro Toolkit for Linux", and it seems to work quite well.

It scans MIME and UUencoded attachments, and can unpack ZIP files etc and
scan those also. Although the virus scanner is not free, it still works out
a lot cheaper than most competing solutions, which are based on a per-user,
or larger per-server, charge (eg scanners for MS Echange etc).

The facility is probably best suited to those sites that user Sendmail as an
MTA, but have a lot of MS Win based mail clients.

The patch will work with other virus scanners (although I have not tried),
but works best with AVP because it runs as a daemon, and thus does not have
to start a new scanning process for every mail message.
