The error message as I read it is that your client is trying to log into
your machine, but the credentials are being regected.

This depends upon how NIS is setup on the system, and what level of access
you have on your PC.

If you are NFS exporting your volume, then the root account is what
controlls this, but NIS gets in the way. It might need some adjustments to
/etc/password, and some looking at how /etc/nsswitch.conf is configured.

It mignt also be necessary in the setup to tell the installation which is
the NIS master (if that option is given).

Alternatly, if you have root access to your box, configure ftp, and do an
install by FTP.
