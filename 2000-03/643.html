comp.talk.ietf -- a week of free lunchtime seminars by IETF meeting attendees


The 47th meeting of the Internet Engineering Task Force is being held
in Adelaide from the 26th to 31st of March. The meeting brings
together the people that built the Internet.

Some attendees at the meeting have graciously offered to give one hour
presentations to staff, students and the public. The planned times and
locations of the reminder of the presentations are:


Tue 28th 12-1pm

  Implementing voice over IP
  Stephen Kingham, CSIRO

  Flentje Lecture Theatre
  Level 2, Plaza Building

Wed 29th, 12-1pm

  Measuring Internet performance with Surveyor
  Matt Zekauskas, Chair of IP Performance Metrics working group

  Horace Lamb Lecture Theatre,
  Level 1, Horace Lamb Building

Thu 30th, 12-1pm

  Developments in IP version 6
  Bob Fink, Chair of Next Generation Transition working group

  Horace Lamb Lecture Theatre,
  Level 1, Horace Lamb Building

Fri 31st, 12-1pm

  T.B.A (waiting for confirmation)

  Horace Lamb Lecture Theatre,
  Level 1, Horace Lamb Building

-----
Jointly organised by the Department of Computer Science at The
University of Adelaide and by the Australian Academic and Research
Network. We thank the presenters and IETF. Enquiries to Glen Turner,
glen.turner@aarnet.edu.au
