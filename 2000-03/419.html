> There are tools to prevent users other those who belong
> to the right 
> groups from accessing past or present status
> information.
> 
> If you don't trust your users, follow the following tips
> to make your 
> machine obnoxious.
> 
> last:
>   chmod 0660 /var/log/wtmp /var/log/utmp
>   chown root.utmp /var/log/wtmp /var/log/utmp
> so only root can access the last login records.
> 
> w who:
>   http://www.openwall.com/linux/ kernel patch
> The "Restricted /proc" part of the patch will prevent
> users from 
> accessing ps, top, who, w information about others.  If
> you do not trust 
> your users, then this patch is a good start to securing
> your box.
> Also, see last.
>

> drwxr-x--x   8 root     root         5120 Mar 12 04:02

>
