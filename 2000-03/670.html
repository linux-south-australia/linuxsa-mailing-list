Re: newsletter

Nick Morrison wrote:

> Out of interest..  who "owns" the copyright to the posts that hit this
> list?  The poster, or "LinuxSA"?

The author (which may actually be the writer's employer).

By posting you may be giving LinuxSA some implied copyright
rights (a licence to reproduce the message to the mailing
list members comes to mind).  But this mess has yet to hit
an Australian court and it will be a sad day if it ever
does.

Some web-based services attempt to gain copyright rights by
making their grant a condition of subscribing.

The only real effect, and a relevant one, is that the author's
permission should be gained when reproducing the e-mail in another
forum or in a newletter or book.
