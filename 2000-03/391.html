Phil Pittard wrote:

> Hi all - before I blow my money :) I have been "playing" with Applixware
> - hasnt clogged up my linux box - doesnt seem nasty with memory like
> star office - so, I cant find anything much wrong with it.. anybody know

I've used it.  It's essentially the opposite to Star Office.
Simple but robust.

It's currently my program of choice for doing spreadsheets
and presentations.

The import/export filters tend to be too dependent on your
version of glibc, so it occassionally stops working after
an OS upgrade.

Vendor support is excellent.  My purchase got superceeded
about a month after.  They sent me an CD of the new version.
The bug database is public.
