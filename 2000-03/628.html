Re: Sound Card Troubles

I had the same problem, it was because certain modules were not loaded.

I ended up putting the following commands in my /etc/rc.d/rc.local file, but
I'm sure there is a more elegant way - I just haven't had the (available) time
to figure it out:


/sbin/modprobe sound
/sbin/modprobe es1370
