Re: moving swap file

Brian - no big deal to do what you suggest - just "swapoff -a" first
then fdisk & do it. You will get fdisk errors cos your linux partition
is still mounted but no drama - just reboot after the fdisk & then run
mke2fs on any new partitions u created & mkswap the new swap. 
** Make sure you hash out the swap drive in fstab BEFORE rebooting after
doing the fdisk & dont add any  new partition to fstab until you have
done the mke2fs or you will get major probs when linux restarts. (ie/
just fdisk, hash swap in /etc/fstab then reboot, then mke2fs & mkswap
THEN uhash swap & add new partitions to /etc/fstab) sorry if this seems
a tad confusing but I have only had 2 hours sleep
