Re: internal UPS

Richard Russell wrote:

> Has anyone heard of such a thing as an internal UPS or an integrated UPS/PC
> case? 
[ bobbit ]
> Also, on a similar train of thought, what about an internal switch or
> hub?  I'd like to see a server appliance that is UPS protected, and
> provides 8-16 100BaseT ports,

Microgram computers (www.microgram.com.au) sell both of these sorts of
devices.  The UPS replaces the ATX power supply (from memory, the
batteries occupy a 5 1/4" bay).  They also sell a combination Ethernet
PCI card with an additional 4 port hub built in (5 ports in total,
although one may be an "uplink" port), but only as a UTP/10 (and I
emailed the guys last year about the card - apparently it's a
NE-2x00 clone, so it _should_ work under Linux/*BSD).

Note:  I'm not affiliated with, or have used, any of Microgram's
products.
