Glen Turner wrote:

> rather than stuff about with vendors).

Yes. IMP. Check out http://www.horde.org/ they have other stuff too.
It's done in PHP3 and can use an SQL backend (supports around a dozen).
All I did is download the RPM for horde and IMP and install. Then you do
a http://server/horde/setup.php3 (or something) and it goes through a
graphical config. Very well done and looks very stable (I want to
implement it as soon as I can).

It also supports cyrus IMAP as well as generic IMAP daemons.

Well worth the look.
