Re: Linux as an ISDN Router?

> If I was to go the linux router option, can anyone recommend a good, locally
> available and linux friendly, ISDN Terminal Adapter??

www.traverse.com.au manufacture quite reasonable ISDN PCI cards based on
the Siemens chipset. This is not a separate TA, it just goes in your
computer like any other PCI card. You can buy them through
www.everythinglinux.com.au or dozens of other resellers. You'd be helping
Traverse if you tell them that you are using their hardware on Linux.

Traverse are about to release (have released?) a new active card. I have
only used their passive cards.
