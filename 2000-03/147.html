Jake Hawkes wrote:
> 
> "Galdes, Andrew (PIRSA)" wrote:
> >
> > howdy,
> >
> > i have a few simple questions for you.
> >
> > 1. how can i find the drive space on a specific drive?
> 
> du - gives a listing of how big the directries are (from the current one
> down)
> df - gives a summary of all mounted(only?) drives
> 
> > 2. how can i mount a second phisical hard disk when the first is
> > partitioned? i don't know what each partition is currently called.
> >
> 
> IDE:
> primary master hda
> primary slave hdb
> secondary master hdc
> secondary slave hdd
> 
> so, if you have 2 hard drives, and the first is partitioned into 3
> partitions, you would have:
> 
> /dev/hda1 }
> /dev/hda2 }- your first physical hard drive in 3 partitions
> /dev/hda3 }
> 
> /dev/hdb1 }- your second physical hard drive in 1 partition

running "fdisk -l" should tell you which partitions you've got.
