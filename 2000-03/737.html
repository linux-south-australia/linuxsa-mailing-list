Re: Linux training?

On Wed, 29 Mar 2000, Ian Hogben wrote:

> Are there linux training and certification courses out there? Are there any
> f2f courses held in Adelaide? Do people have opinions on what's good and
> what's not?

Richard Sharpe (in Adelaide) has developed and runs an excellent Linux
course. He was the first in the country as far as I know. Cybersite
(www.cybersite.com.au) run Linux training but not in Adelaide yet. There
are others.

Regardless of the course provider, you should be aiming towards LPI
certification level, www.lpi.org. There is a place in Adelaide that offers
LPI exams findable on the LPI site.
