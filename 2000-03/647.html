Re: logging

Does 'tail -f > /dev/tty1x' do what you want?
Another nice command is 'tee'.. (t's off stdin to a file(s) )
eg. tail -f /var/log/messages | tee /dev/tty1x 

If you want more than this.. I'd consult the sysklogd man pages.
