Re: PCMCIA

> I was reading the PCMCIa HOWTO again (trying to find a solution to the
> problem I posted about 2 weeks ago regarding my Ethernet card pretending
> to be a memory card). When I insert the PCMCIA card (genuine 3c589b), it
> does a low beep and then a high beep. This was not in the HOWTO. Has
> anyone else had this problem and fixed it?

When this has happen to me it normally means that it found the card
but didn't bring the interface up because the network config had not
been done.

Try just do an 'ifconfig' to see if the device can be configured
manually (eg /sbin/ifconfig eth0 10.1.1.1 netmask 255.255.255.0)

Funnily enough this just happened on a new laptop I was trying to
configure with Linux... the specs on the laptop are PIII 600 with
1400x1050 15.4' LCD.  My PII 333 with 1024x728 15' LCD is now looking
chunky :-)
