Re: office space for small linux-based enterprise

Alan Kennington wrote:

> That worked flawlessly, except that when I changed
> from a single-sided printer to a double-sided printer
> (and HP 8000 something-or-other), it always waited
> 5 minutes before printing the last page.
> I tried adding control-D at the end of documents,
> but it didn't help.

Ah, did you try a ^L (form feed)?  A common "problem" with
laser (and laser like) printers is that they hold onto the
last page until they have either an entire page, or receive
a page eject.  The reason that the printer is spitting it
out after 5 minutes is probably due to a built in timeout.
You can usually spot this by the data light flashing, but
no page coming out (and a manual page eject will spit out
the last page in its entirety).
