LinuxSA March/2000 Meeting Announcement

Hi all,

Time for the LinuxSA March 2000 Meeting:

  When:   7:00pm on
          Tuesday, 21st March, 2000
  Where:  2nd Floor
          Compaq Computer
          139 Frome Street
          Adelaide SA
  Cost:   FREE
  Who:    Anyone and everyone.
          No pre-registration necessary.

Presentation:

  Two presentations this month:

  - David Newall will be bringing along a piece of history for LinuxSA
    meeting-goers to look at; a Stride 440 (equivalent to a Sage IV):
    a 68000 VME bus computer which comes with System V Unix (note the
    loose connection to Linux).

  - Geoffrey D. Bennett will be talking about PostgreSQL, in
    particular some of the features that are now available with the
    new version 7.0 beta 1.

Extras:

  As per usual, if you are having a problem with your Linux
  installation, feel free to bring your machine.  Make sure you warn
  organisers@linuxsa.org.au if you are bringing a machine.

For more information:

  Email:        organisers@linuxsa.org.au
  Web Page:     http://www.linuxsa.org.au/
  Mailing List: linuxsa@linuxsa.org.au

  Mailing list details on the web page.
