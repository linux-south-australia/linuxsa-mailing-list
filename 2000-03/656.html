> Yet on boot I get the message

Both of my main machines, one a mix of IDE/SCSI, the other pure SCSI,
do this; I've never looked heavily into what the message means, as
the kernel boots/finds the SCSI devices fine (which is _really_ odd
on the machine that boots from SCSI).

> Having never really looked at how kernel modules work, how do I
> automatically load the correct module as needed?

On my box with the secondary SCSI drive, I have:

/etc/rc.d/rc.modules:
modprobe scsi

/etc/conf.modules:
alias scsi seagate

(for RedHat 6.[01], which invokes rc.modules during /etc/rc.d/rc.sysinit).
