laptop success continues

I have fixed the 'splattering' and shadowing on the Toshiba laptop, if
anuone is interested. It is a compromise solution, but an improvement
which I am happy to accept for now.

I added:
Option "noaccel" 
in the the device section of /etc/X11/XF86Config
The display is (undrstandably) less smooth when moving windows, but nice
and clean.

The only things left to do:
make the display look like 1024x768 even tho it's physically an 800x600
pixel display. At 800x600 you don't get much on a screen.
Make the pcmcia services not clash with the ltmodem.o module, so we can
use either the network card or the modem depending on where we are,
without having to play with modules by hand.

I will let the list know the solutions when found in case anyone else is
contemplating setting up Linux the same model Toshiba laptop.
