Re: Links

No, this has nothing to do with making SCSI appear as IDE, or vice
versa.
Linking your most-used cdrom device to /dev/cdrom is just a
convienience. It's more useful when you don't want to have to remember
that your harddrive is on /dev/hdd on one machine and /dev/hdc on
another and /dev/scd0 on another..
If they've been linked to /dev/cdrom then you can just do mount
/dev/cdrom /mnt/cdrom on all your machines and have it work.
