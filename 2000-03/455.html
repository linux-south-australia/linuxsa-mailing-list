Hi Folks,

I have a linux box that has started to develop disk problems (the

The disk/controller info is:

  kernel: SIS5513: IDE controller on PCI bus 00 dev 01
  kernel: SIS5513: not 100% native mode: will probe irqs later
  kernel:     ide0: BM-DMA at 0xd000-0xd007, BIOS settings:
          hda:DMA, hdb:DMA
  kernel:     ide1: BM-DMA at 0xd008-0xd00f, BIOS settings:
          hdc:pio, hdd:pio
  kernel: hda: ST38410A, ATA DISK drive

  kernel: ide0 at 0x1f0-0x1f7,0x3f6 on irq 14
