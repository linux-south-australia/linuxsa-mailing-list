Lilo

Re setting up Lilo to boot RH, Suse and win95.

Thanks to Matt, Jake & Richard for your suggestions.

My attempts to twig Lilo so that is could find the Suse kernel were to
no avail. In the end I followed your theme and copied the Suse kernel to
the /boot directory of RHat. I ran Lilo and Suse booted up ok with its
current 2.2.13 kernel.

There are probably other ways that you can do this.

Brian Marr

boot=/dev/hda
map=/boot/map
install=/boot/boot.b
prompt
timeout=60
#Boot RedHat
image=/boot/vmlinuz-2.2.12smp
        label=RedHat
        root=/dev/hdb1

#Boot Suse
image=/boot/vmlinuz.suse        
        label=Suse
        root=/dev/hdc2

                
#Boot old kernel         
image=/boot/vmlinuz-2.2.5-15
        label=linuxOld
        root=/dev/hdb1

#Boot Windows 95
other=/dev/hda1
        label=win95
        table=/dev/hda
