Try: (for example)
ifconfig eth0 192.168.0.1    or   ifconfig eth0:0 192.168.0.1

You might also need:
route default gw -gateway-ip-address-

Have a good read of the 'NET3-4-HOWTO'.. on a Linux Box near you
(or search the web).
