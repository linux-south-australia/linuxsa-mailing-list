>  Wed, 1 Mar 2000 09:42:08 +1030
> Resent-Date: Wed, 1 Mar 2000 09:42:08 +1030

According to this, Netcraft received the email 1st March 09:42, and
then the next hop (looking like a firewall, and most likely yours)
received it on 3rd March 8:20.  If you compare this against a later
email, and that second Received line has changed, then it would
appear that Netcraft are continually resending the email.  If it's
the first (ie, the topmost) Received line that is only changing,
then it's the firewall retransmitting the email to
mail-1.marianna-u.ac.jp.  In either case, one of the receiving
parties is probably giving a 4xx error (temporary error, try
again later) but still passing on the email, so the fault is most
likely with the firewall or mail-1.
