Not surprising - it's the same proxy.

The "sa.edu" network is an extension of the same network used in 
Vic, NSW and Qld.  And, as list users from schools can probably attest,
it sucks ass.  No local servers (because all the schools are NAT'ed,
and their addresses are invisible to the outside world), no permanent
connectivity (it's dial on demand up to 128k), web pages have to be
kept on the sa.edu web server in Victoria at an exorbitant $/Mbyte charge,
etc.

Think for a moment about the "no local servers" requirement:  What happens
when student A at a school sends a 10 Mbyte attachment by email to 
students B, C, D, E and F ?  The school doesn't have a local mail server!
