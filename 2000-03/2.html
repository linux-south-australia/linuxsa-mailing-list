internal UPS

Has anyone heard of such a thing as an internal UPS or an integrated UPS/PC
case? I can see many potential applications for appliance-like machines that
offer UPS resistance to power loss, but still fit in the one box. Is there
some obvious reason why such devices do not exist (if, in fact, they do not
exist?)... Also, on a similar train of thought, what about an internal
switch or hub? I'd like to see a server appliance that is UPS protected, and
provides 8-16 100BaseT ports, has no monitor or keyboard, and is controlled
entirely by a few buttons on the front and a web interface... sort of
Cobalt-like (see www.colbalt.com), but with UPS and Switching (and a few
other things that can wait till later to be discussed)... Any comments?
