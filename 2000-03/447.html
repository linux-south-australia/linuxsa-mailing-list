Re: /dev/fd0

I'd suggest starting with MAKEDEV(8)

I haven't read the man page, but I'd assume that there would be a way of
running MAKEDEV to recreate lost devices... (I would guess that this would
be the default behaviour...)

It can also be done manually, if you know what you are doing...

have a look at /usr/src/linux/Documentation/devices.txt , and use mknod,
chmod, and chown to manually create the right files on the right inodes with
the right owner and group, and the correct permissions
