You could use SSH/OpenSSH to do this.  It does
compression/encryption/tunnelling over a single TCP connection.  You
would need to have an SSH server on the UNI end of the connection...

OpenSSH home page is at
http://www.openssh.org

RedHat 6.1 packages for SSH1/OpenSSH are at
ftp://ftp.redhat.de/pub/rh-addons/security/6.1/i386
