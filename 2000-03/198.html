Re: Lilo

At 04:29 PM 3/10/00 +1030, Brian Marr wrote:
>Re setting up Lilo to boot RH, Suse and win95.
>
>Thanks to Matt, Jake & Richard for your suggestions.
>
>My attempts to twig Lilo so that is could find the Suse kernel were to
>no avail. In the end I followed your theme and copied the Suse kernel to
>the /boot directory of RHat. I ran Lilo and Suse booted up ok with its
>current 2.2.13 kernel.

OK, well, you have to have the appropriate places mounted so LILO can find
the kernel images you need.

The problem with the above is:  What if you have Booted SuSE, and run lilo
without thinking about it? You are likely to end up with a lilo config that
no longer lets you into RedHat.

Another problem is, what if you build a new kernel for SuSE, but forget to
copy it to the right place?

OTOH, the kernel and the distribution are essentially un-related.  That is,
your RH kernel should be able to boot your SuSE distribution.  The only
issues you may find are those to do with kernel symbols and modules ...

>There are probably other ways that you can do this.
>
>Brian Marr
>
>boot=/dev/hda
>map=/boot/map
>install=/boot/boot.b
>prompt
>timeout=60
>#Boot RedHat
>image=/boot/vmlinuz-2.2.12smp
>        label=RedHat
>        root=/dev/hdb1
>
>#Boot Suse
>image=/boot/vmlinuz.suse        
>        label=Suse
>        root=/dev/hdc2
>
>                
>#Boot old kernel         
>image=/boot/vmlinuz-2.2.5-15
>        label=linuxOld
>        root=/dev/hdb1
>
>#Boot Windows 95
>other=/dev/hda1
>        label=win95
>        table=/dev/hda
