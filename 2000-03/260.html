Java IDE (was re: WHICH JDK)

Just a quick one on Java.

A reasonably good free IDE for Java, is Forte for Java via www.netbeans.com

There is a version for JDK1.1 and one for JDK1.2 (Java 2.0).

They do come in rpm form for linux or you can get the jar file.

I am playing with it under win9x (my linux box being re-built at the mo) and
can say it is quite good and well written.

Just thought you might like to know.
