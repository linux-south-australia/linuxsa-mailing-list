>The plus in the passwd file means :
>
>1) If the plus is first, use the NIS Maps first then the local file(s)
>2) If the plus is last, use the local file(s) and then the NIS Maps.
>
>If you do not have a plus, it generally means, on this node, we do not use
>NIS maps, they may be propogated by the yp* processes, but that is it.
