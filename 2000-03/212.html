Re: Kernel

Robyn Manning <robynman@dove.net.au> wrote:

>How can I get a listing of what's happening while it's compiling etc to
>see what the error messages are?

Don't know what your error messages mean but you should be able to
capture the output of your compile with

  make bzImage 2>&1 | tee /tmp/bzImage.out

This works in bash


If you're using tcsh:
  make bzImage |& tee /tmp/bzImage.out 
should work.
