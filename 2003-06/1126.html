I'll wade in here even though I've had no experience with any of the
BDS's & suggest using Gentoo. It's portage (based on the BSD idea) works
fantastically well.
Only thing it won't do for you is select software, unless of course that
software happens to be a dependency to another piece of software you've
already chosen. In which case it will be auto-downloaded & installed.
All packages installed are kept in a database, so that updating is
simply a matter of issuing, 'emerge -u world' every so often.
Check it if you haven't already, you'll be impressed :)
