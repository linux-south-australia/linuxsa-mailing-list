Large Perl Installs

Howdy all,

I'm looking for ideas as to how to manage large installed bases of perl.

Our current situation basically points all our clients to a central perl 
location with a symlink... this is nearly good... but there are problems 
with people expecting certain facilities from their perl that they are 
used to getting from vanilla installs (of Red Hat), but we haven't added 
yet to our central install.

The other side of the coin is that we use _a lot_ of perl scripts and 
modules that are definitely required for the day-to-day maintenance so 
some flexibility to "add" to the existing "stock" perl is important as 
well.

Any ideas?
