IMHO Transport mode is a pain to implement, and as Tunnel mode doesn't 
add that much, I'd personally go for tunnel mode instead. The biggest 
processor hog is the encryption itself anyway! That is why most 
implementations (SonicWALL, Cisco, SafeNet, Free S/WAN, Checkpoint, 
etc.) default to (or even only support) Tunnel mode anyway...

Just my EUR 0.02 worth...
