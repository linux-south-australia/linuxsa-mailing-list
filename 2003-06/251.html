Re: Password protect your system

Michal Mienik wrote:

> Don't forget to Tempest proof your PC case and especially keyboard otherwise
> someone could pick up all your keystrokes.  Not to mention not using a
> wireless keyboard.... ugh ;-)

Actually, it's the power supply that's the greatest Tempest
risk.  They usually lack sufficient smoothing (big capacitors
cost more money).

All keyboards, not just wireless keyboards, are an interception
threat.  Keystroke recorders are the technology of choice for
police and security agencies (as they grab passwords, thus
defeating user cryptography).  Some recorders will even use the
keyboard cable of an idle computer as an antenna to transmit the
interception buffer, avoiding the need for more than one (highly
dangerous) entry to a premises.
