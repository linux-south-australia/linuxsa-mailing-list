Both my PCMCIA and external modems sometimes can't find a ring tone on
my land line, and consequently don't dial.  If I pick up a handset, I
get a ring tone no problems.

There are 4 physical connections for the single line, 3 of which have
devices attached (including the modem).

To get the modem to dial I sometimes need to remove all other devices
(the 2 phones).  Why is this?  Is it line noise?  Is it a volume level?

Now for the kicker, I'm thinking of getting ADSL.  Will this problem
likely propagate itself if I go down that path?  Will I be wasting the
