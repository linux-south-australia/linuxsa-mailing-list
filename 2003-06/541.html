My take is that  a dongle is there to provide some form of security, whether 
it can be used as a pass through or not is a byproduct.  Therefore the word 
dongle applies to a device that is there for security. An adapter is a horse 
of a very different colour even if it fits in the same slot.

Its function is to allow through traffic. It has no by product of providing 
any form of security.

In my book that makes it anything other than a dongle.
