Mandrake 9.1 & kppp

Hey everyone
Just wanted to share this with everyone who uses Mandrake Linux and who 
are gunna update to 9.1. I have just upgraded my Linux box from Mandrake 
9.0 to 9.1. However, still using dial-up 56k modem (god do I need 
broadband), once I had installed the update, I could not find kppp to 
dial-up!! Now, after some googling on the net (courtesy of my wifes 
windoze partition!!), I quickly discovered that support for kppp has 
since been removed from Mandrake. I am not sure about anyone else here, 
but in my view gnome-ppp completely sucks and often crashes. So if 
anyone is in the same boat as me, you are gunna have to download the 
kppp package and manually install it. I have attached a link for all 
those who might wanna use it.

ftp://ftp-stud.fht-esslingen.de/pub/Mirrors/Mandrake-devel/cooker/cooker/Mandrake/RPMS/kdenetwork-kppp-3.1.2-12mdk.i586.rpm

Cheers
Reece
