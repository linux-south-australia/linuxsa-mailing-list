Hi Jake,

> Notice both pin layouts are completely different.
> 
> I tried the overclockers how-to before i realised the difference, the
> weird part is that i am actually getting perfect results apart from
> the contrast issue, e.g. the data is all being displayed right.

Yes the two pin outs are very different. So you're it works? The data
lines for your LCD for DB[7:0] are pins 14 to 7 on your LCD, in the
guide it is pins 8 to 1!

One other difference between the two LCDs is that yours has one enable
line, whereas the one in the guide has 2. Perhaps your enable is only
being asserted half of the time?

Seeing how you have the datasheet of the LCD, it's probably best to read
the documentation of your control software and decide how best to wire
it up since the guide is based on a different LCD part.
