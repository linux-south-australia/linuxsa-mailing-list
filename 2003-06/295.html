gpg --keyserver XXX.XXX.XXX.XXX --send-keys <key identifier>

if you made your key and used your email address, this could be the key 
identifier

e.g.
 gpg --keyserver www.mandrakesecure.net --send-keys dlloyd@microbits.com.au
