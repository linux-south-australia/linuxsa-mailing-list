Re: NetJet-S PCI + Older Computer

I figured it was a good idea. I'm looking at the number of packets getting 
dropped by my firewall, and it's a little scary how often I'm getting 
scanned.  The plan is to have zero services on the gateway box (except maybe 
ssh into it) and a deny all firewall. Should be really easy to keep it 
up-to-date then... just a few packages and a kernel to think about.

It also means I can switch between Windows and Linux on my main desktop box 
without losing the net connection.

Traverse list the minimum system requirements for the NETjet-S as a Pentium 
120 or better (http://www.traverse.com.au/linux/linuxgen.asp).  

I might just give it a whirl on the weekend and see how things turn out.  
Can't be all bad I guess. If it doesn't work I can just whoop it back in this 
box and be back on my feet (net) again.  I'll post my results back to the 
list next week.

Thanks
