Re: ext2/ext3 Partition Recovery Tools?

"Ryan Verner" <xfesty@computeraddictions.com.au> wrote:

> I'm guessing its definitely damage, I just need *a way* to get 
> information OFF the hard drive.

I'm a bit rusty at this, but are you able to access the device
for the whole drive (e.g. /dev/hda as opposed to /dev/hda1,
hda2, etc.)?  I can't remember if Linux will still let you do
that even if you have problems reading the partition table
off of the first sector.

If so, you can use something like gpart to try to discover
where the partitions are located.  You could even possibly
copy the entire hard disk to a file, and edit that copy in place -
this would allow you to work around the faulty sectors and
even correct them as you go.
