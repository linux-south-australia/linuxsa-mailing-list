Hi Michael,

Had an ISA 33.6 internal modem that had the same disease. Trick was to 
turn off the "wait for dialtone" in your dialup. From the ommand line, 
wvdial etc, ATX3DT will do it. Seems that some modems in some telephone 
exchange areas, just don't like the dialtone. I notice that some have 
suggested it may be noise on your line, I don't think so, but if you 
want to listen to your line, dial 0 to get rid of the dialtone is the go.
