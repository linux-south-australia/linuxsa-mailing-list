No, it's the OS that runs on RS/6000 and pSeries machines.

<btw>

IBM have five "series" of servers:

xSeries -- intel based
eSeries -- intel based blades
pSeries (aka RS/6000, "UNIX") -- Power4/PPC based, running AIX or Linux.
iSeries (aka AS/400, "Midrange") -- Power4/PPC based, running OS/400, or
Linux.
zSeries (aka S/390, "Mainframe") -- "z900" processor?, running OS/390, z/OS,
or Linux.

There may be more, but that's all I'm aware of. Confusingly, they also name
their laptops, desktops and other machines similarly, and there is an "X
Series" notebook range.

</btw>
