Re: [O/T] C2T Breakout cable

Beware with video (in fact any pci card) as the slots won't accept some
old cards.
I think they are 3.3v not 5v from memory but don't quote me on that.
Either way, not all pci cards will fit due to the spacing of the gaps on
the card's edge connector.
