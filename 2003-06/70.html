Of course this works for a single machine or maybe two. However let's
say you had 50 servers scattered around the country, it's a teensy pain
to login to each of them manually and then update each package. 

Let's say there are two separate updates released, one on a Monday, you
login update the packages on each machine, then another in found Friday.
You do it all again. It's a vast waste of time / resources.

An OS with an updating system that can recognise what packages are
installed, and what needs to be updated at a given time, is a reasonable
thing to ask for nowadays.
