Re: Using matt was kmail problem.

On Mon, Jun 02, 2003 at 06:14:57PM +0930, Bill - wrote:
> I am impressed by the way that mutt read my Kmail mailbox folders so 
> easily.  The worry of not having access to some important emails has 
> been put to rest. But I have a few questions. Does mutt record sent 
> mail, and if so how ? 

Add to your ~/.muttrc:

  set record="~/mail/outgoing" (or whatever filename you want)

> Secondly  what are the basic steps for using the 
> editor. I have trouble getting in and out when composing or replying to 
> a mail.

Not quite sure what you mean - I've never had any such trouble. It
just drops me straight into the editor when I need to edit, and when I
quit the editor I'm straight into the sending menu screen.

Perhaps your mutt is configured to use some horrible editor that makes
it really unobvious how you get out of it? The cure would be to edit
your ~/.muttrc:

  set editor="your favourite editor"
