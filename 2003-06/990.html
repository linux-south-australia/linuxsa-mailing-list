this would most probably be because the cups daemon has only been activated
on

do a 'netstat -atu' and look at your open ports. I'd bet that there isn't a
*:631 open, but a localhost:631 port open - i.e. the port has only been
opened on the loopback interface.
Theres probably a setting in one of the cups configuration files which you
can
use to list the interfaces which it should operate on.
