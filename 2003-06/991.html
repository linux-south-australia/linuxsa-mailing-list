Spot on David! That saved me a _lot_ of time.

All I had to do in SuSE Linux 8.2 was to change it to read:

<Location />
Order Deny,Allow
Deny From All
Allow From 127.0.0.1
Allow From 192.168.88.*
</Location>

Because my Local Area Network is on the 192.168.88 subnet.
