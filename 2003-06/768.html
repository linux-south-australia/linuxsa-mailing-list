Re: Mandrake 9.1 & kppp

I was lucky that it was a Connextant chipset. I downloaded an rpm for Mandrake 
9.1 from the vendors website (well an affiliate vendor that makes the Linux 
drivers), and all I had to do was "rpm -ivh blah-blah.rpm", and the scripts 
in the rpm package set everything up and loaded the driver (including 
creating a symbolic link for /dev/modem). Couldn't have been easier.
