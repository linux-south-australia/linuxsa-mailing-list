The cost, and problem with antivirus, is not the engine itself, but the
virus definitions, and keeping them updated. If we had an engine that
was GPL'd, which could read definitions from a choice of vendors, then
you could choose who you paid for definitions, and run your free scanner
on whatever OS you wanted... assuming they're willing to sell you
definitions (and that they'd trust you not to onsell or distribute them
on -- which they wouldn't)...
