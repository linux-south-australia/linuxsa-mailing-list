Re: Phone lines and modems

Is it an Australian sourced, Australian purchased modem, or did you 
buy it overseas?

Non-Australian modems can fail to lock on the Australian dial tone.

The answer is simple, whether the above guess is right or not ... 
enable blind dialling in your modems and get on with life.

Blind dialling is usually enabled with ATX4 or ATX3 (depends on the modem).

Fiddling with register S6 may also help (see the second link noted below).


for examples:

http://www.modemhelp.org/inits/motorolasm56.html


http://www.aopen.co.jp/tech/manual/modem/atcom.txt
