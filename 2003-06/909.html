Here we go...

http://www.coda.cs.cmu.edu/maillists/codalist/codalist-1998/0557.html

That's an email that describes someone's attempt at setting up a Coda
network computer... They basically made a linux system that could boot to
the point where it could then chroot into /coda (the single coda
mountpoint), and then floundered a bit... There's some discussion there, and
eventually someone suggests not chrooting at all, and just symlinking from
/* to /coda/*, which would solve a few problems... Don't know what happenned
on this since then -- it was 1998 when this thread happenned...

Then also, check this email
http://www.geocrawler.com/archives/3/153/1998/6/0/600635/ -- this guy seems
to want to put coda on a diskless client... "hmmm"...
