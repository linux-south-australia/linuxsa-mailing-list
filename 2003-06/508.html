IMHO Smoothwall is significantly better. The updates for patches etc are
released quicker. Also Smoothwall 2.0 Beta 5 is released and about ready for
use. I have it (SM 1.0) running and with several hundred GB of data through
it over the last 2 months it hasn't faltered. Everything is as easy as pie
just like ipcop. Web interface for everything. Port forwarding, DMZ, Dialup,
