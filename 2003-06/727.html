PDF seems the *perfect* format to use for Resume's, even not taking the whole
portability issue into account.  It's designed for documents just like this -
you're looking at a proper finalised view of the document, exactly how the
author intended it to look.  Word documents seem pointless - it's not meant to
be editable, and word shows widgets around things (i.e. textboxes, borders,
etc) - which isn't a finalised format ready for viewing.  Also often the
user's particular word configuration can change the way the document looks on
their screen.

I agree completely with your spit; the request seems totally stupid.  Doesn't
surprise me, though.
