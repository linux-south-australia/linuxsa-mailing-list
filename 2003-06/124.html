Re: Geek Boot camp

Hey, 

When I was at high school, we used to have a music camp at Nunyara 
Conference Centre at Belair. It has sleeping facilities, a large dining 
room, suitable for hosting a large quantity of computers, is secluded enough 
to be an 'escape' from the city, but being at the top of Old Belair Road, is 
less than 20 minutes from the city centre. 

Plus, the view over the city at sunset is absolutely beautiful! Combine that 
with a crowd of Linux geeks, their computers, strong coffee... I can't think 
of a better way to spend an evening! 

Hope this is helpful.
