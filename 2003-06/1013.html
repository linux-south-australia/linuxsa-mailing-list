Re: Phone lines and modems

get telstra out to check your line...there may be excessive noise, data is 
sensitive to such, esp if you go adsl. 
if it checksout ok, you prob wont have an issue - see, adsl puts a little 
splittery doobrey in from the wall - one bit goes phone, the other data.

so your topology is two devices attached to phone line bit, adsl modem 
attached to data bit.
