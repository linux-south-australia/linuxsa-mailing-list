Brian,


If you track Sarge you shouldn't be into too much trouble apart from possible
download limits and/or costs. Things that upgrade in Sarge (i.e. the testing
distribution) generally don't break everything.

If you track Sid you might fall into a little bit of trouble. Sid can break
things and not everything plays nicely. For example, the Gnome in Sid doesn't
like my Open Office but I don't know why. Things in Sid can just "break" - for
example last night all the GPG bindings in Sylpheed just, well, disappeared.

On the other hand, I'd consider as Sid to be as stable as a Redhat .0 release.
