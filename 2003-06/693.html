[ANNOUNCE] LinuxSA June Meeting

Hi again,

The LinuxSA June Meeting is on!  It will be held this coming Tuesday
(24/Jun) at 7pm.  If you will be participating in the GnuPG
keysigning, you must bring:

- Photo-id so that people can verify who you are.

- A hard-copy of the output of 'gpg --fingerprint your-id' so that you
  can verify the fingerprints I hand out are correct; this will look
  something like:

  pub  1024D/AE2354E9 2003-06-05 Geoffrey D. Bennett <g@netcraft.com.au>
       Key fingerprint = 76B4 46BE F92D E701 B7EA  3F7E 62AF 92D3 AE23 54E9
  sub  2048g/E03AF1B1 2003-06-05 [expires: 2008-06-03]

- A pen or pencil

- No computer needed; you'll do the actual signing when you get home.
  If you don't know how to do this, that's okay; we'll show you how at
  the meeting.

For those who missed the previous emails, if you want to participate,
please email keys@linuxsa.org.au with the output of:

  gpg --export -a your-key-id

If you don't already have a GnuPG key, you will need to install GnuPG
and run 'gpg --gen-key'.  For more information on keysigning, see
http://www.cryptnet.net/fdp/crypto/gpg-party.html and
http://people.debian.org/~stevenk/keysigning.html or for general GnuPG
info, see http://webber.dewinter.com/gnupg_howto/ and
http://www.gnupg.org/(en)/documentation/

The usual details:

  When:   7:00pm (doors open 6:45pm) on
          Tuesday, 24th Jun, 2003
  Where:  2nd Floor
          Compaq Computer
          139 Frome Street
          Adelaide SA
  Cost:   FREE
  Who:    Anyone and everyone.
          No pre-registration necessary.

Extras:

  As per usual, if you are having a problem with your Linux
  installation, feel free to bring your machine.  Make sure to bring
  everything necessary to make your computer work (eg. monitor,
  keyboard, etc).

Pizza:

  After the meeting, please join us for pizza at San Giorgios, a short
  walk north along Frome Street to the corner of Rundle Street.
