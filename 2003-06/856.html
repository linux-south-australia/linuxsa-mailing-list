Sam,


You could do:

./configure --prefix=/not/where/the/current/one/is

..fiddle with some symlinks:

(example)

cd /usr/bin/php4
mv php4 /usr/bin/php4-backup
ln -s /usr/bin/php /not/where/the/current/one/is/php php

And then if nothing breaks do a recompile.

You may not have to do the moving/ln-ing bit. You could simply point
Apache's DSO line in httpd.conf to the other php library.
