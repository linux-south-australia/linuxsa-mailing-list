Re: Mandrake 9.1 & kppp

Yeah, thats the same chipset that my modem has co-incidentally!! Those 
drivers are a god send, I wish there were more out there for others, but 
that has a lot to do with chipset makers. They are beta tho and as such 
they sometimes crash when dialing up, but there ya go!!

For those of you who haven't seen the website for the linux drivers for 
Conexant chipset based modems, it can be located here:
http://www.linuxant.com/drivers/

The website even has a test script to see if you have a modem based on 
the Conexant style chipset and will tell you what type of modem (HSF or 
HCF) that you have.
