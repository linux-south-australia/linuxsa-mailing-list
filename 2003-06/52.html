Re: Apache's Default Return Type

David Lloyd wrote:

> Is there are reason why most of the distributions in their default
> apache configuration have "text/plain" as their default mime type rather
> than "text/html"?
> 
> (I am just musing really)

Think of the default as being the most useful type
after you've been through the mime.types file.

It's obviously not HTML (as .htm and .html are almost
certainly in the mime.types file).

text/plain has the advantage of being displayed on
the screen by most browsers.
