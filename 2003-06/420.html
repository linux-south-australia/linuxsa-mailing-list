APANA is pleased to announce a free seminar covering ADSL and Virtual
Private Networks (VPN) for people with a technical interest in computer
networking. The seminar is to be held on Saturday June 21, from
12.45pm-3.30pm, at the Henley and Grange Town Hall (cnr Seaview Road and
North St). It will cover technical aspects of ADSL, how ADSL is implemented,
how and why VPN's are used, and why an economical VPN is useful for a
hobbyist. The seminar is free, and will have a technical emphasis. Light
refreshments will be provided. Ample parking is available near the venue.
This will be an interesting and informative seminar, and should be attended
by anyone who has an interest in the technical aspects of computer
networking and the Internet. Visitors are most welcome.

APANA is a not-for-profit hobby group providing internet access, technical
experience, and a hobby community for people who want to learn about all
aspects of computer networking. See http://sa.apana.org.au for more details.

Please phone 82325365 or email pr@sa.apana.org.au if you intend to attend.
