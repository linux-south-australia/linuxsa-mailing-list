Open Relays - New Server Check List

Last year we got flagged as an open relay, perhaps because of the gobs
of spam flowing through us one weekend :-)

Anyway Sendmail, 3128 and 8080 where secured an we got removed from the
active blacklists.

So as a service to newbie administrators could the Linuxsa list 
suggest a checklist of ports and settings to tweak.

I know there are many docs and howto's listed on the open relay
blacklist sites but often this is a case of "closing the door after the
horse has bolted".

The checklist would be like...

1. Ok you have your MX record make sure ports a,b & c are blocked for
but these users etc etc etc...
2. Go read http://howto.....

I just make this point as nearly 12 months on I'm still getting off
some blacklists.

BTW two experienced Linux gurus helped me setup that server.

Just a thought from one who has been burned when the hot spam hit the
fan......
