RE: [OT] CPU Speeds

CPU speed itself is not that accurate. Processing capability relates to Raw
CPU Speed / Front side Bus speed and architecture ( ie Intel or Power PC )
and type of instructions CISC/RISC.

You would be suprised at how low the "Mhz" speed of some really high end
computers. X86 Intel based chipsets are VERY inefficient
