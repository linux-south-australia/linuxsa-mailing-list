RE: Recruiters and MS Word

Quoting Richard Russell <richard@yellowgoanna.com>:

> Yes, but how does Joe Random Jobseeker make a PDF document? I know *I* can,
> at least, under *nix, but I can't easily do so under Windows without handing
> Adobe a bit fat wad of credit card, or going all illegal. 

It's not all that hard, under windows go and get the Adobe PS printer driver:

http://www.adobe.com/support/downloads/product.jsp?product=44&platform=Windows

Install and chose FILE: as the printer port.

Then go and get ghostscript for windows:

http://www.cs.wisc.edu/~ghost/doc/AFPL/get800.htm

Then every time you print to that 'printer' it will save a postscript file, and 
anyone who remembers how DOS commands work can to 'ps2pdf' on the saved file.

Maybe not point-and-clicky enough for Joe, but it's not like he couldn't figure 
it out given some written instructions.
