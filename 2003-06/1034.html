Sorry if this has already been said: 

 - Some Telstra Touchphone models (such as the 200) can cause problems on a 
line because they source their power from the line, resulting in a small 
"beep" every 15 minutes which confuses the modem. They can also cause 
connection problems. 

 - In order to get ADSL, you have to have your line checked, and seeing 
Telstra have been habitually turning down good lines (except if you get it 
from them) you should be okay. On the other hand, line noise will impede the 
speed of an ADSL connection, i dont know if it will decline a connection. I 
think.
