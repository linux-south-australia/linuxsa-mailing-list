RE: Mandrake passwords not working

Step one, try the Mandrake recovery CD. Once you have mounted the / partition, cat your /<mounted>/etc/passwd file and see how it looks.

Step two, check to see what partition the data is on. If its accessible - and you have the need - copy it some place safe.

Two steps to happy system recovery - in this case :-)
