RE: ext2/ext3 Partition Recovery Tools?

And BEFORE you put it in the fridge (or freezer) you need to seal it in a
air tight bag. Before you do either of them, don't bother. The fridge
freezer thing to get disks started again after the bearings have seized up,
it wont help with bad sectors on the disk. You can also just drop the disk
straight down on the bottom from a reasonable height to  possibly dislodge
the platters. This wont help you with a disk that is physically damaged on
the platters. If its scratched, its scratched, temperature, knocks and
Viagra pills wont be able to revive that particular data.
