This one time, at band camp, Robyn Manning <rmanning@adam.com.au> wrote:

> Hi
> 
> I'm in a similar predicament, my phone line and all phone lines in the
> street are on a sub-exchange which I think is like saying a RIMM. There
> is no cable in my street and no time frame for either changing. I may
> wait for untimed ISDN or there's supposed to be some upgrades coming in

So, what I did was to use my austar dish (from an old austar installation) and
stuck in a new LNB $50.00, and pointed it to a nearby satellite suppliers
satellite and bingo, I have 2Mb downloads, downside of course is modem 
back channel, but its cheap and an alternative when no *dsl is available.
and its nice to download those ISO's :)
