Re: Firewalls.. ipchains (appears filtered from outside,nothing from inside)

Richard Russell wrote:

> Exactly how the difference between closed and filtered comes about in
> your case, I'm not entirely sure.

Closed typically means that the host has sent back either a RST response
for a TCP connection, or an ICMP destination port unreachable for UDP.
Filtered is the negation of ports in a closed or open state, which could
include upstream routers sending a "destination administratively prohibited"
ICMP message, which could be the case for Jonathan.
