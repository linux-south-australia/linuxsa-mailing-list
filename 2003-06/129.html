At the time, my friend was probably being the "selfless hero" and trying to 
help with little knowledge of what was to come, or what could possibly come. 

Now that I have Senior First Aid, there is some question of duty of care. I 
am a little unclear of who I have to help, and who I can leave alone, but I 
know that the moment I even stop to check on someone, I am obliged to help 
them to the furthest extent that I can, covered by my training and own 
physical abilities. 

I am sorry to say that it is probably easier to just not pay attention and 
keep walking past. I could never do this, regardless of the consequences, I 
would have to stop and help.
