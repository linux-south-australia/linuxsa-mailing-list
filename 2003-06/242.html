[ANNOUNCEMENT]: SA-AUUG June Monthly Meeting 11/6/2003

The South Australian chapter of the Australian Unix User Group (AUUG)
formally invites you to attend their monthly meeting. Details are as
follows:
+----------------------------------------
| SA-AUUG Chapter Meeting
+--------+-------------------------------
| DATE   | Wednesday 11th June 2003
| TIME   | 7pm Onwards
| VENUE  | Microbits
|        | 35 Magill Road
|        | STEPNEY
+--------+------------------------------
| AGENDA |
+--------+------------------------------

1. Audio on FreeBSD, A musical howto

  Greg Lehey has recently spent some time installing audio
  applications on his laptop, which runs FreeBSD 5.0.  He'll talk
  about the surprises he experienced and will present a reasonable
  configuration for playing CDs and MP3, ripping CDs and encoding
  MP3s.  A lively discussion is to be expected.

----------------------------------------
At the conclusion of the meeting many members of the group continue
to talk as they wine and dine at one of the fine local restaurant
establishments to which all are invited.

Hope to see you there.

Regards,
  SA-AUUG Secretary
