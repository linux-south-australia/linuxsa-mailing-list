    Jon Casimir: The video recorder was introduced in 1975 and in fact a
    lot of people thought it was a failure. It was only ever thought of
    as what they called a time shifter. It was going to be only used for
    people who wanted to tape a TV program and watch it later. The big
    film companies did not see it as a potential distribution mechanism
    for films. That was not the idea when video came in and in fact the

    often the case in history inventions are not used for what the
    inventors suspected they would be.

    made no effort to restrict it. And which technology survived? The
    people who wanted to watch adult entertainment they bought a VHS
