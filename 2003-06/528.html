Re: Wireless Access Point - 802.11b

Unless you're going to buy a high end AP (eg Cisco etc) then the SVECs seem 
pretty good based on my experiences with helping a friend configure his.  
(they're bloody simple compared to the many menus of options on the Cisco 
AP/Bridges - which is good and bad I guess)

Given the price you can buy them for via the TIB (www.wireless.org.au/tib) 
you can't go wrong really.
