Cable chain technology. ie KVM

The IMB X330 Server does not have any Video/Keyboard or Mouse connectors on
the rear. Unless you have a C2T breakout cable for this purpose ( and they
are not supplied ) you need to install a PCI video card and then use a USB
keyboard and USB mouse.
