> The only problem I have with all forms of "mirroring" is that 
> it's dumb to the application. I was once in a situation where

Surely, having mirroring transparent to the application is a good thing, at
least, for most applications (databases being a major exception)...

> 
> Fine we say. There's a backup copy.
> 
> However, it hadn't failed quite enough to kill the machine

right? Without knowing much about DRBD, I would suspect that if it notices
