> Hm - is it a normal ring tone, or the strange ringtones you get with
> services such as Messagebank?
>
> Most modems barf if its a nonstandard ring tone.
>
Most modems support Xn where:
n=0 Send connect only
n=1 Send connect speed
n=2 Look for dial tone, send connect speed
n=3 Look for busy, send connect speed
n=4 Look for busy & dial tone, send connect speed

4 is often the default. If your modem has trouble recognising the dial tone
try adding X3 somewhere in the initialisation string or dial string sent to
the modem, eg

AT&FX3...
ATX3DT...
