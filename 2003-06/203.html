Constitutional right??... by that are you referring to the right to free
speech?? In Australia, we have no equivalent of the USA's "Bill of
Rights. Our rights are inferred but not written. While, I support the
notion of free speech, I don't consider that to extend to to
force people to listen. Hence, the way that i see it what is the
different between using an automated tool to reject the email compared
with having email packages to delete based on keywords in a filter.

You can personally blacklist IPs and even entire domains. It gets tricky
when an entity starts blocking spam on behalf of a group of
people(Either).

Ian, I'd suggest that you install Spam assasin or something like it and
go from there. Otherwise change ISP to one that is proactive.
