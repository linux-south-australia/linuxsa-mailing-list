Re: long delay when trying to telnet/ssh/ftp into Debian 2.2r 2

Sounds like your system can't resolve the IP no from which you are
comming.

(Kludge on) 
try putting an entry in to:
/etc/hosts

like:

192.168.0.15	machine-name

^^ your IP no of ^^ the workstation your on.
