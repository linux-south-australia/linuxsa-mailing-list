Re: How to add a disclaimer message?

Raymond J Y Chin wrote:

> Hi, I'm looking for a way in the near future to add a disclaimer to all my
> companies emails... 

As Neil Rickert said:

"A disclaimer added by the sender MUA is a statement by that sender.

 A statement added automatically by sendmail as the message passes
 through the MTA, and added without the intent of the sender, isn't a
 statement by anyone.  There is no reason to give any credibility to
 such a disclaimer."

If you must implement it, libmilter would be the way to go.  Perl and
Python both have libmilter interfaces.
