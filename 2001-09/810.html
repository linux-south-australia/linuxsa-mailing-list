Mandrake Linux 8.1 Released

Hiya,

Some of you might not care, but I think this is a very important
distribution for general desktop use. Mandrake have announced the release of
Mandrake Linux 8.1 tonight, it has a 2.4.8 kernel with KDE 2.2.1. Watching
the FTP servers Mandrake waited around 8-24 hours while the mirrors were
ready.

Read about the announcement here: http://www.linux-mandrake.com/en/81.php3
Mandrake's FTP mirror list here: http://www.linux-mandrake.com/en/ftp.php3

Otherwise, get it from PlanetMirror
