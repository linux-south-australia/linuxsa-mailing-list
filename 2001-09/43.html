Hi Brian,

We gave up on Mandrake and loaded up RH 7.1 with succcess. Seems it has 
a better driver or coding or whatever for this card, Cirrus Logic GD-
5446. My son did some fiddling during install and lo it worked. He cant 
remember exactly what he did but we are now getting a crystal clear 
screen running at 1024. Before with Mandrake 8 and 8.1 we could not 
even get a VGA signal up at 640 and 256 colours. 

Sorry I cant help any more on what the change was but RH 7.1 is doing a 
great job at the moment.

My next trick will be to get StarOffice in there plus a few other 
applications and games to provide a range of tools for demonstration. 
We also need to get a few handy icons running so as to come close to 
what an average Windows user might expect. When planning on switching 
people over to a new system you must always go from the known to the 
unkown so a familar desktop look will ease the user into the Linux 
environment. Later they can modify the environment to suit themsleves.

My eventual goal is for this to be a test bed system that we can cut 
down to fit into a 1.2 G drive for our other systems. As we get closer 
to that goal I will need to work out how to get an image made or 
perhaps a kickstart file so that we can reproduce what we have built 
onto other systems. 

I appreciate your help and the many others who have offered advice and 
help through this list and also through the Linux Forum on CompuServe. 

Cheers, Ian
