Re: Red Hat 6.2 for Community Computer

Some time ago I had a problem with Mandrake 7.0 and later 8.0 not being able 
to configure a Diamond Stealth card. This happened several times right at the 
end of an install. I was forced to locate and install some files in /etc/X11  
and do some hacking on the XF86Config file. You take a punt in doing this as 
you may stuff up a monitor. My hack worked ok and continues to do its job. 
Using a working XF86Config file may be a good place to start.  
or
Can you get another inexpensive card to get your PC at least operational ?

Brian Marr
