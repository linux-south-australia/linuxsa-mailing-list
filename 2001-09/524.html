> Not quite what you're asking, but still potentially useful:
> 
> Jed Microsystems (www.jedmicro.com.au) in Victoria have for many, 
> many years sold an EPROM programmer that plugs into the parallel 
> port, and which can erase flash ROMs.  Price on the web page 
> is quoted at $330 (including GST).  The unit uses a plug pack 
> to get power from the mains.  
> 
> I've used these programmers over a long time, and while they can't 
> handle the newer, fancier EPROMs, they've excelled at programming 
> the standard EPROMs.  The unit is quite small, compared to most 
> programmers, and so could be carried on service calls and operated 
> via a laptop.
