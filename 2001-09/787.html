device          ie0     at isa? port 0x300 irq 10 iomem 0xd0000

The current kernel lists the ie0 driver as above.  It may be that the card
is either not in PnP mode or your machines does not support/like it in PnP
mode.  If you can run a DOS based config program on the card, try and
change it to IRQ 10, IO 0x300.
