On Tue, Sep 25, 2001 at 09:42:45AM +0930, Tyson Lloyd Thwaites wrote:
> Once nice thing was that scanpci actually says it found an "S3 SavagePro
> Km133", which is the proper spec for the card. On 6.2 it only found a

Some of these spiffy video chips on the motherboard require AGP support
and Direct Rendering Mananger support in the kernel (or compiled as
modules). If you use make menuconfig it's the last 2 items in the
Character devices sub-menu.
After booting dmesg|less will show you what the kernel found and after
startx & /var/log/XFree86.0.log should show you what X found.
The machine I did this on is an Intel i810 with an i815 video chip and
was useless until I upgraded the kernel. Now I can die in Doom in just
a few milliseconds.........
