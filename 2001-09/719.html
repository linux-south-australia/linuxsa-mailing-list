Re: Auto start squid

Ken Evans wrote:
> 
> i have squid running and operating on redhat 7.1 but cannot find or at least
> understand how to automate it at bootup, any help much appreciated

This is fully described in Red Hat's documentation.
  http://www.redhat.com/docs/manuals/linux/RHL-7.1-Manual/custom-guide/access-services.html

To cut to the chase, for a standalone process like Squid
you need something like
  chkconfig --add squid
  chkconfig squid on
