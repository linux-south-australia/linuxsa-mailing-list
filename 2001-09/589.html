Re: OT: Best licence for a new Open Source package ...

On Friday, 21 September 2001 at 12:11:23 +0930, Richard Sharpe wrote:
> Hi,
>
> I am starting a new SourceForge project that I intend to provde code to
> be made available to a wide range of people and projects ...
>
> I am unclear on what licence to use. It seems to me that there are two
> licences I can use, the GPL or the BSD licence.
>
> My intent is that anyone be able to use the project that I am starting,
> without subjecting their project (even if propietary) to the dangers of
> the GPL.
>
> Can anyone advise?

(removes BSD hat)

Sounds like you want the BSD license, then.  I'm sure there are
others, but it's a real pain to compare licenses, as we've seen from
time to time.  Unless you have a real objection to the BSD licence or
GPL, I think you should use one of those two.  In this case, the BSD
license appears to be the more appropriate one.
