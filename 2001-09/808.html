[OT] IBM ships 1,000th Linux eServer

IBM ships 1,000th Linux-based eServer z900 mainframe 
By Ed Scannell 
September 24, 2001 3:45 am PT
  
 HOPING TO OFFER proof that Linux-based mainframes are making inroads 
into the Unix- and Windows-based server markets, IBM on Monday 
announced it has shipped its 1,000th eServer z900 mainframe after just 
nine months of availability.

The company receiving the unit is Boscov's, one of the largest family-
owned department stores in the country, which intends to replace 44 
servers running Windows NT that comprises its server farm.
