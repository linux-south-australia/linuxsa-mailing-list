Re: Recording video

On 10 Sep 2001 12:31:52 +0930, Dean Gawler wrote:
> I'm having trouble finding a decent Linux app that will let me record
> video from my BT848 TV card. I have some home video that I would like
> to turn into either an AVI or MPG video so that I can email it to my
> relatives.
IIRC ffmpeg and mp1e are decent for grabbing stuff from /dev/video and
turning it straight into MPEG. A recent ffmpeg should do "DivX ;-)" too.
(Though I've never actually used either for grabbing video, I've only
done mpeg-mpeg and mpeg-divx converting).
