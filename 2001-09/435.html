AMD vs Intel

Tom's Hardware has an evaluation of thermal protection in modern CPUs,
which starkly points out a huge difference between Intel and AMD chips.

http://www.tomshardware.com/cpu/01q3/010917/index.html

On the basis of this alone, I don't think I'll be touching AMD with a
ten foot barge-pole.  People with AMD chips in servers should take 
particular note of the fire hazard aspect, since those machines often
run unattended...
