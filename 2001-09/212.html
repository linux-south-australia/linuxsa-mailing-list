Re: Dynamic DNS

On Fri, Sep 07, 2001 at 10:01:48PM +0930, Mick Collins wrote:
> Hello Group. Is it possable to setup a Dynamic dns SERVER?? For example I
> have pheermy.homeip.net automatically updating everytime I logonto the
> internet.
> Can I add sub domains to this dynamic address?

as in leet.pheermy.homeip.net and haxor.pheermy.homeip.net and so on?
;)

technically it's possible to set up a dynamic DNS server... however,
in this case, hopeip are highly unlikely to turn over administration
of the pheermy.homeip.net domain, so whatever you do, it will not be
visible to the outside world -- you can claim whatever you like on
your internal net, but no-one on the outside will know to look for
your nameserver for pheermy.homeip.net -- they will first as the root
nameserver, which will point them to the net nameserver, which will
point them to the homeip nameserver which will respond with the
address for the host called pheermy...  you can't take that any
further than that without the cooperation of homeip, which IMHO, you
won't get... :)
