You can usually find out from most MTAs using telnet and SMTP:

$ telnet [hostname] 25
220 [hostname] ESMTP
EHLO [hostname]
250-[hostname]
250-PIPELINING
250 8BITMIME
250 SIZE 10000000
250 SMTP AUTH
quit

Or similar.
