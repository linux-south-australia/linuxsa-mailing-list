> Will
> XF86Config options override detected parameters? I suppose they would.


Yep, what you tell it is what it will try. Two more points that may be 
of use:

1) Make sure you are editing the right XFree86 config file. See 
http://www.xfree86.org/4.0.3/XF86Config.5.html#sect1 for where XFree86 
is going to look, and in what order it's looking.

2) Don't just 'startx', try 'startx 1>/some/log/file 2>&1' to log all 
the output of startx into a file that you can read after it fails to 
work. There are probably some clues in there that explain why X failed 
to start up that will help you track down what you need to set in your 
XFree86 config....
