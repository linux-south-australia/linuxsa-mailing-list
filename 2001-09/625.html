Re: Locating ethernet card I/O address

Hi...

Im thinking the network card is an old ISA non PnP card?
If it is, the only way to find the IO addr the card is at is by looking
at the card and the manual for it.
It should have jumpers on it which set the IO address, so its just a
matter of working out what jumper positioins give what IO addresses. Youll
need the card manual for this, or at least some info on the jumper
settings.

MWP
