I'm not the one to ask. There did seem to be good interest, but I have
never been to an ACS meeting before. One highly-skilled Adelaide developer
walked out, leaving me a voicemail that "he couldn't stand it any more",

about evenly split between very technical people (many with some free
software exposure) and not-so-technical people (many with no exposure to
free software.) 50 copies of a paper-friendly version of "What are Linux
and BSD" from http://shearer.org vanished.

I hope people found it useful.
