Re: New IIS worm??

On Tue, Sep 18, 2001 at 11:13:35PM +0800, Michael Neuling wrote:

 > Just noticed a bunch of weird accesses on my web server.  I've had
 > about 160 hits in the last 2 hours and none before that.  Other people
 > seem to be geting them aswell.  Anyone know anything about it??
 > 210.108.138.74 - - [18/Sep/2001:21:17:43 +0800] "GET
 > /scripts/..%255c../winnt/system32/cmd.exe?/c+dir HTTP/1.0" 404 232

It's a CodeRed exploit.

One of the things CodeRed II did was to copy cmd.exe into the web server's
scripts directory, so that anyone on the Internet could run any command
on a compromised IIS server.  It looks like these log entries are probes
to work out whether your server is one of those hapless victims, probably
a precursor to something more serious.
