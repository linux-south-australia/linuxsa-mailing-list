As a High Quality Desktop OS: Linux?

Hiya,

I'm reading this article on The Register
(http://www.theregister.co.uk/content/4/21899.html) about Microsoft's
admission of Linux becoming a very significant desktop OS for business
users. Now from my own experience with Red Hat 7.1, and especially with
Mandrake 8.0, and with the initial opinions of of Mandrake 8.1. Linux in
these recent distros have become truely accessible enough for a very
