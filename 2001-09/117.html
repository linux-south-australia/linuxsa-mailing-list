Re: Problems with Netgear PS105 Printserverbox and HP 4000T

Hi.

I haven't used a netgear printserver, but usually you need to nominate the
remote queue name. Does the netgear have a queue called 'raw' ?

remote|lp4|192.168.2.5-raw|192.168.2.5 raw:\
        :lp=:\
        :rm=192.168.2.5:\
        :rp=raw:\
        :sd=/var/spool/lpd/192.168.2.5-raw:\
        :lf=/var/spool/lpd/192.168.2.5-raw/log:\
        :af=/var/spool/lpd/192.168.2.5-raw/acct:\
        :ar:bk:mx#0:\
        :tr=:cl:sh:
#
### END   apsfilter: ### remote 192.168.2.5 raw ###


On our DLink printservers, they have a modifiable name for the queue that
I usually rename into something that will help identify the device and
printer - the last part of the ip and the port name is my usual style,
such that a printer on the second parallel port on a printserver at
192.192.177.121 would have it's port renamed to 121-P2 and the rp line
would then read:

       :rp=121-P2
