Re: Netlogon Via Samba

On Tue, 18 Sep 2001, Michael Wardle wrote:

> Please read more about this on <URL:http://www.samba.org/>;, or on the 
> samba(7) and smb.conf(5) manual pages on your system.  You may need to 
> install packages called samba or samba-server.

It's probably also worth noting these three points:

* Samba 2.0.7 and later come with the book 'Using Samba' (in HTML), which
is published by O'Reilly and Associates and now maintained by the Samba
team.

* Samba 2.2.2 is due out within a couple of weeks and will provide much
cleaner Domain Control for clients as new as Windows 2000 with service
pack 2 installed.

* RedHat 6.0 came with a fairly old (perhaps 2.0.[3-5]) version of Samba,
which is did not have a lot of the RPC stuff that was added to Samba
2.x. If it's possible, try to use 2.2.x when using Samba as a Domain
Controller.

Hope it helps,
