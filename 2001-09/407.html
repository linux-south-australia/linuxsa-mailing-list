>  > > tree from ftp.freebsd.org, or whatever), then the package won't

>  > Why can't a cracker update source and md5sums?

> Because they'd need to break into the source repository to update

So what you are saying is that the source and md5sums are located in two 
different locations.  That still just makes it a little harder, not a lot 
harder.

Presumably you need to update your collection of md5sums from ftp.freebsd.org 
(or wherever) occasionally for later versions of things in ports.  That 
appears to be the weakness.  Of course gpg signing patches would be a great 
thing to do to fix that :-)
