If things are really new to u, make you way to linuxnewbie.org and 
linuxhelp.org and spend some time browsing and reading howto's and 
beginners guides.  To explain to a new user how to install a program 
would be a major job, even knowing the distribution. A few hours reading 
and a repost with more details  would help us in helping you :-)

Good luck and be patient, Linux is a great system but needs a steady 
patient learning style.
