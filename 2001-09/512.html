Alfred, OpenSource Internet Software website

ALFRED, OPENSOURCE INTERNET SOFTWARE WEBSITE LAUNCHED

Alfred, OpenSource Internet Software website has been released,
available for all to see at http://www.alfred.cx/

The website, while still needing some content, provides the viewer with
enough information about the software to be practical.

The project has been generously supported by Leader Computer Systems,
South Australia's leading computer wholesaler. With the hardware that
they provide, many of the previously unreachable goals are within our
grasp.

As many of you would know, this is not the first time that Leader has
shown their support of the OpenSource community. The hardware they
provided for the 2001 InstallFest made life for many of us much easier,
as well as giving us the ability to demonstrate the capabilities of
Linux in a wide range of situations.

For those that are interested in Alfred and what it does, please read
the "About" document, located in the "Documentation" section of the
website.

Those interested in helping out with the project should contact me via
email, phone or paper plane at their earliest convenience.
