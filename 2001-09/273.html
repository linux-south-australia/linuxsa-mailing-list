 > Wouldn't you have to fiddle with X security here to allow root
 > to connect to your X display (ie, a secure version of "xhost

No.  Clients will look for $HOME/.Xauthority to find the authenticator
they need to connect to your X server;  "su" doesn't change the value
of $HOME, so your root session will keep looking in the same place for
it.
