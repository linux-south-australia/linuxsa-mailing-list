Re: Outlook alternative for Unix?

Well I don't know where you'll find a more insecure email product, but 
there's OpenMail from HP that's supposed to be an Outlook replacement, but 
it's no longer freely available. They've also stopped development on it.
More info is available at: http://www.openmail.com/

A 'webmail' solution allows users to access their email from any machine 
regardless of where they are or what OS they're using. In my experience this 
is a good solution to users who are typically at different terminals on 
different sites.
