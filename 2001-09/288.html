Re: Digital camera suggestions

On Tue, Sep 11, 2001 at 02:21:52PM +0930, Fraser Farrell wrote:
> Any recommendations for a digital camera that:
> 
>  - works with Linux,
> 
>  - is available in Adelaide (or Australia at least),
> 
>  - doesn't cost more than my car,
> 
>  - doesn't have any proprietary gotchas such as unique memory
> packs or weird batteries.

... or just any camera that has a removable CF card and a file format
recognisable by an application supported by Linux. Then, simply purchase
a USB SanDisk CF Reader. The supported model (ImageMate CompactFlash USB
SDDR-31, although I've had success with SDDR-35) works thanks to the
usb-storage model.
