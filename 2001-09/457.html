Re: Big Pond Gigasat and Linux

ISDN might be better, you might want to examine your end use.  I presume 
you will need a back channel to bigpond through a modem and this will 
slow any outgoing traffic.  Your 'ping' will be a lot slower, so for 
games this might not be too good.

There are some on the list who work with that stuff day in and day out, 
some advise from them might be in order, before your big leap.

On the other hand if downloading is all you are concerned about then SAT 
might be just the thing.

Good luck anyways and have fun :-)

Steve
