Re: Perl search and replace

Vi has excellent search and replace, and I am sure emacs does to, but I
think vi is faster for soemthgn like this.

Appropriate commands are:

vi <filename>
:0,$s/192.168.101.2/192.168.0.200/g
:wq

Of course make a backup, just in case somethign goes wrong, if it is a
file that is hard to reproduce.
