Hi Bruce, 
Personally, I use email redirection through a hosting site. I arranged
for the virtusertable to point to my username instead of my isp's mail
server. I then use procmail rules to filter out spammers at the hosting
site. This works well for me and ensures that most spam never makes it
to my workstation.
