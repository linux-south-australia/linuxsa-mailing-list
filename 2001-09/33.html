> Anyway now that I'm setting up various Linux boxes (mail & web serving etc)
> around the campus I was wondering is there a Linux (I'm running RH 7.1)
> utility and/or script that will import great long delimited lists to create
> heaps of new users on the fly?

yeah:


simple one... which could be written much better by someone who knows
awk better than I, and could be incorrect, but you get the idea...

assuming user_list is "Name, username, Homegroup, passwd"...

# cat user_list | awk -F',' '{print "adduser --disabled-passwd \
	--gecos \"", $1, " ", $2}' > create_users
# cat user_list | awk -F',' '{print "(echo ", $4, "; echo ", $4, ") |
# passwd ", $2}' >> create_users
# chmod u+x create_users
# ./create_users

or something like that...

note, I haven't tried this... you will want to experiment a little and
do some debugging first... Also, you could do similar things with
chfn...
