Hi Matthew

I would say the actual problem is, that the memory is not supported by your
mainboard, There are new standard of memory which I now that most of the
older (1 year to 2 years old) athlon mainboards have problems with.

I was only able to get 2 x 128Meg chips to work correctly.

Hope this is of some help
