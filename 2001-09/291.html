Re: disapearing disk space problem

On Tue, 11 Sep 2001, Horst Poehlmann wrote:
> Well, it may be an inode problem. I used to be able to write a number to
> /proc/sys/fs/inodes-max to increase the inodes.

No, that never increased the number of inodes on disk, it increased the
number of inodes that you could have open.  Increasing the number of inodes
on a filesystem is a subclass of changing the size of a filesystem.  I
believe there is a utility, resize2fs, that might be worth looking at
after you have taken a couple of backups.
