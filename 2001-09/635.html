Re: Locating ethernet card I/O address

Hi Nathan,
	Richard's suggestion is good except that you should try
`modprobe ne` in place of `modprobe 3c509`.  You could also try
`cat /proc/ioports`.  Failing this, download the drivers for the
RTL8019 chipset from Realtek and run the setup utility
(setup.exe or rset8019.exe) from a DOS prompt!

Don.
