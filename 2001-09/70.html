Re: DVD Writer

Well, yes and no.. DVD's are meant to use the UDF filesystem.

However, I don't think there's anything stopping you from using a very large
ISO9660 image.. except possibly size limitations?

The correct thing to use is UDF though.

The cdrecording tools don't care what filesystem it is though; they just
burn the data onto the disc.

Davis - you may need to enable the SCSI-over-IDE feature of the kernel. I
know you require this to use IDE CDR drives.
