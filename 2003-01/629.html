Fast Forward on XMMS

I have just installed Lycoris Desktop/LX (beta build 66) onto a Leader
laptop (Excel 2700C). All is going pretty good now except for when I
want to play any Ogg or MP3 files. at random monemts during the songs
they will literally fast forward. They will change pitch dramatically
and the elapsed time indicator will jump forward to match. Then th song
will continue to play fom the new position till the next fast forward
jump. 

Now these songs have been copied from my desktop machine running the
same version of OS and the same version of XMMS - 1.2.7 - and all songs
play very nicely on the desktop machine. Therefore it is nothing to do
with the original recording/encoding. It has to be something within the
laptop's audio system and its relationship with the decoder.

Any clues or suggestions on how to correct this very annoying problem?

Oh, BTW, the laptop plays fine when the Windows HD is installed and WAV
files play fine through the XMMS player. It is only when playing Ogg and
MP3 files that the effect is noticable.
