Re: Bridging question

On Tue, 2003-01-07 at 09:54, Andrew Reid wrote:

> Anyway, bridging is still useful every now and then.

Many NT administrators prefer to use bridges over routers. The reason
for this is because the network traffic that is responsible for the
lists of machines that 'appear' in 'Network Neighbourhood' (browser
traffic), is not routed by many routers without some hassle. Samba gets
around this sort of problem in a fairly sensible fashion.
