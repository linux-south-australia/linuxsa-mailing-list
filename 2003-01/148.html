[ANNOUNCEMENT]: January SA-AUUG Chapter Meeting Wed 6/01/2003

The South Australian chapter of the Australian Unix User Group (AUUG)
formally invites you to attend their monthly meeting. Details are as
follows:

+----------------------------------------
| SA-AUUG Chapter Meeting
+--------+-------------------------------
| DATE   | 8th, January 2003
| TIME   | 7pm Onwards
| VENUE  | Internode
|        | Level 3, 132 Grenfell Street
|        | ADELAIDE
+--------+------------------------------
| AGENDA |
+--------+------------------------------

1. Voice Over IP Using Internet Scandards (Andrew Rutherford)
    The famous Andrew Rutherford of IAUG Consulting will be giving an
    indepth talk about setting up voice over IP. Learn how to talk for
    free, learn about Quality of Service (QoS) settings to make it more
    reliable, minimising latency and increasing performance. There will
    also be a live demo on Voice Over IP in action.

2. Discussion on SA-AUUG and the year Ahead
    Another Year and a great future lies head for the computing
    community. With so much going on the SA-AUUG committee wants to
    narrow down where peoples interest lie to bring you better talks,
    more demonstrations and make your membership even more worth while.
 
----------------------------------------
At the conclusion of the meeting many members of the group continue
to talk as they wine and dine at one of the fine local restaurant
establishments to which all are invited.

Hope to see you there.

Regards,
  SA-AUUG Secretary
