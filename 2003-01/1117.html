Re: Lots of SSH Clients but....

Well,
I use TTP.  Mainly because it's really good at being a serial terminal 
client as well as doing telnet.  It's also got xwindows style cut and paste!
It starts up with a window that allows you to select a profile (ssh, telnet, 
serial all with specific options).

(I'm primarily a network engineer and I spend a LOT of time connected to 
routers and switches via serial and telnet.  TTP does the job for all of 
these tasks pretty well.  It copes with logging to a file well etc).

My only experiences with PuTTY were brief and I remeber having a bit of an 
issue with not being able to setup something (but I do not remember what).
