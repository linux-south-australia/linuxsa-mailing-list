Well, I have a problem with OSX that I have not been able to get ANY support or interest from Apple from, and has been logged with the apple helpdesk, with a zero result. (It's the dying lookupd problem)

Last time I spoke with the helpdesk was after installing Jaguar, and I had to talk with some moron who was not conversant with anything more detailed than how to log on and start an app.
