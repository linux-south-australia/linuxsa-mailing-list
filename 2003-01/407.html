Brandon Johnson wrote:

> FYI.
>
> If your linux box doesnt respond to pings anymore (even 
> localhost/loopback) it may be because your kernel has been told not to 
> respond to ICMP at ALL!
> My /proc/sys/net/ipv4/icmp_echo_ignore_all was set to 1 (true I 
> think), which meant I could do little to test network connectivity. After
> echo -n 0 > /proc/sys/net/ipv4/icmp_echo_ignore_all
> all was well.
> Just something to jot down in case you ever need a linux box to test 
> connections like if your routing for an installfest or something ;)
>
> At least I know for next time
