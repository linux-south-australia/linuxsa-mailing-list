The reason the iMac looks ok when you change screen res is because 
apart from the monitor stretching the pixels, MacOS X anti-aliases 
quite well. However because of this you loose sharpness at lower 
resolutions (or to put it another way, the display looks "softer" at 
lower resolutions).

My iBook natively does 1024x768 but will quite happily do 800x600 and 
640x480, but both lower resolutions are not as sharp as the native res.
