As David pointed out, you do need an active phone service to have ADSL
installed. 

For the majority of ADSL providers your line has to be a "Telstra
service" to have ADSL provisioned. iPrimus, AAPT etc as a rule resell
Telstra copper, so these are included under "Telstra service".

Optus on the other hand use their HFC network extensively in the eastern
states, and in a few places they use their own copper as well. Other
places it's resold Telstra.

So depending where you are, I'd be a bit careful who you choose as a
phone provider.
