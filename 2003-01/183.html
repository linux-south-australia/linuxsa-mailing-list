Re: XMMS not working

Due to patent issues and releasing mp3 decoders with paying
distributions may incur patent royalties, RH are playing safe and
avoiding mp3 instead offering the most recent version of ogg at distro
release time. So the playing of mp3's under a standard RH8.0 install
won't work. http://www.xmms.org/ have released the mp3 plugin RH8.0
users, it is also available from http://psyche.freshrpms.net/ .
