NOIE home page for Govt CIO/CTOs

http://www.noie.gov.au/projects/egovernment/Better_Infrastructure/oss.htm

This is about a one-day seminar for people who run IT in government
organisations. It is a vendor-neutral event. AUUG will be speaking,
along with a number of suppliers of open and closed solutions. There
will be presentations from successful Australian govt open
source deployments.

NOIE == "National Office of Information Infrastructure".

I am not posting this because people generally might be interested in
going. As far as I understand it, the doors are closed to everyone
except for IT managers in government. But there are a lot of manager.
If you know one, think about passing this message on. There are many
perceptions that this kind of event seeks to explore, including "but
where can I get support for Open Source products from?".
