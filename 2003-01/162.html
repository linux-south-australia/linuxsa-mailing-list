Are you trying to play a mp3?  xmms under RedHat doesn't support mp3
natively 
right now due to licensing concerns - there are RH8 RPMs with support
available 
from 3rd parties on the web which are meant for RH8.  Google will help.

And before people get on the bandwagon, I don't blame RH for going down
this 
path - distributors haven't been given immunity from MP3 licensing
costs, so a) 
Red Hat would have to charge more, and b) their freely downloadable ISOs
would 
have to differ from the "supported" ISOs.  Go Ogg Vorbis go!

OTOH, if other sound files don't work as well, look at what sound
daemons you 
have going, that might help.  I don't believe there's been any change
there, 
but it wouldn't hurt to check.
