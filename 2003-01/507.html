Andrew,

There's a StringBuffer class with a whole bunch of
append() methods.

Not like sprintf though.

Try (something like):

StringBuffer temp = new StringBuffer("My name is ");
temp.append(FirstName);
temp.append(" ");
temp.append(LastName);
temp.append(".");

System.out.println(temp.toString());

There's also a bunch of insert() methods.
