You dont *need* to bridge.

In fact, with wireless networking, its a much preferred solution to either
route, with wireless clients on a different subnet, or to just supply a VPN
on the AP, which once tunneled into will give a local address anyway
(basically giving you an IP on the same subnet, with the same end result as
bridging anyway).
