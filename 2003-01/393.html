Andrew,

You could be having a problem with "Anonymous Users". The anonymous user entries will take precedence first.

A book that I can recommend is Mysql written by Paul DuBois published by New Riders. Pages 429 and 464-465 cover this issue.

To fix Try:-
delete from user where User="";
flush privileges;
