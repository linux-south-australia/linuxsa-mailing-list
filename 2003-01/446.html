Re: duplicating a hard drive

I thought cable select worked by a pin being cut in the cable between the two connectors. Therefor the first plug was always master and the second was always slave (or vice versa). Therefore, one of your drives is always going to be slave, and your bios is still going to need the capability to boot from it.  Perhaps you just had a good bios?
