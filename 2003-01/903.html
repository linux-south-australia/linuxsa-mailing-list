If you want to watch 16:9 on your 800 x 600 monitor with minimal
distortion, it's going to be letterboxed (black bars above and below the
image) Tv's and projectors do all sorts of stuff to eliminate this
problem on a 4:3 screen, but they are either throwing away the image at
the edges or distorting the image by stretching it to the screen height.

Solution? Widescreen projector or display, so that the movie is diplayed
in it's native format. That PowerBook G4 17 incher would do it nicely
for you too...
