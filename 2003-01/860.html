Re: ext2 -> fat32

Luke <guru255@bigpond.com> wrote:
> I'm trying to find any source of info, howto
> convert (without loosing data) ext2 to fat32.

For some definition of loosing [sic], I'd suggest cp.  Keep in mind that
fat32 maintains less meta-data; for example you are absolutely goint to
lose user ids and group ids, symbolic links, and permission bits.  If you
want to convert in-situ, I believe you can do that with vi and gcc. :-)
