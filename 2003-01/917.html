Re: Ogle and 4 speakers

I don't have a direct answer to your question (sorry) but in a related thread 
on emu10k1 sound quality, I became aware of utilities and drivers obtained 
through Creative and Source Forge.  You could get some insight into the 
architecture by reading the documents which come with:
	emu-tools-0.9.4.tar.gz
and	emu10k1-v0.20a.tar.bz2

I've experienced frustration similar to yours whereby once it would work and 
some time later it would be broken with no clue as to what may have happened 
to change it.  The emu-tools include a utility named emu-dspmgr plus some 
plug-ins, I remember reading about surround sound plug-ins, perhaps this is 
what you need.

Good luck,
