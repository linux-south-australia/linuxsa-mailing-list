I haven't used it in anger, but Heroine Virual who used to do
Broadcast2000 have a new mousetrap called Cinelerra. I did d/l it a
while ago, and it did run, but I can't really offer much more info than
that.

I am actually looking for something that easily does multimedia
slideshows like PTE (Pictures To Exe), but without the Exe. There's a
bunch of these happening for Windows, but not anything for Linux that
I've seen yet. One very good package is called SSMM (Slide Show Movie
Maker), which can output a DiVx video from a bunch of still images, with
full transition control and even image zooming/panning. High res
(1024x768 or better) is not a problem as long as you have a fast CPU.
Interestingly, rendering is pretty fast for these DiVx movies, and I
reckon a P4 at around 3.4ghz will render my max quality movies at or
better than real time.
