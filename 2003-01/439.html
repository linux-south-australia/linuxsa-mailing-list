Try running vmstat and iostat. The man pages are not too bad, but if you
run (for example) vmstat and iostat for 2 or 3 minutes updating every
5-10 seconds and then start the copy up and do the same, you can
probably compare the two (even graphing it using something like a
spreadsheet application). This will give you a better idea of where the
bottleneck is (whether it's I/O, CPU, RAM, network, etc).
