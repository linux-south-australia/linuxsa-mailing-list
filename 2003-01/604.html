> EVERYTHING, LINUXSA and the normal INBOX are all working fine.

> rules should be working.

Try replacing this:

 > PATH=/usr/bin;/usr/local/bin

with this:

 > PATH=/usr/bin:/usr/local/bin

It probably can't find cat and gzip because they aren't in a directory
called "/usr/bin;/usr/local/bin"
