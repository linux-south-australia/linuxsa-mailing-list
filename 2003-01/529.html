Re: LCD Screen

On Mon, Jan 13, 2003 at 04:49:09PM +1030, Martin Lewis wrote:

> A guy at the 2002 Installfest was running Viewmaster LCD 15" monitors ($600 ea)
> which looked very nice on RedHat.  The imacs LCD screens seem to look OK when
> the resolution in changed? Is there a recommended minimum res. for each LCD
> monitor?

LCD monitors are designed to work at a specific resolution. Running
them at a lower resolution causes them to employ a work-around, namely
stretching the pixels to make them fit the width of the screen.

The other common work-around is to shrink the screen size, which is
why you sometimes see a black border around the outside of the
picture.
