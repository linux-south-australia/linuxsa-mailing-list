I'd agree with Kim and DSL. I've just setup a test box at work for 
collaborate documentation using phpwiki (it's on sourceforge).

wiki's are amazingly simple to use and phpwiki allows you to export to 
XML for portability.

Our plan with ours is to use it to keep our two teams updated on what's 
going on, bash out some procedures that everyone can agree on, and 
track changes.

The version control aspects of a wiki are ideal for keeping track of 
what changes and by whom.
