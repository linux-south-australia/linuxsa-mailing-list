Lindows Sponsoring Kde-look.org

Here is there announcement...

JL

> As was posted here 
> <https://forum.lindows.com/forum/showflat.php?Cat=&Board=news&Number=58907&page=0&view=collapsed&sb=5&o=0&fpart=>, 
> the KDE-look.org <http://kde-look.org>; website was struggling to 
> survive. After reading the posts from our members, and being big fans 
> of both KDE and "eye candy" (KDE-Look.org's specialty), Lindows.com 
> was happy to step in and provide help.
>
> We're happy to announce that we are now the official sponsor of 
> KDE-look.org and will be assisting them in not only keeping their site 
> in business, but also helping them move to faster, more useable 
> servers. We felt this site has been a critical resource for KDE users 
> and developers, and it would have been a big loss to everyone if it 
> would have been forced to shut down.
>
> Thanks to our Members for bringing this matter to our attention. We're 
> happy to have been able to have helped.
