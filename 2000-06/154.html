Re: Hotmail and Netscape weirdness

CLP wrote:
> Though what is notable, the swap useage shoots through the roof past a certain
> point, and after that almost nothing helps. With 64mb RAM and a further 64mb of
> swap, I fail to see why this amount shouldn't be enough for a box which serves
> only two X sessions at the most (the other is a labtam).

OK, so use "ulimit" so that when Netscape goes odd it dies
rather than using so much memory and swap that it threatens
your machine's stability.

eg:  /usr/local/bin/netscape

  #!/bin/sh -
  ulimit -c0 -v40000
  exec /usr/bin/netscape $*
