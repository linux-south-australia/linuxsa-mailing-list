InstallFest venue offer - pictures

There has been some discussion of the InstallFest venue offer made
by Internode and Agile recently.  Yesterday I noted that lots of the
criticisms it had attracted from some people were being made in the
complete absence of anyone having actually seen it.

To address this, Richard Russell and David Newall visited this afternoon
and I gave them a bit of a look-see.  I'm sure they'll be commenting on
their impressions in due course;  I wont' preempt them here.

Anyway, for the benefit of those who weren't there, I've put a series
of pictures up at http://slash.dotat.org/~newton/31york

Apologies for the lighting;  it was a dull day and it didn't occur
to me to turn on the floodlights, ok? :-)
