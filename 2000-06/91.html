Re: PHP/Postgres/apache probs

Thanks everyone who offered help via email.  The specific problem turned
out to be that php3 doesn't come with postgres support out of the box,
that is the /usr/lib/apache/pgsql.so isn't built by default and isn't
enabled in /etc/httpd/php3.ini

Of course rebuilding from source is the obvious solution - except when
you get this from egcs compiling mod_php3 :-(

cc: Internal compiler error: program cc1 got fatal signal 11

Must upgrade egcs.  Sigh.  Then Geoffrey replied and suggested actually
installing the appropriate RPM!  Of course it worked!
