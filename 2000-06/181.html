Lindsay,

If I am not mistaken with the Sparc 5's, you really can not buy an ethernet
card at all. You need to buy a SCSI card which has the ethernet interface on
it.

We had this problem with Sparc 2's upto and including Ultra 1's. And at
$2000 a throw (well at the time anyway), it was sometimes not justified.
