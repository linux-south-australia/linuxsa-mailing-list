www.apache.org
or various mirror sites. (mirror.aarnet.edu.au should have one)

> Do I have to have a box dedicated to being a server?

You don't /have/ to, but it's probably a good idea if your intranet is
going to get a decent number of hits.

> If I do how fast does it have to be, will a 486DX2-66 be fast enough?

Depends  on the size of your network. ie how many people will be using
the  intranet,  how  often, and what size documents are you shoving up
there... RAM is equally important...
I  could  be  wrong,  but I'd think for a medium to large company with
high  intranet usage, you'd want at least a pentium class machine with
say 64Mb of RAM and a decent swap space... (I'm sure someone else here
will enlighten me if I'm off on the wrong tangent...) ;)
