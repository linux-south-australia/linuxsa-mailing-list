Tux on the telly

On a lighter note to today's serious messages, late last night just
before drifting off to slumberland, the only station without strange
noises or infomercials was airing the US Today Show.... a product of
MSN.   

In their crowd shots, there was a banner on one of the railings with a
large scrawled "Linux Rules" on it. In the crowd, a great big fuzzy
TUX, waving and bobbing with the crowd.   

It's nice to see some people can be such dedicated fans of their
favourite OS. :-)   

I'll have to play spot Tux more often.  You never know where he's
going to turn up these days.
