FOUND: Promising-looking accounting app

Free accounting software is a bit thin on the ground.

http://www.linux-kontor.de/en/ is an application that seems to be
approaching things in a rather more businesslike way than gnucash, qhacc
and many others. A real database and multiuser are two big indications of
this, but there's more.

Features: SQL (currently Adabas D, Postgresql, Oracle), Java, CSV import,
user-defined queries without changing the code, and (allegedly, haven't
worked it out yet) report-printing capability. Reports are the weak point
with all the free accounting packages I have seen. I'm not suprised, I've
worked on several myself and didn't enjoy it at all.
