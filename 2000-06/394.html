Re: Ghostscript and PDF output

On Tue, Jun 13, 2000 at 10:22:57PM +0930, Adam Hawes wrote:

 > I use:
 > 'gs -sDEVICE=pdfwrite -sOutputFile=body.pdf title.ps'
 > Is there any simple way to stop it asking for the enter key???

I had a similar issue on a printcap output-filter which converts postscript
into output suitable for my Deskjet670.  Try this:

gs -sDEVICE=pdfwrite -sOutputFile=body.pdf -q -dNOPAUSE - < title.ps
