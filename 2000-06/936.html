Re: Tracing a client

Tristan Ball wrote:

> I need to find out what process is making NIS+ requests on a solaris
> client. I can see the requests going out via a packet sniffer, but I
> can't find any way to see what process is sending them.

netstat -p should tell you which processes own which sockets, 
which will probably narrow your search down. You should be able
to match the sockets from netstat to the sockets on your sniffed
packets.
