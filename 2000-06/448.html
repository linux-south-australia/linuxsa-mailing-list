Re: Treeware magazines: what do people recommend for linux interests?

Ian Hogben wrote:
> 
> I'm interested in treeware magazines that have a useful focus on linux. Does
> anyone have any suggestions for a good paper mag that I can get with useful
> linux content? I guess I am looking at strong articles on linux aimed at the
> geek level (rather than "how to install linux and make it look like your
> mac/win98 box"). I'm also interested in magazines that regularly ship out
> the latest distros and applications on CD.


Linux Journal (essential)
Linux Magazine (quite good to read)
Linux Format (UK - PC Format for Linux type thing)

Sys Admin (Unix admin stuff. Good for a linux admin, as many articles
are specific to Linux).

They are all around $12-$13 in newsagencies.
