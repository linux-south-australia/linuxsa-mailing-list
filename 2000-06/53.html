RE: Package management in free OSs

> Victor Wodecki (victor.wodecki@baesystems.com, on this list but unable to
> post at the moment) told me of an effort to create a new packaging system.

We have more than enough packaging systems already.  Any new system will fight
ennui and stands a good chance of failing, not on it's merits, but on the merits
of the incumbents.  RPM is fine, not perfect but fine.  The BSD ports is fine,
also not perfect but fine.  They are both well established and effort would be
better served fixing up their perceived deficiencies rather than confusing the
world by trying to convince everybody to adopt a new system.
