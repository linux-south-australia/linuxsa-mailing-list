RE: Notebook booting

There is an option to preload modules in the initrd file.

eg to preload a fastrack ide ultra 66 controller fro 2.2.5-15 kernel

mkinitrd -v -f --preload ultra66 /boot/initrd-2.2.5-15.img 2.2.5-15

use 
	man mkinitrd 

to look at the options

alternatively you may have some success with the pre-install option in
conf.modules.

eg 

pre-install pcmcia_core /etc/rc.d/init.d/pcmcia start


man modprobe


You may also fine other useful info in the HOWTO's. 

EG /usr/doc/HOWTO/PCMCIA-HOWTO if you installed HOWTOS during redhat install
