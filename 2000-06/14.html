When does it give you this message??
My P200MMX with an old 2Gb IDE hdd does this beacuse the hard drive doesnt
support UDMA33 and the kernel is trying to use it.
As soon as the timout has occoured, the kernel then disables UDMA for that
hard drive and everything works normaly.
