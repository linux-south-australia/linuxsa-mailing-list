KDE (not for gurus,just for those interested)

Hi all,

For those ( like myself :o( that are using KDE) and having problems with
loading kfm, form time to time!
By removing the /home/user/.kde/share/config/kfmrc you can reset your
KDE desktop.
There is no need to reboot, just logout and relog. There will be no
changes to your desktop, but a new kfmrc file will created, just say OK
to this popup window.  The desktop icons will be available once again,
kfm can be loaded and also the menu and taskbar buttons will be
functional once again!
Thank you and best wishes,
