This sort of problem occurs because you need a driver for the card at boot
time, but the driver is not in the initrd that is created for LILO, or
there is no initrd. 

You will need to do one of two things:

 1. Build a kernel with the DPT driver included in the kernel
 2. Construct an initrd that has the driver in it and a conf.modules that
    refers to the driver.
