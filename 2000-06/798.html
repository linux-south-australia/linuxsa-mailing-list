You could try adding x3 to your modem init string, that tells most
modems
not to wait for the dial tone if they can't detect it properly. You
might
also want to add couple of commas before the phone number, so the modem
picks up the line, waits for a couple of seconds, then dials regardless.
That's normally enough time for most phone lines to get themselves 
together.
