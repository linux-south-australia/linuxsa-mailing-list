How to get your message through ...

Hi,

HylaFAX is a wonderful tool.

Let us say a lot of Linux aficionados (LAs) wanted to let their government
know how unhappy they are at the government wasting so much money on
Microsoft products instead of using Linux.

These LAs could produce a letter, and then scan their signature in, and
build a job that will append each signature to a copy of the letter with
the appropriate details changed, and then stick it through HylaFAX as one
fax to send to the appropriate office of government, and then each and
every member of parliament in both Liberal, Labour and the window dressing
party.

Imagine a thousand letters going to each, all for the price of a 22c phone
call for each MP!

Modern communications. 

Of course we could bombard their inboxen as well, but the written word is
so much more effective.
