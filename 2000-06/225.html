You can get GPL CDs from www.lsl.com.au in Melbourne for 
about $7 plus postage (about $4). These guys are pretty quick
to provide the latest GPL releases of quite a few distros.

Also, Mandrake does not include all of the compatibility packages
that you get in Redhat. This makes it a bit tricky to get stuff like 
Oracle 8.0.5 running on your system, but you can install the 
missing compat stuff from a Redhat distro.

Finally, my crappy Cyrix MII system runs a bit faster with Mandrake 
due to the Pentium optimised binaries that come with it.
