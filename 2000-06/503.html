T-Shirt Competition

The organisers of the T-Shirt competition reserve the right to select the
winning design by popular aclaim, or by other methods as they see fit.
Voting starts on Monday morning, 19 June 2000, and concludes the next day,
Tuesday, at 5:00pm.  Design entries may be submitted right until close of
voting (although obviously they might get reduced votes.)

The winning entry will be announced at the LinuxSA meeting on Tuesday,
20 June 2000, and by email to linuxsa@linuxsa.org.au.

Please submit your votes by email to tuxshirt@rebel.net.au.  Votes may take
any form you wish, for example, "I like xxxxx, can live with yyyyy, and hate
zzzzz."
