It depends what you mean by polished - a GUI installer? Well then yeah,
they win.

We still have a text base installer (for now), and we've had some issues
with "some" hardware support - we're having some trouble with Dell at
the moment, but that's a political thing, so we're having problems
developing drivers for their stuff.

We have a defined product range: Workstation, Server, ClusterServer &
Enfusion

WE're not just a distro, but we're a software development company as
well.  

Workstation - it more for the developer - you name it and it's under the
bonnet! We have predefined installs - base, network workstation,
graphical workstation, deveopler's graphical workstation and then
there's the Noddy install - All in one! We don't apologise for it not
being aimed directly for the desktop user, other distro are doing a good
job - our primary focus is the Enterprise end so it's more targeted to
the developer or Network Administrator, but in so saying a normal user
can still use the environment without much trouble, you can use KDE or
GNOME.

Server - Our has a mixture of Open Source, our own stuff and licensed
products which you don't find in the other distros, we are the fastest
server install - 8 min, the all in one install (900Mb). We install fully
secure, all ports locked and tcp-wrappers active - what that means is
that if your not a proficient Linix/UNIX type you haven't left the gate
open for some to walk in and steal your milk and cookies! We support a
multitude of netowrk protocal, MARS(ipx), Netatalk (appletalk), MacGate
(appletalk router), SMB (SAMBA - that works first off!), Netbeui and
others right out of the box. All our encryption is 128 for SSH & SSL and
we also have RAS. There is alot more, but if you have specifics pass
them on.

ClusterServer - our proprierty code that hooks into our TL server 4
version. We can cluster 2 to 254 nodea on a network and them some. We
cluster Web, smtp, ftp and other ip servers. It is very different to RH
Pirahna and it's not the same as LVM.

Enfusion - parallel processing - very different to Beowolf. Just check
our website. 

We have an agreement with the Open Source community in that our
ClusterServer Technology source code will released in about 6 to 8 mth.

If you what to know more or something specific just email me. I'm also
in SA next week and I'll drop some stuff off to Netcraft - product and
info foe you guys to have a look at - I'd like to come down to the
InstallFest.

Are you guys looking for a sponsor, etc?

let me know.
