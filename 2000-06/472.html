NEWSFLASH: Installfest Web Page Goes Live!

That's right - LinuxSA's Instalfest2000 Web Page goes live and is ready for
public consumption!

Visit http://www.linuxsa.org.au/installfest/ to see what all the excitement is
about.  

What's new in the version 0.8 "Sleepless in Adelaide" release of the installfest
web page?

  o Registering online is now active - pre-register for needing help installing
or configuring Linux or the BSDs.
  o Registering as an installer/configurer - ie offering your services to help
others.
  o Date, time and place, including a hotlink to pictures showing you how to get
there on the day!
  o Requirements - things you need to do before coming along,
  o Sponsors - find out who's being generous and who's not!
  o T-Shirt information and pointers,
  o Support pointers,
  o People to contact for further information,
  o On-line password-protected administration of the web-site.

All comments on the web page are still overly welcomed, and can be forwarded to
michaeld@senet.com.au

Share and Enjoy!
