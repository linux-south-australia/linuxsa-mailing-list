Re: Treeware magazines: what do people recommend for linux interests?

I agree LJ is great. I get Linux Journal as a subscription direct from
the US of A. Admittedly, a 12 month sub can cause a dent in the wallet
short term; my last subs for 12 months was $US37 which converted to
$AUD57 at the time. But that's only $5 per issue delivered. Cheaper if
you you take the two year contract, and you get the month's current
cover date. I actually ended up getting the June 2000 issue on June 1,
2000, but mostly I get them around the middle of the month. I'm not
complaining at saving over $8 per issue.
