 > Does anyone know where you get these flash ram/rom modules
 > which have IDE interfaces in Adelaide?

We get them pre-installed in the embedded-systems platforms we 
buy from ICP (Industrial Computer Products) in Sydney.  I'm not 
sure who their supplier is, but you could always call them to find
out.

ICP are really great -- They sell the hardware you want pre-configured
to order, and they are capable of doing wierd configurations.  They
specialize in industrial-type systems, very sturdy, very small, very
light or very low-power.  At work we get complete PC systems with
CompactFlash disks and 3 PCI slots in enclosures about the size of a 
lunchbox.
