Re: caveat emptor

Quite right.  I may be dating myself here, but I do remember many old signs
in front of house gates saying "cave canem", which means "beware of the
dog".  Cave is the imperative form.  (Pronounced "karvay"}.  I also used to
remember many old books about English public schoolboys who used to yell
"cave" when a master was heaving into view.  (Billy Bunter and the like).
Until I learnt Latin, I thought it was a strange thing to do.  Nice to see
someone knows Latin.

Rick
