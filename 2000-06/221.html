Good News

An interesting article about about clustering small cheap computers to
create as fast, powerful system.

http://acme.ecn.purdue.edu/

This seems to me to be one way forward for the Open Source Movement, and
possibly something for LinuxSA. I am sure there are people around with
this knowledge. And there will be others wanting to learn. I look
forward to the day when this can happen. The reason -many
countries/people just cannot afford the high cost of late computing
technology and fast access to the internet. 

Maybe sometime some LinuxSA hardware hackers can set one up for others
to learn from. It would need to be done in such a way that the knowledge
is transferable. This is the sort of technology needed for many of
Austalia's neighbors to participate more fully in the internet.

It could be a business for someone. 

It would be good to see that cost was no longer the major factor in
gaining faster PCs and access to the internet.
