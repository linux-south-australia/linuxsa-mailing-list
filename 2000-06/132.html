Problem recording sound

Howdy All,

I have a weird problem trying to record sound on my Mandrake 7
Linux system. I use either "sox" or "rec" to try and record
sound from either the microphone, line input, or an audio CD
with my ESS1868 sound card.

Problem is, it records at 44191 Hz, and *NOT* at 44100 Hz as
it is meant to, even if I specify the sampling rate as 44100. 
This effectively stops bladeenc from being able to encode my 
recording into MP3 format, as it won't recognize input files
that were recorded at 44191Hz. Note, however, that the ESS1868 
quite happily plays audio CDs, MP3s, and basically functions 
perfectly in every other respect.

Any ideas?? This exact same problem happens with Mandrake 6.x,
and RedHat 6.0 on my system, so I'm starting to think that it 
is hardware but I don't know for sure. My system is a Cyrix 
MII-300 with 64Mb RAM, and an ESS1868 sound card.

Any help would be much appreciated!
