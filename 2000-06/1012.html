Re: GST & the ATO

Hi All,
    I received my "ATO Electronic Commerce Interface" for
"The New Tax System" yesterday. Amongst other things on the
wrap that comes with it , it says :

    "Apple Macintosh and Unix (TM) versions of the ECI
Client Software are in the final stages of development and
expected to be available in the near future. Please contact
ESD Technical Support to arrange for a copy of the version
you require to be sent to you once it is available."

    On further enquiry as to if this meant a version would
be available for Linux I was informed "yes" by the
consultant and promptly ordered one. So is this still
"anti-competitive"? I await to see how long it takes, but is
seems to me that the government _May_ have got it right this
time (well the Tax Office anyhow).
