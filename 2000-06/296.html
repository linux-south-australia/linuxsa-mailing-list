Re: What is the difference

At 05:00 PM 6/9/00 +0930, Robyn Manning wrote:
>What is the difference between insmod and modprobe?

insmod just tries to load the module you named. If there are any
dependencies that have not yet been loaded, insmod will fail.

Modprobe looks through the modules database
(/lib/modules/2.x.x/modules.dep) that was built when you booted, and
installs any dependent modules first before loading the module you asked for.

It is all documented somewhere in /usr/src/linux/Documentation.
