Re: splitting flat mail files for cyrus imap

On Wed, 21 Jun 2000, Jake Hawkes wrote:

> anyone now of a util that will split my /var/spool/mail/jake file (200
> meg of it) into the seperate files for cyrus imap?

Search the archive of the cyrus mailing list for the utilities that other
people use to do this. You don't write files directly, you use IMAP to
talk to Cyrus (and maybe too the flat files, via UW imapd.) The best
results seem to come from using the Perl or Python IMAP modules, people
with hundreds of thousands of mailboxes to move report success. Some of
them have posted the scripts they use.

There is a set of likely-looking utilities from UW (imapmove and imapcopy
etc) but don't be fooled, they only partly work and that's on a good day.
