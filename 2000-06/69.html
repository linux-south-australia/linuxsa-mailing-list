Or you could get out of it even cheaper, and do what I
did when i needed a crossover a quick crossover, get a normal
utp cable and cut it in half (or a few inches from one end would
be a wiser idea), then join the wires that you need to, solder up
(or just twist) and tape the wires up.. Crossover done. Incidentally,
the one i made like that i had in constant use for months without
fail.
