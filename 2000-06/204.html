InstallFest TShirt Designs

We will need to be closing entries for the design of the tshirt by Monday,
probably, so crank up your preferred graphics program and churn out the
designs. See www.rebel.net.au/~tuxshirt for current entries... Special
thanks to Phil Hutton, Damien Wilson and Dale Long for putting in the only
designs so far...

Remeber, all it has to do is be distinctive, have a Linux flavour, and
advertise the URL and date. It will be modified before printing, to have
sponsors on the back, and the correct date, etc on the front. It doesn't
need to be elaborate, and should be in few colours for cheap printing...
Personally, I like white text on a black t-shirt for maximum impact, but I'm
sure someone out there can do better. Put a catchy phrase on it, make it
look appealing and professional, not just in presentation, but also in the
message it is sending out. Try to make it appeal to people who don't yet
have Linux, but may be interested. Here ends the lesson... :)

Get those designs in by the end of the weekend!
