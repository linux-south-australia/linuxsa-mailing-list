second CPU still working I would have been in trouble - especially with
50 PCs samba-ring of it :-)

directories when upgrading netscape.  It seems the presence of old
configuration information can be a contributing factor to netscape
instability in my experience.  Of course you may like to save your
bookmarks and export your address book first :-)
