[ANNOUNCE] InstallFest2000 July 15th

Hi all,

We have a confirmed date for the InstallFest. July 15th -- mark it down now!

It will be at Agile Telecommunication's new exchange, at 31 York st,
starting at 10am, and closing at 5pm, with no new installs after 4pm.
Organisers will need to be there early, and leave late -- details TBD. Pizza
will probably be Buongiorno's, which is accessible without going outside.
There will be two phone lines, and aobut 10 powerpoints... 

Sponsors are: 
Internode (Internet Access)
Agile (Venue, phones)
Rebel.net/Tellurian (Tshirt underwriting)
TurboLinux (probably something nice -- confirmation to come)

Other stuff is still being organised, but news will come as it happens...
There is a (beta) webpage up at
www.linuxsa.org.au/meetings/installfest200-beta -- check it out and send
comments to michaeld@senet.com.au or the installfest mailing list.

Special thanks to people:
David Newall
Michael Davies
David Lloyd
Mark Newton & Leigh Hart
Everyone else who has been contributing -- keep it up, and we will have a
really great event on our hands.

Note that time is short, and we need to get things happening really fast
now, so please don't hesitate to contribute -- particularly tshirt designs,
which need to be finalised within a day or two...

Please discuss this on the installfest@linuxsa.org.au list...

Thanks

rr
