Hi.

The only proviso I would suggest, is that if your cables are going to be
used in a 'user' environment, it is better to buy one of the higher
quality crimpers. Why? Because the action of the strain-relief crimping on
the cheap (plastic) crimpers is not very effective. Out in userland that
means you might have to redo the job after someone ham-fists the
connector.
