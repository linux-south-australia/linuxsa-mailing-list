> I need to know the availability if a WYSIWYG editor,
> and graphic manipulation software for Linux/Unix/X11.

There are a few options for a WYSIWYG editor (I presume you mean a
word processor?  vi is WYSIWYG too, you know :).  The two major
native options are Applixware from Red Hat and WordPerfect from
Caldera.  There is also StarOffice, but that is still in beta last I
heard.  Non-native options are Word for Windows running under WABI
from Caldera and Word for MAC running under Executor from Ardi.

We'll have a demo of Applixware at the InstallFest that you can play
with.

There are some graphic-related programs available for Linux.  The
latest developer release of The GIMP is looking *really* good.  That's
the closest "Photoshop-like" app that I know of if that's what you're
looking for (apart from Photoshop for the Mac running under Executor
of course :).

See you there!
