Linux installed.

Dear All,
I wish to proudly announce that I have managed to install the RH4.1 Linux 
over the Easter weekend. Yey!

Pretty straightforward cept that I made a mistake during mounting the 
filesystems and couldn't go back to the previous screen and had to do a 
reinstall.

But the adduser program added users w/o prompting for the users details.

I made a few mistakes like mounting the floppy disk on /tmp/mnt and getting 
email fron cron telling me of errors in deleting files there.
Also, getting Xwindows to work was a bit tricky: my card was a S3 864 
"Made in Taiwan" card and even though I chose generic S3 864 in 
XConfigurator, the config file wasn't properly prepared. I had to use X 
-probeonly to get the card info and edit the config file by hand.
When I typed startx, I got a grey screen with a rather big 'x' and then 
after ~30 secs, just when I was beginning to feel disappointed, there was 
a flurry of disk activity and 'The Next Level' came up on the screen. Yey!!

I just want to share my excitement (and mistakes) with the members in the 
list.

With best wishes,
