Here's my 2c worth,

Could you boot off each kernel in turn, go the the /proc filesystem and get
a copy of the following files:

	/proc/devices
	/proc/dma
	/proc/interrupts
	/proc/ioports

Then compare the details between the two versions.  The last one in
particular _may_ reveal IO address conflicts.  On my machine (a 386 with a
Slackware installation from about 1.2...) /proc/ioports shows that some of
the serial devices were autodetected in a range close the the numbers in
your original post.  

Let us know what you find.
