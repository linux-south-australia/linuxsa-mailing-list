Post-InstallFest stuff

Thank you to all who turned up to the LinuxSA InstallFest on Saturday;
it was a great success!  Approximately 40 people showed up, and many
installs were done.

The photos taken by Paul with his digital camera have been put up on
the web at http://www.netcraft.com.au/linuxsa/installfest/photos.html
(the analogue photos will take a little longer :), and a write-up of
the 'Fest is going to be done by Tique.  If you have any comments
about the day, please send them to tique@netcraft.com.au (and/or the
list of course!).

Some people have discovered extra disks/CDs in their bags after
arriving home, and some people have discovered missing disks/CDs in
their bags.  Please contact me if you fit into either of those
categories so I can sort everything out.

Thanks,
