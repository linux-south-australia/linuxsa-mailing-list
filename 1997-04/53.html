> But the last line (line 54) in my .xinitrc says:
> 
> 	exec fvwm

> before that, it worked OK.

Hi Ismail,

Yes, it was something you did in the middle.  The actual last line is
(mostly) irrelevant.  The error is "unexpected end of file" which
probably means you placed something in the middle that causes bash to
slurp lines until it finds something in particular.  Apparently you
didn't put in there what it was looking for, so it spat the dummy.

My guess is that you have an "if" without a "fi".
