Alan,
sounds suspiciously like your new BIOS is doing something a little different with the HDD parameter control.
If you are using LBA on a big drive then you may have to manually enter the drive parameters instead
of letting it do a auto detect.  If you did a manual config in the CMOS then do a auto detect.
Some BIOSes I have seen, seem to drop a cylinder from the setup which would change the addressing needed
for access at a low level.  This may be what is upsetting LILO.
Just a thought...Don.
