Re: Mouse problem... a key, perhaps?

> OK, now my mouse works.  But I had to swap ports.  COM1: doesn't recognise the mouse, and COM2: does.
> 
> Any help anyone?

Does COM1 work for anything... eg modem, another mouse?
If both win95 and linux detect and are happy with com1 then there probably
isn't anything wrong with it. It could be as simple as the cable being
pluged in the wrong way. Open up the case and take a look at the two com
cables that go between the ports and the motherboard. They should both
have pin1 (the side of the cable with the red striping) on the same side.
Either pointing to the front or back of the case. If not then pull out the
com1 cable and turn it around. Unfortunatly most motherboards allow you to
pug them in the wrong way. (Of course this assumes that it is pugged in at
all.)
