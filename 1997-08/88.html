> Debian users (if there are any out there!) do not need the `-ltermcap'
> since Debian GNU/Linux uses terminfo and not termcap.  Instead one can
> just use ncurses's termcap emulation.  It's as simple as linking with
> ncurses instead of libtermcap (i.e. replace the '-ltermcap' with
> '-lncurses' in makefiles, as applicable). Ncurses' termcap emulation
> routines translate terminfo entries to termcap entries on the fly, so
> you don't even need an /etc/termcap file.

You can change my instructions to:
  change LIBS to equal "-lncurses"
for Red Hat as well.  The original line was "-lcurses -ltermcap", and
I just took the immediately obvious path of getting it working :).
