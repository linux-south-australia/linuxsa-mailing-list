New local Red Hat mirror

Hi all,

NetCraft is now running a full Red Hat mirror (including all
architectures, source, old-releases, and contrib) at
ftp://ftp.netcraft.com.au/

The directory layout is the same as at ftp.redhat.com, and the SITE
EXEC functionality is also supported for finding files and querying
RPM packages before downloading them (see /pub/SITE-EXEC for details).
