Re: Help with Network card

Ismail,
the IO is not out of order, it's due to the PCI addresses.
add this to your /etc/conf.modules and I'd expect all to work as expected:
# ----- /etc/conf.modules ----
alias eth0 3c59x
options eth0 io=0xD400 irq=11

regards...Don.
