Re: Where does my sound go?

On Mon, 11 Aug 1997 08:39 +0930,  "Wood, Alan" wrote:

>I can play audio CD's, xboing gives out appropriate noises, but xgalaga   
>is a funny one. If I don't select sound (I think), then when my missiles   
>first strike, the game vanishes. If I select sound (I think) then the   
>game plays on, but no sound comes out. Where does it go? Anyone else come   
>across this problem? I have RH4.1 and SB16 card.
 
I did have a problem with xgalaga vanishing. The first time I included
sound support while recompiling my kernel, my "DSP_BUFFSIZE" was set
to the maximum (65536 I think) and xgalaga was still vanishing when
the little suckers were hit.  Setting DSP_BUFFSIZE=32768 did the trick
though.

My card is only a SBPro so there will be some differences to your
settings.  These settings have worked for me with RH 4.1 and 4.2

Note: The settings I have not included in the following section are
all "not set".

# Sound
CONFIG_SOUND=y
CONFIG_SB=y
CONFIG_AUDIO=y
CONFIG_MIDI=y
CONFIG_YM3812=y
SBC_BASE=220
SBC_IRQ=5
SBC_DMA=1
SB_DMA2=1
SB_MPU_BASE=0
SB_MPU_IRQ=-1
DSP_BUFFSIZE=32768
# CONFIG_LOWLEVEL_SOUND is not set
