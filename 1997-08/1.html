> The setup I've got at my place is a central unix machine, and about

> the output to one terminal copy itself to another terminal - ie I
> want to 'spy' on another terminal with my own.  I believe the 'tee'

Have a look at ttysnoop.  There's an RPM for it on the Red Hat Power
Tools CD if you happen to be running Red Hat Linux.  Here's the
description from the .rpm:
-----
The package allows you to snoop on login tty's through another tty-device or
pseudo-tty. The snoop-tty becomes a 'clone' of the original tty,
redirecting both input and output from/to it.
-----
