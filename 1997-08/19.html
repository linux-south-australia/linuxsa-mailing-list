Re: missing packages

> Hi all,
> I've been trying to update a few packages and frequently get an error
> saying that it can't find the libncurses.so.4 package.
> I have looked on the 'net but can't find a libncurses package anywhere.  
> Anyone help?

Whenever you can't find a package, go to ftp.redhat.com and type:
site exec locate whatever
Then go to a local mirror to grab the package.

ncurses is available at sunsite.anu.edu.au:
/pub/linux/distributions/redhat/contrib/i386/ncurses*-4.1-12.i386.rpm

> I also get an error that it can't find /bin/sh, in spite of the fact it's
> there.  It's a symbolic link to bash but it's there.  I even replaced it
> with a copy of bash renamed to sh..still no good.  Any help?

The package probably shouldn't have a dependancy on "/bin/sh", as that
isn't "provided" by any Red Hat package.  Use the "--nodeps" switch to
get RPM to install it anyway.
