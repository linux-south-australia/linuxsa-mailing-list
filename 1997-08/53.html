Could people please not quote the LinuxSA mailing list signature back
to the list?

Red Hat have a good guide to mailing list "netiquette"; here's the
part of it which I'd like people on the LinuxSA list to follow:

1. When posting to a list with a reply to a previous message, trim ALL
   irrelevant or unnecessary text from previous messages. Trim ALL
   other signatures of previous posters.  Trim all signatures of
   the list itself.

2. When replying to a post, make sure any attributions in your message
   are correct. People get upset when you say that they said something
   that they never said.

3. If the entire content of your post is "me too" or "thanks", DO NOT
   POST IT. Send it to the original author if you like, but do not
   annoy the list members with it.
