I don't know about Slackware or Debian but for future reference the
requirements of the Red Hat CD are that you place a copy of
ftp.redhat.com:/pub/redhat/redhat-4.2/i386 in the root directory of
a rock-ridge format CD and that you preserve the executable bit on the
executable files.
