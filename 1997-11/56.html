I must confess that I often use Microsoft products.. Particularly,
Excell, Word and Access. They seem to be amongst the the best , or

operating systems.. Funny coincidence that....   :-)

LINUX comes with a large and useful collection of tools. These are
clearly very good at what they do ... I know that I I would have to

functionality to Excell, Word or Access become available for LINUX. 

For me, it is a matter of "horses for courses". I am quite prepared 
to consider various tools to solve the particular problems that I 
have on hand. For me, the problem comes first. I follow it where ever 
it takes me.. I feel that anything else is just being inflexible.
