IE4 for "Unix"

IE4 has been released for "Unix". When MS says unix it means Solaris only,
ie no Linux and there probably never will be.


Here are a few more specs:

Internet Explorer 4.0 Preview 1 for UNIX 

System: A Sparcstation 2 or greater 

Operating System: Solaris 2.5.1

Memory Requirements: 16 MB RAM (32 MB recommended)

Disk Space (after setup completed): 30 MB of available hard-disk space.
Setup requires 60 MB of hard-disk space to perform the installation.
