Kindly note that if you receive an e-mail titled, “JOIN THE CREW”, DO
NOT open it! It will erase EVERYTHING on yow hard drive.
Send this letter out to as many people at yow can. This is a new virus
and not many people know about it as yet. This information was received

Also, If anyone receives mail entitled, “ PENPAL GREETINGS! ”, please
delete it WITHOUT reading it. This is a dangerous virus propagating
across the Internet through e-mail. It appears to be a friendly letter,
but by the time you read it, its too late. It uses the Trojan Horse
Virus and will have already infected the boot sector of your hard drive,
destroying all of the data present. It is a self-replicating virus, and
once the message is read it will AUTOMATICALLY forward itself to anyone
whose address is in your mailbox.
Hope this might prevent someone gets into major problems.
