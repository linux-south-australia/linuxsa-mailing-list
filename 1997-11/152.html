Generally, I don't think that there are many problems connecting F/BSD to
Linux. I've used an older ISP that runs FreeBSD and it worked perfectly on
my Linux box.

What I suggest you do is you turn on verbose logging, and perhaps open up
a session in minicom and login yourself, and then exit out without hanging
up and running pppd manually.

Also, you might like to try the "router type" pap-secrets configuration :-
