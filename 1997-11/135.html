CAE MRad is using Linux to develop and host a radar stimulator for
radar operator training.  RedHat Linux + Motif is being used for the
GUI (Scenario Controller), and our home-grown SHARC DSP cards (PCI and
ISA based) will sit in a Linux-based PC, which in some cases will be
the same PC as the GUI, and other cases will be "embedded".

We chose to use Linux as it allowed us to preserve our Unix-based
software development environment (otherwise SunOS/Solaris) on PC
hardware.
