For anyone wondering what happened to my problems writing a boot floppy 
and getting --crc-- errors, I solved the problem. As usual, it was 
something more simple than all of the complicated possibilities clogging 
my grey matter... The data cable had worked loose on the back of the 
floppy drive!

This is really frustrating! Does anyone have a solution to these weak and 
loose cable connectors? My best solution so far is the hot-melt glue gun. 
(no, I'm not kidding, these systems will have to work after they travel 
to Brisbane or Perth)

everything on one boot disk!
