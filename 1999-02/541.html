Re: GCC Crosscompiling?

Hi Darryl,

There is a FAQ at ftp://ftp.cygnus.com/pub/embedded/crossgcc/FAQ that tells
how to build a cross compiler for the targets that GCC understands.  It's
aimed at embedded compilers, but the info works as well for other targets.

I have successfully used this to build an egcs for h8300-hms-coff and
i386-go32 (djgpp) targets on an x86 Linux host.

Anyway, be prepared to find large quantities of free disk space before you
start. :)

Cheers,
Martin
