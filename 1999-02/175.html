Re: Sendmail DNS Question

Benjamin Close wrote:

 >         I have a dialup connection. Is it possible to get sendmail to
 > queue mail and completely obvoid DNS lookups when offline. Currently it
 > will perform a lookup, the lookup will fail as I'm offline so it is
 > defered. However I have to wait for the timeout before I can do anything.
 > (ie in pine).

If you use ip-up and ip-down scripts to frob ipfwadm access lists,
you can deny UDP/53 when your link is down.  Then name resolutions
will fail immediately with EPERM, meaning they won't have to time
out.

When the link comes back up, remove the UDP/53 block and execute
"sendmail -q" to flush the sendmail queue.
