Re: Grabbing X-Screens

Adam Hawes wrote:

 > How do I grab the entire X display and save it to a file. I have
 > tried XV but that will only take a window or rectangular area.

You're aware, of course, that that "rectangular area" can be the
entire X display?

There's also xwd (and it's companion xwud).  If you tell it to
dump the root window you end up with everything sitting on top of
the root window going with it.  xwud knows how to produce encapsulated
PostScript files, so this is good for screen dumps for manuals...
