I agree with posting just the URL.  Obviously the URL is not so urgent that
you have to read it there and then.  If you really want to know you can dial
back up again; otherwise you can look it up next time you check your mail or

which despite huge increases is still a scarce resource, URLs should be all
that is posted.  Well, maybe a tiny part of the URL; sort of a teaser.
