Shameless plug for the Linux counter

Hi Folks,

Most of you probably read Slasdot, and have already seen the article
published recently about the Linux Counter.  If you haven't, you can read it
at http://www.slashdot.org/articles/99/02/23/1049219.shtml if you like.

If you haven't registered already, now (or maybe a little later this week
due to /. effect) would be an excellent time.

If you have registered, or are just interested, you can access the counter
through http://counter.li.org/ - it has a few interesting statistics,
particularly the report at http://counter.li.org/reports/short.txt about
registered users per 1,000,000 population. Currently Australia is at number
12, just ahead of the US.

Cheers, and enjoy,

Martin
