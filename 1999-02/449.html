Check which version you do have first.
My system has both glibc & some redhat stuff making it libc compliant.
do:

rpm -q libc

This should tell you what version of libc (if any) you have installed.
Glibc is newer than libc so unless you must, I recommend leaving your
system the way it is - especially if WP runs.
