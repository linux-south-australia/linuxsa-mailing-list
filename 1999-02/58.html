Re: Grabbing X-Screens

> Hi all.
> 
> How do I grab the entire X display and save it to a file. I have
> tried XV but that will only take a window or rectangular area.
> I just want a simple command-line or X-based utility that will capture
> the current display and dump it to a file.
> 

Surprised nobodies mentioned this yet... I've always found just
xwd > screen.xwd
to be best. If you want the whole screen to be grabbed, make sure you
actually click on the background, not on any applications. Use the gimp or
ImageMajick's convert to change the .xwd format into something more
useful.
