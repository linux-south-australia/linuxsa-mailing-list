Re: network problems

William,

> I am having problems with telnet on my linux box. I am able to
> telnet out, although it seems to freeze for several minutes at a
> time quite regularly, and I have only been able to telnet to my
> box from another machine once in about 50 tries,all the others have
> timed out.

This could be a misconfigured DNS/resolver.  If hostname lookups
pause you should consider this as a possibility.  When you telnet
out the machine must translate the name you give it to an address;
you say this takes a several minutes.  When you telnet in the
machine translates the incoming address to a hostname; if this
takes more than 'a reasonable time', the login process times out;
and you say incoming telnet connections time out.  So that is my
guess.

Send me private email (davidn@rebel.net.au) for further assistance,
for example on how you should configure your resolver.  (It's not
interesting enough to discuss in open forum.)
