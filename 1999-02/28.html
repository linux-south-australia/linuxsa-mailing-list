Re: Grabbing X-Screens

> How do I grab the entire X display and save it to a file. I have
> tried XV but that will only take a window or rectangular area.
> I just want a simple command-line or X-based utility that will capture
> the current display and dump it to a file.

Electric Eyes (ee) which is usually part of enlightenment or gnome is
quite good at doing image captures.  The main benefit I see with it is that
it allows you to move through the window heirachy to catch *EXACTLY*
what you want.

Other than that I have found that ee not as easy to use as other image
viewers.
