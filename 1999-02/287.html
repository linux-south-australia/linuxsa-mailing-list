Re: Modem pool problems

I had a similar problem with a cyclades multiport serial board, analog
lines are a pain in the behind, even if the modem is working perfectly
they can still have ringouts.  From what I can understand, if the line
that the hunt number is currently switched to, doesn't answer the
linehunt won't shift to the next available line unless the disabled line
has a busy tone, ringouts won't allow the hunt to proceed to the next
available line
