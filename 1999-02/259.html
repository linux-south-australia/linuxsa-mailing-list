Re: Trouble with modules

On Fri, 12 Feb 1999, Fred wrote:

> is there a decent reference I can read to understand how modules work and
> how to configure them?

Documents/modules.txt in the kernel dir will help.

> What have I done wrong ?

By the look of it installing your new modules over the old ones. The
kernel will not delete existing modules when you run make module_install
so the scsi modules will still exist from the default redhat install.

> What can I do to make the modules work OK?
mv /lib/module/modules-(kernelversion-goes-here) /lib/modules/oldmods
make modules_install

> This problem affects most things that are compiled as modules as they now
> do not work properly, iso9660 fs is not supported properly and other things
> do not work.
