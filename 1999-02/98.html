Re: RH 5.2 startup with two ethernet adapters and modules

> Previously, when I used two different adapters, I did the following in
> /etc/conf.modules:
> 
> alias eth0 ne io=0x300
> alias eth1 smc io=0x340

This is from memory, but I seem to remember something along these
lines working for me before:

alias eth0 ne
alias eth1 ne
options eth0 io=0x300
options eth1 io=0x340

Or maybe something like this will work instead:

alias eth0 ne
alias eth1 ne
options ne io=0x300,0x340
?
