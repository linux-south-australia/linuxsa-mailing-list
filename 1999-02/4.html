Re: linux cohabitation with wind98

One thing that is worth nothing is that when you mount a fat16 or fat32
vfat partition, you need to specifically mention this to mount.
eg:
mount /dev/hda5 /mnt/lose95 -t vfat
Failling to do this usually results in you only able to access the short
form (foobar~9.jpe).
If you frequently mount the same partitions, you can add the vfat option
to your fstab.
