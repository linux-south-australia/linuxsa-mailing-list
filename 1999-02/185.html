Sorry I haven't been watching this thread. However if your getting random
modem disconnects it may be because of your modems escape sequence
handling specifically 3 pluses. 

Many modems when they receive the 3 pluses in data mode will go in to
command mode and hence cause a timeout/hangup. This is currently being
discussed on the linux-kernel mailing list. 

You can test to see if you have the problem by using:

ping -p 2b2b2b415448300d <hostname>

The only condition is it must not be a local loop back, ie must go
through your isp then back to you at least!. 

If this causes the hangup let me know & I'll work out a fix for your
modem.
