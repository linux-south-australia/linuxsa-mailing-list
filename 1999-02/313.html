>It is attempting to find a 'FQDN - Fully Qualified Domain name' for your
>host.  If your machine is connected to a network, check your DNS 
>configuration.  If it isn't connected to a network, make sure that the 
>hosts entry for your machine in /etc/hosts has a FQDN - basically a 
>domainname tacked to the end of your hostname.
>
>The time out that you are seeing is from the system trying to do a name
>service lookup for the domain.
>
>This generally doesn't appear if your machine has no NICs since 
>most distributions use something like this in /etc/hosts
>
>127.0.0.1	localhost	localhost.localdomain
>
>You can do something like this if you have a network card
>
>10.0.0.1	myhost		myhost.localdomain
>
