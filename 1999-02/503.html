Re: Gnome/enlightenment/esound problem

i had a problem with gnome 0.99.5 & .7 with esound, but not a path error.
edit your .xinitrc or .Xclients, whichever you load enlightenment from.

put a 'esd &' in before enilghtenment/gnome-session, and a 'killall esd' at
the end.

if you get a message saying 'broken pipe', you will need to get the latest
snapshot sources of esd & recompile.
