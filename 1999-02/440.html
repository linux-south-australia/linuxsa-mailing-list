CONFERENCE: CALU 1999 Attendee Mailing List Opens!

	http://www.linux.org.au

In anticipation of the finalization of speakers, the web page revamp,
and then the opening of registrations all happening in the next few
weeks, Linux Australia presents the calu-attendees mailing list:
subscribe by sending a message containing the word `subscribe' to
`majordomo@linux.org.au'.

We want our organisational efforts to be as open as feasible: the
people going to the conference define its character, and this list
allows us all to keep in touch with what's happening, coordinate
volunteers, get feedback and make sure that the entire event lives up
to the high expectations of Linux people.

Paul Russell,
Chair, Linux Australia Conference Committee.
