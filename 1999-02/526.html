auslinx confirms...

Mark,

On the modem again, Auslinx has confirmed (on the phone
just now) that the redback 33.6k modem has only about
600 bytes memory, and that the RX light shines just after the
data comes out of the RX FEC process. I.e. not when the
data is collected by IRQ handler etc.

This makes it essentially dead certain that the telstra
gateway is to blame.

Therefore all those people who have had similar symptoms
over a telstra line, or a permanent modem line to
an ISP which uses the same sort of router that
Telstra uses, could be justified in believing that
the cause is _not_ their modem or the linux drivers.

"Now we can _all_ get some sleep..." as
Jack Thomson said in the Clayton's ad.

Cheers,
Alan Kennington.
