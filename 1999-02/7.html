For those who are interested, all these questions are answered in

	/usr/doc/HOWTO/mini/BogoMips

Or for those who don't have the file in that location in the BogoMips
mini-HOWTO.

It even gives a calculation to use to check how your processor rates
against the 'established standard'.

       Intel Pentium II       clock * (1.00)                     5.6
       AMD K5/K6              clock * (2.00  plusminus 0.010)   11.1

So in the case above, the K6 is about right, but the Pentium II is 
running a bit hot.
