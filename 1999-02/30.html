While I am not an expert I have used a  discovery modem and  found out
to my shame that the problems I was having with these devices was
related to the swtich "detect Dial tone". It appears that Australian
dial tone is different to the overseas variety to cause problems. So
in setting up the modem try turning off the "detect dial tone" . It
may work. I can not tell you the code to send but it should be in the
modem hand book.
