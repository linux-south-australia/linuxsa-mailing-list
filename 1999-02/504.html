* IBM TO PRELOAD LINUX ON SELECTED SERVERS
Citing demand from Fortune 500 companies, IBM will make the Linux OS 
available on its PowerPC-based RS/6000 and Intel-based Netfinity 
servers. The company will announce details at the LinuxWorld conference 
that starts on March 1, 1999. In addition, the company is adding Linux 
support to its services business and is porting its software to the OS.
