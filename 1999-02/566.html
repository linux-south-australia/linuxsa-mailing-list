Re: Character buffering in C

Benjamin,

To get full control over the screen, you have to put
the user's terminal into non-canonical mode.

Type "man termios" to get the manual for this.

Then you have to turn off automatic echoing, and do your own
echoing. The non-canonical mode is called cbreak mode, I think.
This goes way back into unix history.
You may or may not find the function term_config in my
file

	http://www.topology.org/src/aksl/termdefs.c

enlightening for this.
