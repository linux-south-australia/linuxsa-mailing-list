Re: changing cdrom

Tom wrote:
> 
> I have just swapped over my cdrom from slave on ide 1 to slave on ide 2.
> Don't ask why. Problem is I can't mount it. Under the /dev directory
> cdrom points to hdb, ie:
> 
> cdrom -> hdb
> 
> I want it to point to hdd.
> Since I don't know how these ->'s work, could someone please tell me the
> command and syntax to change this. Or even which man page it's on.
> 
man ln
the command you will need to use is (from within the /dev/ directory)
ln -sf hdd cdrom

ln is the link command.
-s is symbolic link
-f is force (because you already have a cdrom link)

> Could I mount it manually by:
> mount -t iso9660 /dev/hdd /mnt/cdrom
Yes.
