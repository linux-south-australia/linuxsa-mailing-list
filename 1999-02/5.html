Re: S3 Neon AGP & AMD cooling

On the same subject, maybe you or someone else here could help with
'oversize' CPU and videocard cooling fans?
Pentium 2 and Celeron processors are quite nicely overclockable, except
that they do tend to start heating up.
In summer the ambient air temperature in my room usually gets over 40
degrees due to assorted hardware.. At this temperature not only do the
CPUs start getting pretty hot (as reported by the motherboards onboard
temperature sensors), but so does my video card and semi-obsolete
seperate 3d card.
Supposedly there are various 3rd party pentium 2 & celeron cooling fans
available, but I haven't seen any in SA. Likewise, there are supposed to
be dedicated fans for PCI-slot cards.
I'm a dedicated fan for linux, but it doesn't keep anything cool ;)
