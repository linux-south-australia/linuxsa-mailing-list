Re: Turning off unnecessary services

> For every service that I do not wish to start at boot time, I'd change
> the 'S' in front of the filename to an 's' like so:
> 
> mv S80sendmail s80sendmail
> mv S60nfs s60nfs

With recentish versions of Red Hat Linux, there is a program called
'chkconfig' which lets you do things like:

% chkconfig --list gpm
gpm 0:off 1:off 2:off 3:on 4:off 5:on 6:off
% chkconfig gpm off
% chkconfig --list gpm
gpm 0:off 1:off 2:off 3:off 4:off 5:off 6:off
% chkconfig gpm on

Same as shuffling symlinks, but it can do all 6 at once & get them
right.
