Re: Video Cards

On Tue, 16 Feb 1999, Glen Vallance wrote:

> My X server will only run in 800x600 at a really nasty color depth.
> What's the name of the program that can probe my card for it's
> clock-chip etc... better than the built-in thing under RedHat 5.2?
> [It's a really crappy 2MB video card, but it can cope with 1024x768
> under windows].

There is a few. 

Xconfigurator
XF86Setup

However I recommend after running them that you edit /etc/X11/XF86Config
and manually adjust the horizontal & vertical range for your monitor. You
can get the setting from your monitor manual. This will allow xvidtune to
get your desktop perrrfect and at a nice refresh rate.

Also under the display section add:

DefaultColorDepth 16

So X will always start in 16bit colour (24 is not possible at 1024x768
with 2MB)
