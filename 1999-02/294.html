Re: Modem pool problems

I assuming Telstra is providing the line hunt facility for you...

Telstra can configure line hunt groups to use different rotation strategies..

These strategies vary in that the line to receive the next call is 
1) always the lowest possible line in the group not in use
2) the most idle. ie the line that has not had a call the longest

The method you want is number two.  This way if you have a screwy modem in your case
only 1 in 6 calls will fail as successive calls to the hunt number always move on to
the next line...

Your Telstra representative should be able to have your hunt group configured in this manner.
