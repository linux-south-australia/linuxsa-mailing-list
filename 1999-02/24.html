I, too, have an Asus mainboard. I have found a kernel module called
lm_sensors to be most helpful. The module compiles and plugs 
straight in to the running kernel with a couple of insmod commands.
I was running kernel 2.0.36 when I tried it, but lost the archive
on a reinstall. I haven't yet tried it with 2.2.* kernels, but it
should plug into these kernels too.

The web page for lm_sensors is:
	http://www.netroedge.com/~lm78

It works fine on my Asus board, and there are a tonne of links on his
page for software to monitor the health of your machine.
