Re: New User: help needed SB AWE32 Setup under Redhat5.2

Matthew Mills wrote:

> Hi, at last I have been able to install RedHat Linux onto my computer
> thanks mainly due to the CD ROM I bought from Damien from this list (thanks
> Damien) I had an error free installation that took 17 minutes from the 8x
> CDROM connected to my ISA AWE32 Value IDE Sound Card.  Now that is out of
> the way I am wanting to get my Sound Card to work under X but have run into
> a problem.  I ran sndconfig and selected SBAWE 32/64 (as my card is not
> PNP) and it set up the SB16 side of things but then had an error "Bad or
> missing 16bit DMA channel"  then "AWE32 Failed"  (or very similar).  Whats
> the problem?  Is it because my SBAWE32 is only a value AWE32 with only
> 512kb of Memory?

You can force your settings for the soundcard by putting the following lines
in /etc/conf.modules
Simply adjust the  settings to suit your soundcard - you can use windows to
determine them


##################
# Setup the sound
###################

# define our soundcard
alias sound sb
options sb io=0x220 irq=5 dma=1 dma16=5          <--- line that (might) need
adjustment

# setup midi
alias synth0 midi

alias midi opl3
options opl3 io=0x388



>
> Also do I always have to log back in as root to shutdown?  (hey everyone
> I'm a newbie!!)

for people you meet. One person logs in and runs shutdown while you are
