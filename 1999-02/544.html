> Does anybody no why I get a viewable screen (but only a fraction of the 
> whole desktop) when I change virtual to 320x200?

The virtual resolution is like a piece of cardboard cut to the size you
specify. It indicates what size you want the desktop to be. Ie the 320x240
you specified indicates the maximum size of the desktop. Specifying a mode
of 1024x768 tells you that you want your virtual desktop to be used at
that screen rez. Ie set the monitor to 1024x768 then put the peice of
cardboard 320x240 across this rez.
