Re: cgi

Bruce Moffatt wrote:

 > Does anyone know if senet host a form-mail cgi script in their site-wide
 > cgi-bin, or if not an ISP in SA that does????

Do you need one?  You can do a form like this:

<form method=post
      action="user@host.domain?subject=Insert subject here">mailto:user@host.domain?subject=Insert subject here"
      enctype="text/plain">
 Type your name: <input size=30 name="name"><p>
 Gimme some comments:
 <textarea name="comments" cols=80 rows=5>Enter your comments here</textarea>
</form>

If you want the values given in a form you don't need a CGI script to
do it (and if you want them tabulated or further processed you can use
procmail to trigger "something else" based on the Subject: line).
