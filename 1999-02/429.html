Re: extended chars

On Sat, 17 Feb 1996, Robert Mibus wrote:

> how do i put an extended char in under linux.
> 
> under '95, i would do <alt><kp0><kp2><kp2><kp2> and get a Þ character.
> how do i do this under linux. (ps. the Þ was pasted in from another
> email i wrote under windoze :Þ )

First you must realise you are rapping around a 255 boundry.
Anything you enter in 2555 23433 is possible to repeat with a number below
256.

You can do: 

echo -e \\222 

But remember the default linux console can't display all of them like
dos/win can.

If you work out any other methods let me know.
