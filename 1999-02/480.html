Re: patching kernels(?)

On Mon, 22 Feb 1999, Paul Schulz wrote:

> Also, parallel ports seem to be disabled by default in the 
> 2.2.0 and 2.2.1 kernel configurations.

Oops almost missed this.
They are as many people don't use them.

Options required in 2.2.x for parallel support:

General Setup-> Parallel Support. 
                PC Style Hardware.

Character Devices-> Parallel printer support   (if you want the printer)

All of the above work well as modules.

***
Please note as of 2.2.x lpt1 is now lp0 so you may have to change the
configuration in /etc/printcap
