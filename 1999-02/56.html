Re: mpeg video

> I am trying to find a program capable of playing an MPEG-1 live
> video stream coming of a web page (Internet site). mpeg_play
> seems to be the thing to use but I can't see how to get it to
> read from anything other than a file.

try mpegtv.

http://www.mpegtv.com

It is freely available for Linux for non-commercial use. It has a 
nice graphical interface in standalone mode, and plugs into Netscape
to play streaming mpeg in the netscape window as a plugin.

Comes with easy to follow docs on how to install it as a plugin too.
