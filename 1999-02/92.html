RH 5.2 startup with two ethernet adapters and modules

Hi,

I have a machine that runs RH 5.2.  I currently have 2 ethernet adapters in
it.

Previously, when I used two different adapters, I did the following in
/etc/conf.modules:

alias eth0 ne io=0x300
alias eth1 smc io=0x340

However, now, the two cards are the same: ne2000 clones.

The following no longer works:

alias eth0 ne io=0x300
alias eth1 ne io=0x340

as the ne module cannot be reloaded.  If it is to control multiple devices,
then one needs to do something like:

insmod ne io=0x300,0x340

At the moment, I have resorted to gross hackery to get it going: I added
the following two lines to /etc/sysconfig/network-scripts/ifcfg-eth0:

   insmod 8390
   insmod ne io=0x300,0x340

However, I am looking for a cleaner setup than that. 

Any suggestions.
