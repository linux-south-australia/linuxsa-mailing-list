On some distributions this is trapped. If you find the three fingered
salute just locks up your machine, try doing ALT-CTRL-F1 to get to a
text based display first, and then ALT-CTRL-DEL. You may also have to
redefine this in /etc/inittab to be shutdown -h now instead of shutdown
-r now (reboot).
