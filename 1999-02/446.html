Re: E-mail address

On Fri, 19 Feb 1999, Wong Jen Choong, Jimmy wrote:

> Guys & gals,
> 
> Just a simple question.
> 
> How can I set in the aliases file that when an e-mail address is
> undeliverable, the email will be directed to me?
> Thanks,
> Jim

Add or edit the following lines (where root is who you want the mail sent 
to):

MAILER-DAEMON:  postmaster
postmaster:     root   

Remember to run newaliases after changing the file.
