Yesss!!! How to play midi files with playmidi on AWE64

Hi Guys,

A few weeks ago there was a question on this list about playing midi
files with playmidi. Think it was from Gaelyne Gasson. I have tried to
get playmidi and also the wavetable synthesis to work on my AWE64 a few
times before but was not successful.
And yesterday evening, (even though I'm up to my nostrils with
coursework to grade), I decided to have a go at it and managed to
get both to work. The sound is simply superb.

Here's how I did it (mistakes and errors not shown :). It's not
something spectacular but....

stargate{root}:32> /sbin/lsmod
Module         Pages    Used by
3c59x              4            1 (autoclean)
sb                 6            0
uart401            2    [sb]    0
sound             15    [sb uart401]    0
soundlow           1    [sound] 0
soundcore          1    [sb sound]      5

stargate{root}:38> /sbin/insmod /lib/modules/2.0.36/misc/awe_wave.o

stargate{root}:40> sfxload /etc/midi/GU11-ROM.SF2

stargate{mael}:8> playmidi -a -v Lambada.mid
