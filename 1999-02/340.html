Re: fvwm task bar problem

> On the subject of the fvwm task bar,
> is there some really easy way to get either:
> 1.	the X applications to recognize it's there and
> 	avoid using that area, or
> 2.	the task bar to disappear when I'm not using it?

My solution is to make the task bar (and pager, etc) not stay-on-top,
so it disappears when you put another window on top of it.  Presuming
that you're using Red Hat's AnotherLevel: in /etc/X11/AnotherLevel,
look for fvwm2rc.decors.m4 and change
FvwmAlias(StickyOnTop,`StaysOnTop,Sticky')
to
FvwmAlias(StickyOnTop,`Sticky')

And in fvwm2rc.modules.m4, change
Style "FvwmTaskBar"     StaysOnTop, WinCircSkip, NoTitle
to
Style "FvwmTaskBar"     WinCircSkip, NoTitle
