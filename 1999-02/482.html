Re: /etc/procmailrc ?

On Mon, 22 Feb 1999, Steven Ellison wrote:

> Hi all,
>    I have had a little play with procmail files and it looks like I could
> sink any mail with happy99.exe with a global recepie in /etc/procmailrc
> 
>    It looked easy enough but I can't seem to get it to work!!!
> 
> 
> eg 
> 
> :0
> * ^Content-Type: .*happy99.exe
> !/dev/null

I'm not sure if this will solve the problem but ! normally means remail.
So in the case above your saying remail the email to /dev/null. This will
fail so bounce back to the user.

Try the following which puts the message in to a file rather than
reposting it.

:0
* ^Content-Type: .*happy99.exe
/dev/null
