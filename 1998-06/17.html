Re: bad sectors, maybe?

Timothy,
    If you've manually entered the drive parameters in the CMOS/BIOS setup,
check that you haven't added a cylinder or track to many.   Win'95 not
complaining doesn't mean much... it seems to not notice problems on
floppies it itself formats, but which subsequently are found to be faulty.
If it's a SCSI drive you may need to set it to read in linear mode.  eg.
"linux linear" at the boot prompt.

regards.... Don.
