Hmmmf.

Q. How many Pentium 200 processors does it take to make the US goverment
want to slap restrictions on it.

A. about 35.

That is about how big a Beowulf cluster can be before it reaches
the US super-computer limit.  How many Pentium II 300 processors,
about 7.  Once you hit enabling technology for a Super Computer
with about 7000 MTOPS you can't export it to certian countries.

Looks like the US spooks are getting scared by the power of 
a *typical* Beowulf system.
