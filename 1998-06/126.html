InstallFest coming up!

Hi all,

LinuxSA will be holding its second InstallFest soon.  The details so
far:

When: Saturday the 15th of August 1998, 10am to 4pm.

Where: SA Water, 65 Broad Street, Marden (thanks to Terry Schenkel for
  organising the venue).

Why: To assist people new to Linux get it installed on their computer
  & to have fun.

We are currently looking for people in the following categories
(others coming soon):
- Helpers
- Vendors

Helpers:

If you are able and willing to assist people on the day install Linux
on their computer, please email register@linuxsa.org.au with the
following information:
- Your name & email address :)
- Your Linux experience level on a scale of "I once saw it" to Guru.
- Whether you would be able to be there the whole time, and if not,
  when you would be able to be there.
- What distribution(s) you have experience with installing.
- Whether you are able to bring along a CD or two with a current
  release of some Linux distribution, and if so what distribution and
  version number.

We're also looking for a couple of helpers who could bring along their
computer and demonstrate the fun things you can do with Linux.

Vendors:

If you sell Linux products and would like to do so at the InstallFest,
please email register@linuxsa.org.au with your contact information.
