Re: Mystery of the disappearing route

On Mon, 1 Jun 1998, Justin Hawkins wrote:
>
> However, it sometimes forgets the default route! I thought I must have
> configured something wrong, but tonight I booted it up, ran 'route' to
> confirm the default route was there, switched consoles and telnetted to
> uni. No problem.

Make sure you're not running routed or gated, i.e. run
the following:

ps auxww | egrep 'routed|gated'

If either show up, perhaps you should consider not running them
as they both may make dynamic changes to your routing.
