Re: printcap...

On Tue, 30 Jun 1998, Ben Kramer wrote:

> When ever I print from my linux box it always prints out an extra page
> with my details on it. As there are only 1 or 2 user who use it I dont
> need to know who printed it, how do I turn this feature off ?

You need the 'sh' field in /etc/printcap. From the manpage
for printcap(5):

     Name       Type      Description
     sh         bool      false                  suppress printing of burst
                                                 page header
