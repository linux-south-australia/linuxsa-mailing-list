LinuxSA Meeting Reminder

Just your friendly reminder that there is a LinuxSA meeting tonight:

Tuesday, 16 June, 1998 at 7:00pm
Digital Equipment Corporation
139 Frome St
Adelaide

A presentation will be given on "Transaction Processing with Linux" by
Steve O'Connor.  Steve will be presenting a discussion of Transaction
processing with a Postgres database backend.  He will also be
discussing using transaction processing and the GTK+ GUI toolkit in an
application that is being used in Pizza bars in Adelaide.

After the formalish part of the meeting is over at about 9:00, people
are welcome to stay on for a while to chat with others.  You are also
welcome to bring along your computer if you need specific help from a
Linux expert.  The meeting will adjourn to a pizza place a couple of
minutes walk away when the guys from Digital decide they want to lock
up and go home.
