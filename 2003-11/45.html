Re: LG

On Monday,  3 November 2003 at 10:49:46 -0800, Steve wrote:
> Can anyone tell me if the problem with  LG is confined to
> *JUST* the cd-roms or the Read Writers as well.

My understanding is that the problem relates to a misinterpretation of
the IDE flush buffer command, which isn't defined for read-only
devices, and it maps to a firmware download command.  This command
*does* have a meaning for writeable devices, so they're not
susceptible.  My source reminds me, however, that the firmware was
probably designed by the same engineer.
