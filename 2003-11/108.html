Lindows

Michael Robertson of Lindows has sent out his latest Michael's Minute re 
Lindows. The man certainly knows how to market a product. One little snip I 
liked was this

"Lindows.com now works with the majority of hardware companies (HP, Epson, 
nVidia, ATI, Intel, Via, Seagate, FIC, AOpen, etc.) to ensure that their 
printers, video cards, modems, mother boards, and so on, all have Linux 
drivers. Many of these companies are now even working with us to make sure 
LindowsOS is compatible with their new equipment _before_ they release it."

In full at www.lindows.com/lindows_michaelsminutes.php
