If you're not in a hurry, auctions can be your friend - you can get some 
dirt cheap machines there.  Quite often you'll find in goverment 
auctions, and such, older Compaq machines are auctioned off quite 
cheaply; they're great machines as routers, as they're quiet as hell 
(most Compaq's don't seem to use CPU fans, just bloody large heatsinks). 
  Stick a PCI RAID card in them, along with two hard drives, and older 
machines run nice and fast as file servers.
