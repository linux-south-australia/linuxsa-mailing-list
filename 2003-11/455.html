Re: Can I (externally) host my own domain?

another way is to have someone host your domain.
eg. a cheap way is usually your domain registrar.
mine is directnic.com, I could have them receive all
email for my domain which I then collect from them
via pop/imap.  they can also redirect www.mydomain.com
to my ip.  this is what I used to do until I got a
perm connection and static ip.

this way you get to host your own web pages (which it
doesn't matter if they don't work every so often) but
they host your email (which is more critical is done
right).

how ever if you don't mind the extra cost, having a
static IP makes it easier and more reliable
(adam.com.au will give you a static ip for extra $)
