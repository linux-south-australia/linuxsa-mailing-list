Re: [OT] We Will Remember on 11 November

Thanks for a very perceptive post.

To me the respect is to all who have fallen in war. Each has fallen for 
a myriad of reasons and for a myriad of beliefs but their death is a 
loss that the earth has felt and humanity has been lessened by.

I cannot speak for the actions / feelings of the RSL and other groups. 
They will each have to reach their own accommodation with the devils 
that plague them. 

Part of my prayer is for them to reach the peace that comes through 
understanding and forgiveness.

In the same way that Christmas Day was celebrated in the Trenches 
during WW1, they must realise that they all were fighting for something 
they believed in but when all the rest is blown away they are humans 
one and all.
