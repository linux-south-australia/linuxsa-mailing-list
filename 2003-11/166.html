Re: [OT] We Will Remember on 11 November

I tell my children today is a day to honour ALL those who fall in war -
no matter what side, no matter if soldier or innocent bystander. To
remember those vast uncountable, unknowable faces, and grieve for their
potential, and to honour their sacrifice on the path to a better world.

Yes, both sides - for even the wrong serve to show us there is better in
humanity, to show us the dangers of extremes and mindless service.

War makes heroes and villians, sure. But in the end, what war really makes is victims.

Lest we forget. 

Because those who fail to learn from history are doomed to repeat it.
