[news] Open source group wins grant

http://news.com.au/common/story_page/0,4057,7837730%255E15306,00.html
 THE Victorian Government has given Open Source Victoria a $50,000 grant
 to advance the cause of open source software.

 The grant was awarded last month under the government agency MultiMedia
 Victoria's "Next wave" program.

 OSV, which has been in operation for a year and has almost 90 members,
 including 40 businesses, will use the funds for several projects
 including a state-wide open source programming competition for high
 school students.

 "Students will be encourage to use at least partly open source code
 tools... to produce open source entries," OSV spokesman Con Zymaris
 said.

 The next plan is for a roadshow which will travel Victoria
 demonstrating open source solutions for business, government and
 individual computer users.
