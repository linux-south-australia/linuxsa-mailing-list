Re: procmail

On Fri, Nov 28, 2003 at 10:15:28AM -0000, S  Manikkalal said:
> hai
> can u tell me the correct format of .procmailrc to fetch To.Bcc,Cc
> from a 
> I am using procmail in my organisation to fetch mails.
> #vi /root/.procmailrc
> 
> :0:
> * ^(To|To|CC).*john
> ! john


:0
* ^(To|Cc|Bcc).*john
john

> the problem is that if "To" address is corrupted ,Cc's and Bcc's are also
> not delivered even if they r valid e-mail addresses.

The above rule will match john on any line starting with 'To', 'Cc' or
'Bcc' (matching in any case) and put it in the john folder.

Search on Google for some examples of .procmailrc.  There are millions.
