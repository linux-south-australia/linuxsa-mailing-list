Alberto,

     I think you will want to take a look at the ip-sysctl document that is provided with the kernel source.  This document is in the Documentaion/networking directory under the kernel source directory.  On redhat 9.0 (with redhat's rpm) it is:
/usr/src/linux-2.4/Documentation/networking/ip-sysctl.txt
Now this is not a document on the the syscalls you mentioned but rather a list of options for ipv4 that goes along with this document:
http://www.psc.edu/networking/perf_tune.html
That should tell you what you need to know about buffers and TCP as well as comparisons among the different OS's.  I hope this helps, and isn't too far from what you wanted.
