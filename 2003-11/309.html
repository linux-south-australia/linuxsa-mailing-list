I don't know of any, and I don't think you can. The Microsoft lease
agreement is a departmental agreement, not an individual school agreement.

of just not paying, you sort of have to know how the Education Department
works to understand what I mean.

For you interest the cost to the school is determined per student, so the
more students at the school, the more it costs. I cannot tell you how much
the schools I work for pay, but it's enough to buy a few extra computers per
year.

so I doubt anything major will happen for a few years. I've been wrong
before though.
