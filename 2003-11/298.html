Re: internodes mandrake 9.2 cds

 From what the mandrake site says, the released iso's aren't fixed at 
all, its just the fact that LG have released the info on how to fix 
their drives.

http://www.mandrakelinux.com/en/ftp.php3

"*Important note before download:* If you own a LG CD-ROM drive, please 
read this page <http://www.mandrakelinux.com/en/lgerrata.php3>; before 
downloading Mandrake 9.2: a few LG CD-ROM drives aren't fully ATAPI 
compliant, and can freeze by using Mandrake 9.2. If you own one of these 
drives please update your firmware from the LG website. If your CD drive 
is already frozen, use the LG procedure to restore it."
