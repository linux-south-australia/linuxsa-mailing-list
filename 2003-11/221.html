> White Box Enterprise Linux is a re-spin of Red Hat Enterprise Linux, 
> with the intent of being able to keep your system secure over a 
> life-span of 5 years, by using the source RPMs of the security patches 
> Red Hat releases for RHEL.
>
> We're currently living in fear that some enthusiastic person will post 
> this to SlashDot and melt LaNet into lines of free-flowing copper on 
> the ground, so downloading is only available via BitTorrent for now.
>
> Much like RHEL, WBEL will *not* be trying to keep up with the latest & 
> greatest kernels and other packages - pretty much security fixes only, 
> just like Red Hat's policy.  This is hopefully to become a RPM-based 
> *SERVER* distro, not a kiddie's toy for every new shiny ferret package 
> that comes along.
