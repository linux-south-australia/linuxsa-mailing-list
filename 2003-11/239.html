Re: resending mail in mutt

On Fri, Nov 14, 2003 at 08:23:34AM +1030, Brian Marr said:
> 
> 
> I need to resend an email from Mutt. My previous attempts to do this have
> involved cut & paste, and going through the process of adding attachments
> all over again. Can anyone suggest an easy way to do this ? Take for example
> any mail in the "sent-mail" folder. Can i resend it as new ?

You can use 'b' to bounce an email.  It will resend the email with full
headers (including From header) so if you select an email that isn't in
your sent folder and bounce it, it will appear to be coming "From" the
original sender.
