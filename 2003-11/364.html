Re: Webmail solutions

I made the same choice. Squirrelmail works well with imap and is easy to 
extend with plugins. I use the version from debian-unstable.

I have mainly used it with cyrus imap and exim. I have recently been 
running the dovecot imapd and that seems to work well, although I 
haven't tested it with lots of users.

When using cyrus I usually had to increase max processes in inetd.conf 
with something like nowait.200 dependng on the size of the site.

Also you may have to tweak up the php max process size, max file upload 
and timeout if people are going to send huge files around.
