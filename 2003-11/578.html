MS targets Netherlands PC supplier

At http://www.lindows.com/lindows_michaelsminutes.php you can read 
about an attempt by MS Netherlands to take action against a computer 
dealer providing computers with non-MS operating systems.

"
 I've got a strange phone call this morning from Microsoft
 Netherlands. They are saying that they are preparing a trial here
 against Lindows and they want to involve my company DV Computer
 Systems (www.dvcs.nl) in this trial because I sell my computers
 with Lindows Operating System...
"
Seems that the MS behemoth may be looking at targets outside of its US 
monopoly.
