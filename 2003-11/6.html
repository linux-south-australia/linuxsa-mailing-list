I have seen some cases where something extra has been sent by the
ppp/chat process which knocks the modem back on-hook (e.g. an
extra carriage return, or chat not waiting long enough).  I don't
know esmith, but have you been able to find any ppp or chat logs
lying around that are of any use?  /etc/syslog.conf might point
the way here, plus any debug settings you can set on the dialler.
