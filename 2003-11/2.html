[OT] Aurora and Solar Storms

As many of you are probably aware, we are in the midst of a series of 
solar / geomagnetic storms at present, which provide a high probability 
of spectacular aurora displays.

One really good site to follow their progress is at 
http://www.dan.sp-agency.ca/www/rtoval.htm
This site collects information from a series of magnetometers that run 
into the high Arctic.  The picture is updated every five minutes.
