Re: looking for a IEC-320 C19 (16 amp) power cord

Quoting Shaun Branden <shaun@pcuse.com>:

> Does anyone have a high amp power cord for a ups that they are willing
> to sell second hand ? It is a IEC-320 C19 (16 amp) cable.

The easiest way to get those cables is to call APC in Sydney and order one.
 
> The type of cable I am after is like http://pcau.com/l3/APC0266.php
> but with a standard wall power point male connector on one end and the
> c19 female on the other.

These things are often special cables for a reason - standard power outlets in
Australia are only rated for 10 or 15 amps, such a cable should at least have a
larger than normal earth pin if you insist on plugging it into a socket that
wasn't designed for it. UPS's can pull a lot of current when they're charging
the batteries, which may trip a breaker and cause you the very problem a UPS is
supposed to guard against.
