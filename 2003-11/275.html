LinMagAu Update

Hello all,

A quick update regards LinMagAu.

LinMagAu is moving..  roughly 600kms inland Perth to Kalgoorlie.. 

Thus packing and other random (non mag related issues)
are taking my time this month :)

We had a number of choices about what to do ..  as some 
articles have already been submitted.. we plan to go ahead
even if we have a slimmer issue this month..

We will still be running the Software & Apps and Features sections, 
and a look at the various tools of the site, along with some great 
articles from previous months issues, but there will not be the
regular updates/community/tips/ideation or events sections..

Things will all be back to normal come the Dec end of year issue, 
but for this, the 10th issue due out end of Nov.. we have moved up the 
submission by date.. to 22th Nov..  so you need to get your articles 
to me (via email return)..  or using the onsite submission link.. 

Submissions can be of any length, include graphics or charts,
and are accepted in any format.. although open source is prefered.

So if you have written something that we should know about.. 
if you know someone who has, let us know :)

Thanks again for the last few months..
The next edition will be out end of November :)
