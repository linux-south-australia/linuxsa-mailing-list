[OT] Fonts. What do you know about Arial?

In a recent discussion in the Linux Forum on CompuServe the topic
of fonts came up when somebody claimed that Arial was a Microsoft 
property.

This was one response;
"
Actually, it's not. Arial is a Monotype font. This is from the 
Properties of Arial.TTF as displayed by the Microsoft 
OpenType Font Properties Extension: "Arial® Trademark of The Monotype 
Corporation plc registered in the US Pat & TM Off. and elsewhere."

Arial and Helvetica are very similar but not identical. For a brief 
comparison of Helvetica and Arial, see 
http://www.ms-studio.com/articles.html , "The Scourge of Arial."

This one is fun, too:
 http://www.iliveonyourvisits.com/helvetica/ , "Helvetica or Arial? A 
quiz"
"
