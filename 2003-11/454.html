> In the mean time: Any suggestions for a good/reliable (SA) ADSL ISP with

You've got two options; go with a provider such as Internode that offer
some kind of restricted unlimited, such as QoS/bandwidth throttling,
slowed downloads after so much transfer, etc, or go with somebody that
uses Comindico/Connect's networks.

http://www.sirion.net.au/ is the only SA-ran one I know of; dialup plans
are pretty decent, too :-)

Comindico DSL here in Adelaide seems pretty decent; I was on it for a
few months, no complaints at all.
