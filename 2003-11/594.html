Re: pppd dial up problem

Toby Berriman <toby@esc.net.au> wrote:
> My problem is that I can connect to my ISP ok (EscapeNet) but it will
> stall at anytime sometimes during downloading emails or certain web
> sites all activity stops my connection is still there but no ability
> to send or recieve.

Sounds like xon/xoff flow control.  Make sure you have a proper serial
cable (ie at least 5 wires), and that your ppp/options includes crtscts
and doesn't include xonxoff.
