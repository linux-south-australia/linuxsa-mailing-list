Re: Remote Graphic Editing

My suggestion for doing this remotely is to have a number of tricks up your 
sleave as you may not have the ability to get a shell or run a remote X 
session.  Remote access has all sort of limitations.

Consider trying to automate some of it to at least allow:
- downloading the fullsized images if necessary
- at least downsizing the images so you can view them remotely at a size 
that won't take a milenium to download.
