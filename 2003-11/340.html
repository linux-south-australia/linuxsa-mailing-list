Webmail solutions.

Hiya,

I'm curious to hear what webmail solutions people are using out there;
I've always used openwebmail in the past against local mbox folders, but
now that I've switched to courier-imap+postfix, storing in Maildir, I
might need to look at alternatives.

I've played with solutions such as imp+horde and squirrelmail briefly,
but none seemed as featureful as openwebmail.  Unfortunately, it seems
openwebmail has absolutely no support for Maildir, which wouldn't bother
me, but it doesn't appear to support IMAP either (something I assumed
all webmail's did, but I'm evidently wrong, or the debian package
doesn't support it).

IMAP-capable solutions are the most ideal to me as there's some installs
where the webfrontend webmail is actually on a different box to the mail
server.
