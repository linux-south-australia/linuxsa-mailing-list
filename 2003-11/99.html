[REMINDER]: SA-AUUG Nov Meeting Wed 12th: Inside CitiLan (WirelessNetworking in Adelaide)

The South Australian chapter of the Australian Unix User Group (AUUG)
formally invites you to attend their monthly meeting. Details are as
follows:
+----------------------------------------
| SA-AUUG Chapter Meeting
+--------+-------------------------------
| DATE   | Wed 12th November 2003
| TIME   | 7pm Onwards
| VENUE  | Marcellina's Pizza Bar
|        | 273 Hindley Street
|        | Adelaide
+--------+------------------------------
| AGENDA | http://www.citilan.com.au
+--------+------------------------------

Come join us over a beverage and a Pizza to hear about CitiLan.

An exciting development for Adelaide, CitiLan is a world first
for wireless networking. Providing contiguous wireless networking
for the Adelaide CBD, accessing the internet has never been easier.

John Lindsay, famed Internode tech and one of the main instigators
behind the network,
will take us behind the scenes of CitiLan. Explaining its construction,
routing,
authentication and how to companies are combining together to make CitiLan
a world first.

You'll even be able to try it out!

Hope to see you there.
