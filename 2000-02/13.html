ht://Dig

ht://Dig is a web site search engine.

Features are:

 - simple and boolean search.

 - easy to customise into the look and feel of your web site.

 - indexes Word and PDF documents.

 - will index foreign sites and mark these differently in
   search results.

 - compressed index.

Recommended.
