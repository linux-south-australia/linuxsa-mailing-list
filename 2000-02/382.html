Re: What time is it?

Hi,

> Hi everyone, I don't know if this is specifically a Linux question, but
> I first noticed it under Linux. My clock is 12 hours fast. This isn't a
> gradual thing. One day I noticed it was 11:00 at 11 pm. I checked the
> timezone etc, which all seemed fine. Does anyone have any clues?
> TIA, Nic B    =)

I'd check several things...

What does your BIOS say. If you have another OS installed, what settings
do ay clock related prgrams have in them (specifically related to
TimeZone, and GMT).

Do you have any clock setting software installed, (xntp etc) setting it
from a wron clock (a Win NT and Novell server can set clock at log on
time).

If your time zzone IS right, are you sure you don't have the setting for
your BIOS clock set to GMT anywhere (this'll make it between 9.5 and 10.5
hours fast).

Is /etc/TIMEZONE correct ? In RedHat this is set from other values, are
they correct ?
