Re: Mount on FD / CDROM?

Are you mounting a linux (ext2 fs) formatted floppy or a MS-DOS (fat fs)
formatted floppy???

If its a DOS formatted floppy then you will need to do:
mount -t vfat /dev/fd0 /mnt/floppy
or change the "ext2" on the "/dev/fd0" line in fstab (for a more permanent
solution).

as for the cdrom drive, it should be okay.
