Re: Printtool

On Sat, 29 Jan 2000, Daryl Tester wrote:

> I think you'll find this is a parport_pc issue (I can't be 100% sure,
> as my printer is attached to a 6.0 machine at the moment).  I have the
> following in my /etc/conf.modules on my 6.1 machine:
> 
> alias	parport_lowlevel parport_pc
> options	parport_pc io=0x378 irq=7

Thanks Daryl,

I added the alias line to /etc/conf.modules and printtool detected my
printer on /dev/lp0 perfectly (as it should!).  It seems that 6.1 deosn't
add this line automatically, where as 6.0 does somewhere along the line.

And I would think hard if anyone else here with 6.1 considers upgrading
the modutils package.  I tried it and it really messed things up (reading
from different files, and after that was corrected, my sound modules
failed to load properly at boot time).

Thanks again!
