Re: Squid vs Browser cache

My undrestanding is that Squid, like most proxies, can be configured to
listen to a particular TCP/IP port. You need to setup your browser to
use a proxy at that port on your local host. The way to do this is
dependent on the browser.

With that information you should be able to work out whether it is
working. If you need more help I'd suggest you tell us what type of
browser you are using :-)
