Sorry, you are wrong. Almost every pdf file I have supports copy/paste. The only exceptions I have on my hd are a couple of library docs from Flinders Uni that are pdf encapsulations of scanned journal articles. OCR would maybe work on the scanned docs after printing/re-scanning, but it might not be worth the effort - the quality isn't great. (mine aren't anyway)
