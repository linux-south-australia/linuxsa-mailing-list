Re: NIS password authenticate problems

I remember that the standard Makefile for the NIS server which comes
with RH6 doesn't propogate the passwd map for UID's less than a
stupidly large number.

Make sure the UID you are trying to propogate are above this number.

Also ensure that "ypcat passwd" returns the passwd list you want on
the client.
