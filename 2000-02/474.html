Re: Searching the Mailing List archives

The digest version of LinuxSA is particularly nice for 
archiving, and is also useful if you mostly read LinuxSA 
and rarely post messages.  Often questions and answers 
appear together in the digest, which is very helpful.  

To subscribe to the digest, send a message with "subscribe" 
as the subject to:

	mailto:linuxsa-digest-request@linuxsa.org.au

I have digests from (roughly) mid-December last year saved 
in my mailer.  If there's enough interest, I'll tar them up 
and put them on my ftp site (or on Netcraft, perhaps?)
