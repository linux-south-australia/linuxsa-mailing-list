through the packages listing and unselect everything.

First thing you need to ask is "What do the machines need to do?"
From this you can figure out if you need networking, X, development
libraries, c  compilers, emacs (et al.).

By ruling out X, you can also rule out most of:
gnome/kde/afterstep/anotherstep/yetanotherstep/XF86-anything/
