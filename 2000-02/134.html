Re: automatic root login

> I am running linux 6.0 and i am wishing to make the machine
> automatically boot into the root account.  I am not after single user
> mode.  Basically I want the machine to boot to run level 3 and then
> automatically log root in (say on tty1).  I am well aware of the
> security implications of this.
> So i am wondering if someone out there could help.
> cheers

in your /etc/inittab replace

1:2345:respawn:/sbin/mingetty tty1

with somethine like

4:3:respawn:/bin/bash

then do (as root)

kill -HUP 1

or reboot
