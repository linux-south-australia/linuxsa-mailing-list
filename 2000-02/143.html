Re: libpixmap.so

Toby Corkindale wrote:

> Everything seems to work, except that I get thousands of messages on the
> console that was running X that say "Gtk-warning: cannot find module in
> module_path (libpixmap.so)".
> 
> Now, I am guessing that this is to do with the fact that the old gtk and
> glib were in /usr/... and now they're in /usr/local/...
> All I need to do, I presume, is change the module_path to point to the
> correct location for GNOME.
> However I cannot find where to do this.

I think libpixmap.so is a gtk theme engine... mine resides in
/usr/lib/gtk/themes/engines/ . Maybe you're using a gtk theme
which uses the pixmap engine for some or all of the widgets, but
it can't find the lib. Check your .gtkrc file. It might have
the actual theme there, but is more likely to point to another
file. I think your can edit a ModulePath property or something
like that in the file.
