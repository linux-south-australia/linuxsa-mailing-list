Windoze and X do not necessarily set up the video card in the same way
for the same refresh rate; parameters such as the proportion of the
scan line that is actually used for display, and the position/timing
of the sync pulses, may be different. It may be that by using xvidtune
to tweak the sync pulses you could get a stable display at 85Hz.

If your suspicion about the monitor not being up to spec anymore is
correct, you may well find that on a monitor old enough to only go up
to 85Hz there's a horizontal hold preset on the PCB somewhere that you
can adjust, or maybe several for different ranges of scan frequency.

svgatextmode uses X-style modelines, and ISTR its docs being very
detailed and helpful about how to calculate them.
