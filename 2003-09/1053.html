I can't remember the headers from the sample you sent, but I'm guessing

Remember that the virus writes the emails and chooses the subject etc
from a bunch of pre-packaged strings it carries with it. If you extract
the .exe from one of the emails and run strings on it (strings
virusname.exe | less) and page down a while, you'll see the subjects
buried in there.
