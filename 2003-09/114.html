Re: migrating

I recommend kmail if you are using the KDE environment, I haven't really 
played with many others (except for mozilla mail which is what I use at 
work)... currently I'm filtering my mail though spamassassin, you may want to 
do the same at some stage. I'm investigating signing my emails with gpg too.

The filtering capabilities of Linux email clients is waaay more powerful than 
outlook. For example, in kmail, I can filter on X-Spam-Flag=YES. But my 
friend (who uses outlook) could not find a way to filter on any of the tags 
embedded in the email. I could also filter on the number of *'s spamassassin 
gives an email, e.g. > 10 points I just delete it instead of moving it to the 
trash folder.

Hope this helps.

Damien
