Re: Stress testing new servers

Memtest is good for ram as mentioned previously
I normally populate the system with a mirror of the environment (like a DB)
and hammer it till it grinds
Also you can try prime95 (not sure if there is a linux version) but put
win2k/xp on it.
Also if u go with win32 initially use multiple copies of sandra burnin 
that will work the hdds ram/network too.

I do these when I bring up my desktops for home - primarly due to the fact
I overclock and it shows up any problems.
