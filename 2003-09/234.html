On Fri, Sep 05, 2003 at 10:19:15AM +0930, David Lloyd said:

> Brian,
> 
> > I have been trying to set up Mutt & procmail so that Linuxsa mails are
> > directed to their own Mailbox. Now when I invoke fetchmail it starts
> > reading the mail instead of just putting it into its mailbox. Not sure
> > what is happening here. 
> 
> Huh?
> 
> Fetchmail is fetching the mail...I'm not certain what the problem is you're
> describing...

Fetchmail fetches, your .forward file sets up filtering through procmail,
and your .procmailrc file specifies your email rules.

You need a .forward file that contains:

"|exec /usr/local/bin/procmail"

and then you need to set up your .procmailrc with a bunch of rules!  (man
procmailrc) :)

LinuxSA has a handy X-Loop header that you can grab on to to filter it's
emails.
