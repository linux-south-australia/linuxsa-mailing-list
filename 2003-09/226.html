Re: Recursive ls & changing permissions on files

On the subject of how bloody cool find is, at Uni I wanted to set all files 
where I have executeable permission to also have group executeable 
permission. So it was a simple:

find . -type f -perm +100 -exec chmod g+x '{}' \;

Do *that* on windows :)
