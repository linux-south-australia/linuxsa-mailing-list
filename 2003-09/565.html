new local Linux mirror(s)

The 3FL gaming network have just launched a stack of FTP mirror
servers around the country connected to Pipe Networks' peering
exchanges (or WAIX in the case of WA).

SA:			http://mirror.sa.3fl.net/
other locations:	http://mirror.3fl.net/

If your ISP is connected to a peering exchange you'll be able to
reach the local mirror.  If you can't connect to it, your ISP isn't
peering with Pipe (maybe you can convince them ;-)

There's quite a few Linux mirrors and other free software:
	asplinux
	clusterknoppix
	debian
	e-smith
	gentoo
	rsync gentoo portage tree
	ipcop
	knoppix
	mandrake
	redhat
	+ others
and BSD:
	freebsd
	openbsd

If there's something you want, the guys are quite approachable
(request form at http://mirror.sa.3fl.net/?request)
