Re: Seagate to ship HDs with Lindows preinstalled

I have just posted more details of this. Would be easier than everybody 
slashdotting to find the report.

Basically they have a deal with two big wholesalers of Seagate to 
pre-install Lindows 4 on the Seagate 40Gb Barracuda hard drive.
"
The Seagate 40GB Barracuda 7200.7 drive pre-loaded with LindowsOS will 
be available in early October from well-known distributors Bell 
Microproducts Inc. (NASDAQ:BELM) or Synnex Information Technologies 
Inc.* 
"
The system builder gets the HD at same price as blank HD's so cost of 
OS becomes $0.

Combine with their great Help Desk deals and Internet Phone deals and 
you have a very enticing package to offer the low budget computer 
buyer.
