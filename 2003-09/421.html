a) Load the sexiest linux distro you can lay your hands on, on a spare
box. load dosemu and see if your DOS program will run on it.

b) Run your program in real dos running over bochs or vmware on your
linux box

c) If you have the source, convert it to linux. 

d) If you don't have the source, consider recreating it in something
portable and open-source.

d) Network the dosbox to a samba server, map the samba share as a drive
and get your DOS program to save it's stuff to the samba box.

e) keep rebooting :)
