Re: Automatic Proxy URL

It's called WPAD (Web Proxy Auto Discovery).

http://www.cam.ac.uk/WWW/ProxyServer/draft-ietf-wrec-wpad-01.txt

It was submitted as an IETF draft by Microsoft, although it never became a
standard of any kind and has since expired and been removed from the IETF
site.

I think it was implemented in Mozilla around 1.3
