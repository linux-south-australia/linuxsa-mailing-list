Re: argh spammers using my domain!

Alan,

I concur totally, the advantages of having a home mail server are
enormous & it's not really that difficult to set up.

We started administering our own postfix/fetchmail/procmail/spamassassin
server at home during the great *@adelaide.on.net spammage of 2002 when
our ISP based e-mail addresses were flooded & the ISP refused to lift a
finger.

We've never looked back.

Have even been running our email server on dial-up the past few weeks
with no problems, (recently moved house & no DSL re-connected yet).
We put the link up for half an hour or so every couple of days to let
all those queued messages on remote servers come streaming in.
Hell we don't even have a domain as such, just some scripted DNS
forwarding that updates when the link is put up.

Gotta luv the internet 8^)

Regards,
Rick
