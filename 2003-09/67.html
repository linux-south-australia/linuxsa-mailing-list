RE: xmms playing music files as a CD in linux...

As far as I know, some MD recorders insert track marks after at least 2
seconds silence (when using line-in recording).There is no special
"frequency", rather, the MD recorder inserts a track mark when it senses
a long silence and then a loud sound, (eg the end of song silence then
the start of a new song)

Many CDs have a 2 second "gap" between tracks which the MD picks up and
inserts track marks. Some don't which means that songs get joined
together when recorded onto an MD. Some songs have long silences in them
or low volume sounds which trigger this mechanism also and you can get
single songs split into multiple tracks when recorded to MD.

I don't know if you could set a pause between songs in xmms or perhaps
play a song of "2 seconds silence" between each song. See how that goes.
Or use the sync recording feature of the MD if it has it?
