Re: Linmodems and drivers

Hi Ian,

Can you check and see if the ESmith box has the cu command? It's part of
the uucp protocol (I think), and is a very simple way of checking a
modem on a serial port:

cu -l ttyS0

AT

OK

~.  <-- type this to exit the cu session

You may need to be root to access the device like this.
