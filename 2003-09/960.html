Ian Loxton wrote:

>Just got the AirNet newsletter and they have included this comparison 
>between ADSL providers.
>

Comparisons are best left to neutral observers, when it comes from an 
organisation trying to sell you something, it's better described as 
'marketing'.

>Here is a list of several of our  competitors, these figures are based 
>on a 3 month agreement with the internet service  provider.
>
>  ISP          Monthly  Set-up  Inc. Data  Extra Data  Modem Required
>
>  AirNet   $49.95  $139.00  2,500 MB  5.0c per MB On Sale from AirNet
>
>  iHug     $49.95  $159.00  500 MB  11.0c per MB Bring Your Own  
>
>  Chariot  $49.95  $110.00  1,000 MB  12.5c per MB  Bring Your Own 
>
>  SE  Net  $49.95  $99.00  500 MB  15.0c per MB Bring Your Own  
>
>  Adam     $53.08  $139.00  975 MB  15.0c per MB Bring Your Own   
>

Every one of the listed ISP's are happy to sell you a modem, yet this 
list seems to imply that only Airnet will sell you one. For all of the 
listed ISP's, including Adam, caveat emptor - do your homework first 
rather than trust a simple list that covers only one facet of a 
complicated offering.
