Senet. Search the archives and recommend a proper and competant ISP. In
SA, that's Internode or Adam IMO.

Make sure the username for authentication with senet is
<username>@senet.com.au, not just <username>. They do this because
MCI/OzEmail who own senet and are slowly killing it need to be able to
figure out which network this user _was_ connected to so they can
retrieve the auth details etc.

Talking to the helpdesk is very painful unless you are very patient. The
tech department has a MINIMUM 4 hour response time for urgent problems.
I have never had a problem fixed inside 4 hours, and the record is
nearly a month.

Good luck, you'll need it :)
