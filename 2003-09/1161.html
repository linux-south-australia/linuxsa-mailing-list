On Fri, Sep 26, 2003 at 08:17:43AM +0930, Shaun Branden wrote:
> On Fri, Sep 26, 2003 at 07:00:44AM +0930, Brian Marr wrote:
> > 
> > 
> > Can anyone suggest how to move a large number of emails from one mailbox to
> > another ? I have been able to move individual mails with save and copy, but
> > when it comes to tagging files, no luck. 
> 
> Tagging should work, just:
> hit 't' on individual mails, or
> hit 'T' and use a regex, eg '.' would tag all of them
> then ';s' to save all of them.

Yep.  With my settings this works in the index but not in the pager.  
';' is the default setting of 'tag-prefix'.  You can also 'set auto_tag'
which applies operations to tagged groups by default (no need to use
';').
