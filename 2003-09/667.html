Pete,

 if you are close to the city hook onto http://csdn.auug.org.au and 
check out your nearest CSDN contact point. We provide almost all of the 
current distros at a set rate of $2 per CD we burn. Red Hat and 
Mandrake take 3 CDs each.

We don't give help desk support, LinuxSA list is great for that.

If you want documentation that is in your hands then buy the Pocketbook 
series from your Newsagent. Their books are excellent introductions to 
the world of Linux. I think they are around $20.
