[OT] Rescue Dog Festival NYC

Rescue Dog Festival NYC

 This Saturday, Sept 6, at the Intrepid Sea-Air-Space Museum
 at Pier 86, 46th St and the Hudson River in NYC, there will
 be an all-day celebration of Search & Rescue and Service
 Dogs.

 The celebration is in memory of Bear, the golden retriever
 who was the first dog on the scene at Ground Zero on 9/11,
 and who found more people than any other dog. Bear got
 cancer as a result of his exposure at the WTC site, and died
 a year later. But his human, Scott Shields, will be there,
 with Bear's son, Theodore, and many other incredible
 civilian and military service dogs. Admission is free.

 See www.bearsearchandrescue.org for more info.
