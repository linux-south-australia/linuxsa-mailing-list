[OT] Garage Doors Raise DMCA Questions

 Garage Doors Raise DMCA Questions  

 By Katie Dean
 02:00 AM Sep. 17, 2003 PT

 Manufacturers of a seemingly innocuous product -- a garage
 door opener -- are embroiled in a battle that tests the
 limits of a controversial copyright law. 

 Skylink Technologies manufactures a universal garage door
 opener that can be used to open and shut any type of garage
 door. Its competitor, the Chamberlain Group, claims that
 Skylink violates the Digital Millennium Copyright Act, or
 DMCA, by selling such a product.
