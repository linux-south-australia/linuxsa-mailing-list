Wvdial may be helpful in negotiating with the other end and starting 
ppp.  It  got me onto ihug quicker than kppp and you can see the log of 
events.

Suggestion. Log onto the site with another working PC that does "dial 
up" to confirm that it will work. Also you could try ringing the number 
with a handset and confirm that you are getting a modem on the other 
end. Look closely at the files in /etc/ppp and check them against a PC 
that is dialing successfully into that number. Maybe someone who is 
using senet could help with this. Alternatively, borrow a working 
external modem to help in your diagnosis.

Validation of authentication errors imply that you are close. The system 
is wanting to connect. You are absolutely shure about your username and 
password, and that your account has time on it.
