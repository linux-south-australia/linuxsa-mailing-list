My .forward has this for LinuxSA:

if $return_path matches "linuxsa-request@linuxsa.org.au" then
        save $home/Maildir/.lists/
endif

This is the Exim filter language,
http://www.exim.org/exim-html-4.20/doc/html/filter.html. It's meant to
be a language you can more safely hand out to users while easily
allowing nearly all of what procmail can do, and I think it probably is
too. (I use other things for anti-spam that I find much easier to
maintain than procmail.)
