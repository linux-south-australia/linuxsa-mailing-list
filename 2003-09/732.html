EducationaLinux 2004 - Registrations Open

EducationaLinux 2004 will be held on the 11th and 12th January, 2004.

Registrations are open NOW!

EducationaLinux's aim is to promote the use of the open source operating
system, Linux, and other open source products in the Australian
educational settings. It will provide opportunities for all education
professionals and students to come together to discuss and find out about
important open source developments and deployments in Australia.

All registered attendees of Linux.Conf.Au 2004 will be able to attend
EducationaLinux 2004 free of charge. You may also register to come
EducationaLinux 2004 only for just $66 AUD.

Registrations can be made at:

 * http://lca2004.linux.org.au/register.cgi

In addition you may find out more about EducationaLinux 2004 by visiting:

 * http://educationalinux.adam.com.au/

David Lloyd, organiser of the mini-conf, would like to thank Recall Design
for designing the web-site, Adam Internet for providing hosting services
and Netcraft Australia for the announcement mailing list.

IBM is the proud sponsor of Linux.Conf.Au 2004.
