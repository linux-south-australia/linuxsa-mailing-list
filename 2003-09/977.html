Re: ADSL? OT now

Mark,

> Do your friends really want unlimited downloads?  Or do they want the
> ability to use the Internet without incurring an excess bill?

I suspect that people will wear a consistently high bill if the ISP can:

 * prove that it's fair
 * demonstrate a good and consistent quality of service

ISP's that don't do that latter tend to get canned and customers go away
(witness the SeNet debacle).

I conjecture that what people are REALLY after is consistency in their
bills. I think they'll pay slightly more to make sure that their bills don't
suddenly become 200-300 per cent higher just because the latest version of
Mandrake or whatever came out.
