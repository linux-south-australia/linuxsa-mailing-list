Re: Linux newbie looking to migrate to this OS...

Andy,

If you are after a nice business orientated desktop then take a look at
Dropline (http://www.dropline.net/gnome) built upon Slackware 9.0.   Its
very easy to get going and maintain.   It based around gnome 2.2 (new
version 2.4) and has been customised to present quite a nice looking
desktop.  Did I also mention is is capable of self updating ?

It has alot of very good applications already installed that will cover a
majority of your XP desktop requirements, web/mail/office tools etc.   I
would seriously check it out as a possible XP replacement.

I would recomend having Nvidia based video cards and installing the
relevant Nvidia drivers.   This gives you better desktop performance with
multimedia applications like Xine.

In all I've found Dropline to be a very tidy desktop, there are a few
quirks in gnome that are being ironed out.  I'm yet to install the new
desktop based around gnome 2.4 (released on Thursday last week ~300Mb
download).

Not familiar enough with GnuCash to offer any assitance with book keeping.

If you want any of the files/CD's for Slackware or Dropline then hollar.

Cheers

Matthew
