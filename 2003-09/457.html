Re: Mutt Procmail

Brian Marr wrote:
> Maybe I am imagining things, but I just want to be sure the mail is not
> lost.

set LOGFILE in your .procmailrc
and VERBOSE to "on" (man procmailrc)
(you then can do "mailstat /your/logfile" or just
read the logfile yourself)

also might be good to keep a backup of all mail by
having your first recipe (man procmailex):
:0 c
backup

also note your default "fall through" mailbox for
procmail is set by the DEFAULT variable and in
debian at least is /var/mail/$LOGNAME
