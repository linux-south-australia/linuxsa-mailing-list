FYI Reiserfs + md sw RAID = bad experiences

FYI.

Linux Reiserfs 3.[5,6] work well. They have ACL support, are quite quick
and all the other nice things people say about Reiser.  But in
conjunction with the md multiple devices software RAID driver:

	1) Under 2.4 and 2.5, performance is extraordinarily degraded
	compared with ext3

	2) Under 2.5 (valid through to 2.6-test*) there is a bizarre
	problem where the filesystem report no free inodes but all tools
	report that _every_ inode is free

I'm not using it any more, but I have lots of nice things to say about
XFS in 2.6, even better than the patches for 2.4.

I'm looking forward to seeing if Reiser4 makes the database-replacement
features more accessible to ordinary mortals with a scripting language.
