Camera Recomendation

Hi 

I just did my legwork and bought a digital camera. Primary concern was
Linux connectivity, the range of cameras that I found that worked with
Mandrake 9.0 is the Sony range DSC-P32/P52/P72, no fiddling required,
only needed to have GTKam installed and the camera put into PTP mode and
picture transfer was a cinch. so if you are looking for a camera that is
no fuss then give these a try

Cheers Darrin
