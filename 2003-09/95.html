IMO, Mozilla is the best browser out there for linux. check out 
http://www.mozilla.org/ - Mozilla Firebird is the standalone browser, or 
if you want something a little bigger but (arguably) a little more 
mature, try the Mozilla Suite - version 1.4 is the latest final release, 
with 1.5b the latest beta/testing release.

The Mozilla Suite includes an email reader component, and Mozilla 
Thunderbird is the standalone version of the email reader component. 
however, if you;re looking for something closer to outlook, probably 
Evolution is the way to go; i'm not sure where to download it, but 
depending on what distribution of Linux you use, it is probably included 
on the install CDs.
