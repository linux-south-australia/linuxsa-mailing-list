Re: Stress testing new servers

Get yourself a copy of memtest.   That will abuse your RAM/Proc &
Motherboard for a while.   I've had brand new memory (non ECC) fail memtest
in the past.  Just because it's new from the shop doesn't mean it aint
faulty.

Others might be able to suggest good HDD & network testing... never been a
requirement for me.
