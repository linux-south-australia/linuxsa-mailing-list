Re: ADSL? OT now

On Tue, Sep 23, 2003 at 03:28:12PM +0930, jonathan soong wrote:
 
 > my friends (and i) wouldn't want excess bills
 > + we download mp3z
 > + we would probably game
 > + we do a lot of net surfing
 > + we'd download isos and software
 > + we could run terminal services, vnc etc
 > all this is hard with limited bandwidth :)

Well, no, it isn't.  It's hard with "base-rate-plus-excess" billing plans.

Nobody you ever connect to will ever give you "unlimited" bandwidth.
It's always constrained somewhere -- either by explicit performance
limits, sneaky hidden performance limits, or by financial penalization
if you pass a predefined limit.

Base+excess plans are an example of the third category above.  There
are other alternatives which still deliver limited bandwidth but on
a billing plan that doesn't give you an excess bill.
