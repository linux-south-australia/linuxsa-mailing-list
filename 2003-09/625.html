Well personally I prefer SuSE over RedHat. But it's really just personal 
opinion. Either distrobution will be fairly stable, both have graphical 
and easy to follow installers (although if you do a graphical install

I find the SuSE configuration system easier for my purposes than the 
RedHat tools, but that's mainly because I haven't used RedHat to any 
great extent since version 6.0
