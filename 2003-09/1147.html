If I may playing devil's advocate for a moment.

I will not use that service but you mistype a domain name into your
browser and get Versign's sitefinder... Despite that fact that
you don't intend to using it. Hence you have been
coerced/seconded/drafted into accepting Sitefinder T&C despite not

ipfilter/ipfw/you_name_it firewall rules, dns hacks, etc). That could
