Hi Ismail,

Your steps look good.  I've got a couple of small comments on some of
it, but nothing serious (stuff that I agreed with has been removed):

> 4.mv .config config.orig
>   (in case of botched rebuild)

I don't think this is necessary.

> 5.mv /boot/vmlinuz /boot/vmlinuz.orig
>   (in case of botched rebuild)

I'd move this step to just before step 14 (put related steps
together).

> 6.mv /lib/modules/2.0.27 /lib/modules/2.0.27-orig
>   (in case of botched rebuild)

I'd do this just before step 13 (same reason).  Perhaps there should
be a step 5.5 too: rm -rf /lib/modules/2.0.27-orig (in case this is
your second build).

> 7. make mrproper

Generally not necessary.

> 8. make config

Even better 'make xconfig', or if you don't have X, 'make menuconfig'.

> 14. cp /usr/src/linux/arch/i386/boot/zImage /boot/vmlinuz

cp arch/i386/boot/zImage /boot/vmlinuz is a few less characters to
type.

> Any comments?

That's it.  You did a good summary.
