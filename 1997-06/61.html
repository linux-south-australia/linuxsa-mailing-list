Re: ppp solution

> i did not have success after the PPP meeting, so i'm going through the ppp
> howto.  
> 
> after looking at ppphowto, i typed in
> pppd -d -detach /dev/cua3 &
> the difference is either the -detatch or the ampersand at the end, the -d
> is debug (record) mode.

The '-d' shouldn't affect anything, the -detach says to not become a
background process, and the & says to make it a background process
anyway.  None of those should change whether it works or not.

> my next problem (sheesh:) is that netscape (thanks for the help there all:)
> won't browse outside my servers home page!?  have i set something wrong?

Probably because you didn't specify the defaultroute option (or
perhaps your ISP requires the use of a proxy server).  Add
'defaultroute' on the command line, or even better, make your
/etc/ppp/options file look like this so you can just start ppp by
typing 'pppd':
-----8<-----cut here-----8<-----
lock
defaultroute
/dev/cua3
-----8<-----cut here-----8<-----

Hope this helps,
