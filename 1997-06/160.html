I am wholeheartedly in favor of some more formal structure. Linuxsa exists
due to the motivation and enthusiasm of Matthew and Geoffrey; should they
find other things they would rather do the user's group would
disintegrate. A committee of some sort would tend to spread the load and
make it less a case of us watching them and more a communal effort.
Further, if the group is going to trade, incorporation would seem to be
a necessary protection in the event of some unforseen problem.
