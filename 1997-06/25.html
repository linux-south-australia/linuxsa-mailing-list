With the newer kernels ipfw is broken. ipfw is no longer being maintained
and you should use ipfwadm instead.

You can get it from:
ftp://ftp.xos.nl/pub/linux/ipfwadm/ipfwadm-2.3.0.tar.gz

and for Red Hat users:
ftp://ftp.redhat.com/pub/contrib/RPMS/ipfwadm-2.3.0-1.i386.rpm
