Hi there,

The first question is: why do you want to upgrade your kernel?  My
advice is: if it's working fine, don't change it (unless you are bored
and just want something to do/learn).

If you want to upgrade the kernel without recompiling it, then the
kernel and kernel-modules packages are the ones you want (or just do
an upgrade to RHL 4.2).  If you want to recompile it, then you want
kernel-source and kernel-headers.
