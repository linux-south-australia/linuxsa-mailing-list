Re: The Home and End Keys

> Hey ppl,
> 
> How can I make the home and end keys do a ctrl-a (beggining of line) and a
> ctrl-e (end of line) respecivley? I'm using bash from a console, and in a
> vt220 telnet window.

Hi Alex,

Create a file called ".inputrc" in your home directory, and place the
following four lines in it:
"\e[H": beginning-of-line
"\e[F": end-of-line
"\e[1~": beginning-of-line
"\e[4~": end-of-line

The first two are for xterm, the last two are for the console.

See bash(1) for more info (esp. see 'bind').
