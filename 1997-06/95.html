Re: reformat needed? hope not!

Hi, Helen.

Sure is an easy way, with disk prices plummeting.  The only real issue
nowadays is whether your machine's BIOS can "see" the full capacity of
current drives.  Where it can't, we've "discovered" an elegant solution:
Promise makes an EIDE controller with on-board BIOS extension that's
allowed us to use a 4GB drive on a machine whose BIOS wouldn't "see" more
than 2GB.  The "add-on" BIOS enables LBA.

Meaning, I'd guesstimate what I needed, and get 2X to 4X that in a new
drive, and plug it in.  Even for Win95 though, you'll want to partition it
in such a way as to keep cluster sizes down.  (See Partition Magic docs.)

HTH, jb
