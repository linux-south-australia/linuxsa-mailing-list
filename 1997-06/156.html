Netscape

ok the answers are here:

matt's method:
1) mkdir /usr/local/lib/netscape
2) cd /usr/local/lib/netscape
3) tar -xvzf /path/to/netscape.tar.gz .
4) cd /usr/local/bin
5) ln -s ../lib/netscape/netscape .

and a web page:
http://members.ping.at/theofilu/ninst.html
