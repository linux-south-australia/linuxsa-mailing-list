Ummm... a few things don't quite add up here:
- You have /dev/scd0 (the first SCSI CDROM drive) in your /etc/fstab,
  but the above message refers to /dev/hdb (an IDE disk or CDROM).
- The error message allegedly comes from umount, which is the
  unmounting utility, not the mounting utility.
- "default" is not a valid option in /etc/fstab.  "defaults" is, but I
  recommend in the removeable-media tip to use "noauto,user,exec".
