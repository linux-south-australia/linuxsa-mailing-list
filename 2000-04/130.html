Which distribution are you using.. Redhat, Debian, Suse ?
I suspect that it looking for 'ogin' and 'passwd' prompts
and not getting them.  We need to see the output from your
/var/log/messages file.

--How-to-do-it-from-the-command-line----------------------------
Step 1: Open a couple of xterms/consoles. 

In one of them, switch user to root, and tail the system log...
%>  su root
   (passwd)
#>  tail -f /var/log/messages

In another, su to root, and run pppd from the command line..

#> pppd -debug

There are some other options that should on this line, but they depend on
the distrution you're using.

Files to look at.. (use 'more')
/etc/ppp/options
/etc/ppp/pap-secrets
/etc/chatscripts
