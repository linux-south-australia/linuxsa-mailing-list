Presentation:

    Open Sourcing Internally Funded Projects
    ========================================

    By Matthew Tippett (Me!) 
                    
    This presentation discusses some of the currently existing problems
    with Software Development Projects, presents some current alternatives,
    including Open Source Development.

    From there a discussion covers the business benefits of Open Source
    Development, how to get started Open Sourcing a project, some negative
    aspects and finally a few real world examples where companies are 
    beginning to apply Open Source development within their organisation.
