Re: ATX power supplies

You can make an ATX power supply turn back on with the mains.
Youll have to do a bit of fiddling though with the power cables to the
motherboard though.
This means that the power switch on the front of the case will not function
anymore and to turn the machine off, it will have to be done at the wall or
by pulling out the power cord.

I dont have the exact details or guide to do it, but if anyone wants more
info, get back to me and ill make up a step-by-step guide.

MWP
