Hi Rob :-)

With bash (not sure about other shells) you can put -x on the invocation
line (either interactively at the shell prompts, or in the shell script,
ie #!/bin/bash -x), to see what is going on.

Of course it doesn't help you much if you have a logic error, you just get
to *see* getting stomped, rather than it being done silently :-)
