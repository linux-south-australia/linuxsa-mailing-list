RE: Telnet

Edit /etc/hosts.allow to include the IP of the box you want to telnet from.
Or even a range. Try the following to see if this is your reason:
tcpdmatch in.telnetd SCO_UNIX-IP


EG:
/etc/hosts.allow
in.telnetd : 10.1.1.1/24
  OR
ALL : 10.1.1.1
  OR
ALL EXCEPT in.fingerd : 10.1.1.1/255.255.255.0


Your friend:
man hosts.allow

HTH
