Re: Vi question

mark dickeson wrote:

> What I want to do is replace a line like:
> file://localhost/someuser/somedirectory/file.html
> with:
> http://some_www_address/file.html
> but the forward slashes are screwing it up.

You need to "quote" each forward slash with a backslash to get
the effect of what is known as Leaning Toothpick Syndrome (ie,
preface each / with a backslash).

:1,$s/\/\/localhost/\/\/some_www_address/

The ugly side of regular expressions, but the hours are good ...
