This looks like you have modified your config file to need the remote
host to authenticate itself on your system.... (not a normal occurence
in my experience, for dialing into an ISP)
look in /etc/ppp/options - you should have noauth (it use to be hashable
but afaik the remote authentication must now be either auth or noauth.
