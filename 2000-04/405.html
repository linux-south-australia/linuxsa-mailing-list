Re: PHP>MYSQL Connectivity

> php running, but now when trying to add mysql connectivity (using php with
> mysql databases) i got this error message:
> ..undefined function mysql_connect()
> After reading all about it on many sites, it i could only find such ways as
> a total reinstall of apache and php, which i would rather like to avoid.

PHP must be compiled with mysql support.

This is only achievable when compiling PHP with the '--with-mysql' option
to configure.

Depending on how you have got apache and mysql talking together you 
may or may not need to rebuild apache (if you became across the acronym DSO
in your building of apache in the first place then you probably won't).

Sorry, but unless PHP becomes modular, you are stuck with having to recompile
PHP as your needs grow.
