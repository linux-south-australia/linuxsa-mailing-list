RE: PPP

www.users.nf/linux has a good StepByStep on setting up PPP. It worked well
for me, and it was running in minutes. If you are that close to having it
working now, this may be all you need. 

If it drops the connection early, try putting "noauth" in the
/etc/ppp/options file. It can't hurt.
