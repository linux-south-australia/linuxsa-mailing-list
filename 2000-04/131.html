intranet solution

I am looking an linux based intranet solution for a small company which has
a features and not limit to as bellow:

1.Document Management - Securely store all company and personal files in a
central location for remote access, revision control and keyword search
2.Group Calendar - Easily organize group's schedule and prioritize tasks
3.Bulletin Boards - Share ideas company bulletin board
4.Web E-mai
5.Web Publishing - Securely upload files and documents
6.Group Contact - Give whole team access to shared contacts while
maintaining a private list, as well
7.Online Document Viewer - View documents, spreadsheets, presentations and
graphics without downloading or needing original software
8.Access Rights - Control which users have access to department and project
documents, folders and bulletin boards
9.Reminders - Schedule group or personal electronic reminders for today or
anytime in the future
