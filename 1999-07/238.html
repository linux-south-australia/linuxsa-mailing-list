Re: CD sound

On Fri, 9 Jul 1999 susie@iagu.net wrote:

> 
> 
>    sound question: 
> 
>   i have redhat 6.0,  currently operating with the 
>   enlightenment/gnome window manager, and i'd like to play
>   music CD's through the sound card - ie: without having to shift 
>   the speakers to the CD jack.
>   this worked (once) using kde's CD player and sound mixer panel,
>   and also with gnome's CD player. however, as soon as i started
>   the gnome mixer (GMIX), all CD sound was disabled. 
> 
>    has anyone else had this problem and/or
>    does anyone know how to re-enable CD music operation ?
>    (aside from rebooting)

Check that the permissions are set for "other" to read/write to /dev/dsp.
Also check that "other" has read permission to /dev/hdx (where x is your
cddrive).

Try using cdp to get it running as well as it will help you resolve any
permission errors (ie can't open device xxx)

aumix can also adjust sound levels.
