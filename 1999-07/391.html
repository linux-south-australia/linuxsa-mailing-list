Hmmm, while I know very little about QoS, it is different to ToS and seems
to be intended to allow protocols like H323 to guarantee bandwidth for
communicating parties.  ATM seems hot on QoS.  

ToS on the other hand, as stated below is a mechanism to allow routers to
handle packets differently based on the type of data being carried, low
delay, high throughput, high reliability, etc.
