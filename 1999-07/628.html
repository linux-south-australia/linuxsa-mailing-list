if the file has group permisions set to rwx and you are a member of the
same group, you have complete access to the file, no matter who owns it.

-rwxr-xr-x   1 jake     eclectic    62107 Jul 18 23:04 ioit
-rwxrwxr-x   1 bomanj   eclectic       61 Jun  8  2000 jvi

jake and bomanj can both do whatever they please to these files.
