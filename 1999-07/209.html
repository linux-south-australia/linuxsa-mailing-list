How ensure Telstra *is* limiting the bit rate on a connection

Hi,

I manage an Australia-wide Frame relay network, based here in Adelaide.
Well, actually, it covers four out of eight capitals, and will cover more
in the not too distant future.  It also uses Linux for all server functions
... -)

One of the aspects of Frame Relay means that we have a 256kbps link to
Telstra in Adelaide, with PVCs being carried on that link to Big Pond and
to the other capital city offices.

The Big Pond PVC should be limited to 128kbps, while the others can get as
much as they want.

I want to run a job now and then to check that the Internet connection is
not swamping the links to other capitals.

Thoughts welcome.
