Re: My glorious LINUX is UNSTABLE!!

Linux should be pretty blindingly fast on that sort of machine. I know
that my 333mhz celeron with 64mb of ram zips along, and the 458mhz one
at home with 160mb goes even faster.
I suspect that your problem in X is related you having no swap device
though.. linux, plus a full suite of server daemons, plus X and gnome
and enlightenment, adds up to less than 64mb, certainly. But by the time
you've loaded netscape as well I'd say you'd be using at least 32mb of
ram.
On a 64mb machine I wouldn't have thought this would be a hassle of
course!
However, if you could give us the output from running things like 'free'
and 'df' then we may be able to help further. Unfortunately, just saying
"it crashes sometimes" alone cannot help us empathically determine the
fault.

TC
