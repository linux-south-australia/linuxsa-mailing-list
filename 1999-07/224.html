Some of the Q&A comments about Linux that are there are...

What is Perl? 

Perl = Practical Extraction and Report Language 
Perl = Pathologically Eclectic Rubbish Lister 

How do I find the creation time of a file? 

You can't -- it isn't stored anywhere. Files have a last-modified time (shown by ls -l), a
last-accessed time (shown by ls -lu) and an inode change time (shown by ls -lc). 

How do I kill a process? 

Sorry, without a backup you can't. UNIX doesn't allow it.
