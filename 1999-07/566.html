Re: Adding Harddisk

Of course, one of the dangers of "tar xvpf" is when you untar
files that deny the user the right to modify them.
E.g. if you have such a directory, you can't then add files
into that directory if you preserve a u-w permission.

That's why I tend to not use the "p" unless there's some
particularly good reason to do so.
But then again, what kind of person puts u-w directories into
tar files? It has happened to me though.
Personally, I think that "u-w" files should not be tarred.
