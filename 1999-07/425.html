Re: copying or moving directories (500)

Hi... couple of ways I move large amounts of files (and dirs)

A BIG FAT WARNING\!!:  DONT USE mv if you are not sure what you are
doing!!!! If you screw up you have LOST the lot!!! 
Use cp & check that all the files are where you want them &
intact...then, and only then...rm -r or whatever!!

OK... method one:  cp -dpR /dir/files/* /dest/dir
OR    method two:  boot up MC & use it relocate the files...(for those
that havent heard of MC its a Norton Commander(DOS) for LINUX :))

Both methods work .. I use either when I create INSTANT-LINUX HDD's 
just ADD a new HDD as /dev/hdc for example - format
makefs etc etc etc & then copy the lot!!
Also use this method when I start running out of space on 1 hdd - move
dirs & files & symlink to the new location!
