more on that sound thing

You may remember I said that doing an 'esdcat "The Microsoft Sound.wav"'
played it at the right speed, while x11amp or mpg123 outputting 44100hz
stereo 16bit WAVs still went halfspeed when esdcat-ing them.

However, look at this:

[ruiner@tjcorkin media]$ esddsp wavplay "The Microsoft Sound.wav" 
Pathname:       The Microsoft Sound.wav
Sampling Rate:  22050 Hz
Mode:           Stereo
Samples:        173280
Bits:           16

So, running a half-std-rate wav file, went at the right speed, when the
ESD thought it was a 44100 hz file. When running wavplay, which adjusts
it to the correct speed of 22050, the file sounded slow again.
This supports my theory that it is exactly half speed.
However, it doesn't tell me how to fix it, or how it got broken!

Toby.

PS. The microsoft sound was the only wav file I could find around.
