Actually it's generally Unix-related. Every sysadmin or aspiring sysadmin
should have at his/her disposal the extremely handy program 'netcat'. It
is your friend. 

Today I used it to transfer several gigabytes of data between two linux
boxes, using tar to stdout piped into netcat on one end, and netcat piped
into tar on the other. Thus file permissions etc are restored, and I don't
have to worry about having 2x the necessary space to tar/untar the file on
each machine.
