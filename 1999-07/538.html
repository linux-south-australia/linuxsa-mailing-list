Re: ircd - availability and stability

Hi... no big drama setting up ircd - i have been running it on my system
for a month or so... just get hold of it from where
ever you got you installed your linux from (should be one on your
distro) & install it...config files are no big deal. If in doubt just
rtfm but i found it pretty self explanatory.

As to probs with ircd :) weeeeeeeeeeell the only one of significance is
to ensure that  you know how to OP, or someone might take over your ircd
& kick/ban you :) Of course you can always ps ax|grep ircd & kill the
process & get revenge :)

As a "local" chat server it is very good.You can stick it on the
standard irc ports (6667 etc) or you can pick an obscure high number
port if you like.

Cheers, Phil
