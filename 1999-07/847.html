Re: RAM for DECpc Lpv+ ?

Hi Ben... I have one of these pieces of fodder and it works just
fine... What I found out is that you have to place the memory in it in
exact pairs! If you have parity 8meg plate in bank0 then you have to
place andother one in bank1... Same goes for bank2 and bank3... I have
running right now parity in bank0-1 and non-parity in bank2-3... All
are 4meggers and it works... 

Try it you may like it...
