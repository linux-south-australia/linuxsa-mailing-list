RH 6.0 PRINTING

To all,

My current printing setup only works from root. This creates a problem
when printing from Netscape, Star Office or other applications. Most of
the time I run these as user. I have had a look at permissions and
changed them a number of times without any luck. This is probably due to
inexperience.

RH Printool will print directly to port and via spool directory- but
from root. 
When printing from user I am getting a "permission denied" or "cannot
create spool file xxxxxx".

I have tried a number of settings with Owner and Group for /dev/lpr &
/dev/lp. Most of these have owner as root, - and group as either daemon
or user. Do permission changes take place immediately- without reboot?

Any suggestions appreciated.
