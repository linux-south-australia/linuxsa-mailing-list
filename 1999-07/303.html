Re: Mailing list Archives suggestion & tcp/ip book?

Benjamin Close wrote:

> A while back I asked which book was best for a reference to tcp/ip
> programming under unix. At the time I never bought the book :(
> 
> What tha the name/author of the book? (There was a general agreement on
> the best book).

I don't recall the original thread, but odds are good that
the book is:

  Stevens, W. Richard
  "Unix Network Programming : Networking Apis: Sockets
   and Xti (Volume 1)"
  Prentice Hall
  ISBN: 013490012X

You will need some understanding of IP networking,
and the same author wrote the standard text:

  Stevens, W. Richard
  "TCP/IP Illustrated, Volume 1 : The Protocols"
  Addison-Wesley
  ISBN: 0201633469

However, if you have a C programming background, then
save your cash and read the BSD UNIX PSDs 20 and 21
"{Advanced] 4.4BSD Interprocess Communication Tutorial" at

  http://docs.FreeBSD.org/44doc/
