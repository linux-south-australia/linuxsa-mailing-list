Re: bash question

> 
> Hi All !
> 
> Bash's command line editor is normally emacs, but you can change it
> temporally to vi with set -o vi 
> How do you make this the default at login ?

for vi editing, place...

	`set editing-mode vi`

...in your .inputrc file.

for emacs editing, place...

	`set editing-mode real_men_use_vi`

...in your .inputrc file.
