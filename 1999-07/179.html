> On some RedHat boxes I get the following error message during bootup

IIRC the net-pf-[45] relate to appletalk and ipx.  When you do things like
/sbin/ifconfig, the kernel triggers the (attempted) loading of these 
modules.  If my guess is correct, you haven't compiled these as modules
or into the kernel.

The messages themselves are benign, you can safely ignore them.  If you
don't want them anymore, you can ...

1) Recompile them as modules (it won't really hurt will it :).
2) Turn of those modules altogether (such that kerneld won't even 
attempt to load the modules.haven't compiled these as modules
or into the kernel.

	This is done with (Haven't done it for a while :) the following
	lines in /etc/conf.modules

	alias net-pf-4 off 
	alias net-pf-5 off

To confirm for yourself dig around the kernel source in the 
'net' directories and you should find a reference to net-pf
in a couple of places.  This will allow you at least to understand why
it is complaining in the way it is.
