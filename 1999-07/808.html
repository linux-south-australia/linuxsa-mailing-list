Sod - Fixed :)

In case anyone's interested..

I love Linux.  If what had happened to our server last night happened to an
NT machine, I wouldn't be home yet.

The filesystem was corrupted.  fsck fixed the lower level filesystem
problems, but it left missing and truncated files lying about.

I grabbed my RedHat 6.0 "Bleeding Edge" CD, booted from it, chose the
"Upgrade" option, picked a few new packages that weren't in RedHat 5.2, like
logwatch for example, and hit "continue".

After fixing a few minor configuration issues, the system is now Pretty Much
Perfect (TM).  And completely upgraded :)  My total typing time was about 20
minutes max - the rest of the time was waiting for the CD to copy files.

Viva la Linux/Unix/Redhat/Bootable CD's/backups!
