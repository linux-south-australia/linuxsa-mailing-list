> Does Anybody ever try to build a linux system from scratch ?

	Sort of. I built a bootdisk (kernel, base libs, ifconfig + few select
	binaries) on an existing machine, and used said disk to format, 
	partition and network a 'blank' computer by hand (ie even the bootdisk
	was built from the commandline -- I am yet to find a real, up-to-date
	script that builds a decent bootdisk).

	Then it was just a matter of ftp'ing the binaries for gcc across,
	and then spending 2 days (it was only a 486 66) compiling pretty much
	everything else....
