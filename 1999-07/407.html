RE: copying or moving directories (500)

Can you not just say:

  mv /usr/doc/* [destination]

??

Or does * expand into too long an argument list.. :(

If that doesn't work, use find:

  find /usr/doc/* -exec mv \{\} [destination dir] \;

find rocks :)
