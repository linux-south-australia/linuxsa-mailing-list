Re: copying or moving directories (500)

There's lots of ways:

1:  $ mv doc newdoc; mkdir doc
2:  $ mv doc/* newdoc
3:  $ ls -1 doc | sed 's/.*/mv & newdir/' | sh
4:  $ ls -1 doc | xargs echo | sed 's/.*/mv & newdir/' | sh
5:  $ gzcat /usr/man/cat1/* |  more

And so on.
