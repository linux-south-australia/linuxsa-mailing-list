Shit am I the only one who replies on this list?
Log in as root and type:

chmod o+rw /dev/mixer

You have a permission problem. The user your trying to run aumix as
doesn't haver permission to run the mixer.

Oh, can I give you a suggestion. When reporting errors give a bit more
detail.

Ie what soundcard you have, whether you copiled the sound as a module in
the kernel, what cd drive you have and where it is attached. (type dmesg)
