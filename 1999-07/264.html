RE: Where can I download Red Hat Linux 6.0 and source code ?

Try www.redhat.com and follw the links to the download section. You should
be able to find an ftp site near you (I believe that Netcraft and AARNet are
both listed under Australia). You should also be able to find distributors
or user groups near you, and through these, you will probably be able to
find someone who will burn a CD for you (or you could buy the RH Boxed set
for $lots). Using a CD (Genuine or burnt - both equally good & legal) is the
easiest way to install by a long shot, unless you have no CD drive, or have
infinite, reliable bandwidth and storage available to you. Best bet is to
get the CD and download the updates.

HTH
