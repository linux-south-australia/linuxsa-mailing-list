Re: Viper 330 not responding to xconfig

Hi Ben et al...

I hate to state the obvious :)  but if probing your video card causes a
serious lockup... then the obvious solution is to NOT probe it & everything
will be fine.  Unless REDHAT-6.00 has changed XF86Setup/xf86config,  the
"don't probe" option will be there,  and its there for the exact reason you
are having probs....because sometimes some video cards DO cause lockups
when probed!

I have had cards which probed ok under 1 linux distro but not another......
I have also had video cards that only sometimes locked up when probed!!
Just bypass the probe & 99% of the time X is fine!

As to why it happens... I presume you have heard of Murphy's Law...well
meet my mate O'Toole  :)

Cheers , Phil

PS:  O'Toole's Law..... Murphy was an Optimist :)
