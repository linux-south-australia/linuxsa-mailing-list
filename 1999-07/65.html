> Will I need a PS printer?..... or will it support stuff like Canon BJ's

Printing is one of the least attractive aspects of Unix.  The Canon BJ
is supported by whatever programs support it.  Additionally, programs
which support PostScript printers probably could work with your Canon
using "ghostscript", which is a PostScript interpreter.  There is an
adequately good range of printers which work with ghostscript, the Canon
might be one.  Do some research on the web to find out this one.
