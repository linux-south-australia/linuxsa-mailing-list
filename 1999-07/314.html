Phil Pittard wrote:

 > I cant find anything terribly helpful in the docs ... what I want to do
 > is to uncomment the above lines & modify the second to something along
 > the lines of:
 > acl QUERY urlpath_regex  cgi-bin \?
 > no_cache deny www.hotmail.com
 > no_cache deny hotmail.com

acl hotmailsux urlpath_regex hotmail.com
no_cache permit hotmailsux


Note the use of "permit" -- You're defining ACL elements ("hotmailsux")
which will NOT be cached:  You permit the ones which aren't cached, and
deny the ones that will be.  Straightforward and intuitive, yes? :-)
