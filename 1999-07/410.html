Re: Two numbers

Regarding automatic search of phone numbers...


> Would like to check out, how do I insert two phone numbers for dialing
> purposes to my ISP.
> 
> Currently I have one, but that number will be always busy, so if the that
> number is busy, how do I jump to next number for it to dial.
> 
> Is there a way ?

Yes.  The "pppd" daemon typically uses "chat" to negotiate the
connection.  You can read 

	man 8 chat

to find about the things that chat can do, and

	man 8 pppd

to find out how the daemon calls "chat".


Of course, this may not be the answer that you are after.  For
instance, RedHat provides a set of scripts which automate "pppd"
startup, and configuration tools that feed information to these
scripts.  You would first want to investigate your distributions
versions of these tools to see whether your needs are catered for, but
ultimately you may find that you have to do a bit of your own
scripting.
