Signal to noise ratio

The Signal to Noise ratio on this list is rapidly declining. This is caused
primarily by people sending inflammatory comments. If you have a suspicion
that the email you are about to send could offend (or be easily
misinterpreted), please rephrase or refrain. It doesn't take long to think
about what you are sending, and is very important when you consider the
sheer number and variety of people on this list. Don't bring the entire
community into disrepute through stupid emails.
