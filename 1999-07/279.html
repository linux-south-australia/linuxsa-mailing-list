Hmmm, this is one of the most interesting, fundamental misunderstandings I
have seen in a while :-)

The computer talks to the hard drives, via IDE or SCSI controllers, and
drives don't, generally, talk to each other.

That said, what you probably want to know about is how does Computer A make
(parts) of its hard disk available for Computer B to access, and vice versa.

That is where Samba comes it.
