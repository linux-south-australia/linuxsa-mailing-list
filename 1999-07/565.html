Re: Adding Harddisk

> Try using tar like this:
> (cd /original/directory; tar -cf - .) | (cd /mnt/new/directory; tar -xvf
> - .)
> 
> Do it as root, and it should maintain links,ownership, modes, etc.
> `man tar` for details of other ways to use it.

Rather than 'tar -xvf', it's better to use 'tar -xvpf' when you want
to preserve permissions, otherwise your umask will be taken into
account and files that are mode 777 (rwxrwxrwx) will end up mode 755
(rwxr-xr-x) or similar.
