Re: redhat 5.2 Perl no good!!!?

Alan Kennington wrote:

> PS. The redhat 5.2 perl thing does raise the question of whether
> redhat do just include the latest build of other software too,
> in an effort to get the latest and best, without

RedHat took significant flack over distributing an early
version of Gimp in 5.2 because it was marked "stable"
whereas the later versions were marked "beta".

With the devaluing of "alpha" and "beta" it's just plain
impossible for RedHat to get it right all the time.

Although I do agree that part of the role of a distributor
is to have enough package knowledge to make wise version
choices and distributing a flaky version of Perl isn't
encouraging in this respect.

Nor is 6.0 coming out with trivial bugs that are marked
as "fixed" in their Bugzilla database.
