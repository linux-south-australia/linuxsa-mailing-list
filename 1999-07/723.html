G'day Alan,

I'm not really a hardware guru.  

When we bought our last server (I think it was in February or March),
it was from a place in Victor Harbor named All-Biz.  

I was impressed that they honoured our request that we did NOT want
Windows installed on it.  They also provided a network card that was
ISA instead of PCI mainly because they felt the PCI one wouldn't work
with our Linux set up. However, we were able to explain that Red Hat
5.2 could handle it and they swapped cards with us without a problem. 

The fact they were fairly knowledgeable (if just a little out of date
in terms of the Linux kernal at the time) was pretty impressive. 

Perhaps others can list good experience with other stores too?
