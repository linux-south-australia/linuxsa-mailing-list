short note about last night's talk

> Other than the fact Linux has a cool name, could someone explain why I
> should use Linux over BSD?

No.  That's it.  The cool name, that is.  We worked very hard on
creating a name that would appeal to the majority of people, and it
certainly paid off: thousands of people are using linux just to be able
to say "OS/2? Hah.  I've got Linux.  What a cool name".  386BSD made the
mistake of putting a lot of numbers and weird abbreviations into the
name, and is scaring away a lot of people just because it sounds too
technical.
 *** (Linus Torvalds' follow-up to a question about Linux) ***

<note> 
the previous quotation is purely for a cheap laugh, not at Linux, or BSD
but in the simple fact that everything has it's uses and it's followers
and admirers especially in the computer industry.  I mean, I actually
know personally, people that think Windows 9x is wonderful :)

Humourously

Tim
