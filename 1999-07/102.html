New APC Mag

Just noticed walking past the newsagent - there is a monstrous stack of new
APC's with a glorious yellow cover dominated by a life-size Tux penguin,
with the glaring title 'Is Windows Cracking'.

Small print invites readers to look at the competive analysis of various
OSen - Linux, MacOS, BeOS, Win98, Win2000.

I did not bother reading the review - (no doubt they just give everything 5
stars in every category, and make some non-committal summary), but it is
nice to see the banner headline and the penguin being shoved in people's
faces where it cannot be ignored.

It is truly frightening how many Corporate IT Managers determine their
companies future IT strategy solely on the basis of a cursory glance through
a pulp-computer magazine that they flick through at the airport terminal (it
gives them a certain air of 'Hey - watch out, I am into Change Management, -
you know, IT, corporate communications, strategic partnership management,
that sort of thing')  (Gawd I hate those CPA ads on TV)

At least it might mean that in the near future, certain brainless strategic
decisions might actually fall on the right side for once ....
