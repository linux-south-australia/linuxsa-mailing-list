RE: Minimum Suse System dialogue with antiflame content in general

I disagree,

it is much much quicker, more polite and sociable to just say look at
www.*** or look in the HOWTO-XXX than to give some-one a mail-bashing.

Apart form pooling our expertise and knowledge the mailing list can provide
a valuable resource to newbies, many who may be coming from MS environments
and are missing many of the basic unix commands and file structure
conventions we all take for granted.

We like to promote unix based solutions here. By emails such as yours you
are doing just the opposite.

I have only been on this mailing list a very short time, and whilst the
topics are varied and interesting the real disappointing thing is all the
shit that is flung. It really does not portray us as unix users as a
supportive and professional mob.

We all would like to see linux dominate the OS market, but this is
definitely not the way to start on this path.

I always try and help newbies, I remember the dim dark ages when I knew Jack
(and probably still do!).

Like the last one I helped; turned out they got 59 flames to the mail post
and one useful response (mine). Turns out that she is looking at Linux as an
alternative to Netware in her organisation. Demeaning and content-free email
responses => Netware or NT up one, Linux down one.

The trend is here fellows; Keep up the good work, and god only knows how
many people we can scare away from linux, no matter how good it is.


Regards

Tarek
(An ashamed linux user)
