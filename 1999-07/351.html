Re: redhat 5.2 Perl no good!!!?

Mark,

As an on-list response to that, when I fetched the latest perl
source from http://www.perl.com and re-built/installed it,
and then re-made the perl/Tk source, the result was
astonishingly good -- the demos had me saying "wow" about
40-50 times (I lost count) in about 10 minutes.
It's a really neat GUI API.

So the answer is that, yes, the redhat 5.2 perl is a "broken"
pre-alpha distribution that should never have been issued on
redhat disks.

Since this is purportedly fixed with redhat 6.0, I guess this
is just historical interest now, except for anyone
who still has redhat 5.2 and wants to install perl/tk.
