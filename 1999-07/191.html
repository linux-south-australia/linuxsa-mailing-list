Re: Freezing under X Windows

I have a "cheap" Skywell VooDoo2 Banshee card (2D & 3D in one card) and
I haven't had any trouble with it once I found an appropriate X server. 
The standard 3DFX X server (I can't remember the link offhand) that
someone on this list very kindly posted  about a month or so back, works
rather well.  32 bit colour at 1024x768 and it's at least 3 times faster
to start up than winblows 95 on the same machine.  I actually have less
trouble with this card under linux than I do in wb95.  But that's
personal experience :)
Has anyone else used these cards with success?
