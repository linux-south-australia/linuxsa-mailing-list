Re: Squid

Hi...

> I would like to know, how to restrict certain IP Addresses from surfing the
> www.

Try something like this (in squid.conf):

---
acl allowedmachines src 192.168.1.5/255.255.255.0
192.168.1.6/255.255.255.0
acl all src 0.0.0.0/0.0.0.0

http_access allow allowedmachines
http_access deny all
---

Or if that would be too much of a pain
---
acl mydomain src 192.168.1.0/255.255.255.0
acl all src 0.0.0.0/0.0.0.0
acl deniedmachines src 192.168.1.3/255.255.255.0
192.168.1.20/255.255.255.0

http_access deny deniedmachines
http_access allow mydomain
http_access deny all
---

One of the two examples above will be easier than the other depending on
how many machines you have.  The former will always be more secure, but
if you have 200 machines on your network it could get ugly unless you
write some kind of script to do it for you.

I would certainly recommend a http_access deny all at the end, so that
people from outside your domain can't use your proxy.

If you need clarification on any of this feel free to e-mail me direct.
