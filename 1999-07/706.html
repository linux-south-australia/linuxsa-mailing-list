Hi.

C41S, time to open your eyes, move away from the screen for a bit, and
see what is happening in the real world. There _are_ people in
Australia who cannot afford a home pc. To suggest that they go to the
library for net access takes a lot of the power away from the ease of
information access.

If you can't leave the screen, how about dropping by the Australian B.
of Stats, and check out how many families are running subsistance these
days. Guess what? It's growing!

Yes there's plenty of $ in Australia, but only for the lucky (that's
US) Spare a thought for those less fortunate than ourselves, will you?
The proposition that "because _I_ can afford a pc then the world is
rosy, and those who cannot can go to the library" is offensive.

Nobody would WANT their friend/kid/lover accessing the net via C64, but
if that was the only way, I'd welcome it!

Michael
