Why do you want a meeting devoted to what is probably the *worst* way
to install Linux imaginable?  When anyone on the planet can walk down
to their local bookstore, computer shop or mail-order from Netcraft to
spend a few sheckels on a CD, why on *earth* would anyone install 600
Mbytes of stuff (at 19c per Mbyte and/or ~$1 per hour) from the net?

Useful tip for any newbies installing Linux for the first time: Beg,
borrow, buy or steal a CD.  FTPing the bits is Not Cool (tm).
