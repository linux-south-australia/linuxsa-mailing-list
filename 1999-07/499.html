Setting up Dial-up connections without needing Telstra

Hi,

I have no doubt that anyone who has had to set up dial-up connections has
found the whole process very frustrating.

Unless you have access to a PABX and two lines into your cubical, you may
need two lines into your testing site.  Of course, you can hook up a couple
of wires between the modems, but then you don't get dial tone, etc.

There is a device available that allows you to plug two modems into it and
pretend that you have the whole Telstra network right there in a box the
size of a small four port hub.

I have found such devices invaluable for testing client setups at my office
before sending the devices out to the client.

If anyone is interested, I have a line on getting them.
