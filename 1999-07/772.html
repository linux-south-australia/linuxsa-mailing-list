Since a few of you have asked this, I'll regurgitate the answer
I've provided in personal email to some of you:

We source our PC UNIX servers at work from AusCom Computers, contact
David Conway, 0418 803 705 (I'm not connected with AusCom in any way
other than as a satisfied customer).  We've bought lots of them (over
a dozen) and have only had one failure.  We run FreeBSD or Linux on
them, depending on the application we're putting them to.

The ones we buy are in 3RU ATX cases (lots of space for disks).  2RU 
cases are also available.

Penguin Computing has some rather expensive 1RU systems too (about
AU$4k for a system with a 9Gbyte SCSI disk and fast Pentium III).  
You have to buy in US dollars.
