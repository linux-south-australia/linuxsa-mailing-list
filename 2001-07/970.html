I'm no export on this, but...

try running `last`, to see who logged on and when.

If there's an entry there where there shouldn't be, you've found your
intruder.

If not, you've proven nothing -- the intruder could have covered his/her/its
tracks.

Also try looking for binaries that have changed (need to compare file sizes,
modification dates, etc - may need a safe system to check with), hidden
("...", ".. ", etc) directories and files, and so on...

I'm no expert on this, so I'll let the rest of y'all comment...
