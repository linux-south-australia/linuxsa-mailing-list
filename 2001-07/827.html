Re: simple squid setup

On Mon, Jul 16, 2001 at 10:29:57PM +0930, Dave Fitch wrote:
> So obviously I haven't got the hang of the deny/allow stuff!
> I want to deny everyone access to my squid except 
> 192.168.1.0/255.255.255.0 who can do whatever they like.

ok, so the order is important! (makes sense I guess)
I can get to websites now.  Unfortunately it seems
like it's allowing anyone to connect to it!
(mind you I also appear to have 515 - the printer port
open as well which I wasn't aware of)

this is the vital bit of squid.conf:

acl local src 192.168.1.0/255.255.255.0
http_access allow local   
acl all src 0.0.0.0/0.0.0.0
http_access deny all
