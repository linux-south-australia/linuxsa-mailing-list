Hi Damien,

We have them where I am working. I don't know about your problem with
putting multiple networks off two Y's but I can give you the wireing diagram
:


Main: 1 - Jack 1 pin 1
	2 - Jack 1 pin 2
	3 - Jack 1 pin 3
	4 - Jack 2 pin 2
	5 - Jack 2 pin 1
	6 - Jack 1 pin 6
	7 - Jack 2 pin 3
	8 - Jack 2 pin 6

Hope this helps.
