> I'm thinking of buying one, but being poor I'll be stuck with a choice of

What's wrong with that? Linux, with a reasonable amount of memory, will

> There isnt any issues with Linux running on AMD chips is there?

No. In fact, Windows 95 totally bombs on my AMD K6-II's and K6-III's
whereas Linux and FreeBSD go on a treat.

> As far as my (limited) understanding goes, as long as it's an x86 it will

It could also be a Sparc, a DEC Alpha (well, Compaq), the thing that
runs IBM 370's, a PowerPC, possible a G4 notebook...the short answer is
yes. The long answer is that Linux has been ported to many architectures
some of which have been put into laptop form.
