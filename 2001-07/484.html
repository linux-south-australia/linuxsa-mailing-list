Re: Why do we hate HTML mail?

I don't have a problem with html email. I prefer text, because it is
usually more readable, and I dont care about formatting/fonts and I
really hate stupid colours (I just got one html email with black quotes,
blue text and a purple background!). But the main reason I havnt said
anything in this html debate, is I dont really care, and my mailer
handles html fine. I think it is preferable to try an avoid it, but it
is no biggie to me.
