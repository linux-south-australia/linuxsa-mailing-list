Virus attack

Hi All,
    Before you all get home and check your mail and send me
a message saying you received a virus from me.....I KNOW! It
slipped through one of my windoze boxes that I had been
slack about updating.

    If you haven't found it yet, if you receive a strange
e-mail from me with an attachment of Word files or something
called CFGWIZ32.exe or it seems anything else .exe, THEN IT
IS PROBABLY INFECTED - DO NOT RUN IT ON A WINDOZE MACHINE.

    The virus is called PE_Magistr.A. and is a nasty one
that affects Windoze .exe files and can do nasty things to
boot records, and then helpfully sends a copy of itself to
everyone in the address book of your favourite mailer.

    As far as I know, it doesn't affect Linux, so disregard
this message, but alot of people still dual boot or use
Windoze boxes on the other side of a Linux firewall. Sorry
for any inconvenience caused.
