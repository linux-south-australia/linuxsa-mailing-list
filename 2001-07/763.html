Re: filtering mails using pine

On Sunday 15 July 2001 13:26, Ravishankar sarma wrote:
> I have the following queries
> 1.can you explain clearly how i can filter mails using
> pine

Pine does basic internal filtering quite well. Just follow the instructions. 
In the menus just go (S) Setup, (R) Rules, (F) Filters and then (A) Add the 
filters you want. 

> 2.Can I configure PINE in such a that the received
> mail directly goes to folder?if it is so how?

You can filter on the usual basic stuff like To and From and stuff. Just 
create the folders you want and then the filters. It's been a few months 
since I switched from Pine to KMail, but I found it quick and easy. Pine is a 
very nice mailer...
