Re: nslookup and resolving

Dave Fitch wrote:

> yes, but don't forget it works fine on this machine itself, it's
> from other machines on my network that I get the error in my
> original message (where obviously 127.0.0.1 refers to itself and
> not my machine here running bind!).

The problem is with nslookup being anal, and trying to do a reverse
name lookup on your (from memory) 192.168.x.x address, for which I
suspect you don't actually have set up in your BIND config.  Try
using dig instead; "dig hostname" for forward lookups, "dig -x
xxx.xxx.xxx.xxx" for reverse.
