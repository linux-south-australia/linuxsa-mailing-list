[OT, sort of] My filesystem is funny

Hi all,

After hours trying to sort out my mess of a filesystem i found a way to
amuse myself.
Simply giggle! ( ls - lAhRF / > root_filesystem.txt ) But be carefull, you
may find yourself chuckling for ages. My root_filesystem.txt took over 5
minutes to write, is 453976 lines long and weighs 27 Meg.

I wondered who would have the funniest file system (largest lAhRF result) in
the world.
But I hate to think of all the crackers lAhRF(ing) away at ISP's and other
target systems.

Linux is funny sometimes.
