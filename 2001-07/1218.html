Re: linux X windows screen lock useless?

Actually, Mandrake comes with all those groups pre-configured.. If you want
to let your housemate use the cdburner, just add him to the cdburner group.
If you want to let someone use sound, add them to the sound group.

But, point taken, yes, you could develop some new problems for some people..
but I'd say people who are worried about the vty-switching-and-ctrl-z-ing
are probably people who are able to deal with those.
