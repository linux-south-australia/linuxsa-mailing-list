Hi.

The problem appears to be that you want to use your isp as a mail store.
I'm sure there are a lot of people on this list who collect their mail
from the isp and store it on their own mail server. That way it is
accessible and you only have to delete it once. If you wanted, you could
run imap on your own mail server...
