I prefer 'linuxSAhelp' or something similar as this
does not imply 'expert' vs 'newbie'.  I think a 'help'
list would still be of interest to most people on this
list and would recieve the same level of attention -
although 'new initiates' may not feel so overwhelmed
as they would on the 'traditional' list.
