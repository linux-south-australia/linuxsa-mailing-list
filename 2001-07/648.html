Re: Which book for Redhat Apache & Squid

On Fri, Jul 13, 2001 at 11:12:41AM +0930, Danny Hanrahan wrote:

> I'm very new to Linux and would like some help on what/where I could
> I could buy a good book that will guide me through the installation of
> Apache & Squid

There is plenty of online documentation available that takes you through
these tasks.

See http://www.linuxdoc.org/
    http://www.devshed.com/
    http://www.apache.org/
    http://www.squid-cache.org/

However, if you wish to purchase a *real* book made out of *real* paper,
http://www.everythinglinux.com.au/ is a good place to look.

I'd also recommend the Linux PocketBook (from the people who write
Australian Personal Computer magazine). It's a great tool when you're
starting with Linux.
