hardware - scsi card help.

Hi guys.

Have a problem with a friends PC that surprised me. 

He has a new PC with an intel 815 M/B and wants to install a scsi card. The 
card is an aha-2920 (well, future domain 36C70)

Now, this card works fine on my PC with a Soyo M/B, but on the 815 chipset 
M/B the PC locks on bootup just after the first bios screen - ie when it goes 
to the scsi card bios message I guess. On the 315 all you get is a little 
white flashing cursor and that's it. On the the Soyo it has the scsi bios 
message and scans for devices, etc, then away it goes after a brief pause. On 
the Soyo the card is easily detected by MDK 8.0 and so forth...

Anyone see this before? Any solutions? Looks as if the card doesn't like the 
M/B hardware and that would be fairly fatal I guess. Unless a M/B bios 
upgrade would do something...

Anyway, not my machine, so it's more of a curiousity than anything. I've 
never seen a pci card hang a machine like that.
