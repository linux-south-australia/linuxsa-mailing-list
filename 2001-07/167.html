Kernel 2.4.6 changelog

Hi,

I hope people don't mind - but I though this would be
a good opportunity to learn more about the kernel so
I have pasted the 2.4.6 changelog.  Would anyone be
willing to go through interesting bits (what they 
consider interesting or important) and explain what it
means?  For example - what is sync()?

Should the 2.4 kernel have been released before these
bugs were worked out?  Are they worked out?

--final:
-pre9:
