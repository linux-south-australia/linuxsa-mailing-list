This shows that your machine was flooded with packets.



> Jul 18 19:20:30 linux-pdc login: FAILED LOGIN 1 FROM /dev/tty1 FOR root, Authentication failure

This would normally indicate that somebody was sitting at the machine, trying

> Jul 18 19:22:53 linux-pdc init: Id "2" respawning too fast: disabled for 5 minutes

except that somebody else, admittedly from a rival linux distribution, did
