RE: Redhat on Compaq

An update ....

I got my old RedHat 6.1 CD from home and got it to install on the Compaq
server. This then formatted and activated the swap space in the step after
creating it. This then continued with the install and worked OK.

HOWEVER - once booted, the server still only reported 14MB of RAM out of the
64MB that was in there. I then had to add the line
append="mem=64M" to the lilo.conf file. Rebooted and there was the 64MB
sitting in the meminfo, and no kernel panic :-).

Does anyone know why the installer in RH7.1 would not even get to the first
screen? Does the installer need more than 14MB of RAM to install? 

Thanks,
