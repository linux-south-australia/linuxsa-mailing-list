Re: Sendmail over dialup

Lots of places reject mail from an improperly identified relay.  If
you can't correctly tell them who you are, they often will refuse to
accept your spam^H^H^H^Hmail.  Your ISP should be less demanding of
you; they should accept and forward your email regardless of who you
think you are.  Set sendmail's smarthost to your ISP's mail relay.
