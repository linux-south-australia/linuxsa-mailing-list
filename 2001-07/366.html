The output chain in IPTables is only for locally generated packets as
opposed to IPChains/IPFWADM where both locally generated packets and
forwarded packets hit the output chain (ie. anything going to an
interface).  Therefore when it says "some packets" it is not refering
to packets passing thru the box (ie being forwared) as they don't hit
the output chain anyway.  It is referring to those special cases like
ICMP, as it mentioned.  

Interestingly you can't do a similar thing in the input chain as the
there may be multiple processes listening (think tcpdump).  You could
list all processes I guess.
