> The drivers from the nvidia site work really well for the TNT2 card, and 
> XFree86 4.x also supports it. Don't know about RH, but Mandrake 8 supports
> it with no problems. For the money, it's a pretty good card.

Mandrake may have some problems detecting your TNT card. It originally
detected mine as a Riva128, which caused problems with the NVidia 3D
accelerated drivers for XFree86 4.x. It allowed me to choose from a list
of X3, X3 accelerated (not the NVidia one) or X4 on installation.
I chose 4. When I used the Mandrake display tool to change to card
setting, it installed all the X3 software without giving a choice for 
version 4 and reset the X link to the SVGA X3 driver. If you do it buy had
or are prepeared for some traps like this you will do well.
The latest NVidia 3D accelerated module works well and installs cleanly,
unlike earlier versions, and it addresses any potential conflicts with
MESA. After all of that, it works very well.
