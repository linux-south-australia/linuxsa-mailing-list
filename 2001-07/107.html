The real issue is, most people who send mail in HTML won't understand
this message.  "Huh?  What's HTML?  What have these blokes been
smoking?".  And what do we do with multipart/alternative?

We've discussed filtering HTML on the FreeBSD mailing list.  The
biggest issue we have is that messages like this get lost:

> LinuxSA WWW: http://www.linuxsa.org.au/  IRC: #linuxsa on irc.linux.org.au
> To unsubscribe from the LinuxSA list:
>   mail linuxsa-request@linuxsa.org.au with "unsubscribe" as the subject

As a result, these are the idiots who also send unsubscribe messages
to the list.  We've finally given in on this issue: we now process
empty messages with the subject "unsubscribe" as the sender presumably
intended.
