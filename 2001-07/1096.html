John Edwards wrote:

> AFAIK, AT&T research (now known as Lucent) invented unix as software to run

No, they wrote it as something to do when they weren't allowed to
work on Multics anymore (hence Unix, being a pun on Multics).
Originally originally, it was used for writing up patents, hence
tools like nroff and troff go back almost as far as Unix itself.

The exchange stuff came in later, and the PABXs later still (when
computing power allowed it).
