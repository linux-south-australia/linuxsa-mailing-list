Re: Strange C problem ?

> But when I replace the function call with a macro as :
> #define fun1(a) fun2(a,0)
> the results are unchanged.

The #define is only effective if it is included:
a) in all compilands that call fun1; and
b) before the first call to fun1 in each compiland.

Remove the declaration and definition of fun1, leaving just fun2, and
recompile everything; the compiler will hilight your mistakes.
