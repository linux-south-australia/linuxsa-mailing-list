Obviously we have a number of misunderstandings here:

1.  Nobody's trying to segregate newbies.  But we are trying to teach
    them, and explaining the evilness of HTML is one of them.
    Explaining that it may also piss off the only person who is
    capable of answering the message should also help.

2.  Starting additional mailing lists will reduce the traffic, but it
    will also reduce the effectiveness of the lists.  FreeBSD has both
    a questions@ and a newbies@ mailing list; they tend to get used
    for the same thing by different people.  I'd really like to can
    the newbies@ mailing list and migrate the people to questions@,
    and maybe find a non-intrusive way of explaining that you need a
    certain amount of intelligence to perform certain functions, such
    as running a computer or sending a mail message, but we're
    prepared to help with lack of experience.
