Re: Boot problem.

I'd suggest your new kernel is trying to setup a graphical mode that your
video card can't deal with properly.
Boot into one of your old kernels and check out how you setup your 246
kernel - try turning off the framebuffer options, or playing with different
settings.. RTFM as needed. ;)
