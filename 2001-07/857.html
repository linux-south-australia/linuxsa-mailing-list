Re: read from stderr

Fülöp Balázs wrote:
> 
> ok, but how to assign a the contents of it to a variable (or maybe an
> array)?

read name_of_variable

will read from stdin.  You can't read from stderr.  If you
want to read from the controlling terminal explicitly then
say

read name_of_variable < /dev/tty

All this is amazingly well documented: "man bash" accesses
the technical documentation and "info bash" accesses less
compact documentation.

There's also a number of HOWTOs and books that Google
will find.  I'm disappointed that you chose to hassle
David after he listed a huge number of other sources
of information.
