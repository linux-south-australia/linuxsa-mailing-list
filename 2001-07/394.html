Re: USB support in 2.2?

On Tue, Jul 10, 2001 at 12:27:32AM +0930, Stephen White wrote:

> Is USB support available in kernel 2.2, or do I need to go to 2.4?

There is USB support in 2.2, but it's not as comprehensive as 2.4.

> Does gPhoto support the Canon IXUS v via the USB interface?

Last time I looked, the CVS version of gPhoto had some form of support
for it, but it wasn't that reliable.

I'd recommend you purchase a USB CF Card reader (from SanDisk). That
works quite well (Linux 2.2 sees it as a SCSI device). I believe I've
mentioned something about this (CF Card Readers for Linux, I believe
the thread was called).

Have a look through the archives, as well as on the Linux USB supported
devices list, http://www.qbik.ch/usb/devices/
