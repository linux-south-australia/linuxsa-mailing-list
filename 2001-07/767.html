Just a suggestion, but you might want to stop using swat to configure
Samba. The smb.conf file isn't all that difficult to work with once you
get used to it. Swat does a bad job at creating smb.conf files, without
making it any easier to use. If you need documentation on the smb.conf
file, theres the smb.conf (5) man page and the 'Using Samba' book which
was included with most distributions of Samba after and including
version 2.0.7.
