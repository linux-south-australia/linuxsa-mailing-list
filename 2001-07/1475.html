http://www.open.com.au/radiator

Commercial product.  God's own RADIUS server.  Supports everything you
want and a good many things you never even thought about, but suddenly
find that you need :-)

You get the source when you buy it, and can locally modify it as much
as you like;  you can't redistribute it, though.  It's well-written enough
that it's easily extensible, though:  In the unlikely event that you
find something which Radiator doesn't do which you think you might want,
it's very easy to build your own modules to handle it.  And you get 
excellent support in case you get stuck.

Produced in Australia, too.
