Re: squid running to aliased IPs, and user IP accounting

On 09 Jul 2001 15:25:58 +0930, Toby Corkindale wrote:

> I'm still at a total loss as to how to account for inward-bound logins.. Eg.
> if someone ssh's into the box, sshd is not running as the user who proceeds
> to login.

In my experience, SSH traffic doesn't account for much. What I'd do is
count the traffic that hits your SSH ports and then write a script to
count up the data and output it in a reasonable format. Give it a go for
a month to see whether or not it's even worth your while.

I've got one here that works with rinetd (a port forwarding mechanism
useful on 2.2-based systems) that outputs a bunch of interesting data.
It shows SSH traffic taking up about 3MB over 2 1/2 months.
