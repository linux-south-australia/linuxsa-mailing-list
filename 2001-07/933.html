Richard Russell wrote:

> Hmm... I'll have to find out what RST means (/me furthers his TCP/IP
> education)

It's the packet a TCP connection sends when something is amiss (amongst
other reasons).  One of the scan techniques is to send odd packets
without the SYN bit set which circumvents most firewall rules for
blocking TCP connections, and watch what comes back.  Such a scan can
determine what services you're running, but still can't get at you.

about that bit ... :-).
